🆕 OSRS New Player Guide 2026 — From Zero to Hero

Published: July 3, 2026 20+ Beginner Guides Updated: July 2026 All account types

OSRS is massive. Over 20 years of content, 23 skills, 170+ quests, hundreds of bosses, and a player-driven economy that shifts every day. For a brand new player, that's not exciting — it's overwhelming. Where do you even start? Which skills matter? How do you make your first GP without wasting hours?

This hub solves that. We've organized everything you need as a brand new player into an 8-week roadmap. Each week builds on the last: from your first login in Week 1 to killing your first boss in Week 6 and graduating from beginner status in Week 8. No fluff, no detours — just the fastest path from zero to self-sufficient.

If you have 15 minutes, start with the New Player Complete Overview. If you want the full structured plan, follow the 8-week roadmap below. Either way, you'll be playing with confidence by tomorrow.
