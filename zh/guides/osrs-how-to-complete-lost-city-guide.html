OSRS How to Complete Lost City Quest Guide 2026 — Dramen Staff & Fairy Rings— 失落之城任务完整攻略

📜 Quest Guide — Updated June 2026（任务攻略 · 2026年6月更新）

【失落之城是OSRS最重要的任务之一——完成后解锁德拉门法杖(Dramen Staff)，让你能使用精灵圈(Fairy Ring)网络，这是游戏中强大的传送系统。本攻略涵盖逐步流程，包括树精(Tree Spirit)Boss战斗和恩特拉纳(Entrana)武器限制。以下是完整英文攻略内容 👇】

Lost City is one of the most important quests in OSRS — completing it unlocks the Dramen Staff, which gives you access to the Fairy Ring network, arguably the most powerful transportation system in the game. This guide walks you through every step, including the Tree Spirit boss fight and Entrana's weapon restrictions.

2. Step-by-Step Walkthrough（逐步流程攻略）

  1. Start the Quest: （开始任务） Go to Lumbridge Swamp (south of Lumbridge Castle). Find the group of adventurers in the south-east corner of the swamp. Talk to any of them about Zanaris — they'll mention a Leprechaun who knows more.
  2. Find the Leprechaun: （找到小妖精） The Leprechaun is hiding in a tree near the adventurers. Right-click the tree and select "Search" to find him. He'll tell you about the Dramen Tree in Entrana Dungeon and warn about the Tree Spirit that guards it.
  3. Travel to Entrana: （前往恩特拉纳） Go to Port Sarim docks. Take the boat to Entrana. Important: The monks on the dock will search you. You cannot bring any armor or weapons onto the island — they will be confiscated. Bring only food, potions, runes, and a knife.
  4. Enter the Dungeon: （进入地下城） On Entrana, walk to the north-east corner of the island to find the dungeon entrance (a cave). Inside, you'll find zombies and greater demons. Avoid combat — you have no armor.
  5. Find the Dramen Tree: （找到德拉门树） Navigate through the dungeon to the back area where a large glowing tree stands. This is the Dramen Tree. When you attempt to cut it, the Tree Spirit appears.
  6. Defeat the Tree Spirit: （打败树精） See Section 3 for detailed fight strategies. The Tree Spirit is level 101 and uses Melee attacks only.
  7. Cut a Dramen Branch: （砍德拉门树枝） After defeating the Tree Spirit, use your knife on the Dramen Tree to cut a Dramen Branch.
  8. Craft the Dramen Staff: （制作德拉门法杖） Use the Dramen Branch with a knife (in inventory) to carve it into a Dramen Staff. This can be done anywhere — you don't need to be near a crafting station.
  9. Quest Complete! （任务完成！） With the Dramen Staff in your inventory, the quest is complete. You now have access to Zanaris and all Fairy Rings across Gielinor.

3. Tree Spirit Boss Fight — Detailed Strategy（树精Boss战 — 详细策略）

Best Fight Strategies (ranked):（最佳战斗策略排名）

  1. Protect from Melee (43 Prayer): （近战保护祈祷） Activate Protect from Melee before entering the dungeon. The Tree Spirit's attacks deal zero damage. Bring 1-2 prayer potions. This makes the fight completely trivial.
  2. Fire Magic: （火焰魔法） Bring fire runes and a staff. Cast Fire Strike (13 Magic) or Fire Bolt (35 Magic). The Tree Spirit is weak to fire and takes bonus damage. Even with low Magic, Fire Strike chips away at its HP safely.
  3. Ranged: （远程） Bring a bow and arrows. Bows and arrows are allowed on Entrana. Range the Tree Spirit from a distance — it can only attack in melee range.
  4. Found Weapons: （地面武器） The Entrana dungeon contains melee weapons on the ground (combat knife, bronze battleaxe dropped by zombies). You can pick these up and use them — they're not confiscated since they spawn inside Entrana.

6. Common Mistakes（常见错误）

  1. Bringing armor to Entrana. （带盔甲去恩特拉纳） The monks confiscate it. You won't get it back until after the quest. Read the allowed items list carefully.
  2. Forgetting a knife. （忘记带刀） After killing the Tree Spirit, you need a knife or slashing weapon to cut the Dramen Branch. If you forgot, you can pick up a weapon in the dungeon or leave and return.
  3. Not bringing prayer potions with Protect from Melee. （用近战保护没带祈祷药水） If using Protect from Melee (the easiest strategy), bring 1-2 prayer potions. Running out of prayer mid-fight means the Tree Spirit hits you with no armor.
  4. Underestimating the dungeon monsters. （低估地下城怪物） Greater Demons (level 92) and zombies inhabit the dungeon. Run past them — you have no armor and fighting them wastes supplies.
  5. Not banking a spare Dramen Branch. （没存备用的德拉门树枝） Keep an extra Dramen Branch in your bank. If you ever lose your Dramen Staff, you can carve a new one instantly without redoing the quest.

7. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: What do I need for the Lost City quest?（失落之城任务需要什么条件？）

A: Requirements: 31 Crafting, 36 Woodcutting. No quest prerequisites. Recommended: 43 Prayer (Protect from Melee trivializes the Tree Spirit), 60+ Combat, a knife, food (lobsters+), and optionally fire runes + a staff. You cannot bring armor or melee weapons to Entrana — they'll be confiscated.

Q: How do I beat the Tree Spirit in Lost City?（怎么打败树精？）

A: The Tree Spirit (level 101) uses Melee only and is weak to fire. Best strategy: Pray Protect from Melee (43 Prayer) to block all damage. Alternatively, cast Fire Strike/Fire Bolt for increased damage, or use a bow and arrows from a safe distance. The dungeon also contains weapons on the ground you can pick up and use.

Q: What can I bring to Entrana?（能带什么物品去恩特拉纳？）

A: Allowed: Food, potions, magic runes and staves, bows and arrows, jewelry, capes, and a knife. NOT allowed: All armor, melee weapons, shields, and ranged armor. Items found inside the Entrana dungeon (weapons dropped by zombies) can be used freely — they spawn inside so the monks can't confiscate them.

Q: What is the Dramen Staff used for after Lost City?（德拉门法杖有什么用？）

A: The Dramen Staff unlocks the entire Fairy Ring network — a teleportation system connecting dozens of locations across Gielinor. Equip the staff and click any Fairy Ring to use it. Key destinations include Zulrah (BKP), Mount Karuulm (CIR), Feldip Hills (DJR), and Morytania (ALQ). After Lumbridge & Draynor Elite Diary, you no longer need to equip the staff.

📜 Unlock the Fairy Realm（解锁精灵领域）

Lost City is a short quest with an enormous payoff. The 15-25 minutes you spend on it unlocks a teleportation network that you'll use for the rest of your OSRS career. From bossing to skilling to questing, Fairy Rings are the backbone of efficient travel in Gielinor.

Craft that Dramen Staff. The Fairy Ring network awaits.
