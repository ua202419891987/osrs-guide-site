OSRS How to Complete Monkey Madness Quest 2026 -- Full Walkthrough & Boss Strategy

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 14 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🐒 Quest Guide -- Updated June 2026

Monkey Madness I is one of the most iconic quests in OSRS. It unlocks the Dragon Scimitar -- the best-in-slot scimitar for 60 Attack -- and grants access to Ape Atoll for high-level Woodcutting and Hunter training. This guide covers every step, the Jungle Demon boss fight, and all the crucial details for 2026.

1. Quest Requirements & Prerequisites（任务要求与前置条件）

Monkey Madness I has no strict level requirements, but 43 Prayer is functionally mandatory for the Jungle Demon boss fight. Without protection prayers, the demon can hit 32+ damage and will quickly overwhelm low-level players. Complete the prerequisite quests before starting -- they take about 1-2 hours combined.

2. Step-by-Step Walkthrough（逐步攻略）

Part 1: Gnome Stronghold -- Starting the Quest（第一部分：Gnome要塞——开始任务）

  1. Speak to King Narnode Shareen in the Tree Gnome Stronghold (Grand Tree, 1st floor).
  2. He explains that the 10th Squad went missing on Ape Atoll and asks for your help.
  3. Agree to help and speak to Daero on the 1st floor of the Grand Tree (near the spirit tree).
  4. Daero takes you to a hangar. Speak to Waydar to board the glider to Crash Island.

Part 2: Crash Island & Reaching Ape Atoll（第二部分：Crash岛与抵达Ape Atoll）

  1. After crash-landing, speak to Garkor (the wounded gnome on the beach).
  2. Learn about the monkeys' oppressive rule and the 10th Squad's capture.
  3. Take the boat (operated by a gnome on the dock) to Ape Atoll.
  4. On Ape Atoll, immediately activate Protect from Melee -- monkey archers and guards patrol the area and will attack you on sight.
  5. Run to the temple in the center of the island. Enter the dungeon beneath the temple.
🐒 Monkey Guards Warning: The monkey guards on Ape Atoll are aggressive and can hit through prayer with magic attacks. Keep Protect from Melee active and run past them quickly. If you get low on health, teleport out and try again. The guards are the most dangerous standard monsters in the quest.

Part 3: Creating the Monkey Greegree（第三部分：制作猴子Greegree）

  1. In the temple dungeon, find Zooknock (the gnome mage prisoner).
  2. He explains that you need a monkey talisman and monkey bones (or monkey corpse) to create a transformation greegree.
  3. Monkey Talisman: Kill any monkey on Karamja (near the volcano) for a drop -- it's common, roughly 1/20.
  4. Monkey Bones: Kill a monkey on Karamja and pick up the bones. Do NOT bury them.
  5. Return to Zooknock with the talisman and bones. He will craft your greegree.
  6. Wield the greegree to transform into a monkey -- while transformed, monkey guards will not attack you.

Part 4: Infiltrating the Monkey Village（第四部分：潜入猴子村庄）

  1. With the greegree equipped, enter the monkey village (east of the temple).
  2. Speak to King Awowogei in the palace (central building).
  3. Attend the monkey ceremony in the temple (just follow the monkeys).
  4. During the ceremony, signal Garkor (he's hiding in the temple rafters -- look up).
  5. After the signal, the gnome squad will ambush. Prepare for the boss fight.

3. Jungle Demon Boss Fight -- Complete Strategy（丛林恶魔Boss战——完整策略）

The Jungle Demon (level 195) is the final boss. It attacks with both Magic and Melee, and can hit up to 32 damage. Preparation is key.

Fight Strategy -- Step by Step（战斗策略——逐步）

  1. Enter the arena (south of the temple, through the gate after the signal).
  2. Immediately activate Protect from Magic. The demon's magic attack hits harder and is its primary attack.
  3. Attack with melee (or ranged if safe-spotting). Use Piety/Chivalry if available for faster kills.
  4. Keep your HP above 35. The demon can deal a combo hit (magic + melee) in quick succession.
  5. Drink prayer potions at ~20 prayer points. Running out of prayer during the fight is a guaranteed death.
  6. If you die: Your items appear in a gravestone outside the boss arena. The demon does NOT heal between attempts. Return and continue the fight -- its HP stays where you left it.
🎯 Safe-Spot Strategy: For low-level players, you can safe-spot the Jungle Demon using Ranged or Magic by standing behind the pillars (stalagmites) in the boss arena. Stand on one side of the pillar, let the demon get stuck on the other side, and attack from a distance. The demon cannot melee you through the pillar, and Protect from Magic blocks its magic attack. Use a Rune Crossbow with Ruby Bolts (e) for the fastest ranged kill.

The Dragon Scimitar is the most significant reward. It requires 60 Attack and provides +67 slash attack and +66 strength bonus, making it the best scimitar in the game until the Dragon Scimitar (or) upgrade. For many players, this weapon serves as their primary melee weapon from level 60 until they can afford an Abyssal Whip at level 70.

6. Advanced Tips（高级技巧）

  • Get the greegree as early as possible. The moment you can transform into a monkey, navigating Ape Atoll becomes trivial. Prioritise this step above all else in the quest.
  • Keep the greegree after the quest. You will need it for Monkey Madness II, and for accessing Ape Atoll's resources (teak trees, mahogany trees, Hunter training). Multiple greegree variants exist for different monkey types.
  • Use the pillar safe-spot if you're a pure or low defence. 1 Defence pures complete this quest regularly using the safe-spot method. You don't need Defence -- you need patience and Protect from Magic.
  • Ape Atoll agility course: After the quest, the Ape Atoll agility course (level 48 Agility) is one of the best mid-level agility training spots, yielding 580 XP per lap.
  • Bring alch runes. Monkey guards drop Rune Scimitars (~15K GP alch) fairly commonly. Alching them covers your supply costs for the quest.
  • Stamina potions save time. The run from the boat to the temple is long and has obstacles. A stamina potion at the start of the island halves the travel time.

7. Common Mistakes（常见错误）

  1. Walking around Ape Atoll without the greegree. The monkey archers and guards will destroy an unprotected player. If you must travel ungreed, use Protect from Melee and run without stopping.
  2. Forgetting monkey bones. Players often kill a monkey, bury the bones by habit, then realize they need them for the greegree. Turn off auto-bury and double-check before leaving Karamja.
  3. Underestimating the Jungle Demon. Going in with 4 lobsters and 1 prayer potion is a common cause of death. Over-prepare. You can always eat the extra food.
  4. Not bringing anti-poison. Certain traps on Ape Atoll (spike pits) can inflict poison. One dose of anti-poison saves a lot of food.
  5. Dropping the greegree after the quest. The greegree is a permanent unlock and is required for Monkey Madness II, Ape Atoll access, and clue scroll steps. Store it in your bank.
  6. Forgetting to buy the Dragon Scimitar. After the quest, you are so relieved you teleport out and forget to buy the scimitar. Buy it immediately from Daga while you have the greegree equipped.

8. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: Can I do Monkey Madness I as a 1 Defence pure?

A: Yes! Many pures complete Monkey Madness I for the Dragon Scimitar. Use the safe-spot method (stand behind a pillar, attack with ranged/magic). Bring 10-15 sharks, 6 prayer potions, and keep Protect from Magic active at all times during the boss fight. The quest gives Defence XP as a reward -- you can choose to receive it in Attack and Strength instead (20K each) to avoid gaining Defence levels.

Q: What happens if I die to the Jungle Demon?

A: Death is safe in this instance. Your items appear in a gravestone just outside the boss arena. The Jungle Demon does NOT regenerate health between attempts. You can return with more supplies and continue the fight from where you left off. This makes the boss forgiving -- even if you die 3-4 times, the cumulative damage will eventually kill it.

Q: Do I need to keep the greegree after the quest?

A: Absolutely. You need a monkey greegree for: Monkey Madness II quest, accessing Ape Atoll (teak/mahogany trees, Hunter area, agility course), certain clue scroll steps, and buying additional Dragon Scimitars. Store it in your bank. You can also create additional greegrees for different monkey types using different monkey bones.

🐒 One of OSRS's Most Rewarding Quests（OSRS最值得做的任务之一）

Monkey Madness I is a rite of passage. The Dragon Scimitar is your primary weapon from 60 Attack through level 70 -- and for many accounts, far beyond. Ape Atoll unlocks incredible Woodcutting and Hunter training. And the quest itself is genuinely fun, with a memorable boss fight and one of the coolest transformation mechanics in the game.

Complete your prerequisite quests, stock up on prayer potions, and go save the 10th Squad. The Jungle Demon awaits.
