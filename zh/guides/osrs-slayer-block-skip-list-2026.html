1. How Blocking & Skipping Works（屏蔽与跳过机制）

Block (100 pts): Permanently prevents a task from your CURRENT master. Each master has their OWN block list now.
Skip (30 pts): Cancel current task, get a new one. Better than blocking low-weight bad tasks.
Golden Rule: Block HIGH-WEIGHT bad tasks. Skip LOW-WEIGHT bad tasks. Extend HIGH-WEIGHT good tasks.

4. Nieve & Konar Block Lists（Nieve与Konar屏蔽列表）

Nieve (85-100 combat): Metal Dragons → Black Demons → Hellhounds → Drakes → Trolls.

Konar (keys): Metal Dragons → Drakes → Wyrms → Suqah. Use Konar for every 10th task for Brimstone Key bonus (~89K avg per key).

5. Turael Boosting — Infinite Points（Turael刷点法·无限点数）

9 Turael tasks (1-5 min each) → 10th at Duradel/Konar = ~225 points per cycle (~45 minutes). This is how you unlock all 6 block slots and stockpile skips.

7. FAQ（常见问题）

Q: Should I block Wyrms?（应该屏蔽Wyrms吗？）

A: Generally no. Low weight — spend 30 pts to skip instead. Only block if you truly hate them.

Q: Unblock for bossing?（打Boss需要解屏蔽吗？）

A: Only if actively farming that boss. Unblock Hellhounds for Cerberus, Black Demons for Gorillas. Re-block when done.

Q: How to get more block slots?（如何获取更多屏蔽槽位？）

A: Quest Points. Slots at 50/100/150/200/250/300 QP. Rush RFD (175 QP) for 3 slots quickly.
