⑨ 🎯 Hunter Rumours Complete Guide (2025 Update)（⑨ 🎯 猎人传闻完整指南（2025更新版））

Hunter Rumours were introduced in Phase 3 of the Varlamore expansion (July 2025) and have quickly become one of the most popular Hunter training methods in OSRS. They combine the goal-oriented feel of Slayer with Hunter's core trap-and-track mechanics — and they're completely safe from PKers. This complete guide covers everything you need to know: how to unlock the Hunter Guild, exactly how the Rumour system works, how to set up your blacklist for doubled XP rates at 91+, detailed XP tables, loot drop rates, and a head-to-head comparison with Chinchompas.

9.1 🏛️ Hunter Guild — How to Unlock + Location（9.1 🏛️ 猎人公会——解锁方式与位置）

The Hunter Guild is the hub for all Hunter Rumours content. It's located in Varlamore Region, Zone 3 (The Gricolleria District), south-east of the main Varlamore city. You can teleport there quickly using the Varlamore teleport spell (unlocked after partial completion of Children of the Sun) or by using a Xeric's talisman to teleport to the Hunter Guild entrance.

Unlocking Requirements:

  • 50 Hunter — minimum level to enter the Hunter Guild and speak to the Huntmaster. You can walk in at 50, but you can only receive Novice-tier rumours at this level.
  • 65 Hunter + 75 Combat — required to accept Expert-tier rumours (the highest XP tier). The combat requirement exists because some rumour locations have low-level NPCs nearby, but in practice you'll almost never take damage.
  • Quest: Children of the Sun (partial) — unlocks Varlamore access. You don't need to finish the entire quest line; entering Varlamore for the first time is enough to reach the Guild.

Getting there fast: After your first visit, use the Hunter Guild teleport purchased from the Guild shop for 400 tokens (see section 9.4). It gives an instant teleport right outside the Guild doors, saving 30–60 seconds per rumour cycle.

💡 Important: You can start doing low-tier rumours at 50 Hunter, but the real power of Rumours doesn't unlock until 72 Hunter when Expert-tier tasks become available. If you're below 72, stick to Falconry or Chinchompas for faster XP.

9.2 📋 Rumour Task Mechanism — Step by Step（9.2 📋 传闻任务机制——分步详解）

Hunter Rumours work like a simplified Slayer system. Here's exactly how the loop works, step by step:

Step 1: Get a Rumour
Talk to Guildmaster Tirannwn (the Huntmaster) inside the Hunter Guild. She'll give you a rumour — a task to hunt a specific creature a set number of times. The creature and the number of catches required depend on your Hunter level and the rumour tier.

Step 2: Hunt the Creature
Travel to the marked location on the world map (the rumour dialog shows a red pinpoint). Use the appropriate hunting method for that creature:

  • Butterflies / Birds — butterfly net + jar
  • Kebbits — noose wand (falconry area) or box traps (tracking)
  • Salamanders — rope + small fishing net
  • Chinchompas — box traps
  • Herbiboar — track the trail using spade and trowel

Step 3: Report Back
Return to Guildmaster Tirannwn after catching the required number. You'll receive:

  • Hunter XP — scales with creature type and your level (see section 9.4 for exact numbers)
  • Hunter Guild Reputation — used to unlock Guild shop upgrades
  • Hunting Tokens — currency for the Guild rewards shop (9.4)
  • Loot roll — a chance at unique drops (see section 9.5)

Rumour Tiers at a Glance:

9.3 🎯 Golden Tip: Blacklist Setup (XP Doubles at 91+)（9.3 🎯 黄金技巧：黑名单设置（91级后经验翻倍））

This is the single most important section of this guide. Most players (and most other guides) report that Expert Rumours give "80K–120K XP/hour." That's true before you set up your blacklist. Once you blacklist the lowest-XP creatures in the Expert pool, your average XP per task jumps by 80–100%. At 91+ Hunter with a properly configured blacklist, you can realistically hit 200K–250K XP/hour — faster than Black Chinchompas and completely safe from PKers.

How to Unlock the Blacklist:

  1. Complete 10 Expert-level Rumours (tracked automatically). There is no quest requirement — just grind 10 Expert tasks. At 72–80 Hunter, this takes about 3–5 hours.
  2. After your 10th Expert rumour, talk to Guildmaster Tirannwn and select "Configure Blacklist."
  3. You can blacklist up to 5 creatures. They will never appear in your future rumours.
⚠️ Don't Blacklist Too Early: Wait until you've done at least 10 Expert rumours before configuring your blacklist. You need to experience each creature type to know which ones you actually dislike. Also, if you blacklist a creature you later need for a Diary requirement (e.g., certain kebbits for Kandarin Diary), you'll have to temporarily remove it from the blacklist.

🎮 3-Tick Hunter Manipulation — Step-by-Step Guide（🎮 3刻猎人操作技巧——分步指南）

3-tick Hunter manipulation is one of the highest-level Hunter training techniques in OSRS. It reduces your box trap catch animation from 4 ticks to 3 ticks, giving a 33% XP rate boost. This guide explains exactly how it works and how to do it reliably.

📚 How It Works — The Tick Mechanic（📚 如何运作——刻机制）

Normally, when you set a box trap, the catch animation takes 4 game ticks (2.4 seconds). During these 4 ticks, you're stuck — you can't move, reset, or do anything else.

🔧 Prerequisites（🔧 前置条件）

  • Charged Attack Weapon (one of):
    • Dragon Longsword — Special attack (25% spec) "Cleave" — easiest to use, recommended for beginners.
    • Rune Crossbow + Granite Bolts (e) — Special attack (25% spec) "Binding Shot" — also works.
    • Any dragon weapon with a special attack — Must be a "charged" attack that triggers a 1-tick delay.
  • Box Traps: 5–10 (bring extra in case you mis-time).
  • Chinchompas: Red (63+) or Black (73+) — this technique only works on box trap creatures.
  • Tick Timer: Enable in game settings or use an external metronome app.

🎯 Step-by-Step: How to 3-Tick (7 Steps)（🎯 分步操作：如何进行3刻操作（7步））

STEP 1
Place the Box Trap (spacebar)

Walk to a chinchompa spawn. Press spacebar (or right-click → "Set-trap") to place the box trap. Watch your tick counter — remember the tick when you placed it.

STEP 2
Charged Attack (weapon special)

On tick 1 after placing the trap, use your weapon's special attack on nothing (right-click → "Attack" → click ground). For Dragon Longsword, press your spec key (default: x).

STEP 3
Wait Exactly 1 Tick

Do nothing for exactly 1 game tick (0.6 seconds). Don't move, don't click anything. Watch your tick timer.

STEP 4
Second Charged Attack

On tick 2, use your special attack again. Same as Step 2 — attack the ground.

STEP 5
Wait Exactly 1 Tick (again)

Same as Step 3. Wait exactly 1 tick. No movement, no clicking.

STEP 6
Third Charged Attack

On tick 3, use your special attack one last time. After this third attack, the trap catch animation will complete in 3 ticks instead of 4.

STEP 7
Reset and Repeat

As soon as the chinchompa is caught, run to the next trap (or reset the same one). The 3-tick cycle restarts immediately. With practice, you'll maintain a rhythm: place → spec → wait → spec → wait → spec → catch → repeat.
