F2P gives you 15 skills. Membership unlocks 8 more completely new skills and expands 7 existing ones — taking you from 15 to 23+ skills with vastly more depth. This guide breaks down every exclusive skill, what it does, whether it's worth paying for, and which ones you should train first.

Let's be blunt: F2P OSRS is a demo. A generous demo — 15 skills, dozens of quests, the Grand Exchange, and all of Asgarnia and Misthalin — but a demo nonetheless. Membership unlocks the real game. And the single biggest unlock? Skills. Eight brand-new skills that simply don't exist in F2P, plus seven F2P skills that get massive expansions making them feel completely different. If you're on the fence about that $14.99/month subscription, this guide will show you exactly what those skills deliver.

① 🎯 Introduction — Why Skills Matter in OSRS（① 🎯 引言——技能在OSRS中的重要性）

Skills define everything you can do in Old School RuneScape. Your combat level gates which bosses you can fight. Your skilling levels determine how you make GP. Your quest requirements force you to train skills you'd otherwise ignore. In F2P, you have a flat 15-skill experience. In members, that jumps to 23+ skills — and the depth multiplies exponentially.

1.1 📊 F2P vs Members: The Skill Count Reality（1.1 📊 F2P vs会员：技能数量的现实）

Here's the cold, hard numbers. F2P gives you exactly 15 skills. Membership adds 8 completely new skills and dramatically expands the remaining 7.

② 🔑 The 15 Exclusive Skills Breakdown（② 🔑 15个专属技能详解）

Let's go through every single members-exclusive skill.

2.1 🏃 Agility — The Movement Skill（2.1 🏃 敏捷——移动技能）

What it does: Agility determines how fast your run energy regenerates and lets you use shortcuts across the map.

2.2 🌿 Herblore — Potions That Change Everything（2.2 🌿 草药学——改变一切的药水）

What it does: Herblore lets you clean herbs and mix potions.

2.3 🕵️ Thieving — Free GP at Every Level（2.3 🕵️ 盗窃——每级都有免费金币）

What it does: Thieving lets you pickpocket NPCs, steal from stalls, and loot chests.

2.4 🎨 Crafting — Jewellery, Armour, and Millions（2.4 🎨 工艺——珠宝、盔甲和百万收益）

What it does (members expansion): Crafting explodes into one of the most versatile skills in the game.

2.5 🏹 Fletching — Ranged Combat's Best Friend（2.5 🏹 箭术——远程战斗的最佳伙伴）

What it does: Fletching is completely members-only. You turn logs into bows and arrows.

2.6 ⚔️ Slayer — The Skill That Defines Members PvM（2.6 ⚔️ 杀戮——定义会员PvM的技能）

What it does: Slayer assigns you tasks to kill specific monsters.
