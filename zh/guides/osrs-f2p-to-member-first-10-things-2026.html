So you just bought OSRS membership. Congratulations — you've unlocked 80% of the game. But here's the problem: members Gielinor is massive, and if you don't have a plan, you'll waste your first week running around like a headless chicken. This guide gives you the exact 10-step roadmap to follow in your first few days as a member. Follow it in order and you'll have solid combat stats, a reliable income stream, protection prayers, and a clean bank before you know it. For the full picture on what membership unlocks, check our complete F2P-to-P2P transition guide first.

1. Set Up Your Quest Log & Unlock Teleports（设置任务日志与解锁传送）

The single biggest advantage of membership is mobility. F2P players walk everywhere. Members teleport. Your first priority is unlocking the major teleport networks so the map opens up to you.

1.1 Open Your Quest Log & Pick a Starting Quest（打开任务日志并选择起始任务）

Press the Quest tab (or hit the shortcut) and you'll see dozens of new quests that were locked in F2P. Don't panic. Sort by difficulty and start with Novice and Intermediate quests. Good first picks:

  • Cook's Assistant — 300 cooking XP, unlocks range access in Lumbridge
  • Imp Catcher — 875 magic XP, quick to finish
  • Witch's Potion — 325 magic XP, 5-minute quest
  • Tree Gnome Village — Unlocks Spirit Tree teleports (critical!)
Quest Point Tip（任务点数提示）

Many members teleports require Quest Points. The Eagles' Peak quest (and others) unlock themselves at specific QP thresholds. Aim for 32 QP in your first session — it unlocks the Quest Point Cape teleport if you ever max it later.

1.2 Unlock Fairy Rings & Spirit Trees（解锁精灵之环和灵树传送）

Two teleport networks define members gameplay:

  • Fairy Rings — Unlocked after the Fairy Tale Part I quest (requires 49 Farming to start, but you can boost). These codes let you teleport to 40+ locations across the map. You'll use these every single day.
  • Spirit Trees — Unlocked after Tree Gnome Village. No requirements. Connects the Grand Exchange, Battlefield, Gnome Stronghold, and more.

Do These Three Quests Immediately（立即完成这三个任务）

1. Tree Gnome Village — No requirements, unlocks Spirit Trees.
2. Fairy Tale Part I — Starts the Fairy Ring unlock chain.
3. Priest in Peril — Unlocks the route to Canifis and Morytania (needed for many quests).

1.3 Get a Player-Owned House (POH)（购买玩家房屋）

Buy a house in Rimmington or Hosidius for 1,000 GP from any Estate Agent. Even a bare bones house gives you a free teleport home (via the House Teleport spell at level 40 Magic). Later you'll add an altar, portal nexus, and jewellery box — but for now, just own one.

2. Train Attack/Strength/Defence to 50+ Immediately（立即训练攻击/力量/防御到50+）

If you're coming from F2P with combat stats in the 30s or 40s, your next stop is level 50+ across Attack, Strength, and Defence. This isn't optional — almost every members activity, from Slayer to bossing to high-level quests, assumes you have 50+ combat stats.

2.1 Go to Sand Crabs (Best AFK Training)（去沙蟹区——最佳挂机训练）

Head to the Sand Crab area on the southern coast of Zeah (use the Lovakengj mine cart or Xeric's talisman). Sand Crabs are level 15, have 60 HP, and are aggressive — meaning you can sit there and train for 10 minutes without clicking. They're the #1 AFK training spot in the game.

  • XP rates: 30K–50K combat XP/hour (depending on your stats and gear)
  • Required stats: None — accessible at level 3
  • Bring: Food (monkfish or better), strength potions, a set of black or mithril armour
AFK Timer（挂机计时器）

Sand Crabs stop being aggressive after 10 minutes. Just run west 15 squares and run back — they'll re-aggro. This resets the timer without you having to relog.

2.2 Gear Progression: What to Wear（装备进度：该穿什么）

As a new member, your gear upgrades look like this:

Level（等级）Weapon（武器）Armour（护甲）Cost (approx.)（大致价格）
1–20 AttackBlack scimitarBlack set~5K GP
20–30 AttackMithril scimitarMithril set~10K GP

At 40 Attack, pick up a Brine Sabre if you can afford it (~150K GP) — it outperforms the rune scimitar underwater and in certain spots. Otherwise, stick with the rune scimitar until you unlock better weapons through Slayer.

2.3 Use Combat Potions（使用战斗药水）

Members get access to Strength Potions, Attack Potions, and Super Combat Potions. A strength potion (requiring 12 Herblore to make, or buy for ~800 GP each) boosts your strength by 3–12 levels for 6 minutes. This alone increases your XP/hour by 15–20%. Always train with potions active — the cost is trivial compared to the time saved.

Don't Train at Hill Giants Anymore（不要再在丘陵巨人处训练）

Hill Giants were fine in F2P, but members have far better options. Sand Crabs, Rock Crabs, and Ammonite Crabs all give 2–3× the XP. Move on — don't waste membership time on F2P training methods. For a full breakdown, read our beginner combat training guide.

3. Get a Money-Making Method Going（开始赚钱方法）

You need GP. Membership unlocks dozens of money-makers that F2P players can only dream of, but many require stats you don't have yet. Here are the best methods for a fresh member with low stats.

3.1 Wintertodt (Low Requirements, High Profit)（寒冬托德——低要求高收益）

Wintertodt is a members-only boss that requires just 50 Firemaking and some warm clothing. You don't even need combat stats — it's a skilling boss. You chop bruma roots, feed the brazier, and earn supply crates as rewards.

  • GP/hour: 200K–400K (depending on RNG and your Firemaking level)
  • Requirements: 50 Firemaking, warm clothing (clue boots, gloves of silence, etc.)
  • XP/hour: 40K–80K Firemaking XP
  • Bonus: You also get Woodcutting, Construction, and Fletching XP passively

Why Wintertodt Is Perfect for New Members（为什么寒冬托德适合新会员）

No combat needed. No gear needed. The supply crates give you herbs, seeds, logs, and gems — all sellable on the GE. At 99 Firemaking, you can earn 5M+ GP just from the crates. Even at level 50, it's better GP than any F2P method.

3.2 Killing Green Dragons (Combat + GP)（杀绿龙——战斗加金币）

Once your combat stats hit 50+, Green Dragons in the Wilderness are a classic money-maker. Each kill drops dragon bones (~1,600 GP) and green dragonhide (~1,400 GP) — that's 3,000 GP per kill.

  • GP/hour: 400K–600K
  • Requirements: 50+ Attack/Strength/Defence, anti-dragon shield (free from Duke Horacio after Dragon Slayer)
  • Risk: You're in the Wilderness — watch out for PKers

3.3 Early Slayer for Long-Term GP（早期屠杀技能赚长期金币）

Talk to Turael in Burthorpe (or Krystilia in Edgeville for Wilderness tasks) and start training Slayer. Slayer unlocks high-level money-makers like Gargoyles, Zulrah, and Cerberus later. Starting early means you'll hit those money-makers sooner. Early Slayer tasks give modest GP (10K–50K/hour) but the long-term payoff is enormous.

For more early-game GP methods, check our first 5M GP guide for new members and our broader beginner money-making guide.

4. Unlock Key Members Areas（解锁关键会员区域）

F2P has six cities. Members has over thirty. Here are the ones you need to visit and unlock in your first week.

4.1 Varrock & the Grand Exchange（瓦罗克和大交易所）

You already know Varrock from F2P, but members get access to the Varrock West Bank area, the Varrock Diary rewards (Ardougne cloak teleport), and the full Grand Exchange. Set your home teleport to Varrock and use the Lodestone network — it's free and connects to every major city.

4.2 Catherby (Fishing & Cooking Hub)（卡瑟比——钓鱼与烹饪中心）

Catherby is the best fishing/cooking spot in the game. The bank is 5 steps from the fishing spots, and there are cooking ranges nearby. This is where you'll train Fishing and Cooking to 60+ (see section 5). Get there via:

  • Camelot teleport (level 45 Magic), then run east
  • Charter ship from Port Sarim (~200 GP)
  • Fairy ring code BJR (after unlocking Fairy Rings)

4.3 Ardougne & Seers' Village（阿杜根和先知村）

Ardougne unlocks the Ardougne Cloak (free monastery teleport) after the Ardougne Achievement Diary. Seers' Village has the Camelot tele spot, an agility course, and is near the Flax fields. These two towns are central to mid-game content.

4.4 Canifis & Morytania（卡尼菲斯和莫里塔尼亚）

After Priest in Peril, you can access Canifis — a dark, gothic town with a bank, general store, and the route to Morytania (home of Slayer Tower, Barrows, and Mort Myre Swamp). This area is essential for mid-game PvM.

Ectophial — Your Free Morytania Teleport（灵药瓶——免费的莫里塔尼亚传送）

After the Ghosts Ahoy quest, you get the Ectophial — an infinite-use teleport to the Ectofuntus (just outside Canifis). It's one of the best free teleports in the game. Do this quest early.

5. Level Fishing & Cooking to 60+ and Get 43 Prayer（钓鱼和烹饪到60+并获得43祈祷）

Two milestones define your ability to survive members content: 60+ Fishing/Cooking (for food) and 43 Prayer (for protection prayers). Do both before you attempt any boss.

5.1 Fishing & Cooking: The Catherby Route（钓鱼与烹饪：卡瑟比路线）

Head to Catherby and follow this progression:

Level（等级）What to Catch（捕捉什么）XP/HourFood Yield（食物产出）
20–40Trout & Salmon (fly fishing)15K–25KTrout/Salmon

62+Monkfish (small net, after Swan Song)20K–35KMonkfish (best food for the price)

Train Cooking alongside Fishing — cook what you catch. At 60+ Cooking, you'll never burn monkfish at Hosidius range (Hosidius kitchen gives a burn-rate reduction). Monkfish heal 16 HP each and cost ~300 GP — the best budget food in the game.

5.2 Why 43 Prayer Is Non-Negotiable（为什么43祈祷是必须的）

At 43 Prayer you unlock Protect from Melee, Protect from Ranged, and Protect from Magic. These prayers reduce damage from the corresponding attack style to zero (in most PvM situations). Without them, you'll die constantly to bosses and Slayer monsters. With them, the game opens up entirely.

The Gilded Altar method is fastest. Find a host in the "Altar Community" clan chat, bring your bones, and use their altar for 3.5× XP. For full details, see our 1–99 Prayer guide.

5.3 Best Way to Buy Bones（购买骨头的最佳方式）

Buy dragon bones on the Grand Exchange (currently ~1,600 GP each). They give 252 XP each at a Gilded Altar (with both incense burners lit). That's 72 XP per GP — far better value than big bones. If you're on a budget, big bones (~350 GP, 105 XP at altar) still work fine.

6. Organize Your Bank & Start Members Quests（整理银行并开始会员任务）

Membership dumps hundreds of new items into your life. If your bank is a mess, you'll waste hours looking for things. Clean it up now, then dive into the quests that matter.

6.1 Set Up Bank Tabs（设置银行标签页）

Open your bank and click the tab icons at the top. A clean tab structure for a new member:

  • Tab 1: Combat gear (weapons, armour, potions, food)
  • Tab 2: Skilling tools & materials (pickaxe, axe, fishing gear, logs, ores)
  • Tab 3: Quest items & teleport scrolls
  • Tab 4: Runes & teleport jewellery
  • Tab 5: Herbs, secondaries, and potions
  • Tab 6: Junk/sell pile (drop or GE everything here weekly)
Placeholder Trick（占位符技巧）

Withdraw 1 of an item, then right-click the empty slot and select "Placeholder". This locks the item's position in your bank so it always stays in the same spot. Use this for every item you use regularly — it transforms how fast you can bank.

For advanced bank organisation (including the seed vault and STASH units), read our bank and inventory management guide.

6.2 Priority Members Quests (Do These First)（优先会员任务——先做这些）

Not all quests are equal. These five unlock the most content for the least effort:

Waterfall Quest is the best starting quest in the game — it gives zero combat XP requirements but grants 13,750 Attack and Strength XP, instantly jumping you from level 1 to 30 in both. Do it before anything else. For the full quest priority list, check our best quests for new members guide.

6.3 Recipe for Disaster: The Long Game（灾难食谱：长远之计）

Recipe for Disaster (RFD) is a multipart quest that unlocks the Barrows Gloves — the best gloves in OSRS until you reach endgame content. You don't need to finish it immediately, but start working through the sub-quests early. Each sub-quest also gives cooking XP and access to new food types.

7. Join a Clan & Set Up Your XP Tracker（加入公会并设置经验追踪器）

OSRS is a social game. Playing solo is fine, but joining a clan and tracking your progress will keep you motivated and teach you mechanics faster.

7.1 Join a Clan（加入公会）

Clans in OSRS are free to join and give you access to a Clan Chat (persistent group chat), Clan Wars (safe PvP practice), and a community of players who can answer questions. To join one:

  • Open the Clan Chat tab (green icon)
  • Search for open clans like "OSRS Advice", "Reddit CC", or "Altar Community"
  • Click "Join" — most beginner clans accept anyone

Clan Wars: Free PvP Practice（公会战争：免费PVP练习）

Clan Wars has a "FFA Portal" (Free-For-All) where you can fight other players with no risk — you don't lose items on death. It's the best way to learn PvP mechanics and test your combat gear without fear. Spend 30 minutes here after joining a clan.

7.2 Install RuneLite & Set Up XP Tracking（安装RuneLite并设置经验追踪）

If you're not using RuneLite (the approved third-party client), install it immediately. It's allowed by Jagex and includes:

  • XP Tracker — Shows XP/hour, time to level, and total XP gains per session
  • Quest Helper — Highlights quest steps and required items
  • Clue Scroll Helper — Solves clue scroll puzzles automatically
  • Bank Tags — Advanced bank organisation with custom tabs
  • GPU Rendering — Better FPS and visuals
Only Use RuneLite from the Official Source（只从官方来源使用RuneLite）

Download RuneLite only from runelite.net. Third-party clients from other sites have been used to hack accounts. Jagex has explicitly stated RuneLite is the only approved client — anything else risks a ban or account theft.

7.3 Set Goals and Track Them（设定目标并追踪）

OSRS is a long game. Without goals, you'll burn out. Set three types of goals:

  • Short-term (this week): "Get 43 Prayer and 50 Combat"
  • Medium-term (this month): "Complete Recipe for Disaster and unlock Barrows Gloves"
  • Long-term (3+ months): "Get a Fire Cape" or "Reach 70 Slayer"

Use RuneLite's XP Tracker to log daily gains, and check the Wise Old Man tracker for long-term progress. For a structured skill training plan, read our top 10 skills to train first guide.

8. FAQs — First Things to Do as a New OSRS Member（新OSRS会员最先做什么——常见问题）

Q: What should I do literally first after buying membership?（买会员后第一件事做什么？）

A: Open your quest log and complete Tree Gnome Village (unlocks Spirit Trees) and Priest in Peril (unlocks Canifis). Then head to Sand Crabs and start training combat. These three actions take under an hour and unlock the core members infrastructure.

Q: How long does it take to get 43 Prayer from level 1?（从1级到43祈祷需要多久？）

A: About 1–1.5 hours using dragon bones at a Gilded Altar. You'll need ~530 dragon bones (costing ~850K GP). If you use big bones instead, expect 2–3 hours and ~1.1M GP. Either way, it's a one-session grind — do it in a single sitting.

Q: Should I train combat at Sand Crabs or do Slayer?（应该在沙蟹训练战斗还是做屠杀任务？）

A: Both. Train at Sand Crabs until 50+ combat stats, then switch to Slayer for your 50–70 grind. Pure combat training (no Slayer) is faster XP, but you'll arrive at level 70 with 1 Slayer and have to grind it later. Mixing Slayer in early saves time long-term.

Q: What's the fastest way to make GP as a brand-new member?（新会员最快赚钱方法是什么？）

A: Wintertodt — it requires only 50 Firemaking and zero combat stats. You can earn 200K–400K GP/hour from supply crates while also training Firemaking. It's the best early-game money-maker bar none. Once your combat is 50+, Green Dragons in the Wilderness pay 400K–600K GP/hour.

Q: Do I need to do all 10 things on this list before I can boss?（需要在打Boss前做完这10件事吗？）

A: No. You can start low-level bosses (like the Giant Mole or Barrows) with 50+ combat, 43 Prayer, and monkfish. But completing the full list first will make your early bossing far smoother and more profitable. At minimum, get 43 Prayer and 50+ combat before any boss.

Q: Can I still play with my F2P friends after buying membership?（买会员后还能和F2P朋友一起玩吗？）

A: Yes. Members can enter F2P worlds and play all F2P content. Your friends just can't enter members worlds or access members content. You can still train, boss, and hang out together on F2P worlds — you'll just have more options when you're solo.

Q: What if I can't afford membership — can I use bonds?（如果买不起会员——可以用Bond吗？）

A: Yes. Bonds cost ~5.2M GP in-game (as of June 2026) and give 14 days of membership. If you can farm 5.2M GP in two weeks, you can play indefinitely for free. Read our F2P-to-P2P bond guide for the full strategy.

Final Tips for New Members（新会员最终建议）

The jump from F2P to members is overwhelming — there's 5× more content, 10× more items, and 20× more places to go. That's why this roadmap exists. Follow it in order: unlock teleports → train combat → start making GP → get food and prayer → do key quests → join a community → track your progress. Do those seven things in your first week and you'll have a rock-solid foundation for everything else OSRS has to offer.

And remember: there's no rush. OSRS is a marathon, not a sprint. Pick goals you enjoy, use the tools available (RuneLite, clan chats, this site), and don't be afraid to ask questions. Every maxed main started exactly where you are now.
