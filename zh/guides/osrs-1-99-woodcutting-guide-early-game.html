OSRS 1-99 Woodcutting Guide for Early Game 2026 -- F2P to Redwoods

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 13 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🌳 Skill Training -- Updated June 2026

Woodcutting is one of the most approachable skills in OSRS, suitable for both F2P and members. Whether you are just starting out or pushing for the cape, this guide covers every stage from level 1 to 99, including the F2P route, the fastest members path, and the beloved AFK Redwoods method for the final stretch.

1. Axe Upgrades -- Always Use the Best Available

Your axe determines how quickly you chop logs. Always upgrade to the best axe your level allows. Wielding the axe in your weapon slot provides approximately 10% faster chopping than holding it in your inventory.

The Dragon Axe special attack temporarily boosts your Woodcutting level by 3, which can help you access higher-level trees a few levels early. The Infernal Axe burns logs as you cut them (1/3 chance), providing passive Firemaking XP at the cost of losing the logs. The Crystal Axe has a faster chop speed than Dragon and is the best non-Infernal axe.

💡 Lumberjack Outfit: Obtained from the Temple Trekking minigame, the Lumberjack outfit provides up to 2.5% bonus Woodcutting XP (full 4-piece set). It takes about 2-4 hours to obtain and is worth getting. The Forestry outfit is an alternative from the Forestry activity.

2. F2P Woodcutting Route (1-99)

Free-to-play players have fewer tree options but can still reach 99 Woodcutting efficiently. Here is the optimal F2P path:

Key F2P locations: Draynor Village has willow trees right next to the bank -- this is the best overall F2P XP spot for levels 30-60. For Yews, Edgeville has 3 yew trees south of the bank, and Varrock Palace has 2 yew trees. F2P players should note that willows remain competitive XP/hr even at higher levels due to Yews being slow to chop.

💵 F2P Profit Note: Yew logs sell for ~250-300 GP each on the Grand Exchange. From level 60-99, you'll cut approximately 75,000 Yew logs, yielding 18-22M GP in profit. This makes Woodcutting one of the better F2P money-making skills alongside the XP you earn.

Teak Trees -- The Fastest Method

Teak trees on Ape Atoll (south-east corner of the island) are the fastest Woodcutting XP in the game when combined with tick manipulation. There are two teak trees adjacent to each other, allowing non-stop chopping. With a monkey greegree equipped, you are safe from the aggressive monkeys on the island.

  • Location: South-east Ape Atoll (Fairy Ring CLR, then south-east)
  • Requirements: Monkey Madness I, monkey greegree, anti-poison recommended
  • Regular XP: 65,000/hr at 60, scaling to 95,000/hr at 99
  • Tick manipulation XP: 170,000-200,000/hr (2-tick method)
  • Logs banked: Via Ape Atoll teleport + Castle Wars/Edgeville bank

Mahogany Trees on Ape Atoll

Three mahogany trees sit in a triangle pattern on Ape Atoll. At higher levels (80+ Woodcutting), you can rotate between these three trees and almost never wait for a respawn.

  • Location: Eastern Ape Atoll
  • XP/hr at 65: ~80,000
  • XP/hr at 90+: ~115,000
  • Profit: Mahogany logs sell for ~500 GP each (good profit if banked)
  • Ironman note: Banking mahogany logs is excellent for Construction training via the Sawmill or Plank Make spell
🌳 Fossil Island Alternative: If you haven't completed Monkey Madness I, Fossil Island (unlocked via Bone Voyage quest) has teak and mahogany trees in the hardwood grove. XP rates are slightly lower due to more spread-out trees, but the island is safer and has a bank via the Hilltop mushtree.

4. Redwood Trees -- Best AFK Method (90-99)

Redwood trees in the Woodcutting Guild are the most AFK Woodcutting method in OSRS. With enormous tree HP pools that rarely deplete, you can AFK for up to 5 minutes without interaction. This makes Redwoods the #1 choice for players who want 99 with minimal attention.

While Redwoods give less XP per hour than tick-manipulated Teaks or Mahogany, the extreme AFK window allows players to train while working, watching videos, or playing other accounts. Many players switch from Mahogany to Redwoods at level 90 for the convenience factor alone.

6. Advanced Tips for Maximum Efficiency

  • 2-Tick Teaks: The absolute fastest Woodcutting XP in the game. Requires clicking the tree, moving one tile, then clicking the tree again -- repeating every 2 game ticks (1.2 seconds). Hard to learn but yields 170K-200K XP/hr. Watch a YouTube guide for the rhythm.
  • Woodcutting Guild passive +7 boost: While inside the Woodcutting Guild, you receive an invisible +7 Woodcutting boost. This stacks with your Dragon Axe spec and visible boosts. Always train inside the guild when possible.
  • Infernal Axe passive Firemaking XP: The Infernal Axe burns 1/3 of logs automatically. Over the course of 1-99 Woodcutting, this can give you 1-3M+ passive Firemaking XP. Well worth the 3M GP investment if you don't need the logs.
  • Bird Nests: Randomly drop from all trees. Empty nests sell for ~6,000 GP each. Nests with seeds can contain Spirit seeds (rare), Magic seeds (~100K GP), or other valuable tree seeds. Always pick up nests -- they can be worth millions over the course of 99.
  • Ring of Endurance: If you own this ring (from Hallowed Sepulchre), the stamina potion effect doubling is excellent for long run distances like Ape Atoll teaks to the bank.
  • The Forestry update: Forestry added group Woodcutting events that give bonus XP and the Forestry outfit. Participating in Forestry events at popular tree spots adds variety and extra XP. The Forestry kit provides a passive +2 level boost when carried in your inventory.

7. Common Mistakes

  1. Using Yews for XP. Yews are slow and terrible XP compared to Teaks or Mahogany. Only cut Yews if you're F2P or specifically want profit, not XP.
  2. Banking low-value logs. Banking Normal, Oak, or Willow logs wastes enormous time for minimal GP. Drop them and save the banking time for higher-value trees (Mahogany, Yew, Magic).
  3. Not using the Woodcutting Guild. The +7 invisible boost inside the guild makes it the best place to train at any level where you have access to the trees inside. Get 75% Hosidius favour as early as possible.
  4. Ignoring bird nests. Over 1-99, bird nests can generate 5-20M+ GP in seeds. The 10 seconds to pick one up is always worth it. With the Woodcutting Cape, you get 10% more nests.
  5. Using an axe from inventory instead of wielding it. Wielding your axe in the weapon slot gives approximately 10% faster chopping speed than holding it in your inventory. Always equip it.
  6. Not pre-potting with Dragon Axe spec. The +3 boost from the Dragon Axe special attack lasts about 1 minute. Use it whenever possible for faster chopping and earlier access to higher trees.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I reach 99 Woodcutting in F2P?

A: Yes! Many players have achieved 99 Woodcutting in F2P. The optimal F2P path is Willows from 30-99 (35K XP/hr, ~350 hours) or Yews from 60-99 (30K XP/hr, ~300 hours + 18-22M GP profit). It takes longer than members but is achievable. The key is consistency -- a few hours per week over several months.

Q: Should I use the Infernal Axe or keep my Dragon Axe?

A: It depends on your goals. If you want maximum Woodcutting XP and don't care about logs, use the Infernal Axe -- the passive Firemaking XP is a bonus and you'll never have to drop logs. If you want profit or are an Ironman who needs logs for Construction/Fletching, keep using the Dragon or Crystal Axe. The Infernal Axe destroys 1/3 of logs permanently.

Q: What's the best way to get 75% Hosidius favour for the WC Guild?

A: Three fast methods: (1) Plow the fields in Hosidius (0-5% favour, ~5 min), (2) Make Sulphurous Fertiliser from saltpetre and compost (5-75% favour, ~45 min), (3) Complete Client of Kourend quest for 20% favour in all houses. The fertiliser method costs ~100K GP but takes under an hour from 0-75%.

Q: How much GP can I make from 1-99 Woodcutting?

A: It varies dramatically by method. Cutting and banking Magic logs (75-99) can yield 50-80M GP but takes 250+ hours. Cutting and banking Yews (60-99) yields 18-22M GP. Teaks/Mahogany to 99 yields 10-15M GP if banked. Redwoods 90-99 yields ~5M GP. For pure GP, AFK Magic logs at the Woodcutting Guild with the +7 boost is the best balance of profit and AFK.

Woodcutting is one of the most chill skills in OSRS. Whether you're a F2P player grinding Willows in Draynor, a member tick-manipulating Teaks on Ape Atoll, or someone AFKing Redwoods while working from home, the path to 99 is well-worn and straightforward. The cape is a classic -- and that +10% bird nest perk genuinely adds up over time.

Grab your axe, find your spot, and start chopping. Every log gets you closer.
