① 🎯 Why Level a F2P Account First?（为什么先练F2P账户？）

Before you rush to buy a bond or subscription, here's why spending time in F2P is actually smart:

1.1 💰 Learn the Game Without Wasting Membership Days（在不浪费会员天数的情况下学习游戏）

Membership costs money (or bonds worth ~5.2M GP). Every day you spend as a member trying to figure out where Lumbridge Castle is, you're burning real-world value. F2P lets you learn the core mechanics — movement, questing, banking, Grand Exchange trading, and basic combat — for free. When you finally go members, you'll know exactly what you're doing.

② ⚔️ Combat Leveling (Attack/Strength/Defence/Ranged/Magic)（战斗技能升级（攻击/力量/防御/远程/魔法））

Combat is where most F2P players spend their time. Your combat level is the single most visible stat, and it determines what you can kill, what quests you can complete, and whether PKers in the Wilderness laugh at you or run away.

Save your GP for gear upgrades（省下金币用于装备升级）.

2.3 ✨ Magic — F2P Training Methods（魔法 — F2P训练方法）

Magic is expensive to train in F2P (runes aren't cheap), but it unlocks teleports, high-alching, and powerful combat spells. Two main paths:

Path 1 — Combat Magic (costs GP, faster XP)（路线1——战斗魔法，消耗金币，经验更快）:

  • 1–25: Cast Wind Strike on cows/chickens (mind rune + air rune, ~2 GP per cast)（对鸡/奶牛使用风击，每次约2金币）
  • 25–45: Cast Fire Strike on Hill Giants (chaos rune + fire rune, ~60 GP per cast)（对山丘巨人使用火焰打击，每次约60金币）
  • 45–55: Cast Fire Bolt on Moss Giants (chaos rune + fire rune, ~60 GP per cast)（对苔藓巨人使用火焰箭，每次约60金币）
  • 55+: High Alchemy (generates profit! See below) OR Fire Blast on Ogress Warriors（高抛赚钱或对食人魔战士使用火焰爆）

Path 2 — High Alchemy (earns GP, slower XP)（路线2——高抛，赚钱，经验较慢）:

  • Buy items below alch price on the GE (common picks: magic longbows, adamant platebodies, rune items)（在GE购买低于高抛价值的物品，常见选择：魔法长弓、精金板甲、符文物品）
  • Cast High Alchemy while running between training spots（在训练地点之间跑动时施放高抛）
  • XP: 65 XP per cast. GP: 50–200 GP profit per cast（经验：每次65点，利润：每次50-200金币）
  • This method can earn you 100K–300K GP/hour while training Magic（这种方法可在训练魔法时每小时赚取10万-30万金币）
⚠️ Don't Splash in F2P（不要在F2P中泼水练级）

Splashing (wearing full armour to hit '-65 magic attack' and attacking rats) is a members-only strategy — it requires specific gear that doesn't exist in F2P. You can't AFK Magic in F2P the way members can. Read our F2P combat training guide for more combat-specific methods.

3.2 🍳 Cooking（烹饪）

Cooking is the fastest F2P 99 and the cheapest buyable skill（烹饪是F2P最快的99级技能，也是最便宜的可购买技能）.

  • 1–15: Cook shrimp (Draynor or Lumbridge range)（煮虾，德雷诺或洛布里奇炉灶）
  • 15–30: Cook trout (from your fly fishing)（煮鳟鱼，来自飞钓）
  • 30–58: Cook salmon (better XP than trout)（煮三文鱼，经验比鳟鱼高）
  • 58–74: Cook tuna (burns less at Lumbridge castle range)（煮金枪鱼，洛布里奇城堡炉灶烧焦率更低）
  • 74–99: Cook swordfish → cook sharks at 80+ if you buy them（煮剑鱼→80级后煮鲨鱼，如果购买的话）
💡 Always Cook at Lumbridge Castle Range（始终在洛布里奇城堡炉灶烹饪）

The Lumbridge Castle cooking range has the lowest burn rate in F2P. It's on the ground floor, right next to the bank chest. Use it for every single fish you cook — there's no reason to cook anywhere else in F2P（洛布里奇城堡炉灶的烧焦率最低，在一楼紧邻银行箱，每条鱼都在这里煮）.

Smithing is best trained by buying ores and smelting bars — mining your own ores is far too slow（锻造最好通过购买矿石冶炼来训练，自己采矿太慢）.

In F2P, smelting bars at the Al Kharid furnace or Edgeville furnace is the fastest method. Both are close to banks. If you combine smithing with high alching (crafting platebodies and alching them), you can break even or profit slightly.

3.6 🎨 Crafting（制造）

Crafting in F2P has limited options, but you can still reach 99.

  • 1–8: Leather gloves (buy leather from GE)（皮革手套，从GE购买皮革）
  • 8–27: Gold amulets (buy gold bars, use necklace mould)（金护身符，购买金条，使用项链模具）
  • 27–46: Holy symbols (silver bars, holy symbol mould)（神圣符号，银条+神圣符号模具）
  • 46–54: Tiaras (silver bars, tiara mould)（头冠，银条+头冠模具）
  • 54–70: Cut emeralds → emerald amulets（切割翡翠→翡翠护身符）
  • 70–99: Cut diamonds → diamond amulets (members item, can't wear but can sell)（切割钻石→钻石护身符，会员物品不能穿戴但可出售）

F2P crafting is slow and expensive (3–8 GP/XP). Unless you're a completionist, getting 50+ crafting before members is enough — members unlocks battlestaves, dragonhide armour, and glassblowing, all of which are faster and profitable.

💡 F2P Runecrafting Reality Check（F2P符文制作现实检查）

Getting 99 Runecrafting in F2P takes 300–600 hours. Members get access to the Abyss, Guardians of the Rift, and Ourania Altar, which massively increase XP rates. If you want 99 Runecrafting, go members. 44 Runecrafting (for nature runes + profit) is a much more reasonable F2P goal.

④ 🗺️ Best F2P Training Spots Map（最佳F2P训练地点地图）

Knowing where to train is half the battle. Here's a quick reference of the most important F2P locations:

4.1 🔥 Stronghold of Security (Barbarian Village)（安全堡垒（野蛮人村））

The Stronghold of Security is the single most important F2P training location. It has four floors, each with aggressive monsters scaling in difficulty:

  • Level 1 — Minotaurs (level 12)（牛头人）: Good for levels 15–30, drop iron arrows（适合15-30级，掉落铁箭）
  • Level 2 — Flesh Crawlers (level 28/35/41)（血肉爬行者）: Best AFK spot for levels 40–70, aggressive, great XP（40-70级最佳挂机点）
  • Level 3 — Giant Spiders (level 50)（巨型蜘蛛）: Pure XP for levels 55–99, no drops but fast kills（55-99级纯经验）
  • Level 4 — Ankous (level 75/82)（安可斯）: For levels 70+, good drops (death runes, law runes)（70级以上，掉落死亡符文和法符文）

Each floor also gives a reward at the center chest: 1K, 5K, 10K, and the coveted boots (Fancy or Fighting boots). These boots are BIS F2P footwear. Also, if you haven't already, claim your 10K GP from the Stronghold of Security — just navigate to the end of level 4 and collect from the chest. For a step-by-step guide, check our first week progression guide.

4.2 🦎 Corsair Cove (Best All-Round F2P Zone)（海盗湾（最佳全能F2P区域））

Corsair Cove is an underrated F2P area southwest of Rimmington. It has:

  • Ogress Warriors (level 82)（食人魔战士）: Best endgame F2P combat/Ranged training (30K–50K XP/hour, alchable drops worth 80K–120K GP/hour)（最佳后期战斗/远程训练点）
  • Maple trees（枫树）: Behind the gate (requires Dragon Slayer) — 3 maple trees, rarely crowded（门后3棵枫树，很少拥挤）
  • Lobster/swordfish fishing（龙虾/剑鱼钓鱼）: On the pier（在码头上）
  • Bank deposit box（银行存款箱）: Right next to the fishing spots（紧邻钓鱼点）

4.3 ⛰️ Crandor (Dragon Slayer Island)（克兰多尔（屠龙者岛））

Unlocked after the Dragon Slayer quest, Crandor has:

  • Moss Giants (3 spawns, safe spots available)（苔藓巨人，3个刷新点，可卡位）
  • Lesser Demons (for high-level melee/Ranged training)（次级恶魔，高级近战/远程训练）
  • 2 gold rocks for mining（2块金矿）

It's a quiet island — perfect for solo training without competition. Access via the Karamja volcano shortcut (after completing Dragon Slayer) or by boat from Port Sarim.

⑤ 📋 F2P Leveling Order — What to Train First（F2P升级顺序 — 先练什么）

You have 12 skills and limited time. Here's the optimal order to level them for maximum efficiency.

5.1 🥇 Phase 1: The First 10 Hours (New Account)（第一阶段：前10小时（新账户））

Your priorities in the first 10 hours:

  1. Do the Stronghold of Security（完成安全堡垒） — Grab the 10K GP reward and boots. This takes 15 minutes and funds your early game（领取1万金币和靴子奖励，15分钟完成，为前期提供资金）.
  2. Start Waterfall Quest (members) or Cook's Assistant (F2P)（开始瀑布任务或厨师助手） — If you're already members, Waterfall Quest gives 13,750 Attack + Strength XP. In F2P, do Cook's Assistant, Imp Catcher, and Witch's Potion（会员做瀑布任务，F2P做厨师助手、小恶魔捕捉者和女巫药水）.
  3. Train melee to 30/30/30（近战练到30/30/30） — Kill cows in Lumbridge, collect hides for GP. Takes 2–3 hours（在洛布里奇杀牛，收集牛皮卖钱，需2-3小时）.
  4. Buy full mithril + rune scimitar (when 40 Attack)（购买全套秘银+符文弯刀，40攻击时使用） — Use cowhide profits（用卖牛皮的钱购买）.
  5. Train Fishing to 20 (fly fishing)（钓鱼练到20级，飞钓） — For food self-sufficiency（实现食物自给自足）.

5.2 🥈 Phase 2: The Mid-Game Foundation (10–40 Hours)（第二阶段：中期基础（10-40小时））

  1. Melee to 50/50/50（近战50/50/50） — Hill Giants then Flesh Crawlers. This is your main grind（山丘巨人然后血肉爬行者，这是主要刷经验阶段）.
  2. Ranged to 40（远程到40） — For Dragon Slayer preparation（为屠龙者做准备）.
  3. Magic to 37（魔法到37） — Falador Teleport is a huge mobility upgrade（法洛德传送是巨大的移动升级）.
  4. Fishing & Cooking to 40（钓鱼和烹饪到40） — Lobster fishing at Karamja for food and small profit（在卡拉姆贾钓龙虾获取食物和小额利润）.
  5. Woodcutting to 30（砍树到30） — Willows at Draynor for AFK breaks（在德雷诺砍柳树做挂机休息）.
  6. Start Dragon Slayer（开始屠龙者任务） — Aim to complete it by the end of this phase（目标在本阶段结束前完成）.

5.3 🥉 Phase 3: F2P Endgame (40–80+ Hours)（第三阶段：F2P后期（40-80+小时））

  1. All combat stats to 60+（全战斗技能60+） — Ogress Warriors or Giant Spiders（食人魔战士或巨型蜘蛛）.
  2. Prayer to 40+（祷告到40+） — Big bones at Wilderness altar or bury at GE（在荒野祭坛使用大骨头或在GE埋骨）.
  3. All skilling skills to 50+（全生产技能50+） — Pick 2–3 you enjoy, don't grind all of them（选择2-3个你喜欢的，不要全部刷）.
  4. Bank 1M+ GP（存款100万+金币） — Through Ogress alchables, yew logs, swordfish, or high alching（通过食人魔高抛物品、紫杉木、剑鱼或高抛）.
  5. Complete all F2P quests（完成所有F2P任务） — 22 quests for the Quest Point cape (members only) but still worth doing（22个任务虽然任务点数披风是会员专属，但仍值得完成）.

At this point, you should seriously consider going members. F2P XP rates cap at around 50K XP/hour, while members methods can hit 100K–300K+ XP/hour. If you're spending 10+ hours on a single level, that's the signal to upgrade.

⑥ 🚪 When to Switch to Members（何时转为付费会员）

This is the million-GP question: when does F2P stop being worth it? Here's how to know.

6.1 📉 Signs F2P Is Becoming Inefficient（F2P变得低效的迹象）

You should strongly consider going members when:

  • Your next level takes 5+ hours（下一级需要5+小时） — Members methods could cut this to 1–2 hours（会员方法可缩短到1-2小时）
  • You've done all 22 F2P quests（已完成全部22个F2P任务） — There's nothing else to unlock（没有其他内容可解锁了）
  • You have 1M+ GP and can afford members gear（有100万+金币可购买会员装备） — Going members with no GP is painful（没金币就转会员很痛苦）
  • You're grinding skills above level 60（在刷60级以上技能） — Members XP rates pull ahead dramatically（会员经验率大幅领先）
  • You're bored of killing the same 5 monsters（厌倦了杀同样的5种怪物） — Members has 100+ slayer monsters, bosses, raids（会员有100+杀戮者怪物、Boss和团队副本）
  • You want to try a members-only skill（想尝试会员专属技能） — Slayer, Agility, Thieving, Farming, Herblore, Construction won't level in F2P（杀戮者、敏捷、盗窃、农耕、草药学、建筑在F2P无法练级）

6.2 💡 The Sweet Spot: Level 50s Across the Board（最佳平衡点：全技能50级）

After coaching hundreds of new players, here's the consensus: get all F2P skills to 50+, complete Dragon Slayer, and then go members. This takes roughly 40–60 hours of playtime and gives you:

  • Enough combat stats to handle most members content（足够的战斗属性应对大部分会员内容）
  • A solid understanding of game mechanics（扎实的游戏机制理解）
  • 500K–2M GP for starting members gear（50万-200万金币用于起步会员装备）
  • All 22 F2P quests completed (and the QP to start Recipe for Disaster)（已完成全部22个F2P任务）

Going members earlier (e.g., at level 3) isn't wrong — you'll just spend your first week doing things you could have done for free in F2P. Going members later (e.g., 70+ all stats) means you wasted 100+ hours on slow XP. Level 50s is the Goldilocks zone.

💡 The Bond Strategy（债券策略）

If GP is your main concern: grind to 1M+ GP in F2P, then use your first 14 days of membership (from a bond or purchase) to earn enough GP for your next bond. Members money-makers like Wintertodt let you sustain bonds indefinitely. Read our bond strategy guide for the full plan. For a head-to-head comparison, see our bond vs. subscription guide.

⑦ ❓ FAQ — F2P Leveling Guide（常见问题 — F2P升级指南）

Q: What's the fastest F2P skill to 99?（F2P最快到99的技能是什么？）

A: Cooking is by far the fastest F2P 99（烹饪是F2P最快的99级技能）. You can buy raw fish from the GE and cook at Lumbridge Castle range, reaching 200K–300K XP/hour at high levels（从GE购买生鱼在洛布里奇城堡烹饪，高级别可达20万-30万经验/小时）. From level 1, a 99 Cooking grind costs about 2–4M GP and takes 40–60 hours（从1级到99级烹饪约需200万-400万金币和40-60小时）. Firemaking and Prayer are also fast but more expensive.

Q: Should I train Prayer in F2P or wait for members?（应该在F2P练祷告还是等会员？）

A: Wait for members if possible（如果可以的话等会员）. The Gilded Altar (3.5× XP) is members-only and cuts the grind by two-thirds（镀金祭坛3.5倍经验，减少三分之二刷怪时间）. In F2P, your best option is burying big bones (15 XP) at the GE or running them to the Wilderness altar (2× XP but PKer risk)（F2P最佳选择是在GE埋大骨头或跑到荒野祭坛，2倍经验但有PK风险）. Getting 43 Prayer in F2P takes ~4 hours of big bone grinding; in members it takes ~1 hour at a Gilded Altar（F2P到43祷告约需4小时，会员在镀金祭坛只需1小时）.

Q: Where should I train combat for the best XP in F2P?（在F2P哪里训练战斗经验最高？）

A: For pure XP, Giant Spiders in the Stronghold of Security (Level 3) give the best rates（纯经验的话，安全堡垒第3层的巨型蜘蛛给予最佳经验率）. They're aggressive, have 50 HP, and constantly respawn（它们主动攻击，50血量，不断刷新）. For a mix of XP and GP, Ogress Warriors at Corsair Cove are unbeatable — 30K–45K XP/hour plus 80K–120K GP/hour in alchable drops（经验金币兼顾的话，海盗湾的食人魔战士无与伦比）. For a dedicated combat guide, see our F2P combat training guide.

Q: Can I make good GP in F2P?（在F2P能赚到好金币吗？）

A: Yes, but nowhere near members rates（可以，但远不及会员）. The best F2P money-makers are: Ogress Warriors (80K–120K GP/hour via alchables), high alching while skilling (100K–200K GP/hour), yew log cutting (60K–80K GP/hour), and swordfish fishing (80K–120K GP/hour). A dedicated F2P player can earn 500K–1M GP/day. For comparison, members can earn 2M–5M GP/hour with high-level PvM. Check our F2P money-making guide for a full breakdown.

Q: What's the best F2P quest to do first?（F2P最先做哪个任务最好？）

A: Start with Cook's Assistant (300 Cooking XP, 1 minute), then Imp Catcher (875 Magic XP), then Witch's Potion (325 Magic XP). These three take under 10 minutes total and boost your Magic level（这三个总共不到10分钟，提升魔法等级）. Next, do The Knight's Sword (12,725 Smithing XP — skips you from level 1 to 29 Smithing instantly)（跳过锻造1-29级）. Then focus on unlocking Dragon Slayer（然后专注于解锁屠龙者）. For the full quest order, see our F2P quests before membership guide.

Q: Is it possible to enjoy OSRS without ever buying membership?（不买会员也能享受OSRS吗？）

A: Absolutely. Many "F2P purists" have accounts with hundreds of millions of XP and maxed F2P skills. F2P has a dedicated PvP community (Edgeville wilderness, clan wars), its own economy, and dedicated worlds. That said, roughly 80% of OSRS content is members-only. If you find yourself enjoying F2P enough to play 50+ hours, you'll almost certainly enjoy members even more.

Q: What's the best F2P melee weapon at each level bracket?（每个等级区间的最佳F2P近战武器是什么？）

A: 1–10: Iron scimitar（铁弯刀，96金币）. 10–20: Black scimitar（黑弯刀，300金币）. 20–30: Mithril scimitar（秘银弯刀，1200金币）. 30–40: Adamant scimitar（精金弯刀，3600金币）. 40–60+: Rune scimitar (32,000 GP — skip the rune 2h sword, scimitar's 4-tick speed is always better)（符文弯刀3.2万金币，4tick速度始终更优）. After Dragon Slayer, the rune platebody (+10 Defence) is a massive upgrade. For crush training (vs. certain bosses), Viggora's chainmace is the best option if you have 60 Attack and access to the Wilderness.

Q: How much GP should I have before going members?（转会员前需要多少金币？）

A: 1M GP is the minimum for a comfortable members start — enough for basic gear (rune scimitar, rune armour, amulet of power), some food, and a few teleports（100万金币是舒适起步的最低要求，足够购买基础装备、食物和传送）. 2–5M GP lets you buy initial Slayer gear and maybe a Bond (5.2M GP). If you have less than 500K GP, consider staying F2P a bit longer to build your bank — members money-makers are faster, but you need some starting capital.

Q: Can I complete Dragon Slayer at combat level 40?（40战斗等级能完成屠龙者吗？）

A: Yes, but it's tough（可以，但很难）. The Dungeon has level 40-60 monsters, and the final fight against Elvarg (level 83 dragon) hits hard（地下城有40-60级怪物，最终Boss埃尔瓦格攻击很高）. If your combat stats are 40/40/40 with 40 Defence, bring: rune scimitar, rune armour, 10+ lobsters, and an amulet of power. If you're struggling, level Attack to 50 first — the extra max hit makes a big difference. Most players complete Dragon Slayer at 50-60 combat stats for a much easier time.

Q: What's the most efficient F2P Magic training method for low GP?（低金币预算下最有效的F2P魔法训练方法是什么？）

A: If you're on a tight budget, your only free Magic training option is splashing — but F2P doesn't have the gear required for effective splashing (full dragonhide doesn't exist in F2P). Instead: (1) Cast Wind Strike on cows for 1-15 Magic (costs almost nothing — air and mind runes are everywhere)（对奶牛使用风击，几乎零成本）, (2) Steal from the duel arena or do the Demon Slayer quest for rune rewards（从决斗场偷或做屠魔者任务获得符文奖励）, (3) At 31 Magic, buy fire runes in bulk and cast Fire Strike on Hill Giants (drops law runes worth ~200 GP each). The most GP-efficient path: combine Magic training with High Alchemy once you reach 55 Magic — buy items below alch value and profit while training.

⑧ 💡 Final Tips for F2P Leveling（F2P升级最终建议）

After spending hundreds of hours in both F2P and members, here's what I wish someone had told me:

  • Don't try to max everything in F2P（不要在F2P中追求全技能满级）. Getting 50+ in all skills is the smart play（全技能50+是明智之举）. Members training is 2–5× faster for almost every skill（会员训练速度是F2P的2-5倍）. Every hour you spend grinding 70–99 Mining in F2P is an hour you could have spent getting 70–90 Mining in members（在F2P刷采矿70-99的每小时，都可以在会员刷到70-90）.
  • Balance combat and skilling（平衡战斗和生产技能）. Many new players go all-in on combat and neglect fishing/cooking, then get stuck because they can't afford food（很多新玩家全投入战斗而忽略钓鱼/烹饪，结果买不起食物卡住）. Train fishing and cooking alongside combat so you're always self-sufficient（与战斗同时训练钓鱼和烹饪，实现自给自足）.
  • Use the Grand Exchange wisely（明智使用GE）. Check buy/sell margins before you flip items（在倒卖物品前检查买卖价差）. In F2P, margins on common items (ores, logs, fish) are tight — 5–10%（F2P中常见物品的利润率很薄，5-10%）. Don't undercut yourself（不要自己压价）.
  • Do your F2P quests（完成F2P任务）. They unlock critical areas (Corsair Cove, Crandor, Asgarnian Ice Dungeon) and give XP rewards that skip early grinding（它们解锁关键区域并提供跳过早期刷怪的经验奖励）. The Knight's Sword alone saves 3 hours of Smithing training（单单骑士之剑就节省3小时锻造训练）.
  • Join a clan chat（加入公会聊天）. Even in F2P, you can join clan chats（即使在F2P也能加入公会聊天）. "OSRS Advice" and "Reddit CC" are active F2P-friendly chats where you can ask questions and find groups.
  • Protect your account（保护你的账户）. Set up 2FA on your email and OSRS account, use a bank PIN, and never share your password（在邮箱和OSRS账户设置双重验证，使用银行密码，绝不分享密码）. For a complete security walkthrough, read our account security guide.
🎯 The F2P Leveling Mindset（F2P升级心态）

F2P OSRS has a beauty that members has lost: simplicity（F2P OSRS有一种会员已失去的美——简单）. No chaos altar rushing, no 0-time training, no efficiency-scape. You kill cows, you catch fish, you chop trees. The grind is slower, but the satisfaction is real. Treat F2P as your tutorial — learn the game, build your bank, complete Dragon Slayer. Then, when you finally sail to a members world, you'll appreciate every teleport, every new weapon, and every quest reward that much more.
