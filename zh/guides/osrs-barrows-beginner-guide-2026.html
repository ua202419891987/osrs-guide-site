① What Are Barrows Brothers?（Barrows兄弟简介）

Barrows is a low-to-mid-level PvM activity where you fight 6 brothers (plus a 7th hidden brother, Akrisae). Each brother drops their own set piece with a unique set effect when full set is worn.

① Why Barrows Is Perfect for Beginners（为什么Barrows适合新手）

🎯 5 Reasons Barrows = Beginner's Best PvM（5个理由证明Barrows是新手最佳PvM）

  • Solo-able: No team needed — fight at your own pace
  • Budget gear works: <50K GP setup can clear Barrows
  • Unique set effects: No other gear gives these buffs
  • Profit per hour: ~300-800K GP/hour (varies with luck)
  • Excellent starter PvM: Learn boss mechanics before harder content
💡 Barrows Set Effects — Why They're Powerful（Barrows套装效果为什么强）

Ahrim's (Magic): +1% accuracy per piece (6% total) — small but noticeable.
Dharok's (HP-based): Higher HP = higher damage (overkill potential!)
Guthan's (Healing): Chance to heal each hit (zero food needed!)
Karil's (Ranged): Chance to lower target's Agility (minor PvM use).
Torag's (HP-based): Higher HP = higher damage (tankier version of Dharok).
Verac's (Prayer-ignoring): Full set = ANY hit can damage through Protect prayer!

③ Quest Requirements（任务要求）

✅ Priest in Peril (REQUIRED)（必须完成）

This quest unlocks Barrows. You must complete it before entering the tunnels. Requirements: 25 Ranged, 30 Attack, 34 Constitution.

④ Recommended Optional Quests（推荐可选任务）

📋 Quests That Help（有帮助的任务）

  • In Search of the Myreque: Unlocks Morytania (faster Barrows trips)
  • Nature Spirit: Unlocks Prayer grinding (Druidic ritual)
  • Watchtower (Partial): Unlocks Watchtower teleport (close to Barrows)

③ 3 Budget Gear Setups (< 1M / < 5M / < 15M)（3种预算装备搭配）

You DON'T need expensive gear to clear Barrows. Here are 3 budget setups:

⑤ Setup 1: < 1M GP (Budget Melee)（<1M预算近战搭配）

🗡️ Cheap Melee Setup（便宜近战搭配）

  • Weapon: Dragon Scimitar (~50K GP) or Rune Scimitar (~5K GP)
  • Armor: Full Rune (~100K GP total) or Green Dragonhide (~80K)
  • Prayer: Protect from Melee (43 Prayer required!)
  • Inventory: 10 Shark, 2 Prayer potions (4-dose), remainder empty for loot

⑥ Setup 2: < 5M GP (Mid-Level Melee)（<5M中级近战搭配）

⚔️ Mid-Level Melee Setup（中级近战搭配）

  • Weapon: Abyssal Whip (~2.5M GP) or Dragon Scimitar + Dragon Defender (~500K)
  • Armor: Dragon armor set (~1M GP) or Barrows pieces (if you have them)
  • Prayer: Piety (70 Prayer) if available, else Ultimate Strength + Protect prayers
  • Inventory: 8 Shark, 3 Super Attack (2-dose), 2 Prayer potions (4-dose)

⑦ Setup 3: < 15M GP (High-Level Melee + Barrows)（<15M高级近战搭配）

🏆 High-Level Melee Setup（高级近战搭配）

  • Weapon: Abyssal Whip + Dragon Defender OR Osmumten's Fang (~10M GP)
  • Armor: Barrows set (e.g., Dharok's, Torag's) + Neitiznot Faceguard (~1.5M)
  • Prayer: Piety (70 Prayer) + 43+ for Protect prayers
  • Inventory: 6 Shark, 2 Super Attack (2-dose), 3 Prayer potions (4-dose), 1 Super Antipoison
💡 Ranged/Magic Setups Also Work!（远程/魔法搭配也可以）

You can use Ranged (Karil's set) or Magic (Ahrim's set) if you have them. They're safer for learning, but kill speed is slower than melee.

④ Getting There & Navigating the Tunnels（到达路线与隧道导航）

Knowing how to get to Barrows fast = more trips per hour = more profit.

⑧ Fastest Routes to Barrows（最快到达路线）

🚀 Method 1: Fairy Ring (Recommended)（妖精之环 · 推荐）

Code: B.I.P. (northwest of Barrows). From Fairy Ring, run southeast ~30 seconds to the Barrows mounds.

🚀 Method 2: Morytania Teleport (After Quest)（Morytania传送 · 任务后）

After In Search of the Myreque quest, use Morytania Teleport (Lumbridge Diary Medium reward). Runs 5 seconds to Barrows.

🚀 Method 3: Watchtower Teleport + Run（Watchtower传送+跑步）

Teleport to Watchtower (Yanille) → run north through Morytania → ~2 minutes (slowest).

⑨ Tunnel Navigation — Which Brother Is Where?（隧道导航 · 各兄弟位置）

🗺️ Barrows Mound Map（Barrows土堆地图）

  • Ahrim the Blighted (Magic): Southwest mound
  • Dharok the Wretched (HP-based): North-central mound
  • Guthan the Infested (Healing): Southeast mound
  • Karil the Tainted (Ranged): East mound
  • Torag the Corrupted (HP-based): West mound
  • Verac the Defiled (Prayer-ignoring): Northwest mound

⑩ Entering the Tunnels（进入隧道）

Step 1: Kill All 6 Brothers Above-Ground（击杀地上所有6个兄弟）

Click each sarcophagus above-ground → fight the brother → kill him. After all 6 are dead, a tunnel entrance appears near the center of the circle.

Step 2: Enter the Tunnel（进入隧道）

Click the tunnel entrance → you're now in the Barrows tunnels. There are 6 rooms with various monsters + the final chest room.

💡 Puzzle Room Shortcut（谜题室捷径）

Some tunnels have puzzle rooms (you need to pull levers to open doors). These are random each run. Don't overthink — just pull all levers until the door opens. Takes ~30 seconds max.

⑤ Kill Order & Strategy per Brother（击杀顺序与各兄弟策略）

Each brother has a unique attack style. Use the correct Protect prayer to take zero damage.

⑪ Kill Order (Recommended for Beginners)（推荐新手的击杀顺序）

⚔️ Safe Kill Order（安全击杀顺序）

  1. Ahrim (Magic): Use Protect from Magic
  2. Karil (Ranged): Use Protect from Missiles
  3. Dharok (Melee): Use Protect from Melee (he hits hard!)
  4. Verac (Melee/Prayer-ignore): Use Protect from Melee (doesn't fully block, but helps)
  5. Torag (Melee): Use Protect from Melee
  6. Guthan (Melee/Healing): Use Protect from Melee (he can heal — kill fast!)

⑫ Per-Brother Strategy（各兄弟细节策略）

🧙 Ahrim the Blighted (Magic)（魔法系）

Attack Style: Magic (hits through Ranged/Magic armor!).
Prayer: Protect from Magic. He can't hit you.
Strategy: Tank him with Protect Magic + attack with Melee/Ranged.

🏹 Karil the Tainted (Ranged)（远程系）

Attack Style: Ranged.
Prayer: Protect from Missiles. Zero damage.
Strategy: Use Melee or Magic. He's weak to Magic.

🪓 Dharok the Wretched (HP-based)（血量相关伤害）

Attack Style: Melee. Hits HARD at low HP.
Prayer: Protect from Melee.
Strategy: Kill him FAST. If your HP drops low, he'll start hitting 30-40s!

⚔️ Verac the Defiled (Prayer-ignore)（无视祈祷）

Attack Style: Melee. His set effect ignores Protect prayer (full set only).
Prayer: Protect from Melee (still reduces some damage).
Strategy: Bring Antipoison (he poisons) + kill quickly.

🛡️ Torag the Corrupted (HP-based)（血量相关伤害·坦克型）

Attack Style: Melee. Like Dharok but tankier.
Prayer: Protect from Melee.
Strategy: Safe, slowly kill. He's not dangerous.

🩸 Guthan the Infested (Healing)（自我治疗）

Attack Style: Melee. His set effect heals him per hit.
Prayer: Protect from Melee.
Strategy: Kill him FAST (use high DPS). Don't let him heal!

⑥ The Chest Run — Puzzle Solving Fast（宝箱路线与快速解谜）

After killing all 6 brothers, you reach the chest room. This is where the loot is!

⑬ Opening the Chest（打开宝箱）

Step 1: Click the Chest（点击宝箱）

The chest is in the final room of the tunnels. Click it → you'll see a loot interface with examples of possible drops.

Step 2: Take All Loot（拾取所有战利品）

Click "Take All" → all items go into your inventory. If it's full, drops go to the ground (pick them up quickly!).

⑭ Puzzle Rooms — How to Solve Fast（谜题室 · 快速解法）

🧩 Puzzle Room Types（谜题室类型）

  • Lever rooms: Pull all levers. One opens the door. Try all combinations (max 4 levers).
  • Symbol rooms: Match the symbols on the wall. Click the same symbols on the floor.
  • Three-door rooms: One door is correct. Open all 3 quickly (the right one leads onward).
💡 Shortcut: Don't Solve Puzzles!（捷径：跳过解谜）

If you're in a hurry, you can teleport out after killing all 6 brothers WITHOUT opening the chest. You'll miss the loot, but save ~2 minutes per run. Only do this if you're speed-running for XP, NOT for profit!

Barrows profit is RNG-based (luck-dependent). Some runs you get 10K GP, others you get a 2.5M GP Barrows piece!

⑯ Profit Per Hour Calculation（每小时收益计算）

💰 Profit Table (Approximate)（收益表 · 近似值）

  • Bad luck (no Barrows pieces in 10 runs): ~100K GP/hour
  • Average luck (1-2 pieces per 10 runs): ~300-500K GP/hour
  • Good luck (3-4 pieces per 10 runs): ~800K — 1.2M GP/hour
  • Jackpot (Dharok's platebody, Ahrim's robetop, etc.): 2M+ GP in one run!

⑰ Factors That Affect Profit（影响收益的因素）

① Combat Level & Kill Speed（战斗等级与击杀速度）

Higher Combat = faster kills = more runs per hour = more chances at Barrows pieces.

② Number of Brothers Killed（击杀兄弟数量）

Killing all 6 brothers gives the highest chance at loot. Killing only 2-3 = lower profit per hour.

③ RNG (Luck)（随机运气）

Some days you'll get 3 Barrows pieces in 8 runs. Other days you'll get 0 in 20 runs. That's RNG for you!

💡 Break-Even Point（回本点）

With a < 1M GP budget setup, your only costs are food + prayer potions (~20K GP per run). If you get even ONE Barrows piece per session, you've already made a profit!

⑧ Barrows FAQ — Common Beginner Questions（Barrows常见问题）

Can I solo Barrows at Combat level 50?（战斗50级能单挑Barrows吗？）

Yes! With 43 Prayer (Protect prayers), you can solo Barrows at Combat 50. It'll be slow (~15-20 minutes per run), but totally doable. Upgrade your gear as you level.

What's the best brother to farm for profit?（哪个兄弟的掉落最值钱？）

All brothers have the same drop rate for their set pieces. However, Dharok's platebody and Ahrim's robetop are the most valuable (2-2.5M GP each). There's no way to "farm one brother" — you must kill all 6 for the chest to spawn.

Do I need a specific Combat style for Barrows?（打Barrows需要特定战斗风格吗？）

No! Any Combat style works. Melee is fastest (if you have good gear). Ranged/Magic are safer for learning. Use what you have!

Why am I not getting any Barrows pieces?（为什么我拿不到Barrows装备？）

Bad RNG or not killing all 6 brothers. Make sure you kill ALL 6 above-ground BEFORE entering the tunnels. Also, higher Combat level = slightly better drop rates (not confirmed by Jagex, but community-tested).

Can Ironmen do Barrows?（铁人能打Barrows吗？）

YES! Barrows is essential for Ironmen. It's one of the only ways to get Barrows gear (can't buy from GE as Ironman). Expect a LONG grind — RNG is cruel to Ironmen!
