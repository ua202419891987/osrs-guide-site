1. What Is Mid-Game Money Making?（什么是中期赚钱）

Mid-game in OSRS (roughly combat 70-100, total level 1000-1750) is where money making transitions from grinding to strategy. You're past the "pick flax for 100K/hr" phase, but not yet at raids or endgame bosses. The methods here scale from 500K/hr to 3M/hr.

Key insight: The biggest mistake mid-game players make is spending all their GP on gear upgrades instead of investing in money-making capability (construction level for pool, farming level for better herbs, prayer level for Piety). Prioritize skills that unlock better GP/hr.

2. Barrows Runs — 500K-1M GP/hr（巴罗斯 · 每小时50-100万）

Barrows is the entry-level PvM money maker and the most accessible mid-game boss content. Six brothers, a tunnel, and a chest — simple mechanics with solid, consistent profit.

Gear Setup (Budget: ~2M GP)（预算装备 · 约200万）

  • Weapon: Iban's staff (u) — upgrade to Trident of the seas at 75 Magic
  • Armor: Proselyte/Monk robes (prayer bonus) or mystic robes
  • Shield: Tome of fire (with Iban's) or Book of darkness
  • Inventory: 6-8 prayer potions, spade, runes, emergency food, Barrows teleport tablets

Profit Breakdown（利润分析）

Each Barrows piece is worth 100K-3M GP depending on the item. With Morytania Hard Diary completed, you get 50% more runes from the chest, pushing average profit to 1M+/hr.

This diary is the single biggest upgrade for Barrows. The 50% extra runes add ~300K/hr in consistent profit. Requirements: 70 Defence, 70 Prayer (can boost from 65), various other stats. Worth rushing.

3. Zulrah — 1M-2M GP/hr（祖尔拉 · 每小时100-200万）

Zulrah is the first serious PvM money maker most players learn. It has a learning curve but once mastered, it's consistent 2M+/hr profit with no gear repair costs (magic/ranged only).

How to Learn Zulrah（如何学习Zulrah）

  1. Use the Zulrah Helper plugin on RuneLite — it shows rotation maps and where to stand
  2. Start with one rotation only — if it's not your rotation, log out. Focus on learning rotation 3 (green → red → blue)
  3. Expect to die 20-50 times learning. Each death costs 100K to reclaim items. Budget 2-5M for deaths.
  4. Once you can do all 4 rotations consistently, you're at 1.5-2M/hr

Profit: Average kill = ~130K GP (scales + unique drops). At 15-20 kills/hr = 1.5M-2.5M/hr. Zulrah unique drops (tanzanite fang, magic fang, serpentine visage) are 2M-4M each.

4. Vorkath — 2M-3M GP/hr（沃卡斯 · 每小时200-300万）

Vorkath is the king of mid-game consistent profit. No unique drops needed to make money — every kill drops 100K-200K in bones and hides alone. Less RNG-dependent than Zulrah.

Vorkath's advantage: Super consistent profit floor. Even on your worst hour, you're still making 1.2M+. Perfect for grinding toward your first 100M.

6. Herb Runs — 100K-200K Profit Per Run (5 min)（药草循环 · 每次10-20万利润 · 5分钟）

Herb runs are the single best passive income method in OSRS. Each run takes 5 minutes, costs ~30K in seeds, and yields 130K-230K in herbs. Do 5-6 runs per day = easy 1M+ daily income.

Optimal Herb Run Setup（最优药草种植设置）

  • Patches to use: 6-9 patches (Trollheim, Catherby, Ardougne, Morytania, Falador, Hosidius, Weiss, Farming Guild, Harmony Island)
  • Teleports: Trollheim tab, Catherby tab, Ardougne cloak, Ectophial, Explorer's ring, Xeric's talisman
  • Always use ultracompost: Reduces disease chance and increases minimum herb yield

Bottomless Compost Bucket (Hespori)（无尽堆肥桶 · Hespori掉落）

The bottomless compost bucket doubles your ultracompost usage (one bucket holds 2x) and is obtained from Hespori (65 Farming). It pays for itself within a week of herb runs. Priority upgrade.

7. Birdhouse Runs — 30K-50K Per Run (2 min)（鸟屋循环 · 每次3-5万 · 2分钟）

Birdhouse runs are passive Hunter XP + profit that you can stack with herb runs. Each run takes 90-120 seconds and nests sell for 5K-8K each.

Synergy: Do birdhouse runs immediately before herb runs. The Fossil Island teleport (digsite pendant) puts you right next to the birdhouses. Total time: 7 minutes for both, earning 150K-250K in profit.

8. Flipping & Merchanting — 1M-5M GP/hr（倒卖 · 每小时100-500万）

Flipping is the highest potential GP/hr in the mid-game with zero skill requirements — just game knowledge and patience. It scales with your cash stack: more GP = bigger flips = more profit.

Flipping Strategy by Bank Size（按金币规模倒卖策略）

100M+Rares + endgame items (Tbow, Scythe, Shadow, 3A)3M-10M+

Flipping Rules for Beginners（新手倒卖规则）

  1. Start with items you understand (items you've bought/sold before)
  2. Test margins with 1 item first — don't buy 100 of something without testing
  3. Use OSRS Wiki prices as reference, not gospel — real margins differ
  4. 3-5% margin is good. 10%+ margin is exceptional (and rare)
  5. Diversify across 3-5 different items to spread risk
  6. Never flip items being manipulated (sudden price spikes on obscure items)
  7. Check trade volumes — avoid items with <100 daily trades

11. 1M to 100M Roadmap — Step by Step（100万到1亿路线图 · 逐步指南）

Phase 1: 1M to 10M (The Foundation)（第一阶段 · 100万到1000万 · 打基础）

  1. Do herb runs every 80 minutes. At 32 Farming (Ranarr): 100K/run. 5 runs/day = 500K. In 7 days: 3.5M.
  2. Add birdhouse runs. Stack with herb runs. 50K/run. 250K/day extra.
  3. Train Slayer to 65. Kurasks at 70 = consistent 500K-600K/hr.
  4. Buy essential gear: Whip (3M), blowpipe (3M), trident (1M), fury (1M), berserker ring (2M) = ~10M total.

Phase 2: 10M to 50M (Scaling Up)（第二阶段 · 1000万到5000万 · 扩大规模）

  1. Complete Dragon Slayer II. Unlocks Vorkath.
  2. Start Vorkath grinding. 15-20 kills/hr with blowpipe = 1.5M-2M/hr.
  3. Continue daily passive income. Herb runs + birdhouses + Miscellania = 500K-750K/day.
  4. Upgrade gear incrementally: Torture (10M), anguish (12M), tormented bracelet (12M).

Phase 3: 50M to 100M (The Push)（第三阶段 · 5000万到1亿 · 冲刺）

  1. Buy DHCB + DFW (optional). Pushes Vorkath to 2.5M-3M/hr.
  2. Scale up flipping. With 50M+ cash, flipping 1M-3M passive/day.
  3. Consider Gauntlet/Corrupted Gauntlet. 0 GP risk, 2M-3M/hr.

Total mid-game journey: 5-10 weeks to go from 1M to 100M. This is playing 2-3 hours/day.

12. FAQ（常见问题）

Q: What's the fastest way to 10M from scratch?（从零到1000万最快方法？）

Herb runs (ranarr/toadflax) every 80 minutes + Slayer to 65-75. ~7 days to 10M at 2-3 hours/day.

Q: Vorkath or Zulrah — which should I learn first?（Vorkath还是Zulrah先学？）

Vorkath if you want consistent profit and have DS2. Zulrah if you want to learn a harder boss that costs less to die at.

Q: When should I stop herb runs?（什么时候停止药草循环？）

Never. Even at endgame with 500M+ bank, herb runs are still 150K-200K for 5 minutes — effective 1.8M-2.4M/hr rate per active minute.

Q: Is flipping worth learning if I only have 5M?（只有500万值得学倒卖吗？）

Yes, but keep expectations realistic. With 5M, flipping consumables can yield 100K-200K/day with 10 minutes of work.

Q: How much GP do I need before starting Vorkath/Zulrah?（开始Vorkath/Zulrah前需要多少金币？）

Vorkath: 10M minimum. Zulrah: 12M minimum. Add 2-5M for death costs while learning either boss.
