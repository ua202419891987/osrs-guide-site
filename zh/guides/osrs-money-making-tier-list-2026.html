OSRS Money Making Tier List 2026
Every Method Ranked — S Tier to D Tier (Post-Gauntlet Update)

The June 2026 Gauntlet buffs and Summer Sweep-Up have reshuffled the money-making meta. Corrupted Gauntlet is now 27% more profitable. Sanguinesti Staff buff makes Zulrah and raids hit harder. Here's every method ranked honestly — no fluff, just GP/hr and what you actually need.

How This Tier List Works（等级排行说明）

Methods are ranked by effective GP/hr — that's raw profit minus supply costs, factoring in time spent banking, running, and dying. Tiers reflect the average player's experience, not theoretical max rates from tick-perfect speedrunners.

S Tier — 8M+ GP/hr (End-Game Dominance)（S级——8M+ GP/小时·终局统治）

🥇 Theatre of Blood (Hard Mode)（血腥剧场·困难模式） S

💰 10-15M GP/hr (efficient team) | 🔧 95+ Combat, ToB experience

The undisputed king. Scythe of Vitur (~800M) alone makes ToB worth running. With the Sanguinesti Staff buff in June 2026, magic DPS is even stronger for Nylocas room and Verzik P2. Requires a competent team — solo learners should start with Entry Mode first.

🥈 Tombs of Amascut (Expert 300+)（阿玛斯古墓·专家300+） S

💰 8-12M GP/hr (solo expert) | 🔧 90+ Combat, raid knowledge

ToA at Expert 300+ invocation is the best solo money maker in OSRS. Tumeken's Shadow (~1.5B) and Osmumten's Fang (~28M) are consistent big-ticket drops. The better you are, the more you make — and there's no team to split with.

🥉 Chambers of Xeric (Challenge Mode)（沙力克密室·挑战模式） S

💰 7-10M GP/hr (CM teams) | 🔧 90+ Combat, CoX experience

Twisted Bow (~1.5B) and Ancestral pieces drive CoX profits. Challenge Mode bumps the unique rate noticeably. Requires a team that knows Olm mechanics — one bad player tanks everyone's profit.

A Tier — 3-8M GP/hr (Your Daily Money Printer)（A级——3-8M GP/小时·日常印钞机）

🔥 Corrupted Gauntlet（腐化手套） A ⬆️ NEWLY BUFFED

💰 4.5-7M GP/hr | 🔧 80+ Combat, Song of the Elves | ⚡ ZERO supply cost

June 2026 buff made CG 27% faster. With the Crystal Crown drop rate buff and overall kill speed improvement, CG is now legitimately A-tier. The best part? You spend zero GP on supplies. Every single crystal, alchable, and armor seed is pure profit. For accounts at 80-95 combat range, this is now the single best money maker — period.

💀 Phantom Muspah（幻影穆帕） A

💰 3.5-5M GP/hr | 🔧 85+ Ranged, 70+ Agility

Ancient Essence (~1.5K each, drops 100+ per kill) and Venator Bow shards make Muspah consistently profitable. Ranged-only method with Ruby/Diamond bolts (e) is beginner-friendly. Frozen Cache phase adds variety but keeps the fight engaging.

🐉 Vorkath (Dragon Hunter Crossbow)（沃卡斯·龙猎弩） A

💰 3-4M GP/hr | 🔧 Dragon Slayer II, 85+ Ranged

Blue dragonhide + superior bones + consistent alchables. If you have a Dragon Hunter Crossbow (~65M), Vorkath is the most reliable consistent-gold boss in OSRS. Every kill drops 130K+ in guaranteed loot. No RNG dependency — just show up and print GP.

🐍 Zulrah（祖拉） A ⬆️ BUFFED

💰 2.5-3.5M GP/hr | 🔧 Regicide, 80+ Magic/Ranged

Sanguinesti Staff buff in June 2026 makes Zulrah faster than ever. Scales (~250 each, drops 100-300 per kill) are the backbone of profit. Serpentine Visage (3.5M) and Tanzanite Fang (3.2M) add solid variance. The rotation-learning curve is real — expect 2-3 hours before you stop dying.

👹 Demonic Gorillas（恶魔猩猩） A

💰 3-4M GP/hr | 🔧 Monkey Madness II, 75+ Combat

Zenyte shards (~13M each, 1/300 drop rate) are the prize. Each gorilla drops 30-50K in alchables per kill even without the shard. Prayer switching is mandatory but not difficult once you learn the 3-attack pattern.

B Tier — 1-3M GP/hr (Solid Mid-Game Money)（B级——1-3M GP/小时·稳健中期赚钱）

🏰 Hallowed Sepulchre (Floor 5)（神圣墓穴·五层） B

💰 1.5-2.5M GP/hr | 🔧 92 Agility (72 for Floor 3, ~800K/hr)

The Ring of Endurance (~40M) from the grand coffin is the big payday. Floor 5 coffins alone drop 200-400K/hr in alchables. Excellent Agility XP on the side — you're training and making money simultaneously.

⚔️ Slayer (85+)（杀戮85+） B

💰 1-2.5M GP/hr (task-dependent) | 🔧 85+ Slayer, 80+ Combat

Nechryaels, Dust Devils (bursting), Gargoyles, and Abyssal Demons are the best GP tasks. Kurask and Wyvern tasks are solid too. The real money comes from boss-variant tasks (Cerberus at 91, Hydra at 95, Kraken at 87).

⛏️ Zalcano（扎尔卡诺） B

💰 1-2M GP/hr | 🔧 Song of the Elves, 70+ Mining/Smithing/RC

Crystal tool seeds (~8M, 1/200 rate) and consistent onyx bolt tip drops. Mass worlds are easier but less profit per person. 4-man teams are the sweet spot for GP/hr. Mining XP (~15K/hr) is a bonus.

🌿 Herb Runs (Passive)（草药种植·被动） B

💰 1.5-2M GP/hr (effective time) | 🔧 38+ Farming

8 patches × 7-9 ranarrs × 6K each = 400-500K per run. Each run takes 5-7 minutes. Do 5 runs per day and you're printing 2M+ for 30 minutes of work. Best money maker that you can do between other activities.

🌊 Tempoross（坦波罗斯） B

💰 800K-1.2M GP/hr | 🔧 35+ Fishing (better at 81+)

Spirit flakes, fish barrel, and tackle box make Tempoross surprisingly profitable. At 81+ Fishing the reward pool includes raw sharks and sea turtles. Plus you're getting 60K+ Fishing XP/hr — dual-purpose.

C Tier — 500K-1M GP/hr (Low-Mid Accounts)（C级——500K-1M GP/小时·中低级账号）

💀 Barrows（巴罗斯兄弟） C

💰 600K-1M GP/hr | 🔧 43 Prayer, Iban's Staff, 60+ Magic

Ahrim's top (~1.8M) and Karil's top (~1.5M) are the main money drops. With Morytania Hard Diary (+50% runes), each chest gives 30-50K in runes alone. Perfect first boss for accounts at 60-75 combat. Low risk, easy mechanics.

🔥 Blast Furnace（高炉冶炼） C

💰 600-900K GP/hr | 🔧 30+ Smithing (60+ for best bars)

Making mithril/addy/rune bars at Blast Furnace is the most consistent skilling money. Rune bars peak at ~900K/hr at 85 Smithing. Mithril bars (~600K/hr) are accessible from level 50. Requires Coal Bag for efficiency.

🐉 Green Dragons (Myths' Guild)（绿龙·神话公会） C

💰 500-800K GP/hr | 🔧 Dragon Slayer II, 70+ Melee

Dragon bones (~3.5K each) and green dragonhide (~1.5K each) add up fast. Myth's Guild basement has no PKers. 70-80 kills per hour = consistent 500K+ with zero risk.

🗡️ Gargoyles (75 Slayer)（石像怪·75杀戮） C

💰 400-700K GP/hr | 🔧 75 Slayer, 70+ Combat

Granite mauls (~750K, 1/256) and consistent alchables (rune full helms, rune 2hs). Auto-smash unlocked via Slayer points makes it fully AFK. Not exciting but dead-reliable for mid-game accounts.

D Tier — 200K-500K GP/hr (Starting Out)（D级——200K-500K GP/小时·新手起步）

🐮 Cowhide Tanning（牛皮鞣制） D

💰 300-500K GP/hr | 🔧 No requirements

Buy cowhides at 100 gp, tan to hard leather (tan cost 3 gp + tan price), sell for 250 gp. Margin is small but you can do this from level 3 with zero stats. Good for a first 500K.

🪙 High Alchemy (Profitable Items)（高变·盈利物品） D

💰 200-400K GP/hr | 🔧 55 Magic

Certain items (rune arrows, adamant platebodies, dragonhide bodies) alch for more than their GE price. Check OSRS Wiki's alch profit calculator daily. 100% AFK — do it while watching something.

🎣 Fishing (Lobsters / Swordfish)（钓鱼·龙虾/旗鱼） D

💰 100-200K GP/hr | 🔧 40+ Fishing

Lobsters at 40, Swordfish at 50. Pure AFK money while training Fishing. Not a money maker you'd dedicate time to — more of a "I'm already training anyway" bonus.

Meta Shifts — June 2026 Changes That Affected Rankings（2026年6月元变化及其影响）

📊 What Changed and Why（变化内容及原因）

  • Corrupted Gauntlet ⬆️ C-Tier → A-Tier: 27% faster kills = 27% more drops per hour. Crystal Crown buff makes regular completions more profitable even without Enhanced seed.
  • Zulrah ⬆️ B-Tier → A-Tier: Sanguinesti Staff buff = ~15% more DPS on serpentine phase = faster kills = more GP/hr. Not S-tier but solid A now.
  • ToB ⬆️ Top of S-Tier: Sanguinesti buff also helps ToB runs. Scythe value remains sky-high.
  • Phantom Muspah stayed A-Tier: No direct changes but Ancient Essence price rose after Summer Sweep-Up introduced new essence sinks.

FAQ — Money Making Tier List（赚钱等级排行常见问题）

Q: Why isn't Nightmare on this list?（为什么噩梦中没有上榜？）

A: Nightmare is ~3M GP/hr at best and requires a team. The uniques (Inquisitor's, Harmonised Orb) are rare enough that most players go dry for 100+ hours. Phantom Muspah is better money with less RNG dependency.

Q: What's the best AFK money maker?（最佳AFK赚钱方法是什么？）

A: Gargoyles (75 Slayer) with auto-smash is 400-700K GP/hr fully AFK. For lower requirements, Sacred Eels (87 Fishing) gives ~400K/hr and requires clicking once every 2-3 minutes.

Q: I have 50M GP. Should I buy gear or flip?（我有5000万GP，应该买装备还是倒卖？）

A: Buy gear. Good gear (Dragon Hunter Crossbow, Fang, Bandos) lets you do A-tier methods that earn 3-5M/hr. Flipping with 50M maxes out at ~1-2M/hr and requires active monitoring.

Q: Is Corrupted Gauntlet really better than Vorkath now?（腐化手套真的比沃卡斯更强了吗？）

A: For mid-game accounts (80-95 Combat): yes. CG has zero supply cost, zero death cost, and the 27% speed buff puts it ahead of Vorkath in effective GP/hr for that account tier. Once you have Dragon Hunter Crossbow (65M), Vorkath pulls ahead for higher-level accounts.
