How This Tier List Works （等级列表说明）

Methods are ranked by effective GP/hr — that's raw profit minus supply costs, factoring in time spent banking, running, and dying. Tiers reflect the average player's experience, not theoretical max rates. （方法按有效GP/小时排名——扣除补给成本、跑路和死亡时间后的净收益。等级反映普通玩家的实际体验，不是理论极限值。）

S Tier — 8M+ GP/hr (End-Game Dominance) （S级 — 终局统治）

🥇 Theatre of Blood (Hard Mode) S

💰 10-15M GP/hr (efficient team) | 🔧 95+ Combat, ToB experience

The undisputed king. Scythe of Vitur (~800M) alone makes ToB worth running. With the Sanguinesti Staff buff in June 2026, magic DPS is even stronger for Nylocas room and Verzik P2. （无可争议的王。单Scythe of Vitur（~8亿）就让ToB值得打。2026年6月Sanguinesti Staff增强后，Nylocas房间和Verzik P2的魔法DPS更强。）

🥈 Tombs of Amascut (Expert 300+) S

💰 8-12M GP/hr (solo expert) | 🔧 90+ Combat, raid knowledge

ToA at Expert 300+ invocation is the best solo money maker in OSRS. Tumeken's Shadow (~1.5B) and Osmumten's Fang (~28M) are consistent big-ticket drops. No team to split with. （ToA Expert 300+是OSRS最佳单人赚钱方式。Tumeken's Shadow（~15亿）和Osmumten's Fang（~2800万）是稳定的大额掉落。无需和团队分成。）

🥉 Chambers of Xeric (Challenge Mode) S

💰 7-10M GP/hr (CM teams) | 🔧 90+ Combat, CoX experience

Twisted Bow (~1.5B) and Ancestral pieces drive CoX profits. Challenge Mode bumps the unique rate noticeably. （Twisted Bow（~15亿）和Ancestral装备驱动CoX利润。Challenge Mode明显提升稀有掉落率。）

A Tier — 3-8M GP/hr (Your Daily Money Printer) （A级 — 你的日常印钞机）

🔥 Corrupted Gauntlet A ⬆️ NEWLY BUFFED （新增强）

💰 4.5-7M GP/hr | 🔧 80+ Combat, Song of the Elves | ⚡ ZERO supply cost

June 2026 buff made CG 27% faster. The best part? You spend zero GP on supplies. Every single crystal, alchable, and armor seed is pure profit. For accounts at 80-95 combat, this is now the single best money maker. （2026年6月增强使CG快了27%。最棒的是零补给成本。每个水晶、可炼金物品、装甲种子都是纯利润。80-95战斗等级的账号，这是最佳单一赚钱方式。）

💀 Phantom Muspah A

💰 3.5-5M GP/hr | 🔧 85+ Ranged, 70+ Agility

Ancient Essence (~1.5K each, drops 100+ per kill) and Venator Bow shards make Muspah consistently profitable. （Ancient Essence（每个~1.5K，每杀掉落100+）和Venator Bow碎片让Muspah持续赚钱。）

🐉 Vorkath (Dragon Hunter Crossbow) A

💰 3-4M GP/hr | 🔧 Dragon Slayer II, 85+ Ranged

With Dragon Hunter Crossbow (~65M), Vorkath is the most reliable consistent-gold boss. Every kill drops 130K+ in guaranteed loot. No RNG dependency. （有Dragon Hunter Crossbow（~6500万）后，Vorkath是最可靠的稳定产金Boss。每杀至少掉落13万+保证收益。不依赖RNG。）

🐍 Zulrah A ⬆️ BUFFED （增强）

💰 2.5-3.5M GP/hr | 🔧 Regicide, 80+ Magic/Ranged

Sanguinesti Staff buff makes Zulrah faster than ever. Scales (~250 each, 100-300 per kill) are the backbone. Serpentine Visage (3.5M) and Tanzanite Fang (3.2M) add solid variance. （Sanguinesti Staff增强让Zulrah比以前更快。Scales（每个~250，每杀100-300个）是收益支柱。Serpentine Visage（350万）和Tanzanite Fang（320万）增加收益波动。）

👹 Demonic Gorillas A

💰 3-4M GP/hr | 🔧 Monkey Madness II, 75+ Combat

Zenyte shards (~13M each, 1/300) are the prize. Each gorilla drops 30-50K in alchables per kill even without the shard. （Zenyte碎片（每个~1300万，1/300掉落率）是大奖。即使不掉碎片每个猩猩也有3-5万的可炼金物品。）

B Tier — 1-3M GP/hr (Solid Mid-Game Money) （B级 — 稳健中期收入）

🏰 Hallowed Sepulchre (Floor 5) B

💰 1.5-2.5M GP/hr | 🔧 92 Agility (72 for Floor 3, ~800K/hr)

Ring of Endurance (~40M) is the big payday. Excellent Agility XP on the side — training and making money simultaneously. （Ring of Endurance（~4000万）是大奖。同时获得大量Agility经验——边训练边赚钱。）

⚔️ Slayer (85+) B

💰 1-2.5M GP/hr (task-dependent) | 🔧 85+ Slayer, 80+ Combat

Nechryaels, Dust Devils (bursting), Gargoyles, and Abyssal Demons are the best GP tasks. （Nechryaels、Dust Devils（AOE）、Gargoyles和Abyssal Demons是最佳打金任务。）

🌿 Herb Runs (Passive) B

💰 1.5-2M GP/hr (effective time) | 🔧 38+ Farming

8 patches × 7-9 ranarrs × 6K each = 400-500K per run. Each run takes 5-7 minutes. Best money maker you can do between other activities. （8块地×7-9颗ranarr×每个6K=每次40-50万。每次只需5-7分钟。可以在其他活动间隙做的最佳赚钱方式。）

⛏️ Zalcano B

💰 1-2M GP/hr | 🔧 Song of the Elves, 70+ Mining/Smithing/RC

Crystal tool seeds (~8M, 1/200) and consistent onyx bolt tip drops. 4-man teams are the sweet spot. （Crystal tool seeds（~800万，1/200率）和稳定的onyx bolt tip掉落。4人队是最佳配置。）

C Tier — 500K-1M GP/hr (Low-Mid Accounts) （C级 — 低中等级账号）

💀 Barrows C

💰 600K-1M GP/hr | 🔧 43 Prayer, Iban's Staff, 60+ Magic

Perfect first boss for accounts at 60-75 combat. With Morytania Hard Diary, each chest gives 30-50K in runes alone. （60-75战斗等级的最佳首个Boss。完成Morytania Hard Diary后每个箱子仅符文就值3-5万。）

🔥 Blast Furnace C

💰 600-900K GP/hr | 🔧 30+ Smithing (60+ for best bars)

Most consistent skilling money. Rune bars peak at ~900K/hr at 85 Smithing. （最稳定的技能赚钱方式。85 Smithing时Rune bars可达~90万/小时。）

🐉 Green Dragons (Myths' Guild) C

💰 500-800K GP/hr | 🔧 Dragon Slayer II, 70+ Melee

Myth's Guild basement has no PKers. 70-80 kills per hour = consistent 500K+ with zero risk. （Myth's Guild地下没有PK玩家。每小时70-80杀=稳定50万+零风险。）

🗡️ Gargoyles (75 Slayer) C

💰 400-700K GP/hr | 🔧 75 Slayer, 70+ Combat

Granite mauls (~750K, 1/256) and consistent alchables. Auto-smash makes it fully AFK. （Granite mauls（~75万，1/256）和稳定可炼金物品。自动粉碎让它完全可挂机。）

D Tier — 200K-500K GP/hr (Starting Out) （D级 — 入门阶段）

🐮 Cowhide Tanning D

💰 300-500K GP/hr | 🔧 No requirements

Buy cowhides at 100 gp → tan to hard leather → sell for 250 gp. Doable from level 3 with zero stats. （100 GP买牛皮→鞣制成硬皮革→250 GP卖出。3级零属性就能做。）

🪙 High Alchemy (Profitable Items) D

💰 200-400K GP/hr | 🔧 55 Magic

Check OSRS Wiki's alch profit calculator daily. 100% AFK — do it while watching something. （每天查看OSRS Wiki的炼金利润计算器。100%可挂机——边看东西边做。）

FAQ — Money Making Tier List （常见问题）

Q: What's the best AFK money maker? （最佳挂机赚钱方式？）

A: Gargoyles (75 Slayer) with auto-smash is 400-700K GP/hr fully AFK. （Gargoyles（75 Slayer）+自动粉碎，完全挂机40-70万/小时。）

Q: I have 50M GP. Should I buy gear or flip? （我有5000万GP，买装备还是倒卖？）

A: Buy gear. Good gear (Dragon Hunter Crossbow, Fang, Bandos) lets you do A-tier methods earning 3-5M/hr. （买装备。好装备让你打A级方法赚300-500万/小时。）

Q: Is Corrupted Gauntlet really better than Vorkath now? （CG现在真的比Vorkath好吗？）

A: For mid-game (80-95 Combat): yes. Zero supply cost, zero death cost, 27% speed buff. Once you have DHCB (65M), Vorkath pulls ahead for higher accounts. （对中期账号（80-95战斗等级）是的。零补给成本、零死亡惩罚、27%速度提升。有DHCB（6500万）后高级账号Vorkath占优。）
