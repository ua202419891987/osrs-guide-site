🛡️ Ironman Guide — Updated July 2026

F2P Ironman mode is OSRS on hard mode — no Grand Exchange, no trading, no picking up other players' drops. Every GP must be earned through NPC shops or High Alchemy. This guide covers the most efficient early-game money making methods, from cowhide processing to gem jewelry alching.

1. The F2P Ironman Challenge（F2P铁人模式挑战）

As an F2P Ironman, you cannot buy or sell items on the Grand Exchange. Every gold piece enters your pouch through one of two channels: selling items to NPC shops (at heavily discounted rates) or using High Alchemy (at fixed alch values). This fundamentally changes how you approach money making — you must prioritize items with high alch values and process raw materials yourself.

The key difference between a regular F2P player and an F2P Ironman is value-added processing. A regular player can sell raw cowhides on the GE for profit. An Ironman must tan the hides, craft them into items, and then alch those items — each processing step multiplies the final GP value. Understanding this chain is the secret to Ironman wealth.

2. Cowhide Processing — The Best Starting Method（牛皮加工 — 最佳起步方法）

Cowhides are the most accessible resource for a brand-new F2P Ironman. Cows are abundant near Lumbridge, drop hides 100% of the time, and can be processed into items with decent alch values.

🐄 Optimal Route (Lumbridge Cows)（最优路线——洛布里奇奶牛）: Kill cows north-east of Lumbridge castle（击杀洛布里奇城堡东北方向的奶牛）. The Lumbridge bank chest is a 15-second run away（银行箱15秒路程）. Bank cowhides until you have 100+（囤积100+张牛皮）. Tan them in Al Kharid (1 GP per hide)（在阿尔卡里德鞣制，每张1金币）. Craft into hard leather bodies at level 28+（28级制造后制作硬皮甲）. Each body alchs for 78 GP — with 100 hides, that's 7,800 GP（每件高抛78金币，100张=7800金币）. The hides themselves cost 0 GP to obtain, only 100 GP to tan（牛皮获取0成本，仅需100金币鞣制）.

6. High Alchemy — The Ironman Endgame（高抛 — 铁人模式的终极赚钱法）

High Level Alchemy (55 Magic) is the single most important unlock for any F2P Ironman. It converts crafted items into GP at fixed rates, eliminating the need for NPC shops entirely. Here's the priority order for reaching 55 Magic:

  1. Train to 13 Magic（训练到13魔法） — Fire Strike on chickens/cows. Collect feathers and cowhides while training（对鸡/奶牛使用火焰打击，顺便收集羽毛和牛皮）.
  2. 13–37 Magic（13-37魔法） — Fire Strike on Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon). Collect Big Bones for Prayer and limpwurt roots for secondary income（对山丘巨人使用火焰打击，收集大骨头和连翘根作为副业收入）.
  3. 37–55 Magic（37-55魔法） — Fire Bolt or Crumble Undead. Crumble Undead on skeletons is efficient and safe（火焰箭或碎裂亡灵，对骷髅使用碎裂亡灵高效安全）.
  4. 55 Magic achieved（达到55魔法） — Now every crafted item is pure GP. Hard leather bodies give ~70-85 GP each. Emerald amulets give ~1,080-1,200 GP each（现在每个制造物品都是纯金币——硬皮甲每个约70-85金币，翡翠护身符每个约1080-1200金币）.
🔮 Nature Rune Strategy（自然符文策略）: Before 44 Runecrafting（在44级符文制作之前）, kill Dark Wizards south of Varrock for nature rune drops（击杀沃洛克南部的暗黑巫师获取自然符文）. They drop 2–4 nature runes per kill and are easily safe-spotted（每次掉落2-4个，可安全卡位）. Stockpile 500+ nature runes before you hit 55 Magic so you can immediately start alching your crafted items（在55魔法前囤积500+个自然符文，以便立即开始高抛制造的物品）.

7. Advanced Tips for F2P Ironmen（F2P铁人进阶技巧）

  • Always process raw materials before selling/alching（出售/高抛前务必加工原材料）. A raw cowhide sells for 1 GP to an NPC. A hard leather body alchs for 78 GP. That's a 78x multiplier from processing（生牛皮卖给NPC只得1金币，硬皮甲高抛78金币——78倍增值）.
  • Get 28 Crafting as your first major milestone（将28制造作为第一个主要里程碑）. Hard leather bodies are your bread-and-butter alch item. The XP from making them also pushes you toward 40 Crafting for gems（硬皮甲是你的主要高抛物品，制造经验也助你迈向40级制作宝石珠宝）.
  • Mine gold ore at the Crafting Guild（在制造公会开采金矿）. You need 40 Crafting to enter, but once inside, the gold rocks are close to the Falador bank. This is the fastest gold ore source for F2P（需要40制造进入，金矿脉靠近法洛德银行，是F2P最快的金矿来源）.
  • Don't underestimate bones（不要低估骨头）. Burying bones while killing cows gives Prayer XP. 43 Prayer (Protect from Melee) is a massive combat upgrade that you'll want before doing any serious PvM（边杀牛边埋骨头获得祷告经验，43祷告是重要战斗升级）.
  • Save all gems（保存所有宝石）. Sapphires and emeralds from random events and mining should be hoarded, never sold to NPCs. Once you can cut them, they multiply your alch values by 5x–10x over plain gold jewelry（蓝宝石和翡翠应囤积而非卖给NPC，切割后高抛价值是普通金珠宝的5-10倍）.

8. Common Mistakes（常见错误）

  1. Selling raw materials to general stores（将原材料卖给杂货店）. A cowhide sells for 1 GP. A hard leather body alchs for 78 GP. Every raw material you sell instead of processing is GP thrown away（生牛皮卖1金币，硬皮甲高抛78金币——每卖一次原材料就是浪费金币）.
  2. Skipping Rune Mysteries（跳过符文之谜任务）. Runecrafting is a slow skill, but being able to craft your own runes means you never run out of elemental runes for Magic training（符文制作虽慢，但能自制符文意味着魔法训练永不缺元素符文）.
  3. Ignoring Crafting until late game（拖延制造到后期）. Crafting is THE Ironman money skill. Every level from 1–40 directly increases your GP/hour. Get it done early（制造是铁人赚钱技能，每级都直接提升时薪，尽早完成）.
  4. Not banking bones（不存骨头）. Prayer is expensive to train. Every bone you leave on the ground is lost XP. Bury them — 43 Prayer is essential（祷告训练昂贵，地上的每根骨头都是浪费的经验，43祷告至关重要）.
  5. Using High Alchemy on low-value items（对低价值物品使用高抛）. Each nature rune costs you time to obtain. Alch items worth 200+ GP minimum to make the nature rune investment worthwhile（每个自然符文都需要时间获取，至少高抛价值200+金币的物品才划算）.

9. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题解答）

Q: What is the best money making method for a new F2P Ironman?（F2P铁人最佳赚钱方法是什么？）

A（答案）: Killing cows for cowhides and processing them into hard leather bodies for high alching is the best early-game method（击杀奶牛获取牛皮，加工成硬皮甲后高抛是最佳前期方法）. Cows are near Lumbridge, cowhides are free, tanning costs 1 GP each, and hard leather bodies alch for ~70-85 GP（奶牛近洛布里奇，牛皮免费，鞣制每张1金币，硬皮甲高抛约70-85金币）. This method requires zero starting GP and generates 20K-30K GP per hour at low levels（无需起步金币，低级时每小时可赚2万-3万金币）.

Q: How do F2P Ironmen get nature runes for High Alchemy?（F2P铁人如何获得高抛用的自然符文？）

A（答案）: F2P Ironmen obtain nature runes through two main methods（F2P铁人通过两种主要方式获取自然符文）: killing Dark Wizards south of Varrock (they drop nature runes commonly)（击杀沃洛克南部的暗黑巫师，常见掉落） or crafting nature runes at the Nature Altar (requires Rune Mysteries and 44 Runecrafting)（在自然祭坛制作，需要符文之谜任务和44级符文制作）. Dark Wizards are the fastest source early on — they're easily safe-spotted and also drop other useful runes（暗黑巫师前期最快——可安全卡位且掉落其他有用符文）.

Q: How do I get gold bars as an F2P Ironman?（F2P铁人如何获得金条？）

A（答案）: Gold ore can be mined at the Crafting Guild (requires 40 Crafting and a brown apron) or in the Al Kharid mine（金矿可在制造公会或阿尔卡里德矿场开采）. Smelt gold ore into bars at the Al Kharid furnace（在阿尔卡里德熔炉冶炼成金条）. Gold bars craft into rings (alch ~100-110 GP), necklaces (alch ~130-140 GP), and amulets (alch ~200-220 GP)（金条可制作戒指、项链和护身符）. Adding gems multiplies these values dramatically — a sapphire amulet alchs for ~850-950 GP（镶嵌宝石可大幅提升价值——蓝宝石护身符高抛约850-950金币）.

Q: What is the best F2P method for training Crafting as an Ironman?（铁人模式下F2P最佳制造训练方法？）

A（答案）: Kill cows for cowhides, tan them in Al Kharid, and craft the leather into the highest-level item you can make（击杀奶牛获得牛皮，在阿尔卡里德鞣制，制作你能制造的最高级物品）. At level 28, hard leather bodies give 35 XP each and are the best XP per hide（28级时硬皮甲每件35经验，是每张皮革经验最高的）. Alternatively, mine clay in the Varrock south-west mine, soften it with water, and craft clay items at a pottery wheel（或者开采粘土，用水软化后在陶轮上制作）. This gives consistent XP and the items alch for decent values (bowls alch for 6 GP, pie dishes for 9 GP)（经验稳定，物品高抛价值也不错，碗6金币，馅饼盘9金币）.

🛡️ The Ironman Grind Is Worth It（铁人的磨练是值得的）

F2P Ironman is slow, methodical, and deeply satisfying（F2P铁人模式缓慢、有条理且极具满足感）. Every GP you earn represents resources you gathered and processed yourself（每一枚金币都代表你亲自收集和加工的资源）. The early game is the hardest part — once you hit 55 Magic and 40 Crafting, your money-making loop closes and GP becomes a renewable resource rather than a constant struggle（前期是最难的——一旦达到55魔法和40制造，赚钱循环就闭环了，金币变成可再生资源而非持续挣扎）.

Embrace the grind（拥抱磨练吧）. That hard leather body you just alched? You made it from scratch（你刚高抛的那件硬皮甲？那是你从头做出来的）. That's the Ironman way（这就是铁人方式）.
