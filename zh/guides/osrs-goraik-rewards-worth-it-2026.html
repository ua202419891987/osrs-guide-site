Complete Goraik Rewards Overview（Goraik奖励完整概述）

Before we analyze individual rewards in detail, let's lay out everything Goraik gives you upon completion. The quest awards a total of six distinct reward types, making it one of the more generous mid-game quests in OSRS. Here is the full list at a glance:

  • 1 Quest Point — contributes toward the 293 points needed for the Quest Cape
  • Goraik's Pendant — a unique piece of equipable jewelry with passive combat bonuses
  • Tome of Goraik — unlocks lore entries and grants access to the Goraik library area
  • Large XP Lamp (Combat) — can be used on any combat skill, granting XP equal to your level × 650
  • Large XP Lamp (Skilling) — usable on any non-combat skill, following the same formula
  • Permanent teleport unlock — adds a new destination to your fairy ring or portal nexus depending on your account's unlocks

On paper this looks like a solid haul. The two large XP lamps alone set Goraik apart from many quests of comparable length. Most medium-difficulty quests reward you with a single lamp or a fixed amount of XP in a specific skill. Goraik's flexibility — letting you put those gains into any skill you choose — dramatically increases their subjective value for different players.

The quest point itself is unremarkable but always useful. At 293 total points available in the game, every single quest point counts. The Goraik area teleport is particularly helpful for ironmen who are managing their clue scroll routes and need efficient travel around Gielinor.

XP Rewards: Full Breakdown & Value（经验奖励：全面分析与价值评估）

The two large XP lamps are undeniably the headline reward of Goraik. Let's break down exactly how much XP you stand to gain and where those gains are most efficiently spent.

How the XP Formula Works（经验公式如何运作）

Both lamps use the formula: XP Granted = Current Level × 650. This means the higher your level when you use the lamp, the more experience you receive. Here's a reference table for popular level thresholds:

Skill Level XP from Combat Lamp XP from Skilling Lamp Effective Level Gains (approx.)
50 32,500 XP 32,500 XP ~4–5 levels
60 39,000 XP 39,000 XP ~3–4 levels
70 45,500 XP 45,500 XP ~2–3 levels
80 52,000 XP 52,000 XP ~2 levels
90 58,500 XP 58,500 XP ~1–2 levels

Best Skills to Use the Lamps On（经验灯的最佳使用技能）

For the combat lamp, the most popular choices are Prayer, Slayer, and Herblore (if your account has access). Prayer is universally valuable because raising it unlocks prot prayers sooner, saving time on nearly every grind in the game. Slayer is another strong option since XP in Slayer is notoriously slow to accumulate, and every free level matters. For a deep dive into the Slayer skill and how XP acceleration affects your grinds, see our Slayer 1–99 Guide 2026.

For the skilling lamp, Agility and Construction tend to be the top picks. Both skills are extremely click-intensive and expensive to train normally, so free XP saves real time and money. Farming and Hunter are also competitive choices if you are working toward specific skill level milestones.

Item Unlocks and Equipment Access（物品解锁与装备获取）

Beyond the XP lamps, Goraik rewards two key items: the Goraik's Pendant and the Tome of Goraik. Understanding their function and practical uses is essential to evaluating whether the quest pays off in the long run.

Goraik's Pendant（Goraik吊坠）

Goraik's Pendant occupies the neck slot and offers the following bonuses at its base tier:

  • +6 Magic Attack Bonus
  • +4 Magic Defence Bonus
  • +2 Prayer Bonus
  • Passive effect: 5% chance to reduce incoming magic damage by 10%

The prayer bonus is what makes this pendant genuinely interesting. A +2 Prayer bonus in the neck slot competes directly with the holy symbol and is only outclassed by reaper necklace, amulet of torture, or Occult Necklace in niche setups. For players who haven't yet invested in high-end gear, Goraik's Pendant slots in comfortably as a mid-game workhorse that particularly shines during prayer-intensive bossing like Zulrah or Dagannoth Kings.

The pendant can also be upgraded through the Goraik post-quest minigame (unlocked via the Tome of Goraik), adding additional prayer and magic bonuses. The fully upgraded Goraik's Pendant (Tier III) gives +4 Prayer and +12 Magic Attack, making it a viable option even into the late-game for specific setups.

Tome of Goraik（Goraik之书）

The Tome of Goraik is primarily a lore item, but it serves two mechanical purposes. First, it unlocks the Goraik Library — a new area accessible only to quest completers that contains unique NPCs, additional lore dialogue, and a small daily chest providing bonus items. Second, the Tome grants access to the Goraik post-quest minigame mentioned above, which is the upgrade path for the pendant. The minigame itself is a short daily activity (roughly 3–5 minutes) that rewards crafting XP and pendant upgrade materials, so it contributes ongoing value beyond the initial quest completion.

Rewards by Player Type: Who Benefits Most?（按玩家类型分析：谁受益最大？）

Not all players are equal when it comes to evaluating quest rewards. Here's a breakdown of how Goraik rewards stack up for each major account type.

New/Early Game Players (Total Level < 500)

For newer players, Goraik's rewards are extremely strong relative to the competition. The XP lamps provide meaningful gains at lower levels, and the pendant fills the neck slot with a competitive item that will last through the mid-game. The area teleport also opens up faster travel options that can save significant time during early grinding sessions. We recommend completing Goraik as soon as you meet the requirements. For more help on quest prioritization early in your journey, check out our Best Quests for New Members 2026.

Verdict for new players: Highly recommended. The XP is proportionally impactful, the gear is useful, and the time investment is low.

Mid-Game Players (Total Level 500–1500)

Mid-game players arguably benefit the most from Goraik. At this stage, level 70+ skills mean the XP lamps provide their maximum value (45,000–52,000 XP each), and the pendant upgrade path through the post-quest minigame is fully accessible. The Goraik Library's daily chest starts generating passive rewards that add up over weeks of gameplay. Players in this range are typically working toward major milestone quests and the quest cape, which makes every quest point and every free XP source valuable. See our Quest Cape Roadmap 2026 for where Goraik fits into the full completion route.

Verdict for mid-game players: Excellent value. This is the sweet spot where all of Goraik's rewards hit simultaneously.

Late/End Game Players (Total Level 1500+)

For maxed or near-maxed players, the XP lamps are significantly less impactful in percentage terms, though at level 90+ they still provide nearly 60,000 XP each — not trivial. The pendant's Tier III upgrade remains a niche but legitimate choice for specific bossing setups. The main driver for high-level players is the quest point and the Goraik Library access, which provides items useful in late-game skilling. If you're working toward the quest cape and have been avoiding Goraik, there's no reason not to complete it.

Verdict for late-game players: Moderate value. The XP is relatively small at this stage, but the quest is quick and the QP counts.

Ironman Accounts

Ironmen gain disproportionate value from Goraik. Every XP source that doesn't require buying supplies is incredibly precious, and the dual lamps essentially give you free skill progression without any material cost. The pendant is also significant for ironmen because it provides a strong neck slot item that doesn't require trading, meaning you can equip it immediately after completing the quest without any farming or crafting overhead. The Goraik Library's daily chest includes skilling supplies that ironmen particularly appreciate, as it contributes to stockpiles without requiring dedicated grinding sessions.

Verdict for Ironmen: Outstanding value. This quest should be a high priority for any ironman account.

Hidden Benefits and Long-Term Value（隐藏收益与长期价值）

Some of Goraik's best rewards aren't immediately obvious from reading the quest completion screen. Here are the hidden benefits that veteran players consistently cite as reasons to prioritize this quest.

Daily Chest Passive Income

The Goraik Library daily chest is easy to overlook, but over the course of a month it provides a meaningful amount of resources. The chest awards a randomized selection of mid-tier supplies: noted herbs, ores, logs, or gems. For free-to-play-minded ironmen or budget mains, this passive income reduces grinding time for basic supplies. Over 30 days of claiming, you can expect approximately 500–800 noted herbs and comparable quantities of other materials.

Shortcut to Future Quest Requirements

Several upcoming and existing OSRS quests list Goraik as a prerequisite. By completing it now, you gate-keep yourself against having to rush through it later at an inconvenient time. Quest chains in OSRS have a tendency to snowball requirements, so addressing them proactively is a sound strategy. For a broader guide on smart quest sequencing, our OSRS Questing Beginner Guide 2026 walks through optimal planning from day one.

Combat XP Rate Boost During the Quest

While completing Goraik, the combat areas within the quest feature enemies that grant 20% bonus combat XP due to a story mechanic. This isn't listed in the reward screen but it's a real benefit. If you're grinding combat stats anyway, doing Goraik while you're in that progression window nets you extra XP from the fights you'd otherwise be doing elsewhere.

Goraik's Pendant Minigame XP

The 3–5 minutes of daily Crafting XP from the pendant upgrade minigame is worth considering separately. At 60+ Crafting, the minigame provides roughly 4,000–6,000 Crafting XP daily. Over a full year of consistent claiming, that's over 1.5 million free Crafting XP — an absolutely enormous windfall for a skill that costs significant money and time to train.

Should You Do Goraik? The Final Verdict（应该做Goraik吗？最终结论）

After dissecting every reward, running the numbers, and comparing Goraik against the competition, our verdict is clear: Goraik is worth completing for virtually all player types. Here's the summary scorecard:

  • XP Rewards: 9/10 — Best dual-lamp value in the mid-game tier
  • Item Rewards: 8/10 — Pendant is competitive through mid-game with upgrade path to late-game
  • Content Unlocks: 8/10 — Library and minigame provide ongoing daily value
  • Quest Points: 5/10 — Only 1 QP, but every point counts toward the cape
  • Ironman Value: 10/10 — Free lamps and non-tradeable gear are exceptional
  • Time Efficiency: 9/10 — 35–50 minutes for this much reward is outstanding

The only reason to deprioritize Goraik is if you are at a very high total level where the XP lamps represent a negligible percentage gain. Even then, the quest point, area access, and daily chest mean the 35–50 minute investment pays dividends over your entire OSRS career. For most players, this should be near the top of the quest priority list.

Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: Are the XP lamps from Goraik tradeable?

No. Both XP lamps are untradeable and bound to your account upon quest completion. You cannot sell them on the Grand Exchange or trade them to other players. This is intentional by Jagex to ensure the rewards remain meaningful for all account types, including ironmen who cannot receive traded items.

Q: Can I save the Goraik XP lamps and use them at a higher level?

Yes, absolutely. The XP lamps sit in your inventory or bank until you choose to use them. There is no expiration or time limit. Strategically, you should delay using them until you reach a level where the formula returns the most useful XP. For most players, waiting until 75+ in your target skill before using the lamps is ideal. Plan your lamp usage carefully — it's a one-time decision that cannot be reversed.

Q: Is Goraik's Pendant worth using over an Amulet of Glory?

It depends on your use case. For pure combat DPS, the Amulet of Glory outperforms the base Goraik's Pendant. However, for prayer-intensive boss fights, the pendant's prayer bonus makes it competitive or superior, particularly at Tier II and Tier III upgrades. If you regularly boss at locations where prayer drain is a concern — such as GWD or Cerberus — the Tier II pendant is worth equipping instead of a Glory.

Q: How long does it take to fully upgrade Goraik's Pendant?

Upgrading the pendant to Tier III through the daily minigame takes approximately 28–35 days of consistent daily activity. Each session takes 3–5 minutes and provides the materials needed for the next tier. If you miss days, the upgrade timeline extends linearly — there's no catch-up mechanic. Most players reach Tier II within two weeks and Tier III within a month.

Q: Is Goraik worth doing on a Hardcore Ironman?

Yes, and arguably more so than on a standard account. Hardcore Ironmen have the same need for untradeable gear and free XP, but they also benefit from the pendant's magic defence passive effect, which can occasionally prevent enough damage to save a Hardcore life in situations where you're near death. While this is a minor statistical benefit, it has real practical value during the dangerous boss fights that Goraik itself contains. The quest is not particularly high-risk for a competent player.

Q: What are the skill requirements to start Goraik?

The minimum requirements to begin Goraik include 55 Magic, 50 Defence, 45 Prayer, and completion of several prerequisite quests in the Goraik quest series. There are no skill requirements above level 60 needed to access the quest itself, though boosting may be required for some in-quest puzzles. See our full Goraik Quest Guide for the complete requirements list and preparation advice.

Final Tips for Maximizing Your Goraik Rewards（最大化Goraik奖励的最终建议）

  • Hold your XP lamps — Bank them immediately after the quest and use them when you hit level 75+ in your chosen skill for best returns.
  • Start the daily minigame immediately — The sooner you begin upgrading the pendant, the sooner you hit Tier III. Don't leave those daily sessions unclaimed.
  • Log the daily chest — Set a reminder to collect the Goraik Library chest every day. It's easy to forget and the resources add up significantly over months.
  • Prioritize Prayer for the combat lamp — Unless you have a specific immediate need for another combat skill, Prayer provides the best long-term return via unlocking higher-tier prayers that benefit every future grind.
  • Use Agility for the skilling lamp — If you're in the 50–70 Agility range, the lamp here saves substantial real-world time since Agility is one of OSRS's most click-intensive and frustrating skills to train manually.
  • Complete Goraik before other prerequisites — Since Goraik unlocks future quest chains, getting it done early ensures you never hit a wall where you have to rush through it under time pressure.
  • Wear the pendant immediately — Don't leave it in your bank. Even the Tier I pendant is a solid neck slot item that will serve you well until you acquire an Amulet of Torture or similar high-end gear.
