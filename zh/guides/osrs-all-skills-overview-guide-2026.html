Old School RuneScape contains 23 distinct skills that form the backbone of gameplay. Whether you're interested in combat, crafting, collecting resources, or earning gold, understanding each skill's purpose and progression is essential for new players. This comprehensive guide provides an overview of all OSRS skills, their categories, training methods, and practical applications in 2026. We'll help you understand which skills matter most for your playstyle and provide a recommended progression path to maximize your early-game efficiency.

Understanding Skill Categories（技能分类理解）

The Three Pillar System（三大支柱体系）

OSRS skills fall into three primary categories that determine their primary use and training methods. Understanding this categorization helps you plan your progression efficiently and choose skills that align with your gameplay goals.

Combat Skills (11 total)（战斗技能 · 共11项）

Combat skills form the foundation of PvE and PvP gameplay. These include Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Prayer, Magic, and Ranged. Combat skills determine your effectiveness in dungeons, bossing, and wilderness activities. They are essential for every player and should be prioritized early in your progression.

Production Skills (9 total)（生产技能 · 共9项）

Production skills allow you to create items from raw materials. These include Cooking, Crafting, Herblore, Smithing, Fletching, Construction, and Runecrafting. Many production skills generate significant profit and are required for elite quests. They're excellent for gold-making and gear preparation.

Gathering & Support Skills (3 total)（采集与辅助技能 · 共3项）

These skills include Mining, Woodcutting, Fishing, Agility, and Thieving. Gathering skills provide raw materials for production skills or direct profit. Support skills enhance your overall efficiency and unlock new content.

Combat Skills Overview（战斗技能概览）

Attack Skill（攻击技能）

Attack determines your accuracy and ability to use melee weapons. Training attack is essential for all melee-based combat builds. Early training focuses on basic weapons like training dummies and goblins. Attack pairs with Strength for maximum DPS output. Reaching 40 Attack allows access to mid-tier equipment like steel and mithril weapons. Recommended progression: 1-40 at goblins or training dummies, 40-99 through slayer tasks or combat bosses.

Strength Skill（力量技能）

Strength increases your melee damage output. Combined with Attack, it forms the core of melee combat effectiveness. Strength training follows similar progression to Attack, utilizing the same monster encounters. Higher Strength levels significantly increase your DPS potential and reduce kill times. Reaching 60+ Strength opens access to dragon weapons and significantly improves your combat effectiveness in mid-level content.

Defence Skill（防御技能）

Defence reduces damage taken during combat and determines what armour you can wear. This defensive skill is crucial for survival in dangerous content. Training Defence alongside Attack creates a balanced combat character. Defence progression unlocks increasingly better armour: 40 Defence allows rune equipment, 70 Defence unlocks barrows gear and dragon armour. Recommended: prioritize Defence to 40+ early, then continue training through slayer.

Hitpoints (HP) Skill（生命值技能）

Hitpoints represent your health pool during combat. Higher HP provides more margin for error and allows longer bossing sessions without banking. HP trains passively alongside combat skills but can be trained actively through combat damage. At level 99, your maximum HP pool is 1,200 health points. Higher HP is critical for dangerous content like bosses and wilderness activities.

Ranged Skill（远程技能）

Ranged enables distance combat using bows, crossbows, and dart weapons. Ranged offers excellent DPS potential and tactical advantages in many PvE encounters. Training Ranged typically occurs at moss giants, experiments, or through slayer. Learn our complete ranged training guide here. Ranged scales excellently into late-game bossing and is particularly effective against flying enemies.

Magic Skill（魔法技能）

Magic enables spellcasting for damage, utility, and teleportation. Magic is essential for many quests, bossing encounters, and travel efficiency. Check our Magic training guide for efficient progression. Early Magic training focuses on Lumbridge Swamp spells or combat spells. Mid-game Magic unlocks powerful offensive and utility spells. Late-game Magic enables high-damage combat interactions and luxury teleportation spells.

Prayer Skill（祈祷技能）

Prayer unlocks powerful buffs that enhance combat effectiveness. Prayer bonuses include offensive boosts (piety for melee, eagle eye for ranged, mystic might for magic), defensive protection prayers, and utility prayers. Training Prayer requires burying bones, offering bones at altars, or using ectofuntus method. Prayer is optional but highly recommended for serious PvE content. Level 99 Prayer combined with 99 Attack and Strength creates formidable combat builds.

Slayer Skill（屠杀技能）

Slayer doesn't directly enhance combat but unlocks dangerous monsters that train combat skills while earning profit. Slayer masters assign tasks requiring specific combat levels or gear. Slayer generates excellent long-term profit and provides diverse training. Early slayer tasks train low-level monsters for basic experience. Mid-game slayer unlocks superior monsters with valuable drops. Late-game slayer (80+) provides exceptional profit through monsters like Abyssal Demons and Spiritual Creatures.

Production & Crafting Skills（生产与制作技能）

Cooking Skill（烹饪技能）

Cooking transforms raw food into cooked food for healing during combat. Cooking is one of the quickest skills to train and serves a critical purpose in PvE. Training Cooking ranges from 1-99 in approximately 4-8 hours depending on your methods. Early cooking focuses on shrimp and trout. Mid-game cooking trains on better fish like lobsters. Late-game cooking achieves almost zero burn rate on high-tier fish like dark crabs.

Fishing Skill（钓鱼技能）

Fishing provides raw fish for cooking and generates decent profit in early game. Fishing pairs well with Agility for efficient leveling. Different fishing spots yield different fish: fly fishing for trout, net fishing for anchovies, harpoon fishing for tuna. Higher Fishing levels increase catch rates and unlock more profitable fish. Fishing to 99 generates roughly 800k-1.2m gp through fish sales.

Woodcutting Skill（伐木技能）

Woodcutting provides logs for Firemaking and Construction. Early Woodcutting trains on willows near water for AFK progression. Willow logs (level 30) yield decent profit. Higher-level Woodcutting (level 60+) unlocks yew logs with better profit margins. Woodcutting to 99 is extremely AFK-friendly, making it popular for casual players. Late-game Woodcutting earns 800k-1.5m gp through log sales.

Mining Skill（采矿技能）

Mining provides ore for Smithing and direct profit through ore sales. Early Mining focuses on copper and tin. Mid-game Mining (levels 30-70) trains on iron, coal, and mithril. Late-game Mining (70-99) focuses on runite ore or granite for maximum profit. Mining 99 generates excellent profit at runite rocks: 1.2m-1.8m gp/hour with 99 Mining and 70+ Strength.

Smithing Skill（锻造技能）

Smithing converts ore into bars and bars into equipment. Smithing is essential for creating your own gear or contributing to group activities. Early Smithing focuses on bronze items for cheap leveling. Mid-game Smithing trains on steel and mithril bars. Late-game Smithing (levels 80+) focuses on high-tier equipment like dragon equipment through blast furnace method.

Crafting Skill（制作技能）

Crafting creates leather armor, jewelry, and other equipment. Crafting is an extremely profitable skill at high levels. Early Crafting focuses on leather items. Mid-game Crafting trains on silver jewelry for profit. Late-game Crafting (90+) focuses on onyx items for exceptional profit: crafting onyx jewelry generates 2m+ gp/hour at 99 Crafting.

Herblore Skill（草药学技能）

Herblore creates potions for combat buffs and utility. Herblore requires cleaning herbs and mixing them with potions. Early Herblore is expensive but necessary for combat efficiency. Mid-game Herblore focuses on creating attack potions and strength potions. Late-game Herblore creates elite potions: divine bastion potions, divine super combat potions. Creating potions at 99 Herblore generates 1.5m-2m gp/hour.

Fletching Skill（制箭技能）

Fletching creates arrows, bolts, and bows from wood and feathers. Fletching is an AFK-friendly, profitable skill. Early Fletching focuses on shortbows. Mid-game Fletching trains on longbows for better profit. Late-game Fletching (80+) focuses on crossbow bolts for exceptional AFK profit: 1.2m-1.8m gp/hour at 99 Fletching.

Construction Skill（建筑技能）

Construction allows building a player-owned house with useful features. Construction is expensive to train but provides permanent benefits. Early Construction is trained through low-cost benches. Mid-game Construction requires significant investment. Late-game Construction (70+) unlocks necessary features like altars for prayer restoration and portals for teleportation access.

Runecrafting Skill（符文制作技能）

Runecrafting creates runes for magic spells. Runecrafting is infamous for being tedious and slow but provides substantial profit at high levels. Early Runecrafting focuses on air runes. Mid-game Runecrafting trains on fire runes and body runes. Late-game Runecrafting (77+) focuses on blood runes for exceptional profit: 2m-2.5m gp/hour.

Support & Utility Skills（辅助与实用技能）

Agility Skill（敏捷技能）

Agility improves your mobility through rooftop courses and shortcuts. Agility improves your movement speed and unlocks shortcuts across Gielinor. Training Agility requires running courses and failing attempts. Agility is considered tedious but essential for efficiency in PvE. Higher Agility levels unlock shortcuts that save significant travel time. Agility 60+ is highly recommended for quality-of-life improvements.

Thieving Skill（偷窃技能）

Thieving allows stealing items from NPCs and stalls. Thieving provides early-game profit and unlocks unique content. Early Thieving focuses on stalls and basic NPCs. Mid-game Thieving trains on better stalls and chests. Late-game Thieving (80+) focuses on master farmers and chest thieving for excellent profit. Pyramid Plunder provides passive Agility while training Thieving.

Firemaking Skill（生火技能）

Firemaking burns logs for minimal experience and fire. Firemaking is one of the least essential skills but required for certain rituals and quests. Most players train Firemaking passively while training Woodcutting. Late-game Firemaking creates sacred fires for small buffs during activities.

Recommended Skill Progression for Beginners（新手推荐技能升级路线）

Priority 1: Early Combat (Levels 1-40)（优先级1：早期战斗 · 1-40级）

Your first priority should be reaching combat proficiency for basic content. Target 40 Attack, 40 Strength, and 40 Defence simultaneously through combat training. This provides access to steel equipment and basic slayer tasks. Simultaneously train Cooking to 30-40 for food production. Early progression typically takes 8-12 hours of active play.

Priority 2: Mid-Level Combat (Levels 40-70)（优先级2：中级战斗 · 40-70级）

Progress your main combat stats to 70+ through slayer tasks. Slayer simultaneously trains combat and generates profit. Reach 70 Attack, 70 Strength, and 70 Defence for access to barrows equipment and profitable slayer tasks. This phase provides steady income and combat skill development.

Priority 3: Essential Support Skills (Concurrent)（优先级3：关键辅助技能 · 同步进行）

While training combat, develop essential support skills: Fishing to 40+ for food supply, Cooking to 90+ for high-quality healing food, and Agility to 60+ for travel efficiency. These skills provide quality-of-life improvements and support your combat training.

Priority 4: Money-Making Skills (Post-70 Combat)（优先级4：赚钱技能 · 战斗70+后）

Once you reach combat proficiency, develop profitable skills aligned with your goals. If you prefer active gameplay, train Herblore to 70+ and Crafting to 70+ for profit. If you prefer AFK gameplay, train Woodcutting and Fishing to 99. High-level production skills generate 1-2m+ gp per hour.

Priority 5: Late-Game Specialization (99 Goals)（优先级5：后期专精 · 99目标）

Train your 99s based on personal preference: either combat skills for dangerous bossing, production skills for wealth generation, or support skills for completionist goals. Each 99 represents significant time investment but unlocks unique content and maximizes specific activities.

Real-World Skill Applications & Gold-Making Potential（实际技能应用与赚钱潜力）

Combat-Focused Progression（战斗导向发展路线）

If you prioritize combat skills, focus on Training Attack, Strength, Defence, and Prayer to 99. This progression enables access to high-level bosses like Zulrah (requires 78 Magic, 50 Ranged, 40 Defence minimum), Barrows (requires 40+ stats minimum), and Slayer bosses. Combat-focused players generate profit through bossing: 2-5m+ gp/hour at high-level bosses with proper gear.

Production-Focused Progression（生产导向发展路线）

If you prefer wealth accumulation, prioritize production skills: Crafting, Herblore, and Runecrafting. High-level production generates excellent profit: Crafting onyx jewelry at 99 generates 2m+ gp/hour, Herblore creates elite potions worth 2m+ gp/hour, Runecrafting blood runes generates 2-2.5m gp/hour. Production players require initial investment but generate passive wealth.

Hybrid Progression（混合发展路线）

Most successful players use hybrid approaches, training combat skills through Slayer while simultaneously developing production skills. This balanced approach generates steady profit (500k-1.5m gp/hour) while improving combat effectiveness. Hybrid players achieve financial independence while maintaining combat capability for group activities.

Gathering-Focused Progression（采集导向发展路线）

Gathering-focused players prioritize Mining and Woodcutting for AFK profit. While gathering typically generates less profit per hour than production or combat, it requires minimal attention. AFK Mining of runite ore generates 1.2-1.8m gp/hour. Many players combine gathering with secondary activities for optimal efficiency.

Quest & Achievement Progression（任务与成就发展路线）

Some players focus on quest requirements and achievement diaries. This progression requires varied skills and significant content completion. Quest-focused players gain access to unique items, unlocked content, and unique bragging rights. This approach typically generates moderate profit while enabling unique playstyles.

Smart Profit Rotation Strategy（聪明赚钱轮换策略）

Instead of grinding a single money-making skill, rotate between 3 profitable skills to prevent burnout while maintaining consistent GP per hour. A popular rotation among experienced players follows this weekly pattern:

  • Days 1-2: Runecrafting — Generate 2-2.5m GP/hour through blood rune crafting at the Blood Altar. This method requires 77 Runecrafting and completion of Darkness of Hallowvale, but the profit potential is unmatched among gathering skills.
  • Days 3-4: Combat Bossing — Target bosses like Vorkath (2-3m GP/hour) or Zulrah (2-4m GP/hour) for combat-based profit. These methods simultaneously train combat stats and slayer tasks.
  • Days 5-6: Herblore or Crafting — Process raw materials into finished products for 1.5-2m GP/hour. Herblore potion decanting and Crafting battlestaff creation are semi-AFK alternatives.
  • Day 7: Rest and Utility Skills — Train Agility, Thieving, or other quality-of-life skills that reduce frustration in future grinding sessions.

This rotation strategy prevents the burnout that kills most skilling goals while maintaining an average of 1.5-2m GP/hour across the week. The key insight is that variety sustains motivation better than any single money-making method.

F2P vs P2P Skill Access Comparison（F2P与P2P技能对比）

Free-to-play players have access to 16 skills, while members unlock all 23. The 7 member-exclusive skills — Agility, Construction, Farming, Fletching, Herblore, Slayer, and Thieving — provide critical progression advantages. F2P players should focus on reaching maximum efficiency in available skills before purchasing membership, as the transition from F2P to P2P dramatically accelerates training speeds across all skills. A player who reaches 50+ in all F2P skills before subscribing saves approximately 10-15 hours compared to starting membership immediately, because early P2P methods require capital and foundational stats that F2P training provides efficiently.

Final Tips for Skill Mastery（技能精通终极提示）

  • Use efficient training methods: Always research current meta training methods for your chosen skills. Meta methods change with updates, so staying informed ensures maximum efficiency.
  • Combine AFK and active skills: Balance active grinds (slayer, bossing) with AFK skills (Woodcutting, Fishing) for sustainable progression without burnout.
  • Prioritize early combat: Your first 50 hours should focus on reaching 70+ combat stats for slayer access. This foundation enables all future progression.
  • Don't neglect support skills: Cooking, Agility, and Fishing provide critical quality-of-life improvements. Reaching 70+ in these skills dramatically improves your overall experience.
  • Plan your 99s: Before grinding to 99, consider whether that skill provides meaningful benefits for your playstyle. Some 99s are purely cosmetic, while others unlock significant content.
  • Track your progress: Use online tracking tools to monitor your progress and celebrate milestones. This provides motivation during long grinds.
  • Join a community: OSRS communities provide support, advice, and social connection. Active communities help you learn faster and enjoy the game more.

Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

What's the fastest skill to train to 99?（训练到99最快的技能是什么？）

Cooking is typically the fastest skill to reach 99, achievable in 4-8 hours through high-level fish cooking with minimal failures. Fletching is the second-fastest at approximately 8-10 hours through dedicated AFK arrow-flight creation. These rapid 99s make excellent early achievements that provide confidence for longer grinds.

Which skill should I train first?（应该先训练哪个技能？）

Prioritize combat skills first, targeting 40 Attack, 40 Strength, and 40 Defence to access basic slayer content. Simultaneously reach 40+ Cooking for healing food. This foundation typically requires 8-12 hours but enables all future progression. After securing combat proficiency, develop support skills for quality-of-life improvements.

Are all 23 skills essential?（23个技能都是必需的吗？）

No, all 23 skills are not essential for core OSRS gameplay. Combat skills (Attack, Strength, Defence, Prayer) and Cooking are essential for PvE. Slayer and Production skills provide wealth and progression. Support skills improve efficiency. Many players successfully complete OSRS with only 8-10 skills trained to 99, prioritizing based on personal goals.

How long does it take to reach 99 in each skill?（每个技能到99需要多长时间？）

Training times vary dramatically by skill and method: Cooking requires 4-8 hours, Fletching requires 8-12 hours, Agility requires 40-50 hours, Slayer requires 200+ hours, Prayer requires 100-150 hours through training methods. Plan your 99s based on time availability and whether you prefer active or AFK training.

What's the most profitable skill to train?（训练哪个技能最赚钱？）

Late-game production skills generate exceptional profit: Runecrafting (2-2.5m gp/hour), Crafting (2m+ gp/hour), and Herblore (1.5m-2m gp/hour). Combat-focused bossing generates similar profit at 2-5m+ gp/hour but requires significantly more gear investment and active gameplay. AFK skills like Mining generate 800k-1.2m gp/hour with minimal attention.

Should I train all skills to 99?（需要把所有技能训练到99吗？）

Absolutely not necessary. Most successful players specialize in 8-12 skills aligned with their goals rather than pursuing all 99s. Training all 23 skills to 99 requires 3,000+ hours and provides diminishing returns. Focus on skills that enhance your preferred playstyle and generate meaningful progression.
