① Prerequisites（前置要求）

DT2 requires: 75 Thieving, 70 Agility, 65 Construction, 62 Smithing, 60 Slayer, 60 Hunter, 50 Runecraft, 50 Firemaking, 200 Quest Points, and Desert Treasure I completion. The quest takes 6-8 hours. Recommended stats for boss fights: 90+ Combat, 80+ Ranged, 80+ Magic, 75+ Prayer, and high Defence (70+). Each boss has different gear requirements — you'll need good Melee, Ranged, and Magic setups.

② Duke Sucellus Fight（苏塞卢斯公爵 — 最简单的Boss）

Duke Sucellus is the easiest DT2 boss. Strategy: The Duke has two phases. Phase 1 — attack with Melee while avoiding the green orbs. The Duke attacks slowly, giving you plenty of time to dodge. Phase 2 — The Duke enrages and attacks faster. Use Protect from Melee and continue DPS. Recommended gear: Inquisitor's set or Bandos, Scythe of Vitur or Rapier. Key tip: The green orbs deal 15+ damage and reduce your stats. Always move 2-3 tiles away when you see the orb spawn animation.

③ Vardorvis Fight（瓦多维斯 — 节奏型Boss）

Vardorvis is a rhythm-based boss with a unique "axe throw" mechanic. Strategy: Vardorvis attacks in a pattern: 3 normal melee hits followed by an axe throw. When he throws his axe, pray Protect from Magic (the axe is magic damage). The axe bounces between walls — stand in the open area to avoid it. After the axe returns, switch back to Protect from Melee. Repeat this cycle. Recommended gear: Scythe, Torture, Primordial boots, Avernic defender.

④ Leviathan Fight（利维坦 — 远程Boss）

Leviathan is a Ranged-based boss fought in the underwater temple. Strategy: Leviathan attacks with Ranged and Magic. The key mechanic is the Shield ability — the Leviathan raises a shield that reduces damage. Attack with the opposite style (Magic when Ranged shield, Ranged when Magic shield). Special attack: Leviathan creates a whirlpool — run to the edge of the arena to avoid being pulled in. Recommended gear: Toxic Blowpipe, Armadyl crossbow, and a Magic switch for shield breaking.

⑤ The Whisperer Fight（低语者 — 最难Boss）

The Whisperer is the hardest DT2 boss. Strategy: Three phases: Phase 1 — Attack with Magic (Trident). The Whisperer uses Magic attacks — pray Protect from Magic. Phase 2 — The Whisperer spawns clones. Kill the correct clone (the one with the glowing eye) to remove its shield. Phase 3 — The Whisperer uses a powerful beam attack. Move 3+ tiles to dodge it. Critical tip: During Phase 3, the Whisperer spawns tentacles that heal it. Kill tentacles immediately with Ranged. Learning the clone mechanic in Phase 2 is the key to this fight.

⑥ Rewards（任务奖励与Boss刷金）

Completing DT2 gives 100K Attack XP, 80K Strength XP, 50K Defence XP, 50K Magic XP, 50K Ranged XP, and 4 Quest Points. Post-quest boss farming: Duke Sucellus drops the Soulreaper Axe pieces (500M+), Vardorvis drops the Vardovis' Vestige (unique), Leviathan drops the Chromium Ingot, Whisperer drops the Venator Bow (150M). Each boss farm averages 2-5M GP/hr.

FAQ

Which DT2 boss is best for farming?（哪个Boss最值得刷？）

Duke Sucellus is the easiest and most consistent GP. Whisperer has the highest potential but is hardest to learn.
