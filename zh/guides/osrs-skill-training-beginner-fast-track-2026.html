OSRS Fast Track Skill Guide — Reach Mid-Game Stats in 30 Days (2026)

1. What "Mid-Game Ready" Looks Like（1. "中期就绪"是什么样？（"中期就绪"是什么样？））

Before starting the 30-day plan, you need to know what you're aiming for. "Mid-game ready" means your account can efficiently train, make GP, and attempt mid-level bosses. Here are the target stats:

🎯 Target Stats After 30 Days（🎯 30天后目标数据（30天后目标数据））

SkillTarget LevelWhy This Level
Attack60-70Unlocks Dragon Scimitar (60) and Abyssal Whip (70)
Strength60-70High enough for solid DPS
Defence40-60Dragon armor (40) or Barrows armor (60)
Hitpoints55-70Natural growth from combat training
Ranged50-70Rune Crossbow + Broad bolts (requires Slayer 55)
Magic55-70High Alchemy (55) + combat spells
Prayer43-52Protect Prayers (43) + Smite (52 for PvP)
Slayer1-50Access to better tasks and bosses

GP Bank3-10MEnough to buy gear, supplies, and bonds

These stats unlock: (1) Efficient GP making (200-500K per hour). (2) Access to Barrows and mid-level bosses. (3) Ability to train Slayer efficiently. (4) Enough GP to never worry about "can I afford this?" again.

Why 30 Days is Realistic

If you play 2-3 hours per day, you can reach these stats in 30 days. Here's the math: Combat 60-70 takes ~40-60 hours total. Prayer 43 takes ~5-10 hours. Agility 50-60 takes ~10-15 hours. The rest (Gathering, Artisan) can be done "on the side" or in bursts. 2-3 hours/day × 30 days = 60-90 hours total. This is enough time.

② Prerequisites Before Starting（② 开始前的必要条件（开始前的必要条件））

  • Membership: This plan assumes you have membership (not F2P). F2P version is in Section 6.
  • Playtime: 2-3 hours per day minimum. If you play less, extend the timeline to 60 days.
  • Starting stats: Level 3 account (1 Att/1 Str/1 Def/10 HP). If you're F2P with some stats already, adjust accordingly.
  • GP to start: 0 GP is fine. The plan includes making your first 500K GP in Week 2.

2. Week 1: Combat Foundation (Days 1-7)（2. 第1周：战斗基础（第1周：战斗基础，第1-7天））

Week 1 is all about getting your combat stats to a level where you can kill Hill Giants, do early Slayer tasks, and survive in dangerous areas. Don't worry about GP yet — focus on XP.

📅 Week 1 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第1周每日计划（第1周每日计划，2-3小时/天））

Days 1-2: Train at Chickens (Lumbridge). Get Attack/Strength/Defence to 10. Loot feathers and bones, sell on GE for first GP.

Days 3-4: Train at Cows (Lumbridge swamp). Get stats to 20. Loot cowhides (sell for 100-200 GP each). Bury bones for Prayer XP.

Days 5-7: Train at Al-Kharid Warriors or Moss Giants (Edgeville Dungeon, need Ring of Charos or pass). Get stats to 30-40. Start burying Big Bones for Prayer 20+.

End of Week 1 target: Attack 30, Strength 30, Defence 30, Hitpoints 25, Prayer 20, Ranged 1, Magic 1. GP bank: 50-100K.

③ 🗺️ Exact Training Locations (Week 1)（③ 🗺️ 精确训练地点（精确训练地点，第1周））

Location 1: Chickens (Lumbridge)（地点1：鸡（地点1：鸡，Lumbridge））

Where: Behind the Lumbridge general store (north side).

What to bring: Bronze weapons (sword/axe), no armor (train Defence naturally), food (chicken drops raw chicken which you can cook on the nearby fire).

Attack style: "Controlled" (trains Att+Str+Def simultaneously).

XP per hour: ~5-10K Combat XP per hour (slow, but you're learning the game).

Location 2: Cows (Lumbridge Swamp)（地点2：牛（地点2：牛，Lumbridge Swamp））

Where: Lumbridge Swamp (south of Lumbridge castle).

What to bring: Iron weapons, leather armor (bought from the Grand Exchange), lobsters or trout for food.

XP per hour: ~10-15K Combat XP per hour.

GP per hour: ~20-40K GP (from selling cowhides and bones).

Location 3: Al-Kharid Warriors (F2P) or Moss Giants (Members)（地点3：Al-Kharid战士（地点3：Al-Kharid战士（免费版）或苔藓巨人（会员版）））

Where: Al-Kharid Palace (F2P) OR Edgeville Dungeon → Moss Giants (Members, need Ring of Charos or 10 GP to enter).

What to bring: Steel weapons, steel armor, 10+ lobsters.

XP per hour: ~20-30K Combat XP per hour.

Drops: Big Bones (bury for Prayer XP), herbs (sell on GE), coins.

Week 1 Pro Tip

Don't buy gear from the Grand Exchange yet. Loot everything from monsters and use it. Only buy essentials (lobsters for food, better weapons). Save your GP for Week 2 when you'll need it for membership bond or better gear.

3. Week 2: Gathering & First GP (Days 8-14)（3. 第2周：采集与第一桶金（第2周：采集与第一桶金，第8-14天））

Week 2 is when you start making real GP. You'll train Gathering skills (Mining, Fishing, Woodcutting) and sell the resources on the Grand Exchange. By the end of Week 2, you should have 500K-1M GP — enough to buy better gear and supplies.

📅 Week 2 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第2周每日计划（第2周每日计划，2-3小时/天））

Days 8-10: Mine Iron ores (levels 15-30). Sell ores on GE. Target: 100K+ GP.

Days 11-12: Fish Lobsters (level 40+). Cook some for food, sell the rest. Target: 200K+ total GP.

Days 13-14: Woodcut Willows (level 30+). Sell logs on GE. Also do easy quests for XP rewards. Target: 500K+ total GP.

End of Week 2 target: Mining 30, Fishing 40, Woodcutting 30, GP bank: 500K-1M.

④ 🗺️ Exact Training Locations (Week 2)（④ 🗺️ 精确训练地点（精确训练地点，第2周））

Mining Iron — Best XP + GP（采矿铁矿石 — 最佳经验+金币）

Where: Al-Kharid Mine (F2P) OR Legends' Guild Mine (Members, best).

What to bring: Iron pickaxe (or better), no armor (run energy), food (lobsters).

Method: Mine Iron ores. Bank them (use nearby bank). Sell on GE for ~100 GP per ore.

XP per hour: ~20-30K Mining XP per hour.

GP per hour: ~50-100K GP (depending on GE prices).

Fishing Lobsters — Food + GP（钓鱼龙虾 — 食物+金币）

Where: Catherby ( Members) OR Musa Point (F2P, slower).

What to bring: Lobster pot (bought from fishing shop), coins for pot.

Method: Fish Lobsters. Cook half for food, sell half on GE for ~150 GP each.

XP per hour: ~30-40K Fishing XP per hour.

GP per hour: ~50-100K GP (from selling raw/cooked lobsters).

Woodcutting Willows — AFK XP（伐木柳树 — AFK经验）

Where: Draynor Village (World 36 is Woodcutting world).

What to bring: Steel axe (or better), no armor.

Method: Chop Willow trees. Bank logs. Sell on GE for ~30-50 GP per log.

XP per hour: ~20-30K Woodcutting XP per hour.

AFKability: High. You can AFK for 5-10 minutes at a time.

Week 2 Pro Tip

Always check Grand Exchange prices before selling. Sometimes it's more profitable to sell raw fish vs cooked fish, or logs vs bowstrings. Use the OSRS Wiki's "Grand Exchange" pages to check current prices.

4. Week 3: Artisan & Support Skills (Days 15-21)（4. 第3周：工匠与支持技能（第3周：工匠与支持技能，第15-21天））

Week 3 is when you start training Artisan skills (Cooking, Crafting) and Support skills (Agility, Thieving). These skills make your account more efficient and unlock new capabilities.

📅 Week 3 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第3周每日计划（第3周每日计划，2-3小时/天））

Days 15-17: Cook the fish you caught. Level Cooking to 40+. Use Cooking gauntlets (Hosidius kitchen) to reduce burn rate.

Days 18-19: Craft leather armor from cowhides. Level Crafting to 30+. Also do "Fairytale I" quest (unlocks fairy rings for fast travel).

Days 20-21: Train Agility at Gnome Stronghold (levels 1-30). Also start Thieving (pickpocket Men/Women for GP).

End of Week 3 target: Cooking 40, Crafting 30, Agility 30, Thieving 20, GP bank: 1-2M.

⑤ Cooking Training — Efficient Method（⑤ 烹饪训练 — 高效方法（烹饪训练 — 高效方法））

Levels 1-40: Cook Fish From Fishing（1-40级：烹饪钓鱼获得的鱼）

Method: Use the fish you caught in Week 2. Start with Shrimp, then Trout/Salmon, then Lobsters.

Best location: Hosidius kitchen (Zeah). Has a Cooking gauntlet that reduces burn chance by 5 levels.

XP per hour: ~50-80K Cooking XP per hour (at higher levels with gauntlet).

Cost: Free (you use your own fish). If you buy raw fish, it costs ~10-30K GP per hour.

⑥ Crafting Training — Leather Armor（⑥ 手工艺训练 — 皮甲（手工艺训练 — 皮甲））

Levels 1-30: Leather From Cows（1-30级：用牛皮制作皮革）

Method: Kill cows (Lumbridge swamp) → collect cowhides → tan at Al-Kharid tanner (costs 1 GP per hide) → craft into leather armor.

Alternative: Buy cowhides from GE (~100 GP each), tan them, craft into armor, sell armor on GE for profit.

XP per hour: ~30-50K Crafting XP per hour.

Profitability: Usually slightly profitable or break-even.

⑦ Agility Training — Gnome Stronghold（⑦ 敏捷训练 — Gnome要塞（敏捷训练 — Gnome要塞））

Levels 1-30: Gnome Stronghold Agility Course（1-30级：Gnome要塞敏捷课程）

Where: Tree Gnome Stronghold (north-west of Ardougne).

Method: Complete the obstacle course. Each lap gives ~85 XP. Takes ~2-3 minutes per lap at low levels.

XP per hour: ~8-12K Agility XP per hour (slow, but it's the only F2P-friendly method).

Marks of Grace: None at Gnome course. You need level 30+ to get Marks of Grace from Rooftop courses.

Week 3 Pro Tip

Don't stress about getting high levels in Artisan/Support skills yet. The goal for Week 3 is to get them to "useful" levels (Cooking 40 for lobsters, Crafting 30 for dragonhide, Agility 30 for better courses). You'll max them later when you have more GP and time.

5. Week 4: Polishing & Preparation (Days 22-30)（5. 第4周：打磨与准备（第4周：打磨与准备，第22-30天））

Week 4 is when everything comes together. You'll finish your combat stats to 60+, get Prayer to 43 (Protect Prayers), start Slayer, and prepare for mid-game content.

📅 Week 4 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第4周每日计划（第4周每日计划，2-3小时/天））

Days 22-24: Train combat at Rock Crabs. Get Attack/Strength/Defence to 50-60. Bury bones or use Gilded Altar for Prayer 43.

Days 25-27: Start Slayer with Turael (Burthorpe). Do easy tasks (Cows, Chickens, etc.) to get Slayer XP and learn the system.

Days 28-30: Fill in skill gaps. Train Agility to 50-60 (Al-Kharid Rooftop). Buy Graceful armor. Make final GP push (target: 3-5M GP bank).

End of 30 Days target: Combat 60+, Prayer 43, Slayer 10-20, Agility 50-60, GP bank: 3-10M. You're MID-GAME READY!

⑧ 🗺️ Rock Crabs — Best Combat Training (Levels 50-70)（⑧ 🗺️ 岩石蟹 — 最佳战斗训练（岩石蟹 — 最佳战斗训练，50-70级））

Why Rock Crabs?（为什么选岩石蟹？）

Rock Crabs (and Sand Crabs) have HIGH hitpoints but LOW defence. They also respawn quickly and don't deal much damage. This makes them perfect for AFK combat training.

Where: Hosidius (Kourend) for Rock Crabs. Zeah (south of Shilo Village) for Sand Crabs.

What to bring: Best melee weapon (Steel/Black/Mithril scimitar), leather/dragonhide armor, 10+ lobsters, Prayer potions (if Prayer 43+).

XP per hour: ~40-60K Combat XP per hour (with "Controlled" style).

Time to 60 melee stats: ~10-15 hours of crab training.

⑨ 🙏 Getting Prayer 43 — Protect Prayers（⑨ 🙏 达到祈祷43级 — 保护祈祷（达到祈祷43级 — 保护祈祷））

Method 1: Bury Bones (Free but Slow)（方法1：埋骨（方法1：埋骨，免费但慢））

Collect bones from monsters you kill. Bury them. XP per bone: regular bones (4.5 XP), Big Bones (15 XP), Babydragon bones (30 XP).

Time to 43 Prayer: ~2-4 hours of burying Big Bones.

Method 2: Gilded Altar (Members, Fast)（方法2：镀金祭坛（方法2：镀金祭坛，会员版，快速））

Use a Gilded Altar in someone's Player-Owned House (World 330 is official Gilded Altar world). Offering bones at Gilded Altar gives 350% XP per bone.

Time to 43 Prayer: ~30-60 minutes of offering Big Bones at Gilded Altar.

Cost: Free (if using someone else's house) or ~100-200K GP (if buying bones).

⑩ ⚔️ Starting Slayer — Talk to Turael（⑩ ⚔️ 开始杀戮 — 与Turael交谈（开始杀戮 — 与Turael交谈））

How to Start Slayer（如何开始杀戮）

Talk to Slayer Master Turael in Burthorpe (no skill requirements). He gives easy tasks designed for new players.

First tasks: Cows, Chickens, Rats, Spiders. These are trivial but give you Slayer XP and teach you the Slayer interface.

When to switch to harder tasks: At Combat 50+, switch to Mazchna (Canifis). At Combat 70+, switch to Vannaka (Edgeville Dungeon).

Week 4 Pro Tip

Don't rush to finish everything in Week 4. The goal is to be "mid-game ready" — not maxed. If you need 5-10 more days to reach the target stats, that's fine. The 30-day timeline is a guideline, not a hard deadline.

6. Alternative Schedules (1hr/5hrs + Ironman + F2P)（6. 替代计划（6. 替代计划，1小时/5小时 + 铁人模式 + 免费版））

The 30-day plan assumes 2-3 hours/day. But your playtime might be different. Here are adjusted schedules for different situations:

⏱️ Schedule A: 1 Hour/Day (60-Day Version)（⏱️ 计划A：每天1小时（计划A：每天1小时，60天版））

Total playtime: 1 hour per day × 60 days = 60 hours total.

Adjustments: (1) Extend each week to 2 weeks. (2) Focus only on Combat + Prayer (skip Gathering/Artisan for now). (3) Use quest XP rewards to skip levels. (4) Play "ultra-efficient" — only do highest XP/hour methods.

End result: Same mid-game stats, but takes 60 days instead of 30.

⏱️ Schedule B: 5+ Hours/Day (15-Day Version)（⏱️ 计划B：每天5小时以上（计划B：每天5小时以上，15天版））

Total playtime: 5+ hours per day × 15 days = 75+ hours total.

Adjustments: (1) Compress Week 1-2 into 5 days. (2) Train Prayer at Gilded Altar (fast). (3) Burst/Splashing for Magic (fastest). (4) Use power-leveling methods (even if expensive).

End result: Mid-game stats in 10-15 days. Requires 5-10M GP bank to fund fast methods.

⚔️ Ironman Adjustments（⚔️ 铁人模式调整（铁人模式调整））

What's different: Ironman accounts can't trade with other players or use the Grand Exchange. You must gather ALL resources yourself.

Adjustments: (1) Spend more time on Gathering skills (you need your own ores/herbs/fish). (2) Prioritize Farming (grow your own herbs for Herblore). (3) Do "collection" activities (Birdhouse runs, Herbiboar, etc.). (4) Expect 1.5x-2x longer timeline.

Recommended: Follow the normal 30-day plan, but add 30-60 minutes of Gathering/Farming every day.

🆓 F2P-Only Version (Before Membership)（🆓 仅免费版（仅免费版，成为会员前））

What's available: Combat (up to Hill Giants), Mining (up to Coal), Fishing (up to Lobster), Woodcutting (up to Willow), Prayer (up to 43 with babydragon bones).

Adjustments: (1) Train only F2P skills. (2) Aim for Combat 50-60, Prayer 43, Mining 30, Fishing 40. (3) Save 500K-1M GP before becoming a member. (4) Use quest XP (Dragon Slayer, etc.) to boost stats.

Benefits: Starting membership with F2P stats + some GP makes the transition much smoother.

Which Schedule Should You Use?

1 hour/day: Use 60-day version. Focus on efficiency — only do highest XP/hour methods. Use quest XP rewards.

2-3 hours/day: Use the standard 30-day plan (this guide).

5+ hours/day: Use 15-day version. Consider buying some supplies from GE to save time.

Ironman: Add Gathering/Farming time. Expect longer timeline.

💡 How to Make GP During the 30-Day Plan（💡 30天计划中如何赚钱（30天计划中如何赚钱））

You don't need to start with millions of GP. The plan includes making GP as you go:

  • Week 1: Sell cowhides (50-100K GP), feathers (10-20K GP), monster drops (20-50K GP). Total: 100-200K GP.
  • Week 2: Sell Iron ores (50-100K GP), raw/cooked Lobsters (100-200K GP), Willow logs (50-100K GP). Total: 300-500K GP (cumulative).
  • Week 3: Continue selling Gathering loot. Also sell cooked fish (if surplus). Total: 500K-1M GP (cumulative).
  • Week 4: Slayer tasks drop valuable loot (100-300K GP per task). Total: 1-3M GP (cumulative).
Budget Pro Tip

If you're completely broke (0 GP start), do this: (1) Kill Chickens for feathers (sell for 2-5 GP each). (2) Kill Cows for cowhides (sell for 100-200 GP each). (3) Buy Trout/Salmon for food (cheap). (4) Train combat at Al-Kharid Warriors (they drop coins). In 2-3 hours, you'll have 50-100K GP — enough to buy basic gear and food.

📋 FAQ — Fast Track Skill Guide（📋 常见问题 — 快速通道技能指南（常见问题 — 快速通道技能指南））

Can I really reach mid-game stats in 30 days?

Yes, if you play 2-3 hours per day and follow the plan. The math works out: Combat 60 takes ~40-60 hours. Prayer 43 takes ~5-10 hours. Agility 50 takes ~10-15 hours. That's 55-85 hours total — well within 60-90 hours (2-3 hours/day ×30 days).

What if I can't play every day?

That's fine! The 30-day timeline is a guideline. If you miss days, just extend the timeline. Playing 1 hour every day is better than playing 10 hours once a week (consistency > intensity).

Should I follow this plan exactly?

The plan is a template. Adjust it based on your playstyle: (1) If you hate combat, spend more time on Gathering. (2) If you love questing, do more quests for XP rewards. (3) If you have more GP, use faster (but expensive) training methods.

What should I do after 30 days?

After 30 days, you're "mid-game ready." Next steps: (1) Continue Slayer (main combat training method). (2) Train expensive skills (Herblore, Construction) now that you have GP. (3) Attempt Barrows and mid-level bosses. (4) Max your favorite skills to 99.

Is this plan good for Ironman accounts?

Yes, with adjustments (see Section 6). Ironman accounts need more Gathering time because they can't buy supplies. Expect the timeline to be 1.5x-2x longer.
