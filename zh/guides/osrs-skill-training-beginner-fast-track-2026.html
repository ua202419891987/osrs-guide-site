1. What "Mid-Game Ready" Looks Like（1. "中期就绪"是什么样？）

Before starting the 30-day plan, you need to know what you're aiming for. "Mid-game ready" means your account can efficiently train, make GP, and attempt mid-level bosses. Here are the target stats:

🎯 Target Stats After 30 Days（🎯 30天后目标数据）

Skill（技能）Target Level（目标等级）Why This Level（为什么是这个等级）
Attack60-70Unlocks Dragon Scimitar (60) and Abyssal Whip (70)（解锁龙弯刀和深渊鞭）
Strength60-70High enough for solid DPS（足够高的DPS）
Defence40-60Dragon armor (40) or Barrows armor (60)（龙甲或巴罗斯甲）
Hitpoints55-70Natural growth from combat training（战斗训练自然增长）
Ranged50-70Rune Crossbow + Broad bolts (requires Slayer 55)（符文弩 + 宽头弹药）
Magic55-70High Alchemy (55) + combat spells（高等级炼金术 + 战斗法术）
Prayer43-52Protect Prayers (43) + Smite (52 for PvP)（保护祈祷 + 重击）
Slayer1-50Access to better tasks and bosses（解锁更好的任务和Boss）
Agility50-60Graceful armor + shortcuts（优雅套装 + 捷径）
GP Bank3-10MEnough to buy gear, supplies, and bonds（足够购买装备、消耗品和绑定）

These stats unlock: (1) Efficient GP making (200-500K per hour). (2) Access to Barrows and mid-level bosses. (3) Ability to train Slayer efficiently. (4) Enough GP to never worry about "can I afford this?" again.

Why 30 Days is Realistic（为什么30天是现实的）

If you play 2-3 hours per day, you can reach these stats in 30 days. Here's the math: Combat 60-70 takes ~40-60 hours total. Prayer 43 takes ~5-10 hours. Agility 50-60 takes ~10-15 hours. The rest (Gathering, Artisan) can be done "on the side" or in bursts. 2-3 hours/day × 30 days = 60-90 hours total. This is enough time.

② Prerequisites Before Starting（② 开始前的必要条件）

  • Membership: This plan assumes you have membership (not F2P). F2P version is in Section 6.（会员：本计划假设你拥有会员资格）
  • Playtime: 2-3 hours per day minimum. If you play less, extend the timeline to 60 days.（游戏时间：每天至少2-3小时）
  • Starting stats: Level 3 account (1 Att/1 Str/1 Def/10 HP). If you're F2P with some stats already, adjust accordingly.（起始数据：3级账号）
  • GP to start: 0 GP is fine. The plan includes making your first 500K GP in Week 2.（起始金币：0金币也没问题）

2. Week 1: Combat Foundation (Days 1-7)（2. 第1周：战斗基础，第1-7天）

Week 1 is all about getting your combat stats to a level where you can kill Hill Giants, do early Slayer tasks, and survive in dangerous areas. Don't worry about GP yet — focus on XP.

📅 Week 1 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第1周每日计划，2-3小时/天）

Days 1-2: Train at Chickens (Lumbridge). Get Attack/Strength/Defence to 10. Loot feathers and bones, sell on GE for first GP.（第1-2天：在Lumbridge鸡场训练）

Days 3-4: Train at Cows (Lumbridge swamp). Get stats to 20. Loot cowhides (sell for 100-200 GP each). Bury bones for Prayer XP.（第3-4天：在Lumbridge沼泽牛场训练）

Days 5-7: Train at Al-Kharid Warriors or Moss Giants (Edgeville Dungeon, need Ring of Charos or pass). Get stats to 30-40. Start burying Big Bones for Prayer 20+.（第5-7天：在Al-Kharid战士或苔藓巨人处训练）

End of Week 1 target: Attack 30, Strength 30, Defence 30, Hitpoints 25, Prayer 20, Ranged 1, Magic 1. GP bank: 50-100K.（第1周结束目标）

③ 🗺️ Exact Training Locations (Week 1)（③ 🗺️ 精确训练地点，第1周）

Location 1: Chickens (Lumbridge)（地点1：鸡，Lumbridge）

Where: Behind the Lumbridge general store (north side).（位置：Lumbridge杂货店后面）

What to bring: Bronze weapons (sword/axe), no armor (train Defence naturally), food (chicken drops raw chicken which you can cook on the nearby fire).（携带物品：青铜武器）

Attack style: "Controlled" (trains Att+Str+Def simultaneously).（攻击风格：均衡模式）

XP per hour: ~5-10K Combat XP per hour (slow, but you're learning the game).（每小时经验：约5-10K战斗经验）

Location 2: Cows (Lumbridge Swamp)（地点2：牛，Lumbridge Swamp）

Where: Lumbridge Swamp (south of Lumbridge castle).（位置：Lumbridge城堡南面沼泽）

What to bring: Iron weapons, leather armor (bought from the Grand Exchange), lobsters or trout for food.（携带物品：铁武器、皮甲）

XP per hour: ~10-15K Combat XP per hour.（每小时经验：约10-15K战斗经验）

GP per hour: ~20-40K GP (from selling cowhides and bones).（每小时金币：约20-40K）

Location 3: Al-Kharid Warriors (F2P) or Moss Giants (Members)（地点3：Al-Kharid战士（免费版）或苔藓巨人（会员版））

Where: Al-Kharid Palace (F2P) OR Edgeville Dungeon → Moss Giants (Members, need Ring of Charos or 10 GP to enter).（位置：Al-Kharid宫殿或Edgeville地牢）

What to bring: Steel weapons, steel armor, 10+ lobsters.（携带物品：钢武器、钢甲）

XP per hour: ~20-30K Combat XP per hour.（每小时经验：约20-30K战斗经验）

Drops: Big Bones (bury for Prayer XP), herbs (sell on GE), coins.（掉落物：大骨、草药、金币）

Week 1 Pro Tip（第1周专业建议）

Don't buy gear from the Grand Exchange yet. Loot everything from monsters and use it. Only buy essentials (lobsters for food, better weapons). Save your GP for Week 2 when you'll need it for membership bond or better gear.

Week 1 Pro Tip（第1周专业建议）

Don't buy gear from the Grand Exchange yet. Loot everything from monsters and use it. Only buy essentials (lobsters for food, better weapons). Save your GP for Week 2 when you'll need it for membership bond or better gear.

3. Week 2: Gathering & First GP (Days 8-14)（3. 第2周：采集与第一桶金，第8-14天）

Week 2 is when you start making real GP. You'll train Gathering skills (Mining, Fishing, Woodcutting) and sell the resources on the Grand Exchange. By the end of Week 2, you should have 500K-1M GP — enough to buy better gear and supplies.

📅 Week 2 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第2周每日计划，2-3小时/天）

Days 8-10: Mine Iron ores (levels 15-30). Sell ores on GE. Target: 100K+ GP.（第8-10天：开采铁矿石）

Days 11-12: Fish Lobsters (level 40+). Cook some for food, sell the rest. Target: 200K+ total GP.（第11-12天：捕捞龙虾）

Days 13-14: Woodcut Willows (level 30+). Sell logs on GE. Also do easy quests for XP rewards. Target: 500K+ total GP.（第13-14天：砍伐柳树）

End of Week 2 target: Mining 30, Fishing 40, Woodcutting 30, GP bank: 500K-1M.（第2周结束目标）

④ 🗺️ Exact Training Locations (Week 2)（④ 🗺️ 精确训练地点，第2周）

Mining Iron — Best XP + GP（采矿铁矿石 — 最佳经验+金币）

Where: Al-Kharid Mine (F2P) OR Legends' Guild Mine (Members, best).（位置：Al-Kharid矿场或Legend公会矿场）

What to bring: Iron pickaxe (or better), no armor (run energy), food (lobsters).（携带物品：铁镐或更好）

Method: Mine Iron ores. Bank them (use nearby bank). Sell on GE for ~100 GP per ore.（方法：开采铁矿石，存入银行，出售）

XP per hour: ~20-30K Mining XP per hour.（每小时经验：约20-30K采矿经验）

GP per hour: ~50-100K GP (depending on GE prices).（每小时金币：约50-100K）

Fishing Lobsters — Food + GP（钓鱼龙虾 — 食物+金币）

Where: Catherby (Members) OR Musa Point (F2P, slower).（位置：Catherby或Musa Point）

What to bring: Lobster pot (bought from fishing shop), coins for pot.（携带物品：龙虾笼）

Method: Fish Lobsters. Cook half for food, sell half on GE for ~150 GP each.（方法：捕捞龙虾，一半烹饪食用，一半出售）

XP per hour: ~30-40K Fishing XP per hour.（每小时经验：约30-40K钓鱼经验）

GP per hour: ~50-100K GP (from selling raw/cooked lobsters).（每小时金币：约50-100K）

Woodcutting Willows — AFK XP（伐木柳树 — AFK经验）

Where: Draynor Village (World 36 is Woodcutting world).（位置：Draynor村）

What to bring: Steel axe (or better), no armor.（携带物品：钢斧或更好）

Method: Chop Willow trees. Bank logs. Sell on GE for ~30-50 GP per log.（方法：砍伐柳树，存入银行，出售）

XP per hour: ~20-30K Woodcutting XP per hour.（每小时经验：约20-30K伐木经验）

AFKability: High. You can AFK for 5-10 minutes at a time.（AFK友好度：高）

Week 2 Pro Tip（第2周专业建议）

Always check Grand Exchange prices before selling. Sometimes it's more profitable to sell raw fish vs cooked fish, or logs vs bowstrings. Use the OSRS Wiki's "Grand Exchange" pages to check current prices.

4. Week 3: Artisan & Support Skills (Days 15-21)（4. 第3周：工匠与支持技能，第15-21天）

Week 3 is when you start training Artisan skills (Cooking, Crafting) and Support skills (Agility, Thieving). These skills make your account more efficient and unlock new capabilities.

📅 Week 3 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第3周每日计划，2-3小时/天）

Days 15-17: Cook the fish you caught. Level Cooking to 40+. Use Cooking gauntlets (Hosidius kitchen) to reduce burn rate.（第15-17天：烹饪钓到的鱼）

Days 18-19: Craft leather armor from cowhides. Level Crafting to 30+. Also do "Fairytale I" quest (unlocks fairy rings for fast travel).（第18-19天：用牛皮制作皮甲）

Days 20-21: Train Agility at Gnome Stronghold (levels 1-30). Also start Thieving (pickpocket Men/Women for GP).（第20-21天：在Gnome要塞训练敏捷）

End of Week 3 target: Cooking 40, Crafting 30, Agility 30, Thieving 20, GP bank: 1-2M.（第3周结束目标）

⑤ Cooking Training — Efficient Method（⑤ 烹饪训练 — 高效方法）

Levels 1-40: Cook Fish From Fishing（1-40级：烹饪钓鱼获得的鱼）

Method: Use the fish you caught in Week 2. Start with Shrimp, then Trout/Salmon, then Lobsters.（方法：使用第2周钓到的鱼）

Best location: Hosidius kitchen (Zeah). Has a Cooking gauntlet that reduces burn chance by 5 levels.（最佳位置：Hosidius厨房）

XP per hour: ~50-80K Cooking XP per hour (at higher levels with gauntlet).（每小时经验：约50-80K烹饪经验）

Cost: Free (you use your own fish). If you buy raw fish, it costs ~10-30K GP per hour.（成本：免费，使用自己的鱼）

⑥ Crafting Training — Leather Armor（⑥ 手工艺训练 — 皮甲）

Levels 1-30: Leather From Cows（1-30级：用牛皮制作皮革）

Method: Kill cows (Lumbridge swamp) → collect cowhides → tan at Al-Kharid tanner (costs 1 GP per hide) → craft into leather armor.（方法：杀牛取皮，在Al-Kharid鞣制，制作皮甲）

Alternative: Buy cowhides from GE (~100 GP each), tan them, craft into armor, sell armor on GE for profit.（替代方案：从GE购买牛皮）

XP per hour: ~30-50K Crafting XP per hour.（每小时经验：约30-50K手工艺经验）

Profitability: Usually slightly profitable or break-even.（盈利性：通常略有盈利或持平）

⑦ Agility Training — Gnome Stronghold（⑦ 敏捷训练 — Gnome要塞）

Levels 1-30: Gnome Stronghold Agility Course（1-30级：Gnome要塞敏捷课程）

Where: Tree Gnome Stronghold (north-west of Ardougne).（位置：Tree Gnome要塞）

Method: Complete the obstacle course. Each lap gives ~85 XP. Takes ~2-3 minutes per lap at low levels.（方法：完成障碍课程）

XP per hour: ~8-12K Agility XP per hour (slow, but it's the only F2P-friendly method).（每小时经验：约8-12K敏捷经验）

Marks of Grace: None at Gnome course. You need level 30+ to get Marks of Grace from Rooftop courses.（优雅印记：Gnome课程没有）

Week 3 Pro Tip（第3周专业建议）

Don't stress about getting high levels in Artisan/Support skills yet. The goal for Week 3 is to get them to "useful" levels (Cooking 40 for lobsters, Crafting 30 for dragonhide, Agility 30 for better courses). You'll max them later when you have more GP and time.

5. Week 4: Polishing & Preparation (Days 22-30)（5. 第4周：打磨与准备，第22-30天）

Week 4 is when everything comes together. You'll finish your combat stats to 60+, get Prayer to 43 (Protect Prayers), start Slayer, and prepare for mid-game content.

📅 Week 4 Daily Schedule (2-3 hours/day)（📅 第4周每日计划，2-3小时/天）

Days 22-24: Train combat at Rock Crabs. Get Attack/Strength/Defence to 50-60. Bury bones or use Gilded Altar for Prayer 43.（第22-24天：在岩石蟹处训练战斗）

Days 25-27: Start Slayer with Turael (Burthorpe). Do easy tasks (Cows, Chickens, etc.) to get Slayer XP and learn the system.（第25-27天：开始Slayer）

Days 28-30: Fill in skill gaps. Train Agility to 50-60 (Al-Kharid Rooftop). Buy Graceful armor. Make final GP push (target: 3-5M GP bank).（第28-30天：填补技能空白）

End of 30 Days target: Combat 60+, Prayer 43, Slayer 10-20, Agility 50-60, GP bank: 3-10M. You're MID-GAME READY!（30天结束目标：中期就绪！）

⑧ 🗺️ Rock Crabs — Best Combat Training (Levels 50-70)（⑧ 🗺️ 岩石蟹 — 最佳战斗训练，50-70级）

Why Rock Crabs?（为什么选岩石蟹？）

Rock Crabs (and Sand Crabs) have HIGH hitpoints but LOW defence. They also respawn quickly and don't deal much damage. This makes them perfect for AFK combat training.

Where: Hosidius (Kourend) for Rock Crabs. Zeah (south of Shilo Village) for Sand Crabs.（位置：Hosidius的岩石蟹，Zeah的沙蟹）

What to bring: Best melee weapon (Steel/Black/Mithril scimitar), leather/dragonhide armor, 10+ lobsters, Prayer potions (if Prayer 43+).（携带物品：最佳近战武器、皮甲/龙皮甲）

XP per hour: ~40-60K Combat XP per hour (with "Controlled" style).（每小时经验：约40-60K战斗经验）

Time to 60 melee stats: ~10-15 hours of crab training.（达到60近战数据的时间：约10-15小时）

⑨ 🙏 Getting Prayer 43 — Protect Prayers（⑨ 🙏 达到祈祷43级 — 保护祈祷）

Method 1: Bury Bones (Free but Slow)（方法1：埋骨，免费但慢）

Collect bones from monsters you kill. Bury them. XP per bone: regular bones (4.5 XP), Big Bones (15 XP), Babydragon bones (30 XP).

Time to 43 Prayer: ~2-4 hours of burying Big Bones.（达到43祈祷的时间：约2-4小时）

Method 2: Gilded Altar (Members, Fast)（方法2：镀金祭坛，会员版，快速）

Use a Gilded Altar in someone's Player-Owned House (World 330 is official Gilded Altar world). Offering bones at Gilded Altar gives 350% XP per bone.

Time to 43 Prayer: ~30-60 minutes of offering Big Bones at Gilded Altar.（达到43祈祷的时间：约30-60分钟）

Cost: Free (if using someone else's house) or ~100-200K GP (if buying bones).（成本：免费或约10-20万金币）

⑩ ⚔️ Starting Slayer — Talk to Turael（⑩ ⚔️ 开始杀戮 — 与Turael交谈）

How to Start Slayer（如何开始杀戮）

Talk to Slayer Master Turael in Burthorpe (no skill requirements). He gives easy tasks designed for new players.

First tasks: Cows, Chickens, Rats, Spiders. These are trivial but give you Slayer XP and teach you the Slayer interface.（前几个任务：牛、鸡、老鼠、蜘蛛）

When to switch to harder tasks: At Combat 50+, switch to Mazchna (Canifis). At Combat 70+, switch to Vannaka (Edgeville Dungeon).（何时切换到更难的任务：战斗50+级时切换到Mazchna）

Week 4 Pro Tip（第4周专业建议）

Don't rush to finish everything in Week 4. The goal is to be "mid-game ready" — not maxed. If you need 5-10 more days to reach the target stats, that's fine. The 30-day timeline is a guideline, not a hard deadline.

6. Alternative Schedules (1hr/5hrs + Ironman + F2P)（6. 替代计划，1小时/5小时 + 铁人模式 + 免费版）

The 30-day plan assumes 2-3 hours/day. But your playtime might be different. Here are adjusted schedules for different situations:

⏱️ Schedule A: 1 Hour/Day (60-Day Version)（⏱️ 计划A：每天1小时，60天版）

Total playtime: 1 hour per day × 60 days = 60 hours total.（总游戏时间：60小时）

Adjustments: (1) Extend each week to 2 weeks. (2) Focus only on Combat + Prayer (skip Gathering/Artisan for now). (3) Use quest XP rewards to skip levels. (4) Play "ultra-efficient" — only do highest XP/hour methods.（调整方案）

End result: Same mid-game stats, but takes 60 days instead of 30.（最终结果：相同的中期数据，但需要60天）

⏱️ Schedule B: 5+ Hours/Day (15-Day Version)（⏱️ 计划B：每天5小时以上，15天版）

Total playtime: 5+ hours per day × 15 days = 75+ hours total.（总游戏时间：75小时以上）

Adjustments: (1) Compress Week 1-2 into 5 days. (2) Train Prayer at Gilded Altar (fast). (3) Burst/Splashing for Magic (fastest). (4) Use power-leveling methods (even if expensive).（调整方案）

End result: Mid-game stats in 10-15 days. Requires 5-10M GP bank to fund fast methods.（最终结果：10-15天达到中期数据）

⚔️ Ironman Adjustments（⚔️ 铁人模式调整）

What's different: Ironman accounts can't trade with other players or use the Grand Exchange. You must gather ALL resources yourself.（铁人模式账号无法与其他玩家交易或使用GE）

Adjustments: (1) Spend more time on Gathering skills (you need your own ores/herbs/fish). (2) Prioritize Farming (grow your own herbs for Herblore). (3) Do "collection" activities (Birdhouse runs, Herbiboar, etc.). (4) Expect 1.5x-2x longer timeline.（调整方案）

Recommended: Follow the normal 30-day plan, but add 30-60 minutes of Gathering/Farming every day.（建议：遵循标准的30天计划，每天增加30-60分钟的采集/农耕时间）

🆓 F2P-Only Version (Before Membership)（🆓 仅免费版，成为会员前）

What's available: Combat (up to Hill Giants), Mining (up to Coal), Fishing (up to Lobster), Woodcutting (up to Willow), Prayer (up to 43 with babydragon bones).（可用内容）

Adjustments: (1) Train only F2P skills. (2) Aim for Combat 50-60, Prayer 43, Mining 30, Fishing 40. (3) Save 500K-1M GP before becoming a member. (4) Use quest XP (Dragon Slayer, etc.) to boost stats.（调整方案）

Benefits: Starting membership with F2P stats + some GP makes the transition much smoother.（好处：拥有F2P数据+一些金币再开始会员会顺利得多）

Which Schedule Should You Use?（应该用哪个计划？）

1 hour/day: Use 60-day version. Focus on efficiency — only do highest XP/hour methods. Use quest XP rewards.（每天1小时：用60天版）

2-3 hours/day: Use the standard 30-day plan (this guide).（每天2-3小时：用标准30天计划）

5+ hours/day: Use 15-day version. Consider buying some supplies from GE to save time.（每天5小时以上：用15天版）

Ironman: Add Gathering/Farming time. Expect longer timeline.（铁人模式：增加采集/农耕时间）

Training skills costs GP. Here's exactly how much you'll need to spend (and where to get it) during the 30-day plan:

(4) Train combat at Al-Kharid Warriors (they drop coins). In 2-3 hours, you'll have 50-100K GP — enough to buy basic gear and food.

📋 FAQ — Fast Track Skill Guide（📋 常见问题 — 快速通道技能指南）

Can I really reach mid-game stats in 30 days?（真的能在30天内达到中期数据吗？）

Yes, if you play 2-3 hours per day and follow the plan. The math works out: Combat 60 takes ~40-60 hours. Prayer 43 takes ~5-10 hours. Agility 50 takes ~10-15 hours. That's 55-85 hours total — well within 60-90 hours (2-3 hours/day ×30 days).

What if I can't play every day?（如果不能每天玩怎么办？）

That's fine! The 30-day timeline is a guideline. If you miss days, just extend the timeline. Playing 1 hour every day is better than playing 10 hours once a week (consistency > intensity).

Should I follow this plan exactly?（我应该严格遵循这个计划吗？）

The plan is a template. Adjust it based on your playstyle: (1) If you hate combat, spend more time on Gathering. (2) If you love questing, do more quests for XP rewards. (3) If you have more GP, use faster (but expensive) training methods.

What should I do after 30 days?（30天后应该做什么？）

After 30 days, you're "mid-game ready." Next steps: (1) Continue Slayer (main combat training method). (2) Train expensive skills (Herblore, Construction) now that you have GP. (3) Attempt Barrows and mid-level bosses. (4) Max your favorite skills to 99.

Is this plan good for Ironman accounts?（这个计划适合铁人模式账号吗？）

Yes, with adjustments (see Section 6). Ironman accounts need more Gathering time because they can't buy supplies. Expect the timeline to be 1.5x-2x longer.
