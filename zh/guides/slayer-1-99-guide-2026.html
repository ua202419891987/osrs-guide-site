1. Why Train Slayer?
（1. 为什么训练杀戮者？）

Slayer is the most popular combat skill in OSRS because it trains all three combat styles at once (Attack, Strength, Defence, Ranged, Magic, and Hitpoints all progress together). It's also one of the most profitable skills — many Slayer tasks earn 1M+ GP/hr while gaining XP.

Key benefits:

  • Multi-combat XP — Ice Barrage in the Catacombs of Kourend gives 80K-120K XP/hr (highest Slayer XP in the game)
  • Profit while training — many tasks earn 500K-5M GP/hr
  • Boss unlocks — Slayer points unlock Alchemical Hydra, Cerberus, Kraken, and more
  • Superior Slayer drops — Imbued Heart (30M+ GP), Eternal Gem, and Slayer helmet upgrades
  • The Slayer Helmet — best-in-slot helm for melee tasks (+15% accuracy/damage)

6. Slayer Boss Tasks
（6. 杀戮者Boss任务）

These bosses are unlocked via the "Like a Boss" Slayer reward (200-300 points each). They give the highest GP/hr in the game.

🐉 Alchemical Hydra (95 Slayer)
（炼金九头蛇(95级杀戮者)）

The most profitable Slayer boss. 3-phase fight with prayer switching:

  • Phase 1 (Fire): Protect from Magic, attack with Ranged
  • Phase 2 (Poison): Protect from Ranged, attack with Melee
  • Phase 3 (Lightning): Protect from Melee, attack with Magic
  • Vent mechanic: Step on the correct colored vent every 3 attacks to avoid 75% max hit
  • GP/hr: 4.2M-5.4M | Key drop: Hydra's Claw (80M, 1/1001) → Dragon Hunter Lance

🐺 Cerberus (91 Slayer)
（地狱犬(91级杀戮者)）

  • Mechanic: Summons 3 ghosts that must be killed with appropriate combat style (Mage/Range/Melee ghost)
  • Prayer: Uses "Soul Split" — only takes damage when your prayer is drained. Manage prayer switching.
  • GP/hr: 2M-4M | Key drops: Primordial Boots (70M, 1/512), Pegasian Boots (30M), Eternal Boots (5M)

🦑 Kraken (87 Slayer)
（海怪(87级杀戮者)）

  • Mechanic: Extremely simple — just attack the 4 whirlpools then Kraken. Uses Magic only.
  • AFK level: Very high — can literally watch Netflix while killing.
  • GP/hr: 800K-1.2M | Key drop: Kraken Tentacle (30M, 1/400)

🌋 Thermonuclear Smoke Devil (93 Slayer)
（热核烟魔(93级杀戮者)）

  • Mechanic: Wake-up call (disappears, reappears) — stop attacking during wake-up. Lightning attack (jump 1 tile away).
  • GP/hr: 1M-2M | Key drop: Occult Necklace (1.5M, 1/350) — best magic amulet in the game

👹 Abyssal Sire (85 Slayer)
（深渊领主(85级杀戮者)）

  • Mechanic: 4-phase fight. Stun the Sire when it teleports to you (use vigilor's chainmace special).
  • GP/hr: 1.5M-3M | Key drop: Abyssal Whip (2M), Bludgeon pieces (10M each set)

🎫 How to Farm Slayer Points Fast (Ironman Trick)
（如何快速刷杀戮者点数(铁人技巧)）

Ironman players use the "Turael Boost" method:

  1. Get 9 fast Turael tasks (easy low-level tasks, ~2 min each)
  2. On the 10th task, go to Konar and hand it in — you get 100 points for the milestone instead of Turael's 0
  3. Repeat. This gets you 400-600 points/hour for unlocking boss tasks

10. FAQ
（10. 常见问题）

What is the fastest way to get 99 Slayer in OSRS?

The fastest route is: Turael (1-20 combat), Vannaka (40-70), Chaeldar (70-85), then Duradel (85-99). Use Ice Barrage in the Catacombs of Kourend for multi-combat tasks (Dust Devils, Nechryael, Abyssal Demons). With a Tier 80+ weapon and Piety, expect 80K-120K Slayer XP/hr from level 75+.

Which Slayer master should I use for max XP?

Duradel is the best Slayer master for XP from 100 combat / 50 Slayer onward. He assigns high-XP tasks like Nechryael, Abyssal Demons, and Dust Devils. Nieve is a close second if you haven't unlocked Duradel yet. For Wildy tasks, Krystilia gives the most points but has PK risk.

What should I block on Duradel?

For max XP: Block Black Demons, Hellhounds, Greater Demons, Fire Giants, and Wyverns. For max GP: Block Black Demons, Hellhounds, Steel Dragons, and Fossil Island Wyverns. You need 100 Slayer points per block. Skip (don't block) tasks you can do but don't want to.

How much money can you make with 99 Slayer?

From 1-99 Slayer, you can earn 200M-500M+ GP depending on your task choices and boss unlocks. High-value tasks: Alchemical Hydra (4.2M-5.4M GP/hr), Cerberus (2M-4M/hr), Grotesque Guardians (1.5M-2.5M/hr), and Abyssal Demons (800K-1.2M/hr).

When should I unlock Slayer bosses?

Unlock bosses as soon as you have the Slayer points: Alchemical Hydra (95 Slayer, 300 points), Cerberus (91 Slayer, 200 points), Kraken (87 Slayer, 200 points), Thermonuclear Smoke Devil (93 Slayer, 200 points), and Grotesque Guardians (75 Slayer, 200 points).

Do I need to imbue my Slayer Helmet at NMZ?

Yes, absolutely. The imbued Slayer Helmet gives +15-16.67% accuracy and damage for ALL combat styles (melee +15% str bonus, magic/Ranged +15% accuracy). The NMZ grind costs ~1.25M points (~3-4 hours). Without imbuing, you're leaving massive DPS on the table.

What is the best inventory for Slayer?

Standard burst inventory: 2-3 Prayer Potions, 1 Super Combat Potion, 1-2 Super Restores, 1 Emergency Teleport (Royal Seed Pod / Amulet of Glory), rest Sharks/Monkfish. For bursting: 4-6 Prayer Potions, 2 Super Restores, Blood Blitz/Barrage runes, 1 Teleport.

11. Final Tips
（11. 最终建议）

  • Always use a Dwarf Multicannon on single-combat tasks — it adds 20-40% XP/hr for only ~200K GP/hr in cannonballs.
  • Burst in the Catacombs of Kourend — the altar lets you recharge prayer for free, saving millions in Prayer Potion costs.
  • Unlock Bigger and Badder FIRST — the Superior Slayer drops alone (Imbued Heart, Eternal Gem) are worth 30M+ GP over a 1-99 grind.
  • Complete Western Provinces Elite Diary — gives Nieve/Duradel +3 points per task (20% more points).
  • Complete Lumbridge & Draynor Elite Diary — unlocks 6th block slot (saves 100 points over your 1-99 grind).
  • Skip, don't block, task titled "task name" — skip costs 30 points, block costs 100. Only block tasks you never want to see again.
  • Use the Kongor boost method (Ironman) — 9 Turael tasks then 1 Konar for 100 points. Repeat to farm points fast.
  • Wear your Slayer Helmet on task — it's the best helm slot for Slayer. Imbue it ASAP at NMZ.
  • Do quests first — Witch's House, Haunted Mine, Sins of the Father, Monkey Madness II give ~150K free XP.
  • Consider Konar for milestones — she gives 18 points per task (vs Duradel's 15). Hand in your 10/50/100/250 tasks to Konar for bonus points.
