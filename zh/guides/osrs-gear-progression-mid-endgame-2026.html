① Melee Path: Bandos to Torva（近战路线：Bandos到Torva）

From Barrows/bandos, your melee upgrade path: Bandos Chestplate (24M) → Bandos Tassets (26M) > Primordial Boots (30M) > Ferocious Gloves (from Hydra) > Avernic Defender (50M) > Infernal Cape (from Inferno) > Torva Full Helm (100M) > Torva Platebody (200M) > Torva Platelegs (150M). Total: ~600M for full Torva + accessories.

② Ranged Path: Armadyl to Masori（远程路线）

Ranged upgrades: Armadyl Crossbow (30M) > Dragon Hunter Crossbow (80M) > Peculiar Ring (10M) > Armadyl Armor (50M set) > Zaryte Crossbow (150M) > Masori Mask (200M) > Masori Body (350M) > Masori Chaps (250M). Total: ~1.2B for full Masori + ZCB. For budget, skip Masori and use Armadyl + Dragon Hunter Crossbow until you have 1B+.

③ Magic Path: Ancestral to Shadow（魔法路线）

Magic upgrades: Trident of the Swamp (2M) > Toxic Staff of the Dead (30M) > Ancestral Hat (40M) > Ancestral Top (80M) > Ancestral Bottom (60M) > Kodai Wand (120M) > Tumeken's Shadow (900M). The Shadow is the single biggest DPS upgrade in the game — it triples your mage damage in ToA. Save for it last as it costs nearly 1B.

④ Optimal Buy Order（最优购买顺序）

Follow this strict order for your first 1B GP: 1) Dragon Hunter Crossbow (80M) — unlocks Vorkath/Zulrah farming, 2) Bandos set + Avernic (100M) — melee for Slayer, 3) Ancestral set (180M) — for ToA, 4) Zaryte Crossbow (150M) — ranged for raids, 5) Torva set (450M) — best melee, 6) Tumeken's Shadow (900M) — last but most impactful.

FAQ

What's the single best upgrade for under 100M?（100M以下最佳单件升级？）

Dragon Hunter Crossbow at 80M. It unlocks consistent 3-5M GP/hr at Vorkath and is BiS for many dragon tasks.
