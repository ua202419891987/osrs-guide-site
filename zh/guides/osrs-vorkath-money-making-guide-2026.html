① Requirements & Recommendations（① 要求与建议）

① Quest Requirements（① 任务要求）

Before you can fight Vorkath, you must complete Dragon Slayer II — one of OSRS's most challenging grandmaster quests. DS2 requires 66 Magic, 70 Smithing, 70 Agility, 68 Crafting, and 66 Mining, along with completion of several pre-quests (Bone Voyage, Client of Kourend, and Elemental Workshop I through III). You do not need to complete the post-quest fight with Galvek again; Vorkath becomes permanently available on the island of Ungael after finishing the quest. Simply speak to Torfinn at the Rellekka docks to sail there anytime.

② Stat Recommendations（② 属性建议）

Minimum stats for efficient kills:

  • Ranged: 75+ (90+ recommended for DHCB)
  • Magic: 70+ (for crumble undead / teleports)
  • Hitpoints: 75+ (80+ for safety)
  • Defence: 70+ (higher reduces food usage)
  • Prayer: 70+ (for Rigour/Augury)
  • Herblore: 78+ (for anti-venom+)

Higher stats directly translate to faster kills and more GP per hour. Every 5 Ranged levels above 80 adds roughly 1.8M GP/hr to your profit. Max efficiency starts around 95+ Ranged with Rigour.

③ Key Unlocks Worth Getting（③ 值得获取的关键解锁）

  • Rigour (74 Prayer + 70 Defence): Massive DPS boost for ranged methods. Worth 40M+ investment if you plan 100+ kills. Rigour is obtained from Chambers of Xeric (CoX) as a Dexterous Prayer Scroll drop — it pays for itself within roughly 60 hours of Vorkath farming.
  • Dragonfire Ward / Dragonfire Shield: Essential antifire protection alongside your potion. The Ward offers +8 Ranged Attack and +5 Ranged Strength over the standard Dragonfire Shield, making it a worthwhile upgrade for DHCB users.
  • Vorkath Head (50 KC): Allows you to equip the backpack with +2 Ranged Strength. The upgraded Ava's Assembler is a significant accuracy and damage boost over the standard version.
  • Elite Void (Hard Western Provinces Diary): BIS for max-ranged DHCB method. The Elite Void set provides a 12.5% damage and accuracy bonus for Ranged, outperforming Armadyl at Vorkath due to his low Magic level and Defence.
  • Ornate Rejuvenation Pool (85+ Construction): Allows instant stat/prayer restoration between kills, dramatically improving kills per hour.

④ Boss Mechanics & Strategies（④ Boss 机制与策略）

① Vorkath's Basic Attack Pattern（① 韦克思的基础攻击模式）

Vorkath attacks with a mix of melee, ranged, and dragonfire. His standard phase consists of:

  • Regular attacks: Ranged (piercing shot) and magic (fireball) — pray accordingly based on your ranged/melee setup.
  • Dragonfire: Hits hard through pray mage. Wear a dragonfire shield and sip antifire potion for 100% immunity.
  • Melee swipe: Only if you stand in melee range (1 tile). Lance users must be careful.

② Special Attacks — Spawn Phase（② 特殊攻击 — 召唤阶段）

After every 6-7 regular attacks, Vorkath summons a Zombified Spawn. This is a critical mechanic:

  1. The spawn appears beneath Vorkath and walks toward you
  2. Cast Crumble Undead on it immediately — do NOT use any other spell
  3. If the spawn reaches you, you are frozen and Vorkath unleashes a devastating fireball
  4. After killing the spawn, continue DPS on Vorkath

Tip: Do not attack Vorkath while the spawn is alive — you must kill the spawn first. Switch to your magic weapon (or autocast Crumble Undead on your mainhand).

③ Special Attacks — Acid Phase（③ 特殊攻击 — 酸液阶段）

The acid phase is the most dangerous mechanic for beginners:

  1. Vorkath roars and turns the floor into acid puddles — you will see white splats form on the ground
  2. Stop attacking and focus on movement
  3. Walk (not run!) to keep clear tiles beneath you — acid deals 30+ damage per tick if you stand in it
  4. Look for gaps between the acid pools — they form in a pattern
  5. After about 8-10 seconds, Vorkath fires a large fireball that leaves a 3x3 fire zone. Dodge it
  6. Acid clears and normal phase resumes

④ Special Attack Weapons and Spec Timing（④ 特殊攻击武器与技能时机）

Using a special attack weapon at the start of each kill can significantly reduce kill times. The most common setups:

  • Dragon Warhammer: 30% spec chance to reduce Vorkath's Defence by 30%. Use at 100% spec at the start of the fight. Combined with the Bandos Godsword (BGS) for guaranteed Defence reduction.
  • Bandos Godsword: More consistent than DWH — always hits and reduces Defence based on damage dealt. Two BGS specs (50% spec each) at the start is the safest approach.
  • Dragon Claws: Excellent for melee setups. Use as a 50% spec after the spawn phase for massive damage. Four-dragon claw spec combo can deal 80+ damage in one tick cycle.
  • Bone Dagger: Budget option. 75% spec with guaranteed Defence reduction on hit. Not as powerful as BGS or DWH but costs only 30K.

Timing tip: Use your spec immediately after the spawn dies. Vorkath takes 2-3 ticks to resume attacking, giving you a free damage window. Never spec during the acid phase — movement takes priority.

⑤ Death Mechanics and Item Recovery（⑤ 死亡机制与物品回收）

If you die at Vorkath, your items are stored at the Grave of the Lost Adventurer on Ungael. You have 60 minutes to retrieve them. Key points:

  • Always bring a Rune Pouch with 400+ Law Runes and 100+ Dust Runes for Crumble Undead and teleports
  • Death costs 100K GP (item retrieval fee) if you need Torfinn to recover your items
  • Use Ring of Life as a safety net during learning (teleports you at 10% HP)
  • Practice at low risk first: use budget gear until you have 50+ KC without dying

⑤ Advanced: The Woox Walk（⑤ 进阶：Woox 走位法）

① What Is the Woox Walk?（① 什么是 Woox 走位法？）

The Woox Walk is an advanced movement technique named after OSRS content creator Woox. During the acid phase, instead of simply dodging pools, you continue attacking Vorkath while moving in a precise pattern — maintaining full DPS even during the acid special. This increases kills per hour significantly (15-25% more GP/hr) and reduces the time you spend vulnerable during the acid phase.

② How to Execute the Woox Walk（② 如何执行 Woox 走位法）

  1. When the acid phase begins, identify a row of 3-4 safe (non-acid) tiles
  2. Start on the left-most tile of that row
  3. Attack Vorkath → immediately click the tile to your right → your character walks one tile and auto-attacks
  4. Click back to the left tile → repeat back and forth
  5. Use rapid attack style (darts / knives) or crossbow on longrange
  6. With practice, you can woox walk for the entire acid phase without missing a single hit

Key tip: The rhythm is: attack → click tile → attack → click tile → attack... Your character should fire on the tick you move. If you attack first then move, you lose ticks. Practice at the Nightmare Zone or buyable target dummies first.

③ Woox Walk Gear Notes（③ Woox 走位装备说明）

  • Woox walk works best with Ranged weapons (DHCB / Blowpipe / RCB)
  • Melee woox is possible with Lance but harder due to lower attack range — you need to find tiles exactly 2 tiles from Vorkath
  • Blowpipe on rapid loses tick advantage during woox — DHCB is the best woox weapon
  • Use longrange on DHCB during woox phase for better positioning (swap back to rapid after)

④ Common Woox Walk Mistakes（④ 常见的 Woox 走位错误）

  • Moving too early: Wait for the damage splat to appear before clicking the next tile. Moving before the hit registers cancels the attack.
  • Losing your row: If acid spawns on your row, you must find a new 3-tile gap quickly. Keep an eye on the ground for incoming white splats.
  • Over-shooting: Walking 3+ tiles in one direction means you step into unrevealed acid. Stick to 2 tiles back-and-forth.
  • Panic eating: Do not eat during the woox walk — eat before the acid phase starts. Eating interrupts your attack cycle and breaks the rhythm.

⑦ Budget-Friendly Strategies（⑦ 预算友好策略）

① Starting Out — Under 10M Setup（① 起步 — 低于 1000 万配置）

If you have less than 10M in cash, here's how to start farming Vorkath:

  • Weapon: Rune Crossbow with Dragon Ruby Bolts (e) + Diamond Bolts (e). Cost: ~100K total
  • Armor: Full Void (regular — free from Pest Control) + God d'hide boots
  • Shield: Anti-dragon shield from Duke Horacio (free)
  • Necklace: Amulet of Glory or Power amulet
  • Ring: Ring of Wealth
  • Total setup cost: ~1.8M–1M
  • Expected kills/hr: 15–18
  • Expected GP/hr: 3M–2M

② Efficient Progression Path（② 高效进阶路径）

  1. Phase 1 (0–50 KC): Budget RCB setup, focus on learning mechanics. Expect deaths. Do not worry about profit.
  2. Phase 2 (50–400 KC): Upgrade to Blowpipe + Blessed d'hide. Aim for 22+ kills/hr. Save for Rigour.
  3. Phase 3 (400–500 KC): Buy DHCB + Dragonfire Ward + Rigour. You are now in max profit territory.
  4. Phase 4 (500+ KC): Optimize with Woox Walk. Consider DHL for variety or as backup method.

③ Tips to Reduce Death Rate（③ 降低死亡率的技巧）

  • Keep your POH pool (Ornate) as top priority — eliminates suicide runs between kills
  • Eat to 80+ HP before every special attack phase — this gives you a buffer against the fireball + acid combo
  • Turn sound on — Vorkath's roars for acid/spawn phase are distinct audio cues that give you an extra tick to react
  • Keep a Ranging Potion (4) in your inventory; don't use (1) dose pots to minimize juggling
  • If you misclick during acid, teleport out immediately — dying costs 100K and wastes 5+ minutes
  • Use F-keys for fast inventory/prayer switching — muscle memory for F1-F5 will save your life during spawn phase

④ Recommended Quest and Diary Completions（④ 推荐的任务与日记完成）

Several existing quests and diaries make Vorkath farming significantly smoother:

  • Lumbridge & Draynor Hard Diary: Allows you to use the Fairy Ring without a Dramen Staff (BIQ to Ungael)
  • Fremennik Hard Diary: Increases the chance of receiving superior dragon bones as drops
  • Western Provinces Hard Diary: Unlocks Elite Void Knight equipment, the BIS for DHCB method
  • Bone Voyage: Required for the fossil island bank chest (convenient if you prefer banking there)
  • Dream Mentor: Not required but unlocks the Lunar spellbook which has some niche uses at Vorkath

⑤ When to Take a Break（⑤ 何时休息）

Vorkath farming is mentally demanding. After 2-3 hours of continuous kills, reaction time slows and mistakes increase. Signs it is time to stop:

  • You fail the spawn phase twice in a row
  • You take avoidable acid damage
  • Your kill times are 30+ seconds slower than usual
  • You forget to drink a potion or eat food

Taking a 10-minute break every 2 hours will improve your GP/hr more than pushing through fatigue. Stretch, hydrate, and reset before returning.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions（❓ 常见问题）

Is Vorkath worth doing in 2026?（2026 年韦克思还值得打吗？）

Absolutely. Despite being years old, Vorkath remains one of the top 5 consistent GP makers in OSRS. Regular drops maintain their value due to high alch prices, and the Skeletal Visage is still 86M+. Compared to Muspah (unstable supply costs) or Nex (team RNG), Vorkath offers reliable solo profit.

What's better for Vorkath — DHCB or DHL?（打韦克思哪个更好 — DHCB 还是 DHL？）

DHCB is generally better for consistency and the Woox Walk, averaging 30-35 kills/hr. DHL (Lance) offers similar DPS but requires precise melee positioning and makes acid phase harder. DHCB is recommended for 90% of players unless you lack Rigour but have high melee stats.

Do I need Rigour for Vorkath?（打韦克思需要 Rigour 吗？）

Not required, but highly recommended. Rigour adds roughly 15-20% DPS, translating to 1.8M–1.8M extra GP per hour. If you plan 500+ kills, Rigour pays for itself. Without it, you can still make 2M+/hr with Eagle Eye.

How many kills per trip can I expect?（每次行程可以击杀多少次？）

With max DHCB setup + Rigour + Woox Walk: 5-7 kills per trip. With budget RCB: 2-4 kills per trip. Using a POH pool between trips is strongly recommended to maximize kills per hour.

Can I kill Vorkath with 70 Range?（70 级远程能打韦克思吗？）

Yes, but expect slow kills (3-4 minute fight times) and higher supply usage. 80+ Ranged is the practical minimum for comfortable, profitable trips. The extra levels make a significant difference in accuracy and damage.

Should I use Ruby or Diamond bolts?（应该用红宝石还是钻石弩箭？）

Use Dragon Ruby Bolts (e) until Vorkath reaches about 30% HP. Then switch to Dragon Diamond Bolts (e) for the execute phase. Ruby bolts' passive proc deals 20% of current HP (capped at 100) for massive damage early in the fight.

Is Void Knight gear good for Vorkath?（虚空骑士装备适合打韦克思吗？）

Void is excellent for Vorkath specifically because he has low Defence for a boss. Elite Void + DHCB is the meta setup — the accuracy bonus plus the 12.5% damage set effect out-damages Armadyl. For melee (Lance), Bandos is preferred over Void.

What's the fastest way to Ungael?（去恩格尔的最快方式是什么？）

The fastest method is a maxed POH with the Ungael portal unlocked (requires 90 Construction + Ornate Jewellery Box + Portal Nexus). Alternative: Fairy ring BIQ (requires Ardougne Diary for fairy rings without dramen staff) or the boat at Rellekka docks after DS2.

How long does the Skeletal Visage take to get?（骷髅面罩需要多久才能获得？）

At 1/5,000 drop rate, the average player needs about 167 hours of kills (at 30 kills/hr) to see one visage. However, drop rate is pure RNG — some players get it at 12 KC, others go 10,000+ dry. Do not farm Vorkath expecting the visage; treat it as a bonus.

Does the Woox Walk really matter?（Woox 走位法真的重要吗？）

Yes — mastering the Woox Walk adds roughly 3-5 kills per hour (an extra 1.8M–1.5M GP/hr). It also makes the fight safer because you spend less time on the acid phase, reducing overall damage taken. It takes about 50-100 kills of practice to get consistent.

Should I use a Dragonfire Shield or an Anti-Dragon Shield?（应该用龙火盾还是防龙盾？）

Always use a Dragonfire Shield or Dragonfire Ward if you can afford it. The Anti-Dragon Shield offers the same fire protection but no defensive stats. The DFS also allows you to use its special attack for an extra 25% Dragonfire reduction in emergencies. The Ward adds Ranged Strength which directly boosts your DPS.

Can I kill Vorkath on a Slayer Task?（能在 Slayer 任务中击杀韦克思吗？）

Vorkath is not assigned as a Slayer task by any master, but the Blue Dragon task does not include Vorkath. If you want to use a Slayer Helm (i), you need either the Black Mask (which does not work on Vorkath) or a Twisted Slayer Helm (which requires KBD/KQ tasks). In practice, you will always fight Vorkath without Slayer Helm bonuses — Void or Armadyl are the go-to options.

What is the best special attack weapon for Vorkath?（打韦克思的最佳特殊攻击武器是什么？）

For Ranged setups, a Bandos Godsword is the most consistent option. Two BGS specs at the start reduce Vorkath's Defence by 10-30 on average. For Melee (Lance), Dragon Claws are excellent for a 4-tick spec combo. The Dragon Warhammer is high-risk-high-reward — if the 30% spec lands, it is the best Defence reduction in the game, but it misses often.

How do I avoid Vorkath's dragonfire attack?（如何躲避韦克思的龙火攻击？）

You cannot dodge the dragonfire — it is an unavoidable mechanic. Protection comes from three layers: 1) Protect from Magic prayer (reduces damage by 40%), 2) Dragonfire Shield/Ward (absorbs 50-70% of remaining dragonfire damage), and 3) Super Antifire Potion (provides immunity to dragonfire when combined with a shield). With all three active, you take zero damage from standard dragonfire attacks.

Is Vorkath harder than Zulrah or Muspah?（韦克思比祖拉或穆斯帕难吗？）

Vorkath is generally considered easier than Zulrah but comparable to Muspah. Zulrah requires memorizing 4+ rotation patterns and constant prayer switching, while Vorkath has only two special attacks that alternate predictably. Muspah requires similar mechanics (shield phase, freeze phase) to Vorkath but has more RNG elements. Most players find Vorkath the most consistent and least stressful of the three for long farming sessions.

What is the alch value of Vorkath's drops per kill?（韦克思每次击杀掉落的炼金价值是多少？）

The average alch value of Vorkath's regular drop table is approximately 85K–100K GP per kill. Items like Rune Full Helms (alch 21K), Rune Plateskirts (alch 38K), and Dragonhide (alch 6K–8K per hide) provide a stable floor value. This means even if GE prices crash, Vorkath remains profitable through high-alchemy — an important stability factor that bosses like Nex (reliant on unique drops) do not have.

Can I use the Blowpipe on rapid at Vorkath?（打韦克思能用吹管的快速模式吗？）

Yes, and you should — rapid attack style on Blowpipe gives you a 3-tick attack cycle, which is significantly faster than crossbow. However, be mindful that Blowpipe on rapid makes the Woox Walk more difficult because the 3-tick cycle does not align cleanly with walking patterns. Many experienced players use longrange during the acid phase specifically for the Woox Walk and switch back to rapid afterward.

Should I sell my bank for a DHCB?（应该卖掉我的仓库买 DHCB 吗？）

This depends on your overall account progression. If Vorkath will be your primary money maker and you already have Rigour + Elite Void + Dragonfire Ward, selling non-essentials for the DHCB is a good move — the DHCB pays for itself in roughly 50 hours of Vorkath farming. However, do not sell untradeable gear (Fire Cape, Fighter Torso, Barrows Gloves) or essential items for questing. A good rule: if selling leaves you with less than 50M in non-Vorkath gear, keep saving.

How does Vorkath compare to the Phantom Muspah for money?（韦克思与幻影穆斯帕赚钱对比如何？）

Both are excellent money makers, but they target different playstyles. Vorkath has a lower skill floor, more predictable mechanics, and a higher GP floor (consistent alchables). Muspah has higher potential GP/hr (up to 5M+ with the Venator Shard) but requires more attention and has higher variance. For steady, brain-off profit, Vorkath wins. For active farming with higher ceiling, Muspah is competitive. Many mid-game players farm both, alternating based on mood.

What happens if I run out of prayer at Vorkath?（打韦克思时祈祷用完了会怎样？）

Running out of prayer is dangerous because dragonfire will hit through your defences. Always bring 2-3 prayer potions minimum. If you run low, consider using Piety/Rigour sparingly during the last 30% of Vorkath's HP — the kill is almost over and conserving prayer for Protect from Magic is more important than the DPS boost. Keep an emergency dose in your inventory that you never touch unless prayer drops below 15 points.

How do I practice Vorkath without risking gear?（如何在不冒险装备的情况下练习打韦克思？）

You can practice Vorkath mechanics in the Nightmare Zone using a practice mode, but it does not perfectly replicate the fight. A better method: use budget gear (Rune Crossbow, regular Void, Anti-Dragon Shield) for your first 20-30 kills. The supply cost is under 15K per death, and you learn all the timing without risking expensive items. Once you can go 10 consecutive kills without dying, upgrade your gear.

Is it worth using a Ring of Suffering at Vorkath?（打韦克思值得用苦难之戒吗？）

The Ring of Suffering (i) is an excellent choice for Vorkath, especially during the learning phase. Its passive damage recoil adds 10-15 damage per kill from Vorkath's rapid attacks, and the double hitpoints bonus from the (i) version gives you a larger safety buffer. The Archers Ring (i) offers better offensive stats for experienced players, but most people prefer the Suffering for its survivability and quality-of-life benefits.
