1. Method: Collecting Cowhides + Bones (Lumbridge)（方法：收集牛皮+骨头（Lumbridge））

StatDetail
Profit40,000–60,000 GP/hr
RequirementsNone. Walk from Lumbridge spawn.
LocationCow field east of Lumbridge (follow the path east from the castle)
StrategyKill cows, pick up cowhides (150-400 GP each) AND bones (80-120 GP each). Full inventory of 28 hides + bones = ~3,000-5,000 GP per trip. Bank at Lumbridge Castle bank chest. Each trip takes 3-5 minutes.

This is the classic "first day in OSRS" method. The cow field is eternally populated by low-level players, so cows die instantly. You can simply pick up hides that other players leave on the ground (many F2P players don't bother picking up hides, focusing only on combat XP). Stand in the cow field and right-click loot — you'll fill an inventory in 30 seconds during busy hours.

2. Method: Mining Rune Essence（方法：开采符文矿石）

StatDetail
Profit30,000–50,000 GP/hr
RequirementsRune Mysteries quest (10 minutes, no stats needed)
LocationRune Essence Mine (teleport via Aubury in Varrock or Sedridor in Wizard's Tower)
StrategyComplete Rune Mysteries (talk to Duke Horacio in Lumbridge Castle). Then teleport to the Rune Essence Mine, mine a full inventory (essence mines instantly at any Mining level), teleport back via the portal, and bank at Varrock East bank. Rune essence sells for 8-15 GP each on the GE. 1,000 essence = 8,000-15,000 GP.
📦 Bulk Selling Premium: Rune essence sells faster and at better prices in bulk. Accumulate 10,000+ essence before selling. The GE buy limit for essence is 25,000 every 4 hours, so large sellers can command a premium of 2-5 GP per essence over market price.

3. Method: Picking Up Loot at the Wilderness Ditch（方法：在荒野沟渠捡拾战利品）

StatDetail
Profit50,000–400,000+ GP/hr (highly variable)
RequirementsNone
LocationEdgeville Wilderness ditch (level 1 Wilderness border)
StrategyStand near the Wilderness ditch in Edgeville where PvP players fight. When players die, dropped items appear on the ground. Pick them up. This is a "vulture" method — you profit from other players' PvP deaths. Don't cross the ditch yourself (you'll enter PvP).

This method spikes on weekends and during peak hours when PvP worlds are active. You can find rune armor pieces, amulets, food, and occasionally valuable items like Berserker Helms or Obby Capes that PKers drop. The key is patience — sometimes you'll wait 10 minutes with nothing, then find 1.8M in loot from a single fight. Never cross the ditch or you become a target yourself.

4. Method: Picking Bananas at Musa Point (Karamja)（方法：在Musa点摘香蕉（Karamja））

StatDetail
Profit20,000–30,000 GP/hr
Requirements30 GP (for the boat to Karamja)
LocationMusa Point banana plantation, Karamja
StrategyTake the boat from Port Sarim to Karamja (30 GP). Pick bananas from the plantation (unlimited spawn, 5 seconds to fill inventory). Use the Port Sarim deposit box at the docks to bank. Bananas sell for 100-150 GP each. 28 bananas = 2,800-4,400 GP per trip.

Honestly, this is one of the slower methods — but it's included because it's truly zero-skill and requires no combat. It's meditative and consistent. Better for ultra-casual players who just want to zone out and click banana trees.

5. Method: Bone Yard Collecting (Wilderness)（方法：骨场收集（荒野））

StatDetail
Profit80,000–120,000 GP/hr
RequirementsNone (but high risk of death)
LocationBone Yard, level 20-25 Wilderness (north of Varrock)
StrategyRun to the Bone Yard in level ~20 Wilderness (north of Varrock, east of the Lava Maze). Big bones and regular bones spawn on the ground. Pick up an inventory and run south to level 1 Wilderness before teleporting. Big bones sell for 400-400 GP each. A full inventory (28) = 8,400-11,400 GP. Each trip takes 5-8 minutes.
⚠️ Wilderness Warning: The Bone Yard is in multi-combat Wilderness where PKers patrol. You WILL die occasionally. Never bring anything you're not willing to lose (wear nothing, bring no gold). If you see a white dot on the minimap, run south immediately. This method is high-risk/high-reward — expect to lose some trips to PKers, but the profit per trip makes up for it over time.

6. Method: Enchanting Sapphire Rings (Semi-AFK Magic Profit)（方法：附魔蓝宝石戒指（半挂机魔法收益））

StatDetail
Profit400,000–500,000 GP/hr
RequirementsLevel 7 Magic (Lvl-1 Enchant), some starting GP
LocationGrand Exchange
StrategyBuy sapphire rings from the GE (~500-600 GP each). Cast Lvl-1 Enchant on them to create Rings of Recoil. Sell the enchanted rings for ~900-1,400 GP each. Each cast takes 2 ticks (1.2 seconds). At 2,000+ casts per hour, profit is 1.8M-1.5M GP/hr. This requires starting capital (~100K GP to buy rings in bulk).

This is the single best low-requirement F2P money maker once you have a small cash stack. It's semi-AFK — click ring, click spell, repeat. The buy limit for sapphire rings is high enough that you can buy 10,000+ at a time. Pair this with watching videos or other idle activities.

8. Common F2P Money Making Mistakes（常见免费赚钱错误）

  1. Grinding for a bond in F2P. A bond costs 12M-15M GP. At 50K GP/hr (optimistic F2P rate), that's 240-400 hours of grinding. Pay for one month of membership with real money instead — once you're a member, you can earn 1.8M-1M GP/hr with minimal stats.
  2. Ignoring stackable/clue-related items. Many F2P drops (limpwurt roots, uncut gems, law runes, nature runes) are used heavily in members content and sell for more than their apparent value. Check GE prices before dropping anything.
  3. Training combat before making money. You don't need combat stats to make money. A level 3 account with 0 XP can collect cowhides just as fast as a level 50 account. Make your money first, then buy gear to train with.
  4. Not using the Grand Exchange efficiently. Always sell items at the GE price, not below. Check the GE tracker or use the built-in GE price guide. Many new players undercut by 20-30% thinking it's necessary — it's not for high-volume items.
  5. Forgetting to do the Stronghold of Security. The Stronghold of Security (Barbarian Village) awards a free 10,000 GP at the end, plus fancy boots, fighting boots, and a fancy sword. It requires zero stats and takes 15 minutes. This is the single fastest way to earn your first 10K on a new account.

9. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: What's the absolute fastest way to earn 100K on a brand new F2P account?

A: Do the Stronghold of Security first (free 10K in 15 minutes). Then spend 40K on a staff of fire + mind/air runes. Buy sapphire rings with the remaining 60K. Cast Lvl-1 Enchant on them to make Rings of Recoil, selling the enchanted rings for ~400 GP profit each. You'll turn 60K into ~100K in 30-45 minutes. Total time from account creation to 100K: roughly 1 hour.

Q: Can I flip items on the GE with no stats?

A: Yes, but you need starting capital. Flipping involves buying items at a low price and selling at a higher price, profiting from the margin. With 100K starting GP, focus on high-volume items with stable margins: rune essence, cowhides, logs, and runes. Check margins by buying 1 item at instant-buy price, then selling it at instant-sell price. The difference is your potential profit per flip.

Q: Is there any F2P method that makes 1.8M+ per hour?

A: Not without significant requirements. High-alching (55 Magic) can make 1.8M-1.8M GP/hr. Cutting yew logs (60 Woodcutting) makes 60K-100K GP/hr. Enchanting sapphire/emerald rings (7 or 27 Magic) with good GE margins can push 1.8M-1.8M GP/hr but is limited by buy limits. No true zero-stat F2P method exceeds 120K GP/hr consistently. For 1.8M+ GP/hr, you need membership.

💰 Start Small, Grow Steady

F2P money making with no stats is slow — there's no way around it. But it also teaches you the fundamentals of OSRS's economy: what items are valuable, how to find margins, and how to consistently grind toward a goal. Every OSRS billionaire started at Lumbridge killing cows for hides.

Use these methods to earn your first 1.8M-1M GP, buy starter gear, and train toward members money makers. Or bite the bullet and buy one month of membership — the GP you'll earn in members more than justifies the $12.50. Either way, you're on the path now. 💰
