1. The ROI Concept for Skill Training
（1. 技能训练的投资回报率概念）

In OSRS, you have a finite amount of time to play. Every hour you spend training a skill is an hour you're NOT spending doing something else. The question is: which skills give you the most return for every hour invested?

This isn't about which skills are "fun" or "cool" — it's about account value efficiency. If you only have 100 hours to play this month, which skills should you train to get the most powerful account possible?

📊 What "Account Value" Means (Our Scoring System)
（账户价值含义，我们的评分系统）

To rank skills objectively, we scored each one across 4 dimensions:

① Content Unlocks (40% of score)（内容解锁，评分的40%）

How many quests, areas, bosses, and activities does this skill unlock? A skill that unlocks 10 quests and 5 bosses is more valuable than one that only unlocks 1 quest.

Example: 70 Agility unlocks the Ardougne Cloak 4 teleport (shortcut to Ardougne). That's a massive time-saver for many activities. Agility scores high on content unlocks.（示例：70敏捷解锁Ardougne披风4传送）

② Money-Making Potential (30% of score)（赚钱潜力，评分的30%）

Can this skill make you GP? And if so, how much per hour? A skill that lets you make 1M GP/h is more valuable than one that makes 100K GP/h (because GP lets you buy gear, supplies, and membership).

Example: 99 Fishing at Minnows gives ~300K GP/h. That's useful, but 99 Slayer gives ~800K-1.2M GP/h. Slayer scores higher on money-making potential.（示例：99钓鱼约30万金币/小时，但99杀戮者约80-120万金币/小时）

③ Time Savings (20% of score)（时间节省，评分的20%）

Does this skill save you time in other activities? Teleports, shortcuts, and utility effects count here.

Example: 66 Magic unlocks the Camelot Teleport. If you do a lot of activities in the Seers' Village area, this saves you ~30 seconds per trip. Over 100 trips, that's ~50 minutes saved. Magic scores high on time savings.（示例：66魔法解锁Camelot传送，每次节省约30秒）

④ Combat Power (10% of score)（战斗能力，评分的10%）

Does this skill make your character hit harder, take less damage, or kill monsters faster? This is especially important for PvM-focused players.

Example: 99 Strength gives +12 max hit compared to 1 Strength. That's a ~40% damage increase. Strength scores high on combat power.（示例：99力量比1力量多+12最大伤害，约40%伤害提升）

🧮 The ROI Formula
（ROI计算公式）

Our ranking uses this formula:

📐 ROI Score = (Content Unlocks × 0.4) + (Money-Making × 0.3) + (Time Savings × 0.2) + (Combat Power × 0.1) ÷ Hours to Train

Skills with a high ROI score give lots of value and take relatively few hours to train. Skills with a low ROI score either give little value or take hundreds of hours to train.

💡 Key Insight（关键洞察）

The most important factor isn't how "fast" a skill is to train — it's how much account power you get per hour. A skill that takes 10 hours to reach "useful" levels and unlocks massive content is higher ROI than a skill that takes 200 hours to reach 99 and only gives marginal benefits.

💡 The "Main Account" vs "Ironman" Difference（💡 "主号"vs"铁人"的区别）

If you're an Ironman (no GE access), all these skills become much higher priority because you have to gather/make everything yourself.

6. FAQ — Skill Priority Questions
（6. 常见问题——技能优先级问答）

I only have 1 hour per day to play. Which skills should I focus on?（我每天只能玩1小时，应该重点训练哪些技能？）

With limited time, focus on high-impact, low-time-investment skills: Prayer (1-2 hours to 43), Magic (3-5 hours to 55 for High Alchemy), and Agility (12-18 hours to 60 for Graceful). These give massive account value for relatively few hours invested. Avoid long grinds like 99 Slayer (150-250 hours) until you have more playtime available.（时间有限时，专注于高影响力、低时间投入的技能）

Should I follow this ranking exactly, or can I train skills in any order?（我应该严格遵循这个排名吗？还是可以按任何顺序训练技能？）

This ranking is a guideline, not a strict rule. If you enjoy training Fishing, go ahead and train Fishing! The point of this guide is to help you avoid wasting time on low-ROI skills when you have limited time. If you have unlimited time and just want to enjoy the game, train skills in any order you like.

Why is Defence ranked so low? Isn't higher Defence essential for surviving?（为什么防御排名这么低？更高的防御不是生存必备吗？）

Defence is ranked low because it's usually more efficient to train Attack and Strength first, then add Defence later. A player with 75 Attack / 75 Strength / 40 Defence hits much harder (and kills monsters faster) than a player with 40 Attack / 40 Strength / 75 Defence. Since time = GP in OSRS, faster kills = more GP/h. Defence is still important, but it's usually the last Combat skill you should max out.

I'm an Ironman. Should I follow the Main ranking or the Ironman-adjusted one?（我是铁人模式，应该遵循主号排名还是铁人调整排名？）

Follow the Ironman-adjusted ranking (Section 5 of this guide). As an Ironman, you can't buy supplies from the GE, so gathering/production skills (Farming, Fishing, Cooking, Herblore, Mining, Smithing) become much higher priority. The Main ranking assumes you can just buy whatever you need, which isn't an option for you.

How often does the "optimal" training order change? Should I worry about updates making this guide obsolete?（"最佳"训练顺序多久变化一次？我是否需要担心更新让本指南过时？）

The core principles (Prayer is #1, Magic is #2, etc.) have been stable for years. Major updates like the Summer Sweep-Up 2026 can shift some rankings (like Agility moving up due to new courses), but the changes are usually incremental, not revolutionary. This guide will be updated after any major balance changes — check back if a large update drops.

Can I "skip" low-ROI skills entirely? Never train them?（我能完全"跳过"低ROI技能吗？永远不训练它们？）

You can skip them for a long time, but most skills eventually become useful or required. For example, you might skip Construction for 100 hours of gameplay, but eventually you'll want POH teleports for convenience. The point of the ROI ranking isn't to never train low-ROI skills — it's to delay them until after you've trained the high-ROI ones.
