⚔️ 1. What Is Slayer & Why Do It?（什么是Slayer及为何要做？）

Slayer is a combat skill where you kill specific monsters assigned by a Slayer Master. Each task gives you Slayer XP (for the skill) plus regular Combat XP (Attack/Strength/Defence/Ranged/Magic) from the kills themselves.

But the real reason to do Slayer: Profit. Many Slayer tasks drop valuable loot — Rune items, herbs, seeds, dragonhide — that you can sell on the Grand Exchange.

💰 Why Slayer Is the Best Money Maker for Mid-Level Players（为何Slayer是中级玩家最佳赚钱方式）

  • 70+ Combat: 200K–400K GP/hour from task loot
  • 80+ Combat: 400K–700K GP/hour (GWD tasks, boss tasks)
  • Combined XP: Train Slayer + Combat + HP all at once
  • Unlocks: Higher Slayer level unlocks better masters and boss tasks
  • Variety: Every task is different — never gets boring

Compare this to other money makers: Cowhide collecting (50K GP/hour) or mining iron (100K GP/hour). Slayer is 2–5x more profitable, and you're training skills simultaneously.

💡 When to Start Slayer（何时开始Slayer）

Start Slayer as soon as you have 40 Combat (recommended) or finished all F2P quests. You can start earlier (at 20 Combat with Turael), but the tasks will be very easy and low-profit. Waiting until 40 Combat gives you access to better masters and tasks.

📋 2. Slayer Requirements (When to Start)（Slayer要求·何时开始）

You need two things before starting Slayer:

① Complete "The Smuggler" Quest（完成"走私者"任务）

The Smuggler is the tutorial quest for Slayer. It's very short (5 minutes) and unlocks the ability to receive Slayer tasks.

How to start: Talk to The Smuggler in the Smuggler's Den (south of Falador, entrance at the Falador cabbage patch). He gives you a simple task to kill 5 goblins, then unlocks Slayer assignments.

② Meet the Combat Requirement for Your Chosen Master（满足所选大师的战斗等级要求）

🎯 Recommended First Master: Vannaka (40 Combat)（推荐首位大师：Vannaka·40战斗）

Vannaka gives the best balance of XP + profit for beginners. His tasks include monsters like Hill Giants, Moss Giants, Blue Dragons, and Turoth — all profitable and good combat XP. Avoid Turael (too easy/low XP) and Mazchna (also low profit).

🗺️ 3. Your First Slayer Master — How to Choose（第一个Slayer大师·如何选择）

If you're below 40 Combat, start with Turael in Burthorpe. His tasks are things like "kill 5 goblins" or "kill 10 cows" — very easy, but almost no profit.

Once you hit 40 Combat, switch to Vannaka immediately. Here's how to get to him:

Step 1: Get to Edgeville（步骤1：到达Edgeville）

Teleport to Lumbridge (starting teleport), then run north to the Edgeville teleport lever, or walk north from Varrock. Once in Edgeville, enter the Edgeville Dungeon (manhole north of the bank).

Step 2: Find Vannaka（步骤2：找到Vannaka）

Inside Edgeville Dungeon, run south. Vannaka is in a small room with a combat training dummy. Talk to him and select "Assign me a task, please."

Step 3: Get Your First Task（步骤3：获取第一个任务）

Vannaka will give you a task like "Kill 40 Hill Giants" or "Kill 30 Moss Giants." Accept it, then check your Quest List (hotkey: Alt+Q) — there's a Slayer tab that shows your current task and progress.

② How to Skip Bad Tasks（如何跳过差任务）

Some tasks are slow or unprofitable (e.g., "Kill 120 bats"). You can skip a task by right-clicking your Slayer Master and selecting "Cancel task" (costs 0 GP at low level, more at higher levels).

Recommended: Only skip tasks that are very slow (bats, birds, rats) or dangerous (Suqahs, Kurasks if you're low level). Most tasks are worth doing for the XP.

💡 Use Turael to Skip Duradel Tasks (High-Level Trick)（用Turael跳过Duradel任务·高级技巧）

At high Slayer levels (Nieve/Duradel), you can go to Turael in Burthorpe and ask him to assign you an easy task. Completing Turael's task "resets" your Duradel task without the cancel cost. This is the best way to avoid bad tasks at high level.

③ Tasks to Skip (Low Profit)（应跳过的任务·低利润）

These tasks have very low profit or are extremely slow. Consider skipping them:

  • Bats / Birds / Rats: Low HP, low drops, waste of time
  • Desert Lizards: Require Ice coolers, low profit
  • Jungle Horrors: Slow, low drops
  • Cave Bugs: Low XP, low profit

🎯 Pro Tip: Loot Everything（专业建议：捡起所有掉落）

Many beginners make the mistake of only picking up "valuable" drops. Pick up everything. Herbs (Guam → Ranarr), seeds (snapdragon, torstol), and secondary ingredients add up. A single Ranarr weed is worth ~7K GP. Always check what monsters drop before skipping a task.

  • Melee: Abyssal whip + Dragon defender + Barrows armor (Abyssal whip from 70 Attack)

💡 Use the Best Combat Style for Each Task（为每个任务使用最佳战斗风格）

Different monsters are weak to different attack styles. Check the Combat Triangle (Melee strong vs Ranged, Ranged strong vs Magic, Magic strong vs Melee). Use the style the monster is weak to for faster kills and less food usage.

② Slayer Unlocks (After 50 Slayer)（Slayer解锁·50 Slayer后）

At 50 Slayer, you can unlock the Slayer Helmet by combining various Slayer items. The Slayer Helmet gives +15% accuracy and damage against your current Slayer task — it's the best helm for Slayer, period.

How to Make a Slayer Helmet（如何制作Slayer头盔）

Step 1: Complete "Smoking Kills" quest (unlocks Slayer points).

Step 2: Earn 400 Slayer points (about 25–30 tasks).

Step 3: Buy "Unlock Slayer Helmet" from any Slayer Master (400 points).

Step 4: Combine a Facemask + Earmuffs + Nose peg + Spiny helmet + Reinforced goggles on a Slayer Helmet creation tool (bought from any master for 1 GP).

⚡ 7. 7 Slayer Tips for Faster Progress（7个Slayer快速进步技巧）

① Use Cannon on Multi-Combat Tasks（多战斗任务使用加农炮）

A Dwarf Multicannon can double your kill speed on tasks with multiple monsters (e.g., Dagannoths, Kalphites). It costs GP to refill (about 1K GP per 20 minutes), but the XP and loot more than make up for it.

② Bring a Looting Bag (Wilderness Tasks)（荒野任务带掠夺袋）

If you're doing tasks assigned by Krystilia (Wilderness Slayer), bring a Looting bag to store extra loot. It allows you to carry 28 more inventory slots worth of items.

③ Use House Teleports to Bank Faster（用房屋传送加快银行存取）

Build a Teleport to House tablet (or use a rune to cast Teleport to House). In your POH, have a Jewellery Box (to teleport to places like Slayer positions) and a Spirit Tree (to bank at Tree Gnome Stronghold). This cuts banking time in half.

④ Level Prayer to 43 ASAP（尽快将Prayer升到43）

With Protect From Melee/Missiles/Magic, you take zero damage from most Slayer tasks. This means you never have to eat food, saving massive amounts of GP and inventory space. Do Slayer with Protect prayers — it's a game-changer.

⑤ Use the Best Weapon You Can Afford（用你能负担的最好武器）

A Dragon scimitar (60 Attack) is ~100K GP and doubles your kill speed vs a Steel scimitar. An Abyssal whip (70 Attack) is ~2.5M GP but increases DPS by another 30%. These are investments that pay for themselves in 2–3 tasks.

⑥ Don't Waste Time on Low-Value Tasks（不要在低价值任务上浪费时间）

If a task takes 30 minutes and gives 50K GP in loot, that's 100K GP/hour. If you skip it and get a task that gives 300K GP/hour, you're much better off. Your time is valuable — optimize it.

⑦ Join a Clan for Help（加入公会寻求帮助）

Many Slayer tasks are easier (and more fun) with a partner. Join an OSRS clan (through the in-game Clan system) and ask for help with tough tasks. High-level players often enjoy helping beginners.

❓ 8. FAQ（常见问题）

What's the fastest way to get 99 Slayer?（最快获取99 Slayer方法？）

Duradel tasks (95 Combat+) with Guthan's armor set (heals you per hit) and a Slayer Helmet. Use the highest DPS weapon you can afford (Abyssal whip → Bludgeon → Scythe of Vitur). Expect 70–90 hours to 99 Slayer from 75 Slayer.

Should I use Melee, Ranged, or Magic for Slayer?（Slayer应用近战、远程还是魔法？）

Mix of all three. Melee is best for most tasks (higher DPS, cheaper). Ranged is best for tasks where the monster is dangerous up close (e.g., Kurasks, Turoth). Magic is best for tasks where you can safely cast from a distance (e.g., Dagannoths, Fire giants).

Can F2P players do Slayer?（F2P玩家能做Slayer吗？）

Yes, but only up to a point. F2P Slayer is very limited — you can only use Turael and the tasks are weak (goblins, cows, rats). For real Slayer profit and XP, you need membership.

How many Slayer points do I need for the Slayer Helmet?（Slayer头盔需要多少Slayer点数？）

400 points for the "Unlock Slayer Helmet" upgrade. You earn points by completing tasks: 0 points for tasks 1–4, 5 points for tasks 5–9, 15 points per task after that. At 15 points per task, you need 27 tasks (about 20–30 hours of gameplay).

What should I do if I die during a Slayer task?（Slayer任务中死亡怎么办？）

If you die in a safe area (most Slayer locations), you keep your items (they appear at your grave). Run back to retrieve them. If you die in the Wilderness, you lose all unprotected items. Always bring a Teleport to House tablet or Ectophial for emergency escapes.
