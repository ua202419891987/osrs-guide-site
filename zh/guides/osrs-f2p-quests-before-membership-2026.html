1. 🎯 Why Finish F2P Quests Before Buying Membership（为什么先完成F2P任务再买会员）

Buying membership is exciting. You want to teleport straight to the Grand Exchange, grab your best gear, and start exploring member areas. But if you skip the free-to-play quests, you are making a costly mistake — and here is exactly why.

1.1 ⏱️ Every Minute of Membership Costs GP or Real Money（会员每分钟都花金币或真钱）

Membership in 2026 costs $14.99/month or roughly 5.2M GP per bond. That means every hour you spend doing F2P quests as a member costs you about 2 cents in real money or 30,000 GP in bond value. More importantly, those hours could be spent on members-only content that actually makes you money.

If you spend 3 hours doing Cook's Assistant and Doric's Quest as a member, you have wasted 3 hours of your 14-day bond — hours you cannot get back.

1.2 📈 F2P Quests Give You a Level Head Start（F2P任务给等级领先优势）

Many F2P quests reward XP that completely skips the slowest, most boring early levels of skills. For example:

  • The Knight's Sword jumps you from level 1 to level 29 Smithing instantly — saving over an hour of mining and smithing bronze and iron items.
  • The Restless Ghost takes you from level 1 to level 9 Prayer in 5 minutes — skipping the tedious process of burying regular bones.
  • Witch's Potion gives enough Magic XP to cast Fire Strike, which is your best early-game combat spell.

These XP rewards are even more valuable when you consider that as a member, those early levels would still require you to use the same slow F2P methods — there is no "members speed-up" for levels 1-20.

1.3 🎫 Quest Points Are Required for Members Quests（任务点数是会员任务的前置要求）

Many essential members quests have Quest Point (QP) requirements. Dragon Slayer alone requires 32 QP just to start. By completing F2P quests first, you build a QP foundation so you can jump straight into members quests without backtracking.

Some examples of members quests with QP requirements:

  • Lost City — 31 QP (unlocks Dragon weapons)
  • Monkey Madness I — no direct QP requirement, but requires The Grand Tree (which requires completion of other quests)
  • Recipe for Disaster — 175 QP for the full quest line (the best gloves in the game)

1.4 🛡️ Unlock Essential Items and Areas（解锁必备物品和区域）

Several F2P quests unlock items or areas you will use even as a high-level member:

  • Rune Mysteries — unlocks the ability to mine Rune Essence and craft runes (essential for any account)
  • Demon Slayer — unlocks Silverlight, which upgrades to Darklight (used against demons in members)
  • Prince Ali Rescue — unlocks free passage through the Al Kharid gate (saves you 10gp every trip, and you will make hundreds of trips)
💡 Pro Tip（专业提示）

The Dragon Slayer quest gives 18,650 Strength XP and 18,650 Defence XP — that is enough to go from level 1 to level 33 in both skills. Do it as F2P so you enter members with a real combat baseline.

2. 🗺️ Must-Do F2P Quest List (Priority Order)（必做F2P任务清单 · 按优先级）

Below are the F2P quests you should complete, ranked by priority. Priority is based on XP rewards, unlock value, and speed. Quests marked ★ Essential should be done before anything else.

2.1 🍳 Cook's Assistant — ★ Essential (Top Priority)（厨师助手 · 最高优先级）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~2 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards300 Cooking XP · 500 GP
Start LocationLumbridge Castle kitchen

It takes 2 minutes — literally. You grab an egg (near the chicken coop across the river), a bucket of milk (Lumbridge cow field), and a pot of flour (Lumbridge mill, north of the castle). Bring them to the cook in Lumbridge Castle and you are done.

Why do it before membership: The 300 Cooking XP is small, but it unlocks access to the cook's range (burns less food). Plus, 1 QP for 2 minutes is the best QP-per-minute ratio in the game. There is zero reason to skip this.

2.2 ⛏️ Doric's Quest — ★ Essential（多里克的请求 · 必做）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~4 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone (15 Mining recommended)
Rewards1,300 Mining XP · 180 GP · Use of Doric's anvils
Start LocationDoric's house, north of Falador

Doric needs 6 clay, 4 copper ore, and 2 iron ore. You can mine all of this in the Dwarven Mine just north of his house. The 1,300 Mining XP takes you from level 1 to level 10 Mining — skipping the painful bronze pickaxe grind.

2.3 👻 The Restless Ghost — ★ Essential（不安的灵魂 · 必做）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~5 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards1,125 Prayer XP · Amulet of Ghostspeak
Start LocationLumbridge church (Father Aereck)

Talk to Father Aereck in the Lumbridge church, go to the graveyard south of Lumbridge, retrieve the ghost's skull from the Wizards' Tower, and return it. Done in 5 minutes.

The 1,125 Prayer XP takes you from level 1 to level 9 Prayer — skipping 100+ regular bone burials. The Amulet of Ghostspeak is required for several members quests including Animal Magnetism.

2.4 🧪 Witch's Potion — ★ Essential（女巫的药水 · 必做）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~5 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards325 Magic XP
Start LocationHetty the witch, Rimmington

Talk to Hetty in Rimmington. She needs an onion, burnt meat (cook raw rat meat until burnt), eye of newt (buy from Port Sarim magic shop for 3gp), and a rat's tail (kill any rat). Bring all four items back and you are done.

Why do it before membership: 325 Magic XP takes you from level 1 to level 5 Magic — unlocking Water Strike and the ability to use basic teleports.

2.5 🐑 Sheep Shearer — ★ Essential（剪羊毛 · 必做）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~3 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards150 Crafting XP · 60 GP
Start LocationLumbridge sheep field (north of the castle)

Shear 20 sheep. Bring the wool to the spinning wheel in Lumbridge Castle (second floor). Spin 20 balls of wool. Return to the farmer. Three minutes, tops.

2.6 👺 Goblin Diplomacy — ★ High Priority（哥布林外交 · 高优先级）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~5 minutes
Quest Points5 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards200 Crafting XP · 1 Gold Bar
Start LocationGoblin Village, north of Falador

Two goblin generals are fighting over armor color. You need to collect blue, orange, and brown dyes (buy from the dye shop in Lumbridge or make yourself), and bring the dyed armor to the generals.

Why do it before membership: This quest gives 5 QP — the highest QP reward of any novice F2P quest. It is more than 15% of what you need for Dragon Slayer.

2.7 🐔 Ernest the Chicken — ★ High Priority（欧内斯特的鸡 · 高优先级）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~15 minutes
Quest Points4 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards300 GP · Access to Draynor Manor
Start LocationDraynor Manor (Veronica)

Ernest has been turned into a chicken by Professor Oddenstein in Draynor Manor. You need to navigate the manor, collect a pressure gauge, rubber tube, and oil can, fix the machine, and turn Ernest back.

Why do it before membership: 4 QP is substantial. Draynor Manor becomes a useful location — the spade spawn there saves you from buying one.

2.8 🔍 Misthalin Mystery — ★ Medium Priority（米斯塔林谜案 · 中优先级）

📋 Quest Quick Facts（任务快速事实）

DifficultyNovice
Length~15 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards600 Crafting XP · 250 GP · Access to the Gemstone Grotto
Start LocationLumbridge Castle

This murder mystery-style quest takes place in Lumbridge. You investigate a crime scene, collect clues, and identify the culprit. The story is genuinely well-written.

4. ⚡ Fastest Route: Complete All F2P Quests in 3 Hours（最快路线：3小时完成所有F2P任务）

Optimized route for speedrunning all 18 F2P quests. This route minimizes walking time by grouping quests by geographic region. Total estimated time: 2.5–3 hours.

4.1 📍 Phase 1 — Lumbridge Cluster (~40 minutes)（第一阶段 · 兰布里奇区 · ~40分钟）

🏰 Lumbridge Area Quests (Start Here)（兰布里奇区域任务 · 从这里开始）

  1. Cook's Assistant (2 min) — Start at Lumbridge Castle kitchen.
  2. Sheep Shearer (3 min) — Sheep are north of the castle.
  3. The Restless Ghost (5 min) — Church is in Lumbridge. Skull is at Wizards' Tower south.
  4. Misthalin Mystery (15 min) — Also in Lumbridge Castle.

4.2 📍 Phase 2 — Draynor & Falador (~45 minutes)（第二阶段 · 德雷洛与法尔多尔 · ~45分钟）

🏘️ Draynor and Falador Area Quests（德雷洛与法尔多尔区域任务）

  1. Ernest the Chicken (15 min) — Draynor Manor.
  2. Doric's Quest (4 min) — North from Falador to Doric's house.
  3. Goblin Diplomacy (5 min) — Goblin Village just north of Doric's.
  4. Black Knights' Fortress (12 min) — On the way back from Goblin Village.
  5. The Knight's Sword (15 min) — Start in Falador Castle.

4.3 📍 Phase 3 — Rimmington & Port Sarim (~35 minutes)（第三阶段 · 雷明顿与萨里姆港 · ~35分钟）

⚓ Port Sarim and Western Area Quests（萨里姆港与西部区域任务）

  1. Witch's Potion (5 min) — Hetty in Rimmington.
  2. Imp Catcher (10 min) — Wizard Mizgog in Wizards' Tower.
  3. Rune Mysteries (7 min) — Also at the Wizards' Tower.
  4. Pirate's Treasure (10 min) — Starts in Port Sarim at the pub.

4.4 📍 Phase 4 — Al Kharid & Varrock (~40 minutes)（第四阶段 · 阿尔卡里德与瓦洛克 · ~40分钟）

🏜️ Al Kharid and Varrock Area Quests（阿尔卡里德与瓦洛克区域任务）

  1. Prince Ali Rescue (15 min) — Al Kharid palace.
  2. Demon Slayer (15 min) — Varrock Square.
  3. Shield of Arrav (10 min) — Need partner.
  4. Vampyre Slayer (15 min) — Draynor Village, Draynor Manor basement.

4.5 📍 Phase 5 — Dragon Slayer (~30 minutes)（第五阶段 · 屠龙者 · ~30分钟）

🐉 Final Boss: Dragon Slayer（最终Boss：屠龙者）

  1. Talk to the Guildmaster in the Champions' Guild (south of Varrock). Need 32 QP to enter.
  2. Collect the 3 map pieces: Melzar's Maze, Port Sarim dungeon, Oracle on Ice Mountain.
  3. Buy or make an anti-dragon shield (free from Duke of Lumbridge).
  4. Equip the anti-dragon shield, bring your best food, fight Elvarg in Crandor dungeon.
  5. Return the head to the Guildmaster. You can now wear Rune Platebody and Green D'hide Body.
💡 Speedrun Tips（速通提示）
  • Buy all quest items before starting the route.
  • Use the Lumbridge Home Teleport between phases.
  • Do Shield of Arrav last if you cannot find a partner quickly.

5. 🎁 Post-Quest: What to Do Immediately After Buying Membership（任务后：购买会员后立即做什么）

You have finished all F2P quests. You have 44 QP, level 30+ combat stats, and a solid foundation. Here is exactly what to do in your first hour as a member:

5.1 🚀 Hour 1: Unlock Critical Members Teleports（第一小时：解锁关键会员传送）

Step 1: Do Druidic Ritual (10 minutes)（第1步：完成德鲁伊仪式）

Unlocks the Herblore skill. Start in Taverley (walk west from Falador).

Step 2: Start the Ardougne Easy Diary（第2步：开始阿多格内简单日记）

The Ardougne cloak 1 gives unlimited teleports to the Ardougne Monastery.

Step 3: Complete Tree Gnome Village (15 minutes)（第3步：完成树精村庄）

Unlocks spirit trees. Combined with the Ardougne cloak teleport, you can now reach key locations in seconds.

5.2 💰 Hour 2-3: Start Making GP（第2-3小时：开始赚钱）

  • Herb runs (Farming level 9+) — Plant ranarr seeds, harvest ranarr weeds, sell for ~7,000 GP per weed. See our Herb Run Mastery Guide.
  • Fletching (level 10+) — Buy logs from the Grand Exchange, fletch into arrow shafts or unstrung bows.
  • Looting in PvP worlds — Pick up dropped items. See our Low-Effort Money Making Guide.

5.3 🗡️ Hour 4+: Start Questing for Key Unlocks（第4小时起：开始任务解锁）

Use the Best Quests for New Members guide to plan your next 10 quests. Priority unlocks include: Waterfall Quest, Fight Arena, The Grand Tree, Tree Gnome Village.

For a complete new member roadmap, check our First Week Progression Guide and OSRS Membership Guide 2026.

6. 💡 Common Mistakes to Avoid（常见错误避免）

6.1 ❌ Buying Membership Before Doing Any Quests（错误1：做任务前买会员）

🚫 Mistake #1: Activating membership on a fresh account（错误1：在全新账号上激活会员）

If you buy membership before doing any F2P quests, you will either waste membership time doing free quests, or skip them entirely and miss critical XP rewards.

6.2 ❌ Skipping Rune Mysteries（错误2：跳过符文之谜）

🚫 Mistake #2: Ignoring Rune Mysteries（错误2：忽略符文之谜）

Without Rune Mysteries, you cannot mine rune essence or craft runes. Do not lock yourself out of Runecrafting for a 7-minute quest.

6.3 ❌ Doing Shield of Arrav Without Planning（错误3：无计划做盾牌任务）

🚫 Mistake #3: Starting Shield of Arrav with no partner（错误3：没搭档开始盾牌任务）

This quest requires a partner in the opposite gang. Use the official OSRS grouping system or the Shield of Arrav chat channel to find a partner before committing to a gang.

6.4 ❌ Fighting Elvarg Unprepared（错误4：无准备打埃尔瓦格）

🚫 Mistake #4: Underestimating Elvarg（错误4：低估埃尔瓦格）

Elvarg hits through all armour types with dragonfire. If you forget the anti-dragon shield, you will die in 3-4 hits.

6.5 ❌ Buying Bonds Instead of Membership（错误5：用Bond替代订阅）

🚫 Mistake #5: Using bonds when you can afford a subscription（错误5：能买订阅却用Bond）

In 2026, a bond costs ~5.2M GP for 14 days. If your real-life budget allows, paying $14.99/month is far better value.

7. ❓ FAQ — F2P Quests Before Membership（常见问题 — 会员前F2P任务）

Q: How many F2P quests are there in OSRS in 2026?（问：2026年OSRS有多少F2P任务？）

A: There are 18 free-to-play quests in Old School RuneScape as of June 2026.

Q: What is the minimum time to complete all F2P quests?（问：最快多久完成所有F2P任务？）

A: A prepared player can complete all 18 F2P quests in approximately 2.5 to 3 hours. Without preparation, expect 4-5 hours.

Q: Which F2P quest gives the best XP reward?（问：哪个F2P任务经验奖励最好？）

A: Dragon Slayer gives 18,650 Strength XP and 18,650 Defence XP. For early-game, The Knight's Sword gives 12,725 Smithing XP — enough to jump from level 1 to level 29.

Q: Can I do Dragon Slayer at a low combat level?（问：低战斗等级能打屠龙者吗？）

A: Yes, but risky. Minimum recommended: 40+ combat level, anti-dragon shield, 20+ lobsters. With 37 Prayer (Protect from Magic), you can do it lower.

Q: What quest item unlocks carry over to members?（问：哪些任务物品继承到会员？）

A: Silverlight (Demon Slayer) upgrades to Darklight/Arclight, Amulet of Ghostspeak (The Restless Ghost) needed for Animal Magnetism, and Rune Essence access (Rune Mysteries) essential for Runecrafting.

Q: Is it worth doing F2P quests on an ironman?（问：铁人值得做F2P任务吗？）

A: Absolutely — arguably more so. On an ironman, every XP reward matters because you cannot buy supplies from the GE. The 12,725 Smithing XP from The Knight's Sword alone saves ironmen from mining hundreds of bars. See our F2P Ironman Guide.
