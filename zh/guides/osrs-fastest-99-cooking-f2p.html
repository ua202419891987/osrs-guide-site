Karambwan 1-Tick Cooking Technique:（卡兰布万1-tick烹饪技巧）

  1. Complete Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest (required to cook karambwans).
  2. Have raw karambwans in your inventory (buy in bulk from GE — 100K+ at a time).
  3. Stand next to any fire or range. Click on the fire/range to open the cooking interface.
  4. Click "Cook 1" and immediately click the fire/range again — this cancels the animation.
  5. Repeat rapidly. This creates a near-instant cooking loop that bypasses the normal delay.
  6. On a low-ping world, you can cook 4,500-5,000 karambwans per hour.
⚡ 1-Tick Cooking Requirements（1-Tick烹饪要求）: Use a low-ping world (under 50ms) for maximum speed（使用低延迟世界[50ms以下]以获得最大速度）. The Hosidius Kitchen range or Rogues' Den fire are ideal locations since they have 0% burn rate built-in（Hosidius厨房炉灶或Rogues' Den篝火是理想位置，内建0%烧焦率）. At low Cooking levels, karambwans will burn sometimes regardless of range — expect ~15-20% burn rate at level 30, dropping to near-zero at 90+（低烹饪等级时Karambwans仍会烧焦——30级约15-20%烧焦率，90级以上接近0%）. Even with burn losses, this is still the fastest method（即使有烧焦损失，这仍是最快方法）.

Wines of Zamorak — Fastest F2P Method:（萨莫拉克之酒——最快F2P方法）

Combine jugs of water with grapes — no fire or range needed. This creates jugs of wine, giving 200 XP each. You can do this anywhere, even while running between activities. The method requires constant clicking, but at 450K XP/hr, it's over twice as fast as the next best F2P option. Total GP cost from 35-99 is approximately 5-8M GP at current prices.

🏰 Lumbridge Castle Range（伦布里奇城堡炉灶）: This range has a built-in reduced burn rate — roughly 5% lower than cooking on a regular fire（这个炉灶内建烧焦率减免——比普通篝火低约5%）. For F2P players, this is the best cooking location in the game（对F2P玩家来说，这是游戏中最好的烹饪地点）. Always use it over campfires for any food you're cooking（烹饪任何食物时始终使用它而非篝火）. It's on the ground floor of Lumbridge Castle, right next to the bank chest（位于伦布里奇城堡一楼，紧邻银行箱）.

6. Common Mistakes & Pro Tips（常见错误与专业技巧）

  1. Not completing Tai Bwo Wannai Trio before starting 99 Cooking.（未在开始99烹饪前完成Tai Bwo Wannai Trio任务） Without the quest, you cannot cook karambwans. The quest takes 30 minutes and unlocks the fastest XP in the game.
  2. Cooking on campfires instead of ranges.（在篝火上而非炉灶上烹饪） Ranges have built-in burn reduction. Lumbridge castle range and Hosidius kitchen are objectively better than any fire.
  3. Not using Cooking Gauntlets (from Swan Song).（未使用烹饪手套） These reduce burn rate for lobsters, swordfish, monkfish, and sharks. Essential for minimizing GP loss on expensive fish.
  4. Buying small batches of raw food.（购买少量生食材） Buy 50K-100K raw fish at once. You'll get better GE prices on bulk orders, saving 10-20 GP per fish over the long grind.
  5. Wasting time banking between inventories.（在背包之间浪费银行时间） Stand at a range right next to a bank (Hosidius or Rogues' Den). Every second spent walking is XP lost.
  6. Not checking raw vs cooked prices.（未检查生熟价格差） Sometimes it's cheaper to buy cooked food and drop it than to buy raw and cook it. Always check both prices before committing capital.

7. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题解答）

Q: Is Cooking still a good first 99 in 2026?（Q：2026年烹饪还是好的第一个99级吗？）

A: Absolutely.（当然。） Cooking remains the most popular first 99 because it's fast (30-35 hours), affordable, and the cape perk (never burn food) is incredibly useful. The untrimmed Cooking cape is also a flex — it tells other players you've committed to the grind.

Q: Can I make a profit getting 99 Cooking?（Q：练99烹饪能赚钱吗？）

A: Yes, at high levels with specific foods.（可以，高等级搭配特定食物。） Cooking Anglerfish from level 90+ is profitable (cooked sells for more than raw). Jugs of Wine can be profitable depending on the grape and jug price spread. Karambwans sometimes show a small profit at 90+ Cooking when burn rate is near zero.

Q: What's better for F2P — Wines or Lobsters?（Q：F2P用酒还是龙虾更好？）

A: Wines are over 2x faster XP (450K vs 180K/hr).（酒的经验速度是龙虾的2倍以上。） However, wines require constant clicking while lobsters are AFK. If you can tolerate clicking, wines are the clear winner. If you want to watch videos while training, use lobsters or swordfish at the Lumbridge range.

🍳 The Most Satisfying 99（最令人满意的99级）

There's a reason Cooking is the most common first 99 in OSRS. In 30-35 hours of Karambwan cooking (or about a week of casual play), you'll have a skillcape that makes every future food-related activity burn-free. PvM prep becomes trivial when you can cook all your own food with zero waste.

Buy your karambwans, find a low-ping world, and start clicking.
