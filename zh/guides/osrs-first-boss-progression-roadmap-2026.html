1. Why Bossing Matters Even at Low Levels （为什么低等级打Boss很重要）

Most new players make the same mistake: they spend their first 100 hours killing cows, chickens, and Hill Giants for pennies. Meanwhile, smart beginners are bossing at Combat Level 30 and making 200K–500K GP per hour — enough to buy all the gear they need for faster progression. （大多数新玩家犯同样的错误：前100小时杀牛、杀鸡、打Hill Giants赚零钱。而聪明的初学者在战斗等级30就开始打Boss，每小时赚20-50万GP——足够买加速成长所需的所有装备。）

The Conclusion: Even the easiest bosses pay 5–10x more than regular monsters. And you get better combat XP because bosses have higher HP and give more XP per kill. （结论：即使最简单的Boss也比普通怪物多赚5-10倍。而且战斗经验也更多，因为Boss血量更高，每次击杀给更多经验。）

🎯 What This Roadmap Will Give You （本路线图能给你什么）

By following this 10-boss progression in order, you will: （按顺序跟完这10个Boss进阶路线，你将：）

  • Make 5–10M GP total from boss drops (enough to buy full Dragon gear, membership, and your first house) （从Boss掉落总共赚取500-1000万GP——足够买全套Dragon装备、会员和你的第一栋房子）
  • Reach Combat Level 70 naturally through bossing (faster than any other method) （通过打Boss自然达到战斗等级70——比其他任何方法都快）
  • Learn real PvM mechanics that prepare you for endgame content like Raids and Inferno （学到真正的PvM机制，为Raid和Inferno等终局内容做好准备）
  • Get rare drops that could be worth 10M–50M GP (pet drops, unique unlocks) （获得价值1000-5000万GP的稀有掉落——宠物、独特解锁物品）
  • Have fun — bossing is the most exciting part of OSRS, and you can start way earlier than you think （享受乐趣——打Boss是OSRS最刺激的部分，而你比想象中早得多就可以开始）

3. Boss 1: Giant Mole (F2P Accessible, Combat 30+) （巨鼹鼠 — 免费可打，战斗等级30+）

The Giant Mole is the perfect first boss. It's located under Falador Park, accessible to F2P players, and drops loot worth 150K–300K GP per kill. Best of all, the fight is simple: the Mole only uses melee attacks, so Protect from Melee makes you nearly invincible. （巨鼹鼠是完美的第一个Boss。位于Falador公园地下，F2P玩家可打，每次击杀掉落价值15-30万GP。最棒的是战斗简单：鼹鼠只用近战攻击，所以开Protect from Melee让你几乎无敌。）

📍 Location & Requirements （位置和要求）

Requirements: Combat Level 30+, 43 Prayer for Protect from Melee, 200K+ GP for gear.

How to Get There: Falador Park → Use Spade on dirt patch → Underground lair → Giant Mole in northeastern chamber. （Falador公园 → 用铲子挖土 → 地下巢穴 → 东北房间找到巨鼹鼠。）

🧠 Fight Strategy （战斗策略）

  1. Mole burrows and surfaces periodically. Wait for it to emerge, then attack. （鼹鼠会周期性潜入和浮出。等它出现后再攻击。）
  2. Keep Protect from Melee on at all times — Mole deals 0 damage through it. （全程保持Protect from Melee——鼹鼠打你不掉血。）
  3. Mole has 240 HP. Expect 4-6 surface phases per kill. （鼹鼠240血，每次击杀约需4-6个浮出阶段。）

4. Boss 2: Obor (F2P, Combat 35+)

Obor is the F2P giant boss in Edgeville Dungeon. Slightly harder than Giant Mole but drops better loot. （Obor是Edgeville Dungeon的F2P巨人Boss。比巨鼹鼠稍难但掉落更好。）

Entry: Pay 50 Rune Essence (~2.5K GP) at the locked gate. （入场：在锁着的门前支付50个Rune Essence，约2500 GP。）

Strategy: Use Protect from Melee (70% of attacks). Watch for rock throw animation → switch to Protect from Missiles. When Obor raises both arms → Earthquake (unavoidable 10-20 damage, eat food immediately). （策略：主要开Protect from Melee。注意投石动作→切Protect from Missiles。Obor举双手→地震（无法减免10-20伤害，立即吃东西）。）

Key Drops: Rune items (10K–50K each, 2-4 per kill), Obor's Staff (2M–5M, 1/512). Expected: 100K–250K per kill. （关键掉落：Rune物品每次2-4件，Obor's Staff稀有掉落200-500万。）

5. Boss 3: Bryophyta (F2P, Combat 40+)

Bryophyta in Falador Dungeon. Always drops Bryophyta's Essence (50K–100K). （位于Falador Dungeon，必定掉落Bryophyta's Essence价值5-10万。）

Strategy: Use Protect from Magic as default (60% of attacks). Kill summoned roots first (they have 10 HP each). Expected profit: 80K–200K per kill. （策略：默认开Protect from Magic。优先杀召唤的树根——每个只有10血。预期每次8-20万。）

12. Common Beginner Mistakes （常见新手错误）

❌ Not Using Protect Prayers （不开保护祈祷）

The Fix: Activate the correct Protect prayer BEFORE engaging. Prioritize 43 Prayer if you don't have it yet. （进战斗前就开好正确的保护祈祷。还没有43祈祷优先练。）

❌ Getting Discouraged After 1 Death （死一次就气馁）

The Fix: Death is part of learning. Learn from each death — why did you die? Fix that and try again. （死亡是学习的一部分。分析死因，修正后重试。）

13. After Level 70: What's Next? （70级后：接下来做什么？）

If you've followed this roadmap and reached Combat Level 70, you've become a competent PvMer. （如果你按路线图走到了战斗等级70，你已经成为一名称职的PvM玩家了。）

  • God Wars Dungeon (GWD): 4 bosses, unique gear worth 1M–10M GP （4个Boss，独特装备价值100-1000万）
  • Corporeal Beast: Spirit Shield (30M GP) （灵体盾价值3000万）
  • Kraken: Easier than Zulrah, drops Kraken Tentacle (200K) （比Zulrah简单）
  • Join a Clan: Bossing is more fun with friends. （加入公会，和朋友一起打Boss更好玩。）

14. FAQ （常见问题）

What's the minimum Combat Level to start bossing? （最低多少战斗等级可以开始打Boss？）

Answer: Combat Level 30. Giant Mole (Boss #1). You need 43 Prayer for Protect from Melee. （战斗等级30。打巨鼹鼠（1号Boss）。需要43祈祷开Protect from Melee。）

Do I need membership? （需要会员吗？）

Answer: First 3 bosses are F2P. You can make 2-3M GP from them — enough to buy 2-3 months membership. （前3个Boss免费可打。能从它们身上赚200-300万GP——够买2-3个月会员。）

How much GP to start? （启动需要多少GP？）

Answer: 200K-300K GP for basic Rune gear. If you don't have that, kill Hill Giants for 2-3 hours. （基本Rune装备需要20-30万GP。没有的话打2-3小时Hill Giants。）
