1. How Wintertodt Works（Wintertodt机制说明）

Wintertodt is a skilling boss in the snowy north of Zeah. You enter the boss chamber, and for ~5 minutes (mass) or up to 15 minutes (solo), you fend off the Wintertodt's cold by feeding the braziers with kindling. The core loop:

  1. Chop Bruma Roots from the trees inside the boss room
  2. Use the roots on the brazier to create kindling (Fletching XP)
  3. Feed the kindling to the brazier (Firemaking XP)
  4. Occasionally fix broken braziers (Construction XP) or light unlit braziers (Firemaking XP)
  5. Eat food to survive the cold — Wintertodt damages everyone periodically
  6. When Wintertodt's energy reaches 0%, you win and earn supply crates

5. Mass World Strategy — Fastest XP（多人世界策略 — 最快经验）

Mass worlds (309 and 311 are the dedicated WT worlds) have 40+ players per game. Games last ~4-5 minutes.

  1. Teleport in with a Games Necklace → Wintertodt Camp.
  2. Chop Bruma Roots from the 4 trees. The roots are stackable.
  3. Fletch the roots into kindling next to the brazier.
  4. Feed the brazier — this gives the most Firemaking XP.
  5. When brazier breaks: Fix it with Hammer. When brazier goes out: Relight with Tinderbox.
  6. Keep 500+ points minimum per game to qualify for supply crates.

6. Solo Strategy — Maximum Profit & Construction XP（单人策略 — 最高收益&建筑经验）

Solo Wintertodt is slower Firemaking XP but gives significantly more Construction XP and better crate loot.

  1. Enter and light all 4 braziers.
  2. Phase 1 (100%-60% energy): Chop and fletch. Don't feed yet — fix broken braziers for Construction XP.
  3. Phase 2 (60%-5% energy): Keep one brazier lit. Feed kindling steadily. Continue fixing.
  4. Phase 3 (0% energy): Wintertodt dies. Collect crates. Solo games reward 2,500-3,500 points per game.

9. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: Should I fletch the roots or feed them raw?（树根应该制箭后喂还是直接喂？）

A: Always fletch. Fletched kindling gives significantly more points (10 vs 5 per root).

Q: Do I lose items if I die at Wintertodt?（在Wintertodt死亡会丢装备吗？）

A: No. Death at Wintertodt is safe. You respawn outside with all items.

Q: What's the best world for Wintertodt?（Wintertodt最好的世界是哪个？）

A: World 309 (official Wintertodt world). World 311 is the backup.

Q: When should I open my supply crates?（什么时候开补给箱？）

A: Immediately — they scale to your CURRENT levels.
