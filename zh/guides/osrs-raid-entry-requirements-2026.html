Raids are where OSRS goes from "grinding for GP" to "printing GP." A single purple chest at Chambers of Xeric can be a 100M+ split. A successful Theatre of Blood run averages 3-5M per person. Tombs of Amascut is the most accessible raid in OSRS history, designed specifically for mid-level players and solo learners. But most mid-level players never try raiding because they think the requirements are too high. They're wrong. This guide breaks down the exact minimum stats, quests, and budget gear for all three raids — and shows you exactly how a mid-level account can start raiding within a week.

1. Which Raid Should You Start With?（从哪个团队副本开始？）

Not all raids are equal for beginners. Here's the ranking from easiest to hardest entry:

Tombs of Amascut (ToA)EasiestYes (0 invocation)Absolute beginners. Learn raid mechanics at your own pace.

YOUR PATH: TOA → COX → TOB（你的路线）

Start with ToA Entry Mode at 0 invocation. Graduate to 150 invocation. Then learn CoX in teams. When you can run 300+ ToA and 30K+ point CoX, you're ready to attempt ToB Entry Mode.

2. Tombs of Amascut — Best First Raid（ToA——最佳入门团队副本）

ToA was designed with an invocation system that lets you scale difficulty from "brain off" to "Inferno-level." At 0 invocation, ToA is easier than most quest bosses. At 500 invocation, it's the hardest content in the game.

ToA Unique Mechanics（ToA独特机制）

  • Invocation System: Choose difficulty before each run. Start at 0. Add invocations (+ difficulty) as you improve.
  • 4 Demi-Boss Paths: Crondis (crocodile), Het (seal), Scabaras (scarab), Apmeken (baboon). Each teaches a different mechanic.
  • 2 Final Bosses: Wardens P1 (obelisk) and P2 (core). P2 is the actual boss fight.
  • No Death Penalty (Entry Mode): You get 3 lives. Dying resets you to the start of the current room, not the entire raid.

FAQ（常见问题）

Q: Can I really raid with 75 combat stats?（75战斗等级真的能打团队副本吗？）

At ToA Entry Mode? Yes. The invocation system means you can clear ToA with welfare gear at 0 invocation. It won't be fast and purple chance will be near zero, but you'll learn every room's mechanics. That mechanical knowledge transfers directly to higher difficulties.

Q: Is ToA Entry Mode worth doing for loot?（ToA入门模式值得刷战利品吗？）

For learning, yes. For GP, no. At 0 invocation, purple chance is extremely low. Treat Entry Mode as a tutorial. Once comfortable, jump to 150 invocation — that's where purples become realistic and the ~3M/hr profit kicks in.

Q: How long does it take to learn CoX?（学习CoX需要多久？）

With WDR mentors, 3-5 raids (6-10 hours) to understand Olm. Olm is the only hard room in CoX. The other rooms are straightforward. Focus on learning Olm's 4-phase head-turning mechanic — that's 90% of the difficulty.

Q: Should I buy a Dragon Warhammer for CoX?（该为CoX买Dragon Warhammer吗？）

Yes, but borrow one if you can. DWH spec reduces Olm/Tekton defense by 30%. Without it, kills take significantly longer. If you can't afford one (~35M), ask teammates — most CoX teams have 1-2 people with DWH and the defense reduction stacks.

Your First Raid Starts Today（你的第一次团队副本从今天开始）

Beneath Cursed Sands quest → ToA Entry Mode. That's all it takes. One quest, one raid, and you're in the endgame.
