① PvP Account Build Types（PvP账号类型介绍）

OSRS PvP accounts are designed to maximize damage at specific combat levels. The key is training only the stats that contribute to damage while keeping Defence and Prayer as low as possible. There are four main pure builds: 1 Prayer Pure (lowest combat, highest risk), 13 Prayer Pure (access to protective prayers), Zerker (45 Defence, best overall), and Med Level (70+ Defence, maxed combat). Each has different strengths and weaknesses in the Wilderness.

② 1 Prayer Pure Build（1祷告纯号）

Combat level: ~50-70. This is the most restricted but most lethal build at low combat. Stats: 75 Attack, 99 Strength, 75 Ranged, 94 Magic, 1 Defence, 1 Prayer. You cannot use overhead prayers, which makes you extremely strong offensively but vulnerable. This build relies on pure DPS and movement to win fights. Best for Edgeville PKing and PvP worlds. Use dragon weapons and darts for maximum damage per combat level.

③ 13/15 Prayer Pure Build（13祷告纯号）

Combat level: ~55-75. The most popular pure build. Prayer at 13 unlocks Smite (drains enemy prayer), and at 15 unlocks Mystic Might. Stats: 75 Attack, 99 Strength, 85 Ranged, 94 Magic, 1 Defence, 13-15 Prayer. With overhead prayers you can fight other pures more effectively. This build dominates PvP worlds and Wilderness edge fights.

④ Zerker Build（狂战士号）

Combat level: ~75-90. The Zerker has 45 Defence (for Berserker Helm and Vengeance) and access to all prayers. Stats: 75 Attack, 99 Strength, 90+ Ranged, 94 Magic, 45 Defence, 52 Prayer. This is widely considered the best overall pure build — strong enough to fight mains, but still low enough combat to fight pures. Essential unlocks: Vengeance (Lunar Diplomacy), Berserker Helm, and Barrows Gloves.

⑤ Med Level Build（中级号）

Combat level: ~90-100+. Med levels have 70-75 Defence (for piety, rigour, augury prayers) and near-max combat. Stats: 90+ Attack, 99 Strength, 99 Ranged, 99 Magic, 70-75 Defence, 77+ Prayer. These accounts can fight almost anyone in the Wilderness and are popular for deep Wilderness PKing, team PvP, and multi-combat zones.

⑥ Essential Quests（纯号必做任务）

Even with 1 Defence, there are quests that provide huge benefits: The Grand Tree (Dragon Scimitar), Monkey Madness I (Dragon Scimitar + XP), Lost City (Dragon Longsword, DDS spec), Fight Arena (free combat XP), Tree Gnome Village (Spirit Shield access), Desert Treasure (Ancient Magicks — freeze spells), and Lunar Diplomacy (Vengeance for Zerks). Always complete these quests early.

FAQ（常见问题）

Which pure build is best for beginners?（新手推荐哪种？）

Start with a 13 Prayer Pure. It has overhead prayers for protection and is easier to learn PvP mechanics with.

How long does it take to build a pure?（建一个纯号要多久？）

A 1 Prayer Pure takes 40-60 hours. A Zerker takes 100-150 hours. Med levels take 200+ hours.
