① CoX Overview（CoX概述）

Chambers of Xeric (CoX) is the original OSRS raid, released in 2017. It requires 3-5 players (solo is possible but very difficult). The raid generates a random layout of rooms, each with different challenges. CoX is known for the Twisted Bow — the most expensive and powerful weapon in OSRS. The raid takes 20-35 minutes for a normal team. Requirements: 90+ Combat, 75+ Ranged/Magic, 70+ Prayer with Rigour and Augury unlocked.

② Gear Requirements（装备要求）

Minimum gear (under 100M): Melee: Whip + Bandos/Justiciar, Ranged: Blowpipe + DHCB (if available), Magic: Trident of the Swamp + Ancestral/Ahrim's. Recommended gear (200M+): Scythe of Vitur (melee), Twisted Bow (ranged), Sang Staff (magic). The Twisted Bow alone doubles your CoX profit per hour. Most rooms require Ranged and Magic, with Melee used on specific bosses and Olm head phase.

③ Room Mechanics（房间机制详解）

CoX has 8 possible rooms (4 are chosen per raid): Vasa Nistirio: Freeze the crystal to stop Vasa's teleport attack. Vespula: Kill the healer grub first. Tekton: Melee with protect from melee. Muttadile: Ranged/Mage kill while dodging the ice attack. Vangaurds: Attack all three with correct styles. Shamans: Group up and melee. Crabs: Light the fires and kill. Ice Demon: Use magic (fire spells preferred). The raid also has resource rooms with fishing spots and farming patches for supplies.

④ Olm Boss Fight（Olm四阶段战斗）

Olm is the final boss with 4 phases. Phase 1 — Head (Melee): Attack Olm's head with Melee. Stand in melee range and dodge the crystal attack. Phase 2 — Hands (Ranged/Mage): Two hands spawn — one weak to Ranged, one to Magic. Kill them quickly. Phase 3 — Acid Pool: Olm covers the floor in acid. Move 2-3 tiles between attacks to avoid damage. Phase 4 — Enrage: Olm uses all attacks simultaneously. This is the most dangerous phase. Call out special attacks in voice chat. Key tip: Bring Antivenoms for Olm's venom attack and stamina potions for the running phases.

⑤ Team Roles & Strategies（团队分工）

Standard 4-player team: Tank (Melee): Tanks Olm head and Tekton/Muttadile. High defence gear (Justiciar). Ranger (Best DPS): Uses T Bow or DHCB. Focuses on Olm hands and Muttadile. Mage (Freezer): Uses Ice Barrage to freeze enemies. Heals with Sang Staff. Support (Flex): Fills gaps, heals, and provides supplies. In small teams (3 players), each person covers 2 roles. Voice communication is essential for efficient clears.

⑥ Loot & Profit Analysis（掉落与收益分析）

CoX unique drops (the "purple" loot): Twisted Bow (1.2B) — most valuable drop in OSRS, Elder Maul (50M), Kodai Wand (120M), Dragon Claws (80M), Ancestral Robes (250M set), Dinh's Bulwark (15M). Average GP per raid with a normal team: ~500K-1.5M. With Twisted Bow (speed runs): 8-12M GP/hr for experienced teams. The purple chance is approximately 1/25 per raid. Speed runners (sub-15 min raids) can earn 10M+ GP/hr.

FAQ

Is CoX harder than ToA?（CoX比ToA难吗？）

Yes. CoX has more complex room mechanics and a harder final boss (Olm). ToA is the recommended first raid due to its Invocation difficulty system.

How many CoX completions do I need to be good?（多少把能熟练？）

Most players need 50-100 completions to feel comfortable. Speed running (sub-20 min) requires 200+ completions.
