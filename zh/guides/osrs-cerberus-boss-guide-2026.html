Chapter 1: Requirements — 91 Slayer & Gear Check（第一章：要求 — 91 Slayer与装备检查）

Cerberus is a Slayer-exclusive boss, meaning you must meet specific requirements before you can fight her. Here's everything you need:

Slayer Requirements（Slayer要求）

  • 91 Slayer: This is the only Slayer level requirement. However, reaching 91 Slayer takes significant time (~150-200 tasks).
  • Cerberus Unlocked: You must unlock Cerberus as a Slayer task from Duradel, Nieve, or Steve (after 80 Slayer).
  • Task Amount: Cerberus tasks are typically 50-75 kills. You can extend the task once with a Slayer VIP Coupon or by paying 100 Slayer points.

Essential Quests（必要任务）

  • Priest in Peril: Unlocks the ability to enter Morytania (required to reach Cerberus)
  • Nature Spirit: Unlocks some Prayer rewards
  • Dream Mentor: Unlocks the Lunar Spellbook (optional but useful for Vengeance)
  • Dragon Slayer II: Unlocks Ava's Assembler (highly recommended for Ranged setups)
Pro Tip: Don't Rush Cerberus at 91 Slayer（专家提示：不要在91 Slayer时匆忙去打）

While you CAN fight Cerberus at 91 Slayer with 70 combat stats, you'll struggle. Your kill times will be slow (2-3 minutes per kill), and you'll die frequently. We recommend at least 80+ in all combat stats and 70+ Prayer (for Piety/Rigour) before attempting Cerberus.

Chapter 2: Gear Setup — Entry vs Efficient（第二章：装备搭配 — 入门vs高效）

Your gear setup dramatically impacts your kill speed and survival at Cerberus. Here are the best setups for every budget.

Entry-Level Setup (Budget: ~5-10M GP)（入门搭配 · 预算约5-10M）

Chapter 3: Fight Mechanics — 3 Ghosts & Prayer Switching（第三章：战斗机制 — 3个灵魂与祈祷切换）

Cerberus has a complex 3-phase fight mechanic that makes her unique among OSRS bosses. Understanding these mechanics is crucial for survival and efficiency.

Phase 1: The Three Ghosts（第一阶段：三个灵魂）

At the start of the fight (and every 100 HP she loses), Cerberus summons three ghosts: Summoning Mage, Summoning Range, and Summoning Melee. These ghosts attack with their respective combat styles and can quickly kill you if you don't pray correctly.

Ghost Type（灵魂类型）Attack Style（攻击风格）Damage（伤害）Correct Prayer（正确祈祷）
Summoning Mage (Blue)Magic (Air spells)18-24 per hitProtect from Magic
Summoning Range (Red)Ranged (Arrows)20-28 per hitProtect from Missiles
Summoning Melee (Green)Melee (Slash)22-30 per hitProtect from Melee
Pro Tip: Prayer Switching Order（专家提示：祈祷切换顺序）

The ghosts always appear in the same order: Mage → Range → Melee. Memorize this order to switch prayers quickly. The ghosts have low HP (~10 each), so you can kill them quickly to avoid prayer switching entirely.

Phase 2: Cerberus Appears（第二阶段：Cerberus出现）

After the ghosts are dealt with, Cerberus becomes attackable. She has three attack styles that she rotates through:

  • Magic Attack: She shoots a blue ball of magic. Pray Protect from Magic.
  • Ranged Attack: She shoots a red projectile. Pray Protect from Missiles.
  • Melee Attack: She bites or claws you. Pray Protect from Melee OR use Venom (Serpentine Helm) to negate most melee damage.

Important: Cerberus's attacks are predictable. She always uses the attack style you're NOT praying against. If you're praying Magic, she'll attack with Ranged or Melee next. Learn this pattern to efficiently switch prayers.

Phase 3: All Together (100 HP & 200 HP)（第三阶段：全部一起 · 100血与200血时）

When Cerberus reaches 200 HP and 100 HP, she summons the three ghosts again WHILE continuing to attack. This is the most dangerous part of the fight. You must:

  1. Kill or ignore the ghosts (depending on your DPS)
  2. Continue praying correctly against Cerberus
  3. Manage your food and prayer

Chapter 4: Step-by-Step Strategy — All 3 Phases（第四章：分阶段策略 — 全部3个阶段）

Pre-Fight Preparation（战前准备）

  1. Teleport to Cerberus using a Slayer Ring (recommended) or run from the Taverley Dungeon entrance.
  2. Drink a Super Combat Potion (or Ranging Potion if using Ranged).
  3. Activate Piety (Melee) or Rigour (Ranged).
  4. Enter the cave and attack Cerberus to start the fight.

Phase 1 Strategy (Ghosts Appear)（第一阶段策略 · 灵魂出现）

Step 1: The first ghost is always the Summoning Mage (Blue). Immediately switch to Protect from Magic. Kill or tank it, then switch to Protect from Missiles for the Range ghost, then Protect from Melee for the Melee ghost.

Phase 2 Strategy (Cerberus Attackable)（第二阶段策略 · Cerberus可被攻击）

Once ghosts are dead, start attacking Cerberus. She uses the style you're NOT praying against. Keep praying the style she used LAST to be protected against 2/3 attacks. Use best DPS abilities and special attacks.

Phase 3 Strategy (Ghosts Return at 200 & 100 HP)（第三阶段策略 · 灵魂在200和100血时回归）

If high DPS: ignore ghosts and keep attacking Cerberus. If low-mid DPS: kill ghosts quickly. Manage prayer potions — this is the most prayer-intensive phase.

Average kill time: 45-60 seconds with good gear, 90-120 seconds with budget gear.

Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Can I safespot Cerberus?（能安全角落打Cerberus吗？）

No. Cerberus cannot be safespotted. She always walks directly toward you, and there are no obstacles in her arena.

How much GP per hour does Cerberus make?（Cerberus每小时赚多少GP？）

Unlucky (no boots): ~8-10M GP/hour. Average: ~10-13M GP/hour. Lucky (get a boot): ~25-35M GP/hour.

What's the best combat style for Cerberus?（打Cerberus用什么战斗风格最好？）

Melee (Crush) is generally best because Cerberus has lower Crush defence and Venom from Serpentine Helm negates most melee damage.

What should I do if I keep dying at Cerberus?（总是死在Cerberus怎么办？）

Upgrade gear, practice prayer switching, bring more food, use Serpentine Helm for venom, and learn the ghost order: Mage → Range → Melee.

Final Tips & Common Mistakes（最终提示与常见错误）

Tip #1: Bring Teleport Tabs（携带传送卷）

Always bring 2-3 teleport tablets. If your HP drops below 10, teleport out immediately. Don't risk dying and losing loot.

Tip #2: Use the Serpentine Helm（使用Serpentine头盔）

Even uncharged, it applies venom dealing 6 damage every 18 seconds — significant DPS over time.

Tip #3: Don't Get Discouraged by Bad RNG（别被坏运气打倒）

You might go 300-400 kills without a single boot. This is just RNG. Every kill is profit even without boots.
