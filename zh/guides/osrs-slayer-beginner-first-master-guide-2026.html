1. What Is Slayer & Why Start Early（1. 什么是杀戮者及为什么尽早开始）

Slayer is one of the most unique skills in OSRS. Unlike other skills where you train by repeating the same action (fishing shrimp, burning logs), Slayer sends you on varied combat assignments to kill specific monsters that you would never encounter during normal gameplay. Each task is different, each monster has unique mechanics, and the variety keeps the grind fresh even after hundreds of hours.

🎯 Why Slayer Is the #1 Skill for Long-Term Accounts（🎯 为什么杀戮者是长期账号的头号技能）

Most skills in OSRS give you one type of reward. Mining gives Mining XP. Cooking gives Cooking XP. Slayer is different — it gives you combat XP, GP, rare drops, and Slayer XP all at once. Here's why starting Slayer early changes your entire OSRS experience:

🛡️ Slayer Unlocks the Entire Game（🛡️ 杀戮者解锁整个游戏）

  • Access to boss content: Every major boss in OSRS requires a Slayer level or being on a Slayer task to fight. From KBD (level 1) to Vorkath (level 80+) to Grotesque Guardians (level 75), Slayer is your gatekeeper to the best PvM content.（进入Boss内容：每个主要Boss都需要杀戮者等级或在杀戮者任务中才能战斗）
  • Unique drops: Slayer monsters have exclusive drop tables with items you cannot obtain anywhere else. Whips from Abyssal Demons, Dragon boots from Dagannoths, Granite Mauls from Gargoyles — these are often the best GP sources for mid-level players.（独特掉落：杀戮者怪物有专属掉落表）
  • Combat training with purpose: Instead of mindlessly clicking on cows for Attack XP, you're killing Banshees for Slayer XP while also gaining Melee/Ranged/Magic XP. The dual-purpose nature makes every hour of gameplay feel productive.（有目的的战斗训练：同时获得杀戮者经验和战斗经验）
  • Slayer points economy: Completing tasks earns Slayer points that you can spend on unlocking new monsters, buying Broad bolts/arrows, or skipping unwanted tasks. The point system creates a progression loop that rewards consistency.（杀戮者点数经济：完成任务可获得点数，用于解锁新怪物、购买宽头弹药或跳过低效任务）

② ⏰ When Should You Start Slayer?（② ⏰ 你应该什么时候开始杀戮者？）

The optimal time to start Slayer depends on your account type and goals, but here are the general recommendations:

Your Situation（你的情况）Recommended Start（推荐开始时间）Why（原因）
Complete Beginner (F2P)（完全新手）Level 15–20 Combat（战斗等级15-20）Turael is available immediately; tasks are very easy and teach combat basics（Turael立即可用，任务非常简单）
New Member Account（新会员账号）Level 30–40 Combat（战斗等级30-40）Vannaka becomes available; tasks start dropping useful loot worth keeping（Vannaka可用，任务开始掉落有用战利品）
Returning Player (Pre-EOC)（回归玩家）Level 50+ Combat（战斗等级50+）You likely have the stats already; jump straight to Chaeldar or Duradel（你可能已经有这些数据，直接去找Chaeldar或Duradel）
Ironman Account（铁人模式账号）Level 20 Combat (Turael)（战斗等级20）Slayer is the most efficient way to obtain rare drops for untradeable upgrades（杀戮者是最有效获取不可交易升级物品的方式）

The short answer: you can start Slayer at literally any Combat level. Turael assigns tasks to players with Combat level 1. The only question is whether you're ready for the combat difficulty of the tasks you'll receive. If you're struggling to kill Hill Giants at Combat 30, you might want to do some general combat training first before taking on Slayer tasks.

💡 The Slayer Progression Mindset（💡 杀戮者进阶心态）

Don't treat Slayer as a "skill you train to 99." Treat it as your primary way of playing OSRS. Every time you log in, pick up a Slayer task. Over time, your Combat levels, Slayer level, bank wealth, and game knowledge all grow together. This is the most efficient and enjoyable way to play OSRS long-term.

2. Choosing Your First Slayer Master（2. 选择你的第一个杀戮者大师）

Slayer Masters are NPCs who assign you tasks. Each master has different requirements, assigns different monsters, and offers different rewards. Choosing the right master for your current Combat level is critical — too low and tasks are boring/low XP; too high and you'll die repeatedly.

④ 🗺️ All 7 Slayer Masters — Complete Comparison（④ 🗺️ 全部7个杀戮者大师 — 完整对比）

Here is every Slayer Master in the game, ranked by the recommended Combat level to start using them:

① Turael — The Starter Master（① Turael — 入门大师）

Location: Burthorpe (easily accessible from tutorial area)（位置：Burthorpe）
Combat Requirement: None (Level 0+)（战斗要求：无）
Quest Requirement: None（任务要求：无）
Task Length: Very Short (10–20 monsters)（任务长度：非常短，10-20只怪物）
Difficulty: Very Easy（难度：非常简单）
Slayer Points per Task: 0 (none)（每任务点数：0）
Best For: Absolute beginners learning the combat system; players who want very short tasks（最适合：绝对新手，想要非常短的任务）

Pros: No requirements; tasks are trivially easy; located in Burthorpe right next to a bank and lodge. Cons: Awards zero Slayer points (so you can't buy unlocks); tasks are very short (less engaging); monsters assigned are extremely low-level (low GP and XP).（优点：无要求，任务非常简单。缺点：零杀戮者点数）

Verdict: Use Turael for your very first 1–3 tasks to learn how Slayer works. After that, switch to Mazchna or Vannaka.（结论：用Turael做前1-3个任务来学习如何工作，然后换到Mazchna或Vannaka）

② Mazchna — The Early Game Master（② Mazchna — 早期游戏大师）

Location: Canifis (after Ghosts Ah! Woo! quest)（位置：Canifis）

Task Length: Short (20–40 monsters)（任务长度：短，20-40只怪物）
Difficulty: Easy（难度：简单）
Slayer Points per Task: 1 (2 on task #10, #50, #100, etc.)（每任务点数：1）
Best For: Players with Combat 20–50 who want consistent short tasks（最适合：战斗等级20-50的玩家，想要稳定的短任务）

Pros: Low requirement; awards Slayer points (slowly); tasks are manageable for low-level accounts; located near a bank in Canifis. Cons: Tasks are shorter than ideal for efficient training; lower XP rates than Vannaka.（优点：要求低，获得杀戮者点数，任务可控。缺点：任务对高效训练来说太短）

Verdict: Solid choice for Combat 20–40 players who find Vannaka's tasks too difficult. Once you're comfortable at Combat 40+, move to Vannaka.（结论：战斗20-40级玩家的不错选择，40级后换到Vannaka）

Verdict: Vannaka is the single best Slayer Master for players progressing through the mid game. Stay with Vannaka from Combat 40 all the way to Combat 70+ before considering Chaeldar or Duradel.（结论：Vannaka是中期玩家的最佳单一杀戮者大师）

3. Unlocking Each Master (Quest Requirements)（3. 解锁每个大师，任务要求）

Several Slayer Masters require quest completion before you can access them. Here's the exact quest order you should follow to unlock all masters as efficiently as possible.

⑦ 📋 Quests That Unlock Slayer Masters（⑦ 📋 解锁杀戮者大师的任务）

① Ghosts Ah! Woo! — Unlocks Mazchna

Requirements: None (F2P accessible, but you need membership to use Slayer points)（要求：无）
Length: Short (~10 minutes)（长度：短，约10分钟）
Rewards: Access to Mazchna; 600 GP; 600 Crafting XP（奖励：解锁Mazchna）
Why do it: Mazchna is only useful for Combat 20–40 players, and by the time you reach Combat 40 you'll want to be at Vannaka anyway. This quest is low priority unless you're doing a F2P Slayer grind.（为什么要做：只有战斗20-40级有用，优先级低）

② The Feud — Unlocks Sumona

Requirements: None (though 30 Thieving helps)（要求：无，30盗窃有帮助）
Length: Medium (~30–45 minutes)（长度：中等，约30-45分钟）
Rewards: Access to Pollnivneach; 15,000 Thieving XP; ability to use magic carpets（奖励：解锁Pollnivneach）
Why do it: The Feud is actually a very useful quest for multiple reasons (Thieving XP, carpet transport). However, Sumona herself is not a major upgrade over Vannaka. Do this quest early anyway for the transport benefits, but don't feel pressured to switch to Sumona immediately.（为什么要做：非常有用，但Sumona本身不是大升级）

③ Lost City — Unlocks Chaeldar

Requirements: 31 Crafting, 36 Woodcutting, ability to defeat a level 63 Ent (magic-resistant)（要求：31手工艺，36伐木，能击败63级树精）
Length: Medium (~45–60 minutes)（长度：中等，约45-60分钟）
Rewards: Access to Zanaris; 3 Quest Points; ability to wield Dragon Longsword（奖励：解锁Zanaris）
Why do it: Lost City is a milestone quest. It unlocks the Fairy Ring network (massive QoL improvement) and gives access to Chaeldar. Highly recommended once you have Combat 60+ stats. The Ent fight requires Magic attacks (it has high Melee defence), so bring Air Strike or Wind Bolt runes.（为什么要做：里程碑任务，解锁妖精环网络）

④ Shilo Village — Unlocks Duradel

Requirements: 32 Prayer, 20 Crafting, 20 Smithing, ability to defeat threats up to level 100（要求：32祈祷，20手工艺，20锻造）
Length: Long (~60–90 minutes)（长度：长，约60-90分钟）
Rewards: Access to Shilo Village (bank, mine, fishing spots); 8,000 XP in 3 skills; 2,000 GP; access to Duradel（奖励：解锁Shilo Village和Duradel）
Why do it: Shilo Village is one of the most important quests for Slayer players. Duradel is the best master in the game, and Shilo Village itself has a convenient bank. Do this quest as soon as your Combat stats allow (around level 60–70 Combat with reasonable gear).（为什么要做：对杀戮者玩家最重要的任务之一）

⑤ Children of the Sun — Unlocks Konvou (Varlamore)

Requirements: Completion of Varlamore Part I questline (multiple quests)（要求：完成Varlamore Part I任务线）
Length: Very Long (several hours across multiple quests)（长度：非常长）
Rewards: Access to Valamore region; Konvou Slayer Master; new content（奖励：解锁Valamore地区和Konvou）
Why do it: Only relevant for accounts that have progressed into the Varlamore endgame region. If you're a new player in 2026, this is not a priority until you've already completed the major mainland quests.（为什么要做：仅对已进入Varlamore终局区域的账号相关）

Task 1–3: The Tutorial Phase（任务1-3：教程阶段）

Your first few tasks will be extremely easy (Cows, Chickens, Spiders). Use these tasks to:

  • Learn how to read your Slayer task (check the Quest tab → Scroll down to "Current Task")（学习如何读取杀戮者任务）
  • Practice the combat cycle: Attack → eat food when health is low → loot drops → next monster（练习战斗循环：攻击→低血吃食物→拾取掉落→下一只怪物）
  • Start your first Slayer-specific item collection: Earmuffs (from Witch's House quest or buy from Slayer equipment shop)（开始收集第一个杀戮者专属物品：耳罩）
  • Bank valuable drops (feathers, cowhides, Big Bones) for GP（将有价值的掉落存入银行换取金币）

Task 4–6: The Gear Check（任务4-6：装备检查）

Around task 4–6, you'll start getting assignments that require actual gear. This is where many beginners struggle. Make sure you have:

  • Steel armor set (full helm, platebody, platelegs) or Black armor if you can afford it（钢甲套装或黑甲）
  • Steel scimitar (faster attack speed than longsword) or better（钢弯刀或更好）
  • Shield (any steel/kite shield for extra defence)（盾牌，钢盾或风筝盾）
  • Food: 10–15 Lobsters or 20+ Tuna（食物：10-15个龙虾或20+金枪鱼）
  • Anti-dragon shield (if assigned Dragons — buy from Oziach in Edgeville after Dragon Slayer quest)（防龙盾，如果被分配了龙任务）

Task 7–10: The Progression Phase（任务7-10：进阶阶段）

Tasks 7–10 will start assigning mid-level monsters (Moss Giants, Demons, etc.). These tasks can take 30–60 minutes each. Key tips:

  • Use Protect from Melee prayer if you have level 43 Prayer — it makes these tasks trivial（如果43祈祷，使用近战保护祈祷）
  • Bring a teleport (Tablet or runes) in case you get low on food and can't finish the task in one trip（带传送卷轴或符文）
  • Loot everything — even "junk" drops like Iron arrows and Steel plateskirts add up over time（拾取所有物品——即使是"垃圾"掉落也会累积）
  • Note valuable drops with Magic notepaper (if you have it) or bank frequently（用魔法记事本记录有价值的掉落或频繁存银行）

⑩ 🔄 Should You Skip or Stay on a Task?（⑩ 🔄 应该跳过还是继续任务？）

As a beginner, you have very limited Slayer points, so skipping tasks costs you dearly. Here's the decision framework:

Situation（情况）Action（操作）Reason（原因）
Task is too hard (you keep dying)（任务太难，你一直死）Skip (or use Turael skip)（跳过，或用Turael跳过）Dying wastes more time than skipping（死亡比跳过更浪费时间）
Task is boring but easy（任务无聊但简单）Stay（留下）Free XP and GP; build discipline（免费经验和金币，培养纪律）
Task is in the Wilderness（任务在荒野）Skip (beginner) or Stay (if confident)（跳过或留下）Wilderness tasks have PK risk（荒野任务有PK风险）
Task gives bad drops（任务掉落差）Stay (early game)（留下，早期）Every task gives some GP; skipping wastes points（每个任务都给一些金币，跳过浪费点数）
You have 100+ Slayer points（你有100+杀戮者点数）Skip freely（自由跳过）Points are abundant; optimize for fun（点数充裕，优化乐趣）
💡 Turael Skip Trick (Advanced)（💡 Turael跳过技巧，高级）

Once you have 15+ Slayer points, you can use Turael to "skip" tasks for free. Get a task from your main master (e.g., Vannaka), then talk to Turael and ask him to assign you an "easier task." He will overwrite your current task with a Turael-level task. This effectively gives you a free skip. Use this strategically when you get a truly awful task (e.g., Suicide Squad in the Wilderness).

✨ Essential Slayer Items (Don't Skip These!)（✨ 必备杀戮者物品，别跳过这些！）

Required for: Aberrant Spectres（必需用于：变异幽灵）
Effect: Protects from Spectres' stench (reduces all combat stats by 1–5 levels). Without it, you'll be fighting with severely reduced stats.（效果：防止幽灵的恶臭）
Tip: Buy this BEFORE you get your first Spectres task. They're assigned frequently by Vannaka and above.（提示：在接到第一个幽灵任务前购买）

③ Facemask (6,000 GP from Slayer Equipment)（③ 面罩）

Required for: Dust Devils, Sandswirls（必需用于：沙尘恶魔）

④ Slayer Helmet (Combined Item — Endgame)（④ 杀戮者头盔，组合物品，终局）

Required for: Replaces all individual Slayer items（必需用于：替代所有单独杀戮者物品）

6. Block & Skip List for Beginners（6. 新手屏蔽与跳过列表）

One of the most important mechanics in Slayer is the ability to block certain tasks (permanently remove them from your task pool) and skip tasks (temporarily reject a task, costing Slayer points). Managing your block and skip list effectively is the difference between enjoyable Slayer and a miserable grind.

🚫 Tasks You Should BLOCK Immediately (As Soon as You Have 100+ Slayer Points)（🚫 你应该立即屏蔽的任务，有100+杀戮者点数后）

Blocking a task costs 100 Slayer points and removes that monster from your task pool permanently (until you unblock it). You can have up to 5 blocked tasks at once (more if you spend points to increase the limit). Here are the first blocks every player should get:

① TzHaar (Jad) — Block at Slayer Level 1（① TzHaar — 杀戮者1级时屏蔽）

Why block: TzHaar tasks require fighting in the Fight Caves (wave-based minigame). This is an endgame boss fight, not a Slayer task for beginners. You will almost certainly die, waste time, and get zero loot. Block this immediately.
Exception: Only unblock TzHaar when you're specifically going for the Fire Cape and want to use Slayer points to get Fight Caves attempts.（例外：只有在专门去拿火披风时才取消屏蔽）

② Bronze/Spiritual Creatures (Low Priority)（② 青铜/灵体生物，低优先级）

Why block: Spiritual Creatures (assigned by some masters) are located in the God Wars Dungeon, which requires 60 Agility to access and has extremely high-level monsters. As a beginner, you cannot efficiently do this task. Block it until you're at endgame.（为什么屏蔽：位于God Wars地牢，需要60敏捷和极高等级）

③ Suqahs — Block After First Couple Encounters（③ Suqahs — 遇到几次后屏蔽）

Why block: Suqahs are located on Lunar Isle and require Fremennik Diary partial completion or Lunar Diplomacy quest progress to access efficiently. They're also not very profitable for beginners. Block to save time.（为什么屏蔽：位于Lunar Isle，需要Fremennik日记或Lunar Diplomacy任务）

④ Jungle Horrors / Cramps — Block If You Dislike Them（④ 丛林恐怖/痉挛 — 如果不喜欢就屏蔽）

Why block: These tasks are assigned frequently and involve running around collecting dinosaurs or fighting in inconvenient locations. Not "bad" tasks, but if you find them annoying, block them. Your Slayer time should be enjoyable.（为什么屏蔽：频繁分配且位置不便）

⑤ Anything in the Wilderness（⑤ 荒野中的任何任务）

Why block: Any task that requires going into the Wilderness (PKers can attack you) should be blocked if you're not comfortable PvPing. This includes certain Skeleton assignments, Green Dragons, and Revs tasks. Your safety is more important than the GP from these tasks.（为什么屏蔽：任何需要进入荒野的任务都应屏蔽，你的安全比这些任务的金币更重要）

🔄 When to Skip vs. When to Stay（🔄 何时跳过 vs 何时留下）

Skipping a task costs 2–5 Slayer points (depending on your current master). As a beginner with few points, skip sparingly. Here's the framework:

Your Slayer Points（你的杀戮者点数）Skip Behavior（跳过行为）Example（示例）
0–30 pointsNever skip (always stay)（绝不跳过）You need points to buy the Slayer Helmet; skipping wastes progress（你需要点数购买杀戮者头盔）
30–100 pointsSkip only if task is dangerous（只在任务危险时跳过）Wilderness tasks, high-level bosses you can't kill yet（荒野任务，你还不能杀的高级Boss）
100–300 pointsSkip moderately annoying tasks（跳过中等烦人的任务）Tasks that are tedious but not dangerous（繁琐但不危险的任务）
300+ pointsSkip freely（自由跳过）You have enough points for all unlocks; optimize for enjoyment（你有足够点数解锁所有内容）

🎯 Slayer Unlocks Priority (How to Spend Your Points)（🎯 杀戮者解锁优先级，如何花点数）

Slayer points can be spent on various unlocks. Here's the order of priority for beginners:

  1. Slayer Helmet (400 points): Combines all Slayer protection items into one. This is the #1 priority.（杀戮者头盔：将所有杀戮者防护物品合并为一，这是第一优先）
  2. Second Slot (100 points): Allows a 6th blocked task. Quality of life upgrade.（第二个槽位：允许第6个屏蔽任务，生活质量升级）
  3. Broad Bolts/Arrows (300 points each): Required to kill certain Slayer monsters with Ranged. Get Broad Bolts first (more common).（宽头弹药/箭：需要用远程击杀某些杀戮者怪物）
  4. Ring of Wealth (250 points): Teleport to Grand Exchange; note that LoS (double loot) is now unlocked via totems, not points.（财富之戒：传送到Grand Exchange）
  5. Herb Sack (350 points): Stores herbs; useful if you're also training Herblore.（草药袋：储存草药，如果你也在训练药草学很有用）
  6. Extra Block Slots (100 points each): Each additional slot lets you block one more monster type.（额外屏蔽槽位：每个额外槽位可多屏蔽一种怪物）
💡 The 400-Point Milestone（💡 400点里程碑）

Your absolute #1 goal as a new Slayer player is to accumulate 400 Slayer points as quickly as possible. That's roughly 200 tasks with Turael (0 points) — so don't use Turael for long. Switch to Vannaka (2 points/task) as soon as you hit Combat 40. At 2 points per task, you need about 200 tasks to reach 400 points. That sounds like a lot, but by the time you're at Duradel (5 points/task), you'll be earning points much faster. Stay focused on the Slayer Helmet — it transforms the skill.

7. Slayer FAQs for Beginners（7. 新手杀戮者常见问题）

What Combat level should I be before starting Slayer?（开始杀戮者前需要什么战斗等级？）

You can start Slayer at Combat level 1 with Turael. However, the tasks become meaningful (useful loot, decent XP) at around Combat level 30–40 when you unlock Vannaka. If you're a pure account (intentionally keeping Combat low), you can still do Slayer — just stick to Turael and focus on tasks that don't raise Combat levels you want to keep low.

How many Slayer points do I need for all important unlocks?（所有重要解锁需要多少杀戮者点数？）

Here's the approximate total: Slayer Helmet (400) + Broad Bolts (300) + Broad Arrows (300) + Ring of Wealth (250) + Herb Sack (350) + Extra Block Slot (100) = 1,700 points. At 5 points per task (Duradel), that's about 340 tasks. It's a long-term investment, but every unlock makes Slayer more enjoyable and efficient.

Can I do Slayer on an Ironman account?（我可以在铁人模式账号上做杀戮者吗？）

Absolutely — Slayer is even MORE important on Ironman because many rare drops (Dragon items, herbs, seeds) are most efficiently obtained through Slayer. The same master progression applies. The only difference is that you can't buy Slayer equipment from the GE, so you'll need to obtain them through other means (quests, monster drops, or the Slayer Equipment shop using points).

What should I do if I get a task I can't complete?（如果接到无法完成的任务怎么办？）

First, check if you have the required Slayer level. If you don't, the master won't assign that task to you (the game prevents this). If you physically can't complete the task (not enough food, can't access the location), use your Turael skip (free) or spend points to skip it. Don't struggle through a task that isn't fun — Slayer is a long-term skill, and enjoyment matters.

Is Slayer worth it for F2P players?（杀戮者对免费版玩家值得吗？）

Slayer is technically available in F2P, but it's extremely limited. There's only one F2P Slayer Master (Turael, essentially), and the task pool is tiny (mostly cows, spiders, giants). F2P Slayer is mainly useful for getting a few early tasks done while you save up for membership. Once you have membership, the entire skill opens up.

How long does it take to get 99 Slayer?（达到99杀戮者需要多长时间？）

At efficient rates (Duradel, good gear, no skipping), 99 Slayer takes approximately 150–200 hours. At beginner rates (Vannaka, budget gear, skipping some tasks), it can take 300–400 hours. The good news is that Slayer doesn't feel like a grind because every task is different. Most players naturally reach 99 Slayer over 1–2 years of casual play.

Should I use Melee, Ranged, or Magic for Slayer tasks?（杀戮者任务应该用近战、远程还是魔法？）

Melee is the default for 80% of Slayer tasks because it's the most GP-efficient and doesn't consume expensive ammo. Ranged is excellent for tasks where you can safe-spot (Dust Devils, some dragons). Magic is rarely the best choice for general Slayer (expensive runes), but it's essential for certain monsters (Susceptible to Magic attacks). As a beginner, focus on building a good Melee setup first, then add Ranged as a secondary option.
