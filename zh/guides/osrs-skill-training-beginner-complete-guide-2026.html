1. Understanding OSRS's 23 Skills — The Big Picture（1. 理解OSRS的23项技能 — 大局观（理解OSRS的23项技能））

Old School RuneScape has 23 trainable skills (24 if you count the upcoming Sailing). That number alone can be overwhelming to a new player. But here's the secret: you don't need to understand them all at once, and you definitely shouldn't try to train them all at once.

Think of OSRS skills like a massive ecosystem. Some skills feed directly into others. Some are prerequisites for content you actually want to do. Some are "force multipliers" that make every other skill easier. And some are just there for the completionists.

② The 5 Categories of OSRS Skills（② OSRS技能的5大类别（OSRS技能的5大类别））

To make sense of 23 skills, group them into 5 categories. This changes how you approach training entirely:

Combat Skills (7 skills)（战斗技能（战斗技能，7项））

Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Ranged, Magic, Prayer

These determine your ability to fight monsters, complete quests, and survive in dangerous areas. Your Combat Level is calculated from these skills, and many pieces of content have Combat Level requirements.

Gathering Skills (6 skills)（采集技能（采集技能，6项））

Mining, Fishing, Woodcutting, Farming, Hunter, Firemaking

These are "active" skills where you go out into the world and collect raw resources. They are almost always profitable to train (you sell what you gather). The trade-off: they can be click-intensive and repetitive.

Artisan Skills (7 skills)（工匠技能（工匠技能，7项））

Smithing, Cooking, Crafting, Herblore, Fletching, Runecrafting, Construction

These turn raw resources (from Gathering skills) into useful items — gear, potions, food, ammunition. Most Artisan skills are expensive to train (you consume resources for XP), but the items you make are essential for PvM and bossing.

Support Skills (3 skills)（支持技能（支持技能，3项））

Agility, Thieving, Slayer

These don't fit neatly into Combat or Production. Instead, they unlock access to new areas, faster travel, better gear, and (in Slayer's case) the most efficient combat training method in the game.

③ How Skills Connect — The Dependency Web（③ 技能如何连接 — 依赖网络（技能如何连接））

Skills in OSRS don't exist in isolation. They form a web of dependencies:

  • Combat needs Gear: To train combat efficiently, you need good weapons and armor. These come from Smithing (metal gear) and Crafting (leather/dragonhide gear). Those skills need resources from Mining and Hunter.
  • Herblore boosts Combat: Potions (from Herblore) massively boost combat effectiveness. Herblore requires herbs, which come from Farming.
  • Agility saves time everywhere: Agility shortcuts let you bypass obstacles. Higher Agility = faster run energy recovery = less time running between resources.
  • Slayer = Money + XP: Slayer tasks drop valuable loot. Higher Slayer level = access to better monsters = more GP per hour. Slayer also gives the best Combat XP in the game.
  • Construction unlocks QoL: A Player-Owned House (Construction) gives you teleports, a Gilded Altar for Prayer training, and a jewelry box for fast travel.
The Golden Rule of OSRS Skill Training

Never train a skill in isolation. Always ask: "What does this skill unlock for me?" and "Can I train this while also making progress toward something I actually want to do?" The best OSRS players find ways to train multiple skills at once through questing, Slayer, and smart resource management.

④ Why You Should NOT Try to Max All Skills Early（④ 为什么不应该早期尝试将所有技能练满（为什么不应该早期尝试将所有技能练满））

  • Some skills are huge GP sinks: Herblore, Construction, and Prayer can cost 50-200M GP to reach 99. You need to make money first.
  • Some skills are only useful at high levels: Agility below level 60 is barely noticeable. Construction below level 50 gives almost no benefits.
  • Your account "build" matters: A "Pure" account (low Defence, high Attack/Strength) trains skills very differently from a "Main" account.
  • Content unlocks matter more than skill levels: Having 70 Attack is meaningless if you haven't done the quests to wield the Dragon Scimitar.

The smart approach: focus on Combat and Money Making skills first. Get your combat stats to mid-game levels (70s), build up a bank of 10-50M GP, and then start power-levelling the expensive Artisan skills.

2. Combat Skills — Your Foundation (7 Skills)（2. 战斗技能 — 你的基础（战斗技能 — 你的基础，7项））

Combat is the heart of OSRS. Even if you want to be a "skill-only" account, you'll eventually need combat stats to complete quests, access areas, and defend yourself. Here's how each of the 7 combat skills works and the best beginner training methods.

⑤ Attack — Unlocking Better Weapons（⑤ 攻击 — 解锁更好的武器（攻击 — 解锁更好的武器））

What it does: Determines which weapons you can wield and accuracy with melee attacks. Higher Attack = more accurate hits with melee weapons.

Why it matters: At Attack 60, you unlock the Dragon Scimitar (best pre-70 melee weapon). At Attack 70, you unlock Abyssal Whip (best non-degradeable melee weapon for a long time).

Beginner Training Method

Levels 1-40: Train at Chickens (Lumbridge) → Cows (Lumbridge) → Al-Kharid Warriors. Use the best sword/axe you can afford. Focus on controlled attack style (grants XP to Attack, Strength, and Defence simultaneously).

Levels 40-60: Rock Crabs (Hosidius/Kandarin) or Sand Crabs (Zeah). These have high HP and low Defence, making them perfect for AFK combat training. Bring food and a weapon that attacks quickly (Scimitar preferred).

Levels 60-70: Continue at Crabs, or start Slayer (recommended). Slayer gives Combat XP while also progressing your Slayer level, which unlocks better task variety.

⑥ Strength — Hitting Harder（⑥ 力量 — 打出更高伤害（力量 — 打出更高伤害））

What it does: Determines your maximum hit damage. Higher Strength = higher damage per hit.

Why it matters: Strength is the most important damage stat for melee. A player with 80 Strength hits noticeably harder than a player with 60 Strength, even if both have the same weapon.

Beginner Training Method

Same as Attack — use the "Controlled" attack style at Crabs to train Attack, Strength, and Defence equally. Once you reach higher levels (60+), consider using the "Agressive" style to specifically boost Strength if your goal is max melee damage.

Strength Bonus Gear: Even before you have high Strength levels, wearing strength-boosting gear (like the Amulet of Strength or Obsidian Platebody) increases your max hit. Always check the "Strength bonus" on gear before fighting.

⑦ Defence — Taking Less Damage（⑦ 防御 — 承受更少伤害（防御 — 承受更少伤害））

What it does: Reduces the chance that enemies hit you. Higher Defence = you take less damage and get hit less often.

Why it matters: Defence is controversial in OSRS. Some players keep it at level 1 (on "Pures") to keep their Combat Level low for PvP. For PvM (PvE), Defence is valuable because it reduces food costs and death risk.

Beginner Training Method

Train Defence alongside Attack and Strength using the "Controlled" attack style. There's no separate "Defence only" method that's efficient — always train it in combination with melee combat.

Defence Milestones: Defence 40 unlocks Dragon Armor (huge defence boost). Defence 60 unlocks Barrows Armor (even bigger boost). Defence 70 unlocks Bandos Armor (best melee defence in the game).

⑧ Hitpoints — Your Health Bar（⑧ 生命值 — 你的血条（生命值 — 你的血条））

What it does: Determines how much damage you can take before dying. Starts at 10 HP.

Why it matters: More HP = more room for error in combat. However, HP is tied to Combat Level calculation, so some PvP builds keep it as low as possible.

Beginner Training Method

Hitpoints train automatically whenever you deal damage in combat (and when you take damage). You don't need to do anything special — just train Attack/Strength/Defence, and your HP will rise naturally. Most players hit 70+ HP by the time they have 60+ melee stats.

⑨ Ranged — Distance Combat（⑨ 远程 — 远程战斗（远程 — 远程战斗））

What it does: Allows you to fight monsters from a distance with bows, crossbows, and thrown weapons. Also determines accuracy and damage with ranged attacks.

Why it matters: Ranged is essential for "Safe Spotting" (killing monsters without taking damage). Many bosses are weak to Ranged. It's also the best style for Slayer tasks where you want to avoid damage.

Beginner Training Method

Levels 1-40: Cows (collect cowhides and range them) or Chickens. Use a Shortbow with Iron arrows. Train at the Archery range in Lumbridge (F2P) or Cows in Lumbridge swamp.

Levels 40-70: Dark Bow (cheap, high damage) or Adamant Crossbow (accurate). Train on Rock Crabs or Hill Giants. Bring 10+ lobsters for healing.

Levels 70+: Rune Crossbow + Broad bolts (requires Slayer 55). This is the best pre-75 Ranged setup and will carry you through most mid-game content.

⑩ Magic — Spells & Utility（⑩ 魔法 — 法术与实用功能（魔法 — 法术与实用功能））

What it does: Allows you to cast combat spells (damage, freeze, teleport) and utility spells (teleportation, enchanting, alchemy). Also determines accuracy with magical attacks.

Why it matters: Magic is the most versatile combat style. It offers the best teleportation network in the game, can kill monsters safely from a distance, and has unique abilities (like freezing enemies or using Ancient Magicks for multi-target damage).

Beginner Training Method

Levels 1-35: Cast Wind Strike → Water Strike → Earth Strike → Fire Strike on chickenses or cows. Alternatively, do "Splashing" (cast curse spells on a low-defence NPC while wearing full metal armor so you always miss — this is controversial and boring, but it works).

Levels 35-55: Cast Fire Bolt on Hill Giants or Moss Giants. At level 55, unlock High Alchemy (converts items into GP) — this is more important than combat spells for most players.

Levels 55-70: High Alchemy is now your best friend. Buy nature runes and items from the Grand Exchange, alch them for profit. This trains Magic while making GP. It's click-intensive but very effective.

Prayer — Combat Multiplier（祈祷 — 战斗倍增器）

What it does: Unlocks prayers that boost your combat stats, protect you from damage, and (at high levels) massively increase your damage output.

Why it matters: Prayer is covered in detail in a separate guide, but the short version: get to Prayer 43 as soon as possible to unlock the three Protect Prayers. This alone makes you nearly invincible against most monsters.

Beginner Training Method

See the Prayer Training Guide for full details. Quick summary: bury bones from monsters you kill → use a Gilded Altar (members) for fast XP → aim for 43 Prayer first, then 70 (Piety) when you have the GP.

Combat Triangle in Practice

The Combat Triangle (Melee beats Ranged, Ranged beats Magic, Magic beats Melee) is a simplification. In practice, most monsters have a "weakness" to a specific combat style. Check the monster's page in-game (right-click → Examine, or look up on the OSRS Wiki) to see which style does the most damage. Adjusting your style to match the monster's weakness can increase your DPS by 20-30%.

3. Gathering Skills — Where Resources Come From (6 Skills)（3. 采集技能 — 资源来源（采集技能 — 资源来源，6项））

Gathering skills are the "money makers" of OSRS. You go out into the world, collect resources, and sell them on the Grand Exchange. They're almost always profitable to train (unlike Artisan skills which cost GP). Here's every Gathering skill explained:

Mining — Digging for Ores and Gems（采矿 — 挖掘矿石和宝石）

What it does: Allows you to mine rocks for ores, gems, and other resources. Ores are smelted into bars (Smithing) or sold for GP.

Beginner method: Mine Copper and Tin (levels 1-15) at Lumbridge Swamp or Varrock East. Then Iron ores (levels 15-99) — Iron is always profitable because it's used for Smithing training.

Key milestones: Mining 15 unlocks Iron (best XP/GP balance). Mining 30 unlocks Coal. Mining 55 unlocks Adamant. Mining 70 unlocks Runite (high GP but rare).

Mining Tip

Always check the Grand Exchange prices before mining. Sometimes Coal is more profitable than Iron. Also, join a Mining guild (level 60) for faster XP rates due to invisible +7 Mining boost from the成功举办.

Fishing — Catching Fish for Food and GP（钓鱼 — 钓鱼获取食物和金币）

What it does: Catch fish from fishing spots. Fish are used for Cooking training, fed to pets, or sold on GE.

Beginner method: Shrimp (level 1) → Sardines (level 5) → Herring (level 10) → Trout/Salmon (level 20-30, use fly fishing at Shilo Village or Barbarian Village) → Lobsters (level 40) → Swordfish (level 50) → Monkfish (level 62) → Shark (level 76).

Most profitable: Monkfish (level 62) and Shark (level 76) are the best money-making fishing methods. Monkfish is especially good because it's close to a bank.

Woodcutting — Chopping Trees for Logs（伐木 — 砍树获取原木）

What it does: Chop down trees for logs. Logs are used for Fletching (make arrows/bolts), Firemaking (burn for XP), or sold on GE.

Beginner method: Normal trees (level 1) → Oak trees (level 15) → Willow trees (level 30, best AFK method) → Maple trees (level 45) → Yew trees (level 60, profitable) → Magic trees (level 75, very profitable but slow).

Best AFK method: Willow trees at Draynor Village (World 36 is the official Woodcutting world). You can AFK for 5-10 minutes at a time.

Firemaking — Burning Logs for XP（生火 — 烧原木获取经验）

What it does: Burn logs to train Firemaking. Also unlocks the ability to light fires for cooking and travel (via the Ring of Fire).

Beginner method: Burn the logs you get from Woodcutting. There's no "efficient" method — just burn everything. Wintertodt (level 50+) is also a great way to train Firemaking while getting other rewards.

Wintertodt: At Firemaking 50, you can fight the Wintertodt (a boss that trains Firemaking). It gives good GP, seeds (for Farming), and other loot. Highly recommended.

Farming — Growing Resources Over Time（农耕 — 随时间种植资源）

What it does: Plant seeds in patches, wait (or do other things), then harvest. Farming is unique because you can "pre-plant" before going to sleep/work and harvest when you return.

Beginner method: Start with Herb runs (level 9+). Plant herbs in the 4 herb patches (Falador, Catherby, Ardougne, Port Phasmatys). Harvest and sell the herbs, or use them for Herblore training.

Most profitable: Ranarr seeds (level 32) and Snapdragon seeds (level 62) are the most valuable herbs. Also, Tree runs (plant trees every 64 hours) give massive XP and profit.

Farming Routine for Beginners（新手农耕日常）

Every day: do a herb run (plant Ranarr or Snapdragon). Every 3 days: do a tree run (plant Yew or Magic trees). Use Ultracompost on every patch to increase yield. This routine takes 10 minutes per day and makes 200-500K GP profit.

Hunter — Catchning Creatures for Loot

What it does: Catch animals, birds, and other creatures. Loot includes furs (sold on GE), herbs (from catching butterflies), and unique items.

Beginner method: Starts at level 1 with Crimson Swifts (butterflies) in the Piscatoris Hunting Area. Then Chinchompas (level 53+) — these are the most profitable Hunter method. Red Chinchompas (level 63) sell for ~1,200 GP each and you can catch 100-150 per hour.

Why Hunter matters: Chinchompas are used as Ranged ammunition that hit multiple enemies. They're essential for some boss fights and very profitable to sell.

4. Artisan Skills — Turning Raw Into Gold (7 Skills)（4. 工匠技能 — 变原料为黄金（工匠技能 — 变原料为黄金，7项））

Artisan skills consume resources (from Gathering skills) to create useful items. Most are expensive to train because you "burn" resources for XP. However, the items you make are essential for PvM, bossing, and making GP efficiently.

Smithing — Making Metal Gear and Ammo（锻造 — 制作金属装备和弹药）

What it does: Smelt ores into bars, then smith bars into weapons, armor, and ammunition.

Beginner method: Smelt Copper/Tin into Bronze bars (level 1). Smith Bronze into weapons/armor. At higher levels, buy ores/bars from GE and smith them for XP.

Most efficient: Blast Furnace (requires partial completion of the Depot DVD quest, or just use the minigame). Blast Furnace gives 2x smelting speed and uses less coal. At Smithing 70+, make Dragon equipment or Rune equipment for profit.

Smithing Cost Warning

Smithing is a MASSIVE GP sink. Getting 99 Smithing can cost 100M+ GP if you buy all materials. The cheapest method is to mine your own ores (free but slow). Most players train Smithing in bursts when they have surplus GP.

Cooking — Making Food for Healing（烹饪 — 制作回复食物）

What it does: Cook raw fish on a fire or range. Cooked food heals HP during combat.

Beginner method: Cook the fish you catch from Fishing. Start with Shrimp, then Sardines, Trout, Salmon, Lobsters, Swordfish, Monkfish, Sharks.

Efficient method: Buy raw fish from GE in bulk, cook at a range with a cooking gauntlet (chef's guild or Hosidius kitchen). The gauntlet reduces burn chance.

Profit: Cooking can be profitable if you cook high-level fish (Sharks, Manta rays) and sell them. However, most players cook for XP, not profit.

Crafting — Making Gear, Ammo, and Utilities（手工艺 — 制作装备、弹药和实用物品）

What it does: Create leather gear, dragonhide armor, gem jewelry, pottery, and many other items.

Beginner method: Spin wool into balls (level 1). Craft leather from cows (level 7). Make gold jewelry (level 14+). At higher levels, craft Dragonhide armor (level 60+) — this is very profitable.

Most profitable: Green Dragonhide (level 60) and Blue Dragonhide (level 68) bodies sell for high prices. Also, Cutting gems into bolt tips (level 73+) is decent GP.

Herblore — Making Potions for Buffs（草药学 — 制作增益药水）

What it does: Create potions that boost stats, restore prayer, cure poison, and provide other combat buffs.

Beginner method: Buy herbs from GE, clean them (level 1-3), then mix with secondary ingredients. Start with Attack potions (level 3) and Strength potions (level 7).

Essential potions to make: Prayer potions (level 38), Super Attack/Strength/Defence (level 55/55/66), Saradomin Brew (level 66), Super Restore (level 63).

Herblore Training on a Budget（预算有限的草药学训练）

Herblore is VERY expensive. To reduce costs: (1) Grow your own herbs via Farming. (2) Do Herbiboar (Hunter 80+) for free herbs. (3) Buy potions during Double XP weekends (cheaper). (4) Only train Herblore when you have 10M+ GP bank.

Fletching — Making Ranged Ammunition（制箭 — 制作远程弹药）

What it does: Fletch logs into bows, arrows, bolts, and dartss. Essential for Ranged training.

Beginner method: Fletch Shortbows (level 5) → Longbows (level 10) → Oak shortbows (level 20) → Willow shortbows (level 35) → Maple shortbows (level 50) → Yew shortbows (level 65) → Magic shortbows (level 80).

Most profitable: Dragon arrows (level 90) and Amethyst arrows (level 82) are decent GP. However, most players fletch for XP, not profit.

Runecrafting — Making Runes for Magic（符文制作 — 制作魔法符文）

What it does: Craft runes from rune essence. Runes are used for Magic spells (combat, teleportation, alchemy).

Why it's hated: Runecrafting is widely considered the most tedious skill in OSRS. You mine essence, run to an altar, craft runes, run back, repeat. It's slow and click-intensive.

Beginner method: Air runes (level 1) → Mind runes (level 2) → Water runes (level 5) → Earth runes (level 9) → Fire runes (level 14) → Body runes (level 20) → Cosmic runes (level 27) → Nature runes (level 44, very profitable) → Law runes (level 54) → Death runes (level 65) → Blood runes (level 77, most profitable).

Runecrafting Alternative

If you hate Runecrafting (most players do), you can skip training it by: (1) Buying runes from other players or the GE. (2) Using Spirit trees (Farming 83) to teleport to rune shops. (3) Joining a Runecrafting guild (level 50) for faster methods. Many players only train Runecrafting to level 77 (Blood runes) for profit, then stop.

Construction — Building Your Player-Owned House（建筑 — 建造你的玩家住宅）

What it does: Build and upgrade your Player-Owned House (POH). A high-level POH gives you teleports, a Gilded Altar (fast Prayer XP), and a jewelry box (fast travel).

Beginner method: Complete the Construction tutorial (costs ~5K GP). Build basic furniture (chair, table, etc.). At level 20+, build Oak furniture for faster XP.

Most important milestones: Construction 30 unlocks Bedroom (restores HP). Construction 38 unlocks Gilded Altar (350% Prayer XP). Construction 50 unlocks Jewelry box (teleports to jewelry locations). Construction 75 unlocks Eternal teleport spells.

Cost: Construction is the MOST expensive skill to train. 99 Construction can cost 200M+ GP. Most players only train it to level 50-75 (for POH utilities), then stop.

5. Support Skills — The Force Multipliers (3 Skills)（5. 支持技能 — 力量倍增器（支持技能 — 力量倍增器，3项））

Support skills don't fit neatly into Combat or Production. Instead, they unlock access to new areas, faster travel, better gear, and more efficient training methods. Training these makes EVERY other skill faster.

Agility — Move Faster, Unlock Shortcuts（敏捷 — 移动更快，解锁捷径）

What it does: Unlocks shortcuts around the map, increases run energy recovery rate, and grants access to important areas.

Why it's a force multiplier: Higher Agility = faster run recovery = less time running between resources = more XP per hour in EVERY other skill.

Beginner Training Method

Levels 1-30: Gnome Stronghold Agility Course (easiest).

Levels 30-60: Al-Kharid Rooftop Course (best XP rates for F2P and low-level members).

Levels 60+: Seers' Village Rooftop Course or Pollnivneach Rooftop Course.

Graceful Armor — Why Agility Matters

At Agility 60+, you can buy Graceful armor (costs ~260 Marks of Grace). Graceful gives +30% run energy recovery and +5 kg weight reduction. This makes EVERY activity in OSRS faster.

Thieving — Steal Items, Unlock Areas（盗窃 — 偷窃物品，解锁区域）

What it does: Pickpocket NPCs and steal from stalls. Gives GP, unlock keys, and access to certain areas.

Beginner method: Levels 1-5: Pickpocket Men/Women. Levels 5-25: Cake stalls in Ardougne (fastest low-level GP). Levels 25-65: Master Farmers (pickpocket for seeds). Levels 65+: Pyramid Plunder.

Slayer — The Best Combat Training Method（杀戮 — 最佳战斗训练方法）

What it does: Complete tasks assigned by Slayer Masters. Tasks involve killing specific monsters. Grants Combat XP AND Slayer XP.

Why it's the BEST: Slayer tasks drop valuable loot (GP), give XP in ALL 7 combat skills simultaneously, and unlock access to better monsters as your Slayer level rises.

When to Start Slayer（何时开始杀戮）

Most players start Slayer at Combat levels 40-50. Before that, your combat stats are too low to effectively kill most Slayer monsters. Talk to Turael in Burthorpe to start (no requirements).

6. Skill Synergy — Training Multiple Skills Together（6. 技能协同 — 同时训练多项技能（技能协同 — 同时训练多项技能））

The BEST OSRS players don't train skills one at a time. They find ways to train MULTIPLE skills simultaneously. This is called "skill synergy" and it's the key to efficient account progression.

Combo 1: Slayer = 8 Skills at Once（组合1：杀戮 = 同时训练8项技能）

When you do Slayer tasks, you gain XP in: Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Ranged, Magic, Prayer (from burying bones), AND Slayer. That's 8 skills at once!

Combo 2: Farming Herbs + Herblore（组合2：农耕草药 + 草药学）

Plant herbs in Farming patches → harvest herbs → use herbs for Herblore training. A single herb run (10 minutes) gives 50-100K XP in Farming AND provides herbs worth 200-500K GP.

Combo 3: Questing = Multiple Skills + Unlocks（组合3：做任务 = 多项技能 + 解锁）

Many quests give XP in multiple skills. Waterfall Quest gives 13,750 Attack XP AND 13,750 Strength XP. Always check the OSRS Wiki's "Quest Experience Rewards" page before training a skill!

Sample "Synergy Schedule" for a New Member（新会员的"协同日程"示例）

Morning (30 mins): Do a Farming herb run + tree run. Trains Farming, gives herbs for Herblore.

Mid-day (2 hours): Do Slayer tasks. Trains all 7 Combat skills + Slayer.

Evening (1 hour): Train a Gathering skill (Mining/Fishing/Woodcutting). Makes GP for bonds/gear.

This schedule trains 10+ skills daily and makes 200-500K GP profit.

7. Common Beginner Mistakes in Skill Training（7. 技能训练中新手的常见错误（技能训练中新手的常见错误））

Every OSRS player makes mistakes when they're new. Here are the most common ones:

Mistake #1: Trying to Max Every Skill Immediately

OSRS is measured in months and years, not days. Focus on 3-5 skills at a time. Get them to useful levels (Combat 70, Prayer 43, Agility 60).

Mistake #2: Training Skills in Isolation

Training Mining for 10 hours straight is boring AND inefficient. Always combine skills. Do Slayer (trains 8 skills). Do Farming runs.

Mistake #3: Ignoring Quests

Quests give MASSIVE XP rewards. Waterfall Quest gives 27,500 total Combat XP. Check the OSRS Wiki's "Quest Experience Rewards" page before training!

Mistake #4: Training Expensive Skills Too Early

Herblore, Construction, and Prayer cost hundreds of millions of GP to reach 99. Only train them AFTER you have 10-50M GP bank.

Mistake #5: Not Using the Wiki

The OSRS Wiki (oldschool.runescape.wiki) is the most comprehensive game guide ever created. Use it for EVERYTHING.

9. Skill Training FAQs（9. 技能训练常见问题（技能训练常见问题））

What order should I train combat skills?

Most efficient: (1) Attack/Strength/Defence together with "Controlled" style to 40-60. (2) Prayer to 43. (3) Ranged/Magic to 40-60. (4) Finish melee to 70+.

Should I train F2P skills before becoming a member?

Yes! F2P skills are cheaper and give you a head start. Also save 500K-1M GP before becoming a member.

What's the best skill for making GP as a beginner?

Best money-making skills: (1) Mining Iron (50-100K GP/hour). (2) Fishing Lobsters (50-150K GP/hour). (3) Thieving Cake stalls (50-100K GP/hour).

How much GP do I need for Herblore/Construction/Prayer to 99?

Estimates: Prayer 43-99: 10-80M GP. Herblore 1-99: 150-250M GP. Construction 1-99: 200M+ GP. Most players train these after 50M+ GP bank.
