🔄 Ring of Recoil Is Mandatory（反伤戒指是必须的）

Zulrah's snakelings deal damage to you even with prayer. Ring of Recoil reflects 1 damage per hit to snakelings, killing them without you wasting DPS. Bring 2 rings per kill while learning — they break after 40 damage reflected. Once you have a Ring of Suffering (i), it stores recoil charges and lasts indefinitely.

This tells you which of the 4 rotations you're following. Use a Zulrah rotation helper (website overlay or RuneLite plugin) until you memorize them.

4. Phase-by-Phase Survival Guide（分阶段生存指南）

Green Phase (Mage Attacks — Use Ranged)（绿色阶段 · 用远程打）

Zulrah is green. It shoots magic attacks that hit up to 41. Activate Protect from Magic. Switch to your Ranged gear and DPS. Stand on safe tiles (avoid the toxic clouds Zulrah spawns). This is your best DPS window — maximize damage here.

Blue/Tanzanite Phase (Range Attacks — Use Magic)（蓝色阶段 · 用魔法打）

Zulrah is blue. It shoots Ranged attacks that can deal heavy damage. Activate Protect from Missiles. Switch to Mage gear. Watch for snakelings and venom clouds. The blue phase often includes moving toxic clouds — keep an eye on your feet.

Red/Melee Phase — DO NOT ATTACK（红色近战阶段 · 不要攻击！）

Zulrah is red and on the ground. It uses Melee attacks. You CANNOT attack Zulrah in Red form — any attack will be nullified. Just focus on avoiding toxic clouds and repositioning for the next phase.

Jad Phase (Rotation 3 Only) — Prayer Switching Required（Jad阶段 · 仅存在于旋转3 · 需要切换祈祷）

Zulrah alternates between Magic and Ranged attacks, one per tick. You must switch prayers between each attack. Watch the projectile: Green/blue = Magic (Protect from Magic), White/pink = Ranged (Protect from Missiles). Switch your prayer AFTER you see the projectile but BEFORE it hits you. This is the hardest phase for beginners — practice makes perfect.

🔑 Jad Phase Survival Tip（Jad阶段生存技巧）

During the Jad phase, PRIORITIZE PRAYER SWITCHING over attacking. If you attack between switches, you'll lose ticks and potentially miss a prayer swap. Just focus on surviving the phase — you can DPS Zulrah in the next green phase. Many beginners die here because they try to do too much at once.

7. Common Mistakes Beginners Make（初学者常见错误）

  1. Focusing on DPS instead of survival. Your first 20 kills should prioritize staying alive, not speed. Learn the rotations, the safe tiles, and the prayer switches. Speed comes after consistency.
  2. Not memorizing safe tiles. During each phase, specific tiles are safe from toxic clouds. Stand on the east side pillars for most phases. Watch a tile guide on YouTube — knowing exactly where to stand eliminates 50% of beginner damage.
  3. Panic eating during Jad phase. Eat between attacks, not during them. If you try to eat while Zulrah is attacking, you'll miss the prayer switch and take 41 damage. Brews are better than food for Zulrah because they don't delay your attacks.
  4. Forgetting to re-equip Ring of Recoil. When a ring breaks mid-kill, snakelings will overwhelm you. Keep a spare ring in your inventory and check your ring slot after every kill.
  5. Not using the Zulrah helper tool. Keep a rotation image or RuneLite plugin visible on a second monitor. No one memorizes all 4 rotations on their first attempt — use the visual aid.

8. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: How many attempts does it take to get the first Zulrah kill?（第一次击杀需要多少次尝试？）

A: Typically 10-20 attempts. Most players die 5-10 times before surviving long enough to see the Jad phase, and another 5-10 times learning the Jad phase. Don't get discouraged — every death teaches you the rotations. The first kill is the hardest by far.

Q: Is Trident of the Seas required for Zulrah?（打Zulrah必须用Trident of the Seas吗？）

A: Strongly recommended but not strictly required. You CAN use Iban's Blast (level 50 Magic) for budget kills, but kills will be significantly slower (4-5 minutes vs 2-3 minutes) and you'll use more supplies. The Trident of the Seas (75 Magic, ~3M GP) is the single best upgrade for learning Zulrah.

Q: What's the best way to avoid Zulrah's venom?（如何避免Zulrah的毒液？）

A: Anti-venom+ potions (or Serpentine Helm). Anti-venom+ provides immunity to venom for ~12 minutes. Drink one dose at the start of your trip and you'll be immune for the entire session. If you can't afford Anti-venom+, regular Antipoison potions work but require more frequent sipping.

By kill 100, you'll wonder why it ever seemed hard. The 1M+ GP/hr reward is worth every frustrating death along the way.

Teleport to Zul-Andra, gear up, and start learning. The money snake awaits.
