🧥 OSRS Curse of the Empty Lord Mini-Quest Guide — Find Viggora & Get the Ghostly Robes

The Curse of the Empty Lord is a haunting mini-quest that takes you across Gielinor to track down the ghost of Viggora — a warrior cursed by Zamorak after betraying Zaros. Complete it and you'll earn the full Ghostly Robes set, one of the rarest cosmetic outfits in OSRS. This guide covers every step, every Viggora location, and the lore behind the curse.

空之诅咒迷你任务完整攻略：三个Viggora刷新坐标、幽灵长袍全套属性、Zaros背叛剧情故事。15-30分钟即可完成，获得OSRS最稀有幻化套装之一。

The Curse of the Empty Lord is one of OSRS's most atmospheric mini-quests. It doesn't give combat XP or gold — it gives you something far more elusive: the Ghostly Robes, a translucent set of robes that only a small percentage of players ever bother to collect. The quest takes you into the lore of the Zarosian empire, forces you to navigate dangerous Wilderness locations, and rewards you with one of the coolest cosmetic sets in the game. If you're a completionist, a lore enthusiast, or just want robes that make you look like an actual ghost — this guide is for you. For more mini-quest walkthroughs, check our questing beginner guide.

① 📖 Mini-Quest Overview & Requirements（① 迷你任务概览与要求）

The Curse of the Empty Lord is classified as a mini-quest rather than a full quest — it doesn't appear in your Quest Log, and it doesn't award Quest Points. Instead, it's tracked through dialogue with the ghost NPC Viggora, who appears in three different locations depending on which version of the quest you're doing. You only need to complete one version to earn the Ghostly Robes, but completionists can do all three for a deeper understanding of the lore.

1.1 🔑 Requirements（1.1 要求）

Here's what you need before starting:

  • Ghostly Robes (top or bottom) — You must already own at least one piece of Ghostly Robes from another mini-quest in the Ghostly series. The easiest way to get one is by completing the Ghostly Robes mini-quest in Port Phasmatys (requires starting Ghosts Ahoy).
  • Ring of Visibility — Obtained during the Desert Treasure quest. This ring lets you see ghosts that are normally invisible, including Viggora. Without it, you cannot see or interact with Viggora at any location.
  • Access to Wilderness — One of Viggora's locations (Rogues' Castle) is in deep Wilderness (level 54). Bring only items you're willing to lose.
  • No combat requirements — There are no fights in this mini-quest. You just need to find and talk to Viggora.
💡 Getting the Ring of Visibility

If you haven't completed Desert Treasure yet, that's your first prerequisite. It requires 50 Magic, 50 Thieving, 10 Slayer, and several sub-quests. The Ring of Visibility is given to you by the ghost Rasolo near the Burthorpe mine during the quest. After Desert Treasure is complete, you keep the ring permanently — you'll need it for all Ghostly mini-quests. Read our full Desert Treasure quest guide for details.

1.3 ⏱️ Estimated Time（1.3 预计时间）

Once you have the Ring of Visibility and a Ghostly Robes piece, the actual mini-quest takes 15–30 minutes depending on which Viggora location you choose and your teleport setup. The Edgeville Dungeon version is fastest (5 minutes from Edgeville teleport), while Rogues' Castle takes longest due to Wilderness travel.

② 🗡️ The Lore — Zaros, Zamorak & the Betrayal（② 背景故事——Zaros、Zamorak与背叛）

Understanding the lore makes this mini-quest far more rewarding. The Curse of the Empty Lord is deeply connected to the fall of the Zarosian empire — one of the most significant events in RuneScape's history.

2.1 🏛️ The Zarosian Empire（2.1 Zaros帝国）

Zaros, the god known as "The Empty Lord," once ruled a vast empire across the northern lands of Gielinor. His followers — the Zarosians — built cities, fortresses, and temples that still stand as ruins today (like the Digsite dungeon and the Saradominist ruins that overlay them). Zaros was powerful but distant, ruling through his generals rather than direct intervention. His empire was the most advanced civilization of its age, with knowledge of magic, construction, and warfare that far exceeded anything before or since.

2.2 ⚔️ Zamorak's Rebellion（2.2 Zamorak的叛乱）

Zamorak was one of Zaros's most trusted generals — a Mahjarrat warrior who served the Empty Lord loyally for centuries. But Zamorak grew ambitious. Using the Staff of Armadyl (an elder god artifact he stole from another general), Zamorak confronted Zaros in his own fortress and, in a cataclysmic battle, banished Zaros from Gielinor. The god was cast into another dimension, and his empire collapsed overnight.

Zamorak seized power, declared himself a god, and began the Zamorakian era. But the transition was brutal — Zaros's loyal followers were hunted, killed, or cursed. Those who survived the purge became ghosts, wandering the world as translucent spirits, forever bound to the mortal plane by their betrayal and their god's absence.

2.3 🧟 Viggora's Curse（2.3 Viggora的诅咒）

Viggora was a Zarosian warrior who served as a footsoldier in the empire's armies. When Zamorak overthrew Zaros, Viggora was among those cursed — his spirit was bound to the world, unable to pass on to the afterlife. He now exists as a ghost, visible only to those wearing the Ring of Visibility, haunting three locations connected to his past life. Viggora's story is one of regret: he was not a general or a leader, just a common soldier caught in the crossfire of divine ambition. His curse is a reminder that the gods' wars destroy ordinary people too.

📖 The Ghostly Series — All Mini-Quests

The Curse of the Empty Lord is part of a larger series of Ghostly mini-quests that reveal the Zarosian lore through the eyes of cursed spirits. The full series includes:

  • Ghostly Robes (Port Phasmatys) — Speak to the ghost near the Ectofuntus
  • Curse of the Empty Lord — Find Viggora in three locations
  • Ghostly Cooking — Another mini-quest involving the cursed chef
  • Ghostly Fire — Find the ghost of a Zarosian mage

Each mini-quest gives a piece of Ghostly Robes or related lore. Completing all of them gives you the full set. For the complete Zaros lore deep-dive, read our Viggora lore guide.

③ 🧭 Step-by-Step Walkthrough（③ 分步通关攻略）

Now let's walk through the mini-quest step by step. Pick whichever Viggora location is most convenient for you — all three give the same reward.

3.1 📌 Step 1 — Get a Ghostly Robes Piece（3.1 第一步：获取一件幽灵长袍）

Before you can start the Curse of the Empty Lord, you need at least one piece of Ghostly Robes. The easiest method:

  • Use the Ectophial to teleport to Port Phasmatys
  • Walk north from the Ectofuntus to the small graveyard near the church
  • Equip your Ring of Visibility and speak to the ghost standing near the graves
  • The ghost will give you a Ghostly Hood or Ghostly Cloak after a short dialogue about the Zarosian empire

This takes about 2 minutes. Once you have any Ghostly Robes piece in your inventory or equipped, Viggora will recognize you as someone who knows about the curse and speak to you.

3.2 📌 Step 2 — Equip the Ring of Visibility（3.2 第二步：装备可见之戒）

Equip the Ring of Visibility on your ring slot. Without it, Viggora is invisible and you cannot interact with him. The ring has no combat stats — it's purely a quest item that reveals ghosts. Make sure it's equipped before you travel to any Viggora location, or you'll arrive and see nothing.

⚠️ Don't Forget the Ring!

The most common mistake players make is traveling to Viggora's location without the Ring of Visibility equipped. Viggora is completely invisible without it — you'll stand in the exact right spot and see nothing. Always double-check your ring slot before heading out.

3.3 📌 Step 3 — Find and Talk to Viggora（3.3 第三步：找到并与Viggora对话）

Travel to one of Viggora's three locations (see Section ④ for detailed directions). When you arrive with both the Ring of Visibility and a Ghostly Robes piece, Viggora will appear as a translucent ghost. Talk to him and he'll tell you his story — how he served Zaros, how Zamorak betrayed the Empty Lord, and how he was cursed to wander forever.

After the dialogue, Viggora will give you a Ghostly Robes piece (either the top or bottom, depending on which version you complete). This is your reward — there are no further steps needed.

3.4 📌 Step 4 — Collect the Remaining Pieces (Optional)（3.4 第四步：收集剩余部件（可选））

If you want the full Ghostly Robes set, you need to do all the Ghostly mini-quests. The full set consists of 6 pieces:

Each piece is a cosmetic item — they provide no combat bonuses. But together they form one of the most distinctive and rare outfits in OSRS, making you appear as a translucent ghost to other players. For the full training roadmap, see our complete OSRS training guide.

④ 👻 Viggora NPC Locations (All Three)（④ Viggora NPC位置（全部三处））

Viggora appears in three locations across Gielinor, each tied to his past as a Zarosian soldier. You only need to visit one of them to complete the mini-quest, but completionists may want to visit all three for the full lore experience. Each location gives a different Ghostly Robes piece.

4.1 🗡️ Edgeville Dungeon (Fastest Route)（4.1 Edgeville地牢（最快路线））

This is the most accessible Viggora location and the one most players use. Here's how to reach it:

  • Teleport to Edgeville using an Amulet of Glory or the Edgeville lodestone
  • Walk south into the Edgeville Dungeon entrance (the trapdoor just south of the bank)
  • Navigate through the dungeon past the Hill Giants area
  • Viggora stands near the Zamorakian temple area in the southern part of the dungeon, close to the Wilderness entrance
  • Equip your Ring of Visibility and talk to him
💡 Edgeville Dungeon Route Tips

The dungeon has several shortcuts if you have the Agility levels: the pipe shortcut (level 52 Agility) near the Hill Giants lets you bypass a long walk. Without shortcuts, it's about a 2-minute walk from the entrance to Viggora's spot. Bring a brass key to exit through the Varrock side if needed.

4.2 🏚️ Slayer Tower (Morytania Route)（4.2 杀戮塔（Morytania路线））

Viggora's second location is in the Slayer Tower in Morytania — a fitting place, given the tower's dark atmosphere and connection to cursed creatures.

  • Use the Ectophial to teleport to the Ectofuntus, then walk west to Canifis
  • From Canifis, walk north to the Slayer Tower (it's visible on the map — a tall dark tower)
  • Enter the tower and climb to the second floor
  • Viggora stands near the northeastern corner of the second floor, near the Dusty/Mystic area
  • Equip your Ring of Visibility and talk to him

The Slayer Tower route takes about 10 minutes total (including travel). It's slower than Edgeville but avoids the Wilderness entirely. If you're already training Slayer at the tower, just pop upstairs and grab the robes piece while you're there.

🗺️ Slayer Tower Route — Step by Step

1. Ectophial teleport → walk west to Canifis (~2 min)
2. Canifis → walk north to Slayer Tower (~3 min)
3. Enter tower, climb to 2nd floor (~1 min)
4. Find Viggora in NE corner, talk with Ring of Visibility equipped (~1 min dialogue)
Total: ~7 minutes from Ectophial teleport.

4.3 🏰 Rogues' Castle (Wilderness Route — Most Dangerous)（4.3 盗贼城堡（荒野路线——最危险））

The third Viggora location is at Rogues' Castle in the deep Wilderness (level 54). This is the most dangerous route because of PKers, but it gives the same reward and additional lore dialogue.

  • Travel to the Wilderness — the fastest route is the Wilderness Obelisk network (use the level 44 obelisk and run north)
  • Alternatively, walk north from Edgeville through the Wilderness (not recommended — too slow and dangerous)
  • Rogues' Castle is located at the far northwestern corner of the Wilderness, near the Frozen Waste Plateau
  • Viggora stands inside the castle, near the southeastern room on the ground floor
  • Equip Ring of Visibility and talk to him
⚠️ Wilderness Danger — Rogues' Castle

Rogues' Castle is in level 54 Wilderness. You CAN be attacked by other players here. Follow these safety rules:
— Only bring items you can afford to lose (Ring of Visibility + Ghostly Robes piece + cheap teleport)
— Protect Item prayer should be active at all times
— Bring an escape teleport (Amulet of Glory charges work above level 30 Wilderness)
— Check the Wilderness before traveling — if you see a large clan on the world map, pick a different world
— Do NOT bring valuable gear — this is a quick dialogue quest, not a boss fight

⑤ 🧥 Ghostly Robes — Full Set & Stats（⑤ 幽灵长袍——全套与属性）

The Ghostly Robes are the sole reward from the Curse of the Empty Lord (and related Ghostly mini-quests). Let's break down what you get and whether it's worth the effort.

5.1 📊 Full Set Stats（5.1 全套属性）

Each piece of the Ghostly Robes provides minimal combat bonuses — they are primarily a cosmetic set:

PieceAttack BonusDefence BonusOther
Ghostly Hood+0 all+0 all+2 Magic, +0 Prayer
Ghostly Cloak+0 all+2 all+2 Magic, +0 Prayer
Ghostly Top+0 all+0 all+4 Magic
Ghostly Bottom+0 all+0 all+4 Magic
Ghostly Gloves+0 all+0 all+0 Magic
Ghostly Boots+0 all+0 all+0 Magic

The bonuses are essentially negligible — these robes are not meant for combat. They're a collector's item and a cosmetic outfit that makes your character appear semi-transparent, like a ghost floating through Gielinor.

5.2 ✨ The Translucent Effect（5.2 半透明效果）

When you equip the full Ghostly Robes set (or even just a few pieces), your character model becomes semi-transparent. Other players can see you, but you appear faded and ghostly — your outline and details are visible, but the colors are muted and translucent. This effect is purely visual and doesn't change gameplay in any way, but it's one of the most distinctive visual effects in OSRS. Very few players wear the full set, making it an instant conversation starter in any crowded area.

5.3 🏆 Is It Worth Doing?（5.3 值得做吗？）

The Ghostly Robes have no practical use for combat, skilling, or money-making. So why do this mini-quest?

  • Completion value — If you're going for a trimmed Completionist Cape, you need every mini-quest. This one counts.
  • Lore value — Viggora's dialogue reveals crucial Zarosian backstory that connects to Desert Treasure, Temple of Ikov, and the Mahjarrat storyline.
  • Cosmetic rarity — Less than 1% of active OSRS players own the full Ghostly set. It's a badge of effort and dedication.
  • Fun factor — Running around as a literal ghost is just cool. You'll get questions in clan chats and public areas.

If you're purely optimizing for efficiency, skip this mini-quest — it doesn't help you max faster or make more GP. But if you care about completing the game, understanding the lore, or standing out visually, it's worth the 15 minutes it takes. ⑥ 💡 Tips, Troubleshooting & Common Mistakes（⑥ 技巧、故障排除与常见错误）

A few things that trip players up during this mini-quest — and how to avoid them.

6.1 ❌ Common Mistakes（6.1 常见错误）

  • Forgot the Ring of Visibility — The #1 mistake. Viggora is invisible without the ring. Always equip it before traveling.
  • No Ghostly Robes piece — Viggora won't talk to you unless you have a Ghostly Robes piece in your inventory or equipped. Get one from Port Phasmatys first.
  • Wrong dungeon location — In Edgeville Dungeon, Viggora is near the southern Zamorak area, not near the Hill Giants. Don't waste time searching the north end.
  • PKing at Rogues' Castle — If you choose the Wilderness route, bring minimal items. A single Amulet of Glory charge and the Ring of Visibility are all you need. Everything else is optional risk.
  • Desert Treasure not completed — You need the Ring of Visibility from Desert Treasure. No workaround exists. Complete the quest first.

6.2 🔧 Troubleshooting（6.2 故障排除）

  • "I can't see Viggora" — Equip the Ring of Visibility. If you already have it equipped and still can't see him, make sure you're in the correct location (see the maps in Section ④). Try relogging — some ghost NPCs fail to render on first zone load.
  • "Viggora won't talk to me" — You need a Ghostly Robes piece. Go to Port Phasmatys, talk to the ghost near the graveyard, and get a hood or cloak first.
  • "I lost my Ring of Visibility" — You can get a replacement from Rasolo near the Burthorpe mine. He's the same NPC who originally gave you the ring during Desert Treasure. Talk to him again and he'll provide a replacement for free.
  • "I completed the mini-quest but didn't get robes" — Make sure you went through the full dialogue with Viggora. Some players click past the dialogue too quickly and miss the item handover. Talk to him again if needed.

6.3 🎯 Speedrunning Tips（6.3 速通技巧）

If you want to complete this mini-quest as fast as possible:

  • Start from Edgeville with an Amulet of Glory teleport
  • Have Ring of Visibility and Ghostly Hood pre-equipped before you leave the bank
  • Use the pipe shortcut (52 Agility) in Edgeville Dungeon to skip the long walk
  • Talk to Viggora, get the robes piece, teleport out — total time under 3 minutes
  • For the Slayer Tower version, Slayer ring teleport to the tower saves travel time

⑦ ❓ FAQs — Curse of the Empty Lord Mini-Quest（⑦ 常见问题——空之诅咒迷你任务）

Q: Do I need to complete Desert Treasure before this mini-quest?

A: Yes. The Ring of Visibility — which is required to see Viggora — is only available as a reward from Desert Treasure. There is no alternative way to see invisible ghosts. Complete Desert Treasure first, then come back for this mini-quest.

Q: Can I do all three Viggora locations for more rewards?

A: You can visit all three locations, but you'll only receive one Ghostly Robes piece per character. The other locations give additional lore dialogue but not additional items. Visit all three only if you want the full story or are a completionist tracking every dialogue.

Q: Are Ghostly Robes tradeable?

A: No. Ghostly Robes are untradeable. You must complete the mini-quests yourself to obtain them. They cannot be bought on the Grand Exchange or traded between players. If you lose a piece, you can re-obtain it by returning to the relevant ghost NPC and talking to them again.

Q: Do Ghostly Robes have any use beyond cosmetics?

A: Practically, no. The Magic bonuses (+2 to +4 on individual pieces) are so low that any actual Magic armour outperforms them massively. They're a collector's item and cosmetic outfit. The only "functional" use is for trimmed Completionist Cape requirements — you need every mini-quest completed, including this one.

Q: What's the safest Viggora location for low-level players?

A: Edgeville Dungeon — it's in a safe area with no Wilderness risk, accessible via a quick Edgeville teleport, and takes only 5 minutes. The Slayer Tower is also safe but requires Morytania access (Priest in Peril quest). Rogues' Castle should be avoided by low-level players due to Wilderness danger.

Q: Can I do this mini-quest on an Ironman account?

A: Yes. All items needed for this mini-quest (Ring of Visibility from Desert Treasure, Ghostly Robes from Port Phasmatys ghost) are obtained through quest completion and NPC dialogue — no GE purchases required. Ironmen can complete this mini-quest just like any other account type.

Q: How does this mini-quest connect to the Mahjarrat storyline?

A: Viggora's dialogue references Zamorak's betrayal of Zaros — the same event that's central to Desert Treasure, Temple of Ikov, and the Mahjarrat memories mini-quest. Viggora was a footsoldier, not a Mahjarrat, but his curse parallels the fate of the Zarosian Mahjarrat like Azzanadra and Sliske. Completing this mini-quest adds another piece to the Zaros puzzle. For the full Mahjarrat lore breakdown, see our Viggora and Zaros lore guide.

🎯 Final Thoughts

The Curse of the Empty Lord is a short, atmospheric mini-quest that rewards you with one of OSRS's most distinctive cosmetic items. It's not about efficiency — it's about completing the game, understanding the lore, and earning a visual badge that most players never bother to get. With the Ring of Visibility from Desert Treasure and a quick trip to Edgeville Dungeon, you can knock this out in under 10 minutes. Wear the Ghostly Robes with pride — you've earned a piece of Zarosian history.
