🎯 Why Dailies Pay: 47M-129M GP/Month（为什么日常任务回报丰厚：月入4700万-1.29亿）

Most OSRS players approach money making incorrectly. They log in, grind a method for 3 hours, make 500k GP, and then log off feeling like they "earned" their GP. But this approach has a fundamental flaw: it doesn't compound.

Daily and weekly routines are different. They're based on the same principle as compound interest in finance: small, consistent actions that build on each other over time. A herb run that takes 5 minutes and earns 200k GP is infinitely more efficient than grinding Green Dragons for 3 hours — and the herb run gets better as your Farming level increases, while Green Dragons stay the same.

📊 The Math of Daily Routines（日常流程的数学）

Let's say you spend 15 minutes per day on passive income routines. Here's what that looks like over a month:

19.8M to 53.7M GP per month for 24 minutes of play per day. That's 1.24M - 3.36M GP per hour of active play — better than most active bossing methods, with zero risk and almost no effort.

The key insight: daily routines don't compete with active money making — they complement it. Even if you only have 30 minutes to play, spending 15 of those minutes on your daily routine means you're earning GP two ways at once: passive (from the routine) and active (from whatever you do with the remaining time).

Mindset Shift（心态转变）: Treat your dailies like brushing your teeth. Do them every day, at the same time, without thinking about whether you "feel like it." The GP adds up faster than you think, and the habit is more valuable than any single boss kill.

🗺️ Optimizing the Herb Run Route（优化草药跑路线）

The most time-efficient herb run route (for maximum profit per minute) is:

  1. Teleport to Ardougne: Use Ardougne cloak (from Ardougne Diary) or Mythical cape to reach Ardougne Farming patch.
  2. Ardougne patch: Harvest, compost, plant new seed, pay farmer (if desired).
  3. Teleport to Catherby: Use Catherby teleport (POH, Spellbook, or Achievement Diary reward).
  4. Catherby patch: Repeat harvest/plant cycle.
  5. Teleport to Falador: Use Falador teleport.
  6. Falador patch (south): Harvest/plant.
  7. Use Explorer's Ring (4) or teleport: To reach the Trollheim patch (requires My Arms Big Adventure).
  8. Trollheim patch: Harvest/plant. This patch never gets diseased.
  9. Teleport to Morytania: Use Morytania legs (from Morytania Diary) or Ectophial.
  10. Morytania patch (south of Canifis): Harvest/plant.
  11. Port Phasmatys patch (if unlocked): Requires Ghosts Ahoy and Morytania Diary.
  12. Harmony Island patch (if unlocked): Requires Great Brain Robbery.
  13. Kourend patch (Farming Guild): Use Xerus teleport or Arceuus teleport.

With all patches unlocked, a full herb run covers 7-9 patches and takes 8-10 minutes (mostly teleport time). The profit per run ranges from 80k GP (low-level herbs, no protection) to 400k+ GP (high-level herbs with ultracompost and farmer payment).

🛡️ Disease Protection — To Pay or Not to Pay?（疾病防护 — 付费还是不付费？）

  • Always pay for Ranarr, Snapdragon, and Torstol: These seeds are too expensive to risk. The farmer payment (usually 1-3 of a specific herb or item) costs far less than re-buying the seeds.
  • Use Ultracompost always: Ultracompost (made from Supercompost + Volcanic ash) reduces disease chance to nearly zero AND increases average yield by 1-2 herbs. At ≈1k GP per bucket, it pays for itself with one extra herb harvested.
  • The Trollheim patch never diseases: This patch (unlocked after My Arms Big Adventure) is the only completely safe patch. Always plant your most valuable seeds here, even without payment.

🐦 Birdhouse Runs: 120K/run (Mechanics + Profit)（鸟屋跑：12万/次）

Birdhouse runs are a Hunter training method that also generates consistent GP. They were introduced with the Fossil Island update and remain one of the best passive income sources for mid-game accounts.

🔧 How Birdhouse Runs Work（鸟屋跑如何运作）

  1. Craft birdhouses: Use a knife on logs to make a birdhouse frame, then use a clockwork mechanism (made with a clockwork from The Digsite quest) on the frame.
  2. Place birdhouses: Go to Fossil Island, dig 4 spaces, and place a birdhouse in each. The birdhouse will fill with bird nests over 50 minutes.
  3. Collect after 50 minutes: Return to Fossil Island, right-click "empty" on each birdhouse. You'll receive 10-20 bird nests per house (40-80 nests total), containing herbs, seeds, and jewelry.
  4. Bank the loot: Herbs and seeds can be sold on the GE or used for your own Farming/Herblore training.

⚡ Optimizing Birdhouse Runs（优化鸟屋跑）

  • Use a Seed box: The Seed box lets you store 500 of each seed type. This saves bank space.
  • Combine with herb runs: Both have similar grow times (50 minutes for birdhouses, 80-90 for herbs). Do them in the same session to minimize login frequency.
  • Use Fossil Island teleport: After The Dig Site quest, use the Digsite pendant to teleport directly to Fossil Island.
  • Always use Teak or Mahogany: The log cost is negligible compared to the profit difference.

👑 Managing Miscellania: 425K/day (Kingdom Setup)（王国管理：42.5万/天）

Managing Miscellania is a daily passive income activity unlocked after completing The Fremennik Trials quest. You invest up to 75k GP per day and assign workers to collect resources — then sell them on the GE for profit.

🔧 How Kingdom Management Works（王国管理如何运作）

  1. Invest GP: Talk to the Advisor in Miscellania castle and invest up to 75k GP per day.
  2. Assign Workers: You have 15 workers. Assign them to collect specific resources.
  3. Wait 24 hours: Workers collect resources over time. Approval rating must stay above 75% for maximum efficiency.
  4. Collect resources: Return after 24 hours (or up to 7 days later) and collect your loot. Bank it, sell it, or use it for your own skilling.

📅 Weekly Tasks: 1.6M/week (Tears, Circus, Penguins)（每周任务：160万/周）

In addition to daily routines, several weekly activities generate meaningful GP. These take 10-30 minutes per week and should be part of every money-making routine.

💧 Tears of Guthix (Weekly)（古西斯的眼泪 · 每周）

  • Requirements: Tears of Guthix quest completed, 43 Prayer (recommended for protection)
  • Time: 5-10 minutes per week
  • Reward: XP in lowest skill (≈15k-30k XP)
  • GP Value: Indirect — saves training costs. Estimated value: ≈50k-200k GP per week

🎪 The Circus (Weekly)（马戏团 · 每周）

  • Requirements: None (members only)
  • Time: 10-15 minutes per week
  • GP Reward: ≈50k-150k GP per week

🐧 Penguin Hide and Seek (Weekly)（企鹅捉迷藏 · 每周）

  • Requirements: None (members only), Cold War quest started
  • Time: 20-40 minutes per week
  • GP Reward: Redeem points for GP at ≈1,500 GP per point. Better to redeem for XP.

📜 Clue Scroll Stacking (Weekly/Monthly)（线索卷轴堆叠 · 每周/每月）

  • Requirements: Varies by clue tier (combat for hards, 80+ for masters)
  • Time: 30-60 minutes per clue (master)
  • GP Reward: Easy/Medium: 20k-100k GP. Hard: 50k-300k GP. Master: 100k-500k GP + rare chance at 10M-2B GP items.

❓ FAQ + Seasonal Opportunities（常见问题与季节性机会）

❓ Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: Do I really need to do my dailies every single day? What if I miss a day?（真的需要每天做日常吗？错过一天怎么办？）

A: You don't need to be perfect. Missing 1-2 days per week won't ruin your progress. But the compounding effect is real — doing your dailies 7 days a week earns 4x more than doing them once a week. If you can't log in daily, at minimum do your herb runs and birdhouse runs 3-4 times per week. Kingdom can be collected once every 7 days without penalty.

Q: What's the single most valuable daily routine for a new member?（新会员最有价值的单一日常任务是什么？）

A: Herb runs. Even at 32 Farming (Ranarr), your first herb run will earn 80k-150k GP. Do 3 runs per day and you've made 240k-450k GP on day one.

Q: How do I find time for dailies if I only have 1 hour to play per day?（只有1小时玩，怎么安排日常？）

A: Spend the first 15 minutes on dailies, then spend the remaining 45 minutes on active money making. Over a month, those 15-minute daily routines will generate 20M-50M GP.

Q: Are there any "seasonal" money-making opportunities?（有"季节性"赚钱机会吗？）

A: Yes! OSRS has several seasonal events:

  • Halloween Event (October): Candy tokens can be alched. Usually worth 50k-200k GP.
  • Christmas Event (December): Christmas crackers contain 3rd age items worth 10M-2B GP.
  • Easter Event (March/April): Easter eggs can sometimes be sold for GP. Usually modest (50k-100k GP).
  • Double XP Weekends (3-4 per year): Free XP saves massive training costs. Plan Prayer, Herblore, Construction for these.

Q: Should I cancel my daily routine to do League seasons or temporary content?（应该为联赛或限时内容取消日常吗？）

A: League seasons offer unique rewards that can never be obtained again. If a League is active, prioritize it over daily routines — the rewards are time-limited.

Final Thought（最后的话）: The beauty of daily and weekly routines is that they turn OSRS from a "grind" into a habit. You log in, do your routines, and you've made meaningful progress toward your GP goals — even if you only have 15 minutes to play. Consistency beats intensity every time.
