① Why Skilling Beats Monster Looting（为什么技能训练优于杀怪刷钱）

Most new players think "money making = killing monsters." That's wrong. Skilling is better for 5 reasons:

5 Advantages of Skilling（技能训练的5大优势）

💰 Why Skilling = Better Money

  • Passive: You can watch Netflix / do homework while training
  • Predictable: GE prices don't change mid-session (unlike drop RNG)
  • No Combat requirement: Start making GP from day 1
  • AFK: Most skilling methods are 10–30 seconds per action
  • Scales with level: Higher level = exponentially more GP/hour
💡 Realistic Expectations（现实预期）

At level 1–30, expect 20K–80K GP/hour. At level 40–60, expect 100K–350K GP/hour. This is slower than high-level PvM, but requires zero risk and zero gear.

② Mining Money Makers (Level 1–40)（挖矿赚钱 · 1-40级）

Mining is the easiest skill to make GP from level 1. Here's the exact progression:

Level 1–15: Copper / Tin (Skip This)（1-15级：铜/锡 · 跳过）

❌ Not Worth Your Time

Copper and Tin sell for 5–10 GP each. You'll make ~10K GP/hour. Skip these entirely — move straight to Iron Ore at level 15.

Level 15–30: Iron Ore (F2P + Members)（15-30级：铁矿石 · F2P+会员）

⛏️ Iron Ore Method

Location（地点）Requirements（需求）GP/Hour（金币/小时）
South-east Varrock (3 rocks)Mining 15~60K GP
Al Kharid Mine (5 rocks)Mining 15~80K GP
Piscarilius House (Members)Mining 15 + 30% Piscarilius favor~120K GP (faster respawn)

Level 30–40: Coal (Members Only)（30-40级：煤矿 · 仅会员）

Location: Mining Guild (Falador) OR Dwarven Mine (south of Falador). GP/hour: ~150K GP (Coal = ~200 GP each, 400 ores/hour).

Level 40+: Gold Ore (Members Only)（40+级：金矿石 · 仅会员）

🏆 Best Mining Method: Gold Ore (Level 40+)

Location: Arzinian Mine (after Between a Rock quest). GP/hour: ~250K–400K GP (Gold ore = ~500 GP each).

③ Woodcutting Income (Level 1–60)（伐木收入 · 1-60级）

Woodcutting is AFK-friendly and profitable from level 15+. Here's the optimized path:

Level 1–15: Regular Logs (Skip)（1-15级：普通原木 · 跳过）

Regular logs = 2–5 GP each. Skip to Oak trees at level 15.

Level 15–30: Oak Logs（15-30级：橡木）

GP/hour: ~50K–90K GP (Oak logs = ~50 GP each, 1000+ logs/hour).

Level 30–45: Willow Logs（30-45级：柳木）

Location: Draynor Village (10+ willow trees). GP/hour: ~120K–180K GP.

Level 45–60: Maple Logs（45-60级：枫木）

🏆 Best Low-Level Method: Maple Logs (Level 45+)

GP/hour: ~200K–300K GP. Double profit strategy: Cut maples → fletch into maple longbows (u) → sell on GE = 2x profit!

④ Fishing for Profit (Level 1–65)（钓鱼获利 · 1-65级）

Fishing is the most AFK skill in OSRS. You can go 30+ seconds between clicks.

Level 1–20: Shrimp (Skip)（1-20级：虾 · 跳过）

Shrimp = 5–10 GP each. Skip to Fly fishing at level 20.

Level 20–40: Fly Fishing（20-40级：飞钓）

GP/hour: ~60K–100K GP. Pro tip: Cook the fish before selling! Cooked salmon = ~120 GP each (vs raw = ~80 GP).

Level 40–50: Lobsters（40-50级：龙虾）

GP/hour: ~150K–200K GP. AFK time: ~20 seconds per lobster.

Level 50–65: Swordfish / Monkfish（50-65级：剑鱼/僧鱼）

🏆 Best Low-Level Method: Monkfish (Level 62+)

GP/hour: ~250K–400K GP. Quest requirements: Swan Song + The Piscarilius Peril.

⑥ Herblore Unfinished Potions (Level 15–35)（草药未完成药水 · 15-35级）

Herblore is expensive to train, but you can make profit at low levels by making unfinished potions!

How Unfinished Potions Work（未完成药水如何运作）

  1. Buy clean herbs from GE (~10–30% cheaper than grimy herbs)
  2. Buy vials of water from GE (~5 GP each)
  3. Use clean herb on vial of water → make unfinished potion
  4. Sell unfinished potion on GE for 5–15% profit

⑦ Smithing Cannonballs (Level 35)（锻造炮弹 · 35级）

Cannonballs are used by almost every ranged player. Making them is AFK, profitable, and always in demand.

Requirements（需求）

  • Smithing 35 (takes ~2 hours from level 1)
  • ~200K GP startup capital

How to Make Cannonballs（如何制作炮弹）

  1. Buy steel bars from GE (~500 GP each)
  2. Go to Nulodion's Cave (north-west of Edgeville Dungeon)
  3. Use steel bar on furnace → 4 cannonballs per steel bar
  4. Sell cannonballs on GE (~200 GP each, ~60% profit)

⑧ Skilling FAQ — Common Beginner Questions（技能FAQ — 新手常见问题）

Which skilling method is most AFK?（哪种技能最挂机友好？）

Fishing (Lobsters/Monkfish) is the most AFK — ~20–30 seconds per action.

Do I need membership to make GP from skilling?（F2P能用技能赚钱吗？）

No! F2P players can: Mining Iron Ore (level 15+, ~80K GP/hour), Woodcutting Willow Logs (level 30+, ~100K GP/hour).

What's the fastest way to 99 in a moneymaking skill?（最快升到99级的赚钱技能？）

Don't train for 99 unless you have 200M+ GP! Fastest method is usually not profitable. Make profit while training (cannonballs, maple longbows, cooked fish).

Can Ironmen use these methods?（Ironman能用这些方法吗？）

YES! Ironmen must use skilling to get GP (can't buy from GE). Cannonballs are essential for Ironman ranged training.

Why are my skilling profits lower than the guide says?（为什么我的利润比攻略说的低？）

GE prices change daily. Check current price before starting. Higher skill levels = faster collection = more GP/hour.
