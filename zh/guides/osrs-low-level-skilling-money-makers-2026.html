OSRS Low-Level Skilling Money Makers 2026

You don't need high Combat or expensive gear to make GP in OSRS. Skilling is passive, safe, and profitable from level 1. This guide covers every F2P and member skilling method from level 1–60, with exact GP/hour rates and requirements.

① Why Skilling Beats Monster Looting

Most new players think "money making = killing monsters." That's wrong. Skilling is better for 5 reasons:

① 5 Advantages of Skilling

💰 Why Skilling = Better Money

  • Passive: You can watch Netflix / do homework while training
  • Predictable: GE prices don't change mid-session (unlike drop RNG)
  • No Combat requirement: Start making GP from day 1
  • AFK: Most skilling methods are 10–30 seconds per action
  • Scales with level: Higher level = exponentially more GP/hour
💡 Realistic Expectations

At level 1–30, expect 20K–80K GP/hour. At level 40–60, expect 100K–350K GP/hour. This is slower than high-level PvM, but requires zero risk and zero gear.

② Mining Money Makers (Level 1–40)

Mining is the easiest skill to make GP from level 1. Here's the exact progression:

② Level 1–15: Copper / Tin (Skip This)

❌ Not Worth Your Time

Copper and Tin sell for 5–10 GP each. You'll make ~10K GP/hour. Skip these entirely — move straight to Iron Ore at level 15.

③ Level 15–30: Iron Ore (F2P + Members)

⛏️ Iron Ore Method

LocationRequirementsGP/Hour
South-east Varrock (3 rocks)Mining 15~60K GP
Al Kharid Mine (5 rocks)Mining 15~80K GP
Piscarilius House (Members)Mining 15 + 30% Piscarilius favor~120K GP (faster respawn)

Pro tip: Mine iron ore → superheat item (Magic 43) → make steel bars = double profit!

④ Level 30–40: Coal (Members Only)

⛏️ Coal Method

Location: Mining Guild (Falador, 60+ Mining) OR Dwarven Mine (south of Falador).

GP/hour: ~150K GP (Coal = ~200 GP each, 400 ores/hour).

Use: Coal is used for steel/ mithril bars — always in demand on GE.

⑤ Level 40+: Gold Ore (Members Only)

🏆 Best Mining Method: Gold Ore (Level 40+)

Location: Arzinian Mine (after Between a Rock quest) OR Living Rock Caverns (higher level).

GP/hour: ~250K–400K GP (Gold ore = ~500 GP each, 500+ ores/hour with Glorious Pickaxe).

Quest requirement: Between a Rock (unlocks Arzinian Mine — 200+ gold rocks!).

③ Woodcutting Income (Level 1–60)

Woodcutting is AFK-friendly and profitable from level 15+. Here's the optimized path:

⑥ Level 1–15: Regular Logs (Skip)

❌ Not Worth It

Regular logs = 2–5 GP each. You'll make ~5K GP/hour. Skip to Oak trees at level 15.

⑦ Level 15–30: Oak Logs

🪓 Oak Logs Method

LocationGP/HourNotes
Draynor Village (north of bank)~40K GPSlow respawn, but safe
Seers' Village (after Out of the Ashes quest)~80K GPFaster respawn with Seers' Village teleport

GP/hour: ~50K–90K GP (Oak logs = ~50 GP each, 1000+ logs/hour at higher Woodcutting levels).

⑧ Level 30–45: Willow Logs

🪓 Willow Logs Method

Location: Draynor Village (south of bank, 10+ willow trees) OR West of Varrock (close to GE).

GP/hour: ~120K–180K GP (Willow logs = ~80 GP each, 1500+ logs/hour with Dragon hatchet).

Pro tip: Use Draynor Village — it's the most populated world (World 302/305), so trees respawn instantly because other players are chopping too!

⑨ Level 45–60: Maple Logs

🏆 Best Low-Level Method: Maple Logs (Level 45+)

Location: North of Seers' Village (5 maple trees, close to bank) OR Behind the Grand Exchange (2 trees, but very close to GE for instant selling).

GP/hour: ~200K–300K GP (Maple logs = ~50 GP each, BUT maple longbows = ~150 GP each if you fletch them yourself!).

Double profit strategy: Cut maples → fletch into maple longbows (u) → sell on GE = 2x profit!

④ Fishing for Profit (Level 1–65)

Fishing is the most AFK skill in OSRS. You can go 30+ seconds between clicks. Here's the money path:

⑩ Level 1–20: Shrimp (Skip)

❌ Not Worth It

Shrimp = 5–10 GP each. You'll make ~10K GP/hour. Skip to Fly fishing at level 20.

⑪ Level 20–40: Fly fishing (Raw Salmon / Trout)

🐟 Fly Fishing Method

Location: Shilo Village (after Shilo Village quest) OR Barbarian Village (south of Varrock, no quest needed).

GP/hour: ~60K–100K GP (Raw salmon = ~80 GP each, raw trout = ~50 GP each).

Pro tip: Cook the fish before selling! Cooked salmon = ~120 GP each (vs raw = ~80 GP). That's 50% more profit!

⑫ Level 40–50: Lobsters

🐟 Lobster Method

Location: Catherby (after Grand Voyage quest for faster travel) OR Port Khazard (south of Ardougne).

GP/hour: ~150K–200K GP (Raw lobster = ~150 GP each, cooked = ~200 GP each).

AFK time: ~20 seconds per lobster. You can easily watch a YouTube video while fishing!

⑬ Level 50–65: Swordfish / Monkfish

🏆 Best Low-Level Method: Monkfish (Level 62+)

Location: Monk's Restaurant (Piscarilius House, after Swan Song quest + 100% Piscarilius favor).

GP/hour: ~250K–400K GP (Raw monkfish = ~200 GP each, cooked = ~300 GP each).

Quest requirements: Swan Song (unlocks Monkfish) + The Piscarilius Peril (unlocks Piscarilius House). These take ~3 hours to complete, but Monkfish is 2x more profitable than Lobsters!

⑥ Herblore Unfinished Potions (Level 15–35)

Herblore is expensive to train, but you can actually make profit at low levels by making unfinished potions for other players!

⑰ How Unfinished Potions Work

🧪 The Method

  1. Buy clean herbs from GE (they're ~10–30% cheaper than grimy herbs).
  2. Buy vials of water from GE (~5 GP each).
  3. Use clean herb on vial of water → make unfinished potion.
  4. Sell unfinished potion on GE for 5–15% profit.

⑱ Best Unfinished Potions (Level 15–35)

Potions (Herb)Level RequiredProfit per Pot (GE)Notes
Guam Potion (Guam leaf)Herblore 1~50 GPVery high demand (used for Attack potions)
Marrentil Potion (Marrentil)Herblore 5~30 GPLow demand, but cheap to make
Tarromin Potion (Tarromin)Herblore 12~80 GPHigh demand (used for Strength potions)
Harralander Potion (Harralander)Herblore 22~100 GPBest profit per potion at low level

⑲ Strategy: Buy Clean Herbs (Don't Clean Yourself!)

💰 Profit Optimization

Don't waste time cleaning grimy herbs! Buy clean herbs directly from GE — they're cheaper than grimy (because other players don't want to clean them). Then make unfinished potions and sell for profit!

GP/hour: ~50K–150K GP (depending on herb prices, which fluctuate daily).

⑦ Smithing Cannonballs (Level 35) — The Classic Method

Cannonballs are used by almost every ranged player in OSRS. Making them is AFK, profitable, and always in demand.

⑳ Requirements (Simple!)

✅ What You Need

  • Smithing 35 (takes ~2 hours from level 1)
  • ~200K GP startup capital (buy steel bars from GE)
  • Dwarf Multicannon (optional, but you'll want one later anyway!)

⑴ How to Make Cannonballs (Step-by-Step)

🔨 Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Buy steel bars from GE (~500 GP each).
  2. Go to Nulodion's Cave (north-west of Edgeville Dungeon, F2P accessible).
  3. Use steel bar on the furnace → make cannonballs (4 cannonballs per steel bar).
  4. Sell cannonballs on GE (~200 GP each, 4 cannonballs = ~800 GP from 1 steel bar = ~500 GP cost = ~60% profit).

⑧ Skilling FAQ — Common Beginner Questions

Which skilling method is most AFK?

Fishing (Lobsters/Monkfish) is the most AFK — ~20–30 seconds per action. You can easily watch a video or do homework while training. Mining (Iron/Gold) is also AFK (~10–15 seconds per rock).

Do I need membership to make GP from skilling?

No! F2P players can make GP from:
✅ Mining Iron Ore (level 15+, ~80K GP/hour)
✅ Woodcutting Willow Logs (level 30+, ~100K GP/hour)
✅ Fishing Lobsters (level 40+, Members-only, but F2P can fish Shrimp/Trout at low levels for minimal profit).

What's the fastest way to 99 in a moneymaking skill?

Don't train for 99 unless you have 200M+ GP! The fastest method is usually not profitable. Example: Smithing 99 via Steel platebodies costs ~200M GP. Instead, make profit while training (e.g., cannonballs, maple longbows, cooked fish).

Can Ironmen use these methods?

YES! In fact, Ironmen must use skilling to get GP (can't buy from GE). Cannonballs are essential for Ironman ranged training. Herb runs (Stage 1.3) are also critical for Ironmen to get potions.

Why are my skilling profits lower than the guide says?

GE prices change daily. Check the current price before starting any method. Also, higher skill levels = faster collection rates = more GP/hour. At level 50 Woodcutting, you'll cut Willows 2x faster than at level 30!

Congratulations on your first passive GP income! Here's your action plan:

→ Step 3: Use your skilling GP to buy better gear — ranged armor / Dragon weapons!
