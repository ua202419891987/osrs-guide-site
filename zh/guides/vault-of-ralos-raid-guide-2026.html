Raid Overview & Lore（副本概述与背景）

The Vault of Ralos is the first new raid in OSRS since Tombs of Amascut (2022). Set in an ancient underground archive beneath the Southern Islands, this 1-8 player raid tells the story of Ralos, an ancient scholar who sought to preserve all knowledge — but at a terrible cost.

The raid was released in two phases:

  • Phase 1 (Q2 2026): Entry Mode (lower requirements, easier mechanics).
  • Phase 2 (Q3 2026): Normal Mode (higher enrage, better drops).

Raid Lore (Spoiler-Free)

Ralos was a scholar in the Second Age who believed that all knowledge should be preserved. He built a massive underground archive to store every scroll, tome, and artifact. But his obsession corrupted him — he began trapping the souls of adventurers to power his archive. Now, his avatar guards the vault, and only the bravest adventurers can stop him.

💡 Lore Tip: Reading the books and lore scrolls in the raid gives +5% XP bonus for that run. Take a moment to read them!

Raid Mechanics Overview（副本机制总览）

The Vault of Ralos has several unique mechanics that set it apart from other OSRS raids.

Core Mechanics（核心机制）

  1. Knowledge Points (KP): You earn KP by solving puzzles and defeating enemies. KP is used to unlock doors and skip certain mechanics.
  2. Soul Traps: Ralos trapped souls in crystals. Breaking them frees the souls (gives buffs) but alerts enemies.
  3. Archive Guardians: Stone golems that patrol the archive. They have specific weaknesses (melee/ranged/magic).
  4. Ralos's Watchers: Eye-like sentries that detect players. If detected, all enemies in the room become aggressive.
  5. The Collapsing Archive: Throughout the raid, the archive collapses. You must reach safe zones before the room collapses.

Combat Mechanics（战斗机制）

  • Enrage System: Each time you die, the remaining enemies get +5% damage/accuracy (stackable).
  • Prayer Draining: Certain enemies drain prayer rapidly. Bring extra prayer potions!
  • Phase Transitions: Bosses have multiple phases with new mechanics each phase.
  • Shared Damage: Some mechanics deal damage to all players in the room. Spread out!
⚠️ Enrage Warning: If your team dies more than 3 times in a run, the enrage penalty makes the raid extremely difficult. Play safe, don't rush!

Room-by-Room Strategies（逐房间策略）

The Vault of Ralos has 5 main rooms (plus the final boss room). Here's a detailed strategy for each.

Room 1: The Archive Entrance（房间1：档案馆入口）

Mechanic: Navigation puzzle — light the correct braziers in order.

  • Objective: Light 5 braziers in the correct sequence (shown by symbols on the wall).
  • Strategy: Assign 1 player to read the symbols, others to light braziers. If you light the wrong one, all braziers extinguish (restart).
  • Enemies: Archive Spiders (weak to stab attacks).
  • Tip: The sequence is always the same per run. Write it down!

Room 2: Hall of Whispers（房间2：低语大厅）

Mechanic: Stealth — avoid sound-sensitive enemies.

  • Objective: Cross the hall without making sound (running, attacking, or certain prayers make sound).
  • Strategy: Walk (don't run), don't attack, turn off prayers. Use Silence spell (Magic level 55+) to mute footsteps.
  • Enemies: Whispering Shadows (detect sound, insta-kill if they catch you).
  • Tip: If detected, run to the exit! Don't fight — just escape.

Room 3: The Relic Guardians（房间3：圣物守护者）

Mechanic: Defeat 3 mini-bosses simultaneously.

  • Objective: Defeat Guardian of Strength, Guardian of Wisdom, and Guardian of Defence within 30 seconds of each other.
  • Strategy: Split team: 1 player per guardian (if 3 players) or focus fire one at a time (if less than 3).
  • Guardian Weaknesses: Strength (weak to magic), Wisdom (weak to melee), Defence (weak to ranged).
  • Tip: If you kill one too early, the others enrage (+20% damage). Time your kills!
Mini-Boss（小Boss） HP（生命值） Weakness（弱点） Special Attack（特殊攻击）
Guardian of Strength 150 Magic Powerful melee slam (prayer protect from melee)
Guardian of Wisdom 120 Melee (stab) Magic AoE (prayer protect from magic)
Guardian of Defence 200 Ranged Defence buff (+50% defence, dispel with special attack)

Room 4: Ralos's Library（房间4：拉洛斯图书馆）

Mechanic: Memory puzzle — match ancient texts.

  • Objective: Find 5 matching pairs of ancient texts (memory game).
  • Strategy: Each player can flip 2 books at a time. Communicate! "I flipped Book A (symbol: Sun) and Book B (symbol: Moon)."
  • Enemies: Living Books (melee attackers, weak to ranged).
  • Tip: The puzzle is easier with more players. In solo, you'll need to remember all positions.

Room 5: The Collapsing Bridge（房间5：坍塌之桥）

Mechanic: Timed parkour — cross the bridge before it collapses.

  • Objective: Cross a 10-tile bridge while it collapses behind you.
  • Strategy: Use Energy Potion or Stamina Potion to run continuously. Don't stop! If you fall, you take 30 damage and restart.
  • Enemies: None (pure parkour).
  • Tip: Agility level 70+ helps (fewer fails). If you have low Agility, use Agility Potion.
💡 Room 5 Shortcut: If you have 90+ Agility, you can jump the gap (skip 5 tiles). This saves time and reduces collapse risk!

Final Boss: Ralos Avatar Strategy（最终Boss：Ralos Avatar攻略）

The final boss of the Vault of Ralos is the Ralos Avatar, a multi-phase fight with complex mechanics.

Phase 1: The Scholar's Form (HP 1000-700)（P1：学者形态）

  • Attack Style: Magic (艾魔法攻击, 艾魔法攻击).
  • Special Attack: Knowledge Blast — AoE magic attack (prayer protect from magic).
  • Mechanic: Ralos summons Archive Spirits (small adds). Kill them quickly (they heal Ralos).
  • Strategy: Tank with protect from magic, DPS with ranged (Ralos is weak to ranged in this phase).

Phase 2: The Corrupted Form (HP 700-300)（P2：腐蚀形态）

  • Attack Style: Melee + Magic (dual attack).
  • Special Attack: Soul Drain — Drains 50% of current prayer points. Bring extra prayer potions!
  • Mechanic: Ralos gains Enraged buff (+30% damage) if no player is within melee distance. Always have 1 player meleeing!
  • Strategy: Melee tank (protect from melee), others DPS from range. Switch prayers frequently.

Phase 3: The Avatar's True Form (HP 300-0)（P3：真身形态）

  • Attack Style: All styles (melee, ranged, magic).
  • Special Attack: Vault Collapse — AoE that deals 50 damage to all players. Heal immediately!
  • Mechanic: Ralos becomes immune to damage for 10 seconds every 100 HP lost. Attack during vulnerable windows only.
  • Strategy: EVERYONE use Preserve prayer (extends potion effects). Burn all special attacks/ultimates in vulnerable windows.

Which Mode Should You Play?（该选哪种模式？）

  • New to Raids: Start with Entry Mode. Learn mechanics without the pressure of high damage.
  • Mid-Level Account (70-85 stats): Stick to Entry Mode. Normal Mode will be too hard.
  • Endgame Account (85+ stats, BIS gear): Play Normal Mode for better drops and challenge.
  • Ironman: Entry Mode is fine for learning, but Normal Mode has better drops for progression.
💡 Progression Tip: Complete Entry Mode 5-10 times to learn mechanics, then transition to Normal Mode. There's no shame in playing Entry Mode!

Ironman Strategies & Tips（铁人策略与技巧）

Ironmen have unique considerations for the Vault of Ralos. Here are tips for Ironman accounts.

Ironman-Specific Tips（铁人专属技巧）

  1. Prioritize Entry Mode: Lower requirements, easier to get your first few uniques for progression.
  2. Group Up: Find an Ironman clan or group. Raiding solo as Ironman is extremely difficult.
  3. Supply Management: Bring your own supplies (food, potions). Don't rely on teammates to share.
  4. Target Specific Drops: Example: Archivist's Robes are huge for mage Ironmen. Farm until you get them!
  5. Use Loot Share: If playing with non-Ironmen, use loot share (they can't keep your untradeables anyway).

Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them（常见错误与避免方法）

Even experienced raiders make mistakes. Here are the most common ones in the Vault of Ralos.

Mistake 1: Rushing Without Learning Mechanics（错误1：未学机制就 rush）

The Mistake: Jumping into Normal Mode without understanding mechanics.
The Fix: Complete Entry Mode 5-10 times first. Learn each room's mechanic.

Mistake 2: Not Bringing Enough Supplies（错误2：补给不足）

The Mistake: Running out of prayer potions or food mid-raid.
The Fix: Overprepare! Bring 15+ prayer potions and 20+ food for Normal Mode.

Mistake 3: Ignoring the Enrage System（错误3：忽视激怒机制）

The Mistake: Dying multiple times and making the raid impossible.
The Fix: Play safe! If you die, it's okay — but try to minimize deaths.

Mistake 4: Bad Team Composition（错误4：团队配置不当）

The Mistake: All DPS, no tank or support.
The Fix: Have at least 1 tank (high defence, prayer) and communicate roles.

Mistake 5: Not Using Voice Comms（错误5：不使用语音通讯）

The Mistake: Relying on text chat (too slow for raids).
The Fix: Use Discord or in-game voice chat for Normal Mode.

⚠️ Final Warning: The Vault of Ralos is a challenging raid. Don't get discouraged if you don't clear it on your first try. Learn from mistakes and improve!

Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: Can I solo the Vault of Ralos?

A: Yes, Entry Mode is solo-able with 90+ combat stats and BIS gear. Normal Mode is possible solo but extremely difficult (recommended 3-5 players).

Q: What's the best way to find a team?

A: Join the OSRS Raid Discord or find a clan that does raids. You can also use the in-game grouping system (Grouping tab).

Q: How long does a raid run take?

A: Entry Mode: 25-45 minutes (depending on team size and experience). Normal Mode: 35-60 minutes.

Q: Are the drops tradeable?

A: Most drops are tradeable (GP items). Some items (like the pet) are untradeable. Ironmen cannot trade drops.

Q: What's the drop rate of Ralos's Grace (ring)?

A: Approximately 1/200 in Normal Mode, 1/250 in Entry Mode. It's rare — don't expect it quickly!

Q: Can I use a scanner to find the correct brazier sequence in Room 1?

A: No, there's no in-game scanner. You must manually read the symbols and remember the sequence. Write it down!

Q: Is there a hard mode for Vault of Ralos?

A: Not currently, but Jagex may add one in a future update (similar to ToB or CoX). For now, Normal Mode is the hardest difficulty.
