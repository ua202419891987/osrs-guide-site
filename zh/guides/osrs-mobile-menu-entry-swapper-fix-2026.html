📱 OSRS Mobile Menu Entry Swapper (MES) Fix Guide 2026

One of the most-upvoted complaints on OSRS Mobile in July 2026: Menu Entry Swapper (MES) stops working or behaves wrong after an update. This guide explains exactly why it happens and how to fix it on iOS and Android.

📱 Updated: July 2026 — Mobile Fix Guide · 7 min read

⏱️ Quick Summary — 30-Second Read

  • 📌 Symptom: MES options greyed out, swaps not taking effect, or settings reset after an update.
  • 📌 Common causes: Client version mismatch, post-update setting reset, or 32-bit Android being dropped.
  • 📌 Fix: Update the app, clear cache, re-enable MES in Settings → Gameplay, re-pin swaps, log out and back in.
  • 📌 Last resort: Reinstall the app (your account is server-side — totally safe).

1. 📖 What Is Mobile Menu Entry Swapper（移动端菜单项切换是什么）

Menu Entry Swapper (MES) lets you customize right-click / long-press menu options — for example, setting "Pick flax" as the default instead of just "Pick"（让你自定义右键/长按菜单选项）。On mobile, MES is even more valuable than on desktop because tap targets are slower than mouse clicks. When MES breaks, banking, thieving, and bossing all feel clunky.

💡 Where to Find It（在哪里找到它）

Settings → Gameplay → Menu Entry Swapper. On mobile, long-press an entry in the action menu and tap the pin/swap icon.

2. 🔍 Why MES Stops Working After Updates（更新后为何 MES 会失效）

July 2026 reports cluster around a few reproducible causes:

① Client Version Mismatch（客户端版本不匹配）

Your app didn't auto-update, so the server-pushed MES data format no longer matches your local client. Result: swaps silently fail.

② Post-Update Settings Reset（更新后设置重置）

Some updates wipe or partially reset local preferences. Your carefully pinned swaps disappear; the toggle looks off even when it was on.

③ 32-bit Android Dropped（Android 32 位被弃用）

Jagex dropped support for 32-bit Android. Devices stuck on old 32-bit builds may load an outdated client where MES behaves differently — or not at all.

④ Known Mobile Client Bug（已知移动端客户端 Bug）

Updates occasionally introduce mobile-only regressions. If a particular swap fails for everyone, it's likely a Jagex-side bug they will patch.

3. 🛠️ Step-by-Step Fix（逐步修复）

✅ Reliable Fix — Do In Order（可靠修复：按顺序做）

  1. Update the app（更新应用）: Pull the latest from App Store / Google Play. If the store is slow, grab the newest APK from the official OSRS site.
  2. Clear cache（清缓存）: Android → Settings → Apps → OSRS → Storage → Clear cache. iOS → uninstall and reinstall the app.
  3. Re-enable MES（重新开启 MES）: Settings → Gameplay → Menu Entry Swapper → toggle on.
  4. Re-pin your swaps（重新固定切换）: Long-press the menu entry you want, tap the pin icon, confirm.
  5. Log out and back in（退出重登）: Forces the server to push the latest MES data to your client.
⚠️ Still Not Working?（做完还是不行？）

Most likely a Jagex-side client bug. Wait for the next minor mobile build — don't keep toggling on and off, that won't help.

4. 📱 Android 32-bit Note（Android 32 位说明）

If your older Android device can only run 32-bit apps, the OSRS Mobile client may stop receiving updates. Symptoms include MES and other features misbehaving.

  • Check your device architecture (Settings → About → look for "64-bit" support).
  • If you can only run 32-bit, the long-term fix is upgrading to a 64-bit device. The 32-bit client will keep degrading.
  • In the meantime, you can play on the official desktop client or a 64-bit Android emulator.

6. ❓ FAQ — Mobile MES Fix 2026（常见问题）

Will reinstalling the app delete my account?（重装应用会删我账号吗？）

No. Your account lives on Jagex's servers. Reinstalling only clears the local app — log back in and you're good.

After an update, my swaps are gone — did I lose them forever?（更新后我的切换没了，永久丢了吗？）

Swaps are stored locally, so updates can reset them. Just re-pin them following Section 3. They were not lost from your account — only the device.

Is MES a paid feature?（MES 是付费功能吗？）

No, Menu Entry Swapper is free for all players. You don't need membership to use it.

When will Jagex fix the mobile bug?（Jagex 什么时候修移动端 bug？）

Jagex ships regular mobile hotfixes. Check the official news page for the next mobile build's patch notes.
