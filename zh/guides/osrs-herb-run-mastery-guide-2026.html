Herb runs are the single most overlooked money maker in OSRS. In 5 minutes, you can make 150K-300K profit. Do that 5 times a day and you're looking at 750K-1.5M/day for 25 minutes of work — that's 1.8M-3.6M GP/hr of active time. The catch? Most players do herb runs wrong. Wrong herbs, wrong patches, wrong order. This guide fixes all of that. By the end, you'll have a memorized 9-patch route that prints GP while you sleep.

1. Why Herb Runs Are the Best Passive GP in OSRS（为什么药草跑是最佳被动GP）

Here's the reality of OSRS money making:

  • Vorkath: 2-3M/hr but requires full attention, 100+ combat, and Dragon Slayer II
  • Gauntlet: 4-5M/hr but requires Song of the Elves and high skill
  • Herb runs: 1.8M-3.6M/hr of active time, requirements as low as 32 Farming, doable on mobile while watching TV

Herb runs are the ultimate side hustle. You can do them between Slayer tasks, during work breaks, or even on your phone. No bossing skill required. No gear needed. Just teleports, seeds, and 5 minutes.

6. Maximizing Profit — Compost, Secateurs & Diary Perks（收益最大化——堆肥、魔法剪和日记加成）

Compost — Always Use Ultracompost（堆肥——始终使用超堆肥）

Ultracompost reduces disease chance to ~10% and increases minimum yield by 50%. That's ~1 extra herb per patch on average. At 7 patches, that's ~7 extra herbs per run.

Magic Secateurs — Equip, Don't Inventory（魔法剪——装备，不放背包）

The +10% yield bonus only works when equipped. If you forget, you lose ~10% profit on every patch.

FAQ（常见问题）

Q: How often can I do herb runs?（多久可以做一次药草跑？）

Herbs take 80 minutes to grow (4 growth cycles × 20 minutes). You can do a run every 80 minutes. Most players do 3-5 runs per day.

Q: Ranarr vs Snapdragon — which is better?（Ranarr和Snapdragon哪个更好？）

Ranarr is safer. Seeds are cheaper and if a patch dies, you lose less. At 62-73, plant Snapdragon in disease-free patches and Ranarr elsewhere.

Q: Do I need to water herbs?（需要给药草浇水吗？）

No. Ultracompost protects better than watering. Never waste time watering herbs.

Q: What's the fastest way to level Farming?（升级农场最快的方法？）

Tree runs. Fruit tree runs + hardwood trees + calquat. Do tree runs for XP, herb runs for GP.

Final Tips（最后提示）

  • Use the Tool Leprechaun. They note your herbs for free.
  • Bottomless Compost Bucket is worth it. Pays for itself in ~50 runs.
  • Do herb runs on mobile. The route becomes muscle memory after 10 runs.
  • Check OSRS Wiki prices before buying seeds. Profit margins shift.
  • Combine with birdhouse runs for even more GP.

Ready to Start Your First Herb Run?（准备开始第一次药草跑了吗？）

Grab your seeds, ultracompost, and teleports. Follow the 7-patch route above. In 5 minutes, you'll have your first passive profit run done.
