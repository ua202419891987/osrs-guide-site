👻 Viggora OSRS — Complete Lore & Quest Guide 2026

Viggora is one of the most mysterious NPCs in OSRS. Once a powerful general under Zaros, now a spectral figure cursed to wander Gielinor. This guide covers his complete lore, how to complete "Curse of the Empty Lord", where to find him, and Viggora's chainmace stats + price.

Viggora (sometimes misspelled "Vigora") is a fan-favorite character in Old School RuneScape. His story spans the Second Age, the betrayal of Zaros, and a cursed existence that ties into one of the game's most atmospheric mini-quests. Whether you're here for the lore, the quest "Curse of the Empty Lord", or to learn about Viggora's chainmace (one of the most unique weapons in OSRS), this guide has everything you need. Let's dive into the story of the cursed general. 🎮

① 📖 Who Is Viggora? (Complete Lore Breakdown)

Viggora's story is deeply tied to the history of Gielinor. Understanding his lore helps you appreciate why he appears in multiple locations and why he's so hostile to players.

1.1 🏛️ The Second Age — Viggora as Zaros General

During the Second Age, Viggora was one of the most powerful human generals serving Zaros (the Empty Lord). Zaros had conquered much of Gielinor, and Viggora was instrumental in expanding his empire. He was known for his brutality and his signature weapon — the chainmace (a weapon that would later bear his name).

Viggora's power came not just from combat skill, but from his ability to command loyalty. Many of Zaros's most elite forces owed their allegiance to him personally. This made him a threat not just to enemies, but to rival generals within Zaros's court.

1.2 ⚡ The Betrayal — Viggora Switches Sides

The turning point came when Viggora, along with other key figures (including Zamorak, then a mere general), plotted against Zaros. The reasons are debated among scholars:

  • Power hunger: Viggora wanted more authority than Zaros was willing to give
  • Fear: He sensed Zaros's growing instability and wanted to survive the coming fall
  • Manipulation: Some believe Zamorak manipulated Viggora into betraying Zaros

Whatever the reason, Viggora fought alongside Zamorak's forces during the Sentinel Rebellion. Together, they managed to weaken Zaros enough that he was eventually banished (though not killed — Zaros's fate remains one of OSRS's biggest mysteries).

1.3 👻 The Curse — Viggora's Spectral Existence

After Zaros's banishment, something went wrong for Viggora. Despite fighting on the winning side (Zamorak's), Viggora was cursed. The exact nature of the curse varies depending on which in-game source you read, but the result is consistent:

  • Viggora became a spectral entity (a ghostly, invisible figure)
  • He was bound to wander specific locations tied to his past
  • He cannot rest or move on until certain conditions are met (the core of the "Curse of the Empty Lord" quest)
  • He appears as a hostile NPC in multiple locations, attacking players who disturb his wandering
💡 Lore Tip: Why "Empty Lord"?

The "Empty Lord" is Zaros — after his banishment, he was erased from history, and most Gielinor inhabitants forgot he ever existed. Viggora's curse is poetic: he served the Empty Lord in life, and in death he's bound to an empty, wandering existence. The quest "Curse of the Empty Lord" ties these themes together beautifully.

② 🎯 Curse of the Empty Lord — Mini-Quest Walkthrough

"Curse of the Empty Lord" is a mini-quest (sometimes called a "quest-lite" or "subquest") that revolves around Viggora. It's shorter than a full quest but still gives XP and lore rewards.

2.1 📜 Quest Requirements & Starting Location

2.2 🗺️ Step-by-Step Walkthrough

📋 Curse of the Empty Lord — Quick Steps

  1. Find Viggora in Edgeville Dungeon — Enter the dungeon, go to the swamp area (south-west part). Look for a translucent, shimmering NPC.
  2. Speak to him — He'll talk about his curse and ask for help lifting it.
  3. Collect 3 Cursed Artefacts — These are scattered across Gielinor:
    • Artefact 1: In the Edgeville Dungeon swamp (near Viggora's starting location)
    • Artefact 2: In the Slayer Tower (top floor, near the spectral figures)
    • Artefact 3: In Rogues' Castle (Wilderness, level 50+ woods)
  4. Return to Viggora — Bring all 3 artefacts back to him in Edgeville Dungeon.
  5. Defeat Viggora (Boss Fight) — He'll attack you! This is a mini-boss fight with unique mechanics:
    • He uses Magic attacks (protect from Magic recommended)
    • He can turn invisible (use Echo boots or well-lit areas to track him)
    • He summons spectral minions at 50% HP (kill them first)
  6. Complete the quest — After defeating him, he'll be freed from the curse. Reward: 2,500 Prayer XP + access to Viggora's chainmace (drops now available).

⚠️ Wilderness Warning!

Rogues' Castle (where Artefact 3 is located) is in the Wilderness (level 50+). You can be PKed! Bring only items you're willing to lose, or travel with a friend. Consider going during off-peak hours (early morning) when fewer PKers are active.

③ 🗺️ Where to Find Viggora (All Locations)

Viggora appears as a roaming NPC in 3 main locations. You can find him in any of these spots, depending on where he's currently wandering:

3.1 🏚️ Location 1: Edgeville Dungeon (Swamp Area)

This is where the "Curse of the Empty Lord" quest starts. Enter Edgeville Dungeon (the trapdoor in Edgeville), go south-west to the swampy area. Look for a translucent, shimmering figure.

  • Teleport: Use Edgeville teleport (amulet of glory or Edgeville home teleport)
  • Safe for F2P? Yes, Edgeville Dungeon is F2P-accessible (though Viggora himself is members-only content)

3.2 💀 Location 2: Slayer Tower (Top Floor)

Viggora also wanders the Slayer Tower (top floor, near the spectral figures area). This is a members-only area and requires some Slayer progress to access efficiently.

  • Teleport: Slayer Ring (unlocked after 75 Slayer points) or Canifis teleport
  • Combat advisable: 60+ combat recommended (surrounding monsters are aggressive)

3.3 🏰 Location 3: Rogues' Castle (Wilderness)

The most dangerous location — Rogues' Castle in the Wilderness (level 50+). Viggora can appear here as a wandering NPC. This is also where you'll find Artefact 3 during the quest.

  • WARNING: Wilderness PK risk is high here. Bring minimal gear.
  • Teleport: Wilderness Obelisks or Burning Amulet of Glory (dangerous!)

④ ⚔️ Viggora's Chainmace — Stats, Price & Use Cases

Viggora's chainmace is one of the most unique weapons in OSRS. It's a rare drop from Viggora (after completing "Curse of the Empty Lord") and has some interesting properties.

4.2 💰 Current GE Price (June 2026)

Viggora's chainmace typically sells for 6M-8M GP on the Grand Exchange (prices fluctuate based on supply/demand). It's not the most meta weapon, but it's a fun collector's item and useful for undead-heavy Slayer tasks.

4.3 🎯 Best Use Cases

  • Undead Slayer Tasks: Great for Abyssal Demons, Nechryael, and other undead-type Slayer monsters
  • Revenant Hunting: The undead accuracy bonus helps against Revenants (though the Craw's Bow is still better for ranged)
  • Lore/RP Builds: Some players use it for roleplay or "themed" builds
  • Collectors: It's a rare drop and a cool conversation piece
💡 Is Viggora's Chainmace Worth It?

For most players, no — it's outclassed by the Abyssal Whip (faster speed) and Dragon Scimitar (better DPS). But if you love the lore, want a unique weapon, or frequently fight undead monsters, it's a fun pickup. Think of it like the Dragon Claws of chainmaces — cool, but not meta.

⑥ ❓ FAQs — Viggora OSRS

Q: Is "Vigora" the correct spelling?

A: No! The correct spelling is "Viggora" (with a "g" after the "i"). Many players misspell it as "Vigora", but the official in-game spelling is Viggora. This guide uses the correct spelling throughout.

Q: Can F2P players encounter Viggora?

A: No. Viggora is members-only content. However, you can read about his lore on the OSRS Wiki or watch YouTube guides if you're curious.

Q: How hard is the "Curse of the Empty Lord" quest?

A: It's a mini-quest, so it's shorter and easier than full quests. Combat 40+ and 10 lobsters should be enough. The boss fight is the hardest part, but with Protect from Magic, it's very manageable.

Q: Can I get Viggora's chainmace without doing the quest?

A: No. You must complete "Curse of the Empty Lord" first. After that, Viggora becomes a repeatable boss (you can fight him once per day for a chance at the chainmace drop).

Q: Is Viggora's chainmace good for PvM?

A: It's okay for undead monsters (Revenants, Barrows, etc.) due to the +25% accuracy bonus. But for general PvM, the Abyssal Whip or Dragon Scimitar are better choices.

Q: Where is Viggora right now?

A: Viggora roams between 3 locations (Edgeville Dungeon, Slayer Tower, Rogues' Castle). There's no fixed schedule — you just have to check all 3 locations. Some players use the in-game "NPC Follower" plugin to track him.

Q: Can I safespot Viggora?

A: Not really. He can teleport to you if you try to safespot. Your best bet is to fight him fairly with Protect from Magic and good food.

🎯 Final Tips for Viggora Hunters

Viggora is one of OSRS's most atmospheric NPCs. His story ties into the larger Zarosian lore, and completing "Curse of the Empty Lord" gives you a cool weapon plus some great lore rewards. If you're hunting for the chainmace, be patient — it's a 1/128 drop rate, so you might need multiple kills. Bring a friend to make the fights faster, and always watch out for PKers when you're at Rogues' Castle!
