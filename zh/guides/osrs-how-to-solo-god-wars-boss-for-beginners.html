1. God Wars Dungeon — The Basics （God Wars地下城 — 基础知识）

God Wars Dungeon is located north of Trollheim. To enter, you need either 60 Strength or 60 Agility to move the boulder at the entrance, plus a rope on your first visit (the rope stays permanently). Inside, four factions are locked in eternal combat: Bandos, Armadyl, Zamorak, and Saradomin. （God Wars地下城位于Trollheim北部。进入需要60力量或60敏捷推开入口巨石，首次访问还需要一根绳索（绳索永久保留）。地下城内四大阵营永无休止地战斗：Bandos、Armadyl、Zamorak和Saradomin。）

Each boss room requires 40 kill count (KC) of that faction's followers to enter. KC is earned by killing creatures in the main dungeon area. Once inside the boss room, the boss spawns every 90 seconds along with three minions. KC resets when you leave the dungeon, so plan your trips to maximize kills per entry. （每个Boss房间需要对应阵营40击杀数（KC）才能进入。KC通过击杀主区域生物获得。进入Boss房间后，Boss每90秒刷新一次，带三个小怪。离开地下城后KC重置，所以要规划好每次出行最大化击杀数。）

4. Bandos Solo Strategy — Step by Step （Bandos单刷策略 — 逐步教学）

  1. Prayer: Activate Protect from Melee BEFORE entering the room. Graardor hits 30+ through melee and can KO you on entry. （祈祷：进门前先开Protect from Melee。Graardor近战伤害30+，进门瞬间可能秒杀你。）
  2. Positioning: Stand in the north-east corner of the room. This limits minion damage by keeping them in a cluster. （站位：站在房间东北角。让小怪聚在一起，减少受到的伤害。）
  3. Attack Graardor: Use Piety (or Ultimate Strength + Incredible Reflexes) for maximum DPS. Spec with Dragon Claws or DDS at the start. （攻击Graardor：开Piety（或Ultimate Strength + Incredible Reflexes）最大化输出。开局用Dragon Claws或DDS打特殊攻击。）
  4. Minion Priority: After Graardor dies, kill Steelwill (mage minion) FIRST — his magic attacks hit through prayer. Then Strongstack (melee), then Grimspike (ranged). （小怪优先级：Graardor死后，优先杀Steelwill（法师小怪）——他的魔法攻击无视祈祷。然后是Strongstack（近战），最后Grimspike（远程）。）
  5. Heal Between Kills: Use Guthan's spear set or Blood Barrage on the minions to heal between Graardor spawns. This extends your trip significantly. （击杀间隔回血：在Graardor刷新间隙用Guthan's套装或Blood Barrage对小怪回血，大幅延长出行时间。）
  6. Graardor Respawns: He respawns 90 seconds after death. Be ready with Protect from Melee reactivated. （Graardor刷新：死亡后90秒刷新。确保Protect from Melee重新开启。）
🩸 Healing Strategy: （回血策略） Bring a Guthan's warspear, body, chainskirt, and helm in your inventory. After Graardor dies, equip Guthan's set and heal on the minions. Guthan's has a 25% chance to heal you for the damage dealt — you can fully restore HP between kills without using any food. （背包里带Guthan's战矛、盔甲、腿甲和头盔。Graardor死后穿上Guthan's套装打小怪回血。Guthan's有25%几率将伤害转化为治疗——击杀间隔可以完全回满血，不消耗任何食物。）

8. Common Mistakes at GWD （GWD常见错误）

  1. Forgetting protection prayer on entry. Bandos can hit 30+ through melee instantly. If you enter without Protect from Melee, you risk a KO before you can react. （进门忘记开保护祈祷。Bandos近战瞬间30+伤害。没开Protect from Melee进门可能还没反应过来就被秒了。）
  2. Not bringing a teleport out. GWD is deep in the dungeon. Without a teleport tab, you must run through aggressive creatures to escape. Always bring a one-click teleport. （不带传送道具。GWD在地下城深处，没有传送卷轴就只能跑过一堆敌对生物逃出去。一定要带一键传送。）
  3. Killing the wrong minion first. At Bandos, the mage minion (Steelwill) hits through prayer. Killing him first saves more HP than any other single action. （杀错小怪顺序。Bandos的法师小怪（Steelwill）无视祈祷造成伤害。优先杀他能比其他操作省更多血量。）
  4. Standing in melee range of minions. The minions stack up. If all three attack you simultaneously with Graardor, you can get comboed. Position yourself in a corner to limit how many can reach you. （站在小怪近战范围内。小怪会堆叠，如果三个小怪+Graardor同时攻击你，可能被连击秒杀。站在角落限制能打到你的人数。）
  5. Not healing between kills. The 90-second respawn window is your healing opportunity. Don't just stand there — use Guthan's or Blood Barrage to top off. （击杀间隙不回复。90秒的刷新窗口是回血的好时机。别干站着——用Guthan's或Blood Barrage回满血。）
  6. Forgetting KC when world-hopping. If you hop worlds, your KC resets. Only hop inside the boss room if you can survive the boss while waiting for a new world. （换世界忘记KC重置。换世界KC会清零。只在Boss房间内换世界，且要保证能活下来等新世界加载。）

9. Frequently Asked Questions （常见问题）

Q: What stats do I need to solo Bandos? （单刷Bandos需要什么属性？）

A: Minimum recommended stats for solo Bandos: 80+ Attack, 85+ Strength, 80+ Defence, 70+ Prayer (for Piety), and 80+ Hitpoints. You can solo with lower stats but will get fewer kills per trip. A budget setup with Barrows armor and an Abyssal Whip can get 2-3 kills per trip at these stats. （推荐最低属性：80+攻击、85+力量、80+防御、70+祈祷（开Piety）、80+生命值。属性低一些也能打，但每趟击杀数会减少。用Barrows装备+Abyssal Whip的平民配置在这些属性下每趟约2-3杀。）

Q: Which GWD boss should a beginner solo first? （新手应该先单刷哪个GWD Boss？）

A: Bandos (General Graardor) is the best GWD boss for beginners. He uses melee attacks exclusively, which means Protect from Melee negates all of his damage. Bandos gear requirements are lower than Armadyl (you can use Barrows armor), and the fight mechanics are simpler. Start with Bandos, learn the GWD rhythm, then branch out. （Bandos（General Graardor）是最好的入门选择。他只使用近战攻击，开Protect from Melee就能完全免疫伤害。装备要求比Armadyl低很多（可以用Barrows装备），战斗机制也更简单。先打Bandos熟悉GWD节奏，然后向外拓展。）

Q: How do I extend my trips at GWD? （如何延长GWD出行时间？）

A: Three key methods: (1) Bring Guthan's set to heal on minions between boss kills, (2) Use Bones to Peaches tabs to convert minion bones into edible peaches (8 HP each), (3) Bring blood barrage runes in a rune pouch for AoE healing on minion clusters. Combining all three methods can extend trips from 3 kills to 15-20+ kills. （三个关键方法：（1）带Guthan's套装在击杀间隙打小怪回血；（2）用Bones to Peaches卷轴把小怪骨头变桃子（每个8HP）；（3）符文袋里装Blood Barrage符文对小怪群放AOE回血。三种方法结合能把出行从3杀延长到15-20+杀。）

Q: How much profit can I expect from solo GWD? （单刷GWD能赚多少钱？）

A: Solo Bandos averages 800K-1.5M GP per hour with budget gear, and up to 2M/hr with max gear. Solo Armadyl averages 1M-2M GP/hr but requires higher gear investment (15M+ for budget setup). Unique drops (Bandos armor pieces, hilts) are the biggest payouts — a Bandos Chestplate drop (~8M) covers hours of supplies. （单刷Bandos平民装每小时平均80-150万GP，顶配可达200万+/小时。单刷Armadyl每小时平均100-200万GP但需要更高装备投入（平民配置也要1500万+）。稀有掉落（Bandos装备部件、Hilt）是最大的收入——一个Bandos Chestplate（约800万）就够覆盖数小时的补给成本。）

⚔️ The GWD Journey Starts Here （GWD之旅从这里开始）

Soloing God Wars bosses is intimidating at first, but once you get your first Bandos kill, the rhythm clicks. Kill count, boss, minions, heal, repeat. Every trip teaches you something new. Start with Bandos in budget gear, focus on survival over speed, and the GP will flow. （单刷God Wars Boss一开始可能让人畏惧，但当你拿到第一个Bandos击杀后，节奏就自然流畅了。刷KC、打Boss、清小怪、回血、重复。每次出行都让你学到新东西。从平民装Bandos开始，把生存放在速度前面，金币自然会源源不断。）

Your first unique drop at GWD is a memory you'll never forget. Go get it. （在GWD拿到的第一件稀有掉落是你永远不会忘记的回忆。去拿下它吧。）
