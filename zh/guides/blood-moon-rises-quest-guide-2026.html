The Blood Moon Rises is the epic conclusion to the Myreque questline, which began in 2005 with In Search of the Myreque. After years of battling Vyrewatches, uncovering the secrets of the Myreque, and defeating Vanstrom Klause, you'll finally face the ultimate threat: the Blood Moon avatar itself. This guide covers requirements, step-by-step walkthrough, boss fights, puzzle solutions, and rewards for 2026.

1. Quest Overview & Lore（任务概述与背景）

The Myreque have been fighting a secret war against the Vyrewatches and the Vampyres of Morytania. In Sins of the Father, you learned that the Blood Moon — an ancient entity — has been influencing events from the shadows. Now, the Blood Moon is rising, and only you can stop it.

💡 Lore Tip: （剧情提示） Read the quest dialogue carefully! There are many references to previous Myreque quests. If you've forgotten the story, consider replaying the earlier quests first.

3. Step-by-Step Walkthrough（逐步流程）

Step 1: Speak to Veliaf Hurtz（与Veliaf Hurtz对话）

  • Go to the Myreque Hideout in Canifis
  • Speak to Veliaf Hurtz — he'll tell you the Blood Moon is rising
  • Choose: "I'm ready to help" → "Tell me more about the Blood Moon"

Step 2: Investigate the Blood Moon Temple（调查血月神殿）

  • Veliaf gives you the Blood Moon Talisman
  • Travel to the Blood Moon Temple (new area)
  • Use the talisman on the temple altar to enter the Blood Moon Dimension

Step 3: Solve the Blood Moon Puzzle（解血月谜题）

Inside the temple, find 3 Lunar Altars (Sun, Moon, Blood). Solution: Activate Sun first → Moon second → Wait for full moon phase → Activate Blood. Wrong order enrages the avatar (+20% damage).

Step 4: Defeat the Blood Moon Avatar (Phase 1)（击败血月化身—阶段1）

  • Use Blisterwood Weapon for +50% damage
  • Protect from Melee — avatar uses melee in Phase 1
  • At 70% HP, transitions to Phase 2

Step 5: Collapsing Temple (Agility Puzzle)（坍塌神殿—敏捷谜题）

  • Jump gaps, crawl under rocks, run across beams
  • 60 seconds to escape. Fail = 30 damage + restart
  • Use Stamina Potions

Step 6: Final Showdown (Phase 2)（最终决战—阶段2）

  • Regroup with Drezel and Veliaf
  • Avatar uses magic attacks — switch to Protect from Magic
  • Defeat to complete the quest

4. Boss Fight Strategies（Boss战策略）

Phase 1: First Form (HP 1000-700)（第一形态）

  • Attack: Melee only. Prayer: Protect from Melee.
  • Weapon: Blisterwood Weapon (other weapons -50% damage).
  • Special: Blood Drain — drains 20% of prayer points.
  • Enrage at 75% HP: +10% damage boost.

Phase 2: True Form (HP 700-0)（真实形态）

  • Attack: Magic + Melee (switches every 10s).
  • Prayer: Switch between Protect from Magic and Protect from Melee.
  • Special: Blood Moon Eclipse — AoE 30 damage to all. Heal immediately!
  • Enrage at 50%/25%: +15% damage each time.

7. Ironman-Specific Tips（铁人专属建议）

  • Gear: Farm/Barrows/raid for gear — can't buy from GE
  • Supplies: Farm Sharks (76+ Fishing) or Anglerfish (82+). Farm Snape Grass (32+ Farming) for prayer potions
  • Blisterwood Weapon: Must craft yourself (Blisterwood logs + staff from Sins of the Father)
  • Armor alternatives: Dragon Chainbody (DS2), Barrows tank gear, Dragon Platelegs from NPC
💡 Ironman Pro Tip: （铁人专业提示） Farm Sharks (Fishing 76+) or Anglerfish (Fishing 82+, Tempoross) for food. You'll need 20+ food for the boss fight.

8. Common Mistakes（常见错误）

  1. Not bringing Blisterwood Weapon. Regular weapons do -50% damage against the avatar.
  2. Running out of prayer potions. Bring 10+ — the boss drains prayer with Blood Drain.
  3. Failing the Agility puzzle. Train Agility to 50+ before quest. Use Agility Potions.
  4. Not wearing anti-poison. The avatar poisons you. Bring Superantipoison.
  5. Forgetting to upgrade Blisterwood Staff to Sword. After quest, upgrade for 1M GP — faster attacks!

9. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题）

Q: Can I solo the boss fight?（能单挑Boss吗？）

A: Yes, but it's challenging. With 80+ combat stats and good gear, you can solo it. Lower stats? Bring a friend!

Q: How long does the quest take?（任务需要多长时间？）

A: Approximately 2-4 hours depending on your skill and puzzles.

Q: Is Blisterwood Sword better than Staff?（剑比法杖好吗？）

A: Yes! The sword has faster attack speed. Upgrade after the quest.

Q: Can I get the Blood Moon Amulet if I lose it?（丢了能买新的吗？）

A: Yes, buy from Myreque Shop for 500K GP. Don't lose it!
