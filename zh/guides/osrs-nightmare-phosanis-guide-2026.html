噩梦Ashihama——沉睡在Slepe之下的Boss，掉落OSRS中最值钱的独特物品：审判官铠甲和4亿GP的和谐球。涵盖普通组队和单人Phosanis模式

OSRS Nightmare & Phosani's Nightmare Guide 2026 — Curse Flicking & Inquisitor Drops

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 15 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
👹 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

The Nightmare of Ashihama — the Sleeper beneath Slepe — houses some of the most valuable unique drops in OSRS: Inquisitor Armor and the 400M GP Harmonised Orb. This guide covers both the regular team encounter and the solo Phosani's variant, with complete curse flicking mechanics, all 4 phases, full gear progression, and how to maximize your unique drop rate.

🌙 Nightmare — Quick Reference

Location:Slepe Dungeon, MorytaniaTeam Size:1-5 Regular / Solo Phosani's
Best Drop:Harmonised Orb (~400M)Unique Rate (Phosani's):~1/200 (3x Regular)

1. The Curse Mechanic — Master This or Die（诅咒机制——掌握它或死亡）

The Nightmare's signature attack is the curse — a charged attack that bypasses ALL protection prayers. When the Nightmare growls, it's charging a curse with a specific combat style. You must pray the correct style in the exact tick the curse lands. Miss by even one tick = 50-70 damage.

Learning the audio cues: High-pitched growl = Magic curse (Protect from Magic). Low guttural growl = Melee curse (Protect from Melee). Mid-range roar = Ranged curse (Protect from Missiles). Use game audio when learning — this is non-negotiable. After 20-30 KC, you'll start recognizing the visual wind-up animations too. The curse is the #1 killer at Nightmare — master it first, everything else second.

PhaseMechanicsStrategy

2. Sleepwalkers4 Sleepwalkers spawn in corners → crawl toward NightmareKill ALL 4 before they reach her. Each that reaches = massive damage. Blowpipe for farthest, melee for nearest. Split directions with your team.

4. EnrageFaster curses, black flowers on floor, faster attacksDodge flowers (explode for 30+ damage). Curse flicks must be PERFECT. Dump specs at <10%.

3. Phosani's Nightmare — Solo Endgame（Phosani噩梦——单人终局内容）

The solo-only hard mode. You cannot pray against any special attack — curses, flowers, and Sleepwalker damage must all be dodged by movement. Sleepwalkers spawn in EVERY phase, not just Phase 2. Kill time: 8-12 minutes for experienced players, 15-25 for learners. The unique drop rate is ~1/200 vs ~1/400 in regular — triple the chance per kill. Maxed combat + 90+ Prayer strongly recommended.

5. Frequently Asked Questions（常见问题解答）

Q: Regular or Phosani's for loot?

A: Phosani's — if you can handle it. Triple the unique rate. But expect to die repeatedly while learning. 30+ deaths before your first Phosani's KC is normal. Start with 20+ regular Nightmare KC first.

Q: Is Nightmare worth doing without max gear?

A: Yes, but expect slow kills. A budget setup with Bludgeon takes 15-20 minutes per solo. Max gear with Inquisitor's Mace takes 8-12 minutes. The unique table is valuable enough that even slow kills are profitable — the 400M Orb averages out over time.

🌙 The Sleeper's Riches（沉睡者的财富）

The Nightmare is a marathon boss. Kills are long, the unique table is deep, and dry streaks are legendary. But when you see that purple text in the chat box — Harmonised Orb or Inquisitor piece — it's one of the biggest dopamine hits in OSRS. Master the curse flick. Stay patient. The drops will come.
