① Sailing Basics（航海基础）

Sailing is OSRS's 24th skill, released in early 2026. It involves navigating a ship across the seas of Gielinor, discovering islands, trading goods, and engaging in naval combat. The skill has 6 ship tiers: Rowboat (1) → Skiff (20) → Clipper (45) → Brigantine (65) → Frigate (80) → Galleon (95). Each tier unlocks faster travel, better cargo capacity, and access to more dangerous waters.

② Levels 1-20: Coastal Sailing（沿海航行）

Start in Port Sarim. Use the Rowboat to sail between Port Sarim, Rimmington, and Catherby. Complete the tutorial missions for 2K Sailing XP. Your goal at this level: learn the basic controls (raising sails, anchoring, navigating currents). XP rate: 5-10K/hr.

③ Levels 20-45: Island Hopping（岛屿跳跃）

Upgrade to a Skiff at level 20. Visit the small islands south of Karamja — Musa Point, Crandor, and Crabclaw Isle. Complete supply deliveries between these islands for 15-25K XP/hr. Unlock the "Navigation" perk for faster turning speed.

④ Levels 45-70: Trade Routes（贸易路线）

Upgrade to a Clipper. The Clipper opens up the main trade route between Port Khazard, Brimhaven, and Ardougne. Carry goods — silk, spices, and rare woods — between ports for 40-60K XP/hr and 200-500K GP/hr profit. This is the most efficient way to train while making money.

⑤ Levels 90-99: Grand Voyages（大航海）

At level 90+, upgrade to the Galleon — the largest ship in OSRS. Join a crew for Grand Voyages across the Sea of Storms. These 30-45 minute voyages provide 150-200K XP per run with massive loot potential (ancient relics worth 1-10M GP). This is the endgame Sailing content and the best GP method for Sailors.

FAQ

How long does 99 Sailing take?（99航海要多久？）

With optimal methods: ~150-200 hours. From 1-99 using trade routes (best XP+GP balance): ~180 hours, earning 50M+ GP along the way.
