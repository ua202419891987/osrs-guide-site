🐉 Quest Guide — Updated June 2026（任务攻略 · 2026年6月更新）

【龙之杀手2(Dragon Slayer II)是OSRS最重要的任务之一，解锁Vorkath Boss、神话披风(Mythical Cape)和Karuulm地下城等关键内容。本攻略涵盖2026年全部要求、任务流程、Boss战和奖励。以下是完整英文攻略内容 👇】

Dragon Slayer II is one of the most important quests in OSRS, unlocking the Vorkath boss, the Mythical Cape, and access to key content like Karuulm Dungeon. This complete guide covers all requirements, the quest walkthrough, boss fights, and rewards for 2026.

2. Quest Walkthrough（任务流程）

Part 1: Starting the Quest（开始任务）

  1. Speak to Alec Kincade in the Myths' Guild (requires Dragon Slayer I)
  2. Learn about the threat of the dragonkin and Galvek
  3. Investigate the history of Robert the Strong

Part 2: Gathering Information（收集信息）

  1. Travel to various locations following the quest markers
  2. Speak to key NPCs including Bob the Cat
  3. Complete puzzles and agility sections
  4. Obtain the key items needed for the final confrontation

Part 3: The Dragon Key（龙钥匙）

  1. Smith the dragon key using 70 Smithing
  2. Use the key to access the Lithkren Vault
  3. Explore the vault and discover Galvek's plan
  4. Prepare for the final boss fight sequence

Part 4: Final Boss Fights（最终Boss战）

  1. Fight through the dragonkin guards
  2. Battle Vorkath (introductory fight — he's fightable as a boss later)
  3. Face Galvek in the final confrontation

3. Vorkath Boss Fight（Vorkath Boss战）

During the quest, you fight Vorkath as part of the storyline. The quest version is easier than the post-quest boss.

Vorkath Mechanics (Quest Version):（Vorkath机制—任务版）

  • Vorkath uses dragonfire attacks — use an anti-dragon shield or dragonfire shield
  • He fires acid pools on the ground — walk away from them
  • He uses a freezing special attack — use crumble undead on the zombified spawn
  • He occasionally uses a rapid-fire dragonfire barrage — use Protect from Missiles

Post-Quest Vorkath:（任务后Vorkath）

After completing DS2, Vorkath becomes a repeatable boss on Lithkren. He's one of the most profitable bosses in OSRS, with average loot worth 150K–300K per kill.

Post-Quest Vorkath Stats（任务后Vorkath数据）

4. Galvek Final Boss（Galvek最终Boss）

Galvek is the final boss of Dragon Slayer II and the leader of the dragonkin.

Phase 1: Dragon Form（龙形态）

  • Powerful dragonfire — use super antifire + Protect from Magic
  • Move between boats to avoid fire breath sweeping attacks
  • Attack with ranged

Phase 2: Enhanced Attacks（强化攻击）

  • Meteor attacks — run away from shadow markers
  • Stronger dragonfire — eat more aggressively

Phase 3: Final Push（最后冲刺）

  • Berserk phase with faster attacks
  • Use special attacks to burst him down
  • Eat to full HP before each attack

The Galvek fight requires 3–6 minutes with decent gear. Bring 4–6 saradomin brews and super restores, plus super antifire. Ranged is recommended.

6. Dragon Slayer 2 Tips（DS2提示）

  • ✅ Prepare before starting — gather all required items and complete all prerequisites first
  • ✅ Bring super antifire potions — essential for both Vorkath and Galvek fights
  • ✅ Use ranged for Galvek — it's the safest and most effective style
  • ✅ Practice on Vorkath post-quest — he's one of the best bosses in the game
  • ❌ Don't attempt without antifire — dragonfire damage is devastating
  • ❌ Don't stand in acid pools — they deal rapid damage and are easy to avoid
  • ✅ Complete DS2 as soon as you meet requirements — it unlocks too much content to delay
