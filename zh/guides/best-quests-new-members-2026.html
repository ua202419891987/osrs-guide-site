1. The Quick Answer — Top 3 Quests（快速答案 — 3个必做任务）

If you only do three quests, make them these:

The Must-Do Trio（必做三件套）

  1. Waterfall Quest — Instantly jump to 30 Attack & Strength. Zero requirements. 30 minutes.
  2. Lost City — Unlocks Fairy Rings (fast travel across the entire map) and the Dragon Dagger. Low combat.
  3. Recipe for Disaster — Rewards Barrows Gloves, the best-in-slot gloves until endgame. Long but essential.

These three alone give you 100K+ combat XP, global teleport access, and BiS gloves. Everything else on this list is optimization.

2. Why Quest Before You Grind?（为什么先做任务再练级？）

New members often ask: "Shouldn't I train my skills first, then do quests?" The answer is no — quests first, always. Here's why:

Quests Give Free XP（任务送免费经验）

Early quests hand out 200K+ total XP with zero training time. Waterfall Quest alone saves 5+ hours of melee grinding.

Unlock Fast Travel（解锁快速旅行）

Fairy Rings, Spirit Trees, and shortcuts make every future activity faster. Questing now saves hundreds of hours later.

Best-in-Slot Gear（最佳装备）

Barrows Gloves, Dragon Scimitar, and Climbing Boots are quest-locked BiS items. You literally cannot buy them.

Unlock Content（解锁内容）

Many money-makers, bosses, and training methods are behind quest walls. Quests are the key to the rest of the game.

3. Your 2-Week Quest Roadmap（你的两周任务路线图）

Here's the optimal order. Each block is designed to build on the last, unlocking requirements for the next wave:

Phase 1 — Day 1-3 (The Foundation)（第一阶段 · 第1-3天 · 打基础）

  1. Waterfall Quest — 30 Attack & Strength
  2. Fight Arena — 12K Attack XP
  3. Witch's House — 6K Hitpoints
  4. Death Plateau — Climbing Boots
  5. Tree Gnome Village — Spirit Tree access

Result: ~50 Attack/Strength, Climbing Boots, Spirit Tree network. You're no longer a fresh spawn.

Phase 2 — Day 4-7 (The Unlock Phase)（第二阶段 · 第4-7天 · 解锁阶段）

  1. The Grand Tree — Spirit Tree + Glider access
  2. Priest in Peril — Morytania unlock
  3. Lost City — Fairy Rings + Dragon Dagger
  4. Tree Gnome Stronghold quests — Agility course + Giant's Foundry prep

Result: Global travel network, Morytania access, powerful weapons. Ready for mid-game content.

Phase 3 — Week 2 (The Power Spikes)（第三阶段 · 第2周 · 实力爆发）

  1. Monkey Madness I — Dragon Scimitar unlock
  2. Recipe for Disaster — Barrows Gloves
  3. Desert Treasure — Ancient Magicks (optional but powerful)

Result: BiS gloves, best training weapon, high-level spellbook. You are now a mid-game account.

4. Waterfall Quest — Free 30 Attack & Strength（瀑布任务 — 免费30攻击力量）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

13,750 Attack XP + 13,750 Strength XP. Takes a fresh account from level 1 to level 30 in both skills instantly.

Requirements（要求）

None. Zero. You can do this the moment you log into a member world for the first time.

Time（耗时）

20-30 minutes following a guide. No combat required.

Why It's #1（为什么排第一）

This is the single biggest power spike for any new account. Skipping it is like refusing a free level boost.

Pro Tip（专业提示）

Bring food and stamina potions. The quest involves running through aggressive monsters (level 40+). With 1 HP and no food, you'll die repeatedly. Bring 5-10 Salmon or Trout.

5. Tree Gnome Village & The Grand Tree（树精村庄与巨树）

What They Unlock（解锁内容）

Tree Gnome Village: Spirit Tree network (free teleport to 5+ locations). The Grand Tree: Gnome Glider network (fast travel to Karamja, Al Kharid, etc.).

Requirements（要求）

None. Both are low-level quests with no skill requirements.

Time（耗时）

30-45 minutes each. Can be done back-to-back.

Why They Matter（为什么重要）

Together with Fairy Rings (Lost City), these form the fast travel backbone of your entire OSRS career. Slayer, farming, clue scrolls — everything is faster with these networks.

Pro Tip（专业提示）

After The Grand Tree, use the Gnome Glider to travel to Karamja for free. It's the fastest way to reach the Brimhaven Agility Arena and Tai Bwo Wannai.

6. Fight Arena — 12K Attack XP（竞技场 — 1.2万攻击经验）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

12,175 Attack XP (enough for ~15 levels at low levels). Also unlocks the Fight Arena area.

Requirements（要求）

None. Combat recommended: level 20+ to defeat the boss easily.

Time（耗时）

15-20 minutes.

Combat Strategy（战斗策略）

Bring food and safespot the final boss (General Khazard) behind the throne. Or just melee him with food — he's not that hard.

7. Witch's House — 6K Hitpoints XP（女巫之屋 — 6千生命值经验）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

6,325 Hitpoints XP. More importantly, it teaches new players the door safespot mechanic used in many future quests and boss fights.

Requirements（要求）

None. Can be done at level 3 combat if you safespot correctly.

Time（耗时）

15 minutes.

Key Mechanic（关键机制）

The witch's experiment has multiple forms. You can safespot every form by stepping through the door and attacking as it walks through. This is a core OSRS skill.

8. Death Plateau — Climbing Boots（死亡高原 — 攀岩靴）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

Climbing Boots — +2 Strength bonus, best-in-slot for early/mid-game melee training. Costs only 12 GP from Tenzing after the quest.

Requirements（要求）

None. No combat. Just some running around Burthorpe.

Time（耗时）

10-15 minutes.

Why It's Essential（为什么必不可少）

Climbing Boots are the best melee boots you'll wear for a very long time (until Dragon Boots at 60 Slayer or Primordial Boots at endgame). And they're 12 GP.

9. Priest in Peril — Morytania Access（危难神父 — 莫里塔尼亚通行）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

Full access to Morytania: Canifis, Slayer Tower, Barrows, Mort'ton, and the Temple Trekking minigame.

Requirements（要求）

None. Low combat (level 20+ recommended for the temple guardian).

Time（耗时）

20-30 minutes.

Why It Matters（为什么重要）

Morytania is home to some of the best mid-game content: Barrows (2M+ GP/hr), Slayer Tower (great tasks), and the Ectofuntus (best Prayer XP in the game). You need this quest for virtually everything in that region.

10. Lost City — Fairy Rings & Dragon Dagger（失落之城 — 仙环与龙匕首）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

Fairy Ring network (fast travel to 40+ locations across the map) and the Dragon Dagger (powerful special attack weapon).

Requirements（要求）

31 Crafting, 36 Woodcutting. Must defeat a level 101 Tree Spirit (safespottable).

Time（耗时）

30-40 minutes.

Why It's Top Tier（为什么是顶级）

Fairy Rings are arguably the most important travel method in the entire game. With them, a 10-minute walk becomes a 5-second teleport. The Dragon Dagger is also a fantastic budget weapon for mid-level PvM and PvP.

Pro Tip（专业提示）

For full Fairy Ring access (not just partial), complete Fairy Tale Part II after Lost City. You don't need the full quest — just start it to unlock all rings without the wielding requirements.

11. Monkey Madness I — Dragon Scimitar（猴之疯狂I — 龙弯刀）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

Dragon Scimitar — the best 60 Attack training weapon in the game. Also unlocks Ape Atoll (best Agility course until 80+).

Requirements（要求）

The Grand Tree, Tree Gnome Village. 43 Prayer strongly recommended. Must defeat the Jungle Demon (level 195).

Time（耗时）

1.5-2 hours.

Jungle Demon Strategy（丛林恶魔攻略）

Use Protect from Magic, stand under the gnome soldiers so they tank for you, and attack with your best weapon. Bring plenty of food and a Ring of Recoil. Alternatively, safespot him from the gate.

Pro Tip（专业提示）

After the quest, buy the Dragon Scimitar for 100K GP from Daga on Ape Atoll. It outperforms every other 60 Attack weapon for training until you unlock the Ghrazi Rapier or Blade of Saeldor.

12. Recipe for Disaster — Barrows Gloves（灾难配方 — 巴罗手套）

What It Unlocks（解锁内容）

Barrows Gloves — best-in-slot gloves for all combat styles until endgame (Tormented Bracelet, Ferocious Gloves). Also gives 10 sub-quests worth of XP and unlocks the Culinaromancer's Chest.

Requirements（要求）

Cooking 70, Magic 59, Agility 48, many quest completions. This is a long-term goal, not a day-1 quest.

Time（耗时）

5-10 hours total across all sub-quests and the final fight.

Why It's The Ultimate Goal（为什么是终极目标）

Every serious OSRS player gets Barrows Gloves. They're the single most important gear upgrade for mid-game accounts and are required for many high-level PvM activities.

13. Other High-Value Early Quests（其他高价值早期任务）

After the core 9, here are more quests worth doing in your first month:

Desert Treasure（沙漠宝藏）

Unlocks Ancient Magicks spellbook (Ice Barrage, Blood Barrage). Required for many PvM activities. 4 boss fights at ~level 100 combat. Long but very rewarding.

Plague City（瘟疫之城）

Unlocks the Ardougne Teleport and starts the Elf quest series. Also unlocks the Ardougne Sewers for training.

Animal Magnetism（动物磁性）

Unlocks the Ava's Accumulator — retrieves 72% of ammo used in Ranged combat. Essential for any ranged training.

Elemental Workshop I & II（元素工坊I&II）

Unlocks elemental and mind shields (defense against specific attack types) and some Crafting XP.

Ghost Ahoy（幽灵启航）

Free Ectophial teleport (unlimited, instant teleport to the Ectofuntus in Morytania). Great for Prayer training and Slayer.

Fremennik Trials（弗雷姆尼克试炼）

Unlocks the Fremennik helmet (budget helmet), Yt-HurKot access, and starts the Lunar Diplomacy questline.

14. Quest Prep Checklist（任务准备清单）

Before you start questing, stock up on these items. Having them ready will save you dozens of bank trips:

Essential Items（必备物品）

  • Teleport methods: Varrock/Lumbridge/Falador teleports (runes or jewelry), Amulet of Glory (4), Ring of Dueling
  • Food: 50+ lobsters or swordfish. Quests don't need expensive food — quantity over quality.
  • Stamina potions: 10+ for quests with lots of running (Monkey Madness, RFD)
  • Basic gear: Full rune, magic shortbow, or your best available setup
  • GP: 200K+ for quest purchases, boat rides, and unexpected costs

Recommended Stats Before Starting（建议起始属性）

  • 43 Prayer — Protect prayers make combat quests dramatically easier
  • 25 Magic — For basic teleports and utility spells
  • 40 Combat — Makes most early quest bosses trivial
  • 31 Crafting + 36 Woodcutting — Required for Lost City

If you're below these, do Waterfall, Fight Arena, and Witch's House first — the free XP will get you close.

15. FAQ（常见问题）

What order should I do quests in?（按什么顺序做任务？）

Follow the 2-week roadmap above: Waterfall -> Fight Arena -> Witch's House -> Death Plateau -> Tree Gnome quests -> Priest in Peril -> Lost City -> Monkey Madness -> Recipe for Disaster. This order maximizes reward stacking and minimizes backtracking.

Should I do all quests or just the important ones?（所有任务都做还是只做重要的？）

Eventually, do all of them. Many quests have XP rewards and unlock content you'll need later. But prioritize the 10 on this list first — they give the biggest immediate impact. Come back to the rest when you need specific unlocks.

Can Ironmen follow this same roadmap?（铁人可以用同样的路线图吗？）

Yes! In fact, questing is even more important for Ironmen because you can't buy gear — you must unlock it through quests. The roadmap is identical, though Ironmen may need to gather supplies manually instead of buying them.

What combat level do I need for Monkey Madness?（猴之疯狂需要多少战斗等级？）

Theoretically level 40+ with Protect from Magic. Realistically, 50+ combat with 43 Prayer makes it comfortable. The Jungle Demon is level 195 but can be safespotted and tanked by NPCs.

How do I get Barrows Gloves fast?（怎么快速获得巴罗手套？）

Recipe for Disaster requires many pre-quests and skill levels. Use the Quest Cape Roadmap to plan an efficient route. Most players get Barrows Gloves around 2-4 weeks after buying membership.

Do quests give Quest Points? What are they for?（任务给任务点数吗？有什么用？）

Yes — most quests award 1-3 Quest Points. You need 175 Quest Points for the Quest Cape, 32 for Dragon Slayer 2, and various amounts for other unlocks. They also unlock the Tears of Guthix minigame (free weekly XP).
