① What Is ToA?（什么是Tombs of Amascut？）

Tombs of Amascut (ToA) is the newest OSRS raid, released in 2024 and continuously updated. It is considered the most beginner-friendly raid because of its Invocation system — you can adjust the difficulty from "Entry Mode" (easy) to "Expert Mode" (400+ invocation). ToA requires 3 players (or solo), and each player chooses a different "path" through the tomb. The raid takes 25-40 minutes for a normal run. Entry Mode requires minimal gear (under 50M total).

② Invocation System（难度调整系统）

Invocation is unique to ToA. Before starting, the party leader selects a set of "invocations" that increase difficulty and loot quality. Entry Mode (0 invocations): Very easy, minimal loot, good for learning. Normal Mode (50-150 invocations): Standard difficulty with decent loot. Expert Mode (400+ invocations): Maximum difficulty, best loot (purple chance). Recommended beginner invocations: "Softcore Run" (+0), "Walk for it" (+5), "On a Diet" (+10), "Lively Feel" (+10). Avoid: "Insanity" (+50, very hard for beginners).

③ Entry Level Gear（入门装备推荐）

ToA requires all three combat styles. Melee (for Ba-Ba and P2 Wardens): Abyssal Whip or Rapier, Bandos or Fighter Torso + Obsidian legs. Ranged (for Zebak and P1/P3 Wardens): Toxic Blowpipe + Rune Crossbow with Ruby/Diamond bolts. Magic (for Kephri and Crocodile): Trident of the Swamp, Occult Necklace, Tormented Bracelet. Budget total: ~50M. Recommended upgrades: Dragon Hunter Crossbow, Bow of Faerdhinen (for Zebak), Sang Staff (for magic).

④ Four Paths Overview（四路线Boss机制）

Path 1 — Kephri (Scarab): Use Magic. Kill adds quickly, avoid the shockwave attack. Path 2 — Zebak (Crocodile): Use Ranged. Pray Ranged, dodge the poison wave, and attack his jugular when exposed. Path 3 — Ba-Ba (Baboon): Use Melee. Kill the Baboon thralls to reduce Ba-Ba's damage. Avoid the boulder roll attack. Path 4 — Akkha (Statues): Use all styles. Shadow phase — stay in the light. Butterfly phase — avoid orbs. Memory phase — follow the correct path.

⑤ Loot & Rewards（掉落与奖励）

ToA drops the most valuable items in OSRS. Purple drops (unique items): Tumeken's Shadow (900M), Masori Armor (800M set), Lightbearer (30M), Elidinis' Ward (80M), Osmumten's Fang (50M). Expected GP per raid at Normal (150 invocations): ~500K-1M. At Expert (400+): ~2-5M. The purple chance at 150 invocations is approximately 1/30, with the chance increasing with invocation level.

FAQ

What's the minimum combat level for ToA?（最低多少级能打？）

80+ Combat with 75+ Ranged and 75+ Magic. 70+ Prayer recommended for Rigour/Augury.

Is it better to learn ToA solo or in a team?（单刷还是组队学？）

Start with a team of 3 experienced players. Each player covers one path, making it easier to learn. Solo is harder but more profitable once mastered.
