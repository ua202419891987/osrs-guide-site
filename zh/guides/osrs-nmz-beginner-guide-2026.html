① What Is NMZ & Why Everyone Loves It（什么是NMZ以及为什么人人都爱它）

Nightmare Zone (NMZ) is a minigame where you fight quest bosses in a dream-like arena. It's 100% AFK — you can look away for 15-20 minutes and still gain XP!

① 5 Reasons NMZ Is Perfect for Beginners（NMZ适合新手的5个原因）

😴 Why AFK Training Matters（为什么挂机训练很重要）

  • 100% AFK: Set up + walk away for 15-20 min（设置好走开15-20分钟）
  • Zero food cost（零食物成本）: Special NMZ mechanics prevent death
  • Fast XP rates（快速经验率）: 30K-60K XP/hour (Melee) at low levels
  • Points for imbus（积分用于附魔）: Earn points to imbue rings/amuletts
  • No gear needed（无需装备）: Budget setup (<100K GP) works fine
💡 NMZ = Best Low-Level Combat XP（NMZ=最佳低级战斗经验）

Before NMZ, the only way to train Combat was manually clicking monsters for hours. NMZ lets you gain XP while making tea, doing homework, or watching Netflix. This is why every OSRS player loves it!

③ How to Enter NMZ (Step-by-Step)（如何进入NMZ——逐步教程）

Step 1: Teleport to NMZ（第一步：传送到NMZ）

Use Minigame Teleport → select "Nightmare Zone". You'll appear at the NMZ lobby (northwest of Yanille).

Step 2: Talk to Dominic Onion（第二步：与Dominic Onion对话）

Right-click Dominic Onion → "Start Quest". Select "Customizable Rumble" (recommended for beginners).

Step 3: Select Your Bosses（第三步：选择你的Boss）

Check 5-7 quest bosses from the list. Click "Start" — you're now in the arena!

③ Starting Cost & Budget Setup (<100K GP)（启动成本和预算配置——低于10万GP）

You don't need expensive gear for NMZ. Here's the cheapest working setup:

④ Budget Melee Setup (Recommended)（预算近战配置——推荐）

🗡️ <100K GP Setup（低于10万GP配置）

  • Weapon: Dragon Scimitar (~50K GP) or Rune Scimitar (~5K)
  • Shield: Dragon Defender (~500K GP) OR Rune Kiteshield (~10K)
  • Armor: Full Rune (~100K GP total)
  • Prayer: 43+ (Protect from Melee) — MUST HAVE
  • Inventory: 2-3 Prayer potions (4-dose) + rest empty for points

⑤ Budget Ranged Setup (Alternative)（预算远程配置——替代方案）

🏹 Ranged NMZ Setup（远程NMZ配置）

  • Weapon: Magic Shortbow (~5K GP) + Rune arrows (~50 GP/arrow)
  • Armor: Green Dragonhide set (~80K GP)
  • Prayer: 40+ (Protect from Missiles)
  • Notes: Slower XP than melee, but safer for low Combat

⑥ Why You Need Prayer 43+（为什么需要43+祈祷）

💡 NMZ Works Because of Protect Prayers（NMZ因保护祈祷而有效）

NMZ bosses can't hit you if you have the correct Protect prayer ON. This means you never eat food — just sip Prayer potions every 5-10 minutes. This is the key to AFKing!

④ How NMZ Mechanics Work（NMZ机制如何运作）

Understanding NMZ mechanics helps you stay alive longer and earn more points.

⑦ HP Mechanic — You Can't Die (Normally)（HP机制——通常不会死）

How HP Works in NMZ（NMZ中的HP如何运作）

Your Hitpoints (HP) are "locked" at 1 HP when you run out of food. You can't die unless you manually logout or run out of Prayer with bosses swarming you.

Practical effect（实际效果）: You can AFK until your Prayer runs out (~15-20 min), then drink a Prayer potion and continue.

⑧ Absorption Points (The Secret to Long AFK)（吸收点数——长挂机的秘诀）

What Are Absorption Points?（什么是吸收点数？）

Absorption points are a special NMZ mechanic. When active, they absorb damage before your HP. One Absorption potion = ~900 absorption points = ~15 minutes of AFK time.

Strategy（策略）: Overload + Absorption = AFK for 20+ minutes without touching keyboard!

⑨ Overload Potions (Unlock at 75 QP)（超载药水——75任务点解锁）

🧪 Overload = massive Boost（超载=巨大提升）

  • What it does（效果）: Boosts ALL Combat stats (Attack/Strength/Defense/Ranged/Magic) by 5 + 15% of level
  • Duration（持续时间）: 5 minutes per dose
  • Cost（成本）: 700 points per 4-dose Overload
  • How to unlock（如何解锁）: Complete 75 Quest Points (do more quests!)

💡 Pick High-HP Bosses（选择高血量Boss）

Bosses with more HP take longer to kill = more time for XP. Avoid "one-shot" bosses (like Elvarg) — they die too fast and you waste time re-entering.

⑥ AFK Session Management（挂机会话管理）

Here's how to maximize AFK time and minimize clicking:

⑫ The Perfect AFK Setup (20-Minute Cycle)（完美挂机配置——20分钟周期）

Step 1: Enter Arena with Supplies（第一步：带补给进入竞技场）

Bring: 2 Overload (4-dose) + 3 Absorption (4-dose) + 1 Prayer potion (4-dose). This lasts ~20 minutes.

Step 2: Drink Overload + Absorption（第二步：喝超载+吸收药水）

Right-click Absorption potion → "Drink". Your absorption points shoot up to ~900. Do the same for Overload.

Step 3: Walk Away for 15-20 Minutes（第三步：走开15-20分钟）

Your character auto-retaliates and kills bosses. You don't need to click anything. Just check back every 15-20 min to re-supply.

⑬ Pro Tips for Longer AFK（更长挂机的专业技巧）

😴 How to AFK Even Longer（如何挂机更久）

  • Bring more Absorption: 5-6 doses = 30+ min AFK
  • Use Rapid Heal: (unlocks at 85 QP) — heals your HP automatically
  • Lower your HP: Let your HP drop to 1 — you take less damage (weird NMZ mechanic!)

⑦ NMZ Imbue Rewards — Best Items to Buy（NMZ附魔奖励——最佳购买物品）

NMZ gives points per boss killed. Spend them in the Rewards Shop (right-click Domonik Onion → "Rewards").

⑭ Top 5 Imbue Rewards (Priority Order)（前5名附魔奖励——优先顺序）

🏆 Must-Buy Rewards（必买奖励）

  1. Black Mask Imbue (500K points): +15% Melee DPS on Slayer tasks — HUGE boost
  2. Slayer Helmet Imbue (1M points): Same as Black Mask but with defense + style bonuses
  3. Ring of Wealking Imbue (500K points): +10% Magic accuracy — great for Barrage training
  4. Necklace of Anguish Imbue (1M points): +10% Ranged accuracy — end-game ranged training
  5. Occult Necklace Imbue (1M points): +10% Magic damage — best Magic training imbue

⑮ How to Farm Points Fast（如何快速刷取积分）

Method: Customizable Rumble + 7 Bosses（方法：自定义混战+7个Boss）

Pick 7 hard bosses (high HP). Kill them all. Each boss = 10-20 points. A 20-minute session = 200-400 points.

Time to 500K points (Black Mask): ~50-80 hours of AFKing (spread over weeks).

⑧ NMZ FAQ — Common Beginner Questions（NMZ常见问题——新手常见问题）

Can I use NMZ at Combat 40?（我能在战斗等级40时使用NMZ吗？）

Yes!（可以！） With 43 Prayer + Protect prayers, you can AFK NMZ at Combat 40. XP rates will be slow (~20K XP/hour), but it's still 100% AFK. Upgrade your weapon as you level.

Do I need Overload potions to AFK?（挂机需要超载药水吗？）

No!（不需要！） Overloads make it faster (boosted stats = faster kills), but you can AFK with just Absorption + Prayer potions. Overloads just make sessions more efficient.

Why do my absorption points keep running out?（为什么我的吸收点数一直在减少？）

You need to manually drink Absorption potions every ~15 minutes. They don't auto-refresh. Set a timer on your phone = problem solved!

Can Ironmen use NMZ?（铁人模式能用NMZ吗？）

YES!（可以！） NMZ is even MORE important for Ironmen because you can't buy Imbued rings/necklaces from GE. You MUST grind NMZ points to get them. Prioritize NMZ even higher as an Ironman!

What's the fastest way to get 500K points for Black Mask?（最快获得50万积分买黑面具的方法？）

Grind 2-3 hours/day（每天刷2-3小时） of NMZ. That's ~5-10 days of casual play. Don't rush it — treat NMZ as your "background XP while doing other things" activity.
