1. The Golden Rule of OSRS Gear Upgrades（OSRS装备升级黄金法则）

Before we list anything, burn this into your brain:

THE GOLDEN RULE（黄金法则）

Weapon > Necklace > Body/Legs > Ring > Boots > Cape > Shield > Gloves > Helm

This is the priority order for all three combat styles. Weapons give the biggest DPS jump. Capes and gloves are marginal. Never buy a Bandos Chestplate before a Fang.

Why? OSRS combat math is simple: max hit and accuracy come primarily from your weapon and strength bonus. A whip upgrade to a Fang adds ~15% DPS. A Fighter Torso to Bandos Chestplate adds ~1%. Spend where it counts.

CHEAP TRICK（省钱技巧）

Fury gives all three style bonuses and +15 defense. It's the single best all-purpose necklace for 1.5M. Buy it first and wear it everywhere until you can afford style-specific BIS necks.

4. Tier 3: Armor Pieces That Actually Matter（第三优先级：真正重要的护甲）

Armor priority is Body > Legs > everything else. Here's the exact order for each style:

Melee Armor Priority（近战护甲优先级）

  1. Fighter Torso (FREE from Barbarian Assault) — +4 strength, same as Bandos Chestplate. Get this before spending a single GP on melee armor.
  2. Obsidian Platelegs (~800K) — +1 strength. Budget legs.
  3. Berserker Helm / Neitiznot Helm (FREE quest reward) — +3 strength. Mandatory.
  4. Bandos Tassets (~20M) — +2 strength over Obsidian legs. Late upgrade.
  5. Bandos Chestplate (~27M) — +4 strength. Same as Torso. Buy last.

Ranged Armor Priority（远程护甲优先级）

  1. Blessed D'hide Body + Chaps (~1M total) — +1 prayer bonus over black d'hide. Worth it.
  2. Karil's Top + Skirt (~2M total) — Best magic defense for budget. For fight caves and GWD.
  3. Armadyl Chestplate + Chainskirt (~40M total) — +6 ranged attack over blessed. Buy after BowFa.
  4. Masori / Crystal Armor (80M+) — Endgame. Crystal only worth it with BowFa equipped.

Magic Armor Priority（魔法护甲优先级）

  1. Mystic Robes (~200K) — Buy immediately. Budget magic armor.
  2. Ahrim's Top + Bottom (~2M) — Significant magic attack bonus upgrade for the price.
  3. Ancestral Robe Top + Bottom (~120M+) — +2% magic damage each. Endgame luxury.

NEVER DO THIS（绝对不要做）

  • Buy Bandos Chestplate before Tassets (same strength bonus as free Torso)
  • Buy Armadyl armor before BowFa (ranged armor gives tiny attack bonuses)
  • Buy Primordial Boots before Torture/Anguish (boots are marginal)
  • Sell your only good weapon to buy armor (always keep your weapon)
  • Buy Pegasians before Anguish (Pegasians give +5 ranged attack for 35M; Anguish gives +15 for 15M)

FAQ（常见问题）

Q: Should I buy Bandos or save for BowFa?（应该买Bandos还是存钱买BowFa？）

Save for BowFa. Bandos Chestplate + Tassets give +6 strength total for ~47M. BowFa unlocks Inferno, GWD solo, and raids — worth every GP. Buy Bandos when you have nothing else to buy.

Q: Is the Fighter Torso really that important?（Fighter Torso真的那么重要吗？）

Yes. It has the same +4 strength bonus as a Bandos Chestplate (27M) and costs zero GP. You can get it in 2-3 hours at Barbarian Assault. It's literally free DPS.

Q: What's the single best GP-to-DPS upgrade?（性价比最高的DPS升级是什么？）

Abyssal Whip at 1.5M. It's not even close. The whip gives you access to 99% of melee content. If you don't have one, stop everything and buy it.

Q: When should I buy a Twisted Bow?（什么时候该买Twisted Bow？）

When you have literally everything else. TBow is 1.6B. By the time you can afford one, you should already own full Bandos, Ancestral, BowFa, all zenyte jewelry, Rigour, and Augury. TBow is the last thing you buy, not the first.

Final Tips（最终建议）

  • Weapons first, always. A player with a Fang and monk robes out-DPSes a player with full Bandos and a Dragon Scimitar. The math is brutal and consistent.
  • Free upgrades exist. Fighter Torso, Fire Cape, Barrows Gloves, Neitiznot Helm, Dragon Defender, Ava's Assembler, and Mage Arena 2 Cape all cost zero GP. Get every single one before spending on upgrades.
  • Prayers are gear. Piety (70 Prayer, ~1M in bones) gives +25% defense, +23% strength, +20% attack. It's a 23% DPS increase. Rigour (74 Prayer, ~15M for scroll) is the single best ranged upgrade in the game.
  • One style at a time. Don't spread your GP across all three styles equally. Max out melee first (most content), then ranged, then magic last.
