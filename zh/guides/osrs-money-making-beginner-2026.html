1. How the OSRS Economy Works（OSRS经济如何运作）

OSRS has a fully player-driven economy. Items are priced based on supply and demand — there's no fixed store price for most things. As a new player, you need to understand three key concepts:

  • The Grand Exchange (GE)（大交易所） — The central marketplace in Varrock. You put items up for sale/buy and the game matches you with other players automatically. Almost everything is traded here.
  • GE Price vs Shop Price（GE价格 vs 商店价格） — Store prices are usually much lower than GE prices. Selling to the GE is almost always better than selling to a shop.
  • Raw materials vs Processed goods（原材料 vs 加工品） — Raw ores, logs, and hides are worth money. Processed versions (bars, planks, leather) are often worth more — or sometimes less. Always check the GE before processing.
💡 Install the RuneLite GE Price Overlay（安装RuneLite GE价格覆盖层）

With RuneLite, every item in your inventory shows its current GE price when you hover over it. Invaluable for knowing what's worth keeping and what to drop.

2. Your First 100k GP — Day 1 Methods（你的第一个10万GP — 第一天方法）

These methods need zero stats and zero GP to start. They're slow, but they're how every player starts:

② 🐄 Cowhide Tanning (Best Day 1 Method)（牛皮鞣制 - 最佳第一天方法）

  1. Kill cows east of Lumbridge. Pick up cowhides (100% drop rate).
  2. Take cowhides to the Tanner in Al Kharid (just south of Lumbridge, 1 GP per hide to tan)
  3. Sell Soft Leather or Hard Leather on the Grand Exchange (~200–300 GP each)
  4. Profit: ~150–250 GP per hide, 100+ hides/hour = 15,000–25,000 GP/hr

③ 🪨 Mining Copper/Tin Ore（开采铜/锡矿）

Mine copper or tin ore at Varrock East/West Mine, sell at GE for ~100–150 GP each. Very low GP/hr but trains Mining simultaneously.

④ 🌳 Cutting Logs in Draynor（德雷诺砍伐原木）

Willows at Draynor Village bank (need 30 Woodcutting) sell for ~10–15 GP each. With 1,000 logs per hour, that's 10,000–15,000 GP/hr. Not exciting, but AFK and trains Woodcutting.

💡 Which Method Should You Pick?（应该选哪个方法？）

0 combat stats（0战斗属性）: Cowhide tanning (15k–25k/hr) → fastest zero-req method. 10+ combat（10+战斗）: Al-Kharid Warriors (20k–35k/hr). 33+ Magic: Wine of Zamorak (100k–200k/hr) — best F2P method if you have Magic. 40+ combat: Hill Giants or Ogress Warriors (50k–100k/hr). The rule is simple（规则很简单）: always use the highest GP/hr method you qualify for.

🏆 Best F2P Method Under Level 50: Ogress Warriors（50级以下最佳F2P方法：食人魔战士）

After completing Dragon Slayer I (which also gives you access to Corsair Cove), Ogress Warriors drop Rune items, coins, and gems. Bring food and Rune armor. 40,000–80,000 GP/hr at 50+ combat.

⑤ 🌿 Herb Runs (Passive Income — #1 Recommended P2P Method)（草药种植 - 被动收入，最推荐会员方法）

Plant Ranarr/Toadflax seeds every 80 minutes. Each patch takes ~30 seconds to harvest. With 5+ patches (requires some quests), earn 100,000–300,000 GP/run passively. This is the best money-to-time ratio in the early game.

🌿 Herb Run Step-by-Step（草药种植逐步操作）

  1. Buy seeds（买种子） at GE: Ranarr (~15k GP each) if 50+ Farming, or Toadflax (~2k GP) if lower
  2. Get supercompost（获取高级堆肥） from GE (buy or make from high-level herbs)
  3. Visit 5 patches（访问5个田地）: Falador → Catherby → Ardougne → Port Phasmatys → Trollheim
  4. Rake patches, compost, plant seeds（耙地、堆肥、播种） — takes ~3 minutes total for all 5 patches
  5. Wait 80 minutes（等待80分钟） (go train combat or do quests in the meantime)
  6. Harvest（收获）: 5 patches × ~10 herbs = 50 herbs per run. Ranarr = 2k GP each = 100k per run

⑥ 🐦 Birdhouse Runs (Passive Hunter XP + GP)（鸟屋跑图 - 被动猎人XP+GP）

Set up bird houses on Fossil Island every 50 minutes. Each run takes 2 minutes and earns 5,000–15,000 GP in bird nests + Hunter XP. Run it on a timer while doing other activities. Best combined with herb runs: alternate between herb runs (80 min) and birdhouse runs (50 min) for maximum passive income.

⑦ ⚒️ Blast Furnace (Active, 300k–600k GP/hr)（高炉炼钢 - 主动赚钱）

Smelt bars at the Blast Furnace minigame (Keldagrim). Steel bars at 60 Smithing = ~600,000 GP/hr. Gold bars at 40 Smithing (with Goldsmith Gauntlets from Family Crest quest) = ~300,000 GP/hr but also massive Smithing XP. How to get there: Take minecart from Ice Mountain (near Falador) or teleport with Dwarven Helmet.

⑧ 🐟 Fishing Lobsters / Monkfish (AFK)（钓鱼龙虾/僧侣鱼 - 挂机）

Fishing Lobsters at Karamja or Monkfish at Piscatoris (Swan Song quest) earns 80,000–150,000 GP/hr while being almost fully AFK. Great while watching YouTube or studying. Tip: Monkfish heal more than Lobsters (16 HP vs 8 HP), making them better for both food and GP.

5. Using the Grand Exchange Smartly（聪明使用大交易所）

The Grand Exchange (GE) is in northwestern Varrock. It's the beating heart of the OSRS economy. Here's how to use it effectively:

⑨ 🔍 Always Check Prices Before Selling（出售前务必检查价格）

Go to prices.runescape.wiki before listing anything. The GE "guide price" shown in-game is often outdated — the wiki shows live trading prices with 5-minute updates.

⑩ 📊 Instant Buy vs Patient Sell（即时购买 vs 耐心出售）

  • Buying（购买）: Offer 5–10% above GE price to buy instantly. Useful for gear upgrades.
  • Selling（出售）: List at 1 GP below GE price for fast sales. Or list at market price and wait 10–30 min.

💼 GE Slot Limits（GE槽位限制）

Free players get 3 GE slots（3个GE槽位）. Members get 8 slots（8个槽位）. With only 3 slots, prioritize: 1 slot for selling bulk drops, 1 for buying consumables, 1 for buying gear.

📈 Beginner Flip: Cowhide → Hard Leather（新手倒卖：牛皮→硬皮革）

Buy Cowhides at GE (~200 GP), tan them at Al Kharid (1 GP each), sell Hard Leather at GE (~250 GP). 50 GP profit per hide, 1,000+ hides/hour = 50,000 GP/hr passive arbitrage. Not exciting but genuinely profitable with zero risk.

6. Money Making Mistakes to Avoid（应避免的赚钱错误）

  • ❌ Dropping bones and hides（扔掉骨头和皮） — Big Bones are worth 250+ GP each. Always bank them or bury for Prayer XP.
  • ❌ Selling to shops（卖到商店） — General stores pay a fraction of GE price. Always sell valuable items at the GE.
  • ❌ Processing low-value items（加工低价值物品） — Tanning leather adds value. Cutting gems usually doesn't (uncut sapphires ≈ cut sapphires in price early on). Always verify.
  • ❌ Buying expensive gear too early（过早购买昂贵装备） — Dragon Scimitar costs 50k. Worth buying at 60 Attack, not before. Train to the requirement first.
  • ❌ Ignoring seed drops（忽略种子掉落） — Ranarr seeds from Slayer can be worth 30,000–50,000 GP each. Check every seed before dropping.
  • ❌ Skipping quest rewards（跳过任务奖励） — Quests like Waterfall are free level 30 Attack/Strength. That's hours of training time saved.
  • ❌ Not using teleports（不使用传送） — Walking between bank and training spot wastes 20–30% of your time. Buy teleport tabs — they pay for themselves.
  • ❌ Flipping without research（不作调查就倒卖） — Only flip items you understand (cowhides, leather, basic ores). Never flip items you haven't tracked for at least a week.
  • ❌ Staying on low-GP methods after unlocking better ones（解锁更好方法后仍用低GP方法） — Every hour at 10k/hr when you could do 50k/hr = 40k lost forever. Switch immediately.

8. Money Making FAQs（赚钱常见问题解答）

What's the fastest way to make 1M GP as a new player?（新手赚100万GP最快方法是什么？）

With membership: herb runs + birdhouse runs (passive), Blast Furnace (active). Without membership: kill Ogress Warriors after Dragon Slayer I, or Wine of Zamorak with 33 Magic. 1M GP takes 2–3 days of casual play as a member, or 5–10 days F2P.

Is merching (flipping) a good way to make GP as a beginner?（新手倒卖是好方法吗？）

Cowhide flipping is simple and profitable (20k–50k/hr). Advanced merching (buy low, sell high on large items) requires GE knowledge and 1M+ GP starting capital. Learn it after you have 1M+ GP to work with. Only flip items you've tracked for a week.

Can I make good GP in F2P? Is membership worth it?（F2P能赚到好金币吗？会员值得吗？）

Yes, but it's much slower. 100k–200k/hr is achievable F2P with Ogress Warriors or Wine of Zamorak. Members see 3–10x better rates. Buy a Bond (700k GP) as fast as possible — it's the best investment you'll ever make.

What's the first big purchase I should make?（第一个大件购买应该是什么？）

Rune armor (full set ~100k GP) at level 40 Defence. This is a massive survivability upgrade that lets you train more efficiently. Before that: Rune Scimitar at 40 Attack (20k GP) — this single purchase doubles your kill speed.

How do I know if an item is worth keeping or dropping?（怎么知道物品值不值得捡？）

Rule: If you don't know what it is, CHECK THE GE PRICE（如果不认识，查GE价格）. Right-click a GE Clerk → "Item Lookup" → search the item. Common "junk" that's valuable（常见的值钱"垃圾"）: Uncut gems (500–5,000 GP), Grimy herbs (200–2,000 GP), Seeds like Ranarr (~15,000 GP), Big Bones (~200 GP). When in doubt, bank it and check later.

What are "Bonds" and how do I get one?（什么是"绑定券"？怎么获得？）

Bonds give 14 days of membership. Buy from other players for ~700k–900k GP at the GE. This is how F2P players become members without spending real money. Get 700k GP in F2P → buy Bond → become member. Best investment ever.

How much GP should I keep as an "emergency fund"?（应该留多少金币作为"应急基金"？）

Always keep at least 50k–100k GP in your bank for gear repairs, teleport tabs, and emergency food. Don't spend every last coin on gear — having GP flexibility means you can take advantage of good deals on the GE.

Is High Alchemy really worth it for money making?（高等级炼金术真的值得吗？）

Absolutely yes（绝对值得）. At Magic 55, High Alchemy converts items into GP at 60% of their GE value. Buy Yew Longbows (~4k GP), alch them for ~3,900 GP. Profit: ~300 GP per alch + 65 Magic XP. Best combined with Fletching（最好配合制箭术）: buy logs, fletch into bows, alch for profit.

What's the difference between GE "guide price" and actual trading price?（GE"指导价"与实际交易价有何区别？）

GE guide price (shown in-game) is an average that updates slowly (every few hours)（更新缓慢的平均值）. The actual price you'll buy/sell at is usually within 1–5% of guide price. Always check prices.runescape.wiki for live prices (updated every 5 minutes) before listing anything valuable.

Should I bank everything or only valuable drops?（应该全部存银行还是只存值钱掉落？）

Bank everything worth 100+ GP（所有价值100+GP的物品）. That includes: all hides, all bones (Big Bones especially), all gems (uncut), all herbs, all seeds. Drop only（只扔掉）: items with 0 GP value (ashes, burnt food, regular bones if you don't want Prayer XP). Pro tip（专业提示）: With RuneLite, use the "Item Value" plugin to see GE prices on every drop.

How do I start a herb run? What quests do I need?（怎么开始草药种植？需要哪些任务？）

Minimum: 9 Farming + completion of Fairytale I — Growing Pains (unlocks Fairy Rings for fast travel between patches). Ideal（最佳）: Complete My Arm's Big Adventure (unlocks Trollheim patch, which never gets diseased). Buy Ranarr seeds at GE, buy supercompost, plant at 5 patches. Takes ~3 minutes per run. Returns in 80 minutes.

Is killing Green Dragons in the Wilderness worth the risk?（杀野外绿龙值得冒风险吗？）

Green Dragons drop Dragon Bones (~1,500 GP) and Green Dragonhide (~800 GP) = ~2,300 GP per kill. At 40–50 kills per hour = 100k–200k GP/hr. However（但是）: PKers (player killers) actively hunt dragon killers. Only do this with 3-iteming（只带3件物品）: Wear only 3 items (weapon + shield + amulet), protect the rest. If you die, you lose nothing.

What's the best way to make my FIRST Bond (700k GP)?（赚第一个绑定券的最佳方法？）

F2P route (recommended)（推荐F2P路线）: Cowhides → Hill Giants (40+ combat) → Ogress Warriors (after Dragon Slayer I). Accumulate 700k GP over 3–5 days of casual play. Key（关键）: Don't spend GP on gear — wear the cheapest armor that works. Every GP goes toward the Bond. Once you have membership, your income rate jumps to 300k–600k/hr with Blast Furnace.

Final Tips — Money Making for Beginners（新手赚钱终极建议）

  • Don't ignore F2P money methods（不要忽视F2P赚钱方法）. Cowhides, runes from the Dark Wizards, and Essence mining are your bread and butter before membership. They're slow but guaranteed income.
  • Set a weekly GP goal（设定每周金币目标）. Even 50K GP/week adds up fast. Track your bank value on the OSRS Wiki's high scores to stay motivated.
  • Always check GE prices before selling（出售前务必检查GE价格）. Sell in bulk, not one-by-one. The GE tax is lower on large trades and you get better offers.
  • Avoid scam methods（避免诈骗方法）. "Double your money" lures and trust trades will clean you out. If a money method sounds too good to be true, it is.
  • Reinvest your first 500K into a Membership Bond（将第一个50万投资到会员绑定券）. This unlocks P2P, where money-making rates are 5–10× better. It's the single best investment a new player can make.
  • Always have a 50k–100k GP emergency fund（始终保持5-10万GP应急基金）. Don't spend every last coin on gear. Flexibility means you can grab good GE deals and teleport when needed.
  • Switch methods immediately when you unlock a better one（解锁更好方法后立即切换）. Every hour at 10k/hr when you could do 50k/hr = 40k GP lost forever.
