Maxing in OSRS takes roughly 1,700-2,000 hours from a fresh account. If you do skills in the wrong order, that number balloons to 2,500+. The order matters — not just for speed, but for burnout prevention. You don't want to leave Agility, Runecrafting, and Mining for last. You also don't want to max combat stats first and have nothing to do but click rocks for 300 hours. This guide gives you the psychologically optimal maxing order: fastest dopamine hits first, slowest grinds spaced out, and maximum benefit from every cape's perk along the way.

1. The Maxing Philosophy — "Cape Perks First"（满级理念 — "披风技能优先"）

Every skill cape in OSRS has a perk. Some are useless (Firemaking). Some redefine how you play the game (Construction, Crafting, Agility). The golden rule of efficient maxing:

🏆 GOLDEN RULE

Get capes that help you get more capes — as early as possible.

Example: 99 Construction cape = unlimited house teleports. This saves hundreds of hours of running. Get it early. 99 Agility cape = faster run regen + grace shortcuts. Get it before you walk thousands of tiles for other skills.

2. Phase 1: The Fast Drops (99s 1-7)（第一阶段：快速99 · 1-7）

Your first seven 99s should be fast wins that build momentum. Each one takes under 40 hours and gives you a cape perk that helps with later skills:

1. Fletching (First 99)（制箭 · 第一个99）

Time to 9915-20 hours (darts) or 30 hours (longbows, profitable)
Cost0-5M (depends on method)
Cape PerkSearch for mithril grapple + crossbow (saves inventory space for clues)

Why first: It's the fastest 99. Seeing a cape in under 20 hours makes the remaining 22 feel possible. Psychological momentum is real.

2. Cooking (Second 99)（烹饪 · 第二个99）

Time to 9925-30 hours (wines)
Cost3-5M
Cape PerkNever burn food. Huge for PvM food prep.

3. Firemaking (Third 99)（生火 · 第三个99）

Time to 9935-40 hours (Wintertodt)
CostProfit: ~1-2M (+ supplies for other skills)
Cape PerkActs as a light source (useful for quests, slayer caves)

Wintertodt also gives decent supplies: seeds, herbs, ores, logs. These feed your Farming and Herblore later.

4-7. Thieving, Fishing, Woodcutting, Hunter（偷窃、钓鱼、伐木、猎人 · 第4-7个99）

These four are grouped because they alternate active/AFK styles. One AFK while you work, one active while you play:

3. Phase 2: The Utility Block (99s 8-14)（第二阶段：实用技能块 · 8-14）

These skills give cape perks that directly accelerate the remaining skills:

8. Construction (The Game-Changer)（建筑 · 游戏变革者）

Time to 9940-50 hours (Myth Capes / Mahogany Tables)
Cost~60-80M (from 50-99)
Cape PerkUnlimited teleports to your house AND to every house portal. The best teleport item in the game.

99 Construction is the highest priority utility cape. Unlimited house teleports + full restoration pool + jewelry box + portal nexus in one click. It saves 30+ seconds per bank trip. Over 1000+ hours of remaining gameplay, that's thousands of hours saved.

9-14. Farming, Crafting, Herblore, Smithing, Magic, Prayer（农场、制作、草药学、锻造、魔法、祈祷 · 9-14）

Herblore15 hrsPestle and mortar, Nardah tele for unfinished pots

4. Phase 3: The Buyables (99s 15-18)（第三阶段：可购买技能 · 15-18）

These skills cost GP but are fast. By now you should have 100M+ from Slayer + bossing from Phases 1-2:

These are the slowest skills. Space them out — never do two of these back to back:

Critical: Agility Last（关键：敏捷最后）

Agility must be your final 99. When you max, the Agility cape's graceful effect + unlimited run + shorter shortcuts means you'll never walk anywhere again on your maxed account. It's the most impactful perk for a maxed account. Getting it last means you've earned the right to never run out of energy.

FAQ（常见问题）

Q: How long does maxing actually take?（满级实际需要多长时间？）

1,700-2,000 hours for an efficient player who follows this guide. At 4 hours/day, that's 14-17 months. At 8 hours/day, 7-8 months. The key is never doing two slow skills back to back.

Q: Should I max combat at NMZ or through Slayer?（战斗应该在NMZ还是Slayer中训练到99？）

Slayer every time. NMZ is pure AFK but gives zero GP and zero Slayer XP. Training combat through Slayer gets you 99 Slayer + 200M+ GP by the time you max melees.

Q: What's the hardest skill mentally?（心理上最难的是哪个技能？）

Agility. 200 hours of clicking the same rooftop course. But Hallowed Sepulchre (72+) breaks the monotony — it's engaging, profitable, and gives better XP at 92+.

Q: Is Runecrafting still 250 hours?（Runecrafting还是需要250小时吗？）

No longer. Guardians of the Rift (GotR) makes Runecrafting significantly more tolerable and gives 40-50K XP/hr at 77+. Combined with GotR, RC is 150-200 hours now, not 250.

Are You Ready to Max?（准备好满级了吗？）

Start with Fletching. Get that first dopamine hit. Then follow this path one 99 at a time. 1,700 hours later, you'll be wearing a Max Cape.
