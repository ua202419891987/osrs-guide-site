① Prerequisite Quests & Skills（前置要求）

Dragon Slayer II requires 11 quests and high stats. Required skills: 68 Agility, 65 Crafting, 62 Smithing, 60 Magic, 58 Woodcutting, 55 Mining, 50 Firemaking, 50 Ranged, and 200 Quest Points. Essential quests include: Bone Voyage, The Dig Site, The Tale of the Muspah, and Dragon Slayer I. The quest takes 4-6 hours to complete.

② Vorkath Boss Fight（Vorkath战斗攻略）

Vorkath is the first major boss fight. Combat level 468. Recommended gear: Dragon Hunter Crossbow (or Rune Crossbow), Void Knight gear, Salve Amulet (e), Ruby Bolts (e) + Diamond Bolts (e). Attack phases: Phase 1 — Vorkath uses melee, ranged, and magic attacks. Pray Protect from Magic and walk to avoid the fireball. Phase 2 — Vorkath spawns a Zombified Spawn. Kill it immediately with 1-2 hits or attack Vorkath while praying against the spawn's attack. Key tip: Switch to Diamond Bolts (e) when Vorkath's HP drops below 30% for the special bolt effect.

③ Galvek Boss Fight（Galvek四阶段战斗）

Galvek is the final boss. Combat level 612. Four phases: Phase 1 — Air: Galvek uses ranged attacks. Pray Range, use Protect from Magic if he uses dragonfire. Phase 2 — Aerial: Galvek flies away and attacks from above. Use the Run+Hide mechanic behind rocks. Phase 3 — Ground: Galvek lands and uses all three attack styles. This is the most dangerous phase — keep your prayer switching fast. Phase 4 — Enrage: Galvek enrages at low HP. Use all defensive prayers and focus on damage. Recommended inventory: 3 Super Restore potions, 2 Saradomin Brews, 1 Ranging Potion, rest Sharks or better food.

④ Rewards & Post-Quest（任务奖励）

Completing Dragon Slayer II gives: 140K Attack XP, 80K Strength XP, 40K Defence XP, 60K Magic XP, 40K Ranged XP, 4 Quest Points, and the ability to fight Vorkath unlimited times. Vorkath farming is one of the best money-making methods in OSRS at 3-5M GP/hr. Key drops: Dragon Claws, Vorki pet, Draconic Visage, Skeletal Visage. With max gear, you can achieve 25-30 kills per hour.

FAQ

What combat level do I need for DS2?（需要多少战斗等级？）

Recommended: 80+ Combat, 75+ Ranged, 75+ HP. The bosses hit hard and have high defence.

Is DS2 harder than RFD?（DS2比RFD难吗？）

Yes, much harder due to the boss fights. RFD is mostly about quest requirements; DS2 requires real PvM skill.
