① Lumbridge & Draynor Diary（伦布里奇与垂恩日记）

Easy (25QP): Complete 5 tasks including chop a normal tree, pickpocket a man, cook a shrimp, smelt a bronze bar, and catch a shrimp. Reward: Explorer ring 1 — 30 free high alchemy casts daily (amazing for early-game profit). Medium (60QP): 8 tasks including kill a giant, complete Lost City, 10 farming trips. Reward: Explorer ring 2 — 60 free alchs, infinite teleports to cabbage patch.

② Varrock Diary（瓦洛克日记）

Easy (20QP): 5 tasks including mine iron ore, buy from shops, and smith bronze. Reward: Varrock teleport and 1 free daily battlestave. Medium (50QP): 7 tasks including make planks at the sawmill, 40 Firemaking. Reward: 15 free daily battlestaves — 75K GP/day passive income. This is one of the most profitable diary rewards in the game.

FAQ

Which diary should I do first?（先做哪个日记？）

Start with Lumbridge Easy for the Explorer ring (free alchs), then Varrock Medium for the daily battlestaves (passive income).
