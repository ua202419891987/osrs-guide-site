🌿 1. Why Farming Herb Runs Are the Best Passive Income（为什么药草跑是最佳被动收入）

Most money-making methods in OSRS require your active time — you have to be at the keyboard, clicking, fighting, mining, etc. Farming herb runs are different: you plant herbs, go do something else (Slayer, bossing, questing), then come back to harvest.

The math: A full herb run (all 9 patches) with Ranarr weeds costs about 180K GP to plant. 90 minutes later, you harvest ~180K GP worth of herbs (9 patches × 20 herbs avg = 180 herbs × ~1K GP each). Profit: ~0 GP (break-even) to ~500K GP (if using ultracompost + magic secateurs).

💰 Why Herb Runs Beat Every Other Passive Income

  • Time cost: 5 minutes per run (planting + harvesting)
  • Profit per hour (active): ~6M GP/hour (if you count the 90-minute等待时间)
  • Skill XP: Each run gives 50K–100K Farming XP
  • Scalability: More patches unlocked = more profit per run
  • AFK-able: Plant, then go AFK for 90 minutes

Compare this to other passive income: Investing in bonds (risky, requires millions upfront), merchanting on GE (requires market knowledge), or waiting for Birds nests (very low profit). Farming is the most reliable passive income for all account types.

💡 The Best Part: You Don't Need High Stats

You can start herb runs at Farming level 32 (unlocks Harralander, a decent mid-profit herb). Even at level 32, you can make 50K–100K GP per run. At level 73+ (Ranarr), you can make 200K–500K GP per run. The barrier to entry is very low.

🌱 3. Best Herbs to Plant (Profit per Run)（最佳种植药草（每次利润））

The best herb to plant depends on your Farming level and current GP. Here's the priority order:

🎯 Best Herbs by Farming Level

  • Level 32–37: Plant Harralander — cheap seeds, decent profit (~50K/run)
  • Level 38–43: Plant Toadflax — good profit (~180K/run), seeds moderately priced
  • Level 44–72: Plant Irit / Avantoe / Kwuarm — solid profit (~150–220K/run)
  • Level 73+: Plant Ranarr weed — best profit per seed (~200K/run), very high demand
  • Level 85+: Plant Torstol — highest profit (~600K/run), but seeds are expensive (25K each)

① Why Ranarr Weed Is the Best Mid-Game Herb（为什么兰纳草是中后期最佳药草）

Ranarr weed is used to make Defence potions and Prayer potions — two of the most consumed potions in OSRS. This means Ranarr seeds and herbs have consistently high demand on the GE, and the price rarely crashes.

At 100 GP per herb (harvested), and ~20 herbs per patch (with ultracompost), that's 2K GP per patch. With 9 patches, that's 180K GP per run. Minus seed cost (9 × 7K = 63K GP), profit = ~117K GP per run.

Do 4 runs per day (every 90 minutes) = ~468K GP profit per day. That's almost 500K GP for 20 minutes of actual work.

💡 Use Ultracompost Always

Ultracompost increases your average yield per patch from ~6 herbs to ~20 herbs. It costs about 2K GP per patch (18 patches × 2K = 36K GP total). The extra yield (14 more herbs per patch × 9 patches = 126 more herbs) is worth 12.6K GP (at 100 GP/herb). Ultracompost pays for itself 3x over.

🗺️ 4. All Herb Patch Locations (Map Route)（全部药草地点（地图路线））

There are 9 herb patches in OSRS. Here's the optimal route to visit them all in the shortest time:

① Optimal Herb Run Route (9 Patches)（最佳药草跑路线（9个地块））

Step 1: Ardougne South (Teleport to Ardougne)

Teleport to Ardougne (using Ardougne teleport tab or spell). Run south to the Ardougne herb patch (near the zoo). Plant, add ultracompost, harvest if ready.

Step 2: Catherby (Teleport to Catherby)

Use a Catherby teleport tablet (or walk from Ardougne via the submarine). The Catherby patch is north of the bank. Plant and compost.

Step 3: Falador (Teleport to Falador)

Teleport to Falador. The herb patch is in the Falador Park (northwest corner). Plant and compost.

Step 4: Port Phasmatys (Ectophial Teleport)

Use an Ectophial (reward from "Ghosts Ahoy" quest) to teleport to Port Phasmatys. The patch is west of the bank. Plant and compost.

Step 5: Trollheim (Teleport to Trollheim)

Use a Trollheim teleport tablet (or walk from Death Plateau). The patch is at the Troll Stronghold. Plant and compost.

Step 6: Weiss (Fairy Ring AIQ)

Use a Fairy Ring (code: AIQ) to teleport to Weiss. The patch is near the mine. Plant and compost.

Step 7: Harmony Island (Boat from Port Sarim)

Talk to Manual at Port Sarim to sail to Harmony Island (requires "Great Brain Robbery" quest). Plant and compost.

Step 8: Crandor (Quest requirement)

Requires "Dragon Slayer" quest completed. Use a Crandor teleport tablet or walk from Karamja. Plant and compost.

Step 9: Farming Guild (Requirement: 65 Farming)

Teleport to the Farming Guild (south of Ardougne). The patch is inside the guild. Plant and compost.

⏱️ Time per Run: ~5 Minutes

With optimized teleports, you can complete a full 9-patch herb run in 5 minutes. That's 5 minutes of work for 200K–600K GP profit 90 minutes later. No other money-making method in OSRS has this kind of return on time invested.

🚶 5. Step-by-Step Herb Run (5 Minutes)（分步药草跑（5分钟））

Here's exactly what to do at each patch:

① Rake the Patch (If Weedy)

Right-click the herb patch → select "Rake". This removes weeds and makes the patch ready for planting. (If you harvested the previous crop, the patch is already clear.)

② Add Ultracompost

Right-click the patch → select "Add compost". Choose Ultracompost from your inventory. This increases yield by 3x.

③ Plant the Seeds

Right-click the patch → select "Guide" (or "Plant"). Choose the herb seed you want to plant. One seed per patch.

④ Wait 90 Minutes (or do other content)

Herbs take 90 minutes (20 growth cycles × 4.5 min each) to fully grow. You don't need to be online — the timer runs even if you log out. Go do Slayer, bossing, or questing while you wait.

⑤ Harvest (90 Minutes Later)

Go back to each patch and right-click → "Harvest." You'll get 6–20 herbs per patch (depending on compost level and Farming level). After harvesting, the patch is clear and you can plant again immediately.

💡 Use a Rune Pouch for Teleport Runes

Buy a Rune pouch (from the "The Great Orb Project" minigame or ~1.5M GP on GE). Store Law runes, Water runes, and Astral runes (for Catherby teleport) or use teleport tablets to save inventory space.

⚡ 7. 5 Tips to Maximize Farming Profit（最大化农场利润的5个技巧）

① Always Use Ultracompost

We already covered this, but it's worth repeating: Ultracompost triples your yield. Never use regular compost or supercompost for herb runs. The extra profit from ultracompost pays for the cost many times over.

② Get Magic Secateurs (A Fairy Tale Part I)

Magic secateurs (unlocked from "A Fairy Tale Part I" quest) give a +10% yield bonus to all farming patches when equipped (just have them in inventory, no need to wield). This means instead of 18 herbs per patch, you get 19.8 herbs per patch on average. Over 9 patches, that's an extra 16 herbs per run = ~16K GP free profit.

③ Use Farming Potion Before Harvesting

A Farming potion (made from Snapdragon + Raw sweetcorn) temporarily boosts your Farming level by +3. If your level is just below the requirement for a higher-tier herb (e.g., 70 Farming, but Ranarr requires 73), you can drink a potion to plant the higher herb. This trick alone can double your profit per run.

④ Unlock the Farming Guild ASAP

The Farming Guild (requires 65 Farming) has a special herb patch that never gets diseased and has a higher yield. It also has a seed pack shop where you can buy rare seeds. Priority: get 65 Farming ASAP (about 10–15 hours of tree runs).

⑤ Do Tree Runs Simultaneously

While you wait for herbs to grow (90 minutes), do tree runs (plant trees in tree patches). Trees take 4–8 hours to grow, but they give massive Farming XP and can be very profitable (Magic trees, Calquat trees). Combine herb runs + tree runs = maximum Farming efficiency.

❓ 8. FAQ（常见问题）

Can F2P players do herb runs?

No. All herb patches are in members-only areas. F2P players can technically plant herbs in the Edgeville Dungeon patch, but it's very limited and not worth the time. Get membership to do proper herb runs.

What happens if I don't harvest my herbs in time?

Herbs don't "expire" or die if you don't harvest them immediately. However, if you plant new seeds without harvesting the old ones, the old herbs are destroyed. Always harvest before replanting.

How do I get ultracompost cheaply?

Buy supercompost from the GE (~200 GP each), then use a Volcanic ash (bought from "Fossil Island" or ~100 GP on GE) on the supercompost in your inventory. This converts it to ultracompost for 300 GP total per bucket (vs 2K GP buying ultracompost directly).

Should I plant the same herb in every patch?

Yes. Using the same herb in all patches makes your runs simpler and your profit more predictable. The only exception is if you're doing "herb box" runs (Farming Guild perk) — then you might mix herbs based on what the box gives you.

How long does it take to get 99 Farming with herb runs?

If you do 4 herb runs per day (all 9 patches, Ranarr weeds), you get about 80K Farming XP per day. From 70 Farming to 99, that's about 11 million XP = ~137 days (4.5 months). This is the fastest legitimate way to 99 Farming without botting.
