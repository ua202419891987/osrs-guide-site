① Downloading OSRS Mobile

Android: Download the APK from the official OSRS website or the Google Play Store. If the Play Store is inaccessible from China, use the direct APK download from runescape.com. Enable "Install from unknown sources" in your phone settings. iOS: Search for "Old School RuneScape" in the App Store. If it's not available in your region, switch your Apple ID to a US/UK App Store. The app is approximately 80MB for the initial download, plus additional game data (~200MB) on first launch.

② VPN Configuration for MobileVPN

Like the desktop version, OSRS Mobile requires a VPN from China. Set up your VPN before launching the game. Android split tunneling: In your VPN app settings, add OSRS Mobile to the VPN tunnel (only OSRS traffic goes through VPN). iOS: System-level VPN covers all apps automatically. For best performance, choose a Japan or Singapore VPN server. Mobile ping is typically 50-100ms higher than desktop expect 200-350ms from China.

③ Interface & Control Setup

OSRS Mobile has a touch-optimized interface. Key settings: enable "One-Handed Mode" for easier reach (moves UI elements to the bottom), adjust the "Function Button Size" to 150% for better tapping, enable "Long Press Duration" to 400ms (prevents accidental menus), and customize your action bar with your most-used items. For combat, enable "Auto-Retaliate" for AFK training and disable it for boss fights where you need precise control.

FAQ

Can I use the same account on mobile and desktop??

Yes. The same OSRS account works on both platforms. You can switch between devices seamlessly.

Does OSRS Mobile drain battery??

OSRS Mobile uses about 15-20% battery per hour on modern phones. Reduce brightness and enable battery saver mode to extend playtime.
