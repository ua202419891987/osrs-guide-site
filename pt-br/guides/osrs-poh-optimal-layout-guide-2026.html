1. Why Your POH Is a Money PrinterPOH

Before the POH: you spend 50K on teleport tablets per bossing session, run around the map for 2 minutes between herb run patches, and waste 3 minutes at Clan Wars portal resetting stats between kills.

After the POH: House Teleport → Pool click → Jewelry Box click → Portal click. Full restoration + teleport to anywhere in under 5 seconds. This time saving translates directly to more kills per hour, more herb runs per day, and more GP overall.

📊 REAL EXAMPLE

Vorkath without POH: 25 kills/hr (time wasted bank-returning). Vorkath with POH: 30 kills/hr. That's 5 extra kills per hour or ~500K extra GP/hr. Your POH pays for itself in active bossing time.

FAQ

Q: What's the fastest way to train Construction?

Mahogany Homes (50-84): ~150K XP/hr, ~4 GP/XP. Cheaper but slower. Mythical Cape racks (50-84): ~200K XP/hr, ~8 GP/XP. Faster but more expensive. Choose based on your GP situation.

Q: Do I need 99 Construction?

No. 84 gives you the Ornate Pool which is the most impactful unlock. 90 gives the combined Spirit Tree + Fairy Ring. 99 gives unlimited house teleports (via Max Cape perk). 84 is the sweet spot. Everything above is nice-to-have.

Q: Should I build a second floor?

No. A second floor increases load time and reduces the utility of teleport-to-house (you spawn downstairs). Keep everything on one floor. The 4x4 layout fits everything you need.

Q: Occult Altar vs switching spellbooks at the pyramid?

Occult Altar is worth it if you frequently switch between spellbooks (e.g., slayer on Lunars, bossing on Ancients). If you rarely switch, skip it. The 10M price tag only makes sense if you'd otherwise spend dozens of hours running to the pyramid.
