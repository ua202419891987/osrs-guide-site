🎯 Why Slayer Is the Best Money Maker: 400K-4M GP/hrSlayer

Slayer is the single most reliable money-making skill in Old School RuneScape — and it has been for years. Unlike bossing where you can go dry for hundreds of kills with nothing to show for it, Slayer pays you every single task. The consistent drops (alchables, herbs, seeds, runes, noted resources) add up fast across a full task, and the big ticket unique drops are a bonus on top.

Here's why Slayer dominates every other money maker in the game:

  • Guaranteed profit per task. Even "bad" tasks like Hellhounds drop hard clue scrolls worth 100K-1M+ on average. Every task has a floor.
  • You train combat while making money. Every abyssal demon you kill is Melee/Ranged/Magic XP + Slayer XP + GP. Triple value per hour.
  • Unlocks boss variants. Many tasks have boss alternatives — Cerberus (Hellhounds), Kraken (Cave Krakens), Thermonuclear Smoke Devil, Alchemical Hydra, Grotesque Guardians — that multiply profit by 3-5x.
  • Scales with your level. Higher Slayer = better tasks = more GP. It's a snowball system: every level unlocks more profitable monsters.
  • Passive XP in other skills. Bonecrusher gives Prayer XP, Herb Sack banks herbs, Gem Bag banks gems, Rune Pouch holds runes — all passive gains.

Consistent LootRune chains, rune med helms, noted pure ess, chaos/death runes, ensouled heads, hard clue scrolls (1/128)

Method: Enter Catacombs of Kourend. Run to the Abyssal Demon room (northeast area). Stack demons by running around the room gathering them, then freeze with Ice Barrage. Use protect from Melee. The Bonecrusher + Catacombs means your prayer never runs out. Pick up all rune items (alchable), whip/whip drops, and ensouled heads. Leave ashes on the ground unless you have an Ash Sanctifier.

Abyssal Demons - Catacombs Stacking Pattern: Run clockwise around the room to gather 6-9 demons. Freeze at the center intersection. Cast Ice Barrage 3-4 times per stack. Move to next corner, repeat. With 94+ Magic and Kodai Wand, you can clear 350-400 demons per hour, generating 50-100K in alchables alone plus the whip drop chance.

Method: Nechryaels spawn Death Spawns when attacked — this is actually GOOD for barrage because it adds more targets for AoE damage. Run to the Nechryael room (southeast Catacombs). Herb Sack is mandatory here — Nechryaels drop 4-8 grimy herbs per kill including snapdragons (~8K each) and ranarrs (~6K each).

Nechryael Loot Breakdown Per Hour: ~120 rune 2H swords (alch each for 38.4K; that's 4.6M in alchs alone, minus nature runes) + ~80 grimy herbs (Herb Sack banks them) + 30-50K in runes + hard clue scrolls. The death spawns don't have their own drop table but significantly boost AoE DPS, making Nechryaels the #1 GP/hr Slayer task in the game for sustained profit.

❓ FAQ + Pro Tips+

Q: What's the single best Slayer task for GP?:Slayer?

A: Nechryaels (barraged in Catacombs). At 900K-1.2M GP/hr with 100K+ Magic XP/hr, Nechryaels are the gold standard. The combination of rune alchables, grimy herbs (snapdragons, ranarrs), death runes, and hard clue scrolls creates the most consistent profit per task in the game. Abyssal Demons are a close second due to the Whip drop but Nechryael profit is more consistent hour-to-hour.

Q: Should I use Konar or Duradel for money?:KonarDuradel?

A: Duradel for consistency, Konar for the Brimstone Key lottery. Konar's Brimstone Keys average ~100K each and you get 1-3 per task. On good tasks with good locations, Konar beats Duradel. But when Konar assigns you Abyssal Demons in the Abyssal Area (no cannon, no Catacombs, no bursting), you'll wish you were at Duradel. For most players: use Duradel and do every 10th task at Konar for the point bonus.

Q: Is it worth doing boss variants on Slayer tasks?:SlayerBoss?

A: Absolutely yes — but only for specific bosses. The Slayer helm's 15% damage boost applies to boss variants, making on-task bossing significantly more profitable than off-task. Priority boss variants: Cerberus (Hellhounds, 91 Slayer), Alchemical Hydra (Hydras, 95 Slayer), Abyssal Sire (Abyssal Demons, 85 Slayer), Kraken (Cave Krakens, 87 Slayer). Skip the slower bosses: Grotesque Guardians, Thermonuclear Smoke Devil (unless you have specific gear).

Q: How much GP should I expect from 1-99 Slayer?:199SlayerGP?

A: Playing efficiently with a GP-focused block list, expect 300M-500M GP from 1-99 Slayer on a main account. This assumes you barrage profitable tasks, do boss variants where available, and consistently pick up alchable drops. The total varies based on RNG (whip drops, unique boss items) but 300M is a safe floor with 500M+ possible with good luck at Cerberus, Hydra, and Abyssal Demons.

Q: What Slayer unlocks should I prioritize for money?:Slayer?

A: Here's the optimal unlock order for a money-focused Slayer account:

  1. Gargoyle Smasher (120 pts) — Automatically finishes off Gargoyles. Saves 2-3 seconds per kill, ~15% more kills/hr.
  2. Bigger and Badder (150 pts) — Unlocks Superior Slayer monsters. Superiors drop totem pieces (for Skotizo) and imbued heart (40M+, 1/520 from superiors). One heart drop pays for all unlocks.
  3. Extend Nechryaels (100 pts) — More Nechryaels = more GP. Simple math.
  4. Extend Dust Devils (100 pts) — Same logic for the #2 best money task.
  5. Extend Abyssal Demons / Gargoyles (100 pts each) — Depends on which tasks you prefer.
  6. Herb Sack (750 Slayer pts) — Essential for herb-dropping tasks. Worth every point.
  7. Rune Pouch (750 Slayer pts) — Holds nature + fire + law runes for alching and teleporting. Saves 2 inventory slots forever.

Q: Can I make money on Slayer with 50-70 combat stats?:50-70Slayer?

A: Yes, but the money is modest. At 50-70 combat, focus on: Jellies (hard clues 1/64, ~200K/hr), Bloodvelds (blood runes, ~300K/hr), Ankou (death/blood runes, ~200K/hr), and Cave Horrors (Black Mask 1/512, ~500K each, but low kills/hr). Your real goal at this stage should be getting to 70-75 Slayer for Kurasks and Gargoyles. Don't worry about maximizing GP before those unlocks.

Q: What's better bursting in Catacombs or bossing on task?:vs Boss,?

A: It depends on your gear and the specific boss:

  • Bursting Nechryaels/Dust Devils/Abyssal Demons: 700K-1.2M GP/hr. Consistent, low effort, no supply cost beyond runes.
  • Cerberus on task (with Spectral Spirit Shield): 2.5M-3.5M GP/hr. Higher profit but requires 91 Slayer, Spectral, and more attention.
  • Alchemical Hydra on task: 3M-4M GP/hr. Best on-task boss but requires 95 Slayer.
  • Abyssal Sire on task: 1.5M-2.5M GP/hr. Worse than bursting Abyssal Demons unless you get lucky on bludgeon pieces.

For most players, burst regular tasks and boss only Cerberus/Hydra is the optimal GP strategy.

Pro Tip — Point Farming: Do tasks 1-9 at Turael/Spria (fast, trivial tasks), then task #10 at Duradel or Konar for the massive point bonus (75 from Duradel, 90 from Konar on every 10th). This is called "Turael boosting" and can generate 200+ Slayer points per hour. Use the extra points to skip bad tasks and extend the profit tasks.
