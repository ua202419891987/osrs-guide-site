① Optimal Completion Order（最优完成顺序）

For efficiency, complete diaries in this order: Lumbridge Hard > Falador Hard (for Shield 3 prayer restore) > Varrock Elite (for 120 daily battlestaves) > Ardougne Elite (for 99 thieving bonus cape) > Karamja Elite > Fremennik Elite > Western Provinces Elite. Focus on skills: 85 Slayer, 90 Farming, 86 Crafting, 80 Runecraft, 91 Fishing, 95 Cooking are the highest requirements across all elites.

② Falador Shield 4（法尔多护盾4 — 最实用精英奖励）

Falador Shield 4 is widely considered the best diary reward. Effect: Restores ALL your prayer points once per day (right-click "Prayer Restore"). This is effectively a free 2M+ GP value per day (equal to 4 prayer potions + 2 super restores). Additionally, it provides 10% increased melee damage against Saradomin faction monsters. Requirements: 81 Smithing, 70 Prayer, 66 Mining, 63 Farming, 59 Herblore for Hard tasks. Elite requires 85 Smithing.

FAQ

Which diary gives the best passive income?（哪个日记的被动收入最好？）

Varrock Elite (120 daily battlestaves = 600K+ GP/day). Ardougne Elite (Pickpocket bonus). Falador Elite (Free daily prayer restore = 2M+ GP equivalent).
