① Budget PvP Gear Setups

You don't need max gear to PK effectively. For a budget pure setup (under 5M): Dragon Scimitar, Rune Crossbow, Mystic Robes, Climbing Boots, Amulet of Glory, Blessing. For a medium setup (10-20M): Add Toxic Blowpipe, Trident of the Swamp, Barrows Gloves, Ancient Staff. For high risk (50M+): Add Torture, Anguish, Mage's Book, and Avernic Defender. Key rule: Never risk more than you're willing to lose. Always use +1 protection items in the Wilderness.

② Gear Switching 101

Efficient switching wins fights. Set up your RuneLite inventory tags: color-code your weapons (red for Melee, green for Ranged, blue for Magic), food (yellow), and potions (purple). Practice F-Key switching (F1 inventory, F2 prayer, F3 spells, F4 combat). The standard PvP switch order: Weapon first, then secondary item, then prayer. Practice in the Nightmare Zone until switching becomes muscle memory.

③ Tribrid Fighting

Tribrid (Melee-Ranged-Magic) is the standard for high-level PvP. The key is predicting your opponent's protection prayer. If they pray Melee, switch to Ranged. If they pray Ranged, freeze them with Ice Barrage and switch to Melee. Always carry: Vengeance (from Lunar magic), Ice Barrage (for freezing), Dragon Dagger or Claws (for spec). Practice in PvP Arena before risking gear in the Wilderness.

FAQ

How much GP do I need to start PvP??

You can start with 2-3M GP for a basic pure setup. Focus on learning with cheap gear first.

Yes, if you win 55%+ of your fights. Loot from PvP deaths can be very valuable. Start with low-risk gear until confident.
