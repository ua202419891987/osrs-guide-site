🔑 Requirements & Prerequisites

Before you can start birdhouse runs, you need to unlock Fossil Island and have the minimum Hunter level. Here's the full breakdown:

① Mandatory Requirements

  • Hunter level 9+ — This is the minimum to build regular birdhouses. Higher Hunter lets you use better birdhouse tiers.
  • Bone Voyage quest completed — This quest unlocks Fossil Island. It requires:
    • 🔸 35 Crafting
    • 🔸 30 Agility for a short shortcut (optional but helpful)
    • 🔸 Digsite pendant or access to the Digsite
  • Fossil Island access — Use the Digsite pendant to teleport to the Digsite, then talk to Curator Haig Halen or take the barge from Varrock Museum.

② Recommended Items

🎒 What to Bring on Every Birdhouse Run

  • 🔸 4x Birdhouses (regular, oak, willow, teak, or mahogany — depending on your level)
  • 🔸 4x Clockwork (each birdhouse needs one clockwork)
  • 🔸 4x Logs of the corresponding tier (regular, oak, willow, teak, or mahogany)
  • 🔸 10-30 Hunter seeds (barley, hammerstone, asgarnian, jute, yanillian, krandorian, wildblood)
  • 🔸 Digsite pendant (teleport to Digsite)
  • 🔸 Rune pouch with Law runes + Air runes or Teleport to House tabs (for POH teleport)
  • 🔸 1-2 empty inventory spaces for collected bird nests and seeds

③ Optional But Useful

  • 🔸 Fossil Island teleport scrolls — Rare drop from birdhouses themselves, but worth keeping
  • 🔸 RuneLite "Birdhouse Timer" plugin — Tracks when your birdhouses need re-harvesting
  • 🔸 Graceful outfit — Reduces run energy drain during the route
  • 🔸 Stamina potions — Not necessary but helps early on before you memorize the route
💡 Pro Tip

You can store a full birdhouse run setup in your bank and use a bank tag layout. Withdraw all four birdhouses (already made with clockwork), logs, and seeds in one click using bank tags. This cuts your prep time to ~5 seconds.

🪶 How Birdhouse Traps Work

Birdhouse traps work similarly to the passive traps used in normal Hunter training, but with key differences. They are fully passive — you set them and come back later to collect.

① The Core Mechanic

Birdhouses are placed on the four mushroom stumps found around the eastern Verdant Valley on Fossil Island. Once placed with seeds as bait, they automatically attract birds over time. After 50 minutes, the birdhouses are full and ready to harvest.

  • 🔸 Each birdhouse holds up to 10 birds (harvested as bird nests)
  • 🔸 Bird nests contain seeds — this is where the profit comes from
  • 🔸 You also gain Hunter XP for each bird caught
  • 🔸 The birdhouses do NOT need to be checked immediately at 50 minutes — they stay full until you harvest

② Setting Up a Birdhouse

  1. Craft the birdhouse: Use logs on the workbench in the House on the Hill (Fossil Island) or craft them in advance using 2 logs + 1 clockwork per birdhouse.
  2. Bait with seeds: Use your Hunter seeds on the birdhouse before placing it on a stump.
  3. Place on a mushroom stump: Use the baited birdhouse on an empty mushroom stump.
  4. Wait 50 minutes: The birdhouse works automatically. You can log out, train other skills, or do anything else.
  5. Harvest: Return to the stump, click "Empty" to collect the bird nests and XP.
  6. Rebait and replace: Add fresh seeds and place the birdhouse again to start the next cycle.

③ Important Mechanics to Know

  • 🔸 You can have one birdhouse per mushroom stump — 4 total per run
  • 🔸 Birdhouses stay on the ground permanently (even through server reboots)
  • 🔸 Each birdhouse catches birds periodically, not all at once — checking at exactly 50 minutes gives you the full 10 birds
  • 🔸 You can check early to get partial loot, but it's not recommended
  • 🔸 The type of birdhouse determines the quality of bird nests and the XP per bird
  • 🔸 The type of seed used determines the quality of seeds found in nests
💡 Mechanics Insight

Birdhouse traps do NOT fail. Unlike most Hunter training where traps can collapse, birdhouses always catch the maximum number of birds over 50 minutes. This guarantees consistent XP and profit every single run.

🏠 Birdhouse Types & Tiers

There are five tiers of birdhouses, each requiring a higher Hunter level and better logs. Upgrading is always worth it for the increased XP and better seed rewards.

② When to Upgrade

  • Level 25-34: Oak birdhouses — small upgrade, better XP per run

③ Crafting Birdhouses

You need 2 logs + 1 clockwork to craft a single birdhouse of the corresponding tier. Craft them at the workbench in the House on the Hill on Fossil Island (east of the mushroom forest). The crafting requires:

  • 🔸 5 Crafting to build regular birdhouses
  • 🔸 14 Crafting for oak
  • 🔸 24 Crafting for willow
  • 🔸 34 Crafting for teak
  • 🔸 44 Crafting for mahogany
💡 Efficiency Tip

Always craft your birdhouses in bulk. Make 20-40 at a time (80-160 logs + 20-40 clockwork). Store the finished birdhouses in your bank. This way you only need to withdraw ready-made birdhouses before each run instead of crafting them on the spot.

⚙️ Clockwork Mechanism Explained

Clockwork is an essential component for building birdhouses. Without it, you cannot create any birdhouse trap. Here's everything you need to know about clockwork.

① How to Get Clockwork

  • Buy from the Grand Exchange: ~100-200 GP each (very cheap)
  • Player-made: Craft using 1 steel bar on a clockwork bench (requires 8 Crafting) — not worth making yourself unless you're an ironman
  • Ironman alternative: Purchase from Brimstail in the Tree Gnome Stronghold for 200 GP each (unlimited stock)

② How Many You Need

  • 🔸 1 clockwork per birdhouse
  • 🔸 4 clockwork per full run
  • 🔸 If you craft birdhouses in bulk, you never need to touch clockwork again during a run — just withdraw pre-made birdhouses

③ Cost Analysis

Clockworks cost about 100-200 GP each. With 4 birdhouses per run, that's 400-800 GP in clockwork cost per run. Since even the worst birdhouse run makes 50k GP, the clockwork cost is negligible (less than 2% of your profit).

💡 Money-Saving Tip

One clockwork lasts forever once crafted into a birdhouse. The birdhouse itself can be re-used indefinitely — you only lose the clockwork if you dismantle the birdhouse. So the 400-800 GP clockwork cost is a one-time investment, not a recurring cost.

🌾 Seed Types & Loot Table

The seeds you use as bait determine what kind of seeds you get back from the bird nests. This is the core of the profit mechanic — using the right seeds can dramatically increase your GP per run.

① Hunter Seeds (Bait Seeds)

These are the seeds you use to bait your birdhouses. They are grown via the Farming skill and come in six tiers:

  • 🔸 Barley seed (Farming 3) — cheapest bait
  • 🔸 Hammerstone seed (Farming 4)
  • 🔸 Asgarnian seed (Farming 7)
  • 🔸 Jute seed (Farming 13)
  • 🔸 Yanillian seed (Farming 16)
  • 🔸 Krandorian seed (Farming 22)
  • 🔸 Wildblood seed (Farming 28) — best bait, highest value returns

③ Recommended Bait Strategy

  • Best budget bait: Yanillian seeds — cheap to buy (or grow yourself) and gives access to high-value herb seeds like Ranarr and Kwuarm
  • Best overall bait: Wildblood seeds — most expensive to buy but yields the highest-value returns including Torstol and Snapdragon seeds
  • Ironman tip: Use the best seeds you can grow yourself. Krandorian and Wildblood are excellent if you have the Farming levels
  • Each birdhouse consumes: 10 seeds per placement. 4 birdhouses = 40 seeds per run

④ Additional Bird Nest Rewards

Besides seeds, bird nests can contain:

  • 🔸 Bird nest (empty) — used in Herblore for Sara brews
  • 🔸 Bird nest (ring) — good clue scroll reward potential
  • 🔸 Fossil Island teleport scroll — valuable for future runs
  • 🔸 Crushed nest — used in herblore potions

🗺️ Optimal Birdhouse Run Route

A well-planned route minimizes travel time so you can complete a full run in under 2 minutes. Here's the optimal path every OSRS player should use:

① Step-by-Step Route

  1. Start at a bank: Withdraw your pre-made birdhouses, logs, seeds, and Digsite pendant. Grand Exchange or any major bank works.
  2. Teleport to Digsite: Use your Digsite pendant (teleport to Digsite).
  3. Take the barge: Run south-east from the Digsite teleport spot to the barge. Talk to the barge master to travel to Fossil Island.
  4. Run east to Verdant Valley: Once on Fossil Island, run east past the Museum Camp into the Verdant Valley mushroom forest.
  5. Harvest mushroom stumps (x4): There are 4 mushroom stumps in the Verdant Valley area:
    • 🔸 Stump 1: South-east corner of the valley
    • 🔸 Stump 2: Central-eastern area near the trail
    • 🔸 Stump 3: North side near the trees
    • 🔸 Stump 4: North-west section of the valley
  6. Empty each stump: Click "Empty" to collect nests and XP. Wait for the XP drop before moving to confirm collection.
  7. Rebait and replace: Use 10 seeds on each birdhouse, then use the birdhouse back on the stump.
  8. Teleport back: Use a Teleport to House tab or Law runes to get back to a bank. Deposit the nests and seeds.
  9. Done! Total time: ~1.5 to 2.5 minutes.

② Route Map (Text Guide)

🗺️ Quick Reference: Teleport Path

Bank → Digsite pendant → Barge → Fossil Island → Run east → 4 stumps → Harvest/Rebait → Teleport to House → Bank

③ Alternative Teleport Options

  • Fossil Island teleport scroll: Teleports you directly to the Museum Camp on Fossil Island — saves the barge trip. These are rare drops from birdhouses themselves.
  • Digsite pendant (best option): Easiest to obtain, unlimited teleports, and very fast.
  • Fairy ring CKS: Teleports to the Digsite area (requires Fairytale II - Cure a Queen for un-locked rings).
  • POH teleport + Digsite portal: If your POH has a Digsite portal, you can house teleport → portal → barge.

④ Route Optimization Tips

  • 🔸 Pre-make your birdhouses: Craft all 4 birdhouses at a workbench before your first run. Store them in the bank.
  • 🔸 Use bank tags: Create a "Birdhouse Run" bank tag that withdraws all 4 birdhouses, logs, and seeds in one click.
  • 🔸 Grapple shortcut: If you have 31 Agility and a mithril grapple, you can use a shortcut on Fossil Island that slightly reduces run time.
  • 🔸 RuneLite plugin: Install the Birdhouse Timer plugin — it shows a countdown overlay so you know exactly when to run again.
  • 🔸 Combine with herb runs: Birdhouse timer (50 min) is very close to herb run timer (80 min). Do a birdhouse run every 50 minutes and an herb run every other birdhouse run.

④ Profit per Hour (Active Play vs Passive)

Birdhouse runs are not comparable to active money-making methods in terms of GP/hour if you only count the 2-minute run. However, the magic of birdhouse runs is that they're completely passive — you do other activities (slayer, bossing, skilling) while the birdhouses work:

📅 Daily Passive Profit Strategy

To maximize the value of birdhouse runs, integrate them into a daily routine. Here's the optimal strategy for consistent passive income and XP.

① The Perfect Daily Routine

  1. Morning login: Do your first birdhouse run immediately. Most players can sneak in 8-12 runs per day with minimal effort.
  2. Set a 50-minute timer: Use RuneLite's built-in Birdhouse Timer plugin or set a phone timer. When it goes off, drop everything and do the 2-minute run.
  3. Stack with herb runs: Herb runs take 80 minutes. Do birdhouse runs every ~50 minutes. Every other birdhouse run, do an herb run too. This creates a highly efficient skilling loop.
  4. End-of-session check: Before logging out, do one final birdhouse run. When you log in next time, the birdhouses will be ready again.

❓ Birdhouse Runs FAQ

Do I need to complete Bone Voyage to do birdhouse runs?

Yes, absolutely. Birdhouse runs are only possible on Fossil Island, which requires completing the Bone Voyage quest. There is no workaround or shortcut — you must do the quest first.

What's the minimum Hunter level for birdhouses?

You need at least 9 Hunter to build and place regular birdhouses. You can reach level 9 Hunter in about 15 minutes by catching Crimson Swifts or use the museum quiz in Varrock to jump straight to level 9 without any actual Hunter training.

How long do birdhouses take to fill up?

Birdhouses fill up completely after 50 minutes. Each birdhouse catches 10 birds during this time. You can check them early to get partial loot, but this is inefficient — always wait the full 50 minutes for maximum XP and loot per run.

Can I do birdhouse runs on an ironman?

Yes! Birdhouse runs are excellent for ironmen. The herb seeds you get (Ranarr, Snapdragon, Torstol) are incredibly valuable for herblore training. Ironmen should prioritize Krandorian and Wildblood seeds as bait for the best returns.

What seeds should I use for bait?

For maximum profit, use Wildblood seeds or Krandorian seeds. For a good balance of cost and return, use Yanillian seeds. Avoid Barley and Hammerstone seeds once you have access to better options.

Are birdhouse runs better than active Hunter training?

For XP alone, active methods like Black Chins or Maniacal Monkeys give far more XP per hour. However, birdhouse runs give you free XP and GP while you do other content. They are not a replacement for active training — they are a supplement that stacks on top of everything else you do.

Do I need to worry about the clockwork cost?

No. Clockwork is ~100-200 GP each and one clockwork lasts forever once crafted into a birdhouse. The 800 GP total investment is negligible compared to the 50k-150k GP per run. The clockwork cost is a one-time fee, not recurring.

Can I do birdhouse runs on mobile?

Yes, birdhouse runs are very mobile-friendly. The route is simple (Digsite teleport → barge → run east → 4 stumps → bank teleport). Each run takes under 3 minutes even on mobile. The Fossil Island teleport scroll also works on mobile.

What should I do with the bird nests I collect?

You have three options: Sell on the Grand Exchange for immediate profit (most common), crush them for herblore (requires 42 Herblore to use in Sara brews), or keep for your own herblore training. Selling is usually the best option for non-ironmen.

How often should I check GE prices for birdhouse materials?

Check once a week. Seed prices (especially Ranarr, Snapdragon, and Torstol) fluctuate based on demand and supply. If Wildblood seeds become too expensive relative to the return, switch to Krandorian or Yanillian seeds for better margins.

Can I lose my birdhouses?

No. Birdhouses placed on mushroom stumps persist indefinitely, even across game updates and server restarts. The only way to lose a birdhouse is to dismantle it yourself. You can safely leave them on the stumps and come back days later to harvest.

What happens if I harvest early?

If you harvest a birdhouse before the full 50 minutes, you'll receive fewer birds (proportional to the time elapsed). For example, harvesting at 25 minutes gives about 5 birds out of the maximum 10. It's always better to wait for the full timer unless you're about to log out for the day.
