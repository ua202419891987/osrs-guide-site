What does level 40 Ranged actually unlock? When can you enter the God Wars Dungeon? What level do you need for Barrows gloves? This guide lists every significant milestone across all 23 OSRS skills — gear unlocks, quest requirements, shortcut access, and money-maker thresholds. OSRS has 23 skills and over 700,000 possible level combinations. Most levels are incremental — one more step toward 99. But some levels are milestones — they unlock something transformative: a piece of gear that doubles your damage, a shortcut that saves 5 minutes per trip, a quest that opens an entire area, or a money-maker that changes your financial trajectory. This guide focuses exclusively on those milestone levels — the ones worth celebrating and planning for. For the fastest methods to reach each milestone, check our fastest leveling guide.

Until you hit 70, you're using dragon weapons or worse. Get to 70 Attack as fast as possible. For more on combat progression, see our combat training guide.

🎯 The Three Ranged Milestones That Matter Most

1. Level 40 — Green d'hide + Rune crossbow: This is your F2P ranged endgame. Green d'hide body, chaps, and vambs with a rune crossbow and broad bolts (after 55 Slayer) is the cheapest effective ranged setup.
2. Level 70 — Black d'hide + Karil's: The first serious bossing ranged gear. Black d'hide is cheap and provides excellent defence. Karil's crossbow from Barrows adds bolt racks for higher DPS.
3. Level 75+ — Armadyl / Dragon Hunter crossbow: Endgame ranged weapons. The Armadyl crossbow is the go-to for general bossing, while the Dragon Hunter crossbow dominates Vorkath and dragon-type bosses.

⚡ Level 43 Prayer Is the Most Important Milestone in OSRS

Protect from Melee at 43 Prayer reduces melee damage from NPCs to zero in most PvM encounters. Without it, you can't do Barrows, can't survive most Slayer tasks, and can't attempt any boss. With it, the entire game opens up. Get 43 Prayer before you attempt anything beyond basic combat. It costs ~850K GP using dragon bones at a Gilded Altar and takes ~1 hour. ⑩ ❓ FAQs OSRS Leveling MilestonesFAQ

Q: What's the most important milestone level in OSRS?

A: 43 Prayer. Protect from Melee unlocks at 43 and reduces NPC melee damage to zero in most PvM encounters. Without it, you can't do Barrows, can't survive Slayer tasks, and can't attempt any boss. It costs ~850K GP and takes 1 hour at a Gilded Altar. This is the single most impactful level in the entire game.

Q: What level do I need to start bossing?

A: For the easiest bosses (Giant Mole, Barrows): 43 Prayer + 50+ combat stats. For mid-level bosses (Zulrah, GWD): 70+ combat, Piety/Rigour, 70 Defence for Barrows gear. For endgame bosses (CoX, ToB, ToA): 90+ combat stats across all styles. See our boss progression roadmap for the exact path.

Q: Which milestones should I rush first as a new member?

A: In order: 1. Quest skips (Waterfall, Tree Gnome Village — 30 minutes, saves 5+ hours), 2. 43 Prayer (1 hour, opens all PvM), 3. 50+ combat (a few hours at Sand Crabs), 4. 55 Magic (High Alchemy for money + XP), 5. 70 combat (whip + Barrows gear). These five milestones, achieved in your first 2–3 days, give you the foundation for everything else.

Q: What level does Agility need to be for GWD shortcuts?

A: The Saradomin GWD shortcut requires 70 Agility and an Agility potion won't work — you need the actual level. The Zamorak GWD shortcut requires 35 Agility. The Bandos door requires 60 Strength, not Agility. The Armadyl area has no Agility shortcut but requires a crossbow grapple (70 Ranged + 43 Agility for the grapple). 70 Agility is the key target for comfortable GWD access.

Q: When does F2P leveling become inefficient?

A: For most combat and gathering skills, F2P training becomes significantly slower than members alternatives after level 40. F2P lacks Sand Crabs (best AFK combat), Catherby (best fishing), Blast Furnace (best smithing), and dozens of other methods. After level 40 in any skill, members methods give 2–3× faster XP. Read our F2P leveling guide for the full comparison.

Q: What level unlocks Barrows gloves?

A: Barrows gloves come from completing all sub-quests in Recipe for Disaster. The final sub-quest (Culinaromancer's Chest) requires 175 Quest Points and has no individual skill requirement above 70, but earlier sub-quests require various levels up to 65 Cooking, 55 Thieving, etc. The full RFD quest chain is a milestone itself — Barrows gloves are the best gloves in OSRS until endgame content. See our best quests guide.

Q: How do I know which milestone to work toward next?

A: Use this priority framework: 1. Quest skips (always do before manual training), 2. Prayer 43 (non-negotiable), 3. Combat 70 (whip + Barrows), 4. Quest unlocks (Desert Treasure, RFD), 5. Skill-specific gear unlocks (40 Ranged for green d'hide, 70 Agility for GWD shortcut). Follow this order and you'll always be working toward something that changes how you play. For the fastest path to each milestone, see our fastest leveling guide.
