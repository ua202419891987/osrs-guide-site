Achieving 2277 total level and unlocking the Max Cape is the ultimate account goal in Old School RuneScape. 1. The Max Cape — Why Go For 2277?（1. Max披风 — 为什么要追求2277？（Max披风 — 为什么要追求2277？））

The Max Cape is more than just a piece of equipment. It's a multi-year commitment to a single goal, a representation of countless hours of grinding, and one of the most prestigious achievements in Old School RuneScape. But before you commit to the journey, you need an honest assessment of what Max gives you — and what it costs.

🏆 What the Max Cape Actually Gives You（🏆 Max披风实际给你什么（Max披风实际给你什么））

Max Cape Stats & Benefits（Max披风属性与好处）

  • Stats: +5 Strength, +5 Magic, +5 Ranged, +9 Defence, +8 Prayer, +8 Hitpoints — equivalent to a full Bandos + Barrows setup in some categories
  • Worn as Skill Cape: Functions as the Skill Cape of your choice (so you can turn it into a Fire Cape equivalent for firemaking tasks, etc.)
  • Customizable Trim: 3 styles (Max Cape, Max Cape (Old), Max Cape (Roc))
  • Achievement: All skills at 99 (2277 total level) — visible on your character, no ambiguity about your commitment
  • Clan Rank: Most OSRS clans have a "Maxed" rank tier with respect and access to advanced content
  • Quest Cape Synergy: Pairs with Quest Cape for the highest-tier hybrid cape in the game

💰 Total Cost — An Honest Estimate（💰 总成本 — 诚实估算（总成本 — 诚实估算））

Two players can both achieve Max Cape, but their journeys look completely different. Here are realistic time and GP estimates based on playstyle:

Player TypeTotal TimeTotal GP CostReal-World Duration
Efficient (4–6h/day, uses all methods)~1,200 hours~400M GP~9–10 months
Moderate (2–3h/day, balanced methods)~1,500 hours~500M GP~1.5–2 years
Casual (1h/day, AFK methods)~2,500 hours~300M GP~6+ years
Ironman (self-sufficient)~1,800 hours0 GP (but much harder)~3–4 years

The "minimum theoretical" time is around 1,000 hours of pure skilling at max efficiency (called EHP — Efficient Hours Played). No real player achieves this; even top 0.1% Max Capes take 1,200+ hours. The "realistic" goal for motivated players is 1,500 hours.

🎯 Who Should Pursue Max Cape?（🎯 谁应该追求Max披风？（谁应该追求Max披风？））

Max Cape is Right For You If:（Max披风适合你如果：）

  • You genuinely enjoy the core OSRS loop (grinding, planning, optimizing)
  • You want the prestige and recognition within the OSRS community
  • You need a long-term goal to keep the game fresh
  • You don't have a strong interest in PvP, leagues, or other "side content"
  • You have 1+ hours per day consistently available to play

Max Cape is NOT Right For You If:（Max披风不适合你如果：）

  • You primarily play for PvM, PvP, or questing
  • You get bored after 30 minutes of repetitive tasks
  • You have a low GP bank and limited time to earn more
  • You find OSRS more fun in 3-month "seasons" than in multi-year marathons
  • You have real-life commitments (school finals, job transitions) that could interrupt
💡 The "Step Away" Test

Before committing to Max, take a 1-month break from OSRS. When you come back, ask yourself: "Do I genuinely want to grind 1,500 more hours, or am I just feeling the sunk-cost fallacy?" If the answer is yes, you're ready. If you immediately want to do something else, Max isn't your goal right now — and that's fine. There are 100 other things to do in OSRS.

2. Full Skills Roadmap to 2277（2. 完整技能路线图到2277（完整技能路线图到2277））

Below is the complete 5-phase roadmap that takes you from a mid-game account (1,000+ total level) to Max (2277). Each phase is designed to be self-contained — you can pause between phases and your progress won't be wasted.

⚔️ Phase 1: Combat First (1,000 → ~1,400 Total)（⚔️ 第一阶段：战斗优先（第一阶段：战斗优先，1,000到约1,400总等级））

Combat skills are the foundation. They're the most "passively" trained because you need to fight monsters anyway for Slayer, questing, and bossing. By the time you finish Phase 1, you'll have 6 combat 99s and a Slayer master relationship that will drive the rest of your account.

Step 1.1: Strength to 99 (Do This First)（步骤1.1：力量到99级（步骤1.1：力量到99级，先做这个））

Why first: Strength 99 unlocks the Granite Maul (useful for high-level Slayer) and gives you the highest damage output of any combat skill. It's also the fastest combat 99 because Strength has direct multipliers on max hit.

Method: Slayer with optimal weapons (Abyssal Tentacle → Dragon Scimitar). Combined XP: ~50,000 Strength XP/hour.

Time: ~150 hours of Slayer (alongside other combat skills).

Step 1.2: Attack to 99（步骤1.2：攻击到99级）

Why second: Attack 99 unlocks the Dragon Pickaxe special (best Mining boost) and Infernal Pickaxe (smelt bars without Smithing level). Also gives the most damage accuracy in PvM.

Method: Same as Strength — passive through Slayer.

Step 1.3: Defence to 99（步骤1.3：防御到99级）

Why third: Defence 99 unlocks Bandos armour (best mid-game melee protection) and the Defence Cape. But Defence XP is shared with Attack/Strength, so it will reach 99 naturally through Slayer.

Note: Don't isolate Defence training. Always train through Slayer.

Step 1.4: Ranged to 99（步骤1.4：远程到99级）

Why fourth: Ranged is the best PvM damage style (most bosses have Ranged weakness). 99 Ranged unlocks the Twinflame Staff synergies and is required for the Twisted Bow (best ranged weapon).

Method: Red Chinchompas (Hunter 73) at Slayer tasks or Wilderness spots. ~300,000 XP/hour. Cost: ~45M GP (or free if you catch your own chins).

Step 1.5: Magic to 99（步骤1.5：魔法到99级）

Why fifth: 99 Magic unlocks Ancient Magicks (essential for bossing — Barrage, Burst) and Vengeance (for PvP). Also gives access to the Volatile Nightmare Staff and high-level utility spells.

Method: High Alchemy (boring but consistent) or Barraging Slayer (more engaging). Cost: ~28M GP.

Step 1.6: Hitpoints to 99（步骤1.6：生命值到99级）

Why last: Hitpoints 99 unlocks the Hitpoints Cape (teleport to any house) and gives you the HP pool to fight every boss. It levels passively with any combat training, so you won't notice grinding it.

Note: You'll likely hit 99 Hitpoints naturally during Phase 1 without targeted training.

⚔️ Slayer: The Universal Combat Trainer

By completing all 6 combat 99s, you'll also reach 90+ Slayer (likely 99 with good task selection). Slayer is the bridge between Phase 1 and Phase 2 — once you have high Slayer, you unlock Vorkath (best GP source), Hydra (great loot), and the ability to farm high-level herbs/seeds for Herblore/Farming.

⚡ Phase 2: Fast Skiller 99s (1,400 → ~1,700 Total)（⚡ 第二阶段：快速技能99级（第二阶段：快速技能99级，1,400到约1,700总等级））

These skills are fast. Each takes 8–50 hours of active play. They also generate GP, which funds Phase 4. Get these out of the way quickly to build momentum and bank balance.

2.1: Cooking to 99 (8 hours)（2.1：烹饪到99级（2.1：烹饪到99级，8小时））

Method: Anglerfish at Hosidius Kitchen (with Cooking gauntlets). Cost: ~72M GP. Time: 8 hours. GP/h cost: 9M GP/hour.

Alternative: Self-caught Anglerfish from your own Fishing training reduces cost to ~20M GP.

2.2: Fletching to 99 (27 hours)（2.2：制箭到99级（2.2：制箭到99级，27小时））

Method: Dragon darts with 1-tick clicking. Cost: ~105M GP. Time: 27 hours. Output: Sells for 200M GP (profitable 95M GP over 99).

Note: This is the only major "buyable" that's also profitable. The 1-tick clicking makes it engaging.

2.3: Firemaking to 99 (90 hours)（2.3：生火到99级（2.3：生火到99级，90小时））

Method: Wintertodt. Cost: Free. Time: ~90 hours. Bonus: 90+ Construction, ~50 Herblore, ~30 Farming XP per game. Also gives reward crates (GP, seeds, herbs).

Why now: Wintertodt is semi-AFK (you can watch videos). Do it during downtime. Free skill training is the best deal in the game.

2.4: Crafting to 99 (52 hours)（2.4：手工艺到99级（2.4：手工艺到99级，52小时））

Method: Red dragonhide bodies. Cost: ~55M GP. Time: 52 hours. Output: Sells for 90M GP (profitable 35M GP over 99).

Quest Boost: Monkey Madness II + Recipe for Disaster + The Fremennik Isles = ~35,000 free XP. Enough to skip levels 1–35.

2.5: Thieving to 99 (54 hours)（2.5：盗窃到99级（2.5：盗窃到99级，54小时））

Method: Ardougne Knights (level 95+). Cost: Free. Time: 54 hours. Profit: ~300K GP/hour = 16M GP over 99.

Pre-95 Method: Pyramid Plunder (level 70–95). ~150,000 XP/hour. Fun minigame format with consistent loot.

🌲 Phase 3: Slow Gathering 99s (1,700 → ~1,950 Total)（🌲 第三阶段：慢速采集99级（第三阶段：慢速采集99级，1,700到约1,950总等级））

These skills are slow. Each takes 130–200 hours. But they're mostly free or profitable, and they don't burn too much mental energy. Most players do these alongside other skills (the "rotation method").

3.1: Agility to 99 (160 hours)（3.1：敏捷到99级（3.1：敏捷到99级，160小时））

Method: Prifddinas Rooftop Course (75+, after Plague's End). Cost: Free. Time: 160 hours. Output: Graceful set pieces from Marks of Grace (eventually full Graceful).

Why now: Graceful set is one of the most useful QoL items in OSRS. 70% run energy restore makes every other skilling method faster.

Pro tip: Do Agility while watching videos or listening to podcasts. It's the most "background-friendly" skill.

3.2: Mining to 99 (181 hours)（3.2：采矿到99级（3.2：采矿到99级，181小时））

Method: Amethyst (level 82+). Cost: Free (profitable 15M GP from amethyst sales). Time: 181 hours.

Alternative: Volcanic Mine (70+). ~70,000 XP/hour. More engaging than Amethyst but requires supply runs.

3.3: Woodcutting to 99 (136 hours)（3.3：伐木到99级（3.3：伐木到99级，136小时））

Method: Redwood trees (90+). Cost: Free (profitable 40M GP from log sales). Time: 136 hours.

AFK Alt: Yew trees (60+). ~35,000 XP/hour. Very AFK. ~200K GP/hour profit.

3.4: Fishing to 99 (160 hours)（3.4：钓鱼到99级（3.4：钓鱼到99级，160小时））

Method: Minnows (82+) or Anglerfish (82+). Cost: Free (profitable 50M GP from fish sales). Time: 160 hours.

Synergy: Combine with Cooking (use your own Anglerfish for Cooking 99 to save 50M GP).

3.5: Hunter to 99 (72 hours)（3.5：猎人到99级（3.5：猎人到99级，72小时））

Method: Maniacal Monkeys (77+, after MM2). Cost: Free (profitable 100M GP from chins if sold). Time: 72 hours.

Synergy: Maniacal Monkey bones at Gilded Altar give Construction XP. Save chins for Ranged training.

💰 Phase 4: Expensive 99s (1,950 → ~2,200 Total)（💰 第四阶段：昂贵99级（第四阶段：昂贵99级，1,950到约2,200总等级））

By now, you should have 200–500M GP banked from your profitable skills (Hunter, Fishing, RC, Slayer/bossing). It's time to spend it. These 4 skills cost the most GP but are also the most impactful for account progression.

4.1: Prayer to 99 (54 hours, 28M GP)（4.1：祈祷到99级（4.1：祈祷到99级，54小时，2800万金币））

Method: Wyrm bones at Gilded Altar (350% XP). Cost: 28M GP. Time: 54 hours.

Why now: 99 Prayer unlocks Piety, Rigour, Augury — the three prayers that define high-level PvM. Without these, you'll struggle at every boss.

Cost-Saving: Use the Maniacal Monkey bones from Phase 3.5 Hunter training. This saves 10–15M GP.

4.2: Runecrafting to 99 (64 hours, FREE / +40M GP profit)（4.2：符文制作到99级（4.2：符文制作到99级，64小时，免费/赚4000万金币））

Method: Soul Runes (90+). Cost: Free. Profit: 40M GP. Time: 64 hours.

Why now: RC 90 should actually come before this phase (it's a "Tier 1" 99 that funds everything). But if you haven't done it yet, do it first in this phase — the GP it generates funds the other 3 expensive 99s.

4.3: Construction to 99 (54 hours, 120M GP)（4.3：建筑到99级（4.3：建筑到99级，54小时，1.2亿金币））

Method: Mahogany Homes. Cost: 120M GP. Time: 54 hours.

Why now: 99 Construction unlocks the Construction Cape (POH teleport — one of the most useful teleports in the game) and gives you access to the Max Cape.

Pro tip: Construction is the most hated skill. Most players do it in a single weekend binge. Buy all your planks in advance and just power through it.

4.4: Herblore to 99 (36 hours, 180M GP)（4.4：草药学到99级（4.4：草药学到99级，36小时，1.8亿金币））

Method: Super Combat potions (90+). Cost: 180M GP. Time: 36 hours.

Cost-Saving: Farm your own herbs via Farming (Ranarr, Snapdragon, Torstol). Reduces cost to ~80M GP.

Why now: 99 Herblore unlocks Overloads (PVM potions for Inferno, ToB) and Brews (PVM sustainability).

🌾 Phase 5: Clean-Up Phase (2,200 → 2,277)（🌾 第五阶段：清理阶段（第五阶段：清理阶段，2,200到2,277总等级））

You're in the home stretch. These 3 remaining skills are the most "thankless" — they're slow, often boring, and don't give major unlocks. But they're required for Max.

5.1: Farming to 99 (90 hours active, 15M GP)（5.1：农耕到99级（5.1：农耕到99级，90小时活跃，1500万金币））

Method: Tree runs + Herb runs. Cost: 15M GP. Time: 90 hours active (spread over 3+ months).

Why last: Farming is the most "passive" skill. Do 2 runs per day (10 min each) between other activities. Over 3 months, you'll hit 99 without ever "grinding" it.

5.2: Slayer to 99 (Last 0.5–2% of progress)（5.2：杀戮到99级（5.2：杀戮到99级，最后0.5-2%进度））

By now, you likely have 90+ Slayer from Phases 1–4. The last 9 levels of Slayer are obtained passively through combat training. No special method needed — just keep doing Slayer tasks.

5.3: Smithing + Fletching to 99 (IF going for Max)（5.3：锻造+制箭到99级（5.3：锻造+制箭到99级，如果追求Max））

These are the most expensive 99s in the game (Smithing: 230M, Fletching: 105M). Most players either:

  • Buy through: Spend 335M GP, get 99 in 60 hours total. Fastest path.
  • Smith Cannonballs: 500+ hours of profitable but boring clicking. Free 99.
  • Skip Max: Many "non-pure" accounts stop at 2275 total (no Smithing, no Fletching, or only one of them). The "Almost Max" community is large and active.

Recommendation: If you have 300M+ GP banked from profitable skills, just buy the way to 99 in both. 60 hours of clicking beats 500 hours of Cannonballs.

🏆 Total Phase Summary

From mid-game (1,000 total) to Max (2,277 total): ~1,500 hours of active play over ~1.5–2 years. Total GP cost: ~400–500M GP (funded by profitable skilling and bossing). The order above minimizes GP requirements, maximizes skill synergies, and keeps motivation high by completing "fast wins" first.

3. Skill Synergies — Training Multiple Skills to 99 Together（3. 技能协同 — 同时将多项技能训练到99级（技能协同 — 同时将多项技能训练到99级））

The most efficient way to reach Max isn't grinding one skill to 99 at a time. It's training multiple skills in parallel using synergies — methods that give XP in two or more skills simultaneously. The table below maps out the best synergy pairs and triples.

🔗 Tier 1: The Core Synergies (Use These)（🔗 第一梯队：核心协同（第一梯队：核心协同，使用这些））

Synergy 1: Slayer + Combat (The Classic Combo)（协同1：杀戮+战斗（协同1：杀戮+战斗，经典组合））

What it does: Slayer tasks grant XP in Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Ranged, and Magic all at once. A single Slayer task progresses 6 skills.

XP efficiency: ~50,000 combined combat XP/hour + 50,000 Slayer XP/hour. The single best use of time in OSRS for combat 99s.

GP efficiency: High-level Slayer tasks (Gargoyles, Vorkath, Nechryaels) generate 1–3M GP/hour, funding your expensive 99s.

Synergy 2: Woodcutting + Firemaking (Resource Loop)（协同2：伐木+生火（协同2：伐木+生火，资源循环））

What it does: Cut logs (Woodcutting XP) → burn them (Firemaking XP). Both skills progress with no extra effort.

XP efficiency: ~35,000 WC XP/hour + ~35,000 FM XP/hour. Combined: 70,000 XP/hour effective.

Best method: Yew trees (60+) — AFK, profitable (~200K GP/hour), progresses both skills.

Wintertodt alternative: Earns both WC and FM XP, plus 50+ Construction, ~30 Herblore, and ~30 Farming XP per game. Best FM method in the game.

Synergy 3: Fishing + Cooking (Feed Your Own Training)（协同3：钓鱼+烹饪（协同3：钓鱼+烹饪，自给自足训练））

What it does: Catch fish (Fishing XP) → cook them (Cooking XP). Use the cooked fish for HP during Slayer training (no food cost = pure profit).

XP efficiency: ~68,000 Fish XP/hour (Minnows) + ~200,000 Cook XP/hour (Anglerfish). Best paired method in OSRS.

GP efficiency: Anglerfish sell for 2,500 GP each. Cooking them turns raw fish (250 GP) into 2,500 GP food. Massive GP boost per hour.

Synergy 4: Mining + Smithing (GF + BF + Armor Making)（协同4：采矿+锻造（协同4：采矿+锻造，GF+BF+装备制作））

What it does: Mine ores (Mining XP) → smelt bars (Smithing XP) → smith at Blast Furnace (Smithing XP). Two skills from one resource.

XP efficiency: ~60,000 Mine XP/hour (Amethyst) + ~150,000 Smith XP/hour (smelting at BF). Combined: 210,000 XP/hour effective.

Best for Ironman: The "GF + BF" (Gold Furnace + Blast Furnace) method trains both skills efficiently. For mains, just buy bars and skip Mining.

Synergy 5: Agility + Construction (Rooftop GP → House Upgrades)（协同5：敏捷+建筑（协同5：敏捷+建筑，屋顶金币→房屋升级））

What it does: Agility gives Marks of Grace (sell for ~700 GP each on GE) → use GP to fund Construction training.

XP efficiency: ~68,000 Agi XP/hour (Prifddinas) + ~200,000 Con XP/hour (Mahogany Homes).

GP efficiency: Marks of Grace fund 50% of Mahogany Homes material cost. Partial self-funding.

🔗 Tier 2: Secondary Synergies (Nice to Have)（🔗 第二梯队：次要协同（第二梯队：次要协同，锦上添花））

Synergy 6: Hunter + Construction (Maniacal Monkeys → Gilded Bones)（协同6：猎人+建筑（协同6：猎人+建筑，疯狂猴子→镀金骨））

What it does: Maniacal Monkey hunting (77+, after MM2) drops Monkey bones and gives 150,000 Hunter XP/hour. Bury the bones at a Gilded Altar for 350% XP → 70,000 Construction XP/hour bonus.

Total XP: 150,000 Hunter + 70,000 Construction = 220,000 XP/hour effective. Best paired method in the game.

Synergy 7: Slayer + Farming (Herb Drops + Herb Runs)（协同7：杀戮+农耕（协同7：杀戮+农耕，草药掉落+草药种植））

What it does: Slayer tasks drop herb seeds (Ranarr, Snapdragon, Torstol). Plant them in Farming patches → harvest → use in Herblore.

GP efficiency: Each Slayer task gives 5–50 herb seeds. Over 99 Slayer, you'll save 50–100M GP on Herblore.

Synergy 8: Wintertodt + ALL (The King of Synergies)（协同8：Wintertodt+所有（协同8：Wintertodt+所有，协同之王））

What it does: Wintertodt (Firemaking minigame) gives XP in Firemaking, Woodcutting, Construction, Herblore, Farming, and Slayer. 6 skills in one activity.

XP per game: 4,000+ Firemaking + ~500 Woodcutting + ~90 Construction + ~50 Herblore + ~30 Farming + ~1,500 Slayer.

Pro tip: Many Max Capers do 5+ Wintertodt games per day throughout their journey. By the time you finish other 99s, FM is naturally done.

💡 The "Synergy-First" Mentality

When planning your 99 order, prioritize skills that synergize with each other. For example: train Fishing+Cooking together (saves 100h), then use the cooked Anglerfish as Herblore ingredients (saves 80M GP). The total time saved by using all synergies correctly is 200–400 hours — equivalent to 2–4 months of grinding.

📊 Sources of Free / Self-Funded GP（📊 免费/自筹金币来源（免费/自筹金币来源））

Many "expensive" 99s can be partially or fully funded by your other training. Here's how:

Free GP Source 1: Profitable Skills (200M+ GP)（免费金币来源1：盈利技能（免费金币来源1：盈利技能，2亿+金币））

  • Runecrafting (Soul): +40M GP (selling runes)
  • Hunter (Red Chins): +100M GP (selling chins or using for Ranged)
  • Fishing (Anglerfish): +50M GP (selling cooked fish)
  • Woodcutting (Redwood): +40M GP (selling logs)
  • Mining (Amethyst/Runite): +15M GP (selling gems/ores)
  • Thieving (Ardougne Knights): +16M GP
  • Slayer (high-level tasks): +200–500M GP (over 99 Slayer)

Total free GP from skills: ~500M. This funds the majority of your buyable 99s.

Free GP Source 2: Quest Rewards (~50M GP total)（免费金币来源2：任务奖励（免费金币来源2：任务奖励，总计约5000万金币））

Many quests give lamps and lamp-like rewards. Over 200+ quests, you can earn ~5M XP in lamps (saves ~30M GP). Notable: Recipe for Disaster (sub-quests give Combat XP), Monkey Madness II (50K XP in multiple skills), Song of the Elves (40K XP in multiple skills).

💰 Best Money-Making Methods for Funding Max（💰 为Max筹集资金的最佳赚钱方法（为Max筹集资金的最佳赚钱方法））

Method 1: Vorkath (Best GP/Hour, 3–5M)（方法1：Vorkath（方法1：Vorkath，最佳金币/小时，300-500万））

Requirements: 80+ all combat, completion of Dragon Slayer II. GP/Hour: 3–5M. Risk: Medium (can be solo'd safely). Fun factor: 7/10.

Once unlocked, Vorkath is the most consistent GP source in the game. 200 Vorkath kills = 60–100M GP. Do this during your Slayer phase.

Method 2: Zulrah (2–4M GP/Hour)（方法2：Zulrah（方法2：Zulrah，200-400万金币/小时））

Requirements: 75+ Ranged/Magic, completion of Regicide. GP/Hour: 2–4M. Risk: Medium (learning curve is steep). Fun factor: 6/10 (rotation-intensive).

Unique drops (Tanzanite fang, Magic fang) can hit 10M+ per drop, making this the highest-variance GP source.

Method 3: Raids (Chambers of Xeric, 2–6M GP/Hour)（方法3：团队副本（方法3：团队副本，Xeric密室，200-600万金币/小时））

Requirements: 80+ all combat, completion of Dragon Slayer II. GP/Hour: 2–6M. Risk: High (mechanics-intensive). Fun factor: 9/10 (best group content in OSRS).

Solo or team. High variance — some runs are 1M, others are 10M+. Best long-term GP source after you've mastered it.

Method 4: Slayer Tasks (1–3M GP/Hour)（方法4：杀戮任务（方法4：杀戮任务，100-300万金币/小时））

Requirements: 80+ Slayer. GP/Hour: 1–3M depending on task. Risk: Low. Fun factor: 5/10 (monotonous but reliable).

The "passive" money-making method. You're training Slayer for combat 99s anyway, so the GP comes for free. Over 99 Slayer, you'll naturally accumulate 300M+ GP.

📅 When to Start Bank-Rolling for Expensive 99s（📅 何时开始为昂贵99级囤积资金（何时开始为昂贵99级囤积资金））

You should start saving for the "Big 4" expensive 99s (Smithing, Herblore, Construction, Fletching) as early as possible. Recommended schedule:

  1. Total level 1,200+ (early Phase 2): Start Vorkath or Zulrah. Goal: bank 50M GP.
  2. Total level 1,500+ (late Phase 2): Switch to 1 Vorkath session + 1 Slayer task per day. Goal: 150M GP banked.
  3. Total level 1,800+ (mid Phase 3): Bank should be 300M+. Start buying 99 Cooking (saves time for the hardest skills).
  4. Total level 2,000+ (Phase 4): Bank should be 500M+. Start the "Big 4" expensive 99s (Prayer → Construction → Herblore → Smithing → Fletching).
⚠️ The "GP Burnout" Trap

Many players burn out not from grinding, but from the pressure of having to earn 500M GP. If bossing feels like a job, switch to GE flipping (buying low, selling high). It's less GP/hour but more mentally engaging. The "best" money-making method is the one you don't hate.

5. Time Management & Realistic Scheduling（5. 时间管理与现实规划（时间管理与现实规划））

The biggest predictor of Max Cape success isn't skill, GP, or efficiency — it's consistency. This section covers realistic scheduling for different player types and the concept of EHP (Efficient Hours Played).

⏱️ EHP Explained: What is "Efficient Hours Played"?（⏱️ EHP解释：什么是"高效游戏时间"？（EHP解释：什么是"高效游戏时间"？））

EHP is a metric from the OSRS efficiency community that measures the theoretical maximum XP/hour across all skills combined. The current top 0.1% of players achieve ~80 EHP per real hour. To reach 2,277 total level, the minimum EHP is ~1,000.

However, EHP is a theoretical maximum. No real player achieves it. Realistic numbers:

  • Top 1% of players: ~50 EHP/hour (1,200 hours to Max)
  • Top 10% of players: ~30 EHP/hour (1,700 hours to Max)
  • Average motivated player: ~15–20 EHP/hour (2,500 hours to Max)

📅 Realistic Time Tables by Playtime（📅 按游戏时间的现实时间表（按游戏时间的现实时间表））

Schedule A: 1 Hour Per Day (Casual Player)（计划A：每天1小时（计划A：每天1小时，休闲玩家））

Daily playtime: 60 minutes. Time to Max: 4–6 years. Total hours: ~2,000.

Pros: Sustainable for years. Doesn't interfere with work/school. Low burnout risk.

Cons: Long timeline. May lose interest before completion.

Best methods: AFK-friendly (Agility, Thieving, Nightmares, Wintertodt). Maximize passive XP per session.

Schedule B: 2–3 Hours Per Day (Moderate Player)（计划B：每天2-3小时（计划B：每天2-3小时，中等玩家））

Daily playtime: 120–180 minutes. Time to Max: 2–2.5 years. Total hours: ~1,500.

Pros: Reasonable timeline. Can do efficient methods (Slayer, Hunter, etc.).

Cons: Requires consistent daily commitment. Moderate burnout risk.

Best methods: 1 hour active (Slayer, Bossing) + 1 hour passive (Agility, Thieving).

Schedule C: 4–6 Hours Per Day (Dedicated Player)（计划C：每天4-6小时（计划C：每天4-6小时，专注玩家））

Daily playtime: 240–360 minutes. Time to Max: 1–1.5 years. Total hours: ~1,200.

Pros: Fast timeline. Can do all efficient methods. High motivation.

Cons: Significant time commitment. High burnout risk if not careful.

Best methods: 4–5 hours of varied activity (Slayer, Hunter, Agility, Bossing, Farming). Rotate to prevent burnout.

Schedule D: 8+ Hours Per Day (No-Life Player)（计划D：每天8小时以上（计划D：每天8小时以上，全职玩家））

Daily playtime: 480+ minutes. Time to Max: 6–9 months. Total hours: ~1,000.

Pros: Fastest realistic timeline.

Cons: Unsustainable long-term. High burnout. Negative impact on real life.

Recommendation: Use this schedule for short bursts (1–2 weeks) before taking a planned break. Don't sustain 8+ hours daily for months.

📆 The "Pomodoro" Method for OSRS（📆 OSRS的"番茄工作法"（OSRS的"番茄工作法"））

For 2–3 hour sessions, use a 45-15 rotation:

  1. 45 minutes: Active skilling (Slayer, Hunter, Bossing)
  2. 15 minutes: AFK skilling (Agility, Thieving, Wintertodt, Farming runs)

This prevents mental fatigue from 2+ hours of the same activity. The 15-minute AFK blocks also let you check phone, grab water, stretch, etc.

7. After Max — What's Next?（7. Max之后 — 接下来做什么？（Max之后 — 接下来做什么？））

You did it. You're standing in Lumbridge with a Max Cape on your back. Now what? Many Max Capers experience a "post-Max depression" — the goal that drove you for 1.5+ years is suddenly gone. Here's how to fill that void with engaging endgame content.

🏆 Tier 1: "Easy" Post-Max Goals（🏆 第一梯队：Max后"简单"目标（第一梯队：Max后"简单"目标））

Quest Cape（任务披风）

Time: 50–200 hours (depending on your quest progress). Reward: Quest Cape (combines with Max Cape for the hybrid Cape of Accomplishment — the most prestigious cape in OSRS).

Many players achieve this alongside Max (doing quests as they go). If you haven't, this should be your #1 priority after Max.

Achievement Cape（成就披风）

Time: 200+ hours of challenging tasks. Reward: Achievement Cape (different from Max — requires combat achievements, not just 99 skills).

Includes killing every boss, completing all quests, hitting all achievement diary tiers, and more. The "true" measure of an OSRS veteran's accomplishment.

Achievement Diary Cape（成就日记披风）

Time: 50–100 hours. Reward: All diaries completed. Cape: The Achievement Diary Cape is a recolored Achievement Cape for those who complete all diaries (less prestigious but still notable).

Collection Log Completion（收藏日志完成）

Time: 1,000+ hours. Reward: A complete log of every unique drop from every boss, every minigame, every clue scroll tier.

Most players get 50–70% completion naturally. Reaching 100% is a multi-year grind that many players never complete. Notable collections:

  • Chambers of Xeric Log: ~150 unique drops (armor, weapons, supplies)
  • Theatre of Blood Log: ~80 unique drops (Scythe, Avernic, Justiciar)

Have a "main" (Max, Inferno, etc.), a "side" (Pet hunting, Achievement diary), and a "seasonal" (Leagues, DMM). Rotate between them based on mood. This way, OSRS never feels like a chore.

8. FAQ & Motivation（8. 常见问题与动力（常见问题与动力））

How long does Max Cape really take?

For an efficient player playing 4–6 hours/day: ~9–12 months. For a moderate player playing 2–3 hours/day: ~1.5–2 years. For a casual player playing 1 hour/day: ~4–6 years. The variance is enormous. The fastest legitimate Max Cape (no exploits) was achieved in ~3 months; the slowest intentional achievement is over 10 years.

What's the single biggest mistake new Max-hopefuls make?

Burning out on the first 99. Many players try to grind 99 Slayer or 99 Strength as their first 99 and quit after 2 weeks. Better approach: get 6+ "fast wins" (Cooking 8h, Fletching 27h, Thieving 54h, Firemaking 90h) before tackling long grinds. The sense of accomplishment from fast 99s builds the confidence to handle 100+ hour skills.

Can I get Max Cape on an F2P account?

No. Members-only skills (Slayer, Hunter, Construction, Herblore, Farming) are required for 99 in all 23 skills. F2P accounts can only achieve 99 in ~10 skills. Members is required for Max.

Is Ironman Max Cape harder?

Significantly. Ironman accounts take 2–4x longer (3,000–5,000 hours) because you can't buy materials, can't trade, and must self-source everything. The Ironman community is large and active, and Ironman Max is considered more prestigious than regular Max because of the additional difficulty.

What if I lose motivation halfway through?

Normal. Most players have at least one "burnout period" of 2–8 weeks during their Max journey. Strategies to recover:

  1. Take a planned break (1–2 weeks). Don't quit — just pause. Come back refreshed.
  2. Switch to a different activity. Do PvM, questing, or minigames for a while. Skills will be there when you return.
  3. Set a small goal. Instead of "Max Cape," set "99 Cooking in 2 weeks" or "100M GP banked by month-end."
  4. Watch OSRS content. Videos of others achieving Max can re-ignite your drive.
  5. Talk to other Max-hopefuls. Join a clan or Discord. You're not alone.

What's the most common reason players quit before Max?

By far, the most common reason is burnout from grinding expensive 99s (Smithing, Herblore, Construction). The time investment is large, the methods are boring, and the GP cost is high. To avoid this:

  • Save GP aggressively before starting expensive 99s
  • Buy your way through them quickly (don't try to "make it profitable" with Cannonballs unless you genuinely enjoy the method)
  • Reward yourself after each expensive 99 (a new piece of gear, a small PvM session, etc.)

What advice would you give to someone starting their Max journey today?

Three pieces of advice:

  1. Don't optimize too early. Play the game, learn the skills, find methods you enjoy. Optimization comes later. Burnout from trying to be "EHP-efficient" on day 1 is real.
  2. Track your progress. Use WiseOldMan and a simple Google Sheet. The visual progress is incredibly motivating.
  3. Have fun. If OSRS ever stops being fun, take a break. Max Cape isn't going anywhere. The game will be there when you're ready to come back.
