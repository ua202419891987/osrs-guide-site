① F2P Starter Gear（免费初始装备）

As a free player, your goal is to reach Rune equipment as fast as possible. Train Attack/Strength/Defence to 40 and equip full Rune armor. Best F2P weapons: Rune Scimitar (fast training), Rune 2h Sword (big hits, slower), Maple Shortbow for Ranged. Complete Dragon Slayer I for Rune Platebody access. Your F2P goal: Full Rune + Rune Scimitar + Amulet of Power + Climbing Boots + Holy Symbol.

② Early Member Upgrades（会员初期升级）

First week of membership priority upgrades (under 500K GP each): Dragon Scimitar (after Monkey Madness), Dragon Defender (Warriors Guild), Berserker Helm (Fremennik Isles), Climbing Boots (cheap, good stats), Amulet of Glory (dragonstone). These items cost under 100K each and dramatically outperform F2P gear. Don't waste GP on: Granite equipment, Rune 2h (overpriced for stats), or Obsidian weapons before 60+ Attack.
