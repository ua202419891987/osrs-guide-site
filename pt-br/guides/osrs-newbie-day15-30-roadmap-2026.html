📈 OSRS New Player Roadmap: Day 15 to Day 30

Continue your OSRS journey from day 15 to day 30. This roadmap covers Recipe for Disaster progression, establishing passive income, Slayer training, and preparing for mid-game content.

① Days 15-18: Recipe for Disaster ProgressRFD

Recipe for Disaster (RFD) is the most important quest in OSRS. Its final reward Barrows Gloves is the best-in-slot gloves until endgame. You need to complete 10 sub-quests, each requiring specific skills and other quests. Focus on completing all RFD prerequisites this week: Desert Treasure, Legends Quest, and Lunar Diplomacy. Aim for at least 175 Quest Points by day 18.

② Days 19-22: Passive Income Setup

Set up three passive income streams: Herb Runs (Farming 38+ for Ranarr, 45+ for Snapdragon 150K GP per run, 2-3 runs per day), Bird House Runs (Hunter 25+ for bird houses on Fossil Island 100K GP per run), and Kingdom Management (complete Throne of Miscellania and Royal Trouble quests 200K+ GP per day with max coffers). Combined, these generate 700K-1M GP daily with 15 minutes of effort.

③ Days 23-25: Slayer Training

Start Slayer with Vannaka (edgeville dungeon) or Chaeldar (lost city). Focus on efficient tasks: bloodveld, dagannoth, abberant spectres, and dust devils. Set up your block list early with: Spiritual creatures, Suqahs, and Steel dragons. Use Nieve/Steve for optimal task weighting from 85 combat. Aim for 55 Slayer by day 25 to unlock broad bolts for Ranged training.

④ Days 26-28: Key Unlocks

Target these game-changing unlocks: Fairy Rings (complete Fairy Tale II), Graceful Outfit (rooftop agility courses, 260 marks of grace), Dragon Defender (warriors guild, 130 combined Attack+Strength), Neitiznot Helm (Fremennik Isles quest), and Fire Cape (Jad fight cave optional but highly recommended). These unlocks dramatically improve your gameplay efficiency.

⑤ Days 29-30: Mid-Game Preparation

By day 30, your account should have: 70+ Combat stats, 60+ Slayer, 50+ Magic, complete RFD (or very close), Barrows Gloves, Dragon Scimitar, and a cash stack of 5-10M GP. Your next steps after day 30: complete Dragon Slayer II for Vorkath access, start Zulrah for 3-6M GP/hr, and push Slayer to 85 for the best money-making tasks.

FAQ

How long does RFD take?RFD?

If you follow an efficient quest order, RFD can be completed in 15-20 hours of playtime. Focus on prerequisites first.
