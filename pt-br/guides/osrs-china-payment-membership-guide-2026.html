② PayPal China Setup Guide（PayPal中国设置教程）

① Create a PayPal China Account（注册贝宝账户）

  1. Visit paypal.com (use VPN if the site is blocked)
  2. Select "Personal Account" and register with your Chinese phone number
  3. Verify your email and phone number
  4. Link your Chinese bank card (UnionPay, Visa, or MasterCard issued in China)

② Confirm Your Card（确认银行卡）

PayPal will make a small charge (~1-2 CNY) to verify your card. Check your bank statement for the 4-digit code and enter it on PayPal. Once verified, your PayPal is ready for international payments.

③ Make Your First Payment（首次支付）

Log into the OSRS website with VPN connected. Select "PayPal" as payment method. The payment will be processed in USD with automatic currency conversion by PayPal. The conversion rate is typically 1-3% above market rate.

④ Buying & Using Bonds（Bond购买与使用指南）

① Buy Bonds from Jagex（从Jagex购买Bond）

Bonds can be purchased from Jagex for $8.99 each (2 weeks of membership). Using PayPal China, this costs approximately ¥65 per bond. You can then sell the bond on the Grand Exchange for ~8.5M GP (price fluctuates).

② Buy Bonds from GE（从交易所购买Bond）

If you have GP, buy bonds directly from the Grand Exchange. Type "Old school bond" in the GE search and buy at market price (~8.5M GP as of June 2026). Then right-click the bond and select "Redeem" → "14 days of membership".

③ Bond Price History & Trends（Bond价格走势）

Bond prices have risen significantly since the March 2026 price hike (6.5M → 8.5M GP). Prices typically drop during DXP weekends and rise before new content releases. Buy bonds when prices are low and redeem when you need membership.

⑤ Bond Farming Strategy（Bond自给自足策略）

For Chinese players who grind efficiently, it's possible to earn enough GP for a bond in 2-4 weeks of casual play. Here are the best methods:

  • Flipping (GE Merching): Start with 100K GP, flip high-volume items. 8.5M GP target in 2-3 weeks
  • Zulrah (75+ Ranged/Magic): 4-6M GP/hr. ~2 hours for a bond
  • Vorkath (DS2 completed): 3-5M GP/hr. ~2-3 hours for a bond
  • Herb Runs + Bird Houses: 500K-1M GP/day passive. ~10-17 days for a bond
  • Slayer (85+): 1-2.5M GP/hr. ~4-8 hours for a bond

For new players, we recommend starting with a 1-month subscription ($14.99) and using that month to build up your account to a level where you can farm bonds.

⑧ ❓ FAQ（常见问题）

① Can I use Alipay to buy OSRS membership?（能用支付宝买会员吗？）

Unfortunately, Jagex does not accept Alipay or WeChat Pay directly. PayPal linked to a Chinese bank card is the most reliable option. Some third-party resellers accept Alipay, but we do not recommend them due to scam risks.

② How much is OSRS membership in CNY?（会员折合人民币多少钱？）

With PayPal's exchange rate and bank fees, 1-month membership costs approximately ¥105-115 CNY. The annual plan costs approximately ¥70-75 CNY per month, or ¥840-900 annually.

③ Is it cheaper to buy bonds or subscription?（买Bond划算还是订阅划算？）

For the first month, buying a subscription ($14.99) is cheaper than buying 2 bonds ($17.98). For long-term play, annual subscription is cheapest ($9.99/month). Once you have a good account, farming bonds in-game is free.

④ How long does it take a new player to earn a bond?（新手多久能赚到一个Bond？）

With efficient play, a new member can earn 8.5M GP in 2-4 weeks through flipping, herb runs, and slayer. Dedicated players can do it in 1-2 weeks by focusing on high-profit methods like blast furnace or Zulrah.

⑤ What's the cheapest way to get membership for 1 year?（最省钱的一年会员方案？）

The 12-month subscription at $119.88 total is the cheapest option. This works out to $9.99/month — a 33% saving over monthly billing. Pay with PayPal China for the most reliable payment.
