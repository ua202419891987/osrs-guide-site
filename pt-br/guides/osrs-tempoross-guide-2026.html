Tempoross is the fishing skilling boss that turns a traditionally slow skill into an engaging, profitable, and surprisingly fast 99. This guide covers the core loop, mass vs solo strategy, the double-catch method, reward pool, Spirit Angler outfit, and XP optimization.

🌊 Tempoross — Quick Reference

Location:Al Kharid docks (southeast of palace)Min Level:35 Fishing
Max XP/hr:~75K XP/hr (90+ Fishing)Profit:~100K-200K GP/hr

1. How Tempoross Works

Tempoross is a fishing boss on the Al Kharid coastline. You fish harpoonfish from pools → cook them on the ship → load cooked fish into the cannon → fire at Tempoross to drain its energy. The core loop: Fish → Cook → Load Cannon → Fire → Repeat.

The ship has two cannons (left and right). Tempoross summons waves of varying intensity that can knock out fires and damage you. Keep both fires burning at all times. When Tempoross's energy hits 0%, you win and earn reward permits based on contribution.

2. Mass World Strategy — Fast & Easy XP

  1. Arrive: Board the ship at Al Kharid docks. Bring a Rope (required) and optionally a Harpoon (Dragon Harpoon if you have one — its spec boosts Fishing +3).
  2. Fish: Fish from the pools near the cranes. Raw harpoonfish stack in your inventory.
  3. Cook: Use raw harpoonfish on the eternal fire next to the mast. Cooked harpoonfish also stack.
  4. Load Cannon: Click the ammo crate next to either cannon to deposit cooked fish.
  5. Fire: Click the cannon when Tempoross is in range. Each shot deals 8-10 energy damage.
  6. Waves: When Tempoross roars, a wave hits. If fires go out, relight them IMMEDIATELY with a tinderbox from the crate.
  7. Lightning: Tempoross occasionally marks spots on the deck with lightning. Move away — standing in lightning deals 30+ damage.

5. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Should I cook the fish or load them raw?

A: Always cook. Cooked harpoonfish deal twice as much damage to Tempoross (10 vs 5 energy per shot). The cooking XP is bonus XP on top of the Fishing XP you're already getting.

Q: Is Tempoross better than barbarian fishing?

A: Comparable XP but more profit. Barbarian fishing is slightly faster XP at 90+ but gives zero GP. Tempoross gives ~100K-200K GP/hr in rewards plus the Spirit Angler outfit which boosts all future Fishing XP by 2.5%. Do Tempoross for the outfit + Fish Barrel, then decide which method you prefer.

🌊 Fishing, But Make It Fun

Tempoross turns Fishing from a "click and wait" skill into an engaging boss fight with real rewards. The Fish Barrel alone is worth the grind — it fundamentally changes how you fish for the rest of your account. Get that outfit, grab the barrel, and enjoy the sea breeze.
