🗺️ OSRS New Player Roadmap Day 1-14 — What to Do First

Day-by-day guide for brand new OSRS players. From Tutorial Island to earning your first Bond, all planned out for you.

① Day 1: Tutorial Island（第一天：新手岛）

Complete Tutorial Island. This teaches you: movement, combat, skilling, and interface basics. After the island, spawn in Lumbridge. Talk to the Lumbridge Guide. Complete Cook's Assistant for 300 Cooking XP. Train Fishing to level 20 (shrimp → trout) and Woodcutting to 15 (normal trees → oak). Your first hour goal: understand the basics of movement, banking, and skilling.

② Days 2-3: First Quests & Skills（第2-3天：首批任务）

Complete these essential F2P quests: Sheep Shearer, Romeo & Juliet, Doric's Quest, Goblin Diplomacy, The Knight's Sword, Witch's Potion, Imp Catcher. These give critical early XP. Train combat on cows and goblins in Lumbridge. Aim for 20 Attack, 20 Strength, 20 Defence, 15 Ranged by day 3.

③ Days 4-6: Building Skills（第4-6天：技能基础）

Train Fishing to 30 (shrimp → trout → salmon). Start the Waterfall Quest for massive combat XP (13,750 Attack + Strength). Train Mining and Smithing. Your goal: earn 50K GP through fishing and quest rewards. This is your seed money for gear upgrades.

④ Days 11-14: Bond Goal（第11-14天：赚到Bond）

Your goal: Earn 8.5M GP for a bond. Methods: Flipping (start with 100K, aim for 500K profit/day), High Alchemy (explorer ring free casts), Cowhide collecting (if F2P). Alternatively, buy 1 month membership ($14.99) and use that month to establish money-making methods that make bonds sustainable.

FAQ

Which quest should I do first?（先做哪个任务？）

The Knight's Sword gives 12,725 Smithing XP — enough for 29 Smithing instantly. Do this quest first for huge early XP.
