① Gesture Controls（手势操作技巧）

OSRS Mobile supports touch gestures that aren't available on desktop. Master these: Tap to interact (single tap = left click), Hold to right-click (long press = right click), Swipe to scroll camera (two-finger drag = rotate view), Pinch to zoom (zoom in for precision clicking, zoom out for overview). Configure the "Long Press Duration" in settings — set to 300ms for the best balance between speed and accuracy.

② One-Handed Playing（单手操作技巧）

Enable One-Handed Mode in Settings → Mobile Controls. This moves the chat box, inventory, and function buttons to the bottom half of the screen. For right-handed play, shift all controls to the right edge. The most efficient AFK setup: place your inventory above the function button, prayer icon to the right, and combat style selector next to it. This minimizes thumb movement for repetitive tasks like dropping fish or alching items.

FAQ

Is mobile harder than desktop?（手机版比电脑版难吗？）

For simple activities (skilling, AFK training), mobile is easier. For bossing (Zulrah, Raids), desktop is significantly easier. Most players use both.

Can I boss on mobile?（手机能打Boss吗？）

Yes. Simple bosses like Giant Mole, KBD, Barrows, and even Zulrah (with practice) are doable on mobile. High-intensity PvM like Raids is more challenging.
