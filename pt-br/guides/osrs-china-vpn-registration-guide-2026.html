① Why Chinese Players Need a VPN（为什么中国玩家需要VPN）

Old School RuneScape does not have official servers in mainland China, and the game is not directly accessible from Chinese IP addresses due to network restrictions. The Great Firewall (GFW) blocks direct connections to Jagex's servers, making it impossible to log in, download the launcher, or play without a VPN or gaming accelerator.

The reality for Chinese players:

  • Direct connection to OSRS servers: impossible (connection times out)
  • Jagex Launcher website: blocked in China
  • Official RuneScape website: inaccessible without VPN
  • In-game ping with VPN: typically 180-350ms (playable for most content)
  • In-game ping without VPN: N/A (cannot connect)

A reliable VPN is not optional — it is the first requirement for any Chinese mainland player who wants to play OSRS.

② How to Choose the Right VPN（如何选择合适的VPN）

Not all VPNs work well with OSRS. Some are blocked by the GFW, others have high latency that makes gameplay frustrating. Here are the key criteria for selecting a VPN for OSRS from China:

① Server Location: Choose Asia-Pacific or West Coast US（服务器位置选择）

For the lowest ping, choose VPN servers in Japan, South Korea, Singapore, or Hong Kong. These locations typically give 120-200ms ping to OSRS servers. West Coast US servers (Los Angeles, San Francisco) give 180-250ms but are more stable for long sessions.

② Protocol: OpenVPN or WireGuard（协议选择）

WireGuard is faster and more stable for gaming. It uses UDP and typically adds less overhead than OpenVPN. Most modern VPNs support WireGuard. Enable it in your VPN settings for the best OSRS experience.

③ Split Tunneling: Keep OSRS on VPN, Everything Else Direct（分流设置）

Split tunneling lets you route only OSRS traffic through the VPN while keeping your local browsing direct. This reduces overall VPN load and improves connection stability. Look for this feature in your VPN settings.

③ VPN Setup Guide（VPN设置教程）

① Download & Install the VPN Client（下载安装VPN客户端）

Download the VPN client from the official website. If the official site is blocked, use a mirror or contact customer support for alternative download links. Install the application following the standard setup wizard.

② Configure for Gaming（游戏优化配置）

  • Select WireGuard protocol (or OpenVPN if WireGuard is unavailable)
  • Choose a server in Japan, Korea, or Singapore
  • Enable kill switch to prevent IP leaks
  • Enable split tunneling and add only OSRS/Jagex Launcher to the VPN tunnel
  • Disable IPv6 to prevent leaks

③ Test Your Connection（测试连接）

After connecting to VPN, visit oldschool.runescape.com in your browser. If the page loads, your VPN is working. Also check your ping by opening Command Prompt and running: ping worlds.oldschool.runescape.com. A response time under 300ms is acceptable for OSRS.

④ Jagex Launcher Installation & VPN Compatibility（Jagex Launcher安装与VPN兼容性）

Jagex Launcher is now mandatory for OSRS. Here's how to set it up from China:

① Download the Launcher（下载Launcher）

  1. Connect to your VPN (Japan server recommended)
  2. Visit jagex.com and download the Jagex Launcher
  3. If the site is blocked, switch to a different VPN server or use a mirror

② Install & Configure（安装配置）

  1. Run the installer with VPN connected
  2. After installation, keep VPN on before launching
  3. The launcher will detect RuneLite if already installed
  4. If the launcher gets stuck on "Connecting", try switching VPN protocols

③ Common Jagex Launcher VPN Issues（常见Launcher+VPN问题）

  • Stuck on "Checking for updates": Switch VPN server or protocol
  • White screen after login: Clear launcher cache (%LOCALAPPDATA%/Jagex Launcher/cache)
  • "Cannot connect to server": Try WireGuard → OpenVPN, or change server
  • VPN works for browser but not launcher: Enable TAP adapter in VPN settings

⑤ Account Creation Step-by-Step（账号创建步骤）

  1. Connect VPN — Japan or Singapore server
  2. Visit RuneScape account creation page
  3. Fill in details: Email (use Gmail/Outlook), password, display name
  4. Verify email: Check your inbox for the verification link (may take 1-2 minutes)
  5. Download Jagex Launcher (with VPN still connected)
  6. Log in to the launcher with your new account
  7. Choose OSRS from the game selection screen
  8. Complete Tutorial Island — the game's introductory area

💡 Tip: Use an email address that you can access without VPN — this makes account recovery much easier if you have issues later.

⑥ First Login & Configuration（首次登录配置）

① Download RuneLite（下载RuneLite）

Download RuneLite from the official website: runelite.net. Always use the official source — fake RuneLite download sites are common and may contain malware. After installation, RuneLite will appear in your Jagex Launcher as an option.

② Configure RuneLite for Low Ping（低延迟配置）

In RuneLite, go to Settings → World Switcher. Select worlds from "Worlds" list and sort by ping. Choose a world with the lowest ping (typically US East or UK worlds give 200-300ms from Asia).

③ Install Chinese Language Plugin（安装中文汉化插件）

In RuneLite, open Plugin Hub and search for "RuneLingual" or "OSRSCN" to install the Chinese translation plugin. This will translate most in-game text to simplified Chinese. Refer to our dedicated RuneLite Hanhua guide for detailed instructions.

⑧ ❓ FAQ — China VPN & Registration（常见问题）

① Is it legal to play OSRS with a VPN in China?（在中国用VPN玩OSRS合法吗？）

Using VPNs for personal use is in a legal grey area in China. While commercial VPNs are restricted, individual use for gaming is generally not enforced. However, we recommend checking current regulations and using trusted providers.

② What's the best VPN server for OSRS from China?（中国玩家选哪个VPN服务器最好？）

Japan servers typically give the lowest ping (120-180ms), followed by South Korea (150-200ms) and Singapore (180-250ms). West Coast US servers are also viable but give 200-300ms ping.

③ Can I play on mobile with VPN?（手机版能用VPN吗？）

Yes. Both Android and iOS versions of OSRS work with VPNs. On Android, use the VPN app's split tunneling feature. On iOS, the system-level VPN covers all apps automatically. Mobile ping is typically 50-100ms higher than desktop.

④ Why can't I buy membership or bonds from China?（为什么不能在中国直接购买会员？）

Jagex's payment system may reject Chinese credit cards and Alipay. We recommend using PayPal (linked to a Chinese bank card) or purchasing membership through the game client with VPN connected. See our Payment Guide for detailed solutions.

⑤ Will Jagex ban my account for using a VPN?（Jagex会因VPN封号吗？）

No. Jagex does not prohibit VPN usage. However, if your IP changes frequently, the account may be temporarily locked for security. Simply verify your email to unlock it. As long as you are not using the VPN for rule violations, your account is safe.

⑥ What's the minimum internet speed needed?（最低需要多少网速？）

OSRS uses very little bandwidth — approximately 5-15 MB per hour. A stable 5 Mbps connection with a good VPN is sufficient. What matters more is latency stability rather than raw speed. Packet loss under 1% is ideal.

⑦ Do gaming accelerators work for OSRS?（游戏加速器有效吗？）

Chinese gaming accelerators like Xunyou, UU, and QQ Accelerator can work well for OSRS. They are often cheaper than full VPNs and optimized for gaming. However, they route through CN2 lines which may not always have OSRS-specific optimization. VPNs offer more flexibility.

⑧ Can I play F2P without membership?（免费玩家能玩吗？）

Yes. F2P accounts can play OSRS without paying. You need VPN for F2P too — the connection restriction applies to all players, not just members. F2P content includes: Tutorial Island, basic skills (1-99), F2P quests, and some early-game money making.

⑨ How do I know if my VPN is working?（怎么确认VPN工作正常？）

Visit the official RuneScape website. If it loads, your VPN is working. You can also check your IP at whatismyip.com to confirm you're connected through the VPN server. For game connection, try to log in — if the game loads, everything is set up correctly.

⑩ What if my VPN stops working mid-game?（游戏中VPN断了怎么办？）

Enable the kill switch feature in your VPN settings. This automatically disconnects the game if the VPN drops, preventing IP leaks. When the VPN reconnects, the game will typically resume from where you were (within 60 seconds). For safety, avoid dangerous areas (Wilderness) when your VPN is unstable.
