① Trade Routes Overview

Sailing trade routes are the most consistent money-making method. You buy goods at one port, sail to another, and sell at a profit. Profit depends on distance, danger level, and ship cargo capacity. Trade route profit scales with your Sailing level higher tiers unlock more profitable routes. Key trading goods: Silk, Spices, Rum, Exotic Woods, and Ancient Artifacts.
