① Quest Helper（任务助手）

The most popular RuneLite plugin. It shows step-by-step quest guidance directly on screen. Automatically detects quest progress and highlights the next object/NPC to interact with. Setup: Open Plugin Hub → search "Quest Helper" → Install. Once installed, open your quest log and select any quest — the helper activates automatically.

② Ground Items（地面物品高亮）

Highlights valuable items on the ground. Configure price thresholds: items over 10K GP in green, 100K+ in blue, 1M+ in red. This prevents missing valuable drops in cluttered areas.

③ GPU Plugin（GPU显卡加速）

Enables hardware-accelerated rendering. Increases render distance, adds anti-aliasing, and improves FPS. Most systems double their frame rate with this enabled. If you crash, reduce draw distance from 50 to 30.
