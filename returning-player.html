Stepping back into Gielinor after a break can feel overwhelming. New quests, reworked skills, a shifting economy, and content you've never seen before — it's a lot to process. This hub exists to change that. Instead of scrambling through Reddit threads and outdated wiki pages, use these four guides to go from "I'm lost" to "I know exactly what to do" in under 30 minutes.

Whether you've been gone six months or six years, we start from the same question: what changed, and what should you do first? The guides below cover your welcome-back essentials — from a complete what-changed overview and a quick catch-up plan to a fast-track guide for the ambitious returner and an in-depth returning player handbook.

Not sure where to start? If you have 15 minutes, begin with the Catch-Up Guide. If you have an evening, go straight to the Complete Returning Player Guide. Either way, you'll be bossing again by tomorrow.
