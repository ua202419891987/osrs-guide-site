OSRS Boss Profit Tier List 2026
Best Bosses Ranked by GP/hr — Post-Gauntlet Meta Edition

The June 2026 Gauntlet buffs didn't just make CG faster — they reshuffled the entire boss profit meta. Some bosses got indirectly better (Zulrah), some became relatively worse (Vorkath). Here's every OSRS boss ranked by real profit per hour, factoring in supply costs, death frequency, and current GE prices.

📅 June 11, 2026 ⏱️ 13 min read 👹 20+ Bosses

How Boss Rankings Are Calculated

Bosses are ranked by effective GP/hr for an average player who knows the mechanics but isn't speedrunning. We factor in:

  • Raw unique drop value divided by drop rate
  • Consistent loot (bones, hides, alchables) per kill
  • Kills per hour at average skill level (not max-efficiency)
  • Supply costs (prayer pots, food, runes, bolts, scales, divine potions)
  • Death costs (assume 1 death every 50 kills for hard bosses)
  • Banking time included in kills-per-hour estimate

S Tier — 8M+ GP/hr (The Raid Bosses)

🏆 Verzik Vitur (Theatre of Blood — Hard Mode) S

👹 10-15M GP/hr | 🎯 95+ Combat, ToB experience | 👥 3-5 man team

Scythe of Vitur (~800M, 1/19.1 from Hard Mode) is the single most valuable boss drop in OSRS. Sang staff (~80M), Justiciar pieces (~20-40M), and Avernic hilt (~55M) add consistent value between scythes. Hard Mode doubles unique rates. With the June 2026 Sanguinesti buff, Nylocas and Verzik P2 are ~15% faster — meaning more raids per hour.

📊 Meta note: Hard Mode ToB requires a dedicated team that knows all Verzik mechanics (tornadoes, webs, yellows, green ball). One weak player tanks everyone's profit. PUGs are not recommended — find a clan.

🐪 Tumeken / Elidinis' Warden (Tombs of Amascut — Expert 300+) S

👹 8-12M GP/hr | 🎯 90+ Combat, raid knowledge | 👤 Solo or team

Tumeken's Shadow (~1.5B) is the holy grail — 1/24 from Expert 300+. Fang (~28M), Lightbearer (~2M), Masori pieces all add consistent profit. ToA Expert is the best solo money maker in the game. No team to split with, no reliance on others. The better you get, the more you make.

🐲 Great Olm (Chambers of Xeric — Challenge Mode) S

👹 7-9M GP/hr | 🎯 90+ Combat, CoX experience | 👥 3+ man team

Twisted Bow (~1.5B, 1/34.5M points) and Ancestral pieces (~100-300M) drive CoX profits. Dexterous and Arcane scrolls (~23M, ~3.5M) add consistent income. Challenge Mode bumps points by 50%+. Requires a team that knows Olm head-turning and 4:1 melee — learn these or you'll wipe repeatedly.

A Tier — 3-7M GP/hr (Best Daily Grind Bosses)

💀 Phantom Muspah A

👹 3.5-5M GP/hr | 🎯 85+ Ranged, 70+ Agility | 👤 Solo | ⚡ Low risk

Ancient Essence (~1.5K each × 100+ per kill = 150K+ per kill) and Venator Bow shards (~6M, 1/100) make Muspah the most consistent solo boss in the game. Ranged-only method with Ruby/Diamond bolts (e) is beginner-friendly. Frozen Cache phase keeps things interesting without being punishing. Zero unique-dependent profit — the essence alone covers supplies.

🐉 Vorkath A

👹 3-4M GP/hr | 🎯 Dragon Slayer II, 85+ Ranged | 👤 Solo

The people's champion. Superior dragon bones (~20K) + 2 blue dragonhides (~5K) + consistent alchables (rune kites, dragon battleaxes, battlestaves) = 130-170K per kill. With Dragon Hunter Crossbow, you're doing 24-28 kills/hour. Vorkath is boring but beautiful — you show up, you print GP, you leave. No complex mechanics, no RNG despair.

⚔️ Hunllef (Corrupted Gauntlet) A ⬆️ BUFFED

👹 4.5-7M GP/hr | 🎯 80+ Combat, Song of the Elves | 👤 Solo | ⚡ ZERO supply cost

June 2026: 27% faster kills = 27% more profit. Enhanced Weapon Seed (~140M, 1/400) and Armor Seeds (~9M, 1/50) are the big drops. Crystal Crown now drops more frequently, bumping average profit even without the enhanced seed. The real strength of CG? No supplies needed. Every single crystal and alchable is 100% profit. For accounts in the 80-95 combat range, this is now the #1 boss — period.

🔄 Post-buff change: Previously C-Tier (~2.5M/hr). Now A-Tier (~5M/hr average). This is the single biggest boss ranking change in 2026.

🐍 Zulrah A ⬆️ BUFFED

👹 2.5-3.5M GP/hr | 🎯 Regicide, 80+ Magic/Ranged | 👤 Solo

Scales (~250 each × 150-250/kill = 40-60K/kill) plus Serpentine Visage (3.5M, 1/128) and Tanzanite Fang (3.2M, 1/128). Sanguinesti Staff buff in June 2026 makes the magic phase ~15% faster, bumping kills/hr from 24 to 27-28. The rotation learning curve is the only barrier — expect 2-3 hours to memorize the 4 rotation patterns. Once you have them down, Zulrah is a reliable 3M/hr with virtually no variation.

🐕 Cerberus A

👹 2.5-3.5M GP/hr | 🎯 91 Slayer, 80+ Combat | 👤 Solo (Slayer task only)

Primordial Crystal (~30M, 1/512), Pegasian Crystal (~180K, 1/512), Eternal Crystal (~5M, 1/512). Each kill drops 30-50K in ashes/bones/alchables. Ghost-skipping (stand under Cerb when ghosts spawn to skip the attack) is recommended for profit — fewer prayer potions used = more profit per trip. Hellhound tasks are common; stockpile them.

🐲 Alchemical Hydra A

👹 3-4M GP/hr | 🎯 95 Slayer, 85+ Ranged | 👤 Solo (Slayer task only)

Hydra Leather (~45M, 1/512), Hydra's Claw (~55M, 1/1001), Hydra Tail (~120K, 1/512). Each kill drops 40-60K in bones/hides/alchables. The fight is rhythmic once learned — count attacks, step, count attacks, step. Blowpipe + Dragon Darts or Twisted Bow if you have it.

B Tier — 1.5-3M GP/hr (Solid Mid-Game Bosses)

😈 Abyssal Sire B

👹 1.5-2.5M GP/hr | 🎯 85 Slayer, 80+ Melee | 👤 Solo

Unsired (1/100) gives Abyssal Dagger (~6M), Abyssal Bludgeon pieces (~6-10M each), Abyssal Whip (~1.5M). Consistent profit from alchables and rune drops. The respiratory system phase is tedious but the boss itself is simple. Shadow Barrage/Barrage for the spawns — otherwise you'll waste time killing them individually.

🦎 Giant Mole B

👹 800K-1.2M GP/hr | 🎯 Falador Hard Diary, 70+ Melee | 👤 Solo

Mole claws + mole skins (noted with Falador Hard Diary) = 18-22K per kill. With Dharok's at 1 HP, you can do 50-60 kills/hour = ~1M/hr. The mole tunnels under-ground mechanic is annoying but harmless. Pure profit from alchable drops. Very AFK-friendly once you have the mole locator shield.

🕷️ Sarachnis B

👹 800K-1.2M GP/hr | 🎯 65+ Combat, 60+ Prayer | 👤 Solo

Cudgel (~350K, 1/384) and Giant Egg Sac (~50K, 1/80). Each kill drops 15-25K in alchables. A good boss to learn prayer switching on — you need to flick between Melee and Ranged. Web attack roots you, double-hit attack hits twice. Low requirements, decent profit, great for mid-game bossing practice.

🗡️ Grotesque Guardians B

👹 800K-1.5M GP/hr | 🎯 75 Slayer, 75+ Ranged/Melee | 👤 Solo

Granite Dust (~2K per kill, used for cannonballs) and regular alchable drops. Black Tourmaline Core (~5M, 1/500) and Jar of Stone (~5M) are rare but nice bonuses. The fight is basically a DPS check — nothing complex, just damage and avoid the falling rocks.

C Tier — 700K-1.5M GP/hr (Entry Bosses)

👻 Barrows Brothers C

👹 600K-1M GP/hr | 🎯 43 Prayer, 60+ Magic | 👤 Solo

Ahrim's top (~1.8M), Karil's top (~1.5M), Dharok's helm (~800K). With Morytania Hard Diary (+50% runes), each chest gives 30-60K in runes alone. 86.2% potential (max at 6 brothers + 2 skeletons + 1 bloodworm). The classic first boss. Six brothers, six crypts, one chest. Every OSRS player's gateway into bossing.

🦂 Scorpia C

👹 700K-1.1M GP/hr | 🎯 60+ Magic, anti-PK awareness | 👤 Solo (Wilderness)

Odium Shard 3 (~3.5M, 1/256) and Malediction Shard 3 (~900K, 1/256). Scorpia is in deep Wilderness (level 54) so PKers are a constant threat. Use cheap gear (mystic robes, trident), bring minimal risk. If you can handle the PKer interruptions, Scorpia is actually very profitable for the requirements.

🐉 King Black Dragon C

👹 500-800K GP/hr | 🎯 60+ Ranged, anti-dragon shield | 👤 Solo

Dragon Pickaxe (~800K, 1/1000) and KBD Heads (~30K, 1/128). The main profit comes from consistent dragonhide drops (~8K each) and dragon bones. KBD is the easiest "dragon boss" but the drops reflect that. Good for practice but outclassed by Vorkath once you have Dragon Slayer II done.

🐍 Hespori C

👹 200-400K per kill (1 kill/day) | 🎯 65+ Farming | 👤 Solo (daily)

Bottomless Compost Bucket (~200K, 1/35), White Lily Seeds (~30K), Anima Seeds (Iasor/Attas/Kronos — untradeable but valuable for herb runs). One kill per day maximum. Bottomless Bucket is the main profit driver. Treat Hespori as a daily bonus, not a money maker.

D Tier — 200K-700K GP/hr (First Bosses)

🐔 Obor (Hill Giant Boss) D

👹 300-500K GP/hr | 🎯 50+ Melee, Giant Key | 👤 Solo (F2P available)

Hill Giant Club (~450K, 1/118). Each kill requires a Giant Key, limiting kills/hr. Good F2P boss for practice but not worth farming on members accounts — Barrows is better with similar requirements.

💀 Bryophyta (Moss Giant Boss) D

👹 200-400K GP/hr | 🎯 50+ Melee, Mossy Key | 👤 Solo (F2P available)

Bryophyta's Essence (~6M, 1/118) which upgrades to Staff of Bryophyta (~9M). This is a lottery boss — 90% of your profit depends on hitting the 1/118 drop. Without it, you're making ~40K/hr. Not recommended for members. F2P only.

June 2026 Meta Shifts — Boss Ranking Changes

📊 What Moved and Why

  • Hunllef / Corrupted Gauntlet: C → A Tier — 27% faster kills plus Crystal Crown buff = massive profit increase. Now the single best solo boss for mid-game accounts.
  • Zulrah: B → A Tier — Sanguinesti Staff buff = ~15% more DPS on magic phase = more kills/hr = bumped into A-tier range.
  • Vorkath: stayed A Tier — No direct changes but relatively less special now that CG and Zulrah are buffed. Still amazing, just not uniquely dominant.
  • ToB: Top of S Tier — Sanguinesti buff makes ToB runs faster too. Nylocas and Verzik P2 are magic-heavy phases.

FAQ — Boss Profit Tier List

Q: Which boss should I farm at 80 combat?

A: Corrupted Gauntlet if you can do Song of the Elves. Barrows if you can't. CG is now far-and-away the best mid-game boss money maker after the June 2026 buff. Zero supply cost means every kill is 100% profit.

Q: Is Vorkath still worth doing after the CG buff?

A: Absolutely. Vorkath is still 3-4M/hr and more brainless than CG. If you have Dragon Hunter Crossbow already, Vorkath is slightly less effort for similar money. If you DON'T have DHCB, CG is better because it's completely free.

Q: What's the best boss for learning mechanics?

A: Sarachnis. Low requirements, teaches prayer switching, has a telegraphed attack pattern, and doesn't kill you for mistakes. Graduate to Zulrah once you're comfortable with switching prayers mid-fight.

Q: Are Wilderness bosses worth it?

A: Scorpia and Calvar'ion (Vet'ion) can be 1-2M/hr but PKer interruptions cut effective profit by 30-50%. If you're patient and don't mind dying occasionally, they're decent. For most players, stick to safe-zone bosses.

Q: Which boss has the best unique-to-hours ratio?

A: Phantom Muspah. Venator Bow shards at 1/100 and Ancient Essence on every kill means you're always making progress toward something. Unlike CG where you might go 1000+ dry on Enhanced, Muspah rewards you every trip.
