OSRS Phantom Muspah Guide 2026 — Strategy, Gear & 6M GP/hr Profit

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 13 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
👹 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

The Phantom Muspah is a two-phase demonic boss unlocked after Secrets of the North. It's one of the best mid-to-high-level money makers at 4.5M-6M GP/hr with lower supply costs than Vorkath. This guide covers both combat phases, gear setups, and exactly how to handle the ice teleport mechanic.

👻 Phantom Muspah — Quick Reference

Quest:Secrets of the NorthLocation:Ghorrock Dungeon (Frozen Waste)
Phases:2 (Melee → Ranged phase)GP/hr:4.5M-6M

1. Two-Phase Fight Overview

Phase 1 (Melee): Muspah walks toward you and attacks with Melee. Use Protect from Melee. Attack with Ranged (Ruby Bolts e). Stay away — kite Muspah around the room to avoid damage.

Phase 2 (Ranged): Muspah becomes transparent and floats. Attacks with Magic-based Ranged. Switch to Protect from Missiles. Switch to Melee (Fang). Muspah teleports to you — stay calm and keep DPS up.

2. Key Mechanics

5. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Muspah vs Vorkath — which is better GP?

A: Comparable at max efficiency. Vorkath is more consistent (guaranteed 150K+ per kill). Muspah has higher variance from rare drops (Venator Shard spikes). Muspah uses fewer supplies. Master both and switch based on what you enjoy.

Q: Can I range-only Muspah?

A: You can, but it's slower. Phase 2 has high Ranged defence. Using Melee (Fang) in Phase 2 roughly doubles your DPS. Range-only kills take 30-40% longer but are simpler for learning.

👻 A Vorkath Alternative Worth Learning

Phantom Muspah fills the gap between mid-game bosses and raids. Consistent profit, manageable mechanics, and lower supply costs than its dragon counterpart. If you've been farming Vorkath for months, Muspah is a refreshing change of pace that pays just as well.
