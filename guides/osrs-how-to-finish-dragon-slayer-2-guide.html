Quest Walkthrough

Dragon Slayer II takes you on a journey across multiple locations to uncover the truth about Robert the Strong and the dragonkin. Here's a condensed walkthrough:

Part 1: Starting the Quest

  1. Speak to Alec Kincade in the Myths' Guild (requires Dragon Slayer I)
  2. Learn about the threat of the dragonkin and Galvek
  3. Investigate the history of Robert the Strong

Part 2: Gathering Information

  1. Travel to various locations following the quest markers
  2. Speak to key NPCs including Bob the Cat (A Tail of Two Cats connection)
  3. Complete puzzles and agility sections
  4. Obtain the key items needed for the final confrontation

Part 3: The Dragon Key

  1. Smith the dragon key using 70 Smithing
  2. Use the key to access the Lithkren Vault
  3. Explore the vault and discover Galvek's plan
  4. Prepare for the final boss fight sequence

Part 4: Final Boss Fights

  1. Fight through the dragonkin guards
  2. Battle Vorkath (introductory fight — he's fightable as a boss later)
  3. Face Galvek in the final confrontation

Vorkath Boss Fight

During the quest, you fight Vorkath as part of the storyline. This fight introduces the mechanics you'll encounter when fighting Vorkath as a repeatable boss after the quest. The quest version is easier than the post-quest boss.

Vorkath Mechanics (Quest Version):

  • Vorkath uses dragonfire attacks — use an anti-dragon shield or dragonfire shield
  • He fires acid pools on the ground — walk away from them
  • He uses a freezing special attack — use crumble undead on the zombified spawn
  • He occasionally uses a rapid-fire dragonfire barrage — use Protect from Missiles

Post-Quest Vorkath:

After completing DS2, Vorkath becomes a repeatable boss on Lithkren. He's one of the most profitable bosses in OSRS, with average loot worth 150K–300K per kill. Vorkath is considered one of the best money-making bosses in the game.

Galvek Final Boss

Galvek is the final boss of Dragon Slayer II and the leader of the dragonkin. The fight takes place on a series of boats and has multiple phases:

Phase 1: Galvek (Dragon Form)

  • Galvek attacks with powerful dragonfire — use super antifire + Protect from Magic
  • Move between the boats to avoid his fire breath sweeping attacks
  • Attack with ranged while maintaining prayer and antifire

Phase 2: Enhanced Attacks

  • Galvek adds meteor attacks — run away from shadow markers on the ground
  • His dragonfire becomes more powerful — eat more aggressively
  • Continue using ranged attacks and keep your prayer up

Phase 3: Final Push

  • Galvek enters a berserk phase with faster attacks
  • Use your special attacks (dragon claws, DDS) to burst him down
  • Eat to full HP before each attack to survive his max hits

The Galvek fight requires approximately 3–6 minutes with decent gear. Bring 4–6 saradomin brews and super restores, plus a super antifire potion. Ranged is the recommended attack style.

Dragon Slayer 2 Tips

  • ✅ Prepare before starting — gather all required items and complete all prerequisites first
  • ✅ Bring super antifire potions — essential for both Vorkath and Galvek fights
  • ✅ Use ranged for Galvek — it's the safest and most effective style
  • ✅ Practice on Vorkath post-quest — he's one of the best bosses in the game
  • ❌ Don't attempt without antifire — dragonfire damage is devastating
  • ❌ Don't stand in acid pools — they deal rapid damage and are easy to avoid
  • ✅ Complete DS2 as soon as you meet requirements — it unlocks too much content to delay
