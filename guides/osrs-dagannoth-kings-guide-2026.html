OSRS Dagannoth Kings Guide 2026 — Solo, Duo & Tribrid Ring Farming

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 15 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
👹 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

Dagannoth Kings are the original OSRS boss trio — Prime (Magic), Supreme (Ranged), and Rex (Melee). They drop the Berserker Ring, one of the best value upgrades in the game, and the iconic Dragon Axe. This guide covers solo tribrid rotation, Rex-only safespot farming for mid-level accounts, duo strategies, full gear setups, and how to consistently make 1M+ GP/hr.

2. Getting There — The Dungeon Run

Start at the Waterbirth Island dock (boat from Rellekka). Navigate through the dungeon: bring a Pet Rock and Rune Thrownaxe to pass the gate mechanism (stand on one pressure pad, drop pet rock on the other). The DK lair is on the deepest level. 85 Agility shortcut skips most of the dungeon. Wear a Fremennik shield or helm to avoid aggro from Dagannoth spawns in the tunnels. Pro tip: Pray Protect from Magic when descending the ladder into the DK room — Prime is usually the first King to aggro.

3. Rex-Only Safespot — Mid-Level Ring Farm

This is the safest and most accessible method for farming Dagannoth Kings. Rex can be permanently safespotted along the southeastern wall of the DK lair. Stand 3 tiles from the southernmost point, pressed against the eastern wall — Rex will never reach you. Cast Magic (Iban's Blast for budget, Trident of the Seas/Swamp for efficiency) from complete safety.

Entry setup: Pray Protect from Magic before descending the ladder. Immediately run to the southeastern safespot along the east wall. Stay against the wall — Prime and Supreme patrol the northern and western sides and won't aggro if you stay south. Wait for Rex to spawn — he spawns from the southern pool. Kill him → wait ~90 seconds for respawn → repeat. Kill times: ~30-45 seconds with Iban's, ~20-30 seconds with Trident. ~30-40 kills/hr = 500K-1M GP/hr.

4. Solo Tribrid — Full Rotation (Max Profit)

Soloing all three Kings requires precise timing and gear switches. The correct kill order is: Supreme → Prime → Rex → repeat.

Timing is everything. At max combat, Supreme takes ~30 seconds, Prime ~25 seconds, Rex ~20 seconds. The rotation takes ~90 seconds — perfectly aligning with the 90-second respawn timer. If you're too slow, two Kings will be active simultaneously = heavy damage. Use the Rex safespot as your "rest" period — heal and re-potion while killing Rex.

6. Advanced Techniques & Common Mistakes

  1. Gear switch order matters. Always swap to the NEXT style before finishing the current kill. Example: As Supreme dies, already switch to Ranged gear so you can attack Prime immediately.
  2. Blood Barrage for sustain. Bring Blood Barrage runes. Cast on Rex (from safespot) for free healing — saves inventory slots on long trips.
  3. Don't panic when two Kings aggro. If Prime and Supreme both aggro, pray against the HIGHER damage King first (Prime max hit: 60 vs Supreme: 30). Kill the more dangerous one first.
  4. Spinolyps priority. The small Spinolyps drain your prayer. Kill the ones nearest your safespot with Ranged before starting each rotation.
  5. Imbue your rings. Berserker Ring (i) at NMZ = +8 Strength vs +4 unimbued. This is the difference between a ring that's worth 2M and one worth 10M in effective DPS.
  6. Rex-only Ironman trick: Bring alch runes. DKs drop frequent Rune items and Dragon axes worth alching. Extends trips from 1 hour to 2+ hours.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What combat level for Rex-only farming?

A: 60+ Magic with Iban's Blast, 40+ Defence, 43+ Prayer. Rex-only is accessible very early. Ironmen often start DKs at 50-60 Magic using Iban's. Higher Magic = faster kills = more trips per hour. See our Mid-Game Breakthrough Guide for more early-game bossing strategies.

Q: Solo tribrid or duo DKs?

A: Solo tribrid for max GP/hr per person. Duo for easier trips. In a duo, one player handles Supreme + Prime while the other handles Rex. Less gear switching, less stress. Solo tribrid is ~30% more GP per person but requires more focus and faster switches.
