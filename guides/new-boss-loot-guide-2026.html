Summer Sweep-Up 2026 — Gear & PvM Rebalancing

Announced during the May 29 Summer Campfire 2026 blog, the Summer Sweep-Up is Jagex's annual project to rebalance and improve underperforming content. It follows the 2024 Project Rebalance and 2025 Summer Sweep-Up.

Confirmed Changes

  • Gear Buffs: Focus on underperforming items — Jagex specifically stated they want to buff items that "haven't reached their potential" rather than nerfing meta items.
  • Corrupted Gauntlet QoL: The "red prison" is getting improvements to reduce frustration, potentially including changes to prep time or resource spawn consistency.
  • Hunter Rework: Core Hunter training methods are being overhauled for the first time in years.
  • Agility Shortcuts: New shortcuts across Gielinor, plus improvements to existing ones.
  • PvM Pain Points: Jagex is addressing common frustrations identified by the community.
Tip: The detailed Summer Sweep-Up blog was published in early June. Check the OSRS homepage and our Monthly Updates page for the full breakdown. The gear buffs could shift the meta significantly — don't sell niche gear until the full list is revealed.

Blood Moon Rises (June 30) — Grandmaster Quest & Boss Content

Release Date: June 30, 2026

Blood Moon Rises is OSRS's next Grandmaster quest and the long-awaited finale to the Myreque series — a story over 20 years in the making. It transports players to Vampyrium, the vampyre home continent.

Quest Requirements

The quest requires completion of all Myreque quest prerequisites, with difficulty comparable to Desert Treasure II (though with different challenges focused on timing and precision).

Maggot King — New Solo Boss

Available after completing Blood Moon Rises, the Maggot King is a solo boss encounter in Vampyrium. Key mechanics include:

  • Rapid repositioning — The arena changes as the fight progresses, forcing constant movement
  • Environmental hazards — Danger spreads across the arena, increasing pressure over time
  • Awareness + DPS balance — The fight rewards players who can maintain damage output while navigating changing threats

The Maggot King emphasizes awareness and damage output equally, making it a challenging but accessible solo boss for mid-to-high level players.

Vampyrium Post-Quest Content

  • Bloodwood trees — New woodcutting activity
  • Leechfin fishing — New fishing method
  • Stymphike — New Hunter creature
  • Venators — New Slayer creatures that offer an alternative method for obtaining Blood shards
Tip: If you haven't completed previous Myreque quests (In Search of the Myreque, In Aid of the Myreque, Darkness of Hallowvale, etc.), start working through them now. Blood Moon Rises is expected to require completion of the entire quest line.

The Fractured Archive — OSRS's 4th Raid

Scheduled for later in 2026, The Fractured Archive is Old School RuneScape's fourth raid. Players enter a dimension protecting Guthix's most treasured collection of artifacts and knowledge.

Announced Bosses

Boss Description
Archive Guardian The raid's first encounter. Notably "rolls out the red carpet" for players — a unique entrance mechanic.
Bork & Smork A multi-boss encounter testing coordination and footwork. Movement and positioning are key.
Additional Bosses More encounters to be revealed in future development blogs.

Development Timeline

  • Mid-June 2026: First reward proposal blog released
  • Ongoing: Concept art refinement and mechanical design
  • TBD 2026: Beta testing and release

Jagex stated that "numbers and ideas are ready" and they are currently refining concept art. This raid is expected to be a major endgame addition alongside CoX, ToB, and ToA.

The Graveyard — Master Quest Reward

Status: In Polling (Question 7)

The Graveyard is a Master-level quest and the sequel to Olaf's Quest. It's set in a cold, haunted underground cavern and offers:

Requirements (Polled)

  • Completed: Olaf's Quest, The Fremennik Exiles, Pandemonium
  • 65 Sailing
  • 65 Agility
  • 56 Woodcutting
  • 54 Fletching

Rewards (Polled)

  • 3 Quest Points
  • 35,000 Agility XP
  • 15,000 Runecraft XP
  • 35,000 Sailing XP
  • 10,000 Fletching XP
  • 2 XP lamps (Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, or Prayer — 75K XP each)
  • Access to The Graveyard area

New Agility Training Method (65-80)

The Graveyard includes a new Agility course with a dual-path system (safe route and risky route), similar to the Colossal Wyrm. This fills the training gap between 65-80 Agility.

Wyrmscraig

New Game Servers

To support the growing global playerbase, Jagex announced new game server locations:

  • South Africa — Lower latency for African players
