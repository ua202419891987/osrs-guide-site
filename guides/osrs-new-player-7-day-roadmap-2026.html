OSRS 7-Day Beginner Roadmap 2026 — From Zero to Self-Sufficient

You've read the New Player Guide. Now let's turn that knowledge into action. This roadmap takes you from your first chicken kill to killing the Barrows brothers — in 7 days. Each day has exact missions, gear milestones, money targets, and time estimates. Two tracks: F2P (free) and Member (if you've bought a Bond). Let's go.

📅 Sequel to: OSRS New Player Guide · 7-Day Series · 15 min read

⏱️ Quick Summary — 30-Second Read

  • 📌 Day 0: Complete New Player Guide + set up Authenticator/Bank PIN + download RuneLite
  • 📌 Day 1-2 (F2P): Reach 30+ combat through cows/cows → Stronghold of Security (10K GP) → Waterfall Quest
  • 📌 Day 3-4 (Questing): Complete Dragon Slayer, unlock Fairy Rings, get Rune Scimitar
  • 📌 Day 5-6 (Money): Build 100K+ GP bank through cowhides/telegrab/steel platebodies
  • 📌 Day 7: Buy your first Bond or start membership — you're ready for Barrows

📋 Day 0 — Prerequisites (Already Did These?)

This roadmap assumes you've completed the OSRS New Player Guide and have:

  • ✅ Created your account with Authenticator + Bank PIN enabled
  • ✅ Completed Tutorial Island (~20 min)
  • ✅ Downloaded Runelite (don't play without it)
  • ✅ Made your first GE sale (chicken feathers → GP)
  • ✅ Set your respawn point to Lumbridge

Can't check all boxes? Go back and complete the New Player Guide first — it's free and takes 30 minutes.

🎯 Day 1 — Get Your Foundation Going

Time: 60-90 min · Goal: Combat 15+ · Bank: 50K+ GP

Mission 1.1: Kill Cows Until Combat 15 (30 min)

  • Location: Lumbridge cow pen (just north of castle)
  • Kill cows → collect cowhide (not meat, not bones — cowhide)
  • Why: Cowhide sells for 150-200 GP each on the GE
  • Goal: 100 cowhides = 15K-20K GP → enough for your first gear upgrade

💡 Pro Tip: Banking Route

Lumbridge Castle bank is 10 seconds from the cow pen. Fill inventory → bank → repeat. Full banking guide here.

Mission 1.2: Upgrade Your Gear (15 min)

Sell your cowhides on the GE, then buy:

Iron Full Helm~500 GPCheap defense boost
Iron Platebody~2K GPMajor defense upgrade
Iron Platelegs~1.5K GPLeg slot defense
Amulet of Power~10K GPBest budget amulet — +10 all stats
Total~16K GPMassive damage/defense upgrade

Mission 1.3: Complete Your First Quest — Cook's Assistant (10 min)

  • Talk to the Cook in Lumbridge Castle kitchen
  • Bring: Egg (chicken pen), Milk (dairy churn north), Flour (south of Varrock)
  • Reward: 300 Cooking XP + 1 Quest Point
  • Why: First quest done! Momentum is everything.

👉 Ready for Day 2?

⚔️ Day 2 — Strengthen Your Combat Core

Time: 60-90 min · Goal: Combat 25+ · Bank: 150K+ GP

Mission 2.1: Attack + Strength to 25 (45 min)

F2P route: Hill Giants (south of Varrock, Edgeville dungeon)

  • Drop: big bones (sell for 2K each) + limpwurt roots (3K each)
  • Kill until Attack 25 + Strength 25
  • Bank the big bones and limpwurt roots

Member route: Sand Crabs (Kourend) or Rock Crabs (Rellekka)

  • 60+ combat stats with 0 effort — best early training spot
  • Bring food + click once every 30 seconds — near-AFK training

Mission 2.2: Money Run — Sell Your Loot (15 min)

Mission 2.3: Upgrade Weapon — Mithril Scimitar (5 min)

Sell your Iron Scimitar → buy Mithril Scimitar (~5K GP). This is your best weapon until level 40 Attack.

🎮 F2P Player Special: Stronghold of Security

Complete the Stronghold of Security (Barbarian Village) → 10K GP free reward. Plus teaches you account security basics. Full walkthrough here.

Mission 3.2: Skill Up Your Magic (30 min)

Why: Magic 55 unlocks High Alchemy — the single most important money spell in OSRS.

F2P method: Train Magic on cows/hill giants with Wind Bolt. Cost: ~5K GP for runes.

Member method: Quest rewards (The Knight's Sword, Witch's Potion) give big Magic XP. Then train on the Ardougne Zoo splashing method.

💡 Magic Shortcut

Magic 25 → teleport spells open the map. Magic 37 → teleport to Varrock, Lumbridge, Falador. Magic 55 → High Alchemy. Each milestone transforms how you play. Read our full Magic Training Guide.

👉 Ready for Day 4?

💰 Day 4 — Build Your Money Machine

Time: 60-90 min · Goal: 500K+ GP · Key: Establish 100K/hr method

Mission 4.1: F2P — Tea & Wine Method (60 min)

  • Location: Varrock East Bank + Brimhaven (requires 10gp ship)
  • Buy from Karamja General Store: 2GP each → sell on GE for 500-800 GP each
  • Profit: 250-400 GP per item, 28 slots = 7K-11K per trip
  • Easier alternative: Sell cowhides from Hill Giants — 150K GP/hr at your level

Mission 4.1 (Member): Start Slayer (60 min)

  • Talk to Vannaka in Edgeville → get your first task
  • Slayer is the #1 skill to train — you earn combat XP + money at the same time
  • Read our Slayer 1-99 Guide for exact block list and task priorities

Mission 4.2: Unlock Your First Money Milestone (30 min)

F2P: Get Magic 55 → learn High Alchemy → alch your own items for profit
Member: Start Slayer → earn 200K-500K GP/hr by level 50

🎯 Day 4 Target Check

✔️ 500K+ GP in bank
✔️ Established a consistent money method
✔️ Know your next gear upgrade path
✔️ Members: Active slayer task

👉 Ready for Day 5?

🎓 Day 5 — Reach Quest Cape Prerequisites

Time: 90 min · Goal: Unlock Fairy Rings · Bank: 800K+ GP

Mission 5.1: Complete the Key Mid-Game Quests

Members priority (do these in order):

  1. Tree Gnome Village — 20 min, unlocks spirit trees
  2. The Grand Tree — 30 min, massive XP rewards + glider network
  3. Lost City — 20 min, unlocks Dragon Dagger (p++) + Zanaris
  4. Fairy Tale Part I — 30 min, start of fairy ring access
  5. Fairy Tale Part II (partial) — Don't need all skill reqs yet, just unlock fairy rings

Mission 5.1 (F2P): Get 40+ Combat Stats

Grind your combat stats to 40+ at Hill Giants. At this level you can tackle nearly all F2P content:

  • Attack 40 → Rune Scimitar (best F2P weapon)
  • Defense 40 → Rune armor pieces
  • Strength 40 → Max hit increases significantly

Full guide: F2P Combat Training Guide

🧭 Why Fairy Rings Matter

Fairy rings are a teleport network that connects 40+ locations across Gielinor. Unlocking them is the single biggest quality-of-life improvement in the game. You'll use them every single play session from now on. Fairy Ring Guide

🎯 Day 5 Target Check

✔️ Members: Fairy Rings unlocked
✔️ F2P: 40+ all combat stats + Rune Scimitar
✔️ Bank: 800K+ GP
✔️ Quest Cape progress started

👉 Ready for Day 6?

🏹 Day 6 — Ranged Is Your Money Printer

Time: 60-90 min · Goal: Ranged 50+ · Bank: 1.5M+ GP

Mission 6.1: Ranged Training to 50 (60 min)

F2P method: Minotaurs (Stronghold of Security) — drop iron arrows + coins

  • Collect their arrow drops and reuse them — near-infinite free training
  • Ranged 40 → Green d'hide body (equippable!)

Member method: Crabs or Nightmare Zone — fastest XP in game

  • Use MSB (Magic Shortbow) + Rune Arrows
  • Ranged 50 → Broad bolts unlocked (cheapest ammo)

Mission 6.2: Fletching — AFK Money (30 min)

One of the easiest money makers for beginners:

  • Buy arrow shafts + feather → fletch headless arrows → sell on GE
  • Profit: ~100-200 GP profit per 1000 arrows
  • Can do while watching TV — completely AFK
  • Full guide: Fletching Money Making Guide

🏆 Milestone: Ranged 50 + Full Green D'Hide + Magic Shortbow

At this point you can start killing Ogresses (F2P) or Green Dragons (Member). Both drop 200K-500K GP/hr in consistent loot.

🎯 Day 6 Target Check

✔️ Ranged 50+
✔️ Full Green D'Hide equipped
✔️ Magic Shortbow + Rune Arrows
✔️ Bank: 1.5M+ GP
✔️ Fletching 20+ for AFK passive income

👉 Ready for the Final Day?

👑 Day 7 — Your First Boss Kill!

Time: 60-90 min · Goal: Kill Your First Boss · Bank: 3M+ GP (F2P) / 10M+ GP (Member)

Mission 7.1: F2P — Kill Ogress Warriors (60 min)

Location: Corsair Cove Resource Dungeon (requires 30 Ranged + 30 Defense)

  • Drops: Rune full helm, rune kite shield, adamant items, 10K+ coins per kill
  • Profit: 150K-250K GP/hr
  • Best place to earn your Bond money (~8M GP for 14 days membership)
  • Strategy: Safe spot with Ranged on the eastern side

Mission 7.1 (Member): Kill Obor / Bryophyta (30 min)

Your first real boss kills. Read the full strategies:

  • Obor Strategy Guide — drops giant key from hill giants
  • Bryophyta Strategy Guide — moss giant boss with valuable essence drop

Mission 7.2 (Member Upgrade): Kill the Barrows Brothers (45 min)

The ultimate Week 1 achievement: Kill your first Barrows run.

  • Requirements: 43 Prayer (Protect from Melee), Ranged 50+, Magic 40+
  • Gear: Iban's Staff + Rune Crossbow + Monk's Robe top/bottom
  • Profit: 1M-2M GP/hr on average (barrows pieces sell for 500K-3M each)
  • Full guide: Barrows Beginner Guide

FAQ — Common Roadmap Questions

Can I complete this roadmap as a casual player (1 hour/day)?

Yes! The estimates above assume 60-90 min per day. If you play less, just extend each day. The key isn't speed — it's consistency. Even 30 min/day will get you through this roadmap in 2 weeks instead of 1.

I'm stuck on Day X. What should I do?

Re-read the linked guides for that section. If you're still stuck, join our community (links in the footer) or check the OSRS Wiki for specific monster/quest mechanics. Most importantly: don't skip days — every day builds on the previous one.

Should I buy membership first or follow the F2P track?

If you have $7.99 available, buy membership on Day 1. Members content is 10x faster and gives you access to better training spots. If you want to earn membership via Bond, follow the F2P track diligently — by Day 7 you'll have 3M+ GP and be well on your way to the 8M needed for a Bond.

Can I skip days if I'm already ahead?

Absolutely! Use the Quick Reference Table above to find your current position and jump to the next uncompleted day.

I want a different playstyle (Ironman, Skill Pure, etc.)

This roadmap is designed for main accounts. Ironman accounts require completely different early game strategies. We're working on an Ironman-specific roadmap — subscribe to our newsletter to get notified when it launches.
