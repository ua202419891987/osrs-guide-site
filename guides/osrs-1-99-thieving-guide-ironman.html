OSRS 1-99 Thieving Guide for Ironman 2026 — Pyramid Plunder, Ardougne Knights & Master Farmers

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 12 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🎯 Skill Training — Ironman Optimized — Updated June 2026

Thieving is one of the most important skills for ironman accounts. It provides raw coins for daily expenses, seeds for Herblore training, gems for Crafting, and the Pharaoh's Sceptre for efficient teleportation. This guide covers the optimal ironman path from level 1 to 99 Thieving in 2026.

1. Why Ironmen Need Thieving

Unlike main accounts, ironmen cannot buy supplies from other players. Thieving bridges the resource gap by providing:

🔑 Priority Unlock — Rogues' Outfit: Get the full Rogues' outfit from Rogues' Den as early as possible (requires level 50 Thieving + Agility). It doubles ALL coin drops from pickpocketing. Without it, you earn half the GP from 1-99. Expect 2-4 hours to obtain the full set.

Pyramid Plunder Run Strategy

  1. Enter the pyramid — you have 5 minutes per run.
  2. Run straight to the highest room available — do NOT stop in lower rooms.
  3. Loot urns in your highest room first, then work down through rooms 5-8.
  4. Always loot golden chests in every room — they have the Pharaoh's Sceptre drop (1/1,000 per chest).
  5. When time is almost up, rush to the exit or let the timer expire.
  6. Bank, reset, repeat. Each run is self-contained — there's no penalty for failing.
🏺 Pharaoh's Sceptre — Keep It: The sceptre has a 1/1,000 drop rate from golden chests and sarcophagi. Ironmen should KEEP the first sceptre — it teleports directly to the Pyramid (replacing the need for the long desert walk) and to the Ancient Pyramid for switching spellbooks. If you get a second, use it for the Occult Altar in your POH.

The Ardougne Knight method becomes no-fail at level 95 with the Hard Ardougne Diary. At that point, you can literally click once and AFK until the Knight despawns. This makes it the best AFK Thieving method for ironmen who want to train while doing other things.

🔄 Splash World Strategy: On worlds where players are splashing on the Ardougne Knight (trapping it in one spot), you can stand in one tile and spam-click for hours. You'll never need to move or chase the NPC. Check the "OSRS Knight Splash" worlds on community trackers — this is the #1 AFK Thieving method for ironmen.

6. Common Mistakes Ironmen Make

  1. Skipping Rogues' Outfit. Training to 99 without it costs you 6-8M GP in lost coin doubling. Get it at 50 Thieving before any serious pickpocketing.
  2. Not banking seeds from Master Farmers early. Every ranarr seed you skip now is a Prayer potion you can't make later. Start at level 38 — even 15 minutes a day adds up.
  3. Staying at Pyramid Plunder past level 91 if you need GP. If you need coins for Construction, Smithing, or Miscellania, switch to Ardougne Knights at 91+ — the XP is slightly slower but the GP is irreplaceable.
  4. Alching/Selling the Pharaoh's Sceptre. Ironmen cannot buy one back. The teleport utility is worth more than any GP value you could alch it for.
  5. Ignoring Ardougne Diary. The Medium diary gives a 10% pickpocket boost. The Hard diary increases this to 12.5%. Complete at least Medium before grinding Knights.
  6. Not using Dodgy Necklaces. Craft a batch of 20-30 at once and use them whenever pickpocketing. The 25% stun avoidance adds significant XP/hr.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the fastest Thieving XP for ironmen?

A: Pyramid Plunder from level 71 onwards, reaching 280,000 XP/hr at 91+. From 91-99, Ardougne Knights are also ~250,000 XP/hr but much more AFK and provide 12-15M GP.

Q: How many ranarr seeds can ironmen get from 38-99 Thieving?

A: Approximately 160-220 ranarr seeds if you regularly pickpocket Master Farmers alongside your training. Dedicated Master Farmer grinding from 70-99 can yield 300+ ranarr seeds and 150+ snapdragon seeds.

Q: Is Pyramid Plunder worth it for ironmen before level 71?

A: It's decent from 51+, but Ardougne Knights are better GP at those levels. Start Pyramid Plunder at 71 when room 7 unlocks — that's when the XP rates surpass Knights significantly. Before 71, prioritize Knights for the coin income.

Q: How do I get food for early Thieving on an ironman?

A: Cake stalls in East Ardougne. Steal cakes from level 5-25 Thieving and bank them. Each cake heals 12 HP in 3 bites. Alternatively, the Warriors' Guild food shop sells potatoes and stews for GP earned from Thieving.

🎯 Thieving Cape — Ironman Achievement

The Thieving skillcape is one of the most practical capes for ironmen. Its perk (10% increased pickpocket success) stacks with Ardougne Diary and makes Master Farmers significantly more efficient for continued seed gathering. Combined with the 10-15M GP you'll earn along the way, Thieving is arguably the most impactful non-combat skill for ironman progression.
