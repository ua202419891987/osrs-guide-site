OSRS Achievement Diary Guide 2026 — Best Rewards & Optimal Completion Order

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 14 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
📜 Quests & Unlocks — Updated June 2026

Achievement Diaries are your account's most important unlock system. The right diary reward at the right time can transform your entire gameplay experience — free teleports, doubled resources, and passive income. This guide ranks every diary reward by value and gives you the optimal completion order.

3. Optimal Completion Order — Tier by Tier

Easy (all 12): Complete during early game. 5-10 hours total. Do Ardougne FIRST — the cloak teleport helps complete all other diaries faster.

Medium (prioritize): Lumbridge → Varrock → Desert → Karamja → Kandarin. ~15-25 hours. These five give the most impactful account upgrades.

Hard (long-term): Morytania FIRST (double Barrows runes = millions of GP). Then Fremennik → Kandarin. 30-50 hours. Hard diaries require base 70+ skills.

Elite (endgame): Start with Lumbridge (lowest requirements of all Elites) → Fremennik → Kourend. 100+ hours. Base 85-95+ skills needed. This is your endgame grind.

4. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is Ardougne Easy rated #1?

A: The Ardougne Cloak teleports you directly next to a fairy ring AND Spirit Tree. This single item gives you access to the entire fairy ring network — which connects to nearly every major location in Gielinor. It's the single biggest QoL upgrade for a 20-minute diary completion.

Q: Are achievement diaries worth it on an Ironman?

A: They're ESSENTIAL on an Ironman. The free resources, teleports, and bonuses (double ore, noted bones, free alchs) are exponentially more valuable when you can't use the GE. Many Ironmen rush specific diaries specifically for the resource multipliers.

📜 The Best Account Upgrade System in OSRS

Achievement Diaries are the quiet backbone of every great OSRS account. They don't give XP — they give something better: permanent, account-wide bonuses that make everything else faster, easier, and more profitable. Do Ardougne Easy tonight. You'll wonder why you waited so long.
