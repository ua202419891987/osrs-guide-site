🤔 DURADEL VS NIEVE

Duradel assigns more Gargoyles, Nechryael, and Abyssal Demons per task. Nieve/Steve is better for cannon tasks (Dagannoth, Kalphite). For GP, always Duradel. For pure XP to 95, Nieve is slightly faster.

4. Optimal Block List (Minimum 4, Maximum 6 Slots)

Your block list is the most important part of profitable Slayer. Every blocked task increases your chance of rolling S/A-tier tasks by removing junk from the task pool.

Block These (in priority order):

PriorityTaskWhy Block It
#1HellhoundsZero GP. Terrible XP. Only good for Cerberus at 91 — block until then.
#2Spiritual CreaturesSlow kills, bad drops, annoying to reach. Total skip.
#3Iron DragonsSlow kills, bad loot, require antifire. Block all metal dragons.
#4Steel DragonsSame as Iron Dragons but worse. Block all metal dragons.
#5Black DemonsSlow, bad GP. Only worth it for Demonic Gorillas (MM2). Block if you haven't done MM2.
#6DrakesAnnoying mechanics, mediocre drops. High weight from Duradel.

🔑 UNLOCK CERBERUS AT 91?

Unblock Hellhounds. Cerberus is 2.5M/hr at 91 Slayer. The prim crystal alone makes it worth every hellhound task you had to suffer through before 91.

Always Skip These (using points, not block slots):

  • Waterfiends — Bad GP, annoying to kill, high weight.
  • Kalphite (normal) — Only worth it if cannoning in task-only area.
  • Elves — Teleport crystals are okay but kills are too slow.
  • Cave Kraken (normal) — Just do the boss. Skip normal ones.

FAQ

Q: How much GP will I make from 70-95 Slayer?

Following this guide with optimal block/extension list: approximately 150M-350M GP from 70-95, depending on luck with rare drops (primordial crystals, hydra claws, imbued hearts).

Q: Should I barrage or melee Abyssal Demons?

Barrage. In the Kourend Catacombs, barraging stacked Abyssal Demons gives 85K Slayer XP/hr and ~1M GP/hr. Melee is slower XP and comparable GP. The only reason to melee is if you're very low on runes.

Q: Is the Slayer Helm (i) really necessary?

YES. It gives +15% ranged and magic accuracy/damage on task. Without it, you're leaving 15% DPS on the table for every single task. Get it before anything else at 400 Slayer Points.

Q: Should I use Konar for Brimstone keys?

No. Konar assigns specific locations, which often force you into bad spots. Brimstone keys add ~50K per task. Duradel's better task weighting gives you more S/A-tier tasks, which easily out-earn Brimstone keys.

Final Tips

  • Get the Rune Pouch ASAP. It holds 3 types of runes and saves 2 inventory slots. Critical for bursting tasks. Buy with Slayer points or LMS points.
  • Use the Catacombs of Kourend for everything. Bursting, ancient shards, totem pieces, and the prayer-restore effect from bonecrusher necklace make it the best Slayer spot in the game.
  • Bonecrusher + Dragonbone Necklace combo. If you have the Morytania Hard Diary, this combo restores prayer for every bone buried. Infinite prayer on catacombs tasks.
  • Don't skip your first Gargoyle task. Buy the Rock Hammer, unlock auto-smash with points, and enjoy the easiest 1.8M/hr in the game.
  • Bank your loot. Gargoyles and Nechryael drop so many alchables that you'll fill your inventory. Bring alch runes (nature + fire) and alch on the spot. Use Herb Sack + Gem Bag for the rest.

Ready to Print GP With Slayer?

Set your block list. Extend Nechryael and Dust Devils. Grab your Slayer Helm (i) and head to Duradel. 150M+ awaits on the road to 95.
