7. Advanced Ship Optimization Tips

  1. Sync Wind Catcher + Horizon's Lure. The Wind Catcher facility (+5%) stacks with Horizon's Lure (+2.5% from charting all zones) for a total +7.5% permanent Sailing XP boost.
  2. Don't overpay for Cannons early. Ship combat is irrelevant until level 85+ Sailing. Install Cannons at 20 but don't use a facility slot for them until needed.
  3. Rune > Dragon Hook upgrade timing. The jump from Rune (75) to Dragon (87) Hook costs 250K for only +12K XP/hr. If GP is tight, stay on Rune Hook until 92+.
  4. Always keep 1 facility slot free. The Sloop's 3 slots fill fast. Keep one flexible for swapping between Hook/Net/Catcher.
  5. Crew loyalty bonus. Crew members who've been with you for 10+ hours gain a hidden loyalty multiplier — up to +20% bonus effectiveness.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I have multiple boats?

A: No. You own one boat that you upgrade. When you buy the Skiff or Sloop, it replaces your previous boat. All installed facilities transfer automatically.

Q: Can I uninstall a facility to free a slot?

A: Yes. Visit any shipwright at a major port. Uninstalling is free; reinstalling costs the original price again. Plan facility slots carefully.

Q: Do Lookouts stack with Salvagers for wreck finding?

A: Yes! Lookouts increase rare wreck spawn chance. Salvagers increase loot. 3 Salvagers + 2 Lookouts on a Sloop is the optimal AFK Salvaging setup.

🚢 Set Sail with the Perfect Ship

Your boat and crew are the foundation of everything you do in Sailing. With the right upgrades at the right time — Skiff at 15, Sloop at 50, Wind Catcher at 35 — you maximize every hour on the water. This is one of the cheapest skills to fully equip, so don't hesitate to upgrade.
