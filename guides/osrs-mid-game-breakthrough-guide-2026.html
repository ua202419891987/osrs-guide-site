OSRS Mid-Game Breakthrough Guide 2026 — Stop Being Stuck & Progress Faster

📅 Published: June 6, 2026  |  ⏱️ 14 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🧱 Progression — Updated June 2026

You have Barrows gear, base 70-80 stats, and a few million GP. You know you need to get to raids — but everything feels impossibly expensive. This guide bridges that gap.

🧱 Mid-Game Breakthrough — Quick Reference

Goal:~50M bank → raid-readyTimeframe:2-4 weeks
#1 Mistake:Buying marginal upgrades (Bandos, Prims)#1 Rule:Max hit > everything

1. Why You Feel Stuck

You own Barrows gear, a Whip, maybe a Blowpipe. 5-15M in the bank. Every upgrade seems to cost 20M+. You keep buying the wrong things. You hop between bosses without mastering any. The solution isn't "grind more GP" — it's spending your GP smarter.

7. FAQ

Q: Sell bank for BowFa + Crystal?

A: Yes — if you can afford the full set (~160M). It's the single biggest power spike. Wait until 180M+ for supplies.

Q: Slayer or bossing for GP?

A: Bossing for raw GP, Slayer for long-term progression. Alternate between a task and a boss session.

Q: Can't beat CG?

A: Normal to die 20-50 times. Start with Regular Gauntlet first. If still stuck after 50+, pivot to Vorkath or Zulrah.

🧱 You're Not Stuck — You Just Need a Plan

Stop buying marginal upgrades. Focus on max hit: Blowpipe → Berserker Ring → Anguish → Fang → Rigour → Blood Moon. In 4 weeks of focused play, you'll go from feeling stuck to confidently running raids. The gap isn't as big as it looks. 💪
