3. The Ectofuntus — Step by Step

The Ectofuntus is a large temple north of Port Phasmatys. It requires significant running between stations, but the 4× XP multiplier makes it worth the effort.

  1. Travel to Port Phasmatys: Walk through Morytania (requires Priest in Peril). Alternatively, use an Ectophial (obtained from completing Ghosts Ahoy).
  2. Enter the Ectofuntus dungeon: Go down the trapdoor just outside the temple.
  3. Grind bones into bonemeal: Use your bones on the Bone Grinder in the basement. You'll need to click each bone individually — this is the slowest step.
  4. Collect slime: Use an empty bucket on the green slime pool (also in the basement).
  5. Go upstairs: Climb the stairs to the Ectofuntus altar on the ground floor.
  6. Offer bonemeal: Use the bonemeal on the altar. Each offering consumes 1 bonemeal + 1 bucket of slime and grants 4× the bone's base XP. You also receive Ectotokens as a bonus.

4. Farming Big Bones from Hill Giants (Free Method)

If you want to train Prayer without spending any GP, kill Hill Giants for their Big Bones. This is slower but completely free — and you earn combat XP at the same time.

At Edgeville Dungeon, enter via the door near the Edgeville bank (requires a brass key, obtained from the Edgeville Dungeon itself). Hill Giants are level 28 — very easy to kill even with low combat stats. Each kill drops 1 Big Bone 100% of the time, plus occasional limpwurt roots (sell for extra GP).

7. Money-Saving Tips

  • Always use the Ectofuntus, never bury. The difference is 4× more XP per bone. Burying a Big Bone gives 15 XP; the Ectofuntus gives 60 XP. Over hundreds of bones, this saves millions of GP.
  • Stock up during off-peak hours. Big Bone prices on the GE drop when fewer players are online. Buy in bulk on weekday mornings for the best prices.
  • Kill Hill Giants for training synergy. If you need combat XP anyway, kill Hill Giants. You get melee/ranged XP, Big Bones for Prayer, and limpwurt roots for extra GP.
  • Collect Ectotokens. Ectofuntus offerings give Ectotokens (5 per offering). These can be exchanged for Prayer equipment or used for free teleports with the Ectophial.
  • Stop at 43 (unless PKing). The cost from 43 to 99 Prayer in F2P is massive (~38M GP remaining). Unless you specifically need Smite for PvP, invest that GP elsewhere.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the cheapest way to train Prayer in F2P?

A: Killing Hill Giants in Edgeville Dungeon for free Big Bones, then using them at the Ectofuntus for 4x XP. This costs 0 GP (only time). If buying bones, Big Bones at the Ectofuntus costs ~39M GP to reach 99 — dramatically cheaper than burying directly (~156M GP). Big Bones are the best XP/GP ratio for F2P at 0.33 XP per GP spent.

Q: How does the Ectofuntus work for Prayer training?

A: Located north of Port Phasmatys, the Ectofuntus gives 4× bone XP. Grind bones into bonemeal in the basement, collect slime in a bucket, then offer both at the altar upstairs. Each offering gives 4× the base burying XP. Big Bones give 60 XP instead of 15. The process is slower than burying but 4× more efficient per bone.

Q: How much would 99 Prayer cost in F2P using Big Bones?

A: Level 99 requires 13,034,431 XP. Ectofuntus with Big Bones (60 XP each): ~217,241 bones at ~180 GP each = ~39M GP total. Burying Big Bones directly: ~868,962 bones = ~156M GP. The Ectofuntus saves you 117M GP. Farming Hill Giants for free Big Bones eliminates the cost entirely but requires ~1,086 hours of Ectofuntus offerings.

Q: What Prayer level should I aim for in F2P?

A: Level 43 is the most important milestone — it unlocks all three overhead protection prayers (Protect from Melee/Magic/Missiles). This is sufficient for all F2P PvM. Level 55 (Smite) is only useful for PvP. Level 70 (Piety) is members-only. Most F2P players stop at 43, which only requires ~50K XP — achievable with just ~830 Big Bones at the Ectofuntus.

🙏 Pray Smarter, Not Poorer

Prayer doesn't have to drain your bank. The Ectofuntus turns every Big Bone into 60 XP instead of 15 — a 4× multiplier that saves you over 100M GP on the road to 99. For most F2P players, the smart play is hitting 43 Prayer (unlocking all overhead prayers) and stopping there. But if you're going all the way, the Ectofuntus is your best friend.

Grind those bones. Your prayer points (and your wallet) will thank you.
