OSRS Pest Control & Void Knight Guide 2026 — Full Void Set & Elite Upgrade

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 12 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🛡️ Quest / Unlock — Updated June 2026

Void Knight equipment is one of the most powerful untradeable armor sets in OSRS. The ranged set is BIS at Vorkath and Zulrah. This guide covers the fastest point farming strategy, which combat bracket to use, the exact point costs for every piece, and how to upgrade to Elite Void.

🛡️ Pest Control — Quick Reference

Location:Port Sarim dock → Void Knights' OutpostWorld:344 (official PC world)
Full Void Cost:850 pointsElite Upgrade:+200 points (1,050 total)
Best Bracket:Medium (70-99 combat)Points/Game:3 (Medium) / 4 (Veteran)

1. How Pest Control Works

  1. Take the boat from Port Sarim to the Void Knights' Outpost
  2. Choose a boat based on your combat level: Novice (40-69), Medium (70-99), Veteran (100+)
  3. Each game: Defend the Void Knight from waves of pests while destroying 4 portals
  4. Win condition: All 4 portals destroyed before Void Knight dies
  5. Reward: Pest Control points (3/4 per win depending on boat) + Combat XP
  6. Spend points at the Void Knight equipment shop

3. Fastest Point Farming Strategy

  1. Use World 344 — Always packed. Games start every 30 seconds.
  2. Medium boat (70-99 combat) is optimal — 3 points per 2-3 minute game = ~60-90 pts/hr.
  3. Attack portals aggressively. Don't defend the Knight unless the shield drops below 50%. Killing portals wins games faster.
  4. Open gates first. Run to the eastern gate and open it immediately. This lets NPCs flow through and makes portal access faster.
  5. Use Dragon Dagger specs on portals — 4x spec bar on a portal = massive contribution points.

5. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Should I get Elite Void or just regular Void?

A: Get regular Void first (850 pts), then Elite upgrade later (200 pts). Regular Void is already extremely powerful. The Elite upgrade requires Hard Combat Achievements which is a significant time investment. Get Void → use it at Vorkath/Zulrah to make GP → come back for Elite when you're ready.

Q: Which helmet should I get first?

A: Ranger Helm first — no question. Void Range is used at more bosses than any other Void set. Melee Helm second (for Corp). Mage Helm last — it's the least useful.

🛡️ Worth Every Point

Void armor is an account-changing unlock. It transforms your DPS at some of the most profitable bosses in OSRS. The 6-10 hour grind seems long, but every hour at Vorkath with Void Range pays it back many times over. Grab your Dragon Dagger and get on World 344.
