OSRS Corrupted Gauntlet Guide 2026 — Budget Setup & Strategy

📅 Published: June 6, 2026  |  ⏱️ 13 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
👹 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

Master the Corrupted Gauntlet without max stats or billions in gear. Tier 1 & Tier 2 armor prep, Hunllef mechanics, prayer switching, and the path to 5M/hr profit — all with zero supply cost.

⚔️ CG — Quick Reference

1. How the Gauntlet Works

Enter with nothing. You have 7:30 minutes to gather, craft, and prepare. Craft Tier 1/2 armor + two Tier 3 weapons + 20-25 paddlefish. Fight Hunllef. Win = loot. Die = lose nothing but time.

🎯 Why CG Is So Good: Zero supply cost, zero death cost, zero gear requirements. Die 100 times = zero GP lost. Once you learn it, you print money. The best mid-game money maker.

3. Tier 2 Prep (Safe — Beginners)

12 each of Bark/Linum/Ore + ~520 shards. Tighter prep time but Hunllef hits much softer. Start with T2. Only switch to T1 when you rarely die.

4. Hunllef Boss Mechanics

MechanicStrategy
🔄 Prayer SwitchHunllef switches every 6 attacks. Count: 1-2-3-4-5-6 → switch prayer.
🌪️ TornadoesRun, don't attack. Straight lines along edges, diagonal cuts to escape. #1 killer.
🟦 Floor HazardsMove off blue tiles immediately. Movement > DPS.
🛡️ Protection PrayerHunllef protects from your last style. Switch weapons every 6 attacks.

5. Tornado Survival Formula

  1. STOP attacking. DPS doesn't matter if dead.
  2. Run straight lines along edges, cut diagonally to lose them.
  3. Use true tile indicator (RuneLite).
  4. Eat to full HP when tornadoes spawn.
  5. Final phase (<25% HP): 4 tornadoes + floor. Attack only in gaps.

7. FAQ

Q: How many deaths before first kill?

A: 20-50 deaths. Nearly everyone dies 20+ times. After 50 KC you'll rarely die. This is normal.

Q: Regular or Corrupted?

A: Regular first (5-10 KC). 10-min timer, softer boss. Switch to CG when consistent.

Q: Bow + Staff or Halberd?

A: Bow + Staff for learners. Range distance during tornadoes is safer. Halberd + Piety for higher DPS once experienced.

⚔️ Best Zero-Risk Money Maker

Zero supplies, zero deaths, zero gear required, 5M/hr profit. CG is hard to learn but once mastered, it's one of the most reliable money makers. Embrace the deaths — they're free. Keep sending runs.
