1. Getting Started — Quests &Requirements

Before you begin Hunter, a few quests and items will significantly speed up your training:

Recommended Quests

Quest Reward / Why It Matters
Natural History Quiz (no quest) 1,000 XP — skips levels 1–9 instantly. Talk to Orlando Smith in Varrock Museum basement.
Eagles' Peak Required to catch Chinchompas and use box traps. Complete this early.
Bone Voyage Unlocks Fossil Island — required for Birdhouse runs and Herbiboar.
Monkey Madness II Unlocks Maniacal Monkeys in Kruk's Dungeon (AFK method at 60+).
Children of the Sun Unlocks Carnivorous (Red) Chinchompas in Varlamore.

Useful Items &Equipment

  • Noose wand — Required to catch many creatures (Kebbit burrows etc.)
  • Ring of pursuit — Reveals tunnels when chasing kebbits;big DPS gain
  • Stamina potions — Essential for Razor-backed Kebbits
  • Digsite pendant — Fastest teleport to Fossil Island for Birdhouse runs
  • Varrock teleport / fairy ring — Standard mobility for reaching hunter areas
  • Hunter's rumour tracker — Unlocked after a few rumours at Hunter Guild

2. Birdhouse Runs — Best Passive XP (All Levels)

Birdhouse runs are the cornerstone of efficient Hunter training. Each run takes only 73–90 seconds every 50 minutes, giving you a huge amount of passive XP with minimal time investment. Always do Birdhouse runs alongside your active training method.

🏠 Setup: You need a Digsite pendant (teleport to Fossil Island), a hammer, a chisel, 4 logs of appropriate type, 4 clockworks (make at Construction bench), and 40 seeds (hops or low-level farming seeds work perfectly).

💡 Pro Tip: Birdhouses also drop bird nests containing seeds, rings, and sometimes a Ravenous locust (rare). The nests alone can be worth 300K–500K GP per hour of actual playtime at higher levels. Always use the highest-tier birdhouse you can access.

3. Low Level Training (Levels 1–43)

Levels 1–9: Natural History Quiz (Free XP!)

Don't touch Hunter traps until you've done this. Head to the basement of the Varrock Museum and speak with Orlando Smith. Answer the quiz questions about creatures to receive 1,000 Hunter XP — instantly jumping you from level 1 to level 9.

Levels 9–15: Feldip Weasels

  • Location: Feldip Hunter area (fairy ring AKS)
  • XP rate: ~13,000 XP/hr
  • Items: Noose wand, Ring of pursuit
  • Note: Low XP but required stepping stone. Rush through quickly.

Levels 15–21: Ruby Harvests (Butterflies)

  • Location: Piscatoris hunter area
  • XP rate: Up to 20,000 XP/hr (2 traps)
  • Items: Butterfly net, butterfly jar

Levels 21–39: Red Crabs — Pandemonium

  • Location: The Pandemonium (partial quest completion required, 10 Construction)
  • XP rate: ~31,000 XP/hr (2 traps)
  • Items: Hammer, saw, 2 planks, 4 nails, 2 buckets, fish offcuts

Levels 39–43: Embertailed Jerboas

  • Location: Locus Oasis (fairy ring AJP), partial Eagles' Peak required
  • XP rate: ~50,000 XP/hr (3 traps)

4. Mid Level Training (Levels 43–73)

Levels 43–57: Falconry — Spotted Kebbits

One of the most click-friendly mid-level methods. Rent a falcon for 500 GP and catch kebbits across the Piscatoris falconry area.

  • Location: Piscatoris falconry area (east of fairy ring AKQ)
  • Important: Weapon, shield, and glove slots must be empty
  • XP rate: 60,000–70,000 XP/hr (Spotted kebbits)
  • At 57+: switch to Dark kebbits → 75,000–80,000 XP/hr
  • At 69+: Dashing kebbits → 85,000–95,000 XP/hr

Levels 49–73: Razor-backed Kebbits ⭐ Best Active Method Pre-73

The best XP for this level range if you're willing to run a lot. Razor-backed Kebbits give excellent XP rates but require constant movement.

Method XP/hr
Solo (no stamina potions) ~100,000
With stamina potions + weight reduction ~130,000–140,000

Items needed: Noose wand, Ring of pursuit, stamina potions, graceful outfit or weight-reducing gear.

Levels 60–67: Red Salamanders

  • Location: South of Ourania Cave entrance
  • XP rate: 80,000–105,000 XP/hr (no quests required)
  • Items: 5 ropes, 5 small fishing nets (in inventory)
  • Note: Good option if you want to skip Falconry and can't access Kebbits efficiently

Levels 67–73: Black Salamanders (Wilderness)

  • Location: Boneyard Hunter area (low-level Wilderness)
  • XP rate: 90,000–125,000 XP/hr
  • Items: 6 ropes, 6 small fishing nets
  • ⚠️ Warning: Wilderness area — risk of PK. Keep valuable items at home.

5. High Level Training (Levels 73–99)

Levels 72–99: Hunter's Rumours ⭐ Safest Fast Method

Introduced with the Hunter Guild, Rumours are a structured task system offering excellent XP without Wilderness risk. This is the recommended method for players who don't want to deal with PKers.

Hunter Level Rumour Tier XP/hr

99 Master ~250,000

Levels 73–99: Black Chinchompas ⚡ Fastest XP

The fastest Hunter XP in the game — but located in the Wilderness (level 32–36). Bring anti-PK gear and ideally a scout account to warn of incoming PKers.

⚠️ PK Warning: Black Chinchompas are in the Wilderness. This is a heavily PKed area. Never bring items you can't afford to lose. Always have a Royal seed pod or Amulet of glory for emergency teleport.

Levels 77–99: Rainbow Crabs

  • Requirements: 77 Hunter + 64 Sailing
  • Location: The Crown Jewel island
  • XP rate: 155,000 XP/hr (fixed — doesn't scale with level)
  • GP rate: ~625,000 GP/hr
  • Good option for players who have trained Sailing and want a safe non-Wilderness method

7. AFK Methods — Low Effort Options

Levels 80–99: Herbiboar ⭐ Best AFK Option

Herbiboar is the gold standard for low-effort Hunter training. You track a creature across Fossil Island's Mushroom Forest, occasionally clicking to follow the trail, and receive herbs as drops alongside Hunter XP.

✅ Requirements: 80 Hunter, 31 Herblore, completion of Bone Voyage.
Bonus: Also earns ~3,300 Herblore XP/hr. 1/6,500 chance of getting the Herbi pet.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the fastest way to train Hunter in OSRS?

Black Chinchompas in the Wilderness (level 73+) offer the highest XP rates: up to 265,000 XP/hr at level 99 with a scout account. For a no-Wilderness alternative, Hunter's Rumours at the Hunter Guild give 160,000–250,000 XP/hr.

Are Birdhouse runs worth doing?

Absolutely. Each run takes ~90 seconds of actual playtime every 50 minutes. At high levels using Magic/Redwood birdhouses, this gives 172,800–216,000 effective XP/hr for almost zero effort. Birdhouses also generate nests with seeds and rings worth 300K–500K GP/hr of play time.

How much money can you make training Hunter?

Black Chinchompas: 1.4M–2.5M GP/hr (levels 73–99). Red Chinchompas (Carnivorous): 291K–1.05M GP/hr. Herbiboar generates herbs worth roughly 200K–400K GP/hr depending on RNG. Birdhouses add 300K–500K in passive income.

What level do I need for Black Chinchompas?

Level 73 Hunter. However, you also need partial Eagles' Peak quest completion to use box traps. Bring anti-PK gear and a Royal seed pod for emergency teleport — the area is in level 32–36 Wilderness and heavily PKed.

What is the best low-level Hunter training?

Start with the Natural History Quiz in Varrock Museum (1,000 free XP to level 9). Then combine Birdhouse runs (passive) with active methods: Feldip Weasels (9–15) → Ruby Harvests (15–21) → Red Crabs (21–39) → Embertailed Jerboas (39–43) → Falconry (43+).
