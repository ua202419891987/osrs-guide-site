1. What Is Slayer & Why Do It?

Slayer is a combat skill where you kill specific monsters assigned by a Slayer Master. Each task gives Slayer XP (for the skill) plus regular Combat XP (Attack/Strength/Defence/Ranged/Magic) from the kills themselves. The real draw: Slayer tasks are extremely profitable. For an overview of all 23 skills and how they fit together, check the Skills Overview for Beginners 2026.

💰 Why Slayer Is the Best Money Maker for Mid-Level Players

  • 70+ Combat: 200K–400K GP/hour from task loot
  • 80+ Combat: 400K–700K GP/hour (Gargoyles, Nechryael)
  • 85+ Slayer: 800K–1.5M GP/hour (Abyssal Demons, Smoke Devils)
  • Combined XP: Train Slayer + Combat + Hitpoints simultaneously
  • Unlocks: Higher Slayer = better masters and boss tasks

Compare this to other money makers: mining iron (100K GP/hr) or cowhide collecting (50K GP/hr). Slayer is 2–10x more profitable while training combat. Start as soon as you reach 40 Combat — see the Combat Training Guide for Beginners 2026 for the fastest route there.

2. Slayer Requirements & When to Start

You need two things before starting Slayer:

① Complete "The Smuggler" Quest

This 5-minute tutorial quest unlocks the ability to receive Slayer tasks. Talk to The Smuggler in the Smuggler's Den (south of Falador, entrance at the cabbage patch). He assigns 5 goblins to kill, then you're ready.

② Meet the Combat Requirement

You can start at 20 Combat with Mazchna, but 40 Combat is strongly recommended for Vannaka — the first master with decent profit. Tasks below 40 Combat (goblins, cows, rats) give almost no loot. For optimal early-game combat training before Slayer, read the Hitpoints Training Guide 2026.

3. Slayer Master Guide — Which Master to Pick at Every Level

Choosing the right master at each stage dramatically affects your XP rate and profit. Here is the exact progression path from level 1 to 99 Slayer.

🎯 Level 1–20 Slayer: Vannaka (40 Combat)

Start with Vannaka at 40 Combat. His tasks include Hill Giants, Moss Giants, and Blue Dragons — all profitable and good combat XP. Get 20 Slayer here before switching.

🎯 Level 20–50 Slayer: Chaeldar (75 Combat)

Once you reach 75 Combat, switch to Chaeldar in Zanaris (requires Lost City quest). She assigns Turoth, Kurasks, and other profitable mid-level tasks. Complete 50 tasks here to unlock Slayer points for Slayer Helmet.

🎯 Level 50–85 Slayer: Nieve/Steve (85 Combat)

Nieve (or Steve after Monkey Madness II) is the best master for this range. Tasks include Gargoyles (75 Slayer), Nechryael (80 Slayer), and Abyssal Demons (85 Slayer). This is where Slayer becomes seriously profitable — expect 400K–800K GP/hr.

🎯 Level 85–99 Slayer: Duradel (100 Combat)

Duradel gives the best tasks for endgame Slayer: Smoke Devils (93 Slayer), Cerberus (91 Slayer), and Hydra (95 Slayer). At this stage, you can earn 800K–1.5M+ GP/hr while training to 99. For boss-specific strategies, see the Cerberus Boss Guide 2026.

6. Slayer Helmet & Essential Unlocks

The Slayer Helmet is the single most important unlock for Slayer. It provides +15% accuracy and damage against your current task. Here's how to get it and what other unlocks to prioritize. Check the Slayer Block & Skip List 2026 for a complete unlock strategy.

🔓 How to Make a Slayer Helmet

Step-by-Step

  1. Complete "Smoking Kills" quest — unlocks Slayer points
  2. Earn 400 Slayer points (~27 tasks with streak bonuses)
  3. Buy "Unlock Slayer Helmet" from any Slayer Master (400 points)
  4. Combine a Facemask + Earmuffs + Nose Peg + Spiny Helmet + Reinforced Goggles on the Slayer Helmet creation tool (bought for 1 GP)

⚫ Tasks to Block (Permanent Block)

Use your 5 block slots wisely. These tasks are consistently bad across all levels:

  • Steel Dragons: High defence, slow kills, mediocre drops — block immediately
  • Iron Dragons: Same as steel — high defence, time waste
  • Suqahs: Annoying mechanics, low profit, slow
  • Waterfiends: High defence, low value drops
  • Spiritual Creatures: Only if you don't have God Wars Dungeon gear
💡 Turael Skipping Trick

At high Slayer levels (Duradel), if you get a terrible task, go to Turael in Burthorpe and ask him to assign an easy task (5 goblins). Completing Turael's task "resets" your Duradel task slot without cancel cost. This is the best way to avoid expensive cancellations.

8. Slayer FAQs

What's the fastest way to 99 Slayer?

Use Duradel tasks (100+ Combat) with a Slayer Helmet, max gear, and burst/barrage tasks in multi-combat areas. The fastest tasks per hour are Smoke Devils, Nechryael, and Dust Devils using burst/barrage spells. Expect 60–90 hours from 75 to 99 Slayer with optimal methods.

Should I use Melee, Ranged, or Magic for Slayer?

Use all three based on the task. Melee is best for most tasks (higher DPS, cheaper). Ranged is best for monsters that are dangerous up close (Kurasks, Turoth). Magic (burst/barrage) is best for multi-combat tasks (Nechryael, Dust Devils, Smoke Devils). Always use the combat style the monster is weak to.

Can F2P players do Slayer?

Yes, but it's very limited. F2P Slayer only offers Turael with basic tasks (goblins, cows, rats) that give almost no XP or profit. For real Slayer progression, you need membership. Read the F2P to P2P Membership Guide 2026 for the transition plan.

How many Slayer points do I need for essential unlocks?

Minimum 700 points: 400 for Slayer Helmet + 300 for Slayer Ring. Ideally 1,100+ for Bigger & Badder (150) + Malevolent Masquerade (250) + Broader Fletching (300) + task extensions. You earn 15+ points per task with a 10-task streak bonus of 50 points.

What Prayer level should I have for Slayer?

43 Prayer minimum — Protect from Melee makes most tasks zero-food. 70 Prayer for Piety — significantly increases DPS. 74/77 for Rigour/Augury — best-in-slot for Ranged/Magic tasks. Read the Prayer Training Guide 2026 for leveling tips.

Should I use a cannon for Slayer?

Yes, for multi-combat tasks where monsters stack up (Dagannoths, Kalphites, Bloodveld, Trolls). A Dwarf Multicannon costs ~1K GP per 20 minutes to refill but doubles or triples your kill speed. For single-combat tasks (Gargoyles, Nechryael), the cannon is less effective — use burst/barrage instead.

Is Wilderness Slayer (Krystilia) worth it?

Krystilia tasks give 25% more Slayer points than regular masters. If you're comfortable in the Wilderness, tasks like Lava Dragons (200K–400K GP/hr) and Wilderness bosses are very profitable. However, PK risk is real. Start with Krystilia only if you have Wilderness experience and good escape methods.

What's the best Slayer task for ironman accounts?

For ironman, focus on tasks that drop important supplies: Gargoyles (rune alchables, cash), Nechryael (seeds, herbs, rune items), Abyssal Demons (whips, rune items), and Kurasks (seeds, herbs). See Ironman Beginner Guide 2026 for a full ironman Slayer strategy.

How do I make a Slayer Helmet (i)?

After making your Slayer Helmet, buy the "Malevolent Masquerade" unlock for 250 points. Then use your Slayer Helmet on a Black Mask (i) — the imbued black mask from Nightmare Zone. The resulting Slayer Helmet (i) retains the +15% bonus and can be further upgraded with a Slayer Helmet (i) kit from superior Slayer monsters.

What are superior Slayer monsters?

Superior monsters are rare, stronger versions of normal Slayer monsters that spawn randomly on task (unlock with "Bigger & Badder" for 150 points). They give 5x Slayer XP and drop exclusive items (Imbued Heart, Eternal Gem, Mist/Battlestaves). Always kill them when they spawn — the XP boost alone is worth it.

9. Final Tips — Faster 99 Slayer

  • Do Smoking Kills early. This quest unlocks Slayer point rewards — everything else builds on this. Complete it before task #10.
  • Maintain task streaks. After 10 tasks, you earn bonus points. After 50, the bonuses multiply. Never break a steak by canceling tasks unnecessarily.
  • Use Turael to skip bad Duradel tasks. At high levels, a Turael task reset costs zero points and preserves your streak. This is the #1 efficiency trick.
  • Always use your best combat style. Many monsters have specific weaknesses. Check the OSRS Wiki before starting a new task for the first time.
  • Alch valuable drops immediately. Bring nature runes and a fire staff to alch rune items, battlestaves, and other valuable drops on the spot. This saves banking trips.
  • Complete Morytania Hard Diary. The Bone Crusher from this diary turns bones into passive Prayer XP during Catacombs Slayer tasks.
  • Level Prayer to 43 before serious Slayer. Protect prayers make most tasks zero-food. The convenience alone is worth the training time.
