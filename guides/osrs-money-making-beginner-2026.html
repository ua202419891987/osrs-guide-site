1. Your First 100K GP — Day 1 Starter Methods

Every OSRS journey starts with zero GP. These methods require no skills, no quests, and no starting capital. Follow this order to build your first 100K GP in under 4 hours. If you are brand new, start with our OSRS New Player Guide 2026 to set up your account correctly first.

① Stronghold of Security — 10K GP (15 Minutes)

Located beneath Barbarian Village (north of Falador). Complete all four levels of simple mazes. Each level rewards GP — totalling 10,000 GP. No combat required, takes 10–15 minutes even for a level 3 account. This is your starter capital for everything else.

② Cowhide Tanning — 15K–25K GP/hr

Kill cows east of Lumbridge Castle. Collect cowhides (100% drop rate) and bones. Take the hides to the Tanner in Al Kharid (just south of Lumbridge, costs 1 GP per hide to tan). Sell the resulting Soft or Hard Leather on the Grand Exchange for ~200–300 GP each. At 100+ hides per hour, that's 15,000–25,000 GP/hr with zero requirements.

💡 Pro Tip: Pick Up Ground Hides

Many players train combat at the Lumbridge cow field and don't pick up the hides. Walk around collecting ground drops — you can fill an inventory in 2–3 minutes without fighting anything. This is actually faster than killing cows yourself.

③ Mining Iron Ore — 30K–50K GP/hr

At 15 Mining, mine iron ore at the Varrock East or West mine. Iron ore sells for ~150–200 GP each on the GE. With 200+ ores per hour, you earn 30,000–50,000 GP/hr while training Mining. This is the best AFK-friendly starter method. Combine this with the OSRS Mining Guide 2026 for optimal routes.

🎯 Best Starter Path: 0 → 100K GP

  1. Stronghold of Security → 10K GP (15 min)
  2. Cowhide tanning until 50K GP (~2 hours)
  3. Switch to Iron Ore mining or Hill Giants for the remaining 50K GP (~1 hour)
  4. Total: ~4 hours to your first 100K GP

🏆 Best F2P Method: Wine of Zamorak (100K–200K GP/hr)

With 33 Magic and the ability to cast Telegrab, you can collect Wine of Zamorak from the Chaos Temple (level 5 Wilderness, but the temple is safe). Each wine sells for ~1,500–2,000 GP on the GE. With 60–100 wines per hour, you earn 100,000–200,000 GP/hr — the best F2P rate by far.

⚠️ Safety Tip for Wine Collecting

The Chaos Temple is technically in the Wilderness, but the temple building itself is not a PvP zone. Monks inside will attack if you steal the wine, but they're level 7 — harmless. The real risk is other players waiting outside the temple. Bring a one-click teleport (Varrock teleport tab) and watch the minimap.

💪 Ogress Warriors — Best F2P Combat Money

After completing Dragon Slayer I (which gives massive combat XP), head to Corsair Cove. Ogress Warriors drop Rune items, coins, gems, and herbs. With 40+ Combat and full Rune armor, you can earn 60,000–100,000 GP/hr consistently. This is the best method to fund your first bond.

3. The F2P to P2P Transition — Getting Your First Bond

A bond costs ~12.8M GP in June 2026. This is your golden ticket — once you have membership, your GP/hr jumps from 100K to 500K+. Here is the exact roadmap to earn your first bond in F2P. Read the OSRS Bond Farming Strategy Guide for additional tips on sustaining membership.

🎯 Bond Roadmap: 0 → 12.8M GP

Phase 1: 0 → 100K GP (4 hours)
Stronghold of Security → Cowhide tanning → Mining iron ore. See Section 1 above.
Phase 2: 100K → 1M GP (10–15 hours)
Use your 100K GP to buy Rune armor. Kill Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon) for Big Bones. At 50K–80K GP/hr, this takes 10–15 hours of active play.
Phase 3: 1M → 5M GP (20–30 hours)
Unlock 33 Magic for Telegrab. Collect Wine of Zamorak at 100K–200K GP/hr. This cuts your bond time in half compared to Hill Giants. Alternatively, complete Dragon Slayer I and farm Ogress Warriors.
Phase 4: 5M → 12.8M GP (20–30 hours)
Continue with Wine of Zamorak + Ogress Warriors. With 100K+ GP/hr sustained, you can reach 12.8M GP in 20–30 more hours. Start building a GE flipping fund to accelerate the last stretch.

⏱️ Total Time: ~55–75 Hours of F2P Play

Yes, it takes time. But every hour you spend earning your first bond pays off 10x once you have membership. The alternative is paying $7.99 for one month — if you can afford it, buy membership directly and skip the F2P grind entirely. Your first month of P2P earnings will easily cover future bonds.

⚒️ Blast Furnace — 500K–700K GP/hr (Active)

The Blast Furnace in Keldagrim is the best early-game active money maker. Smelt steel bars at 30 Smithing (requires Ice Gloves from the Heroes' Quest or a bucket of water + Ice Gauntlets). Each steel bar takes 1 iron ore + 2 coal. With the Blast Furnace's reduced coal requirement (1 coal per iron), you profit ~500K–700K GP/hr selling steel bars on the GE.

How to Start Blast Furnace

Step 1: Complete The Giant Dwarf quest to access Keldagrim (30 min).
Step 2: Buy Ice Gloves from the Heroes' Guild (requires 53 Herblore or buy from GE).
Step 3: Bank at Keldagrim's Blast Furnace bank chest. Buy iron ore + coal from GE.
Step 4: Click the conveyor belt to dump ore, collect bars, smith them, and sell on GE.

✨ High Alchemy — 100K–200K GP/hr + Magic XP

At 55 Magic, cast High Level Alchemy on Yew Longbows. Each bow costs ~4K GP on the GE and alchs for ~3,900 GP. The profit is ~300 GP per alch plus 65 Magic XP. With 1,200 alchs per hour (manual) or more with mouse keys, you earn 100K–200K GP/hr while training Magic to 75+.

5. Passive Income Methods — Herb Runs & Birdhouses

Passive income is the secret to building wealth in OSRS without burning out. These methods take 3–5 minutes every hour and generate consistent profit while you train other skills. Every serious player runs herb runs and birdhouse runs. Learn how to set up your farm patches with the OSRS Farming Guide for Beginners 2026.

🌿 Herb Runs — 100K–300K+ GP per Run (80 min cycle)

Plant Ranarr seeds (or Toadflax if you have lower Farming) in 5+ herb patches. Each run takes ~3 minutes and yields 40–60 herbs worth 2,000+ GP each. With 5 patches at 32+ Farming, you earn 100,000–300,000 GP per run. Do 6–8 runs per day and you'll make 1M–2M GP daily with just 20 minutes of work.

🐦 Birdhouse Runs — 10K–30K GP per Run (50 min cycle)

Set up birdhouses on Fossil Island. Each run takes 2 minutes and yields bird nests worth 5K–15K GP plus passive Hunter XP. Run birdhouses on the 50-minute timer between your herb runs. This is the most time-efficient GP method in the game — 2 minutes of work for 10K–30K GP.

🌾 Giant Seaweed — 50K–100K GP per Run (70 min cycle)

Once you have 23 Farming (from quests), plant giant seaweed spores underwater at Fossil Island. Each run takes 3 minutes and yields 30–50 giant seaweed worth ~1,000 GP each. This is excellent passive income that most players overlook. Combined with herb runs and birdhouses, you can earn 2M+ GP per day with under 30 minutes of active work.

6. Advanced Active Money Makers (Mid-Game)

Once you have 70+ Combat stats and some gear, these methods unlock serious GP. This is where OSRS money making really takes off. For a deeper comparison of high-level methods, see the OSRS Best Money Making Methods 2026 guide.

⚔️ Slayer (70+) — 200K–800K GP/hr

Slayer is the best all-around money maker because you train combat while earning. At 70+ Combat, tasks like Gargoyles (75 Slayer) drop 500K–800K GP/hr in alchables and rune items. At 85+ Slayer, tasks like Nechryaels and Smoke Devils drop seeds, herbs, and valuable rare items worth up to 10M+ GP per task.

🐍 Zulrah — 500K–3M GP/hr

Zulrah is the first serious boss money maker. With 75+ Ranged and Magic, you can learn Zulrah in 10–20 attempts. Each kill takes 1–2 minutes and drops valuable items (Zulrah scales, snake hides, uniques worth 100K–2M). At 20+ kills per hour, Zulrah earns 500K–3M GP/hr depending on your speed and luck.

🐉 Vorkath — 2M–4M GP/hr

Vorkath is the best consistent money maker for mid-game players. With 75+ Ranged, 70+ Prayer (for Rigour), and a DHCB or Dragon Hunter Lance, Vorkath earns 2M–4M GP/hr consistently. Each kill takes 1–2 minutes and drops ~150K in loot (superior dragon bones + blue d'hides).

7. Money Making FAQs

What's the fastest way to make 1M GP as a new player?

With membership: start herb runs immediately (passive 100K–300K per run) + Blast Furnace for active money (500K–700K GP/hr). You can make 1M GP in 2–3 hours of active play. Without membership: use Wine of Zamorak (100K–200K GP/hr) — that takes 5–10 hours. For a full roadmap, check the First 5M GP Members Guide 2026.

Is flipping (merching) good for beginners?

Simple flipping (cowhides, runes, food) works with 50K+ GP capital and earns 20K–50K GP/hr. Advanced flipping requires 1M+ GP and market knowledge. Start with low-volume, high-turnover items like feathers, runes, and potions. Never flip items you don't understand — research prices on the OSRS Wiki first.

Should I buy a bond with real money or earn it in-game?

If you can afford $7.99, buy membership directly. One month of membership with the methods in this guide will easily earn 50M+ GP — enough for 4+ bonds. If you're committed to F2P, expect 55–75 hours of grinding for your first bond. The time investment is significant but rewarding.

What should I buy with my first 500K GP?

Priority order: (1) Rune Scimitar (20K GP) at 40 Attack — doubles your kill speed. (2) Full Rune Armor (100K GP) at 40 Defence. (3) Amulet of Power or Amulet of Glory (10K–50K GP). (4) A Fire Staff (1K GP) for free fire runes. (5) Save the remaining GP as capital for herb runs or GE flipping.

How do I start herb runs? What quests do I need?

Minimum: 9 Farming + Fairytale I (unlocks fairy rings for fast travel). Complete My Arm's Big Adventure (unlocks Trollheim patch — never gets diseased). Buy Ranarr seeds and supercompost at GE. Plant at 5 patches — Falador, Catherby, Ardougne, Port Phasmatys, Trollheim. Takes 3 minutes per run, returns in 80 minutes.

Can I make good GP with just AFK methods?

Yes. Fishing Monkfish (80K–150K GP/hr), cutting Yew/Magic logs (80K–200K GP/hr), and mining Motherlode Mine (variable) are all AFK-friendly. These earn less than active methods but let you work, study, or watch shows while playing. For a full list, see our AFK Money Making Guide 2026.

What's the biggest money making mistake beginners make?

Sticking to one low-GP method for too long. Every hour you spend at 20K GP/hr when you could be at 100K GP/hr is 80K GP lost forever. As soon as you unlock a better method, switch immediately. The second biggest mistake: not starting herb runs on day 1 of membership.

How much GP should I keep as emergency fund?

Always maintain at least 50K–100K GP liquid for teleport tabs, food, potions, and gear repairs. Never spend your last GP on gear. Having a cash reserve means you can take advantage of GE deals and never get stranded without teleport options.

Is Wilderness money making worth the risk?

Green Dragons (100K–200K GP/hr), Lava Dragons (200K–400K GP/hr), and Wilderness boss lair loot (variable) offer higher GP/hr than safe alternatives. The key is risk management: only bring 3 items, protect your prayer, watch the minimap, and teleport out at the first sign of trouble. For beginners, safe F2P/P2P methods are usually better.

What are the best money makers after the Summer Sweep-Up 2026?

The Summer Sweep-Up 2026 update rebalanced several skilling methods. Post-sweep-up, the best money makers remain: Blast Furnace (unchanged), herb runs (unchanged), and the new Sailing content methods (check our Post-Sailing Money Making Guide). Zulrah and Vorkath were not directly affected.

8. Final Tips — Build Wealth Faster

  • Start passive income methods on day 1. Herb runs and birdhouses take 5 minutes every hour and generate millions passive. There is no excuse not to do them.
  • Always reinvest in better methods. Your first 500K should go toward unlocks (gear, quests, skills) that enable 500K+ GP/hr methods. Don't hoard GP — invest it.
  • Use the Grand Exchange wisely. Check OSRS Wiki prices before selling anything valuable. Sell in bulk (28+ items at once) to minimize GE tax impact.
  • Complete quests that unlock money makers. The Giant Dwarf (Blast Furnace), My Arm's Big Adventure (Trollheim patch), Swan Song (Monkfish), and Dragon Slayer II (Vorkath).
  • Never fall for scams. "Doubling money," trust trades, and phishing links will empty your bank. If it sounds too good to be true, it is a scam.
  • Track your bank value. Use RuneLite's Bank Value plugin or check your OSRS Wiki highscores. Watching your bank grow is the best motivation to keep going.
  • Switch methods the moment you qualify for a better one. Every hour at 20K/hr when you qualify for 100K/hr is 80K GP lost forever. Be aggressive about upgrading your money maker.
