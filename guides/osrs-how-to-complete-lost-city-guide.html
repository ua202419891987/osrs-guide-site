OSRS How to Complete Lost City Quest Guide 2026 — Dramen Staff & Fairy Rings

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 10 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
📜 Quest Guide — Updated June 2026

Lost City is one of the most important quests in OSRS — completing it unlocks the Dramen Staff, which gives you access to the Fairy Ring network, arguably the most powerful transportation system in the game. This guide walks you through every step, including the Tree Spirit boss fight and Entrana's weapon restrictions.

2. Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Start the Quest: Go to Lumbridge Swamp (south of Lumbridge Castle). Find the group of adventurers in the south-east corner of the swamp. Talk to any of them about Zanaris — they'll mention a Leprechaun who knows more.
  2. Find the Leprechaun: The Leprechaun is hiding in a tree near the adventurers. Right-click the tree and select "Search" to find him. He'll tell you about the Dramen Tree in Entrana Dungeon and warn about the Tree Spirit that guards it.
  3. Travel to Entrana: Go to Port Sarim docks. Take the boat to Entrana. Important: The monks on the dock will search you. You cannot bring any armor or weapons onto the island — they will be confiscated. Bring only food, potions, runes, and a knife.
  4. Enter the Dungeon: On Entrana, walk to the north-east corner of the island to find the dungeon entrance (a cave). Inside, you'll find zombies and greater demons. Avoid combat — you have no armor.
  5. Find the Dramen Tree: Navigate through the dungeon to the back area where a large glowing tree stands. This is the Dramen Tree. When you attempt to cut it, the Tree Spirit appears.
  6. Defeat the Tree Spirit: See Section 3 for detailed fight strategies. The Tree Spirit is level 101 and uses Melee attacks only.
  7. Cut a Dramen Branch: After defeating the Tree Spirit, use your knife on the Dramen Tree to cut a Dramen Branch.
  8. Craft the Dramen Staff: Use the Dramen Branch with a knife (in inventory) to carve it into a Dramen Staff. This can be done anywhere — you don't need to be near a crafting station.
  9. Quest Complete! With the Dramen Staff in your inventory, the quest is complete. You now have access to Zanaris and all Fairy Rings across Gielinor.

3. Tree Spirit Boss Fight — Detailed Strategy

Combat Level101Uses Melee attacks exclusively
Hitpoints120Moderate HP pool for its level

WeaknessFire MagicFire Strike/Fire Bolt deal increased damage
Max Hit~12Relatively low for its level (you have no armor)

Best Fight Strategies (ranked):

  1. Protect from Melee (43 Prayer): Activate Protect from Melee before entering the dungeon. The Tree Spirit's attacks deal zero damage. Bring 1-2 prayer potions. This makes the fight completely trivial.
  2. Fire Magic: Bring fire runes and a staff. Cast Fire Strike (13 Magic) or Fire Bolt (35 Magic). The Tree Spirit is weak to fire and takes bonus damage. Even with low Magic, Fire Strike chips away at its HP safely.
  3. Ranged: Bring a bow and arrows. Bows and arrows are allowed on Entrana. Range the Tree Spirit from a distance — it can only attack in melee range.
  4. Found Weapons: The Entrana dungeon contains melee weapons on the ground (combat knife, bronze battleaxe dropped by zombies). You can pick these up and use them — they're not confiscated since they spawn inside Entrana.

6. Common Mistakes

  1. Bringing armor to Entrana. The monks confiscate it. You won't get it back until after the quest. Read the allowed items list carefully.
  2. Forgetting a knife. After killing the Tree Spirit, you need a knife or slashing weapon to cut the Dramen Branch. If you forgot, you can pick up a weapon in the dungeon or leave and return.
  3. Not bringing prayer potions with Protect from Melee. If using Protect from Melee (the easiest strategy), bring 1-2 prayer potions. Running out of prayer mid-fight means the Tree Spirit hits you with no armor.
  4. Underestimating the dungeon monsters. Greater Demons (level 92) and zombies inhabit the dungeon. Run past them — you have no armor and fighting them wastes supplies.
  5. Not banking a spare Dramen Branch. Keep an extra Dramen Branch in your bank. If you ever lose your Dramen Staff, you can carve a new one instantly without redoing the quest.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What do I need for the Lost City quest?

A: Requirements: 31 Crafting, 36 Woodcutting. No quest prerequisites. Recommended: 43 Prayer (Protect from Melee trivializes the Tree Spirit), 60+ Combat, a knife, food (lobsters+), and optionally fire runes + a staff. You cannot bring armor or melee weapons to Entrana — they'll be confiscated.

Q: How do I beat the Tree Spirit in Lost City?

A: The Tree Spirit (level 101) uses Melee only and is weak to fire. Best strategy: Pray Protect from Melee (43 Prayer) to block all damage. Alternatively, cast Fire Strike/Fire Bolt for increased damage, or use a bow and arrows from a safe distance. The dungeon also contains weapons on the ground you can pick up and use.

Q: What can I bring to Entrana?

A: Allowed: Food, potions, magic runes and staves, bows and arrows, jewelry, capes, and a knife. NOT allowed: All armor, melee weapons, shields, and ranged armor. Items found inside the Entrana dungeon (weapons dropped by zombies) can be used freely — they spawn inside so the monks can't confiscate them.

Q: What is the Dramen Staff used for after Lost City?

A: The Dramen Staff unlocks the entire Fairy Ring network — a teleportation system connecting dozens of locations across Gielinor. Equip the staff and click any Fairy Ring to use it. Key destinations include Zulrah (BKP), Mount Karuulm (CIR), Feldip Hills (DJR), and Morytania (ALQ). After Lumbridge & Draynor Elite Diary, you no longer need to equip the staff.

📜 Unlock the Fairy Realm

Lost City is a short quest with an enormous payoff. The 15-25 minutes you spend on it unlocks a teleportation network that you'll use for the rest of your OSRS career. From bossing to skilling to questing, Fairy Rings are the backbone of efficient travel in Gielinor.

Craft that Dramen Staff. The Fairy Ring network awaits.
