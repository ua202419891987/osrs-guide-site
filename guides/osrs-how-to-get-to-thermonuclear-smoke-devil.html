OSRS How to Get to Thermonuclear Smoke Devil 2026 -- Location, Gear & Boss Strategy

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 11 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
💨 Slayer Boss -- Updated June 2026

The Thermonuclear Smoke Devil is one of the highest-level Slayer bosses in OSRS, requiring 93 Slayer to access. Located deep in the Stronghold Slayer Cave, this boss drops the valuable Occult Necklace and is a requirement for the Smoke Devil pet. This guide covers how to get there, the best gear setups, boss mechanics, and kill strategy for 2026.

💨 Dust Protection Is Mandatory: Without a Facemask, Nosepeg, or Slayer Helmet, the dust in the Stronghold Slayer Cave will rapidly damage you (up to 12 damage every few seconds). You will die within 30-60 seconds without protection. This applies to the entire cave, not just the boss room. The Slayer Helmet is the best choice since it provides both dust protection AND the 15% damage/accuracy boost on Slayer tasks.

2. Step-by-Step Route to the Boss

Fastest Route (Fairy Ring DKS)

  1. Teleport to any Fairy Ring (use your POH, Edgeville, or Ardougne cloak).
  2. Enter code DKS -- this teleports you to the entrance of the Stronghold Slayer Cave.
  3. From the fairy ring, run north-west -- the cave entrance is directly ahead (a stone door built into the mountain).
  4. Enter the Stronghold Slayer Cave.
  5. Run north through the cave. You will pass: Bloodvelds (south) → Abyssal Demons (west/north-west) → Nechryaels (centre-north) → Gargoyles (east) → Smoke Devils (far north-east).
  6. The Thermonuclear Smoke Devil boss room is in the far north-eastern corner, behind the area where regular Smoke Devils spawn.
  7. Right-click the boss room entrance to "Peek" (check if another player is inside) or "Enter" to fight.

5. Boss Mechanics & Kill Strategy

AttackDescriptionCounter
💨 Standard Ranged AttackMagic-based ranged attack (single target)Protect from Magic (yes, even though it's a 'ranged' attack)
🌫️ Smoke BarrageCreates smoke clouds on the ground that deal rapid damageMove 2+ tiles away immediately when clouds appear
👾 Dust Devil AoEPeriodic area-of-effect damage around the bossMaintain 4+ tiles of distance from the boss at all times

Kill Strategy

  1. Enter the boss room with Protect from Magic active.
  2. Stay at range (4+ tiles). The Thermonuclear Smoke Devil has no melee attack, but its AoE damage increases the closer you are.
  3. Watch for smoke clouds on the ground. When they appear, move 2+ tiles immediately. Clouds deal rapid damage (up to 8-10 per tick) and can kill you quickly if you stand in them.
  4. Attack continuously. The boss has relatively low defence. Blowpipe with Amethyst darts or Dragon darts shreds through its health. On task with Slayer Helmet (i), kills take 60-90 seconds.
  5. Heal as needed. Use Saradomin Brews with Super Restores in the standard 3:1 ratio (3 Brew doses → 1 Restore dose). Keep your HP above 45 to survive a potential smoke cloud + attack combo.
  6. No special attack to worry about. Unlike many OSRS bosses, the Thermonuclear Smoke Devil has no devastating special attack you must prayer-flick. It's a straightforward DPS race with area denial (smoke clouds).
💨 Smoke Cloud Priority: The smoke clouds are the only dangerous mechanic. When clouds appear, ALWAYS move first, then attack. Tanking an extra 2 seconds of attack delay is better than taking 20-30 damage from standing in clouds. After moving to a clear tile, resume attacking immediately.

7. Advanced Tips

  • Only kill on a Slayer task. Without the 15% Slayer Helmet (i) boost, your kills per hour drop by ~25%, and your profit per hour may turn negative after supply costs. Always wait for a Smoke Devil task from Duradel, Konar, or Nieve.
  • Use Dragon Darts for the Blowpipe. Despite their higher cost (~1.5K each vs ~300 for Amethyst), Dragon Darts significantly increase DPS. On task, the faster kills more than compensate for the dart cost.
  • Thralls add free damage. If you have the Arceuus spellbook unlocked, summoning a Greater Ghost Thrall (or Skeleton Thrall) adds ~1.5 DPS. The boss has low ranged defence, so the thrall's accuracy isn't an issue.
  • Use the Slayer Ring to bank between trips. The Slayer Ring teleports you to the Stronghold Slayer Cave entrance. Teleport to your POH for the pool (restore HP/Prayer), then Slayer Ring back. This makes trip turnaround fast (under 90 seconds).
  • Twisted Bow is BIS here. The Thermonuclear Smoke Devil has a Magic level of 230, making the Twisted Bow's passive damage scaling very effective. With a Twisted Bow, kills take 40-60 seconds and profit per hour jumps to 800K-1.2M GP.

8. Common Mistakes

  1. Forgetting the Facemask / Slayer Helmet. The dust in the Stronghold Slayer Cave will kill you within 60 seconds without protection. Always double-check you have it equipped before entering the cave.
  2. Killing off-task. Without the Slayer Helmet boost, you are leaving 15% damage and accuracy on the table. This turns a moderately profitable boss into a GP loss.
  3. Standing in smoke clouds. The clouds deal rapid damage. If you are not paying attention and stand in a cloud for 5+ seconds, you can take 40-50 damage. Keep your eyes on the ground.
  4. Standing too close to the boss. The close-range AoE damage from the boss stacks with smoke cloud damage. Maintain 4+ tile distance. There is no benefit to being close -- the boss has no melee attack.
  5. Bringing too few darts. Running out of ammunition mid-kill is a waste of a trip. Always bring 1,500+ darts per trip. The Blowpipe burns through darts quickly at its 2-tick attack speed.
  6. Not using an instance. The boss room is instanced -- meaning no one can crash your kill. If you see another player peeking or entering, they'll go to their own instance. Do not worry about being crashed.

9. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I kill the Thermonuclear Smoke Devil at lower than 93 Slayer?

A: No. 93 Slayer is a hard requirement. The boss room entrance will not allow entry without level 93 Slayer, even with boosts (Wild Pie, Slayer's Respite). You must have the base level. This makes it one of the least accessible bosses in the game -- by the time you reach 93 Slayer, your combat stats will be well into the 90s.

Q: Is Magic viable at the Thermonuclear Smoke Devil?

A: Yes, but Ranged (Blowpipe or T-Bow) is better. The boss has lower Magic defence than Ranged defence, so Magic (Trident of the Swamp, Sanguinesti Staff) works well. However, a Blowpipe at 2-tick attack speed out-DPSes Magic options at all gear levels below Twisted Bow. Use Magic if your Magic level is significantly higher than Ranged, or if you're an Ironman with a Trident but no Blowpipe.

Q: How long does it take to get the Smoke Devil pet?

A: At 1/3,000 drop rate and 40-50 kills per hour (on task), expect approximately 60-75 hours of active killing to hit the drop rate. Some players get it in under 100 kills, others go 5,000+ dry. This is one of the rarer pets in OSRS. If you are pet hunting, bring an Imbued Heart or Preserve prayer to maintain Ranged boosts between kills and maximize kills per hour.

💨 A High-Level Slayer Milestone

The Thermonuclear Smoke Devil is one of the highest Slayer milestones in OSRS. At 93 Slayer, you have earned the right to fight one of the most exclusive bosses in the game. While it's not a top-tier money maker, the Occult Necklace drops, the Smoke Devil pet, and the prestige of having reached 93 Slayer make it a meaningful boss to conquer.

Fairy Ring DKS, Slayer Helmet on, Protect from Magic up, and watch for those smoke clouds. You've got this.
