OSRS Desert Treasure Quest Guide — Low Level Strategy 2026

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 15 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🧩 Quest Guide — Updated June 2026

Desert Treasure is one of OSRS's most iconic quests — and the gateway to Ancient Magicks, the most powerful spellbook in the game. But its four bosses have ended countless low-level runs. This guide covers minimum requirements, pre-quest preparation, and safe strategies for Dessous, Kamil, Fareed, and Damis so you can unlock Ice Burst, Blood Barrage, and the full Ancient spellbook without needing max combat.

3. Boss 1: Dessous the Vampire — Air Magic & Safe-Spot

StatDetail
LocationGraveyard south of the Slayer Tower, Morytania
Max Hit50+ (Melee) — Protect from Melee mandatory
WeaknessAir spells (Wind Blast / Wind Wave)
Safe-SpotGravestones in the arena. Stand behind a gravestone — Dessous gets stuck on the opposite side.
Low Level StrategyEnter arena with Protect from Melee ON. Run behind the northern-most gravestone. Dessous will walk around trying to reach you but gets blocked. Cast Wind Blast from behind the stone. If he gets free, cast Entangle (10-second freeze), run behind another stone, continue casting. Bring 3 prayer potions — the safe-spot reduces prayer drain significantly.
🪦 Dessous Safe-Spot Detail: The northern-most large gravestone blocks Dessous's pathfinding perfectly. If you stand exactly 1 tile south of it, Dessous will walk around to the left, get stuck on the stone, and stand there without attacking. You can then cast on him from complete safety. Bring extra air runes — his Magic defence is high and you'll splash occasionally.

4. Boss 2: Kamil the Ice Troll King — Stat Drain & Freeze

StatDetail
LocationIce Path, Trollheim (north of Troll Stronghold). Requires climbing boots and warm clothing to reach.
Special MechanicFreezes you in place + drains combat stats. You'll frequently be frozen and unable to move.
WeaknessFire spells (Fire Blast / Fire Wave)
Safe-SpotNone reliable. Must fight directly.
Low Level StrategyProtect from Melee ON at all times. Wear full warm clothing (Santa hat, Bruma torch, clue hunter garb) to avoid passive damage on the ice path. Kamil will freeze you — when frozen, just keep casting Fire Blast (you can still attack while frozen). After each freeze, check your stats — if Attack/Strength/Magic dropped significantly, drink Super Restore. Bring 4-5 prayer potions + 2 Super Restores. Use Stamina potions to reposition when unfrozen.

5. Boss 3: Fareed the Fire Warrior — Water Magic & Ice Gloves

StatDetail
LocationSmoke Dungeon, Pollnivneach (use a facemask or slayer helmet to avoid smoke damage)
Critical ItemIce Gloves EQUIPPED. Without them, Fareed disarms your weapon and you must pick it up. If you lose it, it's gone.
WeaknessWater spells (Water Blast / Water Wave)
Safe-SpotFire pit in the center of the arena. Stand on the opposite side.
Low Level StrategyEasiest boss if prepared. EQUIP Ice Gloves (obtained from the Ice Queen at the end of Heroes' Quest — you only need the miniquest, not full Heroes'). Enter with Protect from Melee ON. Stand behind the central fire pit to safe-spot. Cast Water Blast repeatedly. Fareed has low Magic defence and dies in 10-20 casts. Bring facemask, tinderbox, and the required diary page. Only 1-2 prayer potions needed.

6. Boss 4: Damis the Shadow Hound — Prayer Drain Nightmare

StatDetail
LocationShadow Dungeon, Baxtorian Falls (enter via the whirlpool near the Waterfall quest area)
Special MechanicDrains Prayer rapidly — 10+ points per hit. Has TWO forms. Second form is faster and hits harder.
WeaknessNo elemental weakness. Use highest-hitting spell (Iban's Blast recommended).
Safe-SpotRock formations in the Shadow Dungeon. Lure Damis behind rocks and attack from the other side.
Low Level StrategyThis is the hardest fight for low levels. Bring 8+ prayer potions (yes, really). Enter with Protect from Melee ON. First form: Cast Entangle/Snare on Damis and safe-spot behind the rock formations — he's slow and stays trapped. Cast Iban's Blast (hits 25 at 50 Magic) from behind the rocks. When first form dies, second form spawns — it's FASTER and may escape the safe-spot. If it escapes, keep Protect from Melee ON and tank it while DPS racing. Pray flick if you know how — it saves massive prayer. If your prayer hits 0, you will die in 2-3 hits. Keep an emergency teleport ready.
💀 Damis Desperation Tip: If you're really struggling with Damis (low combat + no Iban's Blast), bring a friend. Desert Treasure bosses are instanced — but a friend can stand OUTSIDE the boss door and trade you food/potions between attempts WITHOUT resetting the boss. Have them bring a full inventory of prayer potions and food. You run out between attempts, trade them at the door, and re-enter.

Blood Barrage lets you camp God Wars bosses indefinitely. Desert Treasure is genuinely one of the most impactful quests in the game.

8. Common Mistakes That End Runs

  1. Not bringing enough prayer potions. This is the #1 cause of death. Bring at least 12-16 (4) dose potions for the full quest. You can always sell unused ones back.
  2. Forgetting Ice Gloves for Fareed. If you don't have Ice Gloves equipped, Fareed disarms your weapon. It falls on the ground. If you don't notice and teleport out, your weapon is gone forever. Ice Gloves are obtained from killing the Ice Queen in White Wolf Mountain (requires 50 Mining to access, or just buy them from another player).
  3. Not freezing Damis. Many low-level guides say "just safe-spot Damis." Safe-spotting works for his first form but NOT reliably for his second form. Bring Entangle/Snare runes and cast them when he escapes the rocks.
  4. Going to Kamil without warm clothing. The Ice Path before Kamil deals passive damage if you're not wearing warm clothing. You'll arrive at the boss with 30-50% HP eaten away. Wear at least 2 pieces of warm gear.
  5. Underestimating the prerequisite quests. Desert Treasure requires 7 prerequisite quests. Many players focus only on the bosses and forget they need to complete Temple of Ikov, Tourist Trap, Troll Stronghold, etc. Check the quest log before starting.

9. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use Ranged instead of Magic for the bosses?

A: Yes, but it's harder. Ranged with Ruby Bolts (e) + Diamond Bolts (e) works for all 4 bosses, especially if you have 70+ Ranged. However, Magic has elemental weaknesses (Air for Dessous, Fire for Kamil, Water for Fareed) that give you significant damage bonuses, making Magic the faster and safer option at low levels. If you have 75+ Ranged and a Toxic Blowpipe, Ranged becomes competitive.

Q: Do I lose items if I die during Desert Treasure?

A: Standard death mechanics apply. All 4 boss fights are in non-Wilderness areas. If you die, your items go to your gravestone (15-minute timer) or to Death's Office for a retrieval fee. You don't lose items permanently. The exception: Kamil's ice path has aggressive wolves — if you die on the path (not at the boss), you might struggle to reach your gravestone. Use protect item prayer and bring minimal risk.

Q: Is Desert Treasure worth doing on a pure or limited account?

A: Yes, but plan carefully. The quest rewards 20,000 Defence XP, which may level your Defence if you're a pure. However, Ancient Magicks is so powerful for PvP (Ice spells) and PvM (Blood spells) that most pures accept the Defence XP. If you're a 1-Defence pure, you cannot complete this quest without gaining Defence levels.

🔮 Ancient Magicks Changes Everything

The 2-4 hours you spend on Desert Treasure will pay dividends for the rest of your OSRS career. Ancient Magicks is the spellbook behind bursting Slayer tasks for 100K+ XP/hr, healing through boss fights with Blood Barrage, and freezing opponents in PvP. It's not an exaggeration to say Desert Treasure is one of the top 5 most important quests in the game.

Prepare your potions, double-check your Ice Gloves, and tackle those four bosses. Once you see "Quest Complete," you'll wonder why you waited so long. 🔮
