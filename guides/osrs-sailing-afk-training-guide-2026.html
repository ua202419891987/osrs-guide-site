1. Why AFK Sailing Is the Best for Most Players

Not everyone has 70 hours to sweat Barracuda Trials. Shipwreck Salvaging lets you work, study, or watch Netflix while earning XP and GP simultaneously.

  • You can do anything else while training. Work, study, cook, play another game.
  • It makes money. Unlike 99% of OSRS skills where you bleed GP, AFK Sailing earns you 20M+.
  • Zero supplies required. No food, no potions, no prayer, no ammo.
  • Zero death risk. Shipwrecks don't fight back.
  • Ironman-friendly. Planks, ores, gems — all useful for self-sufficient accounts.

2. How Shipwreck Salvaging Works

Sail to a wreck → click → your character salvages automatically for 2-4 minutes → click the next wreck → repeat. That's it.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I AFK Sailing on mobile?

A: Yes, and it's excellent. Set your phone down, check between tasks. Enable OSRS mobile notifications for wreck completion pings.

Q: Is AFK Salvaging better XP than AFK combat?

A: Comparable but more profitable. AFK NMZ is ~80-90K XP/hr with zero profit. Dragon Hook Salvaging is ~90K XP/hr with 400K GP/hr profit.

Q: Can Ironmen benefit from AFK Salvaging?

A: Absolutely. Planks, ores, bars, gems — all extremely useful. Crystal Extractor at 73 provides passive crystal shards.

😴 The Most Relaxed 99 in OSRS

Shipwreck Salvaging is genuinely low-effort, profitable, and useful for all account types. Earn 20M-50M GP while working, studying, or watching shows. Cast your hook, sit back, and let the seas do the work.
