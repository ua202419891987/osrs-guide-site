OSRS Sarachnis Loot Guide for Ironman 2026 -- Cudgel, Seeds & Gear Setups

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 12 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🪨 Boss Guide -- Updated June 2026

Sarachnis is a mid-level boss in the Forthos Dungeon that is particularly valuable for Ironman accounts. The cudgel is a top-tier crush weapon, the seed drops save hours of farming, and the supply drops keep your PvM stocked. This guide covers everything from requirements to max-efficiency kill strategies.

💡 Ironman Tip: Sarachnis is accessible much earlier than most Ironmen realize. With base 70 melee stats and a Dragon Mace, you can start farming kills. The seed drops alone make it worth visiting as soon as you finish A Porcine of Interest.

4. Sarachnis Mechanics & Kill Strategy

Sarachnis alternates between Ranged and Magic attacks, and periodically spawns spiderlings. Understanding each mechanic is key to efficient kills.

AttackVisual CueCounter
🌿 RangedGreen projectile from SarachnisProtect from Missiles
🔥 MagicBlue projectile from SarachnisProtect from Magic
🥠 Web TrapWebs appear on the groundStep away immediately -- 2 tiles minimum
🕷️ Spiderling SwarmMultiple small spiders spawnKill spiderlings quickly or tank them (low damage)

Kill Rotation

  1. Enter the lair with Protect from Magic active (magic is the more dangerous attack).
  2. Attack immediately -- Sarachnis always starts with a magic attack.
  3. Watch the projectiles: Green = switch to Missiles. Blue = switch to Magic.
  4. When webs appear: Step away 2+ tiles. Staying in webs roots you in place and Sarachnis gets free damage.
  5. When spiderlings spawn: Ignore them if you can tank the damage. If they accumulate (4+), kill a few with your weapon between boss attacks.
  6. Prayer switching rhythm: Sarachnis alternates attacks. Get into a rhythm: attack, watch projectile, switch prayer, attack, watch projectile, switch prayer.
  7. Use Piety whenever you have prayer points. The DPS increase is significant and shortens kills by 20-30%.
👀 Pro Tip -- Attack Animation Camping: You can actually predict Sarachnis's next attack by the projectile color. Green always means the next attack will be magic, and blue means the next attack will be ranged (she alternates). Once you master this, you can switch prayers BEFORE the next attack animation even starts -- reducing damage to near zero.

7. Advanced Tips & Efficiency

  • Spec weapon swap: Bring a Dragon Dagger (p++) for special attacks. Use both specs at the start of each kill for free damage. With a Lightbearer ring, you can spec every other kill.
  • Thralls: If you have access to the Arceuus spellbook, summoning a thrall adds ~1.5 DPS -- approximately 8% faster kills for zero inventory cost.
  • Use Xeric's Talisman: The "Xeric's Heart" teleport drops you near the Forthos Dungeon entrance. Combine with a house teleport for the fastest bank-and-return cycle.
  • Alch runes in pouch: Bring nature and fire runes in a rune pouch. Many Sarachnis drops (rune items, dragonhide) alch for more than their GE value.
  • Birdhouse run integration: Fossil Island is close to Great Kourend. Do a birdhouse run between Sarachnis trips for passive Hunter XP.
  • Flick Piety: If you're comfortable with prayer flicking, flick Piety on your attack ticks to save prayer potions while maintaining the DPS boost.

8. Common Mistakes

  1. Using slash weapons. The Abyssal Whip at Sarachnis is significantly worse than a Dragon Mace. Always use crush. The accuracy difference is ~30%.
  2. Ignoring spiderlings completely. While you can tank 2-3 spiderlings, letting them stack to 6+ will drain your food rapidly. Clear them when they accumulate.
  3. Standing in web traps. Webs root you in place and Sarachnis gets free hits. Move immediately when you see the web animation on the ground.
  4. Not bringing alch runes. Many Sarachnis drops are alchables. Without alch runes, you leave 50K-80K GP on the ground per hour.
  5. Forgetting a crush weapon switch. If you're on the Arceuus spellbook for thralls, remember you still need a melee weapon. Thralls + crush weapon = optimal kills.
  6. Over-praying. You only need Protect from Magic/Missiles. Don't use Protect from Melee -- Sarachnis has no melee attack.

9. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I kill Sarachnis on a low-level Ironman?

A: Yes! With base 70 melee stats, 43 Prayer, and a Dragon Mace, you can get kills. It will be slower (15-20 kills/hr) and you'll use more food, but the seed drops are immediately valuable. Train to 80+ Attack/Strength for comfortable trips.

Q: Is the Sarachnis Cudgel worth grinding for?

A: Absolutely for Ironmen. At a 1/500 drop rate and 25-40 kills per hour, expect 12-20 hours to hit the drop rate. The cudgel is your best crush weapon until the Inquisitor's Mace from Nightmare (a much harder boss). It makes Gargoyles, Tekton, and other crush-weak monsters significantly faster. Regular accounts can also profit from it -- the cudgel sells for ~500K-1M GP.

Q: How do I get to the Forthos Dungeon quickly?

A: Fastest method: Xeric's Talisman to "Xeric's Heart" -- run south into the Hosidius area, enter the dungeon entrance just south of the altar. Alternative: Skills Necklace to Woodcutting Guild, run east. Fairy Ring code DJR drops you near the Kourend Castle, then run south-west.

Q: Should I use ranged instead of melee at Sarachnis?

A: Melee (crush) is better. While ranged works and lets you avoid some mechanics at distance, Sarachnis's crush weakness makes melee significantly faster. Ranged is only recommended if your melee stats are substantially lower than your ranged stats, or if you're a Hardcore Ironman who wants the safety of distance.

🪨 Sarachnis -- The Ironman's Best Friend

Few bosses in OSRS offer the same combination of accessibility and Ironman value as Sarachnis. The seed drops alone can sustain your farming runs indefinitely. The cudgel is a genuine account upgrade. And the red spider's eggs solve one of the most annoying supply bottlenecks in the game. Whether you're a mid-game Ironman looking for farming supplies or a main account hunting a consistent 500K+ GP/hr, Sarachnis delivers.

Grab your best crush weapon, stock up on prayer potions, and head to the Forthos Dungeon. The spider awaits.
