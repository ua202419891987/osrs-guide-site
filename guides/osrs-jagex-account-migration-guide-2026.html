① Why Migrate to a Jagex Account?

Jagex announced on June 12, 2026 that the full transition to Jagex Accounts is happening by late 2026 (targeting November/December). Over 85% of active players have already made the switch since the system launched in beta in 2023. Here's why you should join them:

① The Old Account System Was Insecure

The legacy RuneScape account system was built on 20+ year old code. It lacked modern security features, making accounts vulnerable to phishing, social engineering attacks, and data breaches. Most account "hacks" weren't technical breaches — they were social engineering attacks that exploited the outdated recovery system.

③ It Will Be Mandatory by Late 2026

After November/December 2026, you will need a Jagex Account to play OSRS and RuneScape 3. The only exceptions are players who log in through Steam, Apple, Google, or Game Center — those login methods will continue to work without a Jagex Account. If you don't migrate before the deadline, your game progress is safely saved — you'll just be prompted to complete the upgrade when you next log in.

② Migration Steps — Complete Walkthrough

The migration process takes about 10 minutes. Follow these steps carefully:

① Before You Start — What You Need

📋 Prerequisites Checklist

  • ✅ Your current RuneScape/OSRS login email and password
  • ✅ Access to the email address associated with your account
  • ✅ A device with internet access (PC, Mac, or mobile)
  • ✅ Optional: An authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.) for 2FA setup
  • ✅ 10 minutes of uninterrupted time

② Step 1: Visit the Migration Page

Go to the official Jagex Account upgrade page:

https://support.runescape.com/hc/en-gb/articles/10682990186129-Upgrade-your-RuneScape-character-to-Jagex-account

This link is available from the official RuneScape website under Account → Upgrade to Jagex Account. Always verify you're on the official runescape.com domain before entering any credentials.

③ Step 2: Sign In With Your Current Account

Enter your existing RuneScape or Old School RuneScape username/email and password. This authenticates that you're the account owner. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option to reset it before proceeding.

④ Step 3: Create Your Jagex Account

After authentication, you'll be prompted to create a Jagex Account. This involves:

  1. Set a new email — This will be your Jagex Account login ID. It can be the same as your existing account email or a different one
  2. Set a strong password — Jagex Accounts require modern password standards (12+ characters, mixed case, numbers/symbols). Use a password manager if needed
  3. Verify your email — Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to confirm

⑤ Step 4: Add Your Characters to the Account

Your existing characters (OSRS and RS3) will be automatically detected and linked to your new Jagex Account. You can manage up to 20 characters under one Jagex Account. If you have additional characters on separate accounts, you can add them later through the account management portal.

⑥ Step 5: Set Up Security Features (Recommended)

After the basic migration, you'll be offered security setup options. We strongly recommend configuring these immediately (see Section 4 for detailed instructions):

  • Email MFA — Enabled by default. A code is sent to your email on each login from a new device
  • Authenticator App MFA — Optional but recommended for maximum security
  • Backup Codes — Save these! They're your lifeline if you lose access to your MFA device

⑦ Step 6: Download and Install the Jagex Launcher

The Jagex Launcher is the recommended way to access your games with a Jagex Account. Download it from the official RuneScape website. The launcher:

  • Automatically handles your login session (up to 90 days)
  • Manages all your characters in one place
  • Provides one-click launch for OSRS, RS3, and approved third-party clients

Note: The launcher no longer requires administrator permissions to install, making it compatible with managed/controlled devices.

⑧ Step 7: Verify Everything Works

Launch OSRS through the Jagex Launcher and confirm:

  • ✅ Your characters appear correctly
  • ✅ Your game progress, items, and stats are intact
  • ✅ Your membership/subscription is still active
  • ✅ You can log in to the OSRS website with your new Jagex Account
💡 Pro Tip: Don't Rush the Security Setup

The migration itself takes 5 minutes. But the security setup (authenticator app, backup codes) is what makes your account actually secure. Spend an extra 5 minutes on this step. Write down your backup codes and store them somewhere safe — not on your computer, not in your email. A physical notebook or a password manager vault is ideal.

④ Security Setup: 2FA, Backup Codes & Account Recovery

This is the most important section of the guide. Setting up security properly is what makes a Jagex Account meaningfully safer than the old system.

① Email MFA (Enabled by Default)

When you create your Jagex Account, email MFA is enabled by default. This means that whenever you log in from a new device or location, a verification code is sent to your email. You must enter this code to complete the login.

Pros: Easy to use, no additional app needed. Cons: If someone compromises your email, they can intercept these codes. That's why you should also set up an authenticator app.

② Setting Up Authenticator App MFA (Recommended)

📋 Authenticator Setup — Step by Step

  1. Log into your Jagex Account at account.jagex.com
  2. Go to Security Settings
  3. Select "Authenticator App" under Two-Factor Authentication
  4. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator, etc.)
  5. Enter the 6-digit code from your app to confirm setup
  6. Done — future logins will require a code from your authenticator app

③ Backup Codes — Your Most Important Security Asset

When you enable MFA, the system generates 10 backup codes. Each code can be used once to access your account if you lose your phone or authenticator app. This is critical: without backup codes, losing your MFA device means going through a lengthy account recovery process.

How to save backup codes properly:

  • DO: Write them on paper and store in a safe place (fireproof safe, safety deposit box)
  • DO: Store them in a password manager (Bitwarden, 1Password, KeePass)
  • DON'T: Store them in your email inbox
  • DON'T: Take screenshots of them on your phone (if you lose your phone, you lose both MFA and codes)
  • DON'T: Share them with anyone, ever

④ Account Recovery: What to Do If You Lose Access

If you lose access to your Jagex Account:

  1. Use a backup code — If you saved your backup codes, enter one to regain access immediately
  2. Use account recovery — If you don't have backup codes, use the official account recovery process. The new system is more secure and harder to social-engineer
  3. Contact support — Jagex Account holders get priority support including live chat with J-Mods
⚠️ Critical: Don't Skip Backup Codes

Every week, we hear from players who lost access to their accounts because they skipped saving backup codes. The authenticator app is great — until your phone breaks, gets lost, or you reset it. Backup codes are your insurance policy. Take 2 minutes to save them properly.

⑤ Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

Based on player reports and support tickets, here are the most common issues players encounter during migration:

① I Forgot My Old Account Password

Problem: You can't remember the password for your original RuneScape/OSRS account. This blocks the migration process.

Solution: Use the "Forgot Password" option on the RuneScape website before attempting migration. You'll need access to the email associated with your account. If you no longer have access to that email, contact Jagex support first to update your email before migrating.

② My Migration Fails at the Email Verification Step

Problem: The verification email doesn't arrive, or arrives too late.

Solution: Check your spam/promotions folder. Add noreply@jagex.com to your contacts. If it still doesn't arrive, wait 15 minutes and try again. If it fails repeatedly, use a different email address (Gmail works best).

③ I Accidentally Used the Wrong Email

Problem: You entered a typo in your email during Jagex Account creation.

Solution: If you haven't verified the email, you can start over with a different email. If you've already verified, contact Jagex support to update your email — but this is much harder than getting it right the first time.

④ My Characters Didn't Appear After Migration

Problem: After migration, your OSRS or RS3 characters aren't showing up.

Solution: This is usually a sync delay. Log out of the launcher completely, close it, reopen, and log in again. If characters still don't appear after 30 minutes, contact Jagex support through the live chat feature.

⑤ My RuneLite/Third-Party Client Doesn't Work

Problem: You can't launch RuneLite through the Jagex Launcher, or plugins aren't working.

Solution: The Jagex Launcher supports RuneLite and other approved third-party clients. Make sure you've selected the correct client in the launcher settings. If a specific plugin isn't working, check with the plugin developer — the migration itself doesn't affect RuneLite plugins. RuneLite continues to work exactly as before.

⑥ I Already Migrated — Can I Go Back?

Problem: You want to revert to the old system.

Solution: No. Jagex Account migration is permanent. Once you upgrade, you cannot revert to the old login system. This is by design — the old system is being phased out by late 2026. If you're unsure, wait until you're ready, but don't wait past the deadline.

💡 The #1 Piece of Advice

Don't migrate in a hurry. Take 15 minutes when you're not distracted. Read each step. Save your backup codes. Verify your characters appear. The few extra minutes you spend now prevent weeks of frustration later.

⑥ Jagex Account Migration FAQs

Do I have to migrate to a Jagex Account?

Yes, by late 2026. The deadline is November or December 2026. After this date, you will need a Jagex Account to play RuneScape and Old School RuneScape. The only exceptions are players who log in through Steam, Apple, Google, or Game Center — those login methods will continue to work independently. Over 85% of active players have already migrated since the system launched in 2023.

What happens if I don't migrate before the deadline?

Your game progress and characters are safely saved. When you next try to log in, you'll be prompted to complete the upgrade process before you can access the game. You won't lose anything — but you will have to migrate before you can play again.

Will I lose my RuneLite plugins or settings?

No. RuneLite and its plugins are completely unaffected by the Jagex Account migration. All your plugin settings, tile markers, bank tags, and configurations remain exactly as they are. RuneLite continues to work through the Jagex Launcher or as a standalone client.

Will I lose my character name?

No. Your character name stays the same. The Jagex Account is just the login system — once logged in, you still select which character to play, and each character keeps its existing name. Nothing about your in-game identity changes.

Can I have multiple characters on one Jagex Account?

Yes, up to 20 characters. This includes both OSRS and RS3 characters. You can switch between them easily through the launcher without logging out. Note that you cannot play the same character on two different games simultaneously, but you can have different characters logged in at the same time.

Does the Jagex Launcher work on Mac and Linux?

Yes. The launcher supports Windows, Mac, and Linux. Jagex has confirmed that all existing platforms will continue to be supported. If the launcher doesn't currently support your specific platform, they are working on adjustments to ensure everyone can access the game. Mobile access is through the existing mobile apps (not the launcher).

Is the Jagex Launcher mandatory?

While the Jagex Account will be mandatory, the Jagex Launcher is the recommended way to access your games. You can also log in through the game client directly with your Jagex Account credentials, but the launcher provides the best experience with 90-day session persistence and easy character switching.

I'm an Ironman — does migration affect my Ironman status?

No. Your Ironman status is tied to your character, not your login method. Migrating to a Jagex Account does not change your game mode, stats, items, or progress in any way. Ironmen remain Ironmen.

Will I still be able to play on Mobile after migrating?

Yes. The mobile apps will continue to work with Jagex Accounts. You'll log in using your Jagex Account email and password on the mobile app. The 90-day session persistence also applies to mobile, so you won't need to log in frequently.

Do the 20 extra bank slots apply to all my characters?

Yes! Each OSRS character linked to your Jagex Account receives 20 additional bank slots automatically. If you have 5 OSRS characters, that's 100 extra bank slots total. The Fancier Boots reward is also per-character.
