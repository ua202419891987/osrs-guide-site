1. What Is Mid-Game Money Making?

Mid-game in OSRS (roughly combat 70-100, total level 1000-1750) is where money making transitions from grinding to strategy. You're past the "pick flax for 100K/hr" phase, but not yet at raids or endgame bosses. The methods here scale from 1.8M/hr to 3M/hr.

Key insight: The biggest mistake mid-game players make is spending all their GP on gear upgrades instead of investing in money-making capability (construction level for pool, farming level for better herbs, prayer level for Piety). Prioritize skills that unlock better GP/hr.

2. Barrows Runs — 1.8M-1M GP/hr

Barrows is the entry-level PvM money maker and the most accessible mid-game boss content. Six brothers, a tunnel, and a chest — simple mechanics with solid, consistent profit.

Requirements

Prayer43 (Protect from Melee)60+

QuestPriest in PerilIn Search of the Myreque (shortcut)

Gear Setup (Budget: ~2M GP)

  • Weapon: Iban's staff (u) — upgrade to Trident of the seas at 75 Magic
  • Armor: Proselyte/Monk robes (prayer bonus) or mystic robes
  • Shield: Tome of fire (with Iban's) or Book of darkness
  • Inventory: 6-8 prayer potions, spade, runes, emergency food, Barrows teleport tablets

Profit Breakdown

Each Barrows piece is worth 100K-3M GP depending on the item. With Morytania Hard Diary completed, you get 50% more runes from the chest, pushing average profit to 1M+/hr.

Pro Tip: Morytania Hard Diary

This diary is the single biggest upgrade for Barrows. The 50% extra runes add ~1.8M/hr in consistent profit. Requirements: 70 Defence, 70 Prayer (can boost from 65), various other stats. Worth rushing.

3. Zulrah — 1M-2M GP/hr

Zulrah is the first serious PvM money maker most players learn. It has a learning curve but once mastered, it's consistent 2M+/hr profit with no gear repair costs (magic/ranged only).

Requirements

RequirementMinimumRecommended
QuestRegicide (started)Regicide completed
Ranged7580+
Magic75 (Trident)80+
Prayer45 (Mystic Might)77 (Augury)
Agility—76 (Fairy ring shortcut)

How to Learn Zulrah

  1. Use the Zulrah Helper plugin on RuneLite — it shows rotation maps and where to stand
  2. Start with one rotation only — if it's not your rotation, log out. Focus on learning rotation 3 (green → red → blue)
  3. Expect to die 20-50 times learning. Each death costs 100K to reclaim items. Budget 2-5M for deaths.
  4. Once you can do all 4 rotations consistently, you're at 1.5-2M/hr

Gear Setup (Budget: ~12M GP)

  • Magic switch: Trident of the seas, Ahrim's/void mage, occult necklace, MA2 cape, tome of fire
  • Range switch: Toxic blowpipe (addy darts), blessed d'hide/void range, ava's accumulator
  • Ring: Ring of suffering (ri) — recoil effect is essential for snakelings

Profit: Average kill = ~130K GP (scales + unique drops). At 15-20 kills/hr = 3M-2.5M/hr. Zulrah unique drops (tanzanite fang, magic fang, serpentine visage) are 3M-9M each.

6. Herb Runs — 100K-1.8M Profit Per Run (5 min)

Herb runs are the single best passive income method in OSRS. Each run takes 5 minutes, costs ~30K in seeds, and yields 130K-230K in herbs. Do 5-6 runs per day = easy 1M+ daily income.

Optimal Herb Run Setup

  • Patches to use: 6-9 patches (Trollheim with My Arm quest, Catherby, Ardougne, Morytania, Falador, Hosidius, Weiss, Farming Guild, Harmony Island)
  • Teleports: Trollheim tab, Catherby tab, Ardougne cloak, Ectophial, Explorer's ring, Xeric's talisman
  • Tools: Magic secateurs, seed dibber, rake, spade, ultracompost (bottomless bucket recommended)
  • Always use ultracompost: Reduces disease chance and increases minimum herb yield

Bottomless Compost Bucket (Hespori)

The bottomless compost bucket doubles your ultracompost usage (one bucket holds 2x) and is obtained from Hespori (65 Farming). It pays for itself within a week of herb runs. Priority upgrade.

8. Flipping & Merchanting — 1M-5M GP/hr

Flipping is the highest potential GP/hr in the mid-game with zero skill requirements — just game knowledge and patience. It scales with your cash stack: more GP = bigger flips = more profit.

Flipping Strategy by Bank Size

Cash StackStrategyTarget Profit/Day
1M-5MHigh-volume consumables (runes, darts, scales, potions)100K-1.8M
5M-20MMid-tier gear (whips, blowpipes, barrows, zenyte shards)1.8M-1M
20M-100MHigh-value gear + PvM items (DHCB, DWH, Bandos, Armadyl)1M-3M
100M+Rares + endgame items (Tbow, Scythe, Shadow, 3A)3M-10M+

Flipping Rules for Beginners

  1. Start with items you understand (items you've bought/sold before)
  2. Test margins with 1 item first — don't buy 100 of something without testing
  3. Use OSRS Wiki prices as reference, not gospel — real margins differ
  4. 3-5% margin is good. 10%+ margin is exceptional (and rare)
  5. Diversify across 3-5 different items to spread risk
  6. Never flip items being manipulated (sudden price spikes on obscure items)
  7. Check trade volumes — avoid items with <100 daily trades

11. 1M to 100M Roadmap — Step by Step

Phase 1: 1M to 10M (The Foundation)

  1. Do herb runs every 80 minutes. At 32 Farming (Ranarr): 100K/run. 5 runs/day = 1.8M. In 7 days: 3.5M.
  2. Add birdhouse runs. Stack with herb runs. 50K/run. 350K/day extra.
  3. Train Slayer to 65. Kurasks at 70 = consistent 1.8M-1.5M/hr. Do 2-3 tasks per day: 1.8M-1M.
  4. Buy essential gear: Whip (3M), blowpipe (3M), trident (1M), fury (1M), berserker ring (2M) = ~10M total.
  5. Timeline: 1-2 weeks to 10M bank.

Phase 2: 10M to 50M (Scaling Up)

  1. Complete Dragon Slayer II. Requires: 400 QP, various skills ~60-70, boss fights. Unlocks Vorkath.
  2. Start Vorkath grinding. 15-20 kills/hr with blowpipe = 3M-2M/hr. Grind 15 hours for 25M+.
  3. Continue daily passive income. Herb runs + birdhouses + Miscellania = still pulling 1.8M-750K/day.
  4. Optional: Learn Zulrah. If you enjoy bossing variety, Zulrah at 3M-2.5M/hr is comparable to Vorkath.
  5. Upgrade gear incrementally: Torture (10M), anguish (12M), tormented bracelet (12M) — prioritize damage upgrades.
  6. Timeline: 2-4 weeks to 50M bank.

Phase 3: 50M to 100M (The Push)

  1. Buy DHCB + DFW (optional). If you're committed to Vorkath, the DHCB setup (~130M) pushes you to 2.5M-3M/hr. Buy it, grind Vorkath to pay it off, then enjoy pure profit.
  2. Scale up flipping. With 50M+ cash, flipping 3-5 high-value items daily = 1M-3M passive/day.
  3. Consider Gauntlet/Corrupted Gauntlet. Requires Song of the Elves. 0 GP risk, 2M-3M/hr with corrupted completion. High skill ceiling but infinite scalability.
  4. Timeline: 2-4 weeks to 100M bank.

Total mid-game journey: 5-10 weeks to go from 1M to 100M. This is playing 2-3 hours/day. Hardcore players can do it in 2-3 weeks.

12. Final Tips — Maximising Your Mid-Game Wealth

🎯 Key Takeaways

  • Stack multiple income sources: Don't rely on one method. Run herb runs while bossing, flip items while skilling — maximise every minute.
  • Invest in money-making upgrades first: A higher Construction level for a pool, Piety at 70 Prayer, and Morytania Hard Diary pay for themselves faster than any gear upgrade.
  • Track your GP/hr: Use RuneLite's loot tracker to see your actual profit rates. If a method drops below 1.8M GP/hr, switch to something better.
  • Don't upgrade gear incrementally for 1% DPS increases: Save up for meaningful jumps (blowpipe → DHCB, whip → tent whip + Avernic). Small upgrades are often GP traps.
  • Set bank value goals: 10M → 50M → 100M. Each milestone unlocks better earning potential. The first 10M is the hardest — it gets easier as your gear and skills improve.

The mid-game is where OSRS really opens up. With 100M GP, you can afford endgame gear, try any boss, and start saving for mega-rares. Follow this guide's roadmap, stay consistent with passive income, and you'll reach your first 100M faster than you think.

12. FAQ

Q: What's the fastest way to 10M from scratch?

Herb runs (ranarr/toadflax) every 80 minutes + Slayer to 65-75. Herb runs are the most GP/hr per active minute spent. Slayer gives passive income while training combat. Combination: ~7 days to 10M at 2-3 hours/day.

Q: Vorkath or Zulrah — which should I learn first?

Vorkath if you want consistent, low-variance profit and have DS2 requirements. Zulrah if you want to learn a harder boss that costs less to die at. Vorkath is slightly better GP/hr at mid levels.

Q: When should I stop herb runs?

Never. Even at endgame with 500M+ bank, herb runs are still 150K-1.8M for 5 minutes of work — that's an effective 1.8M-2.4M/hr rate per active minute. High-level players do herb runs during AFK activities.

Q: Is flipping worth learning if I only have 5M?

Yes, but keep expectations realistic. With 5M, flipping consumables (runes, darts, scales, potions) can yield 100K-1.8M/day with 10 minutes of work. The skill scales with bank size, so start early.

Q: How much GP do I need before starting Vorkath/Zulrah?

Vorkath: 10M minimum (blowpipe + blessed d'hide + salve amulet (ei) + supplies). Zulrah: 12M minimum (trident + blowpipe + Ahrim's + ring of suffering). Add 2-5M for death costs while learning either boss.
