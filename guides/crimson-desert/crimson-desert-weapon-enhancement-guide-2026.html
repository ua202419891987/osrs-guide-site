√∞¬ü¬î¬ß 1. Refining System Overview √¢¬Ä?Levels, Materials & Mechanics

Refining is the primary method of permanently increasing your weapon and armour stats in Crimson Desert. Unlike many RPGs that rely on RNG, the refining system in Pywel is deterministic √¢¬Ä?every refinement attempt has a 100% success rate as long as you have the required materials. There is no chance of failure, no gear destruction, and no downgrade penalty. If you have the materials, the upgrade is guaranteed.

Each piece of equipment can be refined up to 10 times (levels +1 through +10). Every level provides a permanent boost to base attack (for weapons) or defence (for armour), with higher levels delivering proportionally larger stat increases. The system costs zero silver or gold √¢¬Ä?only materials and, from level 5 onward, Abyss Artifacts are consumed.

A fully refined +10 weapon gains roughly +20 to +30 total base attack over its unrefined state. The jump from +9 to +10 alone is equivalent to the combined gains of +1 through +4. Always prioritise main hand weapon refining first √¢¬Ä?higher damage means faster kills, which means less damage taken.

Required Materials by Level Range

Each refinement level consumes specific material types. Lower levels use common ores and leathers, while higher levels demand rare gems like Bloodstones and Aeserion Scales.

Duplicate Weapon Sacrifice

If you have two identical weapons (same name and type), you can sacrifice one as refinement material instead of using regular materials. This is especially useful for high-level refinement where Bloodstones and Aeserion Scales are scarce. Whenever a duplicate drops, stash it √¢¬Ä?you may use it to skip a costly refinement tier later.

Optimal Upgrade Path Summary

Step 1: Refine main weapon to +4 (basic materials only, zero Abyss Artifacts).

Step 6: Refine to +10 only for endgame gear √¢¬Ä?requires Bloodstones and Aeserion Scales.

√∞¬ü¬î¬ß 4. Kuku Pot Enhancement √¢¬Ä?Crafting, Upgrades & Blueprints

The Kuku Pot is a legendary Dwarven iron pot obtained during Chapter 4 through the quest "Mysterious Pot" (Quest #49) at the Kilnden Workshop, west of Hernand. The pot has 230 internal storage slots for Abyss items, machine parts, and crafting components. It is the foundation for creating unique weapons, utility backpacks, elemental armour, and dragon mount components.

Workshop Repairs √¢¬Ä?Unlocking the Kuku Pot

Before you can obtain the pot, you must repair three furnaces and one smelter at the Kilnden Workshop:

  • Smelter (Chimney Puzzle): Rotate stone rings until runes align. Turn left/middle/right knobs until all runes glow blue. A click sound confirms success.
  • Disconnected Furnace: Connect cables, push lever left until flames ignite.
  • Disassembled Furnace: Use Force Palm to align flat parts, then install the top piece (found behind the smelter √¢¬Ä?it was "blown off"). Climb up and use aerial Force Palm to lock it.
  • Fuel Furnace: Insert 5 fuel cores using Force Palm. Locations: 2 near furnace, 1 in water, 1 in bird statue eye (pull out), 1 near tunnel entrance (reload if missing).

Kuku Pot Upgrades

The Kuku Pot itself can be upgraded to an Enhanced Kuku Pot using 1 Abyss Cell. This unlocks advanced recipes at Grimnir's shop, including the legendary elemental armour set and the Marni Laser Helmet upgrade. Craft the Enhanced Kuku Pot before pushing into endgame content.

Aeserion Scales √¢¬Ä?The Endgame Bottleneck

Farming Tip: Save-Scum Ore Nodes

Ore nodes respawn after a few in-game days. However, you can reset them instantly by saving and reloading near a mining spot. Use this technique at the Hernand River Cliffs to farm 200+ Iron Ore in under 15 minutes. Combine with the Mining Knuckledrill (hidden boss drop at Marni's Quarry) for +10% ore yield.

√¢¬ù?FAQ √¢¬Ä?Enhancement Questions Answered

Q: Can I fail a refinement attempt?

No. Refinement in Crimson Desert has a 100% success rate. If you have the required materials, the upgrade is guaranteed. There is no gear destruction, no downgrade, and no RNG √¢¬Ä?unlike many other action RPGs. The only cost is the materials themselves.

Q: Does refinement cost silver or gold?

No. Refinement is completely free of silver/gold cost. The only resources consumed are crafting materials (ores, leathers, wood) and, from level 5 onward, Abyss Artifacts. Socket creation at witch vendors does cost silver (5-50 per slot depending on slot number).

Q: Should I refine every weapon I find?

Only in the early game (levels +1 to +4), where materials are cheap and abundant. From +5 onward, Abyss Artifacts are required √¢¬Ä?and these compete directly with skill tree upgrades. Reserve +5+ refining for weapons you intend to use through endgame. Swap weapons freely before +5, commit afterwards.

Q: Where is the first blacksmith?

Blacksmith Turnali is in Hernand's north-central district. Complete his request for 3 Fine Timber to unlock the refinement system. He rewards you with a permanent axe, a medium backpack, and the Bekker Shield. Do not skip this quest √¢¬Ä?it is the gateway to the entire enhancement system.

Q: How do I get more Abyss sockets?

Visit any witch vendor and select "Create Socket". The cost scales per slot: 5, 10, 15, 25, and 50 silver for slots 1 through 5 respectively. A fully slotted weapon costs 105 silver total. Two-handed weapons can hold up to 5 sockets, while one-handed weapons max at 3.

Q: What is the best Abyss core setup?

For general PvE: Destruction (attack), Spirit Siphon (spirit regen), and Critical Rate on your main weapon. For bossing: swap in Malicebane (+boss damage) and Momentum (+35% Spin Slash damage). For survival: Life Transference. Embedding and extracting are free √¢¬Ä?change your loadout freely based on the encounter.

Q: Can I transfer Abyss cores between weapons?

Yes. Extraction is free at any witch vendor. You can pull a core out of one weapon and embed it into another at no cost. This makes Abyss Gear highly flexible √¢¬Ä?you only need to collect and upgrade cores once.

Q: What is the Kuku Pot and why do I need it?

The Kuku Pot is a Dwarven crafting tool obtained in Chapter 4. It is required to craft unique weapons (elemental spears with infinite durability), utility backpacks (watcher drones, flight packs, flamethrowers), elemental armour (near-immunity to fire/ice), and endgame footwear (temporary platforms, enhanced dodge). Upgrade it to "Enhanced Kuku Pot" with 1 Abyss Cell before endgame.
