🎯 1. Patch 1.12 Highlights — June 19 Major Update

Pearl Abyss released Crimson Desert version 1.12.00 on June 19, 2026, delivering the second major content update following June's 1.10 drop. Where 1.11 was a refinement patch, 1.12 brings substantial new content: an outdoor housing decoration system with 58 new craftable items, new equipment for Kliff and Damiane, slide-based combat attacks for all weapon types, and massive quality-of-life improvements including Boss retry persistence. If you encounter stability problems after updating, refer to our Crash Fix Guide and GPU Driver Settings Guide for platform-specific troubleshooting.

Key Features at a Glance

  • Outdoor Housing Decoration — Decorate the area outside your house with furniture, fountains, wells, and lighting
  • 58 New Craftable Items — Including 27 facilities, 14 lighting items, and 17 decor/pet furniture pieces
  • New Equipment — 5 new helmets, 1 armor set, 1 glove, and 1 accessory for Kliff and Damiane
  • Slide Chain Attacks — Every weapon type now has unique combo attacks while sliding
  • Mystery Box Shop — "Box of Fate" added to the mysterious shop for 100 silver
  • 51 New Knowledge Entries — Collectible knowledge in the "Contract" category
  • Damiane & Oongka — Can now equip Visiones and ride the Blackstar mount
Housing Tip: Outdoor furniture crafting recipes are obtained from multiple sources — stores, claw machines, Flower Basket crafting quests, Boxes of Fate, and the Roulette Challenge. Check all sources before farming a specific recipe.

Just one day after the 1.12.00 launch, Pearl Abyss pushed a hotfix (1.12.01) on June 20, 2026, addressing five issues that slipped through testing. The patch rolled out simultaneously on Steam (PC/Mac), PlayStation, Xbox, and Mac App Store, with Epic Games Store receiving it shortly after.

Five Fixes in 1.12.01

Note: If you lost outdoor furniture items between June 19 and June 20 due to the disappearing furniture bug, check your in-game mail or storage. Pearl Abyss typically compensates for item loss caused by bugs.

🔥 3. 1.12.02 Hotfix & AMD Crash Fix — June 24 Critical Patch

Version 1.12.02, released on June 24, 2026, is the most critical hotfix of the 1.12 cycle. It addresses three specific issues, one of which has been plaguing AMD RX 5000 series users for weeks.

AMD RX 5000 Series Crash Fix — 1080p Resolution

The headline fix in 1.12.02: players using AMD RX 5000 series graphics cards (including RX 5500 XT, RX 5600 XT, RX 5700, and RX 5700 XT) at 1080p (FHD) resolution were experiencing frequent and unpredictable game crashes. This was caused by a render pipeline conflict specific to the RX 5000 architecture at that resolution. The patch resolves this issue, allowing RX 5000 users to play at 1080p without crashing.

Important: This fix only addresses the 1080p-specific crash on RX 5000 series. If you are on an RX 6000 or RX 7000 series card and experiencing crashes, the issue is likely the AMD driver crash (26.6.2+), which is a separate problem still under investigation.

Photo Mode HDR Crash Fix

Another crash trigger has been eliminated: entering Photo Mode after disabling HDR while HDR was previously enabled was hard-crashing the game. The transition state between HDR-on and HDR-off was not properly clearing the render state, causing a null pointer dereference when Photo Mode attempted to read HDR metadata. This is now fixed.

Ocean Fishing Area Fix

A minor but frustrating bug: certain ocean areas were incorrectly classified as "No Fishing Zones," preventing players from casting lines in perfectly valid fishing spots. The zone classification has been corrected, opening up previously blocked fishing areas.

How to Verify You Are on 1.12.02

On PC (Steam): The patch version displays as 1.12.02 in the main menu bottom-left corner. On consoles, the build number appears as 1.000.358 on PlayStation. If your version matches, you have the hotfix applied.

🏠 4. New Content — Outdoor Housing, 58 Items, New Gear & Skills

Outdoor Housing Decoration System

1.12 introduces the ability to decorate the exterior area around your house — a feature the community has requested since launch. The outdoor area functions similarly to indoor decoration but with larger-scale items: fountains, wells, bonfire pits, sotdaes (traditional poles), street lamps, and more. The space is generous, allowing for elaborate garden layouts, functional workshop areas, or purely aesthetic displays.

The 58 New Craftable Items

All outdoor items are crafted at two new facility types added in this patch:

  • Workstation — Found at Greymane Camp and Timberham Sawmill; used for crafting facilities and large items
  • Loom — Found at Hernand Tailor Shop; used for fabric-based items like rugs and hanging planters

The 58 items break down as follows:

  • Facilities (27): 4 tools, 10 sotdaes, 7 fountains, 6 wells
  • Lighting (14): 8 brazier types, 6 street lamp types
  • Decor/Furniture (17): 4 rugs, 6 hanging planters, 4 automaton toys, 1 music box, 2 pet furniture pieces

New Equipment

Kliff gets five new helmets (Silent Conqueror Plate Helm, Tough Sentinel Helm, Wild Sentinel Helm, Trust Sentinel Helm, Steel Jaw Plate Helm) and a new armor set (Greymane Light Armor). Damiane receives the Honored Greymane Cloth Gloves. A universal accessory, the Greymane Sigil, is available for all characters.

New Combat Skills — Slide Chain Attacks

Every weapon type now has unique combo attacks that can be performed while sliding. This is a significant combat addition that opens new approach angles and stagger opportunities. Slide attacks are initiated by attacking while in a slide state (running + crouch), and each weapon family has distinct animations and damage values. Experimentation is encouraged — the slide attacks for greatswords differ substantially from those for twin daggers.

Combat Tip: Slide attacks consume less stamina than dodge-roll attacks and can be chained into grapple attempts. For PvP, slide approaches are harder to read than sprinting attacks.

❓ FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

Does 1.12.02 fix all AMD crashes?

No. 1.12.02 specifically fixes the RX 5000 series crash at 1080p resolution. The separate AMD driver crash (driver 26.6.2+) affecting RX 6000/7000 series remains unresolved. Roll back to driver 26.6.1 for a stable experience on those cards.

How do I craft outdoor furniture?

Outdoor furniture requires either a Workstation (for facilities and large items) or a Loom (for fabric-based items). Recipes are obtained from various sources including merchants, claw machines, the Flower Basket quest, Boxes of Fate, and the Roulette Challenge. Check all sources to find what you need.

Can I move outdoor furniture after placing it?

Yes. Enter House Mode and interact with placed outdoor items to pick them up. The 1.12.01 hotfix resolved the issue where outdoor furniture would disappear in certain situations during relocation.

Are slide attacks available for all characters?

Yes. Kliff, Damiane, and Oongka all have weapon-specific slide chain attacks added in 1.12.00. The damage and animation vary by weapon type, so test different weapons to find your preferred slide combo.

How do I use the Box of Fate?

The Box of Fate is sold at the Mysterious Shop for 100 silver. Each restock cycle allows you to purchase up to 3 boxes. Opening them yields random items including furniture recipes, crafting materials, and cosmetics.

Does 1.12 fix the GTX 1060 white screen bug?

No. The GTX 1060 white screen issue (FSR Upscaling + Frame Generation) is still listed as a known issue. This remains unresolved as of 1.12.02.

Will my existing outdoor decorations be preserved after future updates?

Pearl Abyss has not made specific guarantees, but in previous updates (1.10 building rework), existing structures were preserved. It is reasonable to expect outdoor decorations to carry forward through future patches.

Is Epic Games Store still behind on patches?

As of June 28, 2026, the Epic Games Store is still awaiting the 1.12.02 patch. EGS users are on 1.12.01. Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, and Mac App Store all have 1.12.02.
