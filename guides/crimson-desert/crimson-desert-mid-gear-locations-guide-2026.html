â?FAQ

Q: Can I buy all mid-game gear from vendors instead of farming?

Not efficiently. While some gear can be purchased (Bolton Plate, basic accessories), the best mid-game items â?Witch's Earring, Necklace of Lightning, Mark of Darkness, Plate Armor of the Shadows â?are all found in strongboxes or boss drops. The only weapon worth buying directly is the Timberham Axe recipe from Rhett in Hernand. Everything else is better farmed or quested.

Q: Where is the best place to farm Abyss Artifacts?

The Ashen Rift is the best solo farm spot for Abyss Artifacts in mid-game. Dense enemy clusters, fast respawns (8-10 minutes), and a high drop rate (~15% per elite enemy). For late mid-game, the Crow's Nest Abyss Island is better â?elite enemies guarantee artifact drops, but the enemies are harder and require boat travel.

Q: Are strongbox locations shared between characters?

No. Each character has their own strongbox loot table. If you loot the Witch's Earring strongbox on Kliff, it will still be available to loot on Oongka and Damiane. This means you can gear all three characters from the same set of strongboxes without waiting for weekly resets.

Q: What is the fastest way to get Master-level lockpicks?

Master lockpicks are crafted from 1 Adept lockpick + 2 Abyss Crystals + 1 Sturdy Spring (drop from mechanical enemies in Scholastone Institute). Alternatively, purchase them from the Silver Wolf Mountain black market vendor for 2,500 silver each (requires reputation rank 2). You need Master lockpicks for Windmere Manor and Eastern Court strongboxes.

Q: Do weapon drops scale with my level?

No, weapon drops in Crimson Desert are fixed-level items. The Darkbringer that drops from Crowcaller at level 28 is the same Darkbringer that drops at level 40. What scales is the Refinement level of the weapon â?a +6 Darkbringer is always better than a +3 Darkbringer, regardless of when you acquired the base weapon.

Q: What should I do if I missed a time-limited quest reward?

Most missed quest rewards can be obtained from alternate sources. For example, if you missed "The Alchemist's Secret" (Engraved Gold Earring), you can still loot the second copy from Lioncrest Manor strongbox. If you missed "The Beast Tamer's Request" (Fallen Kingdom Gloves), loot them from the Southern Court strongbox. The only irreplaceable quest rewards are story-specific Abyss Gears (Groundsurge, Momentum) â?those must be obtained during their respective chapters.

Q: Can I farm multiple copies of the same accessory for different builds?

Yes, but only from weekly strongbox resets and enemy drops. The Demeniss strongboxes reset every Thursday, so you can farm a second Mark of Darkness for a dual-ring build over two weeks. Enemy-dropped accessories (Torn Earring, Scholar's Ring) can be farmed anytime but have low drop rates. The Witch's Ring from the Chapter 9 quest is a one-time reward per character.

Q: Is the Price Comparison Table accurate for all regions?

Prices listed are for the North American (NA) and European (EU) server regions. Asian (AS) and Korean (KR) server prices may vary slightly due to different vendor pricing and market board fluctuations. The silver costs for NPC vendors are consistent across all servers, but faction token and reputation requirements are identical globally.
