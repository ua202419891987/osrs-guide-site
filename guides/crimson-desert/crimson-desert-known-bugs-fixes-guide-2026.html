⚔️ 2. Combat & Character Bugs

Critical Bug — Damiane Shield Loss: If Damiane uses the Shield Toss skill and immediately dies (within 0.5 seconds of the throw), she permanently loses the ability to use her shield and all shield-related skills for the rest of the session. This is the most severe known bug in the game.

Workaround: Save & Reload

If Damiane loses her shield, save your game immediately and reload. The shield and all related skills will be restored upon reload. Pearl Abyss has confirmed a permanent fix is in development but has not provided a timeline. Until the fix arrives, avoid using Shield Toss when Damiane is at low health — the risk of dying mid-throw is not worth the skill's damage.

Evade Key Binding Bug

When the "Evasion Control" setting is set to "Hold" (instead of "Toggle"), changing the evade key binding does not fully override the default key. The default evade key will still register inputs even after rebinding. Workaround: Switch Evasion Control to "Toggle" mode, or leave the evade key at its default setting until a fix is released.

📜 3. Quest & Faction Bugs

Greymane Faction Quest Bug: When playing as Damiane or Oongka, certain Greymane faction quests cannot be progressed. The quest NPC does not respond or the quest objective does not update after completing the required action.

Workaround: Switch to Kliff

Switch your active character to Kliff and approach the quest NPC. The quest will progress normally with Kliff. Once the quest stage is complete, you can switch back to Damiane or Oongka. Pearl Abyss has acknowledged this bug and is working on a fix that allows all characters to progress faction quests.

For more on the companion system and character switching, see our Damiane Companion Guide.

Life Skills Bug

Fishing — Fish Cannot Be Moved

In certain fishing zones (particularly the Ruined Garrison Moat during rainy weather), caught fish cannot be transferred from the fishing inventory to the main inventory. Workaround: Save the game and reload. The fish will transfer correctly after the reload. See our Fishing Guide for more details.

Mount Equipment Bug

Phoenix & Iron Eagle — Sigil of Valor

The Phoenix and Iron Eagle mounts cannot equip the Sigil of Valor item. The equip prompt does not appear when attempting to use the item on these mounts. No workaround available — pending fix.

📞 6. How to Report New Bugs

If you encounter a bug not listed here, report it through Pearl Abyss's official bug report portal at pearlabyss.info/crimsondesertreport. Include:

  • Platform (PC / PS5 / Xbox / Mac)
  • Character and level
  • Exact steps to reproduce the bug
  • Screenshot or video if possible
  • Whether the bug is consistent or intermittent

❓ FAQ — Bug Questions

Q: Will my save file be corrupted by any of these bugs?

No. None of the known bugs cause save corruption. The Damiane shield bug and the fishing bug are both resolved by saving and reloading, which is safe. Always keep multiple save slots as a general precaution.

Q: How often does Pearl Abyss patch the game?

Pearl Abyss releases patches approximately every 2-3 weeks. Major patches that include bug fixes are typically announced 3-5 days in advance on the official Crimson Desert website and social media.

Q: Is the Damiane shield bug going to be fixed soon?

Pearl Abyss has confirmed they are working on a fix but has not provided a specific patch date. Until then, the save/reload workaround is 100% reliable. To avoid triggering the bug, do not use Shield Toss when Damiane is below 30% health.

Q: I found a bug not listed here — what should I do?

Report it at the official bug report portal (pearlabyss.info/crimsondesertreport). Include your platform, character details, reproduction steps, and screenshots. Do not assume someone else has reported it — duplicate reports help Pearl Abyss prioritize fixes.
