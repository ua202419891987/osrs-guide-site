🧙‍♀️ 1. Six Witches Locations — Names, Regions & Sanctums

Witches are the most important NPCs in Crimson Desert's endgame progression system. They are the only NPCs who can embed, extract, and craft Abyss Gear — the game's most powerful equipment customization system. There are six witches total, each tied to a specific region and unlock condition. Three witches (Sylvia, Elowen) are unlocked through story progression and White Crow letters, while the remaining three (Lyselia, Bari, Areciel) are pure exploration finds. The sixth witch, the White Crow herself, requires completing all other witches' Sanctum quests. To track down every Mysterious Stone and other collectibles tied to these NPCs, see our Mysterious Stones & Collectibles Guide.

Below is a complete breakdown of every witch, their location, how to find them, and what services they offer. All witch locations are verified for Patch 1.12 (June 2026).

Witch #1 — Hernand Region

🧹 Sylvia (Hermit Witch)

Title: The Hermit Witch

Region: Hernand

True Location: Shadow's Whisper Cave — a hidden cave on the mountainside north of Hernand City. From Hernand's north gate, follow the road north until you reach a river. Before crossing, turn northwest and hug the cliff wall. The cave entrance is partially obscured by rock overhang.

Disguised Form: None. Sylvia does not disguise herself — she is found directly in her cave.

Unlock Condition: Complete Chapter 1 ("The Woman in White") to unlock flight ability. Wait for the White Crow to deliver a message. After receiving the letter, Sylvia's cave becomes accessible.

Sanctums: None (she is a tutorial witch with no Sanctum quests)

Core Services: Abyss Gear Embedding, Abyss Gear Extraction, Socket Creation. She serves as the introductory workbench for the Abyss system. Do not forget to grab the Palma Pill recipe before leaving the cave, and activate the Abyss Hub fast travel point just outside.
Witch #2 — Hernand Region

📖 Elowen (Witch of Wisdom)

Title: The Witch of Wisdom

Region: Hernand, Witchwoods

Disguised Form: In the Witchwoods, west of Meshley's Trap Workshop. Enter the eastern house to find a bound woman — this is Elowen in disguise. Free her.

True Location: A small cabin a few hundred meters north of the Trap Workshop, in the Witchwoods proper. She will be waiting there after you free her from the trap workshop.

Unlock Condition: Complete Chapter 5 ("The Demeniss Delegation"). After this, the White Crow appears and delivers a letter pointing you toward the Witchwoods.

Sanctums: 4 Sanctums — Absolution, Temperance, Benediction, Penitence

Core Services: Full witch workbench (socketing, extracting, crafting, Abyss Gear fusion). Elowen is the first witch to offer Abyss Gear Fusion — combining two Abyss Gear pieces to create an entirely new piece (stats do NOT merge; a new random piece is generated).
Witch #3 — Demeniss / Serpent Marsh Region

🕊️ Lyselia (Witch of Humility)

Title: The Witch of Humility

Region: Demeniss / Serpent Marsh

Disguised Form: North Demeniss, west of the dead volcano (Beighen area — reached during Chapter 7). Find a broken bridge over the Deng River. A woman is hanging from the bridge's edge. Pull her up.

True Location: A hidden cave on an island in the Serpent Marsh (south Demeniss, naturally reached during Chapter 9). The cave entrance is marked by colorful flags.

Unlock Condition: Exploration-based discovery. No White Crow letter required. Can be found as early as Chapter 7 if you explore the Serpent Marsh thoroughly.

Sanctums: 4 Sanctums — Devotion, Veneration, Faith, Oblation

Core Services: Full witch workbench. Her shop inventory includes Demeniss-exclusive Abyss Gear blueprints that cannot be purchased from any other witch.
Witch #4 — Pailune Region

🤝 Bari (Witch of Kindness)

Title: The Witch of Kindness

Region: Pailune

Disguised Form: Southeast Wayward Woods (Pailune). An old woman huddling under a makeshift tarp shelter. Interact with the lantern next to her to light the fire and warm her. She will reveal her identity.

True Location: North of the disguised location, on an island in Crimson Lake (central Pailune). Her cabin is at the center of the lake. You can reach it by crossing the ice bridge (south side) or swimming.

Unlock Condition: Exploration-based discovery. She is widely considered the hardest witch to find because her disguised form does NOT show a white quest marker on the map. You must discover her organically.

Sanctums: 4 Sanctums — Exaltation, Solace, Deliverance, Atonement

Core Services: Full witch workbench. Her cabin sells frost-based Abyss Gear blueprints and ice-enhancement materials. These are particularly effective against fire-type enemies and southern-region bosses.
Witch #5 — Crimson Desert Region

💪 Areciel (Witch of Strength)

Title: The Witch of Strength

Region: Crimson Desert

Disguised Form: North of Tashkalp Territory, at Tommaso Fork Posthouse. Near the bounty board and waystation, there is a child sitting on a ridge. Give the child 1 Copper Coin. The child transforms — this is Areciel's disguised form.

True Location: Head northwest toward Urdavah, to the area marked "World's Navel" on the map. Find the westernmost fast travel point. Her house is embedded in the cliff face. When you enter, she tells you she is on the roof — climb up to speak with her.

Unlock Condition: Must have completed Chapter 8 and started Chapter 9 (first arrival in the Crimson Desert region). Exploration-based discovery.

Sanctums: 3 Sanctums — Expiation, Renunciation, Revelation

Core Services: Full witch workbench. Her desert-themed Abyss Gear blueprints focus on heat resistance and fire damage bonuses — essential for Crimson Desert exploration and the Abyssal Rift.
Witch #6 — Pailune Region (Final Witch)

🐦⬛ The White Crow (Final Witch)

Title: The White Crow

Region: Pailune, Wayward Woods

Location: Southern Pailune, Wayward Woods. A small, unassuming cabin in the southern part of the woods. The cabin door has 4 keyholes.

Unlock Condition: Complete ALL Sanctum quests for Elowen, Lyselia, Bari, and Areciel (15 Sanctums total). Each of these four witches will give you a Witch's Token upon completing their Sanctum chain. Insert all 4 tokens into the door of the cabin to open it.

Sanctums: 1 Sanctum — Tribulation (unlocked through Chapter 9 main story quest "Fragments of Darkness")

Core Services: The White Crow offers the highest-tier Abyss Gear services in the game, including:
  • Legendary Abyss Gear Crafting — craft the rarest Abyss Gear pieces using materials from all regions
  • Stat Re-rolling — re-roll the bonus stats on existing Abyss Gear (costs Abyss Cores)
  • Exclusive Blueprints — blueprints for the "White Crow" set (best-in-slot for magic builds)
Witch Strategy: We recommend finding all six witches in this order: Sylvia (early-game, Hernand) → Elowen (mid-game, Chapter 5) → Lyselia → Bari → Areciel → White Crow. Do not rush the Sanctum quests — each Sanctum is a significant time investment, but the rewards (Witch's Tokens → The White Crow) are essential for endgame Abyss Gear optimization. The White Crow's Legendary crafting is what separates "good" builds from "god-tier" builds.

💰 2. Taxman Location & Services — Hernand Bank & Tax Quest

The Taxman (technically a Hernandian Guard stationed at the bank) is one of the first important NPCs you will interact with in Hernand City. He is the gatekeeper to the banking system — and banking is essential for managing large sums of gold and storing items. The quest involving him is called "The Price of Coin" and it triggers automatically under specific conditions.

Tax Quest Trigger — "The Price of Coin"

This quest activates automatically when you obtain a large sum of money through illegal means. Specifically:

  • Selling stolen goods to a fence or vendor
  • Exchanging stolen gold bars at a currency exchange
  • Completing certain morally ambiguous side quests that reward "hot" money

Once triggered, a Tax Notice appears in your inventory. You have an unpaid fine of 50 Silver and must visit the Hernand Bank to resolve it.

Location: Hernand Bank

Address: Northwest side of Hernand Town. The bank is a large stone building with iron-barred windows and a prominent sign showing a golden coin.

Key NPC: Hernandian Guard (blocking the entrance)

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

📋 How to Pay Your Taxes

Step 1: Check your inventory. Ensure you have the Tax Notice and at least 50 Silver.

Step 2: Fast travel to Hernand City. Navigate to the northwest side of town to find the bank.

Step 3: Approach the bank entrance. A Hernandian Guard blocks your way. Speak with him.

Step 4: In the dialogue menu, select the option to pay the fine. The guard will accept your 50 Silver and step aside.

Step 5: Enter the bank. You now have permanent access to banking services.

Rewards for Paying the Tax

RewardDetails
Bank AccessPermanent access to Hernand Bank — store silver and manage items
+3 Inventory SlotsPermanent backpack expansion. This alone is worth the 50 Silver fee
Unicorn Glass Art PieceA decorative collectible worth approximately 100 Silver when sold to a merchant
Net ProfitYou spend 50 Silver and receive an item worth ~100 Silver — a net gain of 50 Silver plus the inventory slots
Pro Tip: Sell the Unicorn Glass Art Piece immediately to any merchant for a quick ~100 Silver profit. This turns the tax payment into a net positive transaction. Do NOT hold onto it for decoration — it is a generic item that can be re-acquired (though not easily), and the Silver is more useful in the early game.

What Happens If You Ignore the Tax?

You can safely ignore the tax notice. You will NOT become wanted or hunted by guards. However, the bank remains permanently locked until you pay. Since bank access is critical for storing the large sums of gold you will accumulate in mid-to-late game, we strongly recommend paying the 50 Silver as soon as possible. There is no benefit to delaying.

Important Distinction: The tax system (fines) is separate from the Bounty system. If you commit crimes (murder, theft in sight of guards), you accumulate a bounty — not a fine. Bounties are cleared by purchasing a Writ of Absolution from Alfred at the Hernand Church. Do not confuse the two systems. The tax is a one-time story event; bounties are a recurring crime mechanic.

🏪 3. Key Vendors — Every Region's Essential Merchants

Each region in Crimson Desert has its own set of vendors with region-specific inventory, prices, and crafting materials. Below is a comprehensive directory of every key vendor organized by region.

🏘️ Hernand Region — Starting Hub

🔧 Turnali — Hernand Blacksmith

Location: Hernand City center, near the fountain.

Services: Weapon/Armor Refinement, sells basic forging materials. Available from the start of the game.

Note: Complete the "Blacksmith's Daughter" side quest (before Chapter 7) to receive "Gorin's Gratitude" — permanent -10% discount at all blacksmith merchants.

🧵 Hernand Tailor

Location: Hernand City center, near Turnali.

Services: Sells cloth armor and light armor sets. Available from the start.

⚔️ Hernand Equipment Merchant

Location: Hernand City center.

Services: Sells basic weapons and shields. Inventory refreshes as you progress through story chapters.

🛒 Hernand General Goods Merchant

Location: Hernand City, near the entrance.

Services: Sells cooking ingredients, basic consumables, crafting materials. Automatically unlocked.

⛪ Alfred — Hernand Church

Location: Hernand Church (central Hernand).

Services: Sells Writs of Absolution (clears bounty), provides blessings. Essential for players engaging in criminal activities.

📋 Bounty Board NPC

Location: Various cities and outdoor locations across Hernand.

Services: Posts combat bounties; reputation shop unlocks rare items (including Abyss Gear blueprints) after completing enough bounties.

🏕️ Greymane Camp — Mobile Base (Unlocked Chapter 2+)

🔨 Greymane Camp Blacksmith

Unlock: Chapter 3.

Services: Weapon/Armor Refinement without needing to return to Hernand. The most convenient blacksmith in the game once unlocked.

🍳 Greymane Camp Cooking Station NPC

Unlock: Available from camp establishment (Chapter 2).

Services: Teaches basic recipes; unlocks advanced recipes as camp upgrades progress.

⚔️ Greymane Combat Trainer

Unlock: After camp upgrade.

Services: Provides training dummies for testing weapons and skill combinations. No cost — purely for practice.

🤝 Yann — Greymane Faction NPC

Unlock: Complete related faction quests.

Services: Unlocks the Royal Trade system; grants access to Goldleaf Trading Posts. Yann provides exclusive high-value trade goods and opens a passive income stream. Essential for the economy-minded player.

🌿 Oongka & Naira — Greymane Companions

Unlock: Story progression.

Services: Each provides a unique questline with exclusive rewards. Oongka offers survival-themed rewards; Naira offers combat-focused rewards.

⚔️ Demeniss Region — Political Heart

⚔️ Demeniss Weapon Expert

Location: Demeniss City armory district.

Services: Sells mid-to-high tier weapons, including rare variants with pre-socketed Abyss Gear. Better quality than Hernand vendors.

🛡️ Demeniss Armor Merchant

Location: Demeniss City.

Services: Sells heavy plate armor sets. Best option for defense-focused builds in the mid-game.

🔧 Demeniss Advanced Blacksmith

Location: Demeniss City forge district.

Services: Advanced refinement using materials unobtainable in Hernand. Can reach higher refinement tiers than Turnali.

🏛️ Demeniss Faction NPCs (House Sorell / House Byron)

Location: Demeniss City faction headquarters.

Services: Offer faction-specific questlines. House Sorell rewards heavy armor sets; House Byron rewards agility equipment. Choose your faction carefully — this choice impacts endgame gear options.

❄️ Pailune Region — Frozen North

🔧 Pailune Blacksmith

Location: Pailune City forge.

Services: Refinement using cold-region materials (Frost Iron, Glacier Crystal). Essential for certain weapon types that require cold-environment refinement.

🧥 Pailune Fur Trader

Location: Pailune City market.

Services: Sells cold resistance clothing and gear. Mandatory for exploring Pailune's deeper frozen areas without taking constant cold damage.

🧪 Pailune General Goods

Location: Pailune City.

Services: Sells warming potions, high-calorie foods that extend cold-environment buffs.

🔧 Delesyia Region — Technology Frontier

🔧 Delesyia Master Blacksmith

Location: Delesyia industrial district.

Services: The HIGHEST refinement tier in the game. Uses Ether Alloy and Prism Cores to push weapons to their absolute stat potential. This is the blacksmith you visit for endgame gear.

🔫 Delesyia Ranged Weapon Merchant

Location: Delesyia City.

Services: Sells firearms, arm-mounted micro-cannons, and other tech weapons. The only region where ranged-tech weapons are available for purchase.

⚗️ Delesyia Alchemist

Location: Delesyia alchemist quarter.

Services: Crafts advanced consumables — combat buffs, elemental resistance potions, Abyss Gear efficiency boosters. The materials are expensive, but the potions are game-changing for endgame content.

🧪 Delesyia General Goods

Location: Delesyia City.

Services: The ONLY vendor that sells firearm ammunition crafting components and micro-cannon maintenance materials. Essential for tech-weapon builds.

🏜️ Crimson Desert Region — Lawless Frontier

🏴️ Crimson Desert Black Market Merchant

Location: Various camps in the Crimson Desert.

Services: Sells stolen goods, illegal modification parts, contraband materials. Accepts both Silver and crime-specific currency.

🔄 Crimson Desert Fence

Location: Hidden locations in the Crimson Desert (moves periodically).

Services: Buys stolen goods at higher prices than regular merchants. Sells sandstorm-resistant armor and heat-insulated accessories. Requires some criminal activity to unlock access.

🔍 4. Hidden Merchants — Secret NPCs Worth Finding

Beyond the standard vendors, Crimson Desert hides several secret NPCs that offer unique services or items available nowhere else. These are not marked on the map and require exploration, specific conditions, or puzzle-solving to access.

🗡️ Retired Greymane Weaponsmith

Location: A cave or mountain shrine in Pailune, appearing only during specific weather conditions (heavy snowstorms). Look for smoke trails from cave entrances and unusual animal behavior near hidden paths.

Services: Sells a unique greatsword blueprint — the only one of its kind in the game. The weapon has the highest base damage of any greatsword.

Finding Tip: Visit Pailune's mountain region during a snowstorm and look for smoke that does not match nearby settlements.

📖 Vane's Cache (Hidden Merchant — Missable)

Location: Unlocked after completing "The Conspiracy of Shadows" quest (see our Secrets Guide).

Services: Sells unique items not available anywhere else — including the "Shadow Operative" armor set and exclusive consumables.

Missable Warning: Must complete the quest before Chapter 8. If Vane dies in the main story, this merchant is lost permanently.

⚗️ Desert Hermit (Crimson Desert)

Location: Deep in the Crimson Desert interior, away from all settlements and roads. No map marker.

Services: Provides rare item blueprints, one-time quests with unique rewards, and the "Hermit's Lore" knowledge entry. The hermit is a former adventurer who retired to the desert and only speaks to those who prove their worth by reaching his hideout.

Finding Tip: Talk to NPCs in other regions who drop cryptic hints about "the old man in the sand." Collect these hints to narrow down the search area.

🎭 Masked Trader (Traveling NPC)

Location: Travels between regions on a randomized schedule. Sometimes at Hernand crossroads, sometimes at Demeniss docks, sometimes deep in Pailune.

Services: Sells "Mystery Boxes" — randomized loot containers that can contain anything from common crafting materials to rare Abyss Gear. A gamble, but the potential payoff is significant. The Masked Trader also accepts unusual currency (rare monster drops, unique collectibles) that other merchants do not.

Finding Tip: There is no reliable way to track the Masked Trader. The community maintains a crowdsourced tracking thread on r/CrimsonDesert — check there for recent sightings.

💎 Goldleaf Trading Post Quartermasters

Location: One per major region. Unlocked after Yann's faction questline.

Services: Sell luxury goods and rare items; offer cross-region trade contracts (buy low in one region, sell high in another). The Goldleaf network is how you generate passive income in the late game.

Unlock: Complete Yann's questline at Greymane Camp.

❓ FAQ — NPC Locations & Services Questions Answered

How many witches are there in Crimson Desert?

There are six witches total: Sylvia (Hermit Witch, Hernand), Elowen (Witch of Wisdom, Hernand Witchwoods), Lyselia (Witch of Humility, Demeniss/Serpent Marsh), Bari (Witch of Kindness, Pailune), Areciel (Witch of Strength, Crimson Desert), and The White Crow (Final Witch, Pailune Wayward Woods). The White Crow only becomes accessible after completing all Sanctum quests for the other four regional witches.

Where is the taxman in Crimson Desert?

The taxman is a Hernandian Guard standing at the entrance of the Hernand Bank (northwest side of Hernand Town). He triggers the "The Price of Coin" quest when you have a Tax Notice in your inventory (obtained by selling stolen goods or exchanging stolen gold bars). Paying the 50 Silver fine unlocks bank access, +3 inventory slots, and a Unicorn Glass Art Piece worth ~100 Silver.

What is the difference between a witch and a regular merchant?

Regular merchants sell gear, materials, and consumables. Witches are the ONLY NPCs that provide Abyss Gear services: creating sockets, embedding Abyss Gear, extracting Abyss Gear, crafting new Abyss Gear, fusing Abyss Gear (Elowen+), and stat re-rolling (The White Crow only). Witches also sell Abyss Gear blueprints and Abyss Cores — materials that regular merchants do not stock.

Can I miss NPCs permanently in Crimson Desert?

Yes — several NPCs are missable if you progress the main story too far. The most critical missable NPCs are: Gorin's Blacksmith Daughter quest (before Chapter 7 — rewards permanent -10% blacksmith discount), Spymaster Vane (before Chapter 8 — unlocks hidden merchant Vane's Cache), and the Stray Dog companion (before Chapter 7 — if the dog dies or you progress too far). Create manual saves before Chapters 7 and 8.

Which witch should I visit first?

Sylvia (Hermit Witch) should be your first witch visit. She is located in Hernand, accessible after Chapter 1, and serves as the tutorial witch for the Abyss Gear system. She provides socket creation, embedding, and extraction services — all of which you will need immediately upon unlocking your first Abyss Gear piece. Visit her as soon as you receive the White Crow's letter after Chapter 1.

How do I unlock the White Crow witch?

Unlocking The White Crow requires: (1) Complete all Sanctum quests for Elowen (4 Sanctums), Lyselia (4 Sanctums), Bari (4 Sanctums), and Areciel (3 Sanctums) — 15 Sanctums total. (2) Each of these four witches gives you a Witch's Token after completing their Sanctum chain. (3) Take all 4 tokens to the cabin in southern Wayward Woods (Pailune) and insert them into the 4 keyholes on the door. The White Crow is inside. This is a significant time investment — expect 15-25 hours of Sanctum questing.

Is there a way to reset NPC schedules or force them to appear?

You can use the "Wait" function at any campfire or inn to advance time. This is the primary method for aligning with NPC schedules. However, some NPCs (like the Masked Trader and Desert Hermit) appear on randomized or conditional schedules that cannot be forced. The Retired Greymane Weaponsmith only appears during snowstorms, which are weather events you cannot manually trigger (unless you use the rare "Weather Manipulation" consumable sold by the Delesyia Alchemist).

Which region has the best merchants overall?

Delesyia has the strongest vendor lineup for endgame players: the Master Blacksmith (highest refinement tier), the Ranged Weapon Merchant (unique tech weapons), and the Advanced Alchemist (best consumables). However, for day-to-day convenience, the Greymane Camp NPCs (unlocked progressively through Chapters 2-4) are the most practical — they offer refinement, cooking, and training all in one location without needing to fast travel.
