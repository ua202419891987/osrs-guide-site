💡 Quick Verdict — F2P Gear Progression 2026

F2P gear progression is simple: follow the scimitar path (Iron → Steel → Mithril → Adamant → Rune), get 40 Defence for Rune armor after Dragon Slayer, and skip everything else. The single best F2P melee weapon at every level bracket is the scimitar (4-tick attack speed). Don't buy the 2h swords, don't buy the longswords, don't buy the warhammers. Scimitar or go home. Total gear cost to reach BiS F2P: ~250K GP (Rune scimitar 32K + Rune armor 80K + Amulet of Power 1.2K + Green d'hide 50K + Lobsters 50K).

Free-to-Play gear progression is one of the most straightforward parts of OSRS — but also one of the most confusing for new players who don't know which weapons are worth buying. This guide gives you the exact item to buy at each attack/defence level, how much it costs, where to buy it, and whether it's actually worth your GP. No fluff, no "Maybe consider this..." — just the optimal path.

1.2 🚫 Weapons NOT to Buy (Common Mistakes)

❌ Skip These Weapons
  • 2h Swords (Iron → Rune): 7-tick attack speed. A Rune 2h hits every 4.2 seconds; a Rune scimitar hits every 2.4 seconds. The 2h does ~20% more damage per hit, but the scimitar attacks 2.9× more often. Scimitar always wins on DPS.
  • Longswords: Same speed as scimitars, but lower Strength bonus. No reason to buy.
  • Battleaxes: 5-tick speed, same problem as 2h. Skip.
  • Warhammers: Crush only, slow, low DPS. Only buy if you're doing a Strength pure build (1 Attack) and have no other option.
  • Maces: Crush only, slow. The Dragon mace (members) is good, but F2P maces are trash. Skip.

2.2 🗡️ Dragon Slayer — The Defence 40 Gateway

Dragon Slayer is the most important quest in F2P. After completing it, you can wear Rune platebody (requires 40 Defence). This single item gives +50 Defence and +20 Strength — it doubles your survivability and adds 1-2 max hit in melee.

💡 Should You Rush Dragon Slayer at 40 Combat?

Yes. Even if your stats are low (40/40/40 Attack/Strength/Defence), Dragon Slayer is doable with Lobsters and patience. The Rune platebody is a career-defining upgrade for F2P accounts. If you're struggling, level Attack to 50 first — the extra max hit makes a big difference against Elvarg (level 83).

⑤ ❓ FAQ — F2P Gear Progression 2026

Q: Is the Rune 2h sword ever better than the Rune scimitar?

A: No. The Rune 2h has +82 Strength bonus vs. the scimitar's +66, but its 7-tick speed means it attacks 43% slower. The scimitar's 4-tick speed and higher hit rate (more hits = more chances to max hit) always win on DPS. The only exception: if you're doing a Strength pure build (1 Attack, 99 Strength) and can't use a scimitar, the Rune 2h is your best option — but this is a niche PK build, not a general PvM setup.

Q: Should I buy a Dragon scimitar in F2P?

A: You can't use a Dragon scimitar in F2P (requires members world or trading with a members player). However, if you plan to go members within a week, skip the Rune scimitar and save your 32K GP toward the Dragon scimitar (~120K GP on GE). The Dragon scimitar has +71 Strength bonus (+5 over Rune) and the same 4-tick speed — it's a strict upgrade.

Q: What's the best F2P crush weapon for Vest'ion / Calvar'ion?

A: Viggora's chainmace (requires 60 Attack, obtained from Wilderness content). It has +48 Strength bonus and a 4-tick speed. When charged with Revenant Ether and used inside the Wilderness vs. NPCs, it gains +50% accuracy and +50% damage — making it the best crush weapon for Wilderness bossing. Outside the Wilderness (or uncharged), it's outclassed by the Zamorakian Hasta (members). For pure F2P crush training (e.g., against the Warriors' Guild — members only), the Rune mace (+40 Strength) is your best F2P option.

Q: Do I need Prayer bonus gear in F2P?

A: Not really. F2P has very limited Prayer training (burying Big Bones gives 4.5 XP per bone; the Altar in Lumbridge Church is free but slow). The only Prayer bonus gear in F2P is the Amulet of Power (+1 Prayer bonus) and Rune armor (no Prayer bonus). If you want to use Protection prayers (members feature), you need 43 Prayer — but F2P players rarely have this because training Prayer is so slow. Most F2P players fight with 0 Prayer bonus and bring food instead.

Q: Can I dye my Rune armor in F2P?

A: No. Armor dyeing is a members-only feature (spray cans are members items). Your Rune armor will always look like... Rune armor. If you want cosmetic variety, consider Green d'hide (vambraces + body) for a different look — it's a members item but tradeable to F2P, and it gives Ranged defence bonuses too.

Q: What's the most GP-efficient way to train Defence in F2P?

A: Defence training in F2P is slow because you need to take damage to gain XP. The most GP-efficient method: train Defence by doing Slayer tasks (you gain Defence XP passively while fighting). If you want to train Defence directly, fight Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon) — they have low Defence (crush def ~60), decent HP (36), and drop Big Bones (sell for ~200 GP each). Bring Lobsters and an Amulet of Power (+1 Prayer bonus helps with afk time). Expect ~15K-20K Defence XP per hour.

🎯 Final Tips — F2P Gear in 2026

  • Weapon > Armor: Always upgrade your weapon first. A Rune scimitar with full iron armor is stronger than an Iron scimitar with full rune armor.
  • Dragon Slayer is mandatory: 40 Defence + Rune platebody is the biggest power spike in F2P. Rush it.
  • Scimitar or go home: All other F2P weapons are inferior. Don't overthink it.
  • Save for members: If you're 80% sure you'll go members within 30 days, skip the Rune armor and save your GP for members gear (Dragon scimitar, Abyssal Whip, etc.).
