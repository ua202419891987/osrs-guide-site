1. What Is AFK Money Making in OSRS?

AFK (Away From Keyboard) money making refers to activities where you can earn GP with minimal interaction — typically clicking once every 30 seconds to 5 minutes. These methods trade maximum GP/hour for convenience, allowing you to build wealth while doing other things in real life.

The term "AFK" in OSRS covers a spectrum:

⚡ AFK Intensity Levels

  • True AFK (5-20 minute intervals): Nightmare Zone with absorption pots, Redwood trees, fishing spot (no movement)
  • Light AFK (30-90 second intervals): Most skilling methods, bank-standing skills, motherlode mine
  • Semi-AFK (10-30 second intervals): Bossing, Slayer tasks, some skilling with banking
  • Active (constant clicking): Not covered here — this guide is for low-effort methods

In 2026, the OSRS economy has stabilised around several key AFK methods. The best part? Many of these activities also train your skills, so you are progressing your account while earning GP.

💡 The AFK Trade-Off

AFK methods typically earn 30-60% less GP/hour than active methods at the same level. For example, active Zulrah earns 2-3M GP/hour, while AFK Kraken earns 600-1.8M GP/hour. However, you can AFK for 4-6 hours straight without burning out — making the total GP earned often higher than active methods you would quit after 30 minutes.

2. AFK Skilling Methods

Skilling is the bread and butter of AFK money making. These methods require minimal gear investment and can be started with relatively low levels. Here are the best money-making skilling methods ranked by AFK-friendliness:

🎣 Fishing (AFK Level: High)

Fishing is one of the most AFK-friendly skills in OSRS because of the way fishing spots work. Once you start fishing, your character auto-fishes until your inventory is full or the spot moves.

① Anglerfish (Level 82 Fishing, 180-350K GP/hr)

Location: Piscatoris Fishing Colony (requires Swan Song quest).

Method: Drop your fishing net in the anglerfish pool and wait. Each inventory takes 2-4 minutes to fill. Anglerfish heal 22 HP each and sell for 1,800-2,400 GP each on the GE. With 99 Fishing and the fishing cape, you can catch roughly 100-120 fish per hour.

AFK rating: 9/10 — click once every 2-4 minutes, spot rarely moves.

② Monkfish (Level 62 Fishing, 100-150K GP/hr)

Location: Fishing spots south of Port Piscarilius (unlocked via Client of Kourend).

Method: Similar to Anglerfish but lower level requirement. Monkfish heal 16 HP each and sell for 600-800 GP. The spot moves slightly more often but still offers solid AFK time. Great entry-level AFK money maker.

AFK rating: 8/10 — click once every 1-3 minutes.

③ Infernal Eels (Level 80 Fishing, 120-180K GP/hr)

Location: Tiaf in the TzHaar area (requires completion of the Fight Caves entrance).

Method: Fish Infernal Eels with an infernal harpoon or fishing rod. They are stackable in your inventory and the spot never moves. The GP comes from the onyx bolts you can fletch from the onyx bolt tips — both the raw eels and bolt tips sell well on the GE.

AFK rating: 10/10 — click once and forget until inventory fills. Spot never moves.

🍃 Woodcutting (AFK Level: High)

Woodcutting is another classic AFK skill. The key is finding trees that take a long time to chop so you can click once and wait 60-90 seconds between logs.

① Redwood Trees (Level 90 Woodcutting, 40-80K GP/hr)

Location: Woodcutting Guild (requires 60% Hosidius favour).

Method: Redwood trees take 90-120 seconds to fully chop down. This is the ultimate AFK Woodcutting experience. Each redwood log sells for 3,500-4,500 GP. At 90 Woodcutting you get roughly 12-18 logs per hour, depending on your axe and level.

AFK rating: 10/10 — click a tree, wait 90-120 seconds, click the next one. Nearly zero effort.

② Magic Trees (Level 75 Woodcutting, 50-80K GP/hr)

Location: Woodcutting Guild, Seers' Village, or Mage Training Arena.

Method: Magic trees take roughly 60-90 seconds per log. Each magic log sells for 1,000-1,400 GP. You'll get roughly 50-70 logs per hour. The Woodcutting Guild offers a bank chest nearby for quick banking.

AFK rating: 9/10 — click a tree, wait 60-90 seconds, repeat.

③ Yew Trees (Level 60 Woodcutting, 30-50K GP/hr)

Location: Varrock Castle, Falador, Woodcutting Guild, and many other spots.

Method: Yew logs sell for 400-500 GP each. They take about 30-45 seconds per log. This is the best low-level AFK money maker and doubles as solid Woodcutting XP. Drop or bank when inventory is full.

AFK rating: 8/10 — less AFK time per click but still very relaxed.

⚏ Mining (AFK Level: High)

① Motherlode Mine (Level 30 Mining, 30-120K GP/hr)

Location: Motherlode Mine, north of Falador (requires 30 Mining and the Dwarven Mine entrance).

Method: Mine pay-dirt from the veins, deposit it in the hopper, and collect ore from the sack. The higher your Mining level, the better ore you get. At 85+ Mining you start getting Runite ore worth 10-12K each. Full cycle takes 5-10 minutes of AFK mining followed by 30 seconds of active sifting.

AFK rating: 9/10 — click a vein, wait 30-60 seconds, click next. Needs Prospector outfit for optimal rates. For a full breakdown of MLM mechanics and routes, check our Motherlode Mine Guide.

② Amethyst Mining (Level 92 Mining, 120-150K GP/hr)

Location: Mining Guild basement (requires 60 Mining to enter the guild, but the amethyst veins are on the lower level).

Method: Amethyst is the holy grail of AFK mining. Each vein takes 60-90 seconds to deplete, and amethyst sells for 1,500-1,800 GP each. With expert mining gloves and celestial ring, you can expect 80-100 amethyst per hour. The bank chest in the Mining Guild makes banking instant.

AFK rating: 10/10 — click a vein, wait 60-90 seconds, repeat. Best AFK mining method in the game.

🔮 Magic (AFK Level: Medium-High)

① String Jewelry (Level 84 Magic, 400-1.8M GP/hr)

Location: Edgeville bank (or any bank with a nearby respawn point).

Method: Use the Lunar Spell "String Jewellery" on a full inventory of unstrung diamond amulets. Each inventory takes roughly 10-15 seconds to cast, then you bank and repeat. The profit comes from the difference between unstrung amulets and strung amulets. At 84 Magic with the lunar spellbook, this is one of the best bank-standing AFK methods.

AFK rating: 7/10 — short bursts of activity with full attention needed for banking, but the actual spellcasting is one click per inventory.

② Plank Make (Level 86 Magic, 350-350K GP/hr)

Location: Any bank.

Method: Cast the Lunar spell "Plank Make" on noted logs. You'll need the Lunar spellbook unlocked via the quest "Lunar Diplomacy." Each cast converts one set of noted logs into a plank. Teak planks offer the best profit margin at roughly 400-500 GP profit per cast. You can cast roughly 1,400-1,400 times per hour.

AFK rating: 6/10 — requires constant clicking but the rhythm is steady and predictable.

3. AFK Combat Methods

Combat AFK methods are incredibly popular because they train your combat stats AND generate profit simultaneously. Here are the top options:

⚔ Nightmare Zone (400-1.8M GP/hr)

Requirements: 70+ Combat, 40+ Prayer, completion of several quests for boss unlocks.

Nightmare Zone (NMZ) is widely considered the best AFK money maker in OSRS. The strategy:

🎲 NMZ AFK Setup

  • Gear: Full Obsidian armour + Obsidian sword/maul (or Dharok's set for higher level players)
  • Inventory: 26 Absorption potions (1), Super combat potion, Overload (if available)
  • Bosses: Select only easy bosses like Count Draynor, King Roald, Tree Spirit, Khazard Warlord, and Trapped Soul
  • Prayer: Use Rapid Heal + Ultimate Strength + Incredible Reflexes
  • AFK time: 15-20 minutes per inventory — your absorption potions absorb all damage

The GP comes from NMZ reward points which you spend on Herb Boxes (buyable daily, 15 boxes per day at 1,500 points each = roughly 30K GP profit per box), Rune Packs, and Pure Essence packs. Check our NMZ Beginner Guide for detailed setups and quest requirements.

🌊 Sand Crabs / Ammonite Crabs (50-100K GP/hr)

Requirements: None — just travel to Crab Claw Isle or the Fossil Island beach.

Sand Crabs are famous for AFK combat training, but they also drop decent loot including curved bones and long bones that can be sold for 20-30K each. The real profit, however, comes from picking up the good drops between AFK sessions. With a loot bag and alch runes, you can turn their drops into 50-100K GP per hour without leaving your spot. They max out at the Crab Claw Isle location where you can safespot with Ranged for even longer AFK sessions.

🔥 Soul Wars (400-1.8M GP/hr)

Requirements: 60+ Combat, completion of Soul Wars minigame tutorial.

Soul Wars is an often-overlooked AFK money maker. The strategy is simple: join a Soul Wars game, AFK in the spawn area, and collect reward points at the end. Even if you contribute nothing, you still get Zeal points from participation rewards. Spend Zeal on:

  • Rune Packs: 500+ Nature runes per hour = 100K+ GP profit
  • Ensouled Scourge & Tome: Can be sold for 50-100K GP each to players who want the cosmetic benefits
  • Prayer XP tomes: Sell services to other players for 400-1.8M GP/hr

The best part? You can technically AFK entire games without being kicked, making this extremely low-effort.

4. AFK Bossing Methods

Yes, you can even AFK some bosses in OSRS. These methods require good gear and high stats, but once set up, you only need to click every 20-60 seconds.

🕷 Kraken (600-1.8M GP/hr) — Level 87 Slayer Required

Location: Kraken Cove, accessible after being assigned Kraken by a Slayer master (requires 87 Slayer).

Method: Equip full magic gear (Trident of the Seas/Swamp, Mystic, Occult, Tormented bracelet) and stand in the centre. The Kraken tentacles attack slowly and deal minimal damage if you use Protect From Magic. The boss itself is incredibly AFK — you click to attack and wait until it dies. Each kill takes 30-60 seconds.

Drop value: Kraken tentacle (50-70K), Trident of the Seas (100K), and the rare Kraken pet. Average loot per hour is 600-1.8M GP.

AFK rating: 9/10 — click to attack, wait 30-60 seconds, loot, repeat.

🕸 Sarachnis (400-1.8M GP/hr) — Level 70+ Combat

Location: Sarachnis boss lair in the Varrock Sewers (requires partial Completion of the "Sarachnis" area from the Forthos Dungeon).

Method: Use Protect From Melee and bring decent melee gear (Void Knight equipment or Torags/Dharok's). Sarachnis attacks slowly with clear visual tells. The boss spawns spider minions that you can ignore if you have high enough defence. Each kill takes 60-90 seconds.

Drop value: Sarachnis cudgel (400-1.8M), dragon dagger/med helm drops, and high-value herb drops. Our Sarachnis loot guide breaks down the full profit table.

AFK rating: 8/10 — click to attack, flick protect prayer occasionally, loot every 60-90 seconds.

🌎 Giant Mole (400-350K GP/hr) — No Combat Requirements

Location: Falador Mole Lair (entrance south of Falador Park).

Method: With the Falador Hard Diary completed, the Giant Mole is shown on the minimap, making it effectively AFK. Equip a Dharok's set or high-strength gear and Protect From Melee. The mole burrows frequently but the minimap arrow shows exactly where it surfaces. Each kill takes 30-60 seconds with good gear.

Drop value: Mole claws (30-40K), Mole skin (20-30K), and the rare Mole slippers. With bird nests from the drops, the loot adds up nicely.

AFK rating: 7/10 — requires occasional minimap clicking when the mole burrows, but very relaxed overall.

5. Semi-AFK Methods

These methods require slightly more attention than pure AFK methods but offer better GP rates. They are excellent for when you are watching a show or listening to a podcast.

⚡ High Alchemy (150-1.8M GP/hr) — Level 55 Magic

Method: Buy items that have a high alchemy value above their GE price (check the "Nature rune profit calculator" on the OSRS wiki). Common items include adamant platebodies, rune items, and jewellery. Alching while doing agility courses, motherlode mine cleaning, or any other activity that frees one hand doubles your efficiency.

AFK rating: 5/10 — requires 1 click per alch, but you can do it alongside other semi-AFK activities.

🧺 Slayer with AFK Tasks (400-1.5M GP/hr)

Method: Certain Slayer tasks are naturally AFK-friendly. Gargoyles (75 Slayer), Kurasks (70 Slayer), and Wyverns (72 Slayer) all require minimal attention and drop consistent profit. Use Protect Prayers and good gear, and you only need to click every 15-30 seconds. For a full list of profitable Slayer tasks, see our Low Level Slayer Money Makers guide.

AFK rating: 6/10 — more attention than skilling but much higher GP rates.

💼 Blast Furnace (400-700K GP/hr) — Level 30 Smithing

Method: Use the Blast Furnace in the Dwarven Mines to smelt bars. If you use the "coffer" system to pay the workers (72K GP/hour), you can use the conveyor belt and bar dispenser in a steady rhythm. Gold bars are the most profitable, earning 400-700K GP/hour at 40+ Smithing. The rhythm is steady and repetitive — perfect for a semi-AFK session.

AFK rating: 5/10 — requires constant but low-effort clicking in a predictable pattern.

8. Final Tips — Maximising Your AFK Money Making

🎯 Key Takeaways

  • Stack AFK methods: Do birdhouse runs during herb run cooldowns, high-alch while doing agility — maximise every minute of playtime.
  • Prioritise stat unlocks: 87 Slayer (Kraken), 92 Mining (Amethyst), and 82 Fishing (Anglerfish) unlock the best AFK methods. Rush these stats through quests and efficient training.
  • Balance AFK with active play: Use AFK methods when you're busy IRL or need a break. Switch to active methods (Vorkath, Zulrah, flipping) when you have full attention.
  • Don't neglect gear upgrades: Even AFK methods benefit from better gear. A trident upgrade at Kraken or better fishing gear at Anglerfish noticeably improves GP/hr.
  • Track your actual GP/hr: Use RuneLite's loot tracker. If a method drops below 1.8M GP/hr for your level bracket, consider switching to a more profitable AFK option.

AFK money making is about consistency over intensity. The player who does 4 hours of AFK skilling every day will often out-earn the player who does 1 hour of intense bossing and burns out. Find your rhythm, stack your methods, and watch your bank grow while you relax.

9. AFK Money Making FAQs

What is the most AFK money making method in OSRS?

Nightmare Zone with absorption potions is widely considered the most AFK method. You can sit for 15-20 minutes without a single click while earning 400-1.8M GP per hour. For pure skilling, Infernal Eels (80 Fishing) and Amethyst Mining (92 Mining) both offer 60-90 second AFK intervals with zero inventory management during the session.

Can I make money AFK as a low-level player?

Yes. Low-level AFK methods include cutting Yew trees (60 Woodcutting, 30-50K GP/hr), fishing Monkfish (62 Fishing, 100-150K GP/hr), and fighting Sand Crabs (no requirements, 50-100K GP/hr). While these earn less than high-level methods, they require virtually no gear investment and train your stats simultaneously. For a complete low-level progression path, read our Summer Money Making Sweep-Up article.

Is Motherlode Mine still profitable in 2026?

Yes, Motherlode Mine remains a solid AFK moneymaker in 2026. At 85+ Mining, you can earn up to 120K GP/hour from runite and adamantite ore from the pay-dirt sack. The upper level is also excellent for clue scroll drops (especially easy and medium clues). Plus, it trains Mining to 99, making it a dual-purpose activity.

What is the difference between AFK and semi-AFK money making?

True AFK methods allow you to click once every 60+ seconds and walk away from the keyboard. Semi-AFK methods require attention every 5-30 seconds but offer significantly higher GP rates. Semi-AFK methods like Blast Furnace (400-700K GP/hr) or high-alching while doing other activities are better suited for when you can give partial attention to the game.

Do I need membership for AFK money making?

Yes, almost all profitable AFK methods require membership. Free-to-play AFK options are extremely limited — you could mine iron ore in the Dwarven Mine (10-20K GP/hr) or fish lobsters in Karamja (20-30K GP/hr), but these rates are far below member methods.
