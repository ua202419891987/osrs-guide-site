2. Story Background — Why You Care

Thousands of years ago, vampire clans were divided across their home plane Vampyrium. The Drakan clan ruled through a brutal combat ritual called the "Blood Moon" festival — the winner became the Blood Moon lord.

Loweriel Drakan, the last Blood Moon lord, has returned to Gielinor. His goal: restart the old ways, reclaim his title, and sacrifice all of Morytania to do it. Your job: stop him.

🩸 The Final Boss: Loweriel Drakan

Combat Style: Melee + Magic (hybrid). Will likely require prayer switching — bring high Magic defense gear and consider using a magic shield (e.g., Arcane Spirit Shield) for the magic phase.

Recommended Combat: 110+ (this is a Grandmaster quest). If you're below 90 combat, train combat first.

Expected Mechanics: Based on RS3's Lord of Vampyrium (the equivalent finale), expect phase transitions, vampire minions, and a special "Blood Moon" phase where healing is disabled.

7. FAQ — Blood Moon Rises

Q: Can I do this quest on an Ironman?

A: Yes. All skills are trainable on Ironman. The prerequisite quests are also Ironman-friendly. Start early — Ironman training takes 2-3x longer.

Q: What's the best money-making method to fund my gear?

A: Check our Money Making Guide. Best pre-110 combat methods: Crystal Shards (Zalcano), Vorkath (if 85 Ranged), Chambers of Xeric (team).

Q: Will this quest be members-only?

A: Yes. All Grandmaster quests are members-only. You need an active OSRS membership.

Q: Is the Maggot King a good boss for mid-game players?

A: Likely yes. Based on the "solo boss" description and the 74 Slayer requirement, it's designed for mid-to-high-level players (similar to Vorkath or Alchemical Hydra). If you can kill Vorkath, you can kill Maggot King.
