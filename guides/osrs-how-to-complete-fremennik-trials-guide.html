OSRS How to Complete Fremennik Trials Guide 2026

📜 Quest 🏔️ Fremennik 🌙 Lunar Diplomacy Updated 2026

The Fremennik Trials is a pivotal quest in OSRS that unlocks access to Rellekka, the Fremennik Province, and serves as the prerequisite for Lunar Diplomacy. This guide covers all 8 trials, requirements, and the valuable rewards including the Fremennik helmet in 2026.

⚡ 30-Second Quick Preview

  • 📌 8 trials total, complete only 3: Music, Combat, and Hunting trials are the easiest picks
  • 📌 Requirements: No quest prerequisites — 40+ Crafting/Fletching/Woodcutting recommended
  • 📌 Rewards: 3 Quest Points, three 4,800 XP lamps (14,400 total), Fremennik helmet access
  • 📌 Key unlock: Prerequisite for Lunar Diplomacy — grants access to the Lunar spellbook
  • 📌 Time & difficulty: 1–2 hours, intermediate — only takes 30 min if you pick the right 3 trials

Important: You only need to complete 3 of the 8 trials to finish the quest, not all 8. However, this guide covers all of them so you can choose the easiest three for your skill levels.

Starting the Quest

  1. Travel to Rellekka, north of Seers' Village
  2. Speak to the Chieftain Brundt in the longhall
  3. Ask to become a Fremennik — he'll tell you about the trials
  4. The council of 12 elders must vote to accept you
  5. You need at least 7 votes (majority) to pass
  6. Each trial you complete earns you votes from specific council members

Each council member sponsors a different trial. You need to complete enough trials to earn at least 7 votes from the 12 council members. Completing 3 trials typically gives you the needed majority.

Trial Details & Solutions

1. Music Trial (Olaf the Bard)

Create a lyre and perform in the longhall. To make the lyre: cut a branch from the rare tree east of Rellekka (requires woodcutting), fletch it into an unstrung lyre, and string it with golden wool from the golden sheep. Then play the lyre in the longhall to earn Olaf's vote.

2. Combat Trial (Thorvald the Warrior)

Fight Koschei the Deathless in the warrior's arena. You must defeat him without dying — and he has 4 phases, each progressively harder. You don't need to kill all 4 phases; surviving the first 3 is sufficient. Use food and protect prayers. If you die, you can retry immediately.

3. Hunting Trial (Swensen the Navigator)

Navigate Swensen's maze by following the correct paths through a series of portals. The trick is to go in the direction indicated by the riddle: "always go south from the first, then west, then east, then north." This is a logic puzzle that requires reading the clues carefully.

4. Poetry Trial (Sigmund the Merchant)

Obtain a romantic poem for Sigmund. This involves a chain of NPC trades around Rellekka — get an item from one NPC, trade it to another, and so on until you obtain the poem. It's a simple fetch quest that just requires talking to the right people in order.

5. Mystery Trial (Manni the Reveller)

Switch your drink with Manni's without being caught. This involves a logic puzzle where you must distract the bartender and swap the drinks at the right moment. Use the beer from the barrel to create a diversion.

The biggest long-term value of this quest is unlocking Lunar Diplomacy, which grants access to the Lunar spellbook. The Lunar spellbook contains essential spells like NPC Contact, Superglass Make, and Plank Make — all of which are extremely useful for Ironmen and regular accounts alike.

Fremennik Trials Tips

  • ✅ Pick the 3 easiest trials — Music, Combat, and Hunting are the simplest
  • ✅ Bring food for the combat trial — Koschei hits hard in later phases
  • ✅ Complete this quest early — it unlocks too much content to delay
  • ✅ Use the Rellekka bank after completing the quest — it's conveniently located
  • ❌ Don't attempt all 8 trials — you only need 3, so pick the easiest ones
  • ❌ Don't skip this quest — it's a prerequisite for many important quests
  • ✅ Do Lunar Diplomacy right after — the Lunar spellbook is incredibly useful
