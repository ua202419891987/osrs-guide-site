OSRS 1-99 Prayer Guide 2026 — All Methods

Skill Training Prayer Fast & Cheap Updated 2026

Prayer is one of the most important skills in OSRS for both PvM and PvP combat. From Protect from Melee at level 43 to Piety and Rigour in the 70s, every level unlocks crucial prayers that can turn the tide of battle. This comprehensive guide covers all training methods from F2P to endgame members content, including the new Varlamore updates that have changed the meta significantly.

How Prayer XP Works

In OSRS, you train Prayer by burying bones or offering them on an altar. The base XP depends on the bone type, and the multiplier depends on where you offer it:

The golden rule: always use an altar if possible. Burying bones is only recommended when you have no access to a better method. A Gilded Altar gives 3.5x the XP of burying — meaning each bone goes 3.5 times further toward 99.

F2P Route (Levels 1–43+)

If you are playing free-to-play, your options are more limited but still viable for reaching key milestones like Protect from Melee (level 43).

Recommended F2P Path

  1. Level 1–25: Bury regular bones or Big Bones from Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon). Big Bones at Ectofuntus = best XP/GP ratio.
  2. Level 25–43: Continue with Big Bones at the Ectofuntus. Kill Hill Giants yourself for free bones, or buy from GE (~180 GP each).
  3. Alternative: Use the Chaos Altar in Wilderness (level 38) for 3.5x XP — but beware of PKers.

Members Fastest Route — Gilded Altar

This is the standard method that 90% of players use. It offers excellent XP rates, low effort, and reasonable cost. You need a Player-Owned House with a Gilded Altar (or access to someone else's).

Gilded Altar Setup Guide

  • Requirement: Level 75 Construction (to build a Gilded Altar) OR use someone else's house
  • Altar location: POH Chapel (built at 75 Construction)
  • Burners: Light BOTH incense burners with Marrentill or更高 herbs
  • World 330 / World 366: Popular "host worlds" for Gilded Altars
  • Method: Bring a full inventory of bones → enter house → use bones on lit altar → repeat
  • Inventory size: 28 bones per trip (or use pack yak for 30+ later)

Dragon Bones on Gilded Altar — The Standard Method

  • XP per bone: 252 (72 base × 3.5)
  • XP/hr: ~400,000 – 450,000
  • Afk window: Low-medium (about 20 seconds between trips)
  • Cost per hour: ~650,000 – 750,000 GP (at current DB prices)
  • Time to 99 from 1: ~29–32 hours of active gameplay
  • Total cost to 99: ~19–22 million GP

This is the benchmark method. If you don't want to overthink it, buy dragon bones, find a Gilded Altar host, and grind. You'll hit 99 in under 35 hours.

New 2026 Meta: Varlamore Prayer Methods

The Varlamore expansion (Children of the Sun quest line) introduced game-changing Prayer training options. These methods compete with or even surpass the traditional Gilded Altar route.

Varlamore Bone Offering (Post-Children of the Sun)

  • Requirements: Complete Children of the Sun quest (high-level requirements)
  • Location: Varlamore temple area
  • XP Multiplier: Up to 3.5× (same as Gilded Altar)
  • XP/hr: ~450,000 – 500,000 (slightly faster than Gilded Altar due to closer bank)
  • Cost: Same as Gilded Altar (depends on bone type)
  • Why it's better: No need for a host world — always available, instant banking nearby

Ensouled Heads (Combat-Based Prayer XP)

  • Method: Kill monsters that drop ensouled heads → offer at Dark Altar
  • Location: Dark Altar in Arceuus (Great Kourend)
  • XP/head: Varies by type (e.g., ensouled dragon head = 650 XP)
  • XP/hr: ~350,000 – 500,000 depending on monster type
  • Cost: Combat supplies only (bones are free drops)
