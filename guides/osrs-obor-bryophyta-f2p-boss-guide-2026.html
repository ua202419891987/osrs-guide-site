2. Boss 1: Obor — The Hill Giant Boss (Super Detailed)

Obor is the best F2P boss for new players. He's located in the Edgeville Dungeon, drops consistent loot (Rune items, Big Bones), and has simple mechanics that teach you the basics of bossing. This section will be extremely detailed — follow it step-by-step for your first Obor kill.

📍 Exact Location & How to Get There (F2P & P2P)

Step 1: Travel to Edgeville

From Lumbridge (new players): Walk north through Lumbridge Swamp, then northwest to Varrock, then west to Edgeville. Total walking time: ~5 minutes. Alternatively, use the Lumbridge Home Teleport (spellbook) if you have 31 Magic.

From Varrock: Walk west through the Varrock West Bank, cross the bridge, continue west to Edgeville. ~2 minutes.

From Edgeville Bank: The dungeon entrance is in the southern building of Edgeville (the building south of the bank, looks like a stone house with a red roof).

Step 2: Enter Edgeville Dungeon

Enter the building and climb down the dungeon ladder. You'll appear in a large cavern with various monsters (Skeletons, Zombies, Hobgoblins).

Navigate the Dungeon:

  1. From the entrance, walk east (follow the path, avoid Green Dragons if you see them — they hit hard).
  2. After ~30 seconds of walking east, you'll reach a gate guarded by a Hobgoblin. Walk through the gate (it's open).
  3. Continue south through the dungeon. You'll pass through a narrow corridor — beware of Black Knights (they're aggressive at low Combat levels).
  4. Eventually, you'll see a locked gate with a large goblin-like creature behind it. That's Obor.

Step 3: Pay the Essence Fee (50 Rune Essence)

Obor doesn't let you fight him for free. You need to pay 50 Rune Essence per attempt.

Where to Get Rune Essence:

  • Buy from GE: Rune Essence costs ~50 GP each at the Grand Exchange. 50 essence = 2,500 GP per Obor attempt. This is extremely cheap — one Obor kill can pay for 40+ attempts.
  • Mine it yourself: With 30 Mining, you can mine Rune Essence in the Rune Essence Mine (teleport there via Aubury in Varrock). This is free but slow (30–40 essence/minute).

How to Pay Obor: Right-click Obor (through the gate) and select "Pay-essence". Choose 50 from your inventory. The gate will open, and you have one attempt to kill him.

🛡️ Obor's 3 Attack Modes (And How to Counter Each)

Attack 1: Melee Club Smash (70% of attacks)

Animation: Obor raises his giant wooden club above his head and brings it down on you.

Counter: Activate Protect from Melee (Prayer 43 required). This reduces damage to 0. If you don't have 43 Prayer, wear your best armor and eat food when you get hit.

Damage without protection: 10–18 per hit. With food tanking, you'll need 5–8 lobsters for the whole fight.

Attack 2: Rock Throw (Ranged) (25% of attacks)

Animation: Obor bends down, picks up a boulder, and hurls it at you. You'll see the boulder flying through the air.

Counter: Quickly switch to Protect from Missiles when you see him pick up the rock. Switch back to Protect from Melee after the rock lands.

Timing: You have about 1 second from when he picks up the rock to when it hits you. Don't panic — it's easy to switch prayers in time.

Damage without protection: 8–15 per hit.

⚠️ Attack 3: Earthquake (Special) (Every 30–40 seconds)

Animation: Obor crouches down and slams both fists into the ground. The screen shakes slightly.

IMPORTANT: This attack CANNOT be blocked by prayer. It will deal 15–22 damage regardless of your protection.

Counter: When you see Obor crouch, immediately eat 2–3 lobsters (or 1 swordfish). The earthquake damage is predictable, so you can pre-eat before it hits.

Pro Tip: Count Obor's attacks. After ~8–10 attacks, he'll earthquake. Learn the rhythm and you'll never be surprised.

🧠 Step-by-Step Fight Strategy (Your First Obor Kill)

Pre-Fight Checklist

✅ Prayer 43? If yes, activate Protect from Melee before entering Obor's room.
✅ Inventory: 20 Lobsters, 50 Rune Essence, empty space for loot.
✅ Attack Style: Set to "Attack" (accurate) for best hit chance against Obor's high defense.
✅ Prayer Potions: If you have 43 Prayer, bring 2–3 Prayer Potions (or 1 Super Restore) in case you run low.

Phase 1: Entering the Fight (First 30 Seconds)

  1. Pay Obor 50 Rune Essence. The gate opens.
  2. Walk through the gate. Obor is in the center of the room.
  3. Immediately activate Protect from Melee (if you have 43 Prayer).
  4. Turn on Ultimate Strength (Prayer 31+) if you have extra prayer points.
  5. Walk up to Obor and right-click "Attack".
  6. Obor will attack you with his club. With Protect from Melee, you take 0 damage. Nice!

Phase 2: The Fight (Middle 1–3 Minutes)

  1. Keep attacking Obor with your Rune Scimitar. He has 180 HP, so it'll take 20–40 hits to kill him (depending on your Strength level).
  2. Watch for the Rock Throw: If Obor bends down to pick up a boulder, quickly switch to Protect from Missiles. Switch back to Melee protection after the rock lands.
  3. Watch for Earthquake: Every 30–40 seconds, Obor will crouch and slam the ground. Immediately eat 2–3 lobsters. The damage is 15–22 HP, so you need to heal quickly.
  4. If your prayer points run low (below 10), drink a Prayer Potion or activate Piety (if 70 Prayer) to conserve points.

Phase 3: Obor's Death (Final 10 Seconds)

  1. When Obor's HP drops below 20, he'll start attacking faster (every 2–3 seconds instead of 4). Don't panic — just keep eating food if needed.
  2. Obor dies! Right-click the loot pile and take everything. Don't miss any items — even "junk" like coins add up.
  3. Check your loot: Did you get any Rune items? Big Bones? Obor's Staff (rare)?
  4. Walk back to the gate. It'll be locked again. You need to pay another 50 essence to fight him again.

⚠️ Common Death Causes & How to Avoid Them

Death Cause 1: Forgetting to Eat Before Earthquake

Scenario: Obor earthquakes when you're at 20 HP. You don't eat in time. He hits you for 22 damage → you die.

Prevention: Watch Obor's animation closely. When he crouches, instantly eat 2 lobsters. Don't wait to see the damage number — pre-eat!

Death Cause 2: Running Out of Prayer Points

Scenario: You have Protect from Melee on, but your prayer points hit 0. Obor's next melee hit deals 18 damage. You panic, eat food, but Obor hits again before you can eat → dead.

Prevention: Watch your prayer points. When they drop below 15, drink a Prayer Potion (if you brought one) or switch to running away while eating food to restore prayer at an altar (there's no altar in Obor's room, so this is tricky — better to just bring Prayer Potions).

Death Cause 3: Getting Surrounded by Other Monsters

Scenario: You enter Edgeville Dungeon, run past Hill Giants and Hobgoblins to reach Obor, but accidentally aggro 3–4 monsters along the way. By the time you reach Obor, you're already at 50% HP.

Prevention: Take the safest route to Obor. If you do aggro monsters, kill them quickly (they have low HP) or run past them. Don't enter Obor's room with low HP — go back to the bank (Edgeville) and heal first.

3. Boss 2: Bryophyta — The Jungle Moss Giant Boss

Bryophyta is the second F2P boss, located in the Falador Dungeon. She's a nature-themed boss with magic attacks and a unique drop (Bryophyta's Essence) that's always worth 50K–100K GP. Slightly harder than Obor but more consistent profit.

📍 Location & Access (F2P & P2P)

Getting to Bryophyta (F2P)

Step 1: Go to Falador (use the Falador teleport if you have 37 Magic, or walk from Edgeville).

Step 2: From Falador West Bank, walk south to the Falador Park area. Look for a stone staircase leading underground (southwest corner of the park, near the farming patches).

Step 3: Climb down the staircase into Falador Dungeon. Follow the path south, then east. You'll pass various monsters (Ghosts, Skeletons, Zombies).

Step 4: Continue southeast until you reach a large mossy cavern. Bryophyta is in the center, surrounded by green vines and moss.

No Entry Fee: Unlike Obor, Bryophyta is free to fight. You can fight her as many times as you want without paying anything.

🛡️ Bryophyta's 3 Attack Modes

Attack 1: Nature Magic Spells (60% of attacks)

Animation: Bryophyta raises her vines and shoots a green magic projectile at you.

Counter: Protect from Magic (Prayer 43). Reduces damage to 0.

Without Protection: 12–20 damage per hit. With food tanking, you'll need 8–12 lobsters for the fight.

Attack 2: Root Whip (Melee) (30% of attacks)

Animation: If you stand within 1 tile of Bryophyta, she'll whip you with her roots.

Counter: Protect from Melee OR simply step back 1 tile (ranged combat). Bryophyta can't melee you if you're not adjacent.

Strategy Tip: Always stand 2+ tiles away from Bryophyta. Use long-range weapons (bows, magic) or just step back after each melee attack if using melee.

⚠️ Special: Summon Roots (Every 45–60 seconds)

Animation: Bryophyta shouts "Roots, protect me!" and 3–4 small root creatures spawn around her.

The Danger: These roots have 10 HP each but hit for 5–8 damage. If you ignore them, they'll overwhelm you (4 roots × 8 damage = 32 damage/attack cycle).

Counter: Kill the roots IMMEDIATELY. Switch to "Aggressive" attack style (controls multiple enemies) and kill all roots before returning to Bryophyta. They die in 1–2 hits each with Rune Scimitar.

Priority: Roots > Bryophyta. Always kill adds first in OSRS boss fights.

🧠 Fight Strategy

Phase 1: Opening (First 20 Seconds)

  1. Enter Bryophyta's cavern. She's in the center, stationary.
  2. Activate Protect from Magic (if 43 Prayer).
  3. Attack Bryophyta with Ranged (Magic Shortbow) or Melee (Rune Scimitar).
  4. She'll immediately cast magic at you. With Protect from Magic, 0 damage. Good start!

Phase 2: Mid-Fight (Constant Root Management)

  1. Every 45–60 seconds, Bryophyta shouts "Roots, protect me!"
  2. STOP attacking Bryophyta. Switch to the small roots.
  3. Kill all 3–4 roots (they have 10 HP each → 1–2 hits with Rune Scimitar).
  4. Once all roots are dead, return to Bryophyta and continue DPSing her.
  5. If you're using Ranged, you can kill the roots without moving (stand 2+ tiles away and shoot them with arrows).

Phase 3: Low HP (Bryophyta below 50 HP)

  1. Bryophyta doesn't have an enrage phase, but she spawns roots more frequently (every 30 seconds instead of 45).
  2. Be extra vigilant about killing roots quickly.
  3. If your HP drops below 30, eat food proactively. Don't wait until you're at 10 HP.
  4. Bryophyta dies! Loot her immediately.

4. Boss 3: Giant Mole (F2P Accessible, Best F2P Boss)

The Giant Mole is technically accessible to F2P players (you can enter the Mole Lair with a F2P account), but it's significantly harder than Obor and Bryophyta because the Mole has a burrowing mechanic that requires you to track its movement underground.

📍 Location & Access

Location: Falador Park (same park where you find the Giant Mole hill in F2P). Look for the spade icon on the ground.

Requirements: Combat 30+ (recommended), 40+ Ranged (recommended for safe killing), Protect from Melee (highly recommended).

⚔️ Giant Mole Strategy (F2P Friendly Method)

F2P Method: Melee with Protect from Melee

  1. Dig into the Mole Lair with a spade.
  2. Wait for the Mole to emerge from the ground (it pops up randomly).
  3. When it emerges, activate Protect from Melee and attack with Rune Scimitar.
  4. The Mole will burrow underground again after 20–30 seconds. Stop attacking.
  5. Watch the dirt mounds moving across the floor. The Mole is underground, moving toward a new emergence point.
  6. When the Mole pops up again, repeat steps 3–4.
  7. After 4–6 surface phases, the Mole dies.

F2P Challenge: Without membership, you can't use Hunter to track the Mole underground (no Tracker ability). You'll need to watch the dirt mounds carefully. This makes the fight 2–3x longer than the P2P method.

7. F2P Combat Techniques for Bossing

F2P bossing requires slightly different techniques than member bossing. Here are the key skills every F2P bosser needs to learn:

🎯 Technique 1: Prayer Flicking (Save Prayer Points)

Prayer flicking is turning a prayer on and off rapidly so it only drains prayer points for 1 game tick (0.6 seconds) instead of continuously. This makes your prayer points last 3–5x longer.

How to Practice Prayer Flicking (F2P)

  1. Go to Lumbridge and find a chicken.
  2. Activate Protect from Melee.
  3. Practice clicking the prayer icon ON-OFF-ON-OFF rhythmically. Your prayer points should drain much slower than leaving it on.
  4. Apply this to bossing: Turn Protect prayer ON right before the boss attacks, then OFF immediately after the attack lands.

🎯 Technique 2: Eating Efficiently (Don't Panic Eat)

New players often eat food as soon as they take damage. This wastes food. Instead:

  • Only eat when your HP drops below 50%. Most bosses can hit you for 20 damage max. If you have 60 HP, you can take 2–3 hits before needing to eat.
  • Pre-eat beforeSpecial attacks. For Obor's earthquake, eat 2 lobsters BEFORE the damage lands. Don't wait to see the damage number.
  • Save 1–2 food for emergencies. Never use your last lobster unless you're in danger of dying.

9. F2P Bossing FAQs

Can I really boss as a F2P player with 0 GP?

Answer: Yes, but you'll need to grind for 2–3 hours first to get basic gear (Steel Scimitar, Leather Armor). Kill Hill Giants in Edgeville Dungeon — they drop Big Bones (sell for 200 GP each) and occasionally Rune items. After 2–3 hours, you'll have 25K–50K GP, enough for Tier 2 gear (see Secton 5).

Which F2P boss should I start with?

Answer: Obor. He's the easiest to learn because his mechanics are simple (melee + rock throw + earthquake). Once you can kill Obor consistently (under 4 minutes per kill), move to Bryophyta, then Giant Mole.

Do I need 43 Prayer to F2P boss?

Answer: Technically no, but it's STRONGLY recommended. Without Protect prayers, you'll take full damage from boss attacks and need 10–15 food per kill. With Protect prayers, you take 0 damage from most attacks and only need 5–8 food. The 43 Prayer requirement is worth it — train Prayer by burying bones from your boss kills.

How many kills per hour can I get at each F2P boss?

Answer:

  • Obor: 8–12 kills/hour (2.5K essence fee per kill, ~5 minutes per kill)
  • Bryophyta: 10–15 kills/hour (no entry fee, ~4 minutes per kill)
  • Giant Mole: 10–15 kills/hour (no entry fee, ~4 minutes per kill, but harder to learn)

What if I die during a F2P boss fight?

Answer: If you die in F2P, you'll lose all your items (unless you're in a safe zone, which boss rooms are NOT). Always use the Ring of Life (teleports you to safety at 10% HP) and consider bringing a Teleport Tab (if you have membership, otherwise run to safety). Dying is part of learning — don't get discouraged.
