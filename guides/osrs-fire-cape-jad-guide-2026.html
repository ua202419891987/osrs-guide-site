OSRS Fire Cape Guide 2026 — Jad Strategy, Fight Caves Walkthrough & Budget Setup

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 15 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🔥 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

The Fire Cape is every OSRS player's first major PvM achievement. 62 waves of TzHaar creatures, culminating in the TzTok-Jad boss fight. This guide covers budget gear, wave safespots, Jad prayer switching, Healer management, and everything you need for your first cape.

5. Jad Strategy — The Moment of Truth

Stay calm. You have ONE job: switch prayers correctly.

Attack TypeVisual CueSound CuePrayer
🦶 Stomp (Melee)Jad stomps his feet. Close range only — stay away!ThudProtect from Melee
🏹 RangedJad lifts front legs HIGH and slams down hardQuick thudProtect from Missiles
🔥 MagicJad leans BACK, fireball appears above his headDistinct charging sound (key cue!)Protect from Magic
🔊 USE SOUND — IT'S YOUR #1 CUE: The Magic attack makes a distinct charging sound BEFORE the fireball appears. When you hear it, switch to Protect from Magic IMMEDIATELY. If you DON'T hear the sound, it's Ranged — switch to Protect from Missiles. Many players play with game sounds ON specifically for Jad.

Jad Fight Step-by-Step

  1. Wave 62 END: Kill the final Orange Mage (360) last. As it dies, switch to Protect from Missiles (Jad's default starting attack is often Ranged).
  2. Jad spawns: Stay near Italy Rock. Keep your distance — if you're too close, he'll Stomp (Melee).
  3. Attacking: ONE action per Jad attack. Click Jad → watch for his animation → switch prayer → ONE more action. Never try to do two things between attacks.
  4. Healers appear (50% HP): When Jad hits 50%, four Yt-HurKot (Healers) spawn. Tag each one by hitting it ONCE (to get its attention). Do NOT kill them unless you're confident.
  5. Healer Strategy — LINE THEM UP: After tagging all 4, run behind Italy Rock so the Healers line up single-file. Only ONE Healer can attack you at a time — you can tank one easily.
  6. Finish Jad: Once Healers are tagged and lined up, go back to attacking Jad. ONE action per prayer switch. Keep the rhythm until he dies.
  7. Fire Cape acquired! Congratulations — you've earned one of the most iconic items in OSRS.

6. Safespots & Italy Rock

The Italy Rock (named because it looks like Italy on the minimap) is your home for 62 waves. Here's how to use it:

  • East side (boot): Safespot — melee enemies get stuck here. Stand on the south side of this boot-shaped section.
  • North side: Stand behind the northern tip to block larger enemies (Yt-MejKot 180s).
  • Corner trap: The southeast corner traps enemies perfectly. Lure them to one side, run to the other.

Watch a quick YouTube guide for the exact Italy Rock tile positions — seeing it once is worth a thousand words.

7. Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  1. Panic-switching prayers. After each Jad attack, keep your current prayer ON until you know what the next attack is.
  2. Looking at your inventory too long. Every second your eyes are off Jad is a second you can miss the prayer cue.
  3. Trying to kill Healers. Just tag them. Killing all 4 while prayer-switching Jad is how most first-timers die.
  4. Running into melee range. Stay at range. Jad's stomp is a guaranteed 50+ damage if you're too close.
  5. Not using F-keys. Bind your inventory and prayer tab to F1/F2. You'll need to switch tabs quickly during Healer phase.
  6. Underestimating Wave 61. The double-mage wave before Jad kills more players than Jad himself. Use Italy Rock and take your time.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I log out during the Fight Caves?

A: Yes! Click the logout button between waves. You'll be prompted to log out after the current wave ends. When you log back in, you resume on the next wave. Use this to take breaks — the Fight Caves are mentally draining.

Q: What if I die — do I lose all my items?

A: No! The Fight Caves are a safe death. You keep ALL your items. You only lose the supplies you used. Don't be afraid to fail — it costs you nothing but time and a few thousand GP in potions.

Q: Is a Blowpipe required or can I use a Crossbow?

A: Blowpipe is strongly recommended. You CAN do it with a Dragon Crossbow + Ruby/Diamond Bolts (e), but the Fight Caves will take 90-120 minutes instead of 60. The Blowpipe's attack speed makes prayer switching at Jad much easier since you spend less time locked in attack animations.

Q: Can I practice Jad without doing all 62 waves?

A: Yes — use the Speedrun World method. On a Speedrun world (Quest Speedrunning plugin on RuneLite), you can teleport directly to a Beneath Cursed Sands completion state and access the TzHaar-Ket-Rak challenge, which includes a single Jad fight with no waves. Practice there until you're comfortable with prayer switching.

🔥 You've Got This

Almost every OSRS player dies to Jad on their first attempt. It's a rite of passage. The key is staying calm, listening for that Magic attack sound, and taking ONE action per prayer switch. Once you have that Fire Cape equipped, you'll never take it off — it's one of the best melee capes in the game and a permanent reminder that you conquered the Fight Caves.

Now go earn that cape. 🔥
