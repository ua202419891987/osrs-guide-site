① Daily Reset Activities (Every 24 Hours)

Daily activities reset every 24 hours at 00:00 UTC (midnight London time). Completing them gives you free GP, XP lamps, and unique rewards.

② 🔍 How to Track Dailies (RuneLite Plugin)

Use the Activity Tracker plugin in RuneLite to track your dailies. It shows:

  • ✅ Which dailies you've completed today
  • ⏰ Time until next reset (countdown timer)
  • 📊 Total value of rewards collected this week
💡 Set a Daily Alarm

Pick a specific time each day (e.g., 08:00 UTC) to do your dailies. Spend 15 minutes completing all daily activities, and you'll accumulate millions of GP per month passively.

Weekly activities reset every Monday at 00:00 UTC. These give bigger rewards than dailies but require more time/effort.

② 🏰 Clan Citadel Priority (Best Weekly Reward)

The Clan Citadel is the #1 weekly activity for beginners. You can gather free resources that would cost millions if bought from the GE.

💰 Clan Citadel Resources (Free GP)

  • Woodcutting: ~50-100 logs (Oak, Willow, Maple) = ~50k GP value.
  • Mining: ~50-100 ores (Iron, Coal, Mithril) = ~30k GP value.
  • Herblore: ~20-50 herbs (Guam, Marrentill, etc.) = ~20k GP value.
  • Total Value: ~100-200k GP per week for 15 minutes of work.
💡 Join an Active Clan

To access a Clan Citadel, you must be in a clan. Join an active clan (50+ members online) that upgrades their Citadel regularly. A maxed Citadel gives the best resources.

③ Monthly Reset Activities (Once Per Month)

Some activities reset once per month (usually on the 1st of the month at 00:00 UTC). These are often high-value and shouldn't be missed.

① 📆 List of Monthly Activities

📆 Monthly Activities (Reset: 1st of Month, 00:00 UTC)

  • Gold Premier Club (Membership): If you have Premier Club, you get free membership days on the 1st of each month.
  • Some mini-game rewards: Check specific mini-games (e.g., Castle Wars) for monthly reward resets.

Note: Monthly activities are less common than dailies/weeklies. Check the OSRS Wiki "Monthly Events" page for updates.

② 📱 How to Track Reset Times (Apps & Plugins)

Use these tools to track reset times:

  • RuneLite Activity Tracker: Shows countdown to next reset.
  • OSRS Mobile App: Set alarm notifications for dailies.
  • Google Calendar: Create recurring events for weekly activities (e.g., "Clan Citadel - Mondays 18:00 UTC").
💡 Do Dailies Before Reset

If you miss a daily activity, you can still do it before the next reset. For example, if reset is at 00:00 UTC and you haven't done your herb run yet, do it before midnight to get credit for that day.

⑤ Efficient Daily Routine (15 Minutes/Day)

Here's a sample daily routine that takes ~15 minutes and gives you maximum rewards:

① 🕐 Sample Daily Schedule (15 Minutes Total)

⏱️ 15-Minute Daily Routine (Efficient Order)

  1. Varrock Museum Quiz (2 min): Teleport to Varrock, answer 5 questions, get XP lamp.
  2. Farming Herb Run (5 min): Teleport to herb patches (5 locations), harvest + replant.
  3. Ardougne Monastery (1 min): Teleport to Ardougne, collect 7 bone fragments.
  4. Fossil Island Mushrooms (3 min): Teleport to Fossil Island, collect 10 mushrooms.
  5. Taverley House Chinchompas (1 min): Check trap, reset if needed.
  6. Check GE (3 min): Collect sold items, place new buy orders.

② 📊 Total Value of Daily Routine

💰 Daily Routine Rewards (Per Day)

  • GP from Herb Runs: ~100-300k GP
  • GP from Mushrooms: ~20k GP
  • XP from Museum Quiz: ~5,000 XP (any skill)
  • XP from Prayer Fragments: ~2,000 Prayer XP
  • Total Value: ~150-350k GP equivalent per day

Monthly Value: ~4.5-10.5 million GP equivalent per month for 15 minutes of work per day. That's an insane ROI (Return on Investment).

⑦ Pro Tips for Dailies

Maximize your efficiency with these pro tips for daily and weekly activities:

① 🚀 Efficiency Tips (Save Time & Maximize Rewards)

✅ Pro Tips (Do These)

  • Use Teleports: Always use teleport spells/tablets to minimize travel time.
  • Bundle Activities: Do activities that are close to each other in the same trip (e.g., Varrock Museum + GE).
  • Set Alarms: Use phone alarms or RuneLite notifications to remind you of reset times.
  • Prioritize High-Value: If you only have 5 minutes, do herb runs + Museum Quiz (highest value).
  • Join a Clan: Clan Citadel alone gives ~100k GP/week. Must be in a clan.
  • Use RuneLite Plugins: Activity Tracker + GE Tracker + NPCHighlight save hours per week.

② ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Avoid These)

❌ Mistakes to Avoid

  • Forgetting Reset Times: Set alarms! Missing a daily = losing that day's rewards.
  • Doing Low-Value Activities: Don't waste time on dailies that give <10k GP value.
  • Not Using Teleports: Walking to activities wastes 10+ minutes per day.
  • Ignoring Clan Citadel: Biggest mistake — free 100k+ GP/week wasted.

❓ FAQ — Daily/Weekly Activities Questions

Q: What happens if I miss a daily activity?

A: You simply don't get the reward for that day. The next day, you can do the activity again. There's no "penalty" for missing dailies, but you do lose the reward permanently for that day.

Q: Can I do daily activities on F2P (free-to-play)?

A: Most dailies require membership (Farming herb runs, Clan Citadel, etc.). F2P players can only do Varrock Museum Quiz and a few others. Get membership ASAP to access all dailies.

Q: How do I know which NPCs have daily activities?

A: NPCs with dailies have a blue icon above their head (minimap). Also, the RuneLite "NPCHighlight" plugin can highlight these NPCs automatically.

Q: Are daily activities worth the time for high-level players?

A: Yes! Even high-level players do dailies because the rewards are passive income. 15 minutes per day = ~5M GP/month. That's worth it even for maxed players.

Q: Can I automate daily activities?

A: No. OSRS doesn't allow automation (botting). You must do dailies manually. However, you can optimize your route to minimize time (use teleports, bundle activities).
