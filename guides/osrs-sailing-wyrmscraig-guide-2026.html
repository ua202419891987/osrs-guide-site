1.1 How Wyrmscraig Was Chosen

In February 2026, Jagex held a player vote for 4 proposed island designs. Wyrmscraig won by receiving the fewest last-choice votes (most "likable" island per the official poll results). The other 3 islands may appear in future updates.

2. New Quest: Fallen From Grace

Confirmed Details

Name: Fallen From Grace (exact name may change before release)

Requirement: 62 Sailing (confirmed) + additional quest prerequisites (TBD)

Rewards: Will unlock Mortimer (Slayer Master) and The Mad Angel boss access

Speculation — Difficulty & Length: Based on similar island-unlock quests (Client of Kourend, The Fremennik Exiles), we expect Medium to Master difficulty and 30–50 minutes of playtime. This is not confirmed.

3. New Skilling Activities

3.1 Goat Hunting (Hunter)

Confirmed

Jagex confirmed Goat Hunting is a new Hunter activity on Wyrmscraig. It takes place on the island's mountain peaks.

Confirmed drops: Goat horns (use TBD) and Goat skins (use TBD)

Speculation — Mechanics: Based on the mountainous terrain, Goat Hunting may work similarly to existing Hunter methods like Falconry or Deadfall trapping. XP rates and exact mechanics are unknown until release.

3.2 Golem Crafting

Confirmed

Jagex confirmed Golem Crafting is a new activity where players gather materials on Wyrmscraig to craft and assemble Golem companions.

Speculation: Golem Crafting may involve Construction and/or Crafting. The crafted Golems might function as skilling helpers (similar to the Beaver pet for Woodcutting) or purely as cosmetic companions. This is unconfirmed.

4. Mortimer — New Slayer Master

Confirmed

Name: Mortimer

Location: Wyrmscraig island

Unlock: Requires completing the Fallen From Grace quest

Task theme: Island-themed monsters (confirmed to include Wyrmscraig-specific creatures)

Speculation: Mortimer will likely require high Slayer level (possibly 85+) based on the pattern of new Slayer Masters being endgame content. His task list may include the new Wyrmscraig monsters and boss. Point rewards and exact level requirement are unconfirmed.

5. The Mad Angel — New Solo Boss

Confirmed

Name: The Mad Angel

Type: Solo repeatable boss

Location: Wyrmscraig island

Unlock: Requires Fallen From Grace quest

Loot: New unique drops (details TBD)

Speculation: As a solo repeatable boss, The Mad Angel will likely be comparable in difficulty to Vorkath or Alchemical Hydra. Recommended combat level is probably 90+. Attack style is unknown — prepare for both melee and ranged/magic based on typical boss design.

6. Preparation — What You Can Do Now

6.1 Train 62 Sailing (Confirmed Requirement)

This is the only confirmed gate to Wyrmscraig. If you don't have 62 Sailing, you cannot access the island on release day.

Level RangeTraining Approach
1–30Complete the Sailing tutorial and early port tasks
30–50Port tasks in accessible harbors
50–62Deep sea exploration and sailing tasks
Speculation — Training Time: Based on similar 1–62 skilling grinds, expect roughly 15–25 hours of focused training. AFK methods may take longer.

6.2 Consider Banking Potential Resources

The island is described as "mountainous" with "aggressive wildlife." Based on this terrain:

  • Stone resources (basalt, granite) may be relevant for Golem Crafting
  • Prayer training may be useful if new Prayer methods are introduced
  • Crafting may be relevant if new armor types use processed materials
Speculation: If Golem Crafting requires stone materials, prices for basalt and granite may rise after release. Banking some now is low-risk preparation. This is not confirmed.

6.3 Combat Preparation

To fight The Mad Angel and complete Mortimer's tasks, you'll need solid combat stats. While exact requirements are unconfirmed, similar solo bosses typically require:

  • 90+ Combat level (speculation based on Vorkath/Hydra)
  • Good ranged gear (most solo bosses favor ranged)
  • Arcane Spirit Shield or similar magic defense (if boss has magic attacks)
