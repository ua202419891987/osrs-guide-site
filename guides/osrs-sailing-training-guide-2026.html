🎯 Getting Started: The Pandemonium Quest

Before you can train Sailing, you must complete The Pandemonium — the skill's introductory miniquest. It has no requirements and is designed to teach you the core mechanics.

① How to Start

Head to the Port Sarim docks and speak with Will and Anne. They will guide you through the quest, which involves:

  • Learning basic navigation controls
  • Your first short voyage to Pandemonium port
  • Interacting with your ship's facilities

② Rewards

RewardDetails
Starts you at level 6 SailingYou skip the first 5 levels entirely
Raft (starter ship)Basic vessel with Cargo Hold facility
Captain's LogRecords your voyages, Sea Charting progress, and quests
⛵ Pro Tip: Talk to Everyone at Port Sarim

Before setting sail, talk to the Shipwright (ship upgrades), Notice Board (Port Tasks), and Chartin' Charles (Sea Charting tutor, found in Pandemonium cave area). Understanding all training methods from the start saves you hours.

① Passive Wind Mechanic

Every 60 seconds while sailing, a wind icon appears. Clicking it gives your ship a 12-second speed boost and awards 150-300 bonus XP. This passive mechanic can add up to 10,000-15,000 XP per hour — always watch for the wind icon.

📦 Port Tasks (Courier & Bounty)

Port Tasks are your main source of consistent XP. Available immediately after completing The Pandemonium, these are jobs posted on Notice Boards at every major port.

① Courier Tasks (Cargo Transport)

Transport cargo from one port to another. Your ship needs a Cargo Hold (included with your Raft).

  1. Visit the Notice Board at any port to pick up a courier task
  2. Collect cargo from the Cargo Supplies box at the departure port
  3. Store it in your ship's Cargo Hold
  4. Sail to the destination port and deliver the cargo

XP rates: 15,000-30,000/h at low levels. High-level routes (Lunar Isle) can reach ~150,000 XP/h.

② Bounty Tasks (Naval Combat)

Unlocked at level 28. Requires a Cannon facility on your ship.

  1. Equip your ship with a Cannon (Bronze at 28, up to Dragon tier)
  2. Stock up on cannonballs (crafted through Smithing)
  3. Accept a Bounty Task from the Notice Board
  4. Sail to the target area and engage sea monsters
  5. Collect the bounty item and return to port

XP rates (67+): Up to ~200,000 XP/h using Dragon cannonballs and Perildance bitter (damage boost).

💡 Estimated XP Rates (Port Tasks)

1-30: 15,000-30,000 XP/h (courier tasks). 30-50: 40,000-70,000 XP/h (mix of courier and early bounty). 67+: Up to 200,000 XP/h with high-tier ammo and upgrades.

🔧 Shipwreck Salvaging (AFK Method)

Unlocked at level 15 Sailing, Shipwreck Salvaging is the AFK-friendly training method — ideal for second-monitor play.

① How It Works

  1. Build a Salvaging Hook facility on your ship (requires level 15)
  2. Sail to a Shipwreck Salvage zone (marked on your map)
  3. Stop your ship and activate the Salvaging Hook to pull items from the wreck
  4. Return to port and use the Salvaging Station to sort and process your haul for bonus XP

② XP Progression by Level

③ Profit Potential

Salvaging from level 15 to 99 can earn 20M+ GP in salvaged materials. The Sailor's Amulet (rare drop, ~1/300 at high-level zones) provides important teleport unlocks and can be sold if duplicate.

💡 Key Milestone: Level 42 Salvaging Station

At level 42, you can build a Salvaging Sorting Station (schematics found on Chinchompa Island). This lets you sort salvage while aboard your ship, dramatically improving AFK efficiency. This is the single biggest quality-of-life upgrade for the salvaging method.

🌊 Sea Charting (Exploration)

Sea Charting is an exploration-based training method — you discover and map the seas of Gielinor. It provides one-time XP rewards for each charting point discovered, totaling 691,000 XP across all 70 sea regions.

① Charting Tools

Captain's LogRecord and review charting progress

② Bonus Rewards

  • Completing regions unlocks Rapids — permanent speed boosts for that area
  • Rewards consumable buff drinks that can be stored in a Keg facility
  • Complete all charting for the Horizons Lure — permanent 2.5% Sailing XP bonus

Some charting locations require a Raft or Skiff (not Sloop) to access shallow waters.

💡 Do Sea Charting Early

Chart regions as you unlock them — the 2.5% XP bonus from Horizons Lure applies to all future training. Don't leave it all for 99. The 691K one-time XP is equivalent to ~3.5 hours of high-tier training.

🏁 Barracuda Trials (Fastest XP)

Unlocked at level 30 Sailing, Barracuda Trials are time-limited navigation challenges. These are the highest XP-per-hour method in Sailing, but they require focus and skill — similar to the Hallowed Sepulchre in intensity.

① Trial Tiers & XP Rates

Trial NameUnlock LevelBest ShipXP/h (Basic Gear)XP/h (Optimal Gear)
Tempor Tantrum30Skiff~20,000/h~40,000/h
Jubbly Jive55Skiff / Sloop~80,000/h~120,000/h
Gwenith Glide72Skiff (optimal)~150,000/h~200,000+/h

② Optimal Gear for Trials

For maximum trial efficiency, use a Skiff (not Sloop) — it has better maneuverability for obstacle courses. Key upgrades:

  • Teak Hull (31) — 15% speed boost, critical for all trials
  • Rosewood Hull (92) — major speed upgrade for Gwenith Glide
  • Crystal Extractor — provides 250 XP/min while actively clicking, stacks with trial XP
  • Wind Catcher / Gale Catcher — stores wind energy for speed boosts

③ RuneLite Plugins for Trials

Install the Sailing plugin and Tile Packs on RuneLite. These show optimal paths through trial courses and help you learn routes faster. Recommended by the OSRS Wiki.

💡 Cost Note: Trials Are Cheap

Unlike many buyable skills, Barracuda Trials cost very little — basic gear (Iron Helm, Oak Mast, Linen Sail, 30 cannonballs) runs about 350K GP. Even premium gear stays under 500K for competitive trial times.

🎣 Deep Sea Trawling (Hybrid Method)

Unlocked at level 56 Sailing, Deep Sea Trawling is a hybrid method that trains both Sailing and Fishing simultaneously. It requires a Trawling Net facility installed on your ship.

① How It Works

  1. Install a Trawling Net on your Sloop (requires 2 facility slots)
  2. Equip the Trawler's Trust drink (optional, from Sea Charting rewards)
  3. Sail to deep sea trawling zones and deploy the net
  4. Haul in fish while maintaining your ship's position

② Benefits

  • Moderate Sailing XP + Fishing XP simultaneously
  • Profitable — high-value fish like Marlin (heals 24 HP) are caught here
  • Less intensive than Barracuda Trials, more engaging than Salvaging

④ Crew Members

Unlock your first crew member at level 40, with additional slots every ~15 levels. Crew have three stats:

  • Helmsmanship — Better ship control
  • Privateering — Cannon and combat effectiveness
  • Deckhandiness — Salvaging, trawling, and repair efficiency

Notable crew unlocks: Tempoross (100 kills), Fremennik Elite Diary completion.

⛵ Priority Order: Hull First, Always

Teak Hull at level 31 is the single most impactful upgrade in early Sailing. It provides roughly 15% speed improvement, which translates to 5,000-10,000 extra XP per hour. Prioritize hull upgrades over everything else.

💰 Sailing Is a Semi-Buyable Skill

Unlike some skills, Sailing does not require constant spending — Barracuda Trials are quite cheap. However, premium upgrades (Rosewood hull, Dragon Salvaging Hook at 50M+) are expensive. Plan your budget based on your preferred training method.

🎯 Your Sailing Training Game Plan

Follow this roadmap for the fastest path to 99:

✅ Levels 1-6 (Pandemonium): Complete the introductory quest at Port Sarim. Starts you at level 6 with a Raft.
✅ Levels 6-30 (Port Tasks + Charting): Courier tasks at Port Sarim + nearby ports. Do all available Sea Charting. Buy a Skiff at 15. Build a Salvaging Hook at 15 for AFK options.
✅ Levels 30-55 (Tempor Tantrum Trial): Unlock Barracuda Trials at 30. Upgrade to Teak Hull (31) — priority #1. Run Tempor Tantrum for ~20-40K XP/h. Start Bounty tasks with a Bronze Cannon (28).
✅ Levels 55-72 (Jubbly Jive Trial): Buy a Sloop (50). Run Jubbly Jive trial for 80K-120K XP/h. Build Salvaging Sorting Station (42) if AFKing.
✅ Levels 72-99 (Gwenith Glide Trial): Mahogany Hull (65) → Master Rigging (80). Run Gwenith Glide for 150K-200K+ XP/h. Optional: Rosewood Hull (92) for best times. Complete Sea Charting regions as you explore.

Remember: Click the wind icon every 60 seconds for free XP. Prioritize hull upgrades. Do Sea Charting early for the 2.5% XP boost.

❓ Sailing Training FAQs

Is Sailing worth training in 2026?

Yes. Sailing unlocks transportation benefits, exclusive resources, new Slayer monsters (Gryphons, Lava Strykewyrms, Aquanites, Frost Dragons), and profitable AFK methods. Most accounts benefit from at least level 70 for Mahogany Hull and mid-tier Barracuda Trials.

How long does 1-99 Sailing take?

Approximately 70-80 hours using optimal Barracuda Trials, 120-150 hours with mixed methods, or 160-170 hours AFK Shipwreck Salvaging. The active methods are significantly faster.

What's the fastest training method?

Barracuda Trials — three difficulty tiers unlocked at levels 30, 55, and 72. The highest tier (Gwenith Glide, 72+) reaches 200K+ XP/h with optimal gear. Requires focus but pays off with the fastest rates.

How much GP does 1-99 Sailing cost?

A budget Barracuda Trials setup costs ~350K-500K GP. Premium upgrades (Rosewood hull, Dragon Salvaging Hook at 86) can raise the cost to 20-60M GP. Unlike Herblore or Prayer, you don't buy XP directly — you invest in ship components.

What's the best AFK training method?

Shipwreck Salvaging, available from level 15. At level 42 with a Salvaging Sorting Station, it becomes very AFK-friendly. From 15 to 99, it earns 20M+ GP in salvaged materials.

Can Ironmen train Sailing?

Yes, all Sailing content is available to Ironmen. You must gather ship-building materials yourself through Mining, Woodcutting, and Smithing. A Construction level of 87 is needed for the highest-tier ship upgrades. Thieving stalls (ore at 82, cannonballs at 87) are especially valuable.

What happens if my ship is destroyed?

Your ship respawns at your last visited port. You lose any unsecured cargo. Repair costs depend on your ship's tier. Always carry a Repair Kit for emergency fixes.

Is Sailing required for any quests?

Yes. The Pandemonium is required to start Sailing. Troubled Tortugans (the main Sailing quest) unlocks access to the Great Conch area. Red Reef is a planned future quest.

What skills support Sailing training?

Construction (up to 87), Woodcutting (up to 94 for Rosewood), Smithing (up to 74 for Nickel bars), and Mining are the most important supporting skills for building ship upgrades.

What's the one-time Sea Charting XP?

Completing all 70 charting regions gives 691,000 total XP plus the Horizons Lure (permanent 2.5% XP bonus). Do this as you train rather than leaving it for later.
