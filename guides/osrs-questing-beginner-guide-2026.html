1. 什么是Quest？为什么先做任务？

Quest是OSRS的核心玩法 — 不是"杀10只狼"那种无聊任务，而是有剧情、解谜、Boss战的完整冒险。目前有160+个任务，新手第一个月只需要做其中约20个。

📋 Quest的核心价值

  • 跳过练级：Waterfall Quest 直接送你Attack 30 + Strength 30（从1级开始）
  • 解锁地图：做完任务解锁Fairy Ring、Spirit Tree、Magic Carpet等免费传送
  • 获得关键道具：Dramen Staff、Ava's Device、Barrows Gloves 都只能从任务获得
  • Quest Cape：做完所有任务获得全游戏最帅的披风之一

2. 为什么先做任务而不是先练级？

很多新手犯的最大错误：从1级开始手动练级。这浪费大量时间。任务给的XP奖励让你直接跳过最痛苦的初期等级。

3. 如何开始一个任务？分步指南

  1. 打开任务日志 — 按 Alt+Q 或点右下角盾牌图标，查看所有可用任务
  2. 点击任务名称 — 查看描述、要求、推荐物品
  3. 前往任务起点 — 日志会告诉你找哪个NPC、在哪里
  4. 和NPC对话 — 右键NPC选"Talk-to"，开始任务
  5. 完成任务目标 — 跟着任务指引做，日志会追踪进度
  6. 领取奖励 — 过场动画后获得XP Lamp、道具或新能力
💡 永远开着Wiki

在第二个屏幕或标签页打开 oldschool.runescape.wiki。搜索任务名查看分步攻略。不要自己摸索 —— Wiki就是为此而生的。

6. Best Quest Rewards to Unlock Early

🌐 Travel Unlocks (Priority #1)

  • Fairy Rings (Fairytale I) — Teleport to 50+ locations instantly. Best travel system in the game.
  • Spirit Trees (Grand Tree + Tree Gnome Village) — Fast teleport to key locations.
  • Gnome Gliders (The Grand Tree) — Free air travel to major cities.
  • Dramen Staff (Lost City) — Required to use fairy rings.

⚔️ Combat Unlocks (Priority #2)

  • Slayer unlocked at 0 stats — No quest needed, but Priest in Peril opens Slayer Tower.
  • Ava's Accumulator (Animal Magnetism) — Picks up spent arrows/bolts. A must for Rangers.
  • God Books (Horror from the Deep) — Powerful offhand slot fillers at low combat.

💰 Money-Making Unlocks (Priority #3)

  • Rune Mysteries — Unlocks Runecrafting, which becomes a major money-maker later.
  • Shilo Village — Opens Shilo Village Slayer Master (Duradel) and gem mines.
  • Dwarf Cannon — Unlocks cannon for fast Slayer XP and better loot rates.

7. The Quest Cape — A Long-Term Goal Worth Chasing

Completing ALL quests in OSRS earns you the Quest Cape — one of the most prestigious capes in the game. It's a very long journey (months to years), but every quest you complete brings you closer.

🏆 Quest Cape Benefits

  • The cape itself has stats similar to Ava's Assembler
  • Unlocks the ability to teleport to the Quest Hall in Legends' Guild
  • Proves mastery of the entire OSRS story — a community badge of honor
  • Completion rewards a huge pile of XP across multiple skills from quest rewards

For now, don't worry about the Quest Cape. Focus on the 10 quests in section 5. You'll naturally chain toward more quests as your skills improve.

8. Beginner Quest FAQs

Can I fail a quest?

No — you can't permanently fail a quest. If you die mid-quest, you respawn and can try again. Some quests restart if you leave mid-way, but you never lose progress permanently.

Do I need high stats to start questing?

No. Many of the best quests (Waterfall Quest, Fairytale I) have zero combat requirements. A few require specific skill levels, but the wiki clearly shows what you need.

What's the fastest way to get 32 Quest Points for Dragon Slayer?

Do every F2P novice quest in order — you'll hit 32 QP in 1–2 hours. Cook's Assistant, Romeo & Juliet, Cooks' Assistant, Rune Mysteries, and the other novice quests add up fast.

Should I use guides for every quest?

Yes, especially at first. The wiki has step-by-step walkthroughs with maps. There's no shame in following a guide — even veterans do it for complex quests.

Are quests worth it for Ironmen?

Absolutely. Ironmen rely even more on quest rewards since they can't trade. Questing early is essential for Ironman accounts to unlock gear, XP, and skilling methods.

How many Quest Points do I need for Dragon Slayer I?

You need 32 Quest Points (QP). Complete all F2P novice quests (Cooks' Assistant, Romeo & Juliet, Sheep Shearer, Rune Mysteries, The Restless Ghost, Imp Catcher, Witch's Potion, Doric's Quest) to easily reach 32 QP in about 1 hour.

What happens if I lose a quest item?

Most quest items can be re-obtained by talking to the NPC who gave them to you. If you accidentally drop or destroy a quest item, return to the NPC and ask for another. For rare items (like the Silverlight sword), you can get a replacement from the NPC who originally gave it.

Can I do quests in any order?

Most quests can be done in any order, but some require prerequisites (other quests completed first). Always check the quest journal or Wiki before starting. The priority table in Section 5 lists quests in optimal order considering prerequisites.

Do I need to complete ALL quests?

No. Most players complete 50–80 quests over their entire OSRS career. The Quest Cape requires all 160+ quests and is a very long-term goal. Focus on the 15 quests in the priority table first — they give 90% of the value.

What's the best quest for money making unlocks?

Fairytale I (fairy rings = fast travel to farming patches) and Animal Magnetism (Ava's device = free ammo = save thousands). For combat money: Priest in Peril (unlocks Barrows, a major GP source at mid levels).

How do I know which quests give the most combat XP?

Check the Quest XP Rewards table in Section 5. The top 3 are: Waterfall Quest (27,500 XP), Fight Arena (43,740 XP), and Tree Gnome Village (11,450 XP). These three alone give you ~82,000 free combat XP — that's about Level 50 in a combat skill from zero.

Final Tips — Questing for Beginners

  • Complete all F2P quests first. Before buying membership, finish every F2P quest — you'll reach 40+ in multiple skills for free and save weeks of grinding.
  • Use the OSRS Wiki quick guide. For every quest, Google "[quest name] osrs quick guide" to get a step-by-step walkthrough with maps and item lists.
  • Quest early for free XP. Waterfall Quest, Fight Arena, and Tree Gnome Village give huge XP rewards with zero requirements — do them before training skills manually.
  • Keep quest items. Many quest items like the Silverlight sword and Ghostspeak amulet are needed for future quests. Don't drop them.
  • If you get stuck, bank and reset. Dying during a quest is not permanent. Retrieve your items from Death's Office and try again.
