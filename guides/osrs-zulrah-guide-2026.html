OSRS Zulrah Boss Guide 2026 — The Money Snake's Four Rotations

Advanced

Zulrah is the classic solo profit boss of OSRS — a 500-HP serpent you fight in a private instance at Zul-Andra. The whole fight is a memory test: learn the four rotations, switch Protect prayers on cue, dodge venom clouds, and bank 2.5–4.5M GP/hr. This gold-standard guide gives verified July-2026 drop rates, tiered gear, a GP/hr table, a rotation diagram, and a live rare-drop calculator.

⏱️ Quick Summary — 30-Second Read

  • 📌 Four fixed rotations. Green = Ranged (Pray Missiles), Blue = Magic (Pray Magic), Crimson = Melee stun (no prayer, run), Jad phase = flick Ranged↔Magic.
  • 📌 50% fire weakness. Since the 29 May 2024 update Zulrah takes +50% damage from fire spells — Eye of Ayak / Fire Surge melt the Green & Crimson phases.
  • 📌 No Slayer needed. Requirements: Regicide (started, for the Zul-Andra teleport), 75+ Magic & Ranged (90+ ideal). Anyone can kill it.
  • 📌 Top loot: Magic fang 1/512, Tanzanite fang 1/512, Serpentine visage 1/512, Pet snakeling 1/4,000.
  • 📌 Profit: ~3.0–4.5M GP/hr before supplies at 24–35 kph (OSRS Wiki money-making calc, July 2026).

1. 🐍 Overview & Why Farm Zulrah in 2026

Zulrah is a level-725 boss with 500 Hitpoints, fought alone inside a personal instance reached by boat from Zul-Andra. There is no Player-versus-Player risk and your items are reclaimable on death (free under 50 KC, 100,000 GP after), so the only thing standing between you and millions of GP is learning the dance. In 2026 it remains one of the highest, most consistent solo GP/hr activities in the game.

Elemental weaknessFire spells (+50% damage, since 29 May 2024 update)
RotationsFour fixed patterns (see Section 4)
InstanceSolo by default — no instance fee; loot stays ~3 hours
Average kill value~135,900 GP (more on a Konar task)

🎯 The Three Big Draws

  • Top-tier, reliable GP. Two rolls per kill on a fat drop table, plus a steady stream of Zulrah's scales (100–299/kill) that always sell.
  • Crafting paydays. The Magic fang builds the Trident of the swamp, the Tanzanite fang builds the Toxic blowpipe, and the Serpentine visage builds the Serpentine helm.
  • Self-contained. No Slayer task, no team, no Wilderness. Teleport in, kill, teleport out — perfect for solo grinds.
💡 The One Skill That Wins the Fight

Zulrah is a memory and movement fight, not a DPS check. If you can predict the next rotation and pre-switch your prayer and gear, you will take almost no damage and chain 30+ kills per hour. Everything else is detail on top of that.

3. 🗺️ How to Get There

Zulrah's shrine sits on a remote island reached by a sacrificial boat. The fastest repeatable routes:

Step 1 — Teleport to Zul-Andra

Use a Zul-andra teleport scroll (obtained free from the High Priestess after Regicide, or bought on the GE ~15–16K GP each). This lands you right at the boat.

Step 2 — Fairy ring alternative

Use fairy ring BJS, then run south and use the 76-Agility Zul-Andra shortcut to reach the boat in seconds. Without 76 Agility, you must run the long way from Port Tyras (boat fee 250 coins each way).

Step 3 — Board and fight

Board the boat (quick-board is enabled). You load into your own instance — no world-hopping, no competition. Zulrah always opens in Green (Ranged) form, so enter with Protect from Missiles already on.

💡 Scale Up With the Bank

Most farmers use a Player-Owned House (Ornate Rejuvenation Pool + Portal Nexus) or Ferox Enclave to restore stats and prayer between kills. A Zul-andra scroll per trip is cheaper than the run once you pass ~15 kph.

② The Four Rotations

Each kill is one of four fixed sequences. The first two phases tell you which rotation you are on; the final phase is always the Green "Jad" phase where you flick prayers. Exact pillar positions are easiest with the RuneLite Zulrah plugin, but the form/prayer order below is all you need:

Rotation 4 (Delta)
🟢 Green → 🔵 Blue → 🔴 Crimson → 🔵 Blue → 🟢 Jad

③ Step-by-Step Kill

Phase 1 — Identify the rotation

Enter on Green with Protect from Missiles up. After two attacks Zulrah dives and re-emerges in the second form — that second form (Crimson or Blue) tells you the rotation. Pre-switch your prayer and gear for the whole pattern.

Phase 2 — Dodge venom & Snakelings

Venom clouds deal 12/tick inside a 3×3 area — step off them. Snakelings have 1 HP; let a Ring of Suffering (i) or Recoil finish them, or barrage them during a Crimson phase. Use Anti-venom+ or a Serpentine helm so venom never stacks.

Phase 3 — The Jad phase

In the final Green phase Zulrah alternates Ranged and Magic every attack. Flick Protect from Missiles ↔ Protect from Magic. Read the projectile colour in the air: green = Ranged, blue = Magic. Keep DPS up with Vengeance if you have 94 Magic.

💡 Fire Is Your Friend

Since the 29 May 2024 update Zulrah has a 50% weakness to fire spells. An Eye of Ayak or Fire Surge on the Green/Crimson phases (and a Tumeken's Shadow at BiS) dramatically cuts kill time. Confliction Gauntlets also re-roll missed magic hits, smoothing out the mid-tier setup.

🏆 Recommended Default: Mid-Game Magic + Blowpipe

Trident of the swamp (or Sanguinesti) + Ahrim's / Ancestral + Occult for the Green phases, and a Toxic blowpipe + Blessed d'hide + Anguish for the Blue phase, with Augury + Rigour up and a Ring of Suffering (i). This comfortably hits 24+ kph and ~3M GP/hr.

💡 Serpentine Helm vs Anti-venom+

A Serpentine helm makes you immune to venom for "free," but it costs Zulrah's scales to charge and hurts your Magic accuracy. For high-tier setups an Anti-venom+ potion with a Farseer/Neitiznot faceguard is usually more profitable per kill.

🪸 Fang Facts (Don't Mix Them Up)

The three uniques each craft something different: Magic fang → Trident of the swamp; Tanzanite fang → Toxic blowpipe (empty) with a chisel + 20,000 scales; Serpentine visage → Serpentine helm. A common mistake is assuming the Tanzanite fang makes the blowpipe alone — it needs the chisel and scales.

9. ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Myth-Busting)

❌ Myth 1: "Just tank the Jad phase with one prayer."

Wrong. The final Green phase alternates Ranged and Magic every attack. You must flick Protect from Missiles ↔ Protect from Magic by reading the projectile colour (green = Ranged, blue = Magic). One missed flick can delete you.

❌ Myth 2: "Crimson phase — pray Melee."

Ineffective. The Crimson stand attacks your previous tile and stuns on hit; no protection prayer stops it. Run away to a fresh tile the moment Zulrah stands.

❌ Myth 3: "Melee Zulrah for DPS."

Usually a trap. Zulrah was made no longer immune to melee (22 Feb 2023 update) but only halberds can reach it and the DPS is poor. Magic (fire-weak) and Ranged remain the efficient styles.

❌ Myth 4: "Stand in the venom — it's only a little."

Venom clouds hit 12/tick inside a 3×3 area and stack fast. Step off them, and bring Anti-venom+ or a Serpentine helm so venom never accumulates.

❌ Myth 5: "The Tanzanite fang alone makes the blowpipe."

The Tanzanite fang needs a chisel + 20,000 Zulrah's scales (78 Fletching) to make the Toxic blowpipe (empty). The Serpentine visage makes the Serpentine helm, and the Magic fang makes the Trident of the swamp.

10. ❓ FAQ

Do I need a Slayer task to kill Zulrah?

No. Zulrah requires only that you have started Regicide (for the Zul-Andra teleport). A Slayer task is optional and only adds Brimstone keys if assigned by Konar.

What is the best combat style for Zulrah?

Both Magic and Ranged. Magic (especially fire spells, thanks to the 50% weakness) handles the Green and Crimson phases; Ranged handles the Blue phase. Bring a switch for each — a single-style setup leaves DPS on the table.

Is Zulrah safe? Can I lose my items?

Yes, it is safe. The fight is a personal instance with no PvP. On death you reclaim items from Priestess Zul-Gwenwynig — free under 50 KC, 100,000 GP after.

How many kills per hour can I get?

Learners manage ~16–20 kph; experienced players 24–30; max-gear Twisted Bow / Shadow pushers reach 35+. The OSRS Wiki cites up to 35 kph at peak efficiency.

What do I do with a Magic fang / Tanzanite fang?

Magic fang + uncharged trident + 10,000 scales → Trident of the swamp. Tanzanite fang + chisel + 20,000 scales → Toxic blowpipe (empty). Both are tradeable at millions of GP if you'd rather sell.

11. 🏁 Final Tips

  • Learn Rotation 1 first. It is the most common and easiest; once it is automatic, add the others one at a time.
  • Pre-switch, don't react. The moment you know the rotation, set your prayers and gear for the whole pattern — reactions get you killed.
  • Fire wins. Eye of Ayak / Fire Surge / Shadow exploit the 50% fire weakness on Green & Crimson.
  • Venom insurance. Anti-venom+ or a Serpentine helm every trip — never let venom stack.
  • Ring of Suffering (i). Its recoil finishes the 1-HP Snakelings so you never lose DPS to them.
  • Next boss: once Zulrah is comfortable, step up to Vorkath — the dragon that pays even more per hour.
