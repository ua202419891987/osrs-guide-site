1. Slayer Overview — Why Train It

Slayer is the most important combat skill in OSRS. It unlocks the best money-making monsters (Gargoyles, Nechryaels, Abyssal Demons, Kraken, Cerberus, Hydra), provides Slayer-exclusive gear (slayer helm, slayer rings), and trains your melee/ranged/magic simultaneously.

XP rates: Low levels (1-70): 10K-30K/hr. Mid levels (70-85): 30K-60K/hr. High levels (85-99): 60K-100K/hr with efficient blocking, bursting, and cannoning.

3. Block & Skip Strategy — The Most Important Section

Smart blocking and skipping is the difference between 30K/hr and 70K+ Slayer XP/hr. Each Slayer master has different task weights, so your block list changes as you switch masters.

Block/Skip Terminology

  • Block: Permanently prevents a task from being assigned by that master. Costs 100 Slayer points per block slot. Max 6-7 block slots (depends on quest points).
  • Skip: Cancel a specific task assignment. Costs 30 Slayer points. Use for tasks that are bad but not worth a block slot.
  • Turael Skip: Ask Turael for a task to reset your streak. Free but resets streak counter. Use only when out of points.

4. Cannon Tasks — Best XP/hr

Cannon tasks are the fastest Slayer XP in the game. A dwarf multi-cannon hits up to 30, doubling or tripling your kill speed in multi-combat zones. It costs ~350K-500K GP/hr in cannonballs, but the XP rate increase is worth it.

5. Burst/Barrage Tasks — Multi-Combat Magic Slayer

Bursting and barraging Slayer tasks is the highest possible Slayer XP/hr method and gives massive Magic XP simultaneously. Use Ice Burst (70 Magic) or Ice Barrage (94 Magic) in multi-combat areas where monsters stack.

Best Burst/Barrage Tasks

NechryaelsCatacombs of Kourend~80K~250K~1M

Abyssal DemonsCatacombs of Kourend~75K~250K~800K
JelliesCatacombs of Kourend~40K~120K~200K

Bursting Setup

  • Weapon: Ancient staff (auto-cast ancients) or Kodai wand (saves 15% water runes, infinite water)
  • Armor: Proselyte (prayer bonus) for Protect from Melee, or Ahrim's (magic accuracy)
  • Off-hand: Tome of fire (50% fire runes saved) or Elidinis' ward (magic damage %)
  • Prayer: Protect from Melee + Augury (+25% magic damage). Prayer potions needed.
  • Inventory: 6-10 prayer potions, rune pouch (death + water + blood for burst, add chaos for barrage), emergency food

How to Stack Monsters for Bursting

Run around the room to aggro all monsters. Run to a corner or doorway. Monsters will line up in a 3x3 stack — hit them with burst/barrage for maximum AoE damage. This takes practice but doubles your XP/hr compared to killing monsters one at a time. Watch a YouTube guide for the exact stacking spots.

Superior monsters spawn randomly during Slayer tasks (1/200 chance per kill) when "Bigger and Badder" is unlocked. They have 3x HP and improved loot, including two of the rarest drops in OSRS.

Superior Strategy

  • Use Protect from Melee + piety against melee superiors

9. 1-99 Roadmap Per Slayer Master

Phase 1: Levels 1-50 — Foundation (Vannaka)

  • Master: Vannaka (Edgeville dungeon)
  • No blocks or skips — just do every task. Focus on melee stats.
  • Unlock: Bigger and Badder immediately (150 pts).
  • Unlock: Slayer Helm at 400 points.
  • Target: Get base 40-50 melee stats from Slayer tasks.

Phase 2: Levels 50-70 — Acceleration (Chaeldar, then Nieve at 85 combat)

  • Master: Chaeldar until 85 combat, then Nieve.
  • Block: Black Demons, Hellhounds (first 2 blocks).
  • Skip: Iron/Steel Dragons, Waterfiends.
  • Do: All cannon tasks you can afford. Start bursting Dust Devils at 65 Slayer.
  • Unlock: Broader Fletching at 300 points. More block slots.

Phase 3: Levels 70-85 — The Burst Era (Nieve/Duradel)

  • Master: Duradel (100 combat + 50 Slayer required). Nieve if below 100 combat.
  • Block: The full 6-block optimal list (Black Demons, Hellhounds, Spiritual Creatures, Drakes, Wyrms, Fire Giants).
  • Skip: Dragon tasks, slow melee-only tasks. Use Turael skip if out of points.
  • Burst: Dust Devils (65), Nechryaels (80). These are your best Slayer XP.
  • Cannon: Dagannoths, Kalphites, Suqahs. These round out your fast tasks.
  • Unlock: Gargoyle Smasher (120 pts) for Gargoyles at 75.

Phase 4: Levels 85-99 — The Final Push (Duradel only)

  • Master: Duradel exclusively.
  • Block list: Same 6. Unblock Hellhounds at 91 Slayer (for Cerberus).
  • Burst: Nechryaels (80), Smoke Devils (93), Abyssal Demons (85). These are 60K-90K Slayer XP/hr.
  • Boss tasks: Unlock "Like a Boss" if you enjoy bossing. Cerberus (91), Kraken (87), Thermy (93), Hydra (95).
  • Konar every 10th task: Get the 90-point bonus on milestone tasks (every 10th, 50th, 100th, etc.).
  • Extend: Dust Devils, Nechryaels, Smoke Devils, Abyssal Demons.

11. FAQ

Q: How long does 1-99 Slayer take?

Optimal (bursting + cannoning everything): ~400-500 hours. Casual (doing all tasks, no skipping): ~600-800 hours. Profit-focused (AFK tasks only): ~800-1000 hours. At 2 hours/day, expect 6-12 months to 99.

Q: Nieve or Duradel — which is better?

Duradel for 90+ combat with optimal block list. He assigns higher quantities of better monsters (more Nechryaels, Dust Devils, Smoke Devils). Nieve is good for 85-95 combat. Hot take: Duradel is better at every level past 100 combat.

Q: Should I burst or melee Abyssal Demons?

Burst in Catacombs for ~75K Slayer XP/hr + 250K Magic XP/hr. Melee in Slayer Tower for ~35K Slayer XP/hr but more AFK. Bursting is better but costs more in runes. If you can afford it, always burst.

Q: When should I start doing Konar tasks?

Use Konar only for milestone tasks (every 10th, 50th, 100th). The location restriction usually slows down regular tasks, but the 90+ point bonus is worth it. Do your 1st-9th tasks at Duradel, then the 10th at Konar.

Q: Is Wilderness Slayer worth it?

Wilderness Slayer (Krystilia) gives Larran's keys (100K-150K each) and mysterious emblems (100K+ each). Profit is high (1M-2M/hr) but PK risk is real. Only do Wilderness Slayer if you accept dying occasionally. Use cheap gear (<100K risk).

Q: What's the best Slayer master for Ironmen?

Ironmen benefit more from Konar (brimstone keys give supplies) and Duradel (more high-level tasks). Nieve is also good. The key difference: Ironmen can't buy cannonballs on GE, so cannon tasks are expensive. Prioritize bursting tasks instead.

Q: Slayer Helm (i) — is the imbue worth it?

Absolutely yes. The imbue extends the Slayer helm's damage/accuracy bonus to Ranged and Magic. Without imbue, it only affects Melee. 1.25M NMZ points for the imbue scroll. This is the first thing you should do after getting the helm.
