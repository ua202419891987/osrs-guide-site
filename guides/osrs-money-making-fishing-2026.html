🎣 Money Making — Fishing — Updated July 2026

Fishing is one of the most AFK-friendly money makers in OSRS. You can earn 50K-300K GP/hr while watching videos, working, or playing other accounts. This guide covers every profitable fishing method from level 1 to 99, with GP/hr rates updated for 2026 Grand Exchange prices.

2. Low Level Methods (1-50): Getting Started

Lobsters at Musa Point (Level 40) — First Profitable Fish

Raw lobsters sell for ~200 GP each. At Musa Point (Karamja), use the deposit box to bank efficiently. Expect ~250 lobsters per hour = ~50,000 GP/hr. This is the classic first money maker for new accounts and works on F2P.

Swordfish + Tuna (Level 50)

Swordfish sell for 350-450 GP each. You'll catch tuna alongside swordfish (worth ~90 GP each). Best locations: Musa Point (F2P) or Fishing Guild (requires 68 Fishing, members). Expect ~80,000 GP/hr at this level.

🎣 F2P Fishing Money Path: Trout/Salmon (1-40) → Lobsters (40-50) → Swordfish/Tuna (50-99). At 50+ Fishing on F2P, swordfish at Musa Point is your best money maker. Use the deposit box and expect 80K-100K GP/hr. Not competitive with members, but reliable for F2P-only accounts.

Monkfish (Level 62 — Swan Song Quest Required)

Monkfish are caught at the Piscatoris Fishing Colony after completing Swan Song. The bank is very close to the fishing spot, making this a relaxed AFK method. Monkfish heal 16 HP and are popular mid-game food, keeping demand stable.

Karambwans (Level 65 — Best Mid-Level Profit)

Karambwans are dual-eat food used heavily in PvP and bossing. After completing Tai Bwo Wannai Trio, fish them on Karamja using a Karambwan vessel with raw karambwanji as bait. The fairy ring (code DKP) provides fast banking. Karambwans are the single best mid-level fishing money maker at ~200,000 GP/hr.

Infernal Eels (Level 80) — Best AFK Money Maker

Fish infernal eels in Mor Ul Rek (TzHaar City) with a hammer in inventory. Crack them open for Onyx bolt tips (3,000-4,000 GP each), Lava scale shards (800-1,200 GP each), and Tokkul. This is THE most AFK fishing method — you click once every 1-2 minutes. Perfect for working from home or playing a second account.

Minnows → Sharks (Level 82) — Highest GP/Hour

Fish minnows at the Fishing Guild and exchange 40 minnows for 1 raw shark at the nearby trader. At peak efficiency, this yields ~300,000 GP/hr. However, minnows require active attention — the fishing spot moves every 15 seconds and you must click to continue fishing. Not AFK, but the highest raw GP from fishing.

6. Common Mistakes & Pro Tips

  1. Not completing quest unlocks. Swan Song (Monkfish at 150K/hr) and Tai Bwo Wannai Trio (Karambwans at 200K/hr) are short quests that triple your fishing profits versus swordfish. Do them early.
  2. Ignoring Infernal Eels for "better" GP/hr. The 150K/hr rate underestimates Infernal Eels because you can do them for 6+ hours while working. Pure GP/hr isn't the only metric — AFK time matters too.
  3. Not using the Fish Barrel (Tempoross reward). The Fish Barrel stores 28 raw fish and effectively doubles your inventory. This dramatically extends AFK time for Karambwans and Monkfish.
  4. Selling raw fish at the wrong time. Raw food prices spike on weekends (more players bossing). Bank your fish during the week and sell Friday-Sunday for 10-15% more.
  5. Not combining with Cooking. If you have high Cooking, check whether cooking + selling gives more profit than selling raw. Karambwans and Anglerfish often have a cooked premium.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is fishing or Woodcutting better money in OSRS?

A: Fishing wins at every level. Lobsters (50K/hr) beat yew logs (40K/hr). Monkfish (150K/hr) beat magic logs (100K/hr). Infernal Eels (150K/hr) beat redwoods (80K/hr). Fishing also has more AFK options. The only Woodcutting advantage is that you can burn logs for Firemaking XP if you're an ironman.

Q: What is the most AFK fishing money maker?

A: Infernal Eels. You click once every 60-90 seconds to stay logged in. They never move spots, never require banking (you crack them on the spot), and produce stackable loot. It's the gold standard for second-screen fishing money.

Q: Should ironmen train Fishing for money?

A: Yes — Karambwans are essential. Ironmen should prioritize Karambwans (level 65) as their primary food source for bossing. The GP from selling to shops isn't the goal; having a self-sustaining food supply is. Infernal Eels provide Tokkul for onyx jewelry, which is crucial for ironmen.

🎣 Fishing — The Ultimate AFK Income

Fishing won't make you rich overnight, but it will make you GP while you're barely paying attention. Stack Infernal Eels during work hours, Karambwans during your evening Netflix sessions, and Minnows when you have full attention. Over a month of casual fishing, you'll earn 10-20M GP with minimal effort.

Cast your line and let the gold come to you.
