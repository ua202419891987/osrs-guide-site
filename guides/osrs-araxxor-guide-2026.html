OSRS Araxxor Guide 2026 — Slayer Boss, Venom Arena & Noxious Halberd

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 13 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
👹 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

Araxxor is one of the most profitable Slayer bosses in OSRS — 92 Slayer required, a unique venom arena mechanic, and the chance at the 80M GP Noxious Halberd. This guide covers the poison management system, all 3 phases, gear from budget to max, and detailed kill strategies for consistent 5.5M+ GP/hr.

🕷️ Araxxor — Quick Reference

Slayer Level:92Location:Araxxor Lair, Morytania Swamp
Best Drop:Noxious Halberd (~80M)GP/hr:5.5M+ (on-task)

1. The Venom Arena — Unique Mechanic

Araxxor's lair slowly fills with venom gas. The poison level rises over time — if it maxes out, you take rapid 10+ damage per tick. Clear vents are scattered around the room. Step on a vent to clear the poison back to zero. Managing the poison WHILE fighting Araxxor is the core skill check. Strategy: Clear vents only when poison reaches 60%+ — this minimizes time off DPS. Each vent clear takes 2 ticks and has a 30-second cooldown per vent.

2. Three-Phase Fight

PhaseAttacksStrategy
🕸️ WebRanged + Web snareProtect from Missiles. Avoid webs on floor — they root you for 3 seconds. Clear vents at 60% poison.
🦵 MeleeMelee + Stomp AOEProtect from Melee. Run 3 tiles away from Stomp indicator. Kite if low HP.
🕷️ MinionSummons 5-8 spiderlingsKill minions FAST with Blowpipe switch. Each spiderling stacks venom on you — cumulatively deadly. Anti-venom+ is essential here.

3. Gear & Inventory

Primary: Osmumten's Fang (Stab is BIS) or Abyssal Tentacle. Switch: Toxic Blowpipe for minion phase. Essential supplies: 2 Anti-venom+ potions (venom from spiderlings is brutal), 3-4 Prayer Potions, 6-8 Saradomin Brews, 2 Super Restores, 1 Super Combat Potion, and a House Teleport tab for emergency escape. Armor: Blood Moon/Bandos for Melee phases, Karil's/Black D'hide switch if you need extra Ranged defence during Web phase.

4. Advanced Techniques

  1. Vent timing optimization: Clear vents during Araxxor's attack animation — you lose zero DPS ticks. The perfect vent clear happens in the 3-tick window after Araxxor's attack lands.
  2. Minion stacking: In Minion phase, run to a corner so spiderlings cluster up. Blowpipe hits multiple targets — you can clear all 8 spawns in 4-5 shots when they're stacked.
  3. Stab weakness exploitation: Araxxor is weakest to Stab. Fang's passive accuracy reroll makes it the best weapon here by a significant margin. If you don't have Fang, use the best Stab weapon available (Hasta > Abyssal Dagger > Leaf-Bladed Sword).
  4. Always kill on-task. The Slayer Helm bonus (+15% damage/accuracy) is the difference between 4M and 5.5M GP/hr. Off-task Araxxor is not worth doing — Turael skip until you get the task.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Off-task or on-task?

A: Always on-task. Slayer Helm bonus makes a ~30% DPS difference. Off-task kills take 25-30% longer and are significantly less profitable. Turael skip to hunt Araxxor tasks — see our Slayer Block & Skip List.

Q: Fang or Whip at Araxxor?

A: Fang — by a huge margin. Araxxor is weakest to Stab. Fang's passive accuracy reroll mechanic makes it the clear BIS. Whip (Slash) will feel noticeably worse. If you don't have Fang yet, use Zamorakian Hasta as the best budget Stab option.

🕷️ The Noxious Hunt

Araxxor combines Slayer, boss mechanics, and environmental management into one of the most engaging fights in OSRS. The Noxious Halberd is a genuinely exciting drop — a 2-tile melee weapon that opens up new strategies at many bosses. Manage your vents, respect the venom, and that 80M drop will come.
