OSRS Royal Titans Guide 2026 — Duo Boss, Free Combat Prayers & Mid-Game Power Spike

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 14 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
👹 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

The Royal Titans are the single best thing to happen to mid-game OSRS accounts. This duo boss drops two FREE combat prayers — Mystic Vigor and Deadeye — that bridge the gap to Rigour and Augury. Every mid-game account needs to farm this boss. Here's everything you need to know: the dual-phase fight, role assignments, gear from budget to max, and exactly how to maximize your prayer scroll drop rate.

👑 Royal Titans — Quick Reference

Team Size:Duo (2 players)Best Unlock:Mystic Vigor + Deadeye (FREE)
Min Stats:70+ combat, 62 PrayerValue Saved:~20M GP (vs buying Rigour + Augury)

1. Why Royal Titans Is the Most Important Mid-Game Boss

Before this boss, every mid-game player faced the same wall: Rigour costs 15M GP and requires 77 Prayer. Augury costs 5M GP and also requires 77 Prayer. That's 20M GP and millions of Prayer XP most mid-game accounts simply don't have. The Royal Titans solved this problem. Mystic Vigor (62 Prayer) and Deadeye (63 Prayer) are free drops — kill the boss, get the scroll, unlock the prayer. No GP cost. No 77 Prayer requirement.

2. The Dual Boss Fight — Brand & Solar Titans

BossAttack StyleSpecial AttackCounter
☀️ Solar TitanMagic (fire-based, Protect from Magic)Solar Beam — line AOE, leaves burning groundMove 3 tiles sideways when beam charges. Never stand in burning ground.
🔥 Brand TitanMelee + Ranged (prayer flick)Brand Slam — AOE knockback + stunRun away when Brand raises both fists. 2-tile minimum distance.

Shared mechanics: Both Titans share a linked HP pool — when one reaches 25% HP, BOTH enrage simultaneously. Attacks become faster and special attacks more frequent. Focus fire one Titan at a time. Kill Brand first (his slam is more dangerous), then Solar. During enrage, prioritize dodging specials over DPS.

3. Role Assignment

Player 1 (Tank): Handles Brand Titan. Higher Defence, uses Protect from Melee. Melee gear (Fang/Whip). Responsible for calling out Brand's slam animation.

Player 2 (Mage): Handles Solar Titan. Uses Protect from Magic. Magic gear (Trident). Stays at range to avoid Brand's slam splash damage. Responsible for calling out Solar's beam direction.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Should I still buy Rigour if I have Deadeye?

A: Yes — eventually. Deadeye is your stepping stone. Use it for free +2% Ranged damage at 63 Prayer. Save 15M GP for Rigour while enjoying the free DPS boost. When Rigour's +8% damage becomes your next major upgrade, buy it. Don't skip Deadeye just because you plan to buy Rigour later — the free scroll drops in 20-30 kills.

Q: How many kills for both prayer scrolls?

A: ~50-100 kills total. Scroll drops are fairly common (~1/30 each). Most duos get both scrolls within 2-3 hours of farming. The boss is designed to be accessible — you won't go dry for long.
