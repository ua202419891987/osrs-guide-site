OSRS Returning Player Guide 2026

① Welcome Back! What's Different in 2026

If you havent logged in since 2020-2023, OSRS is barely recognizable. Here's the 30-second summary:

① 10 Biggest Changes (2020–2026)

🎮 What You Missed

  • NEW SKILL: Sailing (2025) — you can now sail ships, explore, and fight naval battles
  • NEW AREA: Varlamore (2024) — massive continent with new quests, bosses, and skilling areas
  • NEW AREA: Wilowvale (2025) — high-level content with new ores and herbs
  • NEW BOSSES: Royal Titans, Duke Sucellus, The Leviathan — all added 2023-2025
  • NEW QUEST LINE: Desert Treasure II (2022) — unlocks Ancient Sceptre + new boss
  • Leagues IV (2025) — massive limited-time game mode with 10x XP/Rates
  • Fort Forinthy (2023) — new skilling area with reset D&Ds
  • Group Ironman Mode (2022) — play Ironman WITH friends!
  • GE Tax (1%) (2022) — selling items now has a 1% fee (max 10M GP)
  • New PvM Mechanics: Prayer flicking is MORE important (new bosses punish bad prayer management)
💡 Don't Panic — You Don't Need to Re-Learn Everything

The core mechanics (clicking, prayer, inventory) are IDENTICAL. Only the content is new. You can pick up where you left off — just read this guide first!

② New Areas You Need to Know About

These areas didn't exist when you last played. Here's the quick tour:

② Varlamore (2024) — The Biggest New Area

🗺️ What Is Varlamore?

Varlamore is a massive new continent south of Kepos. It has:

  • New city: Valemore — huge city with new shops and quests
  • New skilling area: The Glade — Hunter + Woodcutting + Fishing
  • New quest line: "The Varlamore Saga" (3 quests) — rewards include new teleports and XP lamps
  • New boss: Fortis Boss (mid-level PvM) — drops new gear set

③ Willowvale (2025) — High-Level Content

🗺️ What Is Willowvale?

Willowvale is a high-level area (unlocks at 70+ skills). It has:

  • New ores: Willow ore — best Smithing XP after 99
  • New herbs: Willow weed — ultra-rare herb for new potions
  • New slayer monsters: Level 120+ tasks — best GP/xp in the game

④ Fort Forinthry (2023) — Skilling Hub

🏰 What Is Fort Forinthry?

Fort Forinthry is a new skilling area where you build defenses and repair structures for rewards. It's a daily reset D&D (do it once per day for massive XP).

Requirements: 30+ in most skills. Rewards: Huge XP lamps (50K-200K XP each).

③ Sailing — The Brand New Skill (2025)

Sailing is the first new skill added to OSRS since launch (2025). Yes, it's controversial — but it's here to stay.

⑤ How Sailing Works (Simple Version)

🚢 Sailing Basics

  1. Build a ship: Use Construction + Smithing to build your first ship (small boat)
  2. Sail around: Click your ship → sail to destinations (new islands!)
  3. Fight naval battles: Enemy ships attack you — use Cannon + Range to sink them
  4. Loot wrecks: Destroyed ships drop trasure (GP + unique loot)

⑥ Sailing Rewards (Why You Should Care)

🏆 Sailing Exclusive Rewards

  • Ship cosmetics: Customize your ship (cosmetic only, no stats)
  • New islands: Skull Island (high-level resource area) + Pirate Bay (new bosses)
  • Naval combat gear: Sailing armor set (+5% XP in Sailing) — not BIS for anything else
  • Teleport: Ship teleport — instant travel to any visited port (HUGE time-saver!)

⑦ How to Start Sailing (Level 1–30 Roadmap)

🚢 Fastest Route to Level 30

LevelActivityXP/Hour
1–10Sail between Port Sarim ↔ Musa Point~15K XP
10–25Do "Tutorial Sailing Quests" (3 short quests)~30K XP
25–40Naval combat (easy seas) — sink 5 pirate ships per hour~50K XP

💡 Is Sailing Worth Your Time?

Yes — if you want 99 in everything. Otherwise, it's optional. The teleport feature is NICE (saves time), but you can 100% play OSRS without ever training Sailing. Your call!

④ New Bosses Since You Left (2023–2025)

The boss meta has completely changed. Here are the bosses you missed:

⑧ New Boss #1: Royal Titans (2023)

👑 Royal Titans Quick Facts

  • Location: Titan Grove (north of Varlamore)
  • Requirements: 100 Combat, 75 in 3 combat styles (tanky boss!)
  • Combat Style: All 3 styles — MUST prayer-switch every 10 seconds
  • Drops: Titan platebody (2.5M GP) + Titan boots (1.2M GP)

⑨ New Boss #2: Duke Sucellus (2024)

🧙 Duke Sucellus Quick Facts

  • Location: Duke's Manor (Wilowvale area)
  • Requirements: 110 Combat, 80+ Prayer (you'll need 70+ prayer points!)
  • Combat Style: Magic (he's weak to Magic) — use Trident/Blowpipe
  • Drops: Duke's Gown (3M GP set) + Spectral spirit shield (5M GP!)

⑩ New Boss #3: The Leviathan (2025)

🐋 The Leviathan Quick Facts

  • Location: Ocean Trench (reachable via Sailing)
  • Requirements: 115 Combat, 85 Ranged/Magic (big AoE attacks!)
  • Combat Style: Ranged (use Dragon crossbow + BCP) — melee is too dangerous
  • Drops: Leviathan spine (4M GP) + Ocean amulet (+10% Ranged strength)

⑪ Older Bosses You Might Have Missed (2020–2022)

🏆 Bosses Added 2020–2022

  • Phosani's Nightmare (2021) — solo version of Nightmare (harder, better loot)
  • Tombs of Amascut (2022) — new Raid (like CoX/ToB but HARDER)
  • Desert Treasure II bosses (2022) — 4 new bosses after the quest
  • Chambers of Xeric: Challenge Mode (2020) — ultra-hard mode (insane loot!)

⑤ New Quest Lines Worth Doing (2022–2025)

These quests weren't there when you last played. ALL are worth doing:

⑫ Quest Line #1: Desert Treasure II (2022)

🏜 Desert Treasure II — The Big One

Length: ~6 hours (very long!)
Requirements: 70+ in most skills, Desert Treasure I done
Rewards: Ancient Sceptre (best mage weapon pre-75), +4 boss fights, +200K total XP

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (very hard — bring best gear + food!)

⑬ Quest Line #2: The Varlamore Saga (2024–2025)

🗺️ Varlamore Quests (3 Total)

  1. "The Lost Expedition" (start) — unlock Varlamore access
  2. "Secrets of Valemore" (middle) — unlock new teleports + skilling areas
  3. "The Varlamore Crown" (end) — unlock Fortis Boss + new quest cape trimmed requirement

Total XP: ~350K across all skills. Time: ~8 hours total.

⑭ Quest Line #3: Sailing Tutorial Quests (2025)

🚢 Sailing Quest Line (3 Short Quests)

Requirements: None! Do these at level 1 Sailing.
Rewards: 150K Sailing XP (gets you to level 25!), +free ship repair toolkit.
Time: ~45 minutes total (very short!).

💡 Which Quest to Do FIRST?

If you have 70+ combat, do Desert Treasure II FIRST (unlocks Ancient Sceptre = 15% DPS boost). Otherwise, do Varlamore Saga (easier, gives teleports).

⑥ GE & Economy Changes (2022–2026)

The OSRS economy has massively shifted. Here's what you need to know:

⑮ Change #1: 1% GE Tax (2022)

💰 How the Tax Works

When you sell an item on GE, you now pay 1% tax (minimum 1 GP, maximum 10M GP per item).

Example: Sell an item for 50M GP → you get 49.5M GP (500K tax).

Workaround: For items >100M GP, sell to players directly (use "Price Check" FC) to avoid 10M tax.

⑰ Change #3: Item Prices Have Shifted

📈 Items That CRASHED (2022–2026)

  • Servers' rings: Were 40M GP → now 8M GP (too many bots farmed them)
  • Dragon items (non-BIS): Dragon platebody was 5M → now 800K (PowerCreep inflation fix)
  • Farmland patches: Herb prices crashed 40% (too many Ironmen farming)

  • Sailing gear: New items = 2M–10M GP each (supply low, demand high!)
  • Willow herbs: 50K GP each (rarest herb in the game)
  • Group Ironman gear: GIM-only items = 5M–20M GP (very rare!)

⑦ Your First Week Back — Day-by-Day Action Plan

Don't try to "catch up" in one day. Follow this 7-day roadmap:

⑪ Day 1: Account Check & UI Re-Familiarization

✅ Day 1 Tasks (2–3 hours)

  1. Log in: Make sure your account still exists (type your password — if you forgot, use Jagex account recovery)
  2. Check skills: What did you train last? (check your Skills tab)
  3. Check bank: How much GP do you have? (you might be richer than you think!)
  4. Play tutorial island again: (just the first 5 minutes) — reminds you of the UI

⑫ Day 2: Do 3 "Welcome Back" Quests

✅ Day 2 Tasks (3–4 hours)

  1. "The Lost Expedition" (Varlamore start) — unlocks new area + teleport
  2. Sailing tutorial quests (3 short ones) — gets you Sailing 25
  3. Check Quest Cape progress: How many quests done? (quests tab → quest list)

⑬ Day 3: Train 1 Skill to 50+ (Your Choice)

✅ Day 3 Tasks (2–3 hours)

  1. Pick ONE skill you remember enjoying (Combat? Fishing? Woodcutting?)
  2. Train it to 50+ (use this site's guides!)
  3. Goal: Get a real sense of progression — you're not "behind", you're just at a different starting point now

⑭ Day 4: Make Your First 1M GP (Post-Return)

✅ Day 4 Tasks (2–3 hours)

  1. Do a money-making method from this site (herb runs? Fishing? Combat?
  2. Goal: Earn 1M GP — proves you can still make GP in the new economy
  3. Celebrate: Buy something small (new gear? Crafting supplies?) — feels good!

⑮ Day 5: Do a New Boss (Easy One!)

✅ Day 5 Tasks (2–4 hours)

  1. Read one boss guide on this site (Obor? Barrows? Sarachnis?)
  2. Try the boss 3–5 times (die? That's fine! You're learning!)
  3. Goal: Kill the boss at least ONCE — shows you the new boss mechanics

⑯ Day 6: Join a Clan (Seriously!)

✅ Day 6 Tasks (1–2 hours)

  1. Join a clan (use the Clan Guide on this site!)
  2. Do a clan event: Many clans do group bossing or group skilling — way more fun with others!

⑰ Day 7: Plan Your Long-Term Goals

✅ Day 7 Tasks (1 hour + thinking)

  1. Write down 3 goals: (e.g., "Get 99 Fishing", "Kill Duke Sucellus", "Max all skills")
  2. Check this site weekly: New guides + new updates covered here FIRST
  3. Play casually: You're back! Don't burn out — OSRS is a marathon, not a sprint
💡 Your Account Is NOT "Behind"

OSRS is not compettive. Having 99 in 2020 vs 99 in 2026 = zero difference. Play at your own pace — the game will wait for you!

⑧ Returning Player FAQ — Common Questions

My account was hacked! What do I do?

Immediately use Jagex Account Recovery: https://www.runescape.com/account-recovery. Provide as much info as possible (old passwords, email, transaction IDs). Jagex is very good at recovering accounts — don't give up!

Do I need to re-buy Membership?

Yes. Membership expired while you were away (it doesn't "pause"). But the first month is sometimes discounted (check the website!). Also, use Bonds (buy with GP from GE) if you have 10M+ GP saved!

What's the FASTEST way to catch up?

There is no "fast" way. OSRS is a grind game. But the most efficient route = Quests → Diaries → Gear upgrades → Bosses. Use this site's guides for each step!

Are my old strategies still good?

Some yes, some no. Safe-spotting still works. BUT — new bosses need prayer flicking (learn it!). Also, GE economy shifted — don't use 2020 GDP prices for anything!

Should I start a new account or keep my old one?

KEEP YOUR OLD ONE! All your stats + GP are still there. Starting over = wasting hundreds of hours. The only reason to start new = you want to experience the new tutorials/quests "fresh".
