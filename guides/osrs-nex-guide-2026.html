OSRS Nex Guide 2026 — Strategy, Gear Setup & Zarosian Loot

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 15 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
👹 Boss Guide — Updated June 2026

Nex is Gielinor's most feared general — a 5-phase Zarosian boss locked deep in the Ancient Prison beneath the God Wars Dungeon. This guide covers every phase mechanic, duo and trio strategies, gear setups, and how to farm her 10M-16M GP/hr profitably.

🧊 Nex — Quick Reference

Location:Ancient Prison (God Wars Dungeon)Team Size:Duo/Trio (best GP/hr)
Phases:5 (Smoke/Shadow/Blood/Ice/Zaros)GP/hr:10M-16M (duo)

1. Requirements & Access

Frozen Key: Assemble from 4 pieces dropped by GWD generals (Zamorak, Saradomin, Bandos, Armadyl). Ancient Ceremonial Robes (or 40 Zarosian KC) to enter. 70+ Agility for the Ancient Prison shortcut. Recommended: 90+ Ranged, 90+ Defence, 77+ Prayer (Rigour), 80+ Agility.

3. Phase-by-Phase Strategy

PhaseNex AttacksStrategy
💨 SmokeRanged + Poison cloudsSpread out. Don't stand in poison clouds. Use Protect from Missiles.
🌑 ShadowMelee + Shadow damageStay away from dark spots. Protect from Melee. Nex charges shadow bombs — dodge.
🩸 BloodHeals from all attacksCRITICAL: When Nex summons red blood orbs, STOP attacking. Every hit heals her. Wait for orbs to despawn.
❄️ IceFreeze + Protect from MeleeUse Ranged only (Nex prays melee). Dodge ice prison attack — if you get frozen, teammate must free you.
⚡ ZarosAll attacks + Turmoil curseSwitch to Melee (Fang). Nex uses Turmoil — drains your stats. Stay on Protect from Magic. Final phase — dump specs.

4. Duo Roles

Player 1 (Main DPS): Ranged for Smoke/Shadow/Blood/Ice → Melee (Fang) for Zaros. Focuses on dealing damage.

Player 2 (Support): Same style setup, but prioritizes freeing frozen teammates in Ice phase and managing blood orb awareness in Blood phase. Brings spec transfer weapon for Player 1's Fang specs in Zaros phase.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Nex harder than Theatre of Blood?

A: Different difficulty types. ToB requires perfect team coordination across 6 rooms. Nex is a single 5-phase boss fight. Nex is easier to learn than ToB but still punishing — especially the Blood and Zaros phases.

Q: Can I solo Nex?

A: Technically yes, but extremely difficult. Nex solo requires maxed stats, perfect prayer flicking, and BIS gear. Most players trio Nex for consistent kills. Duo is the sweet spot for profit.

🧊 The General Awaits

Nex is one of the most profitable and mechanically rewarding bosses in OSRS. Learn her 5-phase rhythm, master the Blood phase discipline, and the Zarosian treasure will flow. Find a reliable duo partner and start farming.
