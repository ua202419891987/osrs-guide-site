1. What Is Quest Cape & Why Get It

The Quest Point Cape is awarded upon completing all quests in Old School RuneScape (currently 170+ quests as of 2026). It's one of the most prestigious untrimmed capes in the game, showing you've mastered every piece of story content Jagex has released.

Benefits of Quest Cape

BenefitDetails
Quest Cape TeleportUnlimited teleports to the Legends' Guild (closest teleport to a bank)
Lumbridge Elite DiaryQuest Cape required for elite completion — fairy rings without dramen staff!
Trimmed Cape PotentialPrerequisite for trimmed Achievement Diary Cape
Full Game AccessAll areas, bosses, mini-games, and transportation unlocked
~250 Quest PointsMassive block of quest point rewards (tears of Guthix time, RFD gloves)

Total quest XP rewards: Over 3 million XP across all skills from quests alone. Smart routing turns quests into your primary training method for many skills.

2. Before You Start — Preparation

Essential Setup

  • RuneLite: Install the official client. Enable the "Quest Helper" plugin — it provides step-by-step guides in-game.
  • Stamina potions: Buy a stack. You'll run. A lot.
  • Teleport tabs/jewelry: Games necklace, ring of dueling, skills necklace, combat bracelet. Buy 20+ of each.
  • Cash stack: ~5-10M GP for quest items, teleports, and supplies. You can earn this back easily during questing.
  • Graceful outfit: Get this as early as possible (rooftop agility from level 1). You'll wear it 90% of the time.

Questing Mindset

Spacebar warriors get quest capes in 2-3 weeks of casual play. If you read dialogue, double the time. Either way is valid — just be consistent.

8. Tier 5: Grandmaster & Endgame Quests

These are your final hurdles. By now you should have 70+ in all combat stats and solid gear. Each grandmaster quest typically takes 1-3 hours.

Final Quest Checklist

  • Desert Treasure II — The Fallen Empire: The hardest quest boss fights in OSRS. Requires 75 Magic, 70+ combat stats. Bring best gear.
  • Song of the Elves: Seren fragment fight is notorious. Bring Phoenix necklaces + nightshade method if struggling.
  • Dragon Slayer II: Galvek (final boss) is a grandmaster difficulty fight. Ruby bolts (e) are essential.
  • Monkey Madness II: The platform puzzle is frustrating. Glough fight has multiple phases.
  • Sins of the Father: Vanstrom Klause final phase is very punishing. Bring best melee gear + super combat potions.

11. Ironman Quest Cape Strategy

Ironmen face additional challenges: you can't buy quest items from GE, and boss fights must be done with self-obtained gear. But quest XP rewards are even MORE valuable for Ironmen.

Ironman-Specific Tips

  • Quest XP is your best friend: Use quest XP to skip the worst Ironman grinds (early Herblore, early Construction, early Prayer)
  • Item gathering: Keep a list of required quest items and gather them in batches. Many quests share items.
  • Herblore is the bottleneck: You need 70 Herblore for Song of the Elves. Plant every herb seed you get. Do farm runs constantly.
  • Boss quests: For DS2/MM2/SOTE, get 70+ Ranged with RCB + broad bolts minimum. Iban's staff (Underground Pass) carries magic until trident.
  • Graceful rush: Get full graceful before starting heavy questing. You'll use 1000+ stamina potions otherwise.

13. FAQ

Q: How long does Quest Cape take?

Spacebarring: 40-60 hours of gameplay (2-3 weeks casual). Reading dialogue: 80-120 hours. Ironman: Add 30-50% more time for gathering items and training skills without GE.

Q: Can I get Quest Cape as a low-level account?

No. You need minimum ~85 combat, 70+ in most skills, and solid gear for grandmaster boss fights. The lowest combat Quest Cape is typically around ~85-90 combat for experienced players.

Q: What's the hardest quest?

Desert Treasure II has the hardest boss fights by far (The Whisperer, The Leviathan, Duke Sucellus, Vardorvis). Song of the Elves has a punishing final boss (Seren fragment). Dragon Slayer II has Galvek, which is a DPS check.

Q: Do I lose Quest Cape when new quests release?

Yes. You must complete all quests including new releases. When a new quest drops, you lose the ability to equip the cape until you complete it. This is why Quest Cape is considered a "maintenance" achievement.

Q: Should I get 175 QP for RFD first?

Absolutely. Barrows gloves are the single most important account upgrade from quests. Rush 175 QP, get Barrows gloves, then continue toward Quest Cape with proper gear.

Q: Best order: RFD first or Quest Cape first?

RFD first (175 QP) → you'll naturally be halfway to Quest Cape (~125 QP remaining). The Barrows gloves make remaining quest boss fights significantly easier.
