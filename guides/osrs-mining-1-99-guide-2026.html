1. ⛏️ Overview & Why Train Mining in 2026

Mining is one of the three original gathering skills and the gateway to Smithing, Crafting, and a healthy stockpile of GP. In 2026 it remains one of the most flexible skills in the game: you can train it completely AFK in the Motherlode Mine, push for the fastest XP with 3-tick granite, or turn it into a money maker with Blast Mine and Amethyst. The 1–99 grind is long but forgiving — there is a method for every playstyle.

🎯 The Three Routes

  • Speed route: 3-tick granite from 45–99 (or Volcanic Mine 70+). Highest XP, highest attention.
  • AFK route: Motherlode Mine 30–99 with the Prospector kit. Lower XP but almost free attention.
  • Profit route: Blast Mine / Amethyst for GP while still gaining XP. Slower XP, positive cash flow.
💡 Quest XP Skips the Boring Levels

Doric's Quest (1,300 XP, no requirements), The Dig Site, Plague City, The Giant Dwarf, The Lost Tribe and Another Slice of H.A.M. combine for ~27,525 Mining XP — enough to jump from level 1 to 37 without mining a single rock. Always do these first.

🏆 Recommended Default: Hybrid Route

Quests to ~37 → Motherlode Mine 37–72 (grab Prospector kit) → Upper MLM or Volcanic Mine 72–85 → 3-tick granite 85–99 for speed, or Amethyst 92–99 for GP. This balances attention, cost, and progress.

💡 "Amethyst" — Not "Ammonite"

A common newbie mix-up: Ammonite is a combat training method (Ammonite Crabs), not a Mining method. The high-level profitable Mining method is Amethyst crystals in the Mining Guild. If a guide tells you to "mine ammonite," it is wrong.

🏆 Recommended Budget → BiS

Budget: Rune pickaxe (wield it), no outfit. Mid: Dragon pickaxe + Prospector kit (+2.5% XP). BiS: Crystal pickaxe + full Prospector + Celestial ring (i) + Mining gloves (expert, from MLM) + Varrock Armour (where it works). 💡 Mining Gloves Save Clicks

Expert Mining Gloves (from the Motherlode shop) reduce the 3-tick granite cycle to 3t3g, the fastest Mining XP in the game. Pair with the Crystal pickaxe for the ~126K XP/hr ceiling.

8. ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Myth-Busting)

❌ Myth 1: "Mine ammonite for XP."

Wrong. Ammonite Crabs are a combat AFK method, not Mining. For Mining XP use Amethyst (the purple crystal) or 3-tick granite. The names sound alike but the skills don't overlap.

❌ Myth 2: "Carry the best pickaxe in your inventory."

Inefficient. A pickaxe only works while wielded. An unwielded dragon pickaxe gives you zero speed — you are mining at bronze speed. Wield it.

❌ Myth 3: "Motherlode Mine is a waste of time."

False. MLM is slow per hour but gives the Prospector kit (+2.5% XP) and nuggets for gloved upgrades. It pays off across the whole 99. Skip it only if you already own the kit.

❌ Myth 4: "Varrock Armour boosts every method."

No. Varrock Armour's double-ore effect does not work at Motherlode Mine, Blast Mine, or Volcanic Mine. Use it at conventional ore rocks only.

❌ Myth 5: "3-tick granite is easy GP."

It costs GP (waterskins / Humidify supplies, ~35K/hr) and is the most click-intensive method in the skill. It is the fastest XP, not a money maker.

10. ❓ FAQ

What is the fastest way to 99 Mining?

3-tick granite (3t4g) is the fastest, reaching ~126K XP/hr at 99 with a Crystal pickaxe and Expert Mining Gloves. Volcanic Mine (70+) is a faster team alternative at ~94K XP/hr. Both are far ahead of AFK methods.

Best AFK method for low attention?

The Motherlode Mine — mine pay-dirt, deposit, collect. Upper level (72+) is the most AFK-friendly, and you earn the Prospector kit. Expect 40–62K XP/hr while barely clicking.

Can Mining make money?

Yes. Blast Mine (~200–400K GP/hr at 75+) and Amethyst (~300–500K GP/hr at 92+) are solid money makers. Gem Rocks (Shilo Village) also pay 200–400K GP/hr from level 40.

Do I need quests?

For the best methods, yes: The Dig Site (Motherlode), Shilo Village (gem rocks), Bone Voyage (Volcanic Mine), and Song of the Elves (best Amethyst spot). Doric's Quest and a handful of low-req quests give ~27K free XP to level 37.

Does Varrock Armour help everywhere?

No — it only gives a 10% double-ore chance at conventional rocks. It does not work at Motherlode Mine, Blast Mine, or Volcanic Mine.

11. 🏁 Final Tips

  • Wield your pickaxe: an unwielded dragon pickaxe is dead weight. Upgrade 41 → 61 → 71.
  • Get the Prospector kit first: +2.5% XP pays back across the whole grind.
  • Pick your priority: granite for speed, MLM for AFK, Blast/Amethyst for GP.
  • Stack Shooting Stars: free Celestial ring charges (+4 invisible) while AFK.
  • Verify GE prices: Amethyst/Blast GP/hr swings daily — check before big grinds.
