1. How Zero-Requirement Money Making Works

Every method in this guide meets ALL of these criteria:

  • Zero skill levels required — Level 1 everything, including combat skills
  • Zero quests required — No need to complete any quest
  • Zero GP to start — You can begin with 0 GP in your inventory
  • Zero gear required — No special equipment needed
  • Zero Grand Exchange access required — GE makes things faster but isn't mandatory

Note: "Zero skills" means the method itself doesn't require skill levels. You may incidentally level skills (like collecting items gives XP in the associated skill), but no skill check blocks you from starting.

💰 GP/hr Ranges Are Based on OSRS Wiki Data

The GP/hour numbers in this guide are sourced from the OSRS Wiki Money Making Guide and represent mid-range estimates for players with minimal experience. Your actual rates depend on efficiency, luck, and competition from other players doing the same method.

🏆 The #1 Zero-Requirement Method: Mort Myre Fungi

Collecting mort myre fungi in Canifis is the undisputed king of zero-requirement money making. At 300K-400K GP/hr, it outperforms most low-skill P2P methods. You just need to: (1) Enter Mort Myre Swamp (requires completion of Priest in Peril quest — takes 30 minutes), (2) Cast Bloom on the swamp vegetation (requires any nature runes, which cost ~200 GP each), (3) Pick up the fungi that spawn. Each fungi sells for ~1,500 GP on the GE. With good routing, you collect 200-300 per hour.

5. Zero to First Million: Step-by-Step

This exact sequence works for both F2P and P2P accounts starting from absolutely nothing:

① F2P Route (0 to 1M GP in ~15 hours)

  1. Hours 1-3: Collect monk robes at Monastery (Edgeville). Hop worlds, loot 4 robes per world. Sell on GE for ~2K each. Target: ~100K GP.
  2. Hours 4-6: Collect cowhides in Lumbridge/Falador while training combat. Sell 1,000+ hides for ~200K GP total. By now you have ~300K GP.
  3. Hours 7-9: Buy runes from shops and resell on GE. Air runes from Runecrafting guild, mind runes from shops. Safely earns 50K/hr. You now have ~450K GP.
  4. Hours 10-12: Level Magic to 33 via splashing or quests. Invest 200K into runes. Start telegrabbing Wine of Zamorak. Earn 150K-200K/hr.
  5. Hours 13-15: Continue wines. You'll hit 1M GP within 3-4 hours of telegrabbing. Congratulations — you now have your first million.

② P2P Route (0 to 1M GP in ~4 hours)

  1. Hour 1: Complete Priest in Peril quest (takes ~30 min). Go to Canifis, collect 100-150 mort myre fungi. Sell for ~150K GP on GE.
  2. Hour 2-3: Use your GP to buy nature runes. Continue fungi collection. Earn 300K-400K/hr. Total ~750K GP.
  3. Hour 4: Final fungi push. You reach 1M GP in under 4 hours of active play as a member.

📊 P2P vs F2P: The Time Gap

F2P route to 1M GP: ~15 hours. P2P route: ~4 hours. The difference is massive because membership unlocks mort myre fungi (350K/hr vs 40K/hr for F2P methods). If you can afford the 14-day bond (~10M GP in 2026), the time savings more than justify the cost. Alternatively, reach 1M in F2P (15 hours), buy a bond? Not quite — bonds cost ~10M GP in 2026.

7. Hidden Gem Methods Most Guides Miss

These methods are less well-known but offer excellent GP/hr with zero requirements. Most money making guides overlook them completely:

① Collecting Desert Goat Horns (P2P, 150K-200K/hr)

In the Kharidian Desert, goats drop horn spawns. Collect them, sell on GE for ~8K each. Requires: nothing but walking through the desert. Bring waterskins (free from Shantay Pass).

② Picking Potato Cactus (P2P, 120K-180K/hr)

Found in the Kalphite Lair and around the desert. Each cactus spine sells for ~3K GP on the GE. Requires no skills, just a knife to cut them (free from general store).

③ Collecting Snape Grass (P2P, 60K-90K/hr)

On Waterbirth Island's surface. Snape grass spawns on the ground. Collect, bank at the chest, repeat. Each sells for ~1,000 GP. The route is circular and takes 2-3 minutes per lap.

④ Reselling from Ogre Shop (F2P, 50K-80K/hr)

The Ogre shop in the Training Camp sells various items below GE price. Buy arrows, javelins, and runes — resell on GE for profit. Requires some starting capital (50K GP recommended).

🎯 Why These Are "Hidden Gems"

These methods are less popular because they require specific knowledge of spawn locations and routes. The trade-off? Less competition means more consistent profit. While 50 players compete for the same monk robes, you'll have a hidden gem spot entirely to yourself.

8. Money Making No Skills FAQs

What's the absolute fastest zero-requirement method?

Mort Myre fungi at 300K-400K GP/hr. But it requires membership and completion of Priest in Peril (30 minutes). For pure F2P with no skills: Wine of Zamorak telegrabbing at 150K-200K GP/hr (requires 33 Magic, achievable in 2-3 hours from level 1).

Can I make enough for a bond with zero skills?

In theory, yes, but it will take a very long time. Bonds cost ~10M GP in 2026. At 40K GP/hr (best F2P zero-skill), that's 250 hours of grinding. At 350K GP/hr (mort myre fungi, P2P), that's ~29 hours. Most players use zero-requirement methods to build starter GP, then pivot to actual money making methods with minimal skill training.

Are these methods affected by competition?

Some are. Monk robes and cowhides are popular — you'll compete with other players on busy worlds. Mort myre fungi and swamp toads are less contested. Hidden gems (goat horns, potato cactus) face almost no competition. The OSRS Wiki money making guide gives rates assuming "some competition" — expect 10-20% less in peak hours.

Do I need the Grand Exchange for these methods?

Most methods benefit from GE access, but several don't need it. Monk robes can be sold to the Runecrafting guild shop. Cowhides can be sold to the crafting tutor. Bananas can be sold directly to the Karamja store. GE gives better prices, but non-GE alternatives exist for every method.

What's the best method if I only have 30 minutes to play?

Bird house runs (P2P, requires 5 Hunter — takes 20 minutes to unlock). One bird house run takes 5 minutes and earns ~20K-40K GP. Check them twice per day for optimal passive income. For F2P: Cowhide collecting — a 30-minute session earns ~15K-25K GP with zero investment.

Can I do these methods on an Ironman?

Ironmen cannot trade on the GE, so selling items is not applicable. However, collection methods still work — you keep the items for your own use. Mort myre fungi is excellent for Ironmen (used in prayer potions). Cowhides are used for Crafting. The GP values listed don't apply to Ironmen, but the item utility value often exceeds the GE price.

Which method also trains my account the best?

Cowhide collecting (combat training + crafting) and woodcutting (firemaking + fletching synergy) are the best dual-purpose zero-requirement methods. You earn GP while building your account. Wine of Zamorak traps Magic (33 required), which doesn't progress your account much but earns good GP on the side.

Are there any risks with these methods?

Most zero-requirement methods are safe in non-Wilderness areas. Wine of Zamorak is located in deep Wilderness (level ~16) — you can be PKed. Carry only what you can afford to lose. Mort myre fungi is safe but requires running through Canifis (watch for werewolves at low combat). All other methods in this guide are 100% safe.

Will these methods get nerfed?

Jagex occasionally adjusts item values and spawn rates. The OSRS Wiki updates money making rates within days of game updates. Mort myre fungi has been a consistent 300K+/hr method since 2020 with only minor adjustments. Item collection methods are generally stable because they require manual effort and can't be botted easily.
