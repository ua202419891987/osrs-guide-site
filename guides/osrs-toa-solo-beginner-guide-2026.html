4. Optimal Beginner Boss Order

Kephri → Akkha → Ba-Ba → Zebak → Wardens. This saves the trickier fights for later when you have smelling salts and extra supplies.

5. Kephri (Path of Scabaras) — ⭐⭐

  • Stab ONLY with Hasta or Keris Partisan.
  • Bait dung piles into one corner.
  • Pray against spawns or kill eggs with Blowpipe.
  • Shield phase: kill scarabs in Mage → Range → Melee order.

6. Akkha (Path of Het) — ⭐⭐⭐

  • Switch attack style based on Akkha's overhead prayer.
  • Memory puzzle: stand in matching safe quadrant. Fail = massive damage. Use audio cues.
  • Clone phase: kill clone → drag Akkha to spot → deal damage.
  • Final phase: dump Dragon Dagger specs, dodge orbs.
⚠️ Akkha is the #1 beginner wall. Practice on 0 invocation. Turn on game audio for distinct puzzle sound cues.

7. Ba-Ba (Path of Apmeken) — ⭐⭐⭐

  • Run away from ground smash AOE.
  • Stand near debris for falling rocks.
  • Switch to Range for boulder phase. Blowpipe excels.

8. Zebak (Path of Crondis) — ⭐⭐

  • Ranged ONLY — never melee!
  • Prayer switch: Protect Magic vs pots, Protect Missiles vs rocks.
  • Roar + poison: push jugs into rocks for safe zones.
  • Wave attack: move between gaps — similar to Galvek waves.

9. Wardens (Final Boss) — ⭐⭐⭐⭐

P1 Obelisk: Attack with any style. Tank orbs, move when ~30 HP lost.

P2 Elidinis' Warden: Mage → guardian prays → core spawns → Melee + Dragon Dagger specs. Repeat with Range → Mage cycles. Dodge windmill and triangle laser.

P3 Tumeken's Warden (Enrage): Use Range. 3-attack cycle: left → right → center. Kill all red skulls at HP breakpoints. At 5% — tiles disappear, survive and you win.

11. FAQ

Q: Can I really solo ToA as a beginner?

A: Yes! ToA was designed to be soloable at Entry/Normal mode. Start at 0 invocation and work up.

Q: Best purple chance vs difficulty?

A: 150 (Normal mode). Reasonable unique rates without punishing Expert mechanics.

Q: Do I need Rigour and Augury?

A: Helpful but not mandatory for Entry. Prioritize Rigour first (~15M) — it's the largest ranged DPS upgrade.

ToA is the best entry point to OSRS raiding. Start at 0 invocation, learn one boss at a time, and before you know it you'll be farming 150s. Everyone started where you are now. See you in the tombs! ⚱️
