Why 100M Is the Magic Number

The first 100M GP transforms your account. With it, you can buy every zenyte jewellery piece, unlock Rigour and Augury, afford solid gear for all three styles, and still have a flipping stack left over. The gap from 0 to 100M feels bigger than the gap from 100M to 1B — and it is. But with the right system, it takes 4-8 weeks of casual play.

100M Buys You

  • 💰 Torture (12M) + Anguish (15M) + Tormented (12M) = 39M
  • 💰 Rigour (15M) + Augury scroll (~3M) = 18M
  • 💰 Blowpipe (3M) + Trident of Swamp (3M) = 6M
  • 💰 Bandos Chestplate + Tassets = 55M
  • 💰 Remaining: ~20M for supplies, flipping, and emergency GP

Passive Income: Herb Runs & Dailies

Passive income is the foundation. It's guaranteed GP that requires almost no attention. Stack these and your active PvM profits become bonus GP, not survival GP.

Herb Runs: Your #1 Income Source

With 7 patches (8 with Harmony Island), Ranarr or Snapdragon seeds, and ultracompost, each run takes 5 minutes and nets 250-350K profit. Do this 5 times per day and you're making over 1M/day for 25 minutes of work.

  • Seeds: Ranarr (35K seed → ~7 herbs avg = 55K profit/patch) or Snapdragon (50K seed → ~7 herbs = 70K profit/patch with risk)
  • Patches: Ardougne, Catherby, Falador, Hosidius, Morytania (Ectophial), Troll Stronghold (unlocked via My Arm's Big Adventure), Weiss (Making Friends with My Arm)
  • Ultracompost: Mandatory. Without it, your herbs die 3x more often. Buy volcanic ash and compost potions.
  • Magic Secateurs: Equip them during harvesting for ~10% more herbs. Quest item from Fairytale I.

Daily Money Makers

  • Varrock Battlestaves (Medium Diary): Buy 30 for 210K, sell on GE for 270K = 60K/day, 2 min
  • Managing Miscellania: 10 workers on herbs + 5 on maples = ~100K/day profit after costs. Requires Royal Trouble quest and 750K+ coffer. Check once per week.
  • Birdhouse Runs: 4 birdhouses per run, 50 min growth, ~50K/run. Great Hunter XP too.

Active Money: Zulrah, Vorkath, Gauntlet

Vorkath (Best Consistent GP: 2-4M/hr)

Requirements: Dragon Slayer II complete. Recommended gear: Elite Void + Salve amulet (ei) + Dragon Crossbow + Ruby/Diamond bolts (e). Vorkath is the most consistent money maker in the game — no RNG dependency, just consistent 130-160K per kill. At 3-4 min kills, that's 2-4M/hr. Learn the Woox Walk during acid phase to push towards 4M/hr.

Corrupted Gauntlet (Highest Potential: 4-6M/hr)

Requirements: Song of the Elves. Zero supply cost, zero gear requirement. Complete CG runs and the Enhanced Crystal Weapon Seed alone is worth 120M+. Even without the big drop, regular loot averages 500K/run. At 8-10 completions per hour, you're making 4-5M/hr guaranteed — and 120M+ when the seed drops.

Zulrah (Good Learning Boss: 1-2M/hr)

Lower GP than Vorkath, but easier to learn. Zulrah teaches you gear switching, prayer switching, and rotation memorization — skills that translate to every other boss. Use Trident of the Swamp + Blowpipe. Western Provinces Hard diary gives a free resurrect per day, making learning forgiving.

GE Flipping for Extra Profit

Flipping turns your cash stack into a passive income machine. While you're bossing, your GE offers are working.

Flipping Strategy for 20-100M Bank

  1. High-volume items: Zulrah scales, blood runes, dart tips. 1-3% margins, but instant turnover.
  2. Mid-volume gear: Ahrim's pieces, godswords, whips, blowpipes. 2-5% margins, slower turnover.
  3. Never flip one item with your whole stack. Diversify across 6-8 slots. If one flips poorly, others compensate.

Basic Flip Math

With a 50M flipping stack, averaging 2% return per flip, and flipping 3 times per day: 50M × 2% = 1M/day = 30M/month. That's a free zenyte per month for 15 minutes of work per day.

Money Traps to Avoid

  • Don't stake or gamble. The sand casino (Duel Arena is gone, but gambling exists elsewhere) is the fastest way to 0 GP. Don't.
  • Don't sell gear to buy other gear. Never sell your blowpipe to afford a torture. You need both. Save up.
  • Don't chase the latest "OP" method. Every new YouTube "BEST MONEY MAKER 2026!!" video causes a temporary crash as everyone floods in. Stick to proven methods.
  • Don't upgrade side-grades. Primordial boots (+1 strength over dragon boots) for 30M is worse than buying Rigour for 15M. Buy the big upgrades first.
  • Don't ignore supplies. Prayer potions, sharks, and scales cost GP. Track your supply costs and don't use sara brews where monksfish will do.

FAQ

What's the single fastest way to 100M?

Corrupted Gauntlet. Zero supply cost, 4-6M/hr effective rate with enhanced seed chance. But it requires SotE completion and a steep learning curve. Budget 20-50 deaths before your first completion.

I hate PvM. How else can I make 100M?

Herb runs (5x/day) + Managing Miscellania + GE flipping + Runecrafting blood runes at 77 RC. Slower (2-3 months), but 100% consistent with zero bossing required.

Should I prioritize gear or stats?

Stats almost always give more DPS per GP than gear. 85 Range + Rigour + budget gear beats 75 Range + Armadyl + no Rigour. Train stats first, buy gear second.

How do I protect my GP from inflation?

Don't sit on cash. Gold loses value over time in OSRS. Convert your GP into usable gear, supplies, and prayers. A torture amulet bought today costs the same as a torture amulet bought next year — but your cash stack will buy less due to inflation.

⭐ The First 100M Rule

The players who reach 100M fastest aren't the ones with the best gear or the highest stats. They're the ones who pick a system and stick to it. Do your herb runs every single day. Do your Vorkath or Gauntlet hour every single day. Don't hop between methods. Consistency beats optimization every time.
