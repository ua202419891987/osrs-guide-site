1. Quest Prerequisites — The Full Chain

✅ Action 1: Complete All 7 Prerequisite Quests CRITICAL

Blood Moon Rises requires every previous Myreque quest plus the Theatre of Blood entry quest. This is 8 quests total, and you can't skip any of them.

The full prerequisite chain, in order:

  1. Priest in Peril — Unlocks Morytania. 5 minutes. Non-negotiable.
  2. Nature Spirit — Unlocks the swamp. Requires 18 Crafting. 15 minutes.
  3. In Search of the Myreque — First real Myreque quest. Requires 25 Agility. 20 minutes.
  4. In Aid of the Myreque — Requires 15 Mining, 25 Crafting, 7 Magic. 30 minutes.
  5. Darkness of Hallowvale — Long maze section. Requires 5 Construction, 20 Mining, 22 Thieving, 26 Agility, 32 Crafting, 33 Magic, 40 Strength. 60-90 minutes.
  6. A Taste of Hope — First boss fight (Ranis Drakan). Requires 40 Attack, 38 Crafting, 45 Herblore, 40 Slayer. 45 minutes + boss fight.
  7. Sins of the Father — Major boss fight (Lowerniel Drakan phase 1). Requires 62 Woodcutting, 60 Fletching, 56 Crafting, 52 Agility, 50 Attack, 50 Slayer, 49 Magic. 90-120 minutes.
  8. A Night at the Theatre — ToB entry quest. Requires Theatre of Blood completion (can be done in a team). 20-45 minutes.

⏱️ Total time if you have all stats: ~6-8 hours

⏱️ Total time if you need to train stats too: 20-50+ hours

If you've done zero Myreque quests, start with Priest in Peril TODAY.

  • Magic (70-90): Bursting dust devils or nechryaels on task — ~100K XP/hr. 10-15 hours.

7. Ironman-Specific Prep

✅ Action 7: Ironman Prep — Same Checklist, 3x the Time HIGH (Ironmen)

Ironmen can't buy supplies on the GE. Everything must be farmed or crafted. Start earlier and focus on self-sufficiency.

Ironman supply checklist (before June 30):

  • Prayer potions: Farm ranarrs (38 Farming required, 5 patches, ~40 herbs per run). Do 2-3 herb runs daily starting today = ~500 ranarrs by June 30. That's 125 prayer potions (4-dose).
  • Sharks: Fish at the Fishing Guild (76 Fishing) or Tempoross for raw sharks. Minnows at 82 Fishing = 300-400 sharks/hr.
  • Karambwans: Karamja tai bwo wannai spot (65 Fishing). Fish 200+ per hour. Cook them on a fire nearby.
  • Super combat potions: Requires 90 Herblore. If below 90, use Super Attack + Super Strength separately.
  • Runes: Death runes (for ancients if needed) — buy from Mage Arena shop or craft via the Abyss.
  • GP for death fees: Alch your slayer drops. Aim for 3-5M GP in alchables before June 30.

⚡ Ironman time estimate: Start prepping 30+ days before release if you have zero supplies. At 19 days, focus on: ① quest requirements ② slayer level ③ prayer potion stockpile. Everything else is secondary.
