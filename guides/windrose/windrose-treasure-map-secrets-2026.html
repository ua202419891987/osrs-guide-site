Decoding a Map Step-by-Step

Step 1: Identify the starting landmark (always the first symbol). Travel to that island and locate the landmark. Step 2: Read the direction symbol. Turn to face that direction from the landmark. Step 3: Count the distance symbols (footprints) and walk that distance. Step 4: Look for the environment symbol (waves, mountain, fire) to fine-tune your position. Step 5: When all symbols align, the X should be within 2 meters of your current position. Use the Shovel (right-click on ground) to dig.

Digging Warning: Digging consumes 5% Shovel durability per use. If you have dug 5 times within a 5-meter radius and found nothing, you misread a symbol. Reread the map.

â?FAQ â?Treasure & Secrets Questions

Q: Do treasure maps respawn or are they one-time only?

Treasure map chests respawn on a timer: Common chests respawn every 3 in-game days, Uncommon every 7 days, Rare every 14 days, and Epic/Legendary do not respawn (one-time loot per playthrough). However, you can buy infinite maps from the Cartographer â?the chest locations are randomly generated each time you use a new map.

Q: Can I trade treasure maps with other players?

Yes. Treasure maps are tradeable items. You can give a map to a friend, and they can decode it and loot the treasure. Note: the person who loots the chest gets the loot â?the map owner does not automatically receive it. Coordinate with your trading partner to avoid loot disputes.

Q: I found a cave but it's too dark to see. What do I do?

You need a Lantern (crafted: 5 cloth + 2 oil). Equip it in your off-hand (shield slot). The Lantern provides 15 meters of illumination. Upgrade to the Everlight Lantern (reward from the "Spelunker" achievement) for unlimited range and never-depleting fuel.

Q: Are there any missable secrets?

Yes. The "Ancient Vault" caves do not respawn â?if you loot them and miss a Lore Page, you cannot get it again in that playthrough. Also, if you sink a Ghost Ship without boarding it, you miss the "Cursed Crew" title forever (in that playthrough). Always board Ghost Ships before destroying them.

Q: How do I know if I've found all the secrets?

Check your Achievement progress in the menu. The game tracks: treasure maps looted, caves discovered, Lore Pages collected, secret camps visited, and easter eggs found. There is also a "Secrets Found" counter on your pause screen â?it displays X/47 (47 is the total number of secrets in the game).
