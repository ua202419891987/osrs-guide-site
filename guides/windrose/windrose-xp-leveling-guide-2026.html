Q: Do I get XP for killing bosses?

Only the first time you kill each unique boss. The game grants a one-time XP reward (500â?,000 depending on the boss) on your very first kill. Subsequent kills of the same boss give zero XP. There are currently 6 major bosses in Windrose, offering a total of ~4,000 XP in one-time boss rewards.

Q: Can I reset my level and re-earn XP?

No. Windrose has no prestige, rebirth, or reset system. Your level is permanent. Once you hit level 15, your XP bar is gone forever. The developers have stated they have no plans to add a reset mechanic as it would undermine the quest-and-exploration-focused design of the XP system.

Q: What happens if I miss a chest in a POI?

You get zero XP for that POI. The game checks whether every lootable container in the POI boundary has been opened. If even one chest remains closed when you leave the POI radius, the completion trigger does not fire. You can re-enter the POI and loot the missed chest â?the XP will then register. Use the interaction list (Tab key) to scan for missed containers.

Q: Is there any repeatable XP source?

No. Every XP source in Windrose is one-time only. Once you complete a quest, clear a POI, discover an island, or collect a lore note, that XP is permanently consumed. This is why the developers set the level cap at 15 â?there is exactly enough one-time XP in the game to reach cap with some room to spare, but not enough to overshoot by a significant margin.

Q: Which faction gives the most XP?

The Sovereign Coalition gives the most accessible XP because their quests are more numerous and generally safer. The Crimson Fleet gives less total faction XP but their quests are faster to complete (more combat, less travel). The Order of the Deep gives the least faction-specific quest XP but their exploration bonuses complement discovery XP well. For pure XP efficiency: Sovereign Coalition > Crimson Fleet > Order of the Deep.

Q: How do I check my exact XP number?

Open your inventory (I key) and hover over your level indicator in the top-left of the character panel. A tooltip displays your current XP and the XP needed for the next level. The tooltip also shows your total accumulated XP â?useful for tracking exactly how close you are to the 48,500 cap.

Q: Does cooperative play affect XP gain?

No. XP rewards are identical whether you complete content solo or in a group. There is no XP split or bonus. Each player who participates in a quest completion or POI clear receives the full XP reward independently. The game does not divide XP among party members. This makes co-op strictly better for efficiency since you can clear content faster with no XP penalty.

Q: Will the level cap increase remove unearned XP from completed content?

No. When the cap rises to 20 (expected late 2026), all content you have already completed will remain completed. You will need new content â?the upcoming underwater biome, new quests, new POIs â?to earn XP for levels 16â?0. The developers have confirmed this. If you are level 15 now, you will not be able to go back and re-earn XP from old content. Save some undiscovered islands for the update!
