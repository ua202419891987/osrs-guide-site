OSRS Killing Green Dragons Money Per Hour 2026 — 400K-800K GP/hr Wilderness Guide

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 9 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🐉 Money Making — Wilderness Combat — Updated June 2026

Green dragons are one of the best entry-level combat money makers in OSRS. Every kill drops green dragonhide (~1,500 GP) and dragon bones (~2,500 GP) — two items with permanent high demand from Crafters and Prayer trainers. With moderate stats and smart Wilderness awareness, you can earn 400K-800K GP per hour in 2026.

6. Anti-PK Survival Strategy

  1. Stay below level 20 Wilderness. East Green Dragons (17-19) is your safest option. You can teleport freely below level 20 — this is your #1 escape tool.
  2. Watch your minimap CONSTANTLY. The minimap shows white dots for other players. If you see a white dot approaching, teleport immediately. Do NOT wait to see if they're friendly.
  3. Keep your teleport ready. A Ring of Dueling (1-click to Castle Wars or Ferox Enclave) is your emergency escape. Equip it or keep it in a visible inventory slot.
  4. Prayer flick Protect from Melee. When not in combat with a dragon, keep Protect from Melee active. If a PKer attacks, you're already protected from their first hit.
  5. Use low-population worlds. Fewer players = fewer PKers = less competition for dragon spawns. Worlds 400-500 are generally quieter for Wilderness activities.
  6. Bank frequently. Don't get greedy. Bank every 100K-150K GP worth of loot. The longer you stay, the more you risk losing.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it worth killing green dragons for money in 2026?

A: Yes, especially for mid-level accounts. At 450K-680K GP/hr with minimal requirements (60+ melee stats), green dragons outperform most skilling methods and require zero upfront investment. For accounts with 70-80 combat stats, this is one of the best GP/hr available until you unlock bossing.

Q: Should I use a looting bag at green dragons?

A: Always. The looting bag doubles your inventory capacity for dragonhide and bones. Without it, you'll bank twice as often, cutting your effective GP/hr. Looting bags are cheap (~1K GP) and can be obtained from any Slayer monster in the Wilderness.

Q: What if I can't access the Myths' Guild for safe dragons?

A: Stick to East Wilderness dragons. The PK risk is manageable if you follow the survival rules (below level 20, watch minimap, teleport immediately). Most PKers at green dragons are low-level pures — if you have 70+ Defence and Protect from Melee up, you have a good chance of tanking them to the wilderness ditch or teleporting.

🐉 Consistent Mid-Game Income

Green dragons remain one of the most reliable mid-game money makers in OSRS. The two guaranteed drops (hide + bones) create a stable GP floor that doesn't depend on rare drops. With smart Wilderness awareness and a looting bag, you'll earn consistent cash to fund your first whip, better gear, or buyable skills.

Gear up, stay alert, and start collecting those hides.
