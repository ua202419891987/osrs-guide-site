Sailing Overview — How the Skill Works

Sailing is OSRS's first new skill since 2005 (Hunter & Construction). You build and captain a ship, explore the ocean, trade cargo, fight naval battles, and discover uncharted islands.

Key Sailing Mechanics

  • Ship Building: Start with a small dinghy. Upgrade to bigger ships (Gold, Magic, Dragon-tier) for more cargo space and combat power.
  • Navigation: Use a Sextant + Watch to plot courses. Higher Sailing = more accurate navigation = faster travel.
  • Cargo & Trade: Buy cargo at one port, sail to another, sell for profit. Profit depends on distance, demand, and Sailing level.
  • Island Exploration: Discover new islands for XP and unlock fast-travel waypoints.
  • Naval Combat: Fight pirate ships and sea monsters for combat XP and loot.
  • Fishing at Sea: Special fishing spots only accessible by ship (higher-tier fish than mainland spots).

Best Islands: Levels 1-30 (Beginner Sailing)

At low Sailing levels, stick to short, safe routes near Misthalin and Karamja. Fallig back to port if your ship takes damage.

Recommended Islands & Routes (1-30)

Island/Route Sailing Req XP per Trip Notes
Lumbridge Swamp Docks → Draynor Port 1 50-100 XP Safest route. No combat.
Port Sarim → Karamja (Musa Point) 5 150-250 XP Short ocean crossing. Watch for low-level pirates.
Karamja → Crandor (shore) 15 300-500 XP DANGER: Elvarg nearby. Stay near shore.
Port Sarim → Pest Control island (outpost) 20 400-700 XP Unlocks Pest Control teleport later.
Brimhaven Docks → Ardougne Port 25 600-1,000 XP Medium route. Small chance of pirate ambush.
Karamja → Mos Le'Harmless 28 800-1,200 XP Unlocks access to Pirate questline.
💡 Beginner Tip: At level 1-10, train by ferrying passengers between Port Sarim and Musa Point. It's slow XP but zero risk and you learn the controls.

Best Early Cargo (Levels 1-30)

  • Bananas (Karamja → Port Sarim): Buy ~500 GP, sell ~700 GP. Low profit but zero risk.
  • Jug of wine (Port Sarim → Brimhaven): Buy ~200 GP, sell ~450 GP. Small profit.
  • Steel nails (Port Sarim → Ardougne): Buy ~400 GP, sell ~700 GP. Decent early profit.

Ship Upgrades at 40-60 Sailing

  • Oak Ship (40 Sailing): +2 cargo slots. Faster speed.
  • Willow Ship (55 Sailing): +4 cargo slots. Can equip 1 naval cannon.

⚠️ Warning: At levels 30-60, pirate ambushes become common. Always carry food (sharks or lobsters) and a weapon. Consider bringing a Familiar (Bunyip for healing) if you have Summoning.

Cooking at Sea

You can cook fish on your ship's stove (unlocked at 40 Sailing). This gives Cooking XP while Sailing. At 80 Cooking + 80 Sailing, you can cook afk on your ship while doing trade runs — dual skill training!

💡 Dual Training: At 80+ Sailing and 80+ Cooking, you can load your ship with raw fish, sail long routes, and cook them all during the voyage. You get Sailing XP (from sailing) + Cooking XP (from cooking) simultaneously.

Treasure Map Locations & Loot Tables

Sailing adds a new Treasure Map system. Maps are found in cargo holds, from naval combat loot, or bought from port cartographers.

Treasure Map Sources

  • Cargo Loot Rolls: 1/50 chance when buying cargo at a port (higher Sailing = better odds).
  • Naval Combat Loot: 1/10 chance from defeating pirate ships.
  • Island Exploration: 1/5 chance when discovering a new island.
  • Cartographer NPC (Port Sarim): Buy maps for 5,000-50,000 GP (price scales with map rarity).

Treasure Map Locations (Where to Dig)

Map Type Sailing Req Dig Location (General) Rewards (GP range)
Beginner Map (common) 1 Near starting ports (Port Sarim, Musa Point) 1,000-10,000 GP + low-tier loot
Intermediate Map (uncommon) 30 Karamja, Ardougne, Catherby areas 10,000-50,000 GP + medium loot
Advanced Map (rare) 60 Zeah, Morytania, Tirannwn coasts 50,000-200,000 GP + high-tier loot
Legendary Map (very rare) 80 Eastern Islands, Dragon Isle, hidden islands 200,000-1,000,000+ GP + unique rewards

Unique Treasure Map Rewards (Predicted)

  • Ship Cosmetics: Figureheads, sails, hull paints.
  • Sailing Pet: "Sailor" pet (baby sailor) from Legendary maps.
  • XP Lamps: 2,000-10,000 XP in any skill (from maps).
  • Rare Cargo: Unsellable unique cargo that gives huge XP when delivered.

Phase 1 Tips & Efficient Training Routes

Tip 1: Quests Give Free Sailing XP

Several quests reward Sailing XP. Prioritize these:

  • Pirate's Treasure (req none): 2,000 Sailing XP + access to Mos Le'Harmless.
  • Cargo of the Kharidian (req 20 Sailing): 5,000 Sailing XP + unlock new trade routes.
  • Eastern Expedition (req 50 Sailing): 15,000 Sailing XP + access to Eastern Islands.
  • Sojourn to the Sunken Temple (req 65 Sailing): 25,000 Sailing XP + new altar access.

Tip 2: Use the Optimal Ship for Your Level

Upgrade your ship as soon as you meet the Sailing req. A better ship means more cargo = more profit per trip = more XP per hour.

Tip 3: Combine Sailing with Other Skills

Sailing is one of the best skills for multi-tasking:

  • Sailing + Cooking: Cook raw fish on your ship stove during long voyages.
  • Sailing + Fishing: Stop at deep-sea spots during your route.
  • Sailing + Agility: Some ports have Agility shortcuts nearby — train both in the same trip.
  • Sailing + Prayer: Sunken Temple Island has a boosted Prayer altar. Sail there, bury bones, get +2x XP.

Tip 4: Join a Sailing Clan or Discord

Sailing has group content (naval battles, group trade runs). A clan makes this much easier. Search "OSRS Sailing" on Discord to find active communities.

Tip 5: Daily Sailing Contracts (When Added)

Like Farming and Hunter contracts, Sailing may get a contract system. Check with Boss Killers NPCs at ports for daily Sailing contracts — they give bonus XP and loot.
