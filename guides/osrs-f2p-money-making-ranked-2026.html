③ 🔍 Detailed Strategy for Top 10 Methods

3.1 🔥 Ogress Warriors — Complete Strategy

Ogress Warriors are the single best F2P money maker. Here's the exact strategy:

  • Location: Corsair Cove (talk to Cabin Boy at Cove bar to enter). Requirement: Dragon Slayer partial completion (get the map piece from the Cove).
  • Gear: Iron scimitar (level 1-20) → Rune scimitar (level 40+) → Dragon scimitar (level 60+). Bring lobsters (20+ per trip) and a teleport tab.
  • Inventory: 5 lobsters, 1 teleport tab, rest empty (pick up drops). When full, run to the deposit box (10 seconds away) and deposit alchables.
  • Alchables to Watch For: Rune full helm (8K GP), rune platebody (32K GP), adamantite longsword (4K GP), rune longsword (12K GP).
  • High Alchemy Combo: While running to the deposit box, cast High Alchemy on 2-3 alchables. This adds 50K-100K GP/hour on top of the base 120K.

3.2 🎣 Swordfish Fishing — Complete Strategy

  • Location: Karamja dock (south of the volcano). Use the "stiles" (pay 30 GP to deposit fish directly into the bank).
  • Requirements: 50 Fishing (for swordfish), 53 Cooking (to cook without burning — optional but doubles profit if you cook + sell swordfish).
  • Strategy: Fish swordfish → pay stiles to deposit → repeat. AFK for 10-15 minutes at a time. Swordfish sell for ~350 GP each on the GE.
  • Profit Math: 400-400 swordfish per hour × 350 GP = 50K-75K GP base. If you cook them (53 Cooking), swordfish sell for ~500 GP = 100K-150K GP/hour.

3.3 🌿 Nature Runecrafting — Complete Strategy

  • Route: Karamja General Store (buy earth talismans or use Abyss) → nature altar (short walk). Each inventory: 28 nature runes × ~100 GP = 2,800 GP per run. 30-40 runs per hour = 100K-150K GP/hour.
  • At 54 Runecrafting: You craft 2 nature runes per essence. Profit doubles to 1.8M+ GP/hour (but the click intensity also doubles).
  • Tip: Use the Abyss (members only) for faster runs. In F2P, you must walk from Karamja General Store to the nature altar (1-2 minutes per run).

3.4 🪓 Yew Log Cutting — Complete Strategy

  • Location: Edgeville (3 yew trees south of the bank) OR Corsair Cove (3 yew trees behind the gate, requires Dragon Slayer).
  • Competition: Yew trees in Edgeville are heavily contested. Hop to world 380+ (less populated) or use Corsair Cove yews (quieter).
  • Profit Math: 400-400 yew logs per hour × ~400 GP = 60K-90K GP/hour. Add 10-15 minutes of chopping per "bank run" (yew logs stack to 28, so you bank frequently).

3.5 🐄 Cowhide Collecting — New Player Friendly

  • Location: Lumbridge cow field (north of the castle).
  • Method: Kill cows → collect cowhides (sell for ~100 GP each on GE) → bank at Lumbridge (30 seconds away).
  • Profit: 400-400 cowhides per hour = 20K-40K GP. It's slow, but it's new-player-friendly (no stats required, safe zone, AFK).
  • Upgrade Path: Use cowhide profit to buy rune scimitar (32K GP), then move to Hill Giants (20K-40K GP/hour).

④ ✨ High Alchemy — The F2P Profit Multiplier

High Alchemy is the single most important money making skill for F2P players. It lets you add 50K-150K GP/hour to ANY method — for free.

4.1 📊 How High Alchemy Works

High Alchemy converts an item into GP at 60% of its Grand Exchange value. If an item sells for 1,000 GP on the GE, High Alchemy gives you 600 GP. The profit comes from buying items below 60% GE value (or getting them as drops).

ItemGE ValueHigh Alchemy ValueProfit (if free)
Rune full helm~8,000 GP~4,800 GP+4,800 GP (if dropped)
Rune platebody~32,000 GP~19,400 GP+19,400 GP (if dropped)
Adamantite longsword~4,000 GP~2,400 GP+2,400 GP (if dropped)

The Ogress Warrior + High Alchemy Combo: When you kill Ogress Warriors, they drop alchable items. Instead of running to the bank to sell them, cast High Alchemy on them immediately. You get 60% of the GE value instantly — no bank run needed. This adds 50K-100K GP/hour to your Ogress Warrior profit.

4.2 💰 Buying Items to High Alchemy (The "Flip")

If you have GP to invest, you can buy items below 60% GE value and High Alchemy them for profit:

  • Buy: Magic longbows (often sold below alch value during double XP weekends)
  • Alch: Each longbow gives ~1,000 GP profit per alchemy
  • Rate: 120 alchemies per hour (1.8 seconds per cast) = 120K GP/hour profit
  • Risk: GE prices fluctuate. Never buy large quantities without checking the current alch profit margin.
💡 High Alchemy While Running — The Micromanagement Trick

You don't need to stop skilling to cast High Alchemy. While running between Ogress Warrior spawns (3-5 seconds), open your spellbook and cast alchemy on 1-2 items. By the time you reach the next Ogress, the alchemy is done. This "micromanagement" adds 50K-100K GP/hour without slowing down your base method. ⑥ ❓ FAQ — F2P Money Making GP/Hour

Q: What's the absolute fastest way to 5.2M GP in F2P?

A: Ogress Warriors + High Alchemy. It's 150K-1.8M GP/hour, which means 30-50 hours to a bond. If you're an Ironman (can't use the GE), your best method is Ogress Warriors for alchables + using them for your own High Alchemy training (saves buying runes).

Q: Is F2P money making worth it, or should I just buy a bond?

A: If you can afford £5-£8 — just buy the bond. F2P money making is slow (80K-1.8M GP/hour vs. 1.8M-2M GP/hour in members). The 30-50 hours you spend grinding in F2P could be spent enjoying members content. That said, some players enjoy the F2P grind — it's slower, simpler, and has its own community. If you enjoy F2P, keep doing it.

Q: Why are my GP/hour numbers lower than the guide says?

A: Three reasons: (1) Your combat stats are too low (if killing Ogress Warriors with 40 Combat, your DPS is half of 60 Combat), (2) You're not using High Alchemy, (3) You're competing with other players for resources (e.g., yew trees in popular worlds). Upgrade your stats, use Alchemy, and hop worlds.

Q: Can I make money in F2P without any combat stats?

A: Yes — Cowhide collecting (20K-40K GP/hour), Swordfish fishing (if you have 50 Fishing from a members trial), and Rune essence mining (15K-30K GP/hour). But the profit is low. If you have 0 GP and 0 stats, spend your first 2 hours killing cows for cowhides — that gets you 40K-80K GP, enough to buy rune scimitar and start combat training.

Q: What's the best AFK F2P money maker?

A: Swordfish fishing (50 Fishing required). It's 80K-120K GP/hour and you can AFK for 10-15 minutes at a time. Yew log cutting (60 Woodcutting) is also AFK but more competitive. If you want true AFK (walk away for 20 minutes), fishing is your best bet.

Q: How do I know if a GE price is accurate?

A: Use the RuneLite GE Prices plugin (members only) or check prices.runescape.wiki for current prices. GE prices change daily based on supply and demand. Always check the current price before starting a money making method — what was profitable yesterday might not be today.

Q: Is the Wilderness worth the risk for F2P money making?

A: For most players, no. The best Wilderness method (Runite mining, 80K-150K GP/hour) has a high PK risk. You'll lose more GP to PKers than you earn from mining. Unless you're an experienced PK-er yourself (can fight back), stay in safe zones (Corsair Cove, Karamja, Edgeville). For more on Wilderness money making (and how to survive PKers), see our Wilderness survival guide.

Q: Can I use these methods on an Ironman account?

A: Yes — but Ironman accounts can't use the GE, so you can't sell items for GP. Instead, use these methods to gather resources for your own use. Ogress Warriors drop alchables — use them for your own High Alchemy training (saves runes). Swordfish fishing feeds your own Slayer training. The methods are the same, but the "profit" is in resources, not GP.

⑦ 💡 Final Tips for F2P Money Making

F2P money making is all about consistency over intensity. You're not going to earn 5M GP in one hour — but you can earn 5M GP in 30 days of casual play.

  • High Alchemy is mandatory. If you're F2P and not using High Alchemy, you're leaving 50K-150K GP/hour on the table. Train Magic to 55 (takes 2-4 hours of combat casting) and start alchemizing.
  • Don't compete for resources. If the Edgeville yew trees are taken, hop to world 380+. If the Corsair Cove Ogress are taken, wait 2 minutes or hop worlds. Competing is a waste of time.
  • Combine methods. Do Swordfish fishing while your Magic is training via High Alchemy. Multi-tasking = more GP per hour.
  • Track your GP/hour. After each session, check: how much GP did I earn? If it's below 80K/hour — something's wrong. Check your stats, your gear, your method.
  • Upgrade as you go. Use your first 1.8M GP to buy Dragon scimitar (120K GP) — that upgrades your Ogress Warrior DPS by 30%. Use your next 1.8M to buy Amulet of Power (+10% melee damage). Small upgrades = big GP/hour improvements.
  • Don't burn out. F2P money making is slower than members. If you find yourself dreading the grind — go members. The 5.2M GP you spend on a bond unlocks methods that earn 1.8M-2M GP/hour. The bond pays for itself in 3-10 hours of members play.
🎯 The F2P Money Making Mindset

F2P OSRS has a different pace. You're not here for the fastest GP/hour in the game — you're here to learn the game, build your bank, and enjoy the slower pace. If you embrace that, F2P money making is actually fun. If you're rushing to get to members as fast as possible — you're missing the point. Enjoy the cows, the swordfish, and the occasional Ogress Warrior alchable drop. The bond will come.
