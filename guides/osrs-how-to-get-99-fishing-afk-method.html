OSRS How to Get 99 Fishing AFK Method 2026

⏱️ 30-Second Quick Summary

Fishing is one of OSRS's best AFK skills. Set up at the right spot and level passively:

  • Levels 1–58: Shrimp → Trout/Salmon fly fishing at Barbarian Village
  • Levels 58–99: Barbarian Fishing — AFK, also trains Strength and Agility
  • Other AFK options: Infernal Eels (profit) and Karambwans (food + money)
  • Time to 99: ~150–200 hours of relaxed training
Skill Training Fishing AFK Updated 2026

Fishing is one of OSRS's most beloved AFK skills — you can leave your character fishing for minutes at a time while watching TV, studying, or doing other tasks. This guide covers the best AFK fishing methods from level 1 to 99, with a focus on Barbarian Fishing as the ultimate AFK training spot, plus comparisons with Infernal Eels and Karambwans.

Why AFK Fishing?

Fishing offers some of the highest AFK potential in OSRS. Unlike skills such as Agility or Runecrafting that require constant clicks, Fishing allows you to set up at a spot and only interact every 20–90 seconds depending on the method. This makes it ideal for players who want to level a skill passively alongside other activities.

  • Barbarian Fishing simultaneously trains Fishing, Strength, and Agility.
  • Most AFK fishing methods require no banking — fish are dropped or cooked on the spot.
  • Completion of certain quests unlocks faster methods earlier.
  • Fishing cape (level 99) perk: unlimited teleports to Otto's Grotto.

Early Levels: 1–58 Fast Progression

The early levels of Fishing are fast regardless of method. Here is the recommended progression before unlocking Barbarian Fishing:

  1. Level 1–20: Shrimps & Anchovies — Fish at Draynor Village or Lumbridge swamp. Very low XP but quick to level through.
  2. Level 20–40: Trout & Salmon (Fly Fishing) — Barbarian Village river. Fly fishing is click-light and surprisingly fast XP for the level range.
  3. Level 40–58: Lobsters or Fly Fishing — Continue fly fishing for speed, or switch to lobsters for Members money. Fly fishing is still faster XP.
  4. Level 58+: Barbarian Fishing — Unlocked at level 58 with completion of Barbarian Training. This becomes your main method for the rest of 99.

Barbarian Fishing — Best AFK Method

Barbarian Fishing at Otto's Grotto is widely considered the best overall AFK Fishing training method in OSRS. You catch Leaping Trout, Leaping Salmon, and Leaping Sturgeon, which grant Fishing XP and also award Strength and Agility XP with every catch. Fish are simply dropped (or you can cook them for Cooking XP).

Barbarian Fishing Method Summary

  • Location: Otto's Grotto, near Baxtorian Falls
  • Requirements: Level 58 Fishing, partial Barbarian Training completed
  • Fishing XP/hr: 50,000 – 110,000 (scales with level)
  • Strength XP/hr: ~14,000 (bonus)
  • Agility XP/hr: ~7,000 (bonus)
  • AFK window: 20–60 seconds between interactions
  • Cost: Free (drop fish or cook them)
  • Recommended gear: Dragon harpoon (boosts +3 levels)

How to Set Up Barbarian Fishing

  1. Complete the Barbarian Training miniquest with Otto Godblessed (start in his grotto south of Baxtorian Falls).
  2. Bring a barbarian rod and bait (feathers or fishing bait work).
  3. Fish the grotto lake — there are multiple fishing spots right next to each other.
  4. Drop all fish or use Shift+Click to mass drop quickly.
  5. Set up an auto-drop script or use the drop-all method for maximum AFK time.

Infernal Eels — Best Semi-AFK for Profit

Infernal Eels are caught in the TzHaar area after completing the Karamja Diary (Elite recommended). They can be cracked open for Tokkul, onyx bolt tips, and lava scale shards, making them a modest money-maker while being very AFK-friendly.

Infernal Eels Method Summary

  • Location: Mor Ul Rek (Inferno City), requires Fire Cape or Tokkul
  • Requirements: Level 80 Fishing, Karamja Diary recommended
  • XP/hr: ~26,000 – 35,000
  • Profit/hr: ~100,000 – 200,000 GP
  • AFK window: 60–90 seconds (extremely AFK)
  • Best for: Players who want money alongside low-effort Fishing XP

Karambwans — Fast Click-Intensive Option

Karambwans are caught at the Karambwan fishing spots on Karamja and are one of the fastest Fishing XP methods when done with 1-tick fishing. However, this method is NOT AFK — it requires clicking every game tick (0.6 seconds). It is included here for comparison but is not recommended for AFK training.

Karambwans Method Summary

  • Location: North-east Karamja coast
  • Requirements: Level 65 Fishing, Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest, Raw karambwanji bait
  • XP/hr (1-tick): ~100,000+
  • AFK window: 0 seconds (requires constant clicking)
  • Profit: Good (karambwans sell well)
  • Best for: Speed runners who want 99 fast regardless of effort

Tips for AFK Fishing to 99

  • Always use the best rod/harpoon available — Dragon harpoon gives a passive +3 Fishing boost.
  • Crystal harpoon (from Gauntlet) is the best in slot for harpoon fishing methods.
  • Use the Fishing Guild (level 68 required) for a passive +7 Fishing boost, making Barbarian Fishing even faster.
  • Barbarian Fishing is one of the best Agility/Strength training methods for Ironmen who need those stats passively.
  • At higher Agility levels, your run energy depletes slower, which slightly improves Barbarian Fishing efficiency.
  • The Rada's Blessing increases the frequency of double fish catches and is free from the Kourend & Kebos diary.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How long does 1-99 Fishing take using AFK methods?

Using Barbarian Fishing (58-99), expect ~150-200 hours of relaxed training at 30K-50K XP/hr. For faster methods, 1-tick Karambwans can reach 100K+ XP/hr but require active play. Infernal Eels give ~26K-35K XP/hr but are highly profitable.

❓ What is the best AFK Fishing method?

Barbarian Fishing at Otto's Grotto (level 58+) is the best AFK method. It trains Fishing, Strength, and Agility simultaneously. Fish are dropped automatically, requiring no banking. You only interact every 30-90 seconds.

❓ Can I make money while training Fishing to 99?

Yes! Infernal Eels (level 80+) give ~150K GP/hr while training. Karambwans (level 65+) can be profitable. Anglerfish (level 82+) give decent profit at high levels. Barbarian Fishing is free but makes no money (fish are dropped).

❓ What's the fastest Fishing XP method?

1-tick Karambwan cooking method (level 65+) gives ~100K+ XP/hr but is very click-intensive and not AFK at all. For the best balance of speed and AFK, Barbarian Fishing is recommended.

❓ What Fishing requirements do I need for Barbarian Fishing?

You need level 58 Fishing and completion of the Barbarian Training quest (started by speaking to Otto Godblessed at Otto's Grotto). The quest takes about 30 minutes and unlocks the ability to catch leaping fish.

❓ What gear should I use for Fishing?

Dragon harpoon gives a passive +3 Fishing boost. Crystal harpoon (from Gauntlet) is best-in-slot. At 68 Fishing, use the Fishing Guild for a passive +7 boost. Rada's Blessing increases double fish catch rates.
