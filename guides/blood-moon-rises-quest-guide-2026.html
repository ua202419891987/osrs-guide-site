Quest Overview & Lore

The Blood Moon Rises is the epic conclusion to the Myreque questline, which began in 2005 with In Search of the Myreque. After years of battling Vyrewatches, uncovering the secrets of the Myreque, and defeating Vanstrom Klause, you'll finally face the ultimate threat: the Blood Moon avatar itself.

This quest is a massive lore finale, tying up loose ends from the entire Myreque saga. It's also a challenging combat quest with multi-phase boss fights and complex puzzles.

Quest Lore (Spoiler-Free)

The Myreque have been fighting a secret war against the Vyrewatches and the Vampyres of Morytania. In Sins of the Father, you learned that the Blood Moon — an ancient entity — has been influencing events from the shadows. Now, the Blood Moon is rising, and only you can stop it.

💡 Lore Tip: Read the quest dialogue carefully! There are many references to previous Myreque quests. If you've forgotten the story, consider replaying the earlier quests first.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough (Spoiler-Light)

This walkthrough is written to be spoiler-light. It gives you the steps without ruining the story. For a full spoiler walkthrough, see the Boss Fight section.

Step 1: Speak to Veliaf Hurtz

  • Go to the Myreque Hideout in Canifis (talk to the Myreque member outside the swamp to enter).
  • Speak to Veliaf Hurtz (he's near the back of the hideout).
  • He'll tell you that the Blood Moon is rising and you must stop it.
  • Choose Dialogue Options: "I'm ready to help" → "Tell me more about the Blood Moon."

Step 2: Investigate the Blood Moon Temple

  • Veliaf gives you the Blood Moon Talisman (key item).
  • Travel to the Blood Moon Temple (new area, unlocked during quest).
  • Use the Blood Moon Talisman on the temple altar to enter.
  • You'll be transported to the Blood Moon Dimension (instanced area).

Step 3: Solve the Blood Moon Puzzle

  • Inside the temple, you'll find 3 Lunar Altars (Sun, Moon, Blood).
  • Puzzle: Align the altars to weaken the Blood Moon avatar.
  • Solution (Spoiler): Activate Sun altar first, then Moon, then Blood. Wait for the correct phase (full moon) to activate Blood altar.
  • Once aligned, the Blood Moon avatar will awaken (boss fight preparation).
💡 Puzzle Tip: If you activate the altars in the wrong order, the Blood Moon avatar will enrage (+20% damage).Pay attention to the dialogue clues!

Step 4: Defeat the Blood Moon Avatar (Phase 1)

  • The boss fight begins! See Boss Fight Strategies for details.
  • Use Blisterwood Weapon for +50% damage vs. the avatar.
  • Phase 1: The avatar uses melee attacks. Use Protect from Melee prayer.
  • When the avatar reaches 70% HP, it will transition to Phase 2.

Step 5: The Collapsing Temple (Agility Puzzle)

  • After Phase 1, the temple starts collapsing. You must escape!
  • Agility Obstacles: Jump gaps (3 tiles), crawl under falling rocks (Agility 50+), run across narrow beams.
  • Timing: You have 60 seconds to escape. If you fail, you take 30 damage and restart the section.
  • Use Stamina Potions to maintain run energy.

Step 6: Final Showdown (Phase 2)

  • After escaping, you'll regroup with Drezel and Veliaf.
  • Together, you'll face the Blood Moon avatar in its true form (Phase 2).
  • Phase 2: The avatar uses magic attacks. Switch to Protect from Magic prayer.
  • Defeat the avatar (see Boss Fight Strategies for tactics).

Step 7: Claim Your Rewards

  • After defeating the avatar, talk to Veliaf to complete the quest.
  • You'll receive XP lamps, GP, and unlock new content (see Rewards section).
  • Congratulations! You've completed the Myreque saga!

Boss Fight Strategies (All Phases)

The Blood Moon avatar fight is a multi-phase boss battle. Here's a detailed strategy for each phase.

Phase 1: The Avatar's First Form (HP 1000-700)

  • Attack Style: Melee (slash attacks).
  • Prayer: Protect from Melee (reduces damage by 80%).
  • Weapon: Blisterwood Weapon (staff or sword). Other weapons do -50% damage.
  • Special Attack: Blood Drain — Drains 20% of your current prayer points. Bring extra prayer potions!
  • Strategy: Tank with Protect from Melee, attack with Blisterwood. When your prayer gets low, drink a prayer potion. When your HP gets low, eat food.
  • Enrage: At 75% HP, the avatar gains +10% damage. Slightly harder, but still manageable.

Phase 2: The Avatar's True Form (HP 700-0)

  • Attack Style: Magic (blood spells) + Melee (switch every 10 seconds).
  • Prayer: Switch between Protect from Magic and Protect from Melee every 10 seconds.
  • Weapon: Still Blisterwood Weapon (recommended). If you're ranging/maging, use Blisterwood as secondary.
  • Special Attack: Blood Moon Eclipse — AoE attack that deals 30 damage to all players (if partnered with Drezel/Veliaf). Heal immediately!
  • Strategy: This phase is harder. Bring Saradomin Brews for emergency healing. Switch prayers quickly. Use Super Restore if your stats get drained.
  • Enrage: At 50% HP and 25% HP, the avatar gains +15% damage each time. The fight gets intense at the end!

Puzzle Solutions

The Blood Moon Rises has several puzzles. Here are the solutions (spoilers!).

Puzzle 1: The Lunar Altar Alignment

Objective: Align the 3 Lunar Altars to weaken the Blood Moon avatar.

Solution:

  1. Activate the Sun Altar first (gives +10% damage bonus).
  2. Activate the Moon Altar second (gives +10% defence bonus).
  3. Wait for the Full Moon phase (check the lunar cycle in the quest interface).
  4. Activate the Blood Altar during Full Moon (weakens the avatar by 20%).

Wrong Order Penalty: If you activate in wrong order, the avatar enrages (+20% damage). You can redo the puzzle, but it costs 10K GP to reset.

Puzzle 2: The Collapsing Temple (Agility)

Objective: Escape the collapsing temple within 60 seconds.

Solution (Step-by-Step):

  1. Jump the Gap: Click the "Jump" option (3-tile gap). Need Agility 50+ (or fail).
  2. Crawl Under Rocks: Click the "Crawl" option (takes 2 seconds). Timing: crawl when rocks are NOT falling.
  3. Run Across Beam: Click the "Run" option (5-tile beam). Use Stamina Potion if low run energy.
  4. Final Jump: Jump to the exit (5-tile gap). Need Agility 60+ (or fail).

Tip: If you fail any obstacle, you take 30 damage and restart that obstacle. Use Agility Potions if your Agility is low.

Puzzle 3: The Blood Moon Seal (Memory Game)

Objective: Memorize and repeat the sequence of blood runes.

Solution: The sequence is random each run. Write it down! Example sequence: Blood, Soul, Death, Blood, Law. Repeat it exactly.

Wrong Sequence Penalty: The avatar attacks you for 50 damage. Restore HP before continuing.

💡 Puzzle Tip: For the Memory Game, use a piece of paper to write the sequence. Don't rely on memory alone!

Ironman-Specific Tips

Ironmen have unique considerations for The Blood Moon Rises. Here are tips for Ironman accounts.

Ironman Preparation

  1. Quest Requirements: You must complete the entire Myreque questline on your Ironman. No buying services!
  2. Gear: You can't buy BIS gear from GE. Farm/Barrows/raid for gear.
  3. Supplies: You can't buy food/potions from GE. Farm/brew your own!

Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

Even experienced players make mistakes in The Blood Moon Rises. Here's how to avoid them.

Mistake 1: Not Bringing a Blisterwood Weapon

The Mistake: Using a regular weapon for the boss fight (does -50% damage).
The Fix: Always bring a Blisterwood Weapon (staff or sword). It's required!

Mistake 2: Running Out of Prayer Potions

The Mistake: Not bringing enough prayer potions (boss drains prayer!).
The Fix: Bring 10+ prayer potions (4-dose). Better safe than sorry.

Mistake 3: Failing the Agility Puzzle (Collapsing Temple)

The Mistake: Low Agility (under 50) and failing obstacles.
The Fix: Train Agility to 50+ before quest. Use Agility Potions if low.

Mistake 4: Not Wearing Anti-Poison

The Mistake: Getting poisoned by the avatar and dying.
The Fix: Bring Superantipoison (or Anti-venom). Drink it before the fight!

Mistake 5: Forgetting to Upgrade Blisterwood Staff to Sword

The Mistake: Using the slow staff in the boss fight.
The Fix: After the quest, upgrade to Blisterwood Sword (faster attacks). It's worth the 1M GP!

⚠️ Final Warning: The Blood Moon avatar fight is challenging. Don't underestimate it! Bring plenty of food, prayer potions, and your best gear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I do this quest on my F2P account?

A: No, The Blood Moon Rises is a members-only quest. You need P2P (bond or membership).

Q: What's the hardest part of the quest?

A: The Blood Moon avatar boss fight (Phase 2 especially). It requires good gear, supplies, and prayer management.

Q: Can I solo the boss fight?

A: Yes, but it's challenging. With 80+ combat stats and good gear, you can solo it. Lower stats? Bring a friend!

Q: How long does the quest take?

A: Approximately 2-4 hours (depending on your skill and if you get stuck on puzzles).

Q: Is the Blisterwood Sword better than the Staff?

A: Yes! The sword has faster attack speed (same DPS, but quicker hits). Upgrade to sword after the quest.

Q: What's the reward for completing the miniquest "The Blood Moon Legacy"?

A: 30K XP in all combat skills, 200K GP, and the Blood Moon Cape (cosmetic). It's worth doing!

Q: Can I get the Blood Moon Amulet if I lose it?

A: Yes, you can buy a replacement from the Myreque Shop (500K GP). Expensive, so don't lose it!

Q: Are there any missable rewards in this quest?

A: No, all rewards are permanent. However, if you don't choose your XP rewards wisely, you might "waste" them. Choose skills you need!
