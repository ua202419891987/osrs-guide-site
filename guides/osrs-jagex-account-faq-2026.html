🔐 Jagex Account Migration FAQ — Everything You Need to Know Before Migrating

Jagex is making a full transition to Jagex Accounts later in 2026. If you're an OSRS player, you'll need to migrate your existing character(s) to a Jagex Account to continue playing. This FAQ covers everything — from RuneLite compatibility and Nvidia plugin support to managing multiple characters and whether migration is reversible. All answers are verified against official Jagex sources and the OSRS Wiki.

📅 Published: June 28, 2026 — Account Guide · 12 min read

⏱️ Quick Summary — 30-Second Read

  • RuneLite fully compatible — all plugins, HDOS, and GPU rendering work fine
  • Manage up to 20 characters under one Jagex Account — switch with one click in launcher
  • Migration is irreversible — characters, stats, items all carry over intact

⚡ Quick Verdict

Jagex Accounts are mandatory for continued play — the old OSRS launcher and login system will be phased out by the end of 2026. Migration is free, irreversible, and preserves all your progress. You can still use RuneLite (launched through the Jagex Launcher), and all your existing characters, items, membership, and stats carry over. The biggest wins: enhanced security with 2FA, one-click character switching, and a unified login for both OSRS and RS3. The migration takes about 5 minutes — do it early to avoid the last-minute rush.

① 🔑 What Is a Jagex Account?

A Jagex Account is a unified account system that replaces the old character-specific login (username/email + password). It's similar to how other game platforms work (like Steam, Blizzard Battle.net, or Riot Games accounts).

1.1 🎯 Key Features

  • Single login for all your Jagex games (OSRS, RS3, and future titles)
  • Character management — all your characters under one roof
  • Enhanced security — mandatory 2FA (two-factor authentication) support
  • Jagex Launcher — the new gateway to launch OSRS and RS3
  • Account recovery — streamlined process through the Jagex Account system

1.2 🗓️ Timeline

DateEventImpact on Players
June 12, 2026Full Transition announcementOfficial notice that old accounts will be phased out
Mid-2026Gradual migration periodPlayers encouraged to migrate voluntarily
Late 2026Full transition to Jagex AccountsOld login methods disabled; Jagex Accounts mandatory
💡 Do I Have to Migrate?

Yes. Jagex has confirmed that old-style accounts (username/email + password login) will be phased out entirely. By late 2026, you must have a Jagex Account to play OSRS or RS3. There is no opt-out. The sooner you migrate, the sooner you benefit from improved security and convenience.

② 📋 Migration Basics — The Essentials

2.1 ✅ What Carries Over

  • All characters — every OSRS and RS3 character on your email address
  • All progress — levels, quest points, items, GP, stats, achievements
  • Membership — all active membership days transfer to the new account
  • Character names — completely unchanged
  • Friends lists, ignore lists, settings — every account preference carries over

2.2 ❌ What Changes

  • Login method — old username/email login replaced by Jagex Account credentials
  • Launcher — old OSRS launcher replaced by Jagex Launcher
  • Account recovery — old recovery questions/process replaced by Jagex Account recovery
  • Third-party logins — Steam/Apple/Google login still works but routes through Jagex Account

2.3 ⏱️ How to Migrate (5-Minute Process)

📋 Migration Steps

  1. Visit jagex.com and click "Create Jagex Account"
  2. Enter your email — use the same email associated with your OSRS/RS3 characters
  3. Verify your email — check your inbox and click the verification link
  4. Set up 2FA — recommend using an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  5. Import your characters — the system will detect all characters linked to your email
  6. Download Jagex Launcher — install from jagex.com/launcher
  7. Log in — use your new Jagex Account credentials and launch OSRS or RS3
⚠️ Important: Use the Correct Email

If your OSRS character was created with a specific email address, you must create your Jagex Account with that same email. If you use a different email, the system won't detect your existing characters. If you've changed emails since creating your OSRS account, check the OSRS account management page first to confirm which email is linked.

③ 🎮 RuneLite Compatibility After Migration

This is the #1 concern for most OSRS players. Here's the definitive answer:

3.1 ✅ Yes — RuneLite Works with Jagex Accounts

RuneLite is fully compatible with Jagex Accounts. However, there's a change in how you launch it:

  • Old method: Run RuneLite directly from the RuneLite launcher
  • New method: Launch RuneLite through the Jagex Launcher

3.2 🔧 How to Launch RuneLite After Migration

📋 Step-by-Step

  1. Open Jagex Launcher (download from jagex.com/launcher if you haven't already)
  2. Log in with your Jagex Account email and password
  3. Select your character from the character list (if you have multiple)
  4. Click the dropdown next to the "Play" button (default is the official OSRS client)
  5. Select "RuneLite" from the dropdown menu
  6. Click "Play" — RuneLite will launch automatically

3.3 📦 All Plugins Still Work

Every RuneLite plugin — including third-party plugins, the Plugin Hub, and your custom configurations — works exactly as before. Your profiles, tile markers, and saved settings all carry over because RuneLite stores them locally, not on your account.

💡 Pro Tip: Set RuneLite as Default

In the Jagex Launcher settings, you can set RuneLite as your default client for OSRS. This way, when you click "Play" on a character, it automatically launches RuneLite instead of the official client. Go to Launcher Settings → Old School RuneScape → Default Client → Select "RuneLite."

4.3 🔍 What About HDOS?

HDOS (the high-definition OSRS client) is also supported through the Jagex Launcher. Simply select "HDOS" from the client dropdown instead of RuneLite or the official client, and it will launch automatically. HDOS is fully compatible with Jagex Accounts.

5.2 ➕ Can I Add Characters Later?

Yes. If you create a new OSRS character in the future, you can add it to your existing Jagex Account. The system supports up to 20 characters per Jagex Account (across OSRS and RS3). This is more than enough for even the most alt-heavy players.

5.3 🔗 Can I Merge Multiple Jagex Accounts?

No. Jagex Accounts cannot be merged. If you carelessly create two Jagex Accounts with different characters on each, there is no way to combine them. Plan carefully: import all your characters into a single Jagex Account during the initial migration.

💡 Alt Management Tip

The Jagex Launcher supports multiple windows. You can launch the same character on different worlds, or launch different characters simultaneously. This is a huge quality-of-life improvement for players who run multiple accounts (ironman + main, skiller + PvPer, etc.).

⑥ 🎨 Nvidia & Third-Party Plugin Compatibility

This is a nuanced topic. Here's what you need to know:

6.1 🖼️ Nvidia Freestyle / Game Filters

Status: Use at your own risk

  • Jagex has not officially banned Nvidia Freestyle or game filters
  • However, the Jagex Launcher includes enhanced security detection that may flag overlay software
  • If you use Nvidia filters for color enhancement or sharpness, test them after migration
  • Features that provide "competitive advantage" (e.g., night vision, player highlighting) are more likely to be flagged
  • Recommendation: Simple color/sharpness filters are probably fine. Advanced visibility-enhancing filters are risky.

6.2 🎤 Other Common Overlays & Plugins

⚠️ The Safe Approach

If you're unsure about a plugin or overlay, default to not using it for the first week after migration. Test it on a F2P account or alt first. Jagex's anti-cheat detection may evolve after the full transition to Jagex Accounts, so what works today might not work tomorrow. When in doubt, play without modifications — the game looks great on its own.

⑦ 🔙 Can You Reverse Migration?

Short answer: No. Migration is permanent and irreversible.

7.1 ❌ Why Can't You Reverse It?

Once you import a character into a Jagex Account, the old login credentials for that character are deactivated permanently. The character is now bound to your Jagex Account. This is by design:

  • Security: Reversing would require reactivating old, less secure login methods
  • Technical: The character data structure is fundamentally changed during migration
  • Policy: Jagex has explicitly stated migration is one-way

7.2 🚫 What You CANNOT Do After Migration

  • Go back to using the old OSRS launcher
  • Log in with your old username/email + password
  • Transfer a character from your Jagex Account to a different Jagex Account
  • Split characters across multiple Jagex Accounts

7.3 ✅ What You CAN Still Do

  • Change your Jagex Account email address
  • Change your Jagex Account password
  • Update your 2FA settings
  • Contact Jagex Support for account issues (via the Jagex Account system)
  • Remove a character from your Jagex Account... but only by deleting it (not reversible)
💡 Think Before You Migrate

Because migration is irreversible, make sure you're ready:

  • ✅ Can you access the email associated with your OSRS account?
  • ✅ Have you written down all your characters and their details?
  • ✅ Are you ready to use the Jagex Launcher going forward?
  • ✅ Have you set up 2FA on your email account (important for account recovery)?
If you answered yes to all four, you're ready to migrate.

⑧ 🛡️ Security & 2FA After Migration

One of the biggest benefits of Jagex Accounts is significantly improved account security.

8.1 🔐 What Changes

  • 2FA is mandatory — you set it up during migration (authenticator app recommended)
  • No more recovery questions — the old "recovery question" system is gone
  • Jagex Account recovery — streamlined process that verifies your identity via email + 2FA
  • Session management — view and revoke active sessions from your Jagex Account dashboard

8.2 📱 Recommended 2FA Setup

  • Best: Google Authenticator or Authy (app-based, no SMS interception risk)
  • Good: SMS-based 2FA (better than nothing, but SMS interception is possible)
  • Avoid: Using the same device for both game and 2FA (if your device is compromised, both are at risk)

8.3 📧 Old Account Recovery vs. Jagex Account Recovery

💡 Critical: Save Your 2FA Backup Codes

When you set up 2FA, Jagex provides backup codes (usually 8-10 one-time-use codes). Write these down and store them in a safe place (NOT on your computer). If you lose your authenticator device and don't have backup codes, recovering your Jagex Account can be extremely difficult. This is one area where you really don't want to learn the hard way.

⑨ ❓ Full FAQ — 20 Essential Questions Answered

Q1: Can I still use the old OSRS launcher after migrating?

A: No. Once you migrate your character to a Jagex Account, the old OSRS launcher (the one you downloaded from the OSRS website) will no longer work with that character. You must use the Jagex Launcher to access OSRS. The old launcher is being phased out entirely by late 2026.

Q2: Does RuneLite still work with a Jagex Account?

A: Yes, absolutely. RuneLite is fully compatible with Jagex Accounts. You just launch it through the Jagex Launcher instead of running it directly. All plugins, settings, and profiles work exactly as before. See Section 3 above for setup instructions.

Q3: Are Nvidia plugins (Freestyle, Game Filter) still compatible?

A: Use at your own risk. Jagex hasn't explicitly banned Nvidia Freestyle, but the Jagex Launcher's enhanced security detection may flag certain overlay features. Simple color/sharpness adjustments are likely fine. Avoid features that could provide a competitive advantage (night vision, player highlighting). Test cautiously before relying on any Nvidia plugin.

Q4: What happens to my multiple OSRS characters?

A: All characters linked to your email address are imported into your Jagex Account during migration. You can switch between them with one click in the Jagex Launcher. Up to 20 characters can be on a single Jagex Account. Membership remains per-character (unchanged).

Q5: Can I reverse migration after doing it?

A: No. Migration is permanent and irreversible. Once your character is imported into a Jagex Account, the old login credentials are deactivated. You cannot move a character back to the old system or to a different Jagex Account. Make sure you're ready before migrating.

Q6: Can I change my Jagex Account email after migrating?

A: Yes. You can change the email associated with your Jagex Account from the account management page on jagex.com. This does not affect your characters in any way. However, keep in mind that account recovery uses this email, so keep it accessible.

Q7: Do I lose my membership days when migrating?

A: No. All active membership days transfer to the new Jagex Account. Your membership duration is completely unaffected. You don't lose any time, and you don't need to re-subscribe.

Q8: What about my Steam-linked OSRS account?

A: You can still link your Steam account to your Jagex Account after migration. The process is: migrate to Jagex Account first, then in your Jagex Account settings, go to "Linked Accounts" and add Steam. This allows you to continue launching through Steam if you prefer, though it routes through the Jagex Account behind the scenes.

Q9: Can I use the same Jagex Account for OSRS and RS3?

A: Yes, that's the whole point! A Jagex Account unifies your OSRS and RS3 characters. You can see all your characters from both games in the Jagex Launcher and switch between them effortlessly. If you have an RS3 character but primarily play OSRS, you can safely ignore the RS3 side — it doesn't affect your OSRS gameplay.

Q10: What happens if I don't migrate by the deadline?

A: Jagex has stated that the old login system will be disabled entirely. If you haven't migrated by that point, you will lose access to your characters. However, Jagex will likely provide a grace period and recovery option. Our advice: don't wait. Migration takes 5 minutes. Do it now and avoid the risk of losing access.

Q11: Is the Jagex Launcher required, or can I use RuneLite directly?

A: The Jagex Launcher is required to authenticate. You cannot launch RuneLite directly with a Jagex Account — you must launch it through the Jagex Launcher. However, many players set RuneLite as their default client in the launcher, making the extra click trivial.

Q12: Can I play on multiple worlds with the same character?

A: No. The same character cannot be logged in on multiple worlds simultaneously (this was already true before migration). However, you can log in different characters from the same Jagex Account on different worlds at the same time using multiple launcher windows.

Q13: Will my RuneLite plugin settings transfer?

A: Yes. RuneLite plugin settings are stored locally on your computer, not on your account. As long as you're using the same computer (and the same RuneLite profile), all your settings, tile markers, and configurations will be there. If you switch computers, use RuneLite's built-in profile sync feature or manually copy your settings.

Q14: Can I still play F2P after migrating?

A: Yes. F2P characters work exactly the same way on a Jagex Account. You can have a mix of F2P and member characters on the same Jagex Account. Each character retains its own membership status.

Q15: What if I forget my Jagex Account password?

A: Use the "Forgot Password" option on the Jagex Launcher or jagex.com. You'll receive a password reset link via email. If you've also lost access to your email, recovery is more difficult but possible through Jagex Support — this is why keeping your email secure and accessible is critical.

Q16: Can I have a Jagex Account without migrating my characters?

A: You can create a Jagex Account without immediately importing characters. However, since the old system is being phased out, there's no benefit to delaying. Import your characters as soon as you create the Jagex Account.

Q17: Will the migration affect my position on hiscores?

A: No. Hiscores positions are tied to character data, not login method. Your stats, XP, and rankings are completely unchanged. You won't lose any progress or ranking due to migration.

Q18: Does the Jagex Launcher work on Linux?

A: The official Jagex Launcher supports Windows and macOS only. However, the OSRS Wiki and community have documented workarounds for Linux using RuneLite's native Linux support combined with the Jagex Account authentication. Check the RuneLite Discord or Wiki for current Linux instructions.

Q19: Can I use the Jagex Launcher on multiple computers?

A: Yes. You can install and log into the Jagex Launcher on as many computers as you want. Your characters are stored on Jagex's servers, not on your computer. Each session is independent — playing on one computer doesn't affect the other (other than the normal "already logged in" restriction for the same character).

Q20: Will migration affect my clan or friends list?

A: No. Your friends list, ignore list, clan membership, and all social features are tied to your character, not your login method. Everything carries over intact. Your friends won't even notice you've migrated unless you tell them.
