Where You Are: The Mid-Game Plateau

The mid-game plateau is real. You've got Barrows gloves, a fire cape, and base 70 combat stats. You can kill most slayer monsters comfortably, and your bank is probably sitting around 20-50M GP. But the gap between here and raids-ready feels enormous — and it is.

Most players get stuck here because they lack a clear priority system. They hop between goals, waste GP on side-grades, and spend hours doing activities that don't move the needle. This roadmap fixes that.

Starting Point Assumptions

  • ✅ Barrows Gloves (Recipe for Disaster complete)
  • ✅ Fire Cape obtained
  • ✅ Dragon Defender acquired
  • ✅ Base 70 Melee / Range / Magic
  • ✅ ~175 Quest Points
  • 💰 Bank: 20-50M GP

Phase 1: Unlock Everything (Weeks 1–2)

Before you grind money or skills, unlock the tools that make everything else faster. This phase is about quests, diaries, and transport.

Priority Quests

Quest Unlock Requirements
King's Ransom Piety prayer (23% boost) 65 Defence, Holy Grail
Desert Treasure I Ancient Magicks 53 Thieving, 50 Magic
Lunar Diplomacy Lunar spellbook, NPC Contact 65 Magic, 61 Crafting
Monkey Madness II Demonic Gorillas, Zenytes 69 Slayer, 60 Thieving
Song of the Elves Corrupted Gauntlet, Zalcano 70 in 8 skills

Diary Milestones (Easy-Hard)

Target these diary tiers in order of impact. Medium diaries give you the best ROI for mid-level accounts.

  • Ardougne Medium — Cape teleport to monastery (fairy ring + altar nearby)
  • Varrock Medium — 30 battlestaves daily (60K profit/day minimum)
  • Lumbridge Medium — 3 daily teleports to farming patch
  • Kourend Medium — Double favour gain, Xeric's talisman teleports
  • Morytania Hard — Double runes at Barrows, bonecrusher upgrade

Transport Network

Build your fast-travel system now. Every teleport you unlock saves hours over the next six months.

  • POH Portal Chamber — 50 Construction for Kharyrll/Canifis/Lunar Isle portals
  • Fairy Rings — Start Fairytale II (don't need to finish, just start for rings)
  • Spirit Trees — Tree Gnome Village + The Grand Tree
  • Jewellery Box — Mounted glory (47 Con), eventual ornate box (91 Con)

Phase 2: Build Your Bank (Weeks 3–6)

You don't need a tbow to do endgame content. But you do need about 150-200M GP for solid mid-high gear across all three combat styles. This phase builds that bank.

Money-Making Progression

Tier 1 (0-50M): Herb runs (5 runs/day = 1M+ profit), Barrows (500K-1M/hr), Slayer tasks (passive), Varrock diary battlestaves

Tier 2 (50-100M): Demonic Gorillas (2M+/hr with zenytes), Vorkath (2-4M/hr), Zulrah (1-2M/hr), Zalcano (1M/hr AFK)

Tier 3 (100-200M): Corrupted Gauntlet (4-5M/hr), Chambers of Xeric teams, Nightmare, flipping high-volume items

Tier 4 (200M+): Theatre of Blood, Tombs of Amascut experts, Nex masses

GP Not to Waste

  • Don't buy Bandos tassets until you have all zenyte jewellery — torture + anguish are better upgrades
  • Don't buy a DHCB for Vorkath before rigour — rigour is a bigger DPS boost for less GP
  • Don't train Prayer at the Chaos Altar unprotected — use a gilded altar or don't bother

Phase 3: Gear Up (Weeks 7–10)

Your gear upgrade order matters more than your total GP. Buy in this sequence for maximum DPS per GP spent.

Melee Upgrade Priority

  1. Dragon Defender (free, already have it) → Avernic (90M)
  2. Fighter Torso (free, Barbarian Assault) → Bandos Chestplate (30M)
  3. Obsidian Legs (1M) → Tassets (25M)
  4. Dragon Boots (200K) → Primordial Boots (30M)
  5. Torture Amulet (12M) — buy this before tassets
  6. Ferocious Gloves (8M, DS2 required)

Ranged Upgrade Priority

  1. Ava's Assembler (free, DS2)
  2. Anguish Necklace (15M) — biggest ranged upgrade per GP
  3. Dragon Crossbow (2M) → DHCB (80M, only if camping Vorkath)
  4. Blowpipe (3M + scales) — essential for Slayer and light bossing
  5. Armadyl Set (50M) → Masori (150M+)

Magic Upgrade Priority

  1. Trident of the Seas (50K) → Trident of the Swamp (3M)
  2. Occult Necklace (500K) — best magic DPS per GP in the game
  3. Ahrim's Set (4M) → Ancestral (300M+)
  4. Tormented Bracelet (12M)
  5. Imbued God Cape (free, Mage Arena II)

Phase 4: Master Bossing (Weeks 11–16)

This is where the game transforms. Bossing turns your GP into more GP and your stats into experience. Follow this ladder.

Tier Boss GP/hr What You Learn
Entry Barrows 500K-1M Inventory management, prayer switching basics
Entry Sarachnis 600K-1M Prayer flicking, movement mechanics
Mid Zulrah 1-2M Gear switching, rotation memorization
Mid Vorkath 2-4M Woox walk, acid phase, timing
High Corrupted Gauntlet 4-5M Prep efficiency, multi-phase bossing
High God Wars (Solo) 1-3M Sustain, kiting, kill count efficiency

Phase 5: Raid-Ready (Weeks 17–24)

Raids are the ultimate goal. You don't need max stats, but you need the right combination of stats, gear, and knowledge.

Minimum Stats for ToA Entry Mode

  • 80 Attack / 85 Strength / 80 Defence
  • 85 Ranged / 85 Magic
  • 70 Prayer (Piety + Rigour + Augury)
  • 78 Herblore (for brews if iron, otherwise buy them)

Minimum Stats for CoX Teams

  • 85 Attack / 90 Strength / 85 Defence
  • 90 Ranged / 90 Magic
  • 77 Prayer (Rigour + Augury unlocked)
  • 78 Herblore recommended
  • 55 Farming (for resource rooms)

Skills to Prioritize Before Raiding

  1. Prayer to 77 — Rigour and Augury are non-negotiable for efficient raids
  2. Herblore to 78 — Raid potions (overload-likes) massively boost DPS
  3. Construction to 83 — Ornate jewellery box + ornate pool = best POH hub
  4. Agility to 70+ — Run energy in long encounters, shortcuts in raids

FAQ

How long does mid → high take?

Playing 2-3 hours daily, expect 4-6 months from base 70s to raids-ready. Efficient players can do it in 2-3 months. Casual players: 8-12 months.

Should I train Slayer or NMZ for combat?

Slayer. Always Slayer. NMZ gives slightly faster XP, but Slayer gives you GP, unlocks boss tasks (Cerberus, Hydra, Thermy), and trains multiple skills simultaneously. The Slayer helm (i) is a 15% damage boost on-task — that's bigger than most gear upgrades.

When should I buy Rigour?

As soon as you have 15M GP and 74 Prayer. Rigour is the single biggest ranged DPS upgrade in the game — bigger than Armadyl armour, bigger than a DHCB. Buy it before any expensive ranged gear.

Construction costs too much. Is it worth it?

The first 50 Construction levels cost about 5M GP and give you portal chambers, mounted glory, and a basic altar. This is the best value GP you'll ever spend — the time saved on teleports alone pays for itself in weeks. Push to 83 Con (ornate pool + jewellery box) when you have 30M+ spare GP.

Do I really need 77 Prayer before raiding?

Yes. Rigour and Augury combined increase your DPS by roughly 20-25% in raids. Without them, you're effectively doing 25% less damage than everyone else on your team. Teams will check your prayers. Get 77 Prayer before your first raid.

⭐ Final Tips

  • Stick to the order. Players who jump to Phase 4 bossing before Phase 2 money-making burn out and quit.
  • Herb runs are not optional. 5 runs/day at 2M+/day profit = 60M/month. That's a zenyte set in 30 days, for 25 minutes of work per day.
  • Join a PvM clan. Learning raids alone is 10x harder. A good clan teaches you mechanics, loans you gear, and makes the grind fun.
  • Don't sell your bank for one upgrade. A DHCB with black d'hide is worse than a dragon crossbow with full Armadyl + rigour.
  • Bossing GP scales with experience, not gear. A player with 500 KC in budget gear makes more GP than a rich player with 5 KC in max gear.
