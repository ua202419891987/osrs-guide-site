OSRS Magic 1-99 Guide 2026 — Splashing, Alching & Bursting

Intermediate

Magic is the most versatile skill in OSRS — it trains combat, unlocks teleports, and can make GP via High Alchemy. This 2026 guide covers every tier from splashing to barraging, with verified XP/hr + GP/hr (OSRS Wiki, July 2026), a live cost calculator, quest XP boosts, and Common Mistakes.

⏱️ Quick Summary — 30-Second Read

  • 📌 Quests first: Witch's Potion, Witch's House, The Grand Tree, Desert Treasure (20k XP) and Lunar Diplomacy (5k) give free early levels.
  • 📌 High Alchemy (55+): 65 XP/cast, ~200k XP/hr, can break even or profit — the default training method.
  • 📌 Bursting/Barraging (70/94): 150k–305k XP/hr but costs −1.3M to −2.5M GP/hr (Ancient Magicks).
  • 📌 Splashing: cheapest AFK method, ~12k–40k XP/hr, near-zero cost — great for passive training.
  • 📌 Enchant bolts: fastest XP (90k–100k/hr) but very expensive (~7–10 GP/XP).

1. ✨ Overview & Why Train Magic in 2026

Magic is the only combat skill that also makes money (High Alchemy), unlocks the teleport network, and powers end-game PvM (Ancient Magicks, powered staves). Three reasons it's a top priority:

🎯 The Three Big Draws

  • Utility: Varrock (25), Falador (37), Camelot (45) and dozens more teleports save hundreds of hours of travel across your account.
  • Profit path: High Alchemy turns junk into GP while training — often break-even or better.
  • PvM power: Trident, Sanguinesti, Tumeken's Shadow, Ice Barrage and Arceuus thralls are gated behind Magic levels.
💡 The One Rule That Saves Millions

Decide your goal before you start. If you only need 55 for Alchemy or 75 for a Trident, stop there — the 70–99 burst/barrage stretch costs 100M+ GP. Train to your use case, not to 99 by reflex.

💡 Splashing Needs Negative Accuracy

To splash (always miss) you need a Magic attack bonus of −65 or lower. Wear full iron/d'hide with no Magic bonus and cast on a splashing dummy or caged monster. This is the cheapest way to train Magic while AFK.

8. ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Myth-Busting)

❌ Myth 1: "Train Magic to 99 by casting combat spells on monsters."

Slow and costly. Past level 43, High Alchemy and Superheat are faster and cheaper, and bursting/barraging on multi-targets dwarfs single-target casting.

❌ Myth 2: "Splashing trains for free."

Splashing is cheap, not free — you still burn runes (though very few with a staff of air). It is the budget AFK method, not a money maker.

❌ Myth 3: "You must barrage to 99."

Barraging costs −2.3M to −2.5M GP/hr. Unless you have billions, High Alchemy (break-even) plus Slayer-task bursting is the smarter 99.

❌ Myth 4: "Any armour works for splashing."

You need −65 Magic attack bonus or lower or you'll actually hit and gain less AFK time. Wear full iron/d'hide with zero Magic bonus.

❌ Myth 5: "High Alchemy always loses money."

Alching the right items (yew longbows, rune items, d'hide bodies bought below High Alch value) is frequently break-even or profitable. The loss only comes from alching overpriced items.

10. ❓ FAQ

What's the cheapest way to 99 Magic?

High Alchemy from 55 (break-even on good items) plus splashing for the early levels. Avoid burst/barrage unless you have GP to burn. Many players also train Magic passively via Slayer.

How much does barraging to 99 cost?

Roughly 100M–200M GP from 70–99 depending on method (Ice Barrage ~−2.3M to −2.5M GP/hr; Cure Me even more). On Slayer tasks the drops offset part of it.

Do I need Ancient Magicks to train fast?

For bursting/barraging, yes — Ancient Magicks come from Desert Treasure. Without it, your fastest methods are High Alchemy, Superheat, and enchant bolts.

Is High Alchemy profitable?

Often break-even or slightly profitable if you buy items below their High Alch value (yew longbows, rune items, d'hide bodies). It is rarely a big earner but is the best "free" Magic XP.

What level do I need for a Trident / powered staff?

A Trident of the seas needs 75 Magic to wield; Trident of the swamp 75 as well. These are excellent all-round Magic training AND combat weapons — a great early goal.

11. 🏁 Final Tips

  • Quests first: Desert Treasure (20k) and Lunar Diplomacy (5k) are free levels — never skip them.
  • Train to use-case: 55 for Alch, 75 for Trident, 94 for Barrage. Don't over-train.
  • Alch while skilling: High Alchemy during Agility laps or farm runs is "free" XP.
  • Burst on Slayer: offset rune cost with task drops (Dust Devils, Nechryaels).
  • Splash for AFK: −65 Magic attack bonus, staff of air, caged monster.
  • Next skill: pair Magic with Runecrafting (your runes) and Slayer (your burst tasks).
