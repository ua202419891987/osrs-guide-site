OSRS Mobile Money Making 2026

You don't need a gaming PC to build your bank. OSRS Mobile is perfect for low-click, high-profit activities like GE flipping, herb runs, bird houses, and clue scrolls. This guide breaks down the best GP methods you can run straight from your phone.

  • 📌 Bird houses are passive Hunter XP + nests for ~150k per set

① Can You Actually Make Money on Mobile?

Short answer: yes — but the way you make GP on phone is different from PC. The mobile app is a full client, so every Grand Exchange trade, farming patch, and clue step works exactly as it does on desktop. The catch is input speed: tap-hold replaces right-click, and precise prayer switching or rapid clicking is awkward on a touchscreen.

① The Mobile Money-Making Rule

🎯 Pick "Low-Click, High-Yield" Methods

  • Low click count: Activities you can do in a few taps, then walk away
  • Low pressure: No enemies forcing split-second decisions
  • Set-and-forget: Things that keep earning while you do something else
  • No Wilderness: If a PKer appears, your slow taps = a free kill

If you follow that rule, a casual mobile player can realistically pull in 500k–2M GP per day across flipping, farm runs, and bird houses — without ever touching a boss. Think of mobile as your "bank-building background mode": do it on the bus, in bed, or between PC sessions.

💡 Your Account Is Cloud-Saved

Start a herb run on mobile during lunch, finish it on PC at home. The world, bank, and GE offers are identical across devices. Many players earn the bulk of their GP on mobile and spend it on PC.

② Grand Exchange Flipping on Mobile

Grand Exchange flipping is hands-down the best mobile money maker. You buy items below market value and sell them above — and the entire workflow is just tapping the exchange interface. No walking, no combat, no risk.

② How Flipping Works (The 30-Second Version)

📈 The Buy-Low / Sell-High Loop

  1. Check an item's price on the GE (tap it → "View offer") or a price-tracking site
  2. Place a BUY offer slightly above the instant-sell price to get filled fast
  3. Wait (minutes to hours) for your buy offer to complete
  4. Place a SELL offer slightly below the instant-buy price to flip quickly
  5. Collect profit and reinvest — compounding is the real power

③ Reading Margins on the Mobile GE Interface

The mobile GE screen shows instant buy and instant sell prices side-by-side. The gap between them is your per-item margin. Tap an item, then tap "View offer" to see live buy/sell limits and price history.

🔁 Beginner Flipping Starter List

  • Nature runes & law runes: Huge volume, tiny margins, great for learning
  • Yew / magic logs: Steady demand, predictable price swings
  • Unfinished potions: Tied to herb prices — check before each flip
  • Sharks / anglerfish: Always in demand, easy to move
💡 Use Price-Tracking Before You Commit

Before buying on mobile, glance at a live price tracker in your phone browser. The in-game GE updates hourly, so off-game data keeps you from overpaying. Start with small limits (a few hundred items) until you learn an item's rhythm.

Rough GP/hr: Capital-dependent. A 5M bank flipping runes/supplies commonly nets 200k–1M+ per hour of active time, but most of that is "waiting" you can do while the app runs in the background.

③ Herb & Tree Runs

Farming is the king of passive GP. You plant, wait (real-time, hours later), and harvest — and the actual tapping takes 5 minutes. Perfect for mobile, and it scales hard as your account grows.

④ Herb Runs — The Core Money Maker

With 9 herb patches (including Trollheim, Harmony Island, and the Farming Guild), a single run of high-value herbs (ranarr, snapdragon, toadflax, irit) can be worth 100k–300k+. Use ultracompost and magic secateurs (or the Fortune perk from the Farming Guild) to boost yields.

🌱 Herb Run Checklist (Mobile)

  1. Teleport to each patch (Farming Guild, Trollheim, Catherby, Falador, Ardougne, etc.)
  2. Tap-hold the patch → "Rake" (clears dead weeds)
  3. Tap-hold → "Plant" your herb seed
  4. Apply ultracompost (tap-hold → "Use" compost on patch)
  5. Repeat across all patches, then log off and let them grow
  6. Come back in ~80 minutes (herbs) and harvest

⑤ Tree & Fruit Tree Runs

Trees take longer (a few hours to a full day) but need almost zero attention. Plant a tree run once, forget it, collect logs/nuts later. Palm and papaya trees are steady earners; magic trees are high-value but slow.

💡 Tip-Hold Is Your Friend Here

On mobile, tap-hold every patch to get the full option menu. Set compost and plant from the same menu. At scale (multiple daily runs), herb runs alone can clear 1M+ GP/day — all from your phone.

④ Bird Houses

Bird house trapping (Hunter, unlocked after the Bone Voyage quest) is the laziest GP in the game. You craft bird houses, place them at 4 spots, wait ~50 minutes, and collect bird nests packed with seeds, rings, and alchables.

⑥ Setting Up a Bird House Run

🐦 The 4-Stop Bird House Loop

  1. Craft 4 bird houses (logs + clockwork + chisel) — do this anywhere
  2. Mushroom Meadow (Morytania): tap-hold dig spot → place house
  3. Lake Molch: same placement
  4. Verdant Valley (after Bone Voyage): place house
  5. Hopeless Grove (after Bone Voyage): place house
  6. Wait ~50 min, then tap each house → "Empty" to collect nests

🥚 What You Get From Nests

  • Seeds: Ranarr, snapdragon, and tree seeds sell for solid GP
  • Ring nests: Gold/diamond rings are alchable
  • Empty nests: Used in nest boxes (Construction) — steady demand
  • Hunter XP: Bonus levels while you profit

Rough GP/set: ~100k–200k per full 4-house set, with 2–3 sets per day realistic = 200k–600k/day plus Hunter XP. The mobile map makes hopping between houses trivial — just tap the minimap and go.

💡 Combine With Herbs

Bird houses and herb runs share teleports and timers. Run both in one "mobile session" and you've got two passive income streams ticking at once.

⑤ Easy/Medium Clue Scrolls on Mobile

Clue scrolls are tap-around treasure hunts — no combat pressure on easy/medium tiers, which makes them ideal for mobile. You read a clue, walk to a spot, dig or emote, and open a reward casket.

⑦ Why Easy/Medium Clues Are Mobile-Friendly

✅ No Boss Fights Required

  • Easy clues: Mostly "dig here" or "emote here" steps — pure tapping
  • Medium clues: A few puzzle boxes (slide puzzles) — doable slowly on a phone
  • Anagram steps: Just walk to an NPC and talk — no fighting
  • Reward: God pages, gp, runes, and occasionally valuable cosmetics

⑧ Where to Get Clues on Mobile

You'll naturally pick up clue scrolls from low-level monsters, fishing, and pickpocketing — all activities you can do on mobile. Or buy specific clue scrolls (e.g., easy) off the GE and complete them.

Keep a stack of easy/medium clues in your bank and burn through them during a commute. The emote + dig steps are the easiest on mobile — just follow the on-screen hint.

⑥ Low-Effort Skilling (AFK on Phone)

Some skilling is genuinely AFK — tap once, wait 20–30 seconds, tap again. Perfect for mobile while you watch TV or ride transit. The GP is lower per hour than flipping, but the effort is near zero.

🎣 A Simple Mobile AFK Loop

  1. Open the app, teleport to your spot (Fishing Guild, Woodcutting Guild, etc.)
  2. Tap the resource node once to start
  3. Set your phone down — the action repeats automatically
  4. When the inventory fills, bank (tap-hold bank chest → "Bank")
  5. Repeat until you hit your GP goal or the session ends

⑦ What NOT to Do for GP on Mobile

Mobile's weak spot is reaction speed. Avoid anything where a 2-second delay means losing your stack. These are the traps that clean out new mobile players.

⑩ Money Traps to Avoid on Phone

❌ #1: Wilderness Money Making

Any Wilderness activity (rogues' chest, rune chests, shooting stars in wild) puts you one PKer away from total loss. Mobile tap-hold → eat/prayer takes 2–3 seconds. A PC PKer kills you before you react.

❌ #2: High-Risk PvM for Drops

Bosses like Vorkath or Zulrah need tight prayer switches. On mobile, swapping protect prayers is clunky — you'll take avoidable hits and waste supplies. Leave bossing for PC.

❌ #3: Risky Flips / Instance Trading

Don't "trust trade" for profit or flip items you don't understand. Mobile makes it easy to mis-tap a sell price. Stick to the GE and verified margins.

❌ #4: Carrying Valuables Through Dangerous Content

Even a "safe" slayer task near the Wilderness edge can turn deadly. Keep your bank value low while playing mobile away from home.

💡 Safe = P2P Zones, GE, and Farms

Every method in this guide (flipping, herbs, birds, clues, AFK skilling) happens in safe P2P areas. If you can die and lose it, don't do it on mobile.

⑧ FAQ — Mobile Money Making

What's the absolute best GP method on mobile for a brand-new account?

GE flipping. It needs no stats, no quests, and almost no clicking. Even a 500k starting bank can grow steadily. Herb runs come second once you hit Farming ~32 and unlock more patches.

How much GP per hour can I realistically make on mobile?

GE flipping: 200k–1M+/hr (capital-dependent, mostly waiting). Herb runs: ~100k–300k per 5-min run. Bird houses: ~150k per set. Easy clues: ~150k–300k each. AFK skilling: 40k–250k/hr. Combined daily: 500k–2M+.

Do I need membership to make GP on mobile?

Strongly recommended. F2P has almost no farming, no bird houses, and a tiny GE. A bond (buyable in-game with GP) unlocks the full mobile money-making toolkit described here.

Is GE flipping safe from scams on mobile?

Yes, if you use the Grand Exchange only. Never "trade" outside the GE for "better prices." Mis-taps happen more on mobile, so double-check every buy/sell price before confirming.

Can I do herb runs on iOS (no RuneLite)?

Yes. The official mobile app handles farming perfectly — tap-hold patches, use compost, plant. You don't need RuneLite plugins for herb or tree runs.

How many herb runs should I do per day?

Herbs grow in ~80 minutes. Realistically 2–4 runs/day (morning, lunch, evening, night) is sustainable and nets 300k–1M+ daily at higher Farming levels with ultracompost.

Are bird houses worth it on a low Hunter level?

Mostly, yes. You need Bone Voyage done (for Fossil Island access) and ~5–9 Hunter to start (regular bird houses). Higher Hunter unlocks better houses (oak, willow, teak, maple) for more nests and XP.

Should I buy clue scrolls to complete on mobile?

For easy/medium, sure. They're cheap on the GE and the steps are mobile-friendly. Avoid hard/elite on mobile — they often need combat or precise puzzle work that's painful on a phone.

What's the safest way to "stack" multiple methods?

Run herb + bird house timers together, then AFK fish while those grow. Check GE buy/sell offers whenever you open the app. That's how casual mobile players hit 1M+/day without sweating.

Does mobile use a lot of data for all this?

No. OSRS Mobile uses only ~30–50MB per hour. A few herb runs and GE checks cost almost nothing on mobile data. Save WiFi for long AFK skilling sessions. See OSRS Mobile Battery & Data Guide.
