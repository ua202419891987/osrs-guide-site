1. Why Train Agility?

Agility is one of the most important skills in OSRS because it directly affects your run energy recovery rate. At higher levels, you regenerate run energy so fast that you essentially never stop running — which saves time on literally everything you do in the game.

Beyond run energy, Agility unlocks over 50 shortcuts across Gielinor that drastically cut travel time. Some of the most impactful:

  • Taverley Dungeon blue dragon shortcut (level 70) — skip the entire dungeon walk
  • Zul-Andra grimy herb shortcut (level 76) — essential for Zulrah trips
  • Prifddinas agility shortcuts (level 75+) — quick access to multiple content areas
  • GWD boss room shortcuts — faster God Wars Dungeon access

And the Giant Squirrel pet — one of the most iconic skill pets — has a 44–59% drop chance over 1–99 depending on your training method.

Over a full 1–99 grind on rooftop courses, expect to earn 15M–20M GP from Marks alone.

6. Wilderness Agility Course

The Wilderness Agility Course sits in deep Wilderness (level 50+), making it high-risk, high-reward. It offers the fastest XP from levels 47–62 and the highest GP/hr of any Agility method — but you WILL get PK'd.

StatDetails
Level required52 (can boost from 47 with Summer Pies)
XP/hr50,000–60,000 (including ticket XP)
GP/hr (loot)Up to 2,000,000 GP/hr from alchables, blighted items, clue scrolls
Pet rate~44% over 1–99 (same as rooftops)
Entry cost150,000 GP (one-time, starts ticket/loot drops)
Premium accessWilderness sword 3/4 or 150K GP payment

🛡️ Anti-PK Strategy

  1. Wear cheap gear only — Graceful + Amulet of glory + Royal seed pod
  2. Keep Protect from Magic on — most PKers use Ice Barrage
  3. Phoenix necklace — auto-heals 30% of HP when you drop below 20%
  4. Never carry valuables — if PK'd, you only lose cheap gear
  5. Use Ice Plateau teleport tabs — fastest access to the course
  6. World-hop when you see PKers — check the minimap constantly

⚠️ Warning: This course is in Level 50+ Wilderness. You cannot teleport above level 30 Wilderness normally. The Royal seed pod works up to level 30. For deeper escapes, use your Amulet of glory or accept the death. Never bring items you can't afford to lose.

💰 Loot Breakdown

Each lap generates random loot from the ticket dispenser and obstacle drops:

  • Alchable items — various weapons and armor pieces
  • Blighted items (notes) — food, super restores, magic/sap supplies
  • Medium clue scrolls — ~1 per 30 laps
  • Wilderness agility tickets — exchangeable for XP or rewards

7. Hallowed Sepulchre — Fastest XP in the Game

The Hallowed Sepulchre is a minigame beneath Darkmeyer that offers the highest Agility XP rates in OSRS: up to 105,000 XP/hr at level 92+. It also generates over 1,000,000 GP/hr from loot, making it both the fastest and most profitable Agility method.

📋 Requirements

  • Quest: Sins of the Father (completion required)
  • Agility level: 52 (Floor 1), 62 (Floor 2), 72 (Floor 3), 82 (Floor 4), 92 (Floor 5)
  • Thieving level: 66 (recommended, for Grand Coffin on Floor 5)
  • Secondary skills for coffins: 56 Construction, 54 Prayer, 7+ Magic, 62 Ranged

  • Dark acorn — used for the Dark squirrel pet recolour

🛒 Reward Shop Purchase Order

Each item costs 100 Hallowed Marks. Buy in this order for maximum efficiency:

  1. Hallowed Grapple — essential for faster floor completion
  2. Hallowed Focus — for Saradomin braziers
  3. Hallowed Hammer — for Construction coffins
  4. Hallowed Symbol — for Prayer braziers
  5. Hallowed Ring — protects from trap damage (great for learning)

💡 Private Instances: After accumulating ~200 Hallowed Marks, purchase a private instance (same cost). Private instances have synchronized trap timings (they always start on the same tick), making them significantly more consistent and faster than public instances.

11. FAQ

What is the fastest way to train Agility in OSRS?

The Hallowed Sepulchre (level 62+) offers the highest XP rates in the game: up to 100,000–105,000 XP/hr at level 92+. For rooftop courses, Ardougne at level 90 gives ~70,000 XP/hr. The Wilderness Agility Course gives ~50,000 XP/hr at level 47 but carries PK risk.

How long does it take to get 99 Agility in OSRS?

Using the fastest methods (Hallowed Sepulchre from level 62), reaching 99 Agility takes approximately 180–200 hours. Using rooftop courses exclusively, expect around 230 hours. Brimhaven Arena spike-trap method can speed up the early levels significantly.

How much money can you make training Agility?

From 1–99 using rooftop courses, Marks of Grace converted to Amylase crystals earn roughly 15,000,000–20,000,000 GP total. The Wilderness Agility Course can earn up to 2,000,000 GP/hr from loot. The Hallowed Sepulchre earns 1,000,000+ GP/hr from Grand Coffin loot, with the rare Ring of Endurance worth ~6,000,000 GP.

Where should I farm Marks of Grace?

Canifis rooftop course (level 40) has the highest Marks of Grace spawn rate for most players. With the elite Ardougne Diary completed, the Ardougne course (level 90) gives the most Marks per hour. With the hard Kandarin Diary, Rellekka (level 80) is also excellent.

Is the Graceful outfit required for Agility training?

The Graceful outfit is highly recommended but not strictly required. It reduces weight to near zero, which slows run energy drain significantly. Purchase it with Marks of Grace from Grace in the Rogues' Den. Most players collect the full set at Canifis (level 40) before moving to higher courses.

Should I use Summer Pies to boost Agility?

Yes, Summer Pies (+5 boost) are very useful for accessing courses 3–5 levels early. They're cheap (~1,800 GP each) and the time saved from training at a faster course is worth far more than the pie cost. Use them when you're within 5 levels of a much better course.

Is the Hallowed Sepulchre worth learning?

Absolutely. The learning curve is steep (expect 5–10 hours of practice before you're efficient), but the payoff is enormous: 85K–105K XP/hr versus 52K–70K from rooftops. Plus you earn 1M+ GP/hr and have a chance at the 6M GP Ring of Endurance. It's the best Agility method from 62–99 by a wide margin.

12. Final Tips

  • Always use Stamina potions — this is non-negotiable. The XP difference is 20–40%.
  • Get Graceful ASAP — farm Canifis at level 40 until you have the full set before moving on.
  • Learn the Hallowed Sepulchre — the initial time investment pays for itself many times over.
  • Use Summer Pies — cheap boost that lets you access better courses earlier.
  • Don't skip Brimhaven Arena — the spike trap method at level 20 is legitimately the fastest early XP.
  • Wilderness course is optional — 47–62 can be covered with rooftops if you don't want PK stress.
  • Use Runelite plugins — agility course highlights, Sepulchre tile packs, and Marks of Grace timers are all essential.
  • Check your weight — any positive weight hurts your run energy. Graceful + Boots of lightness = near-zero weight.
  • Diary bonuses matter — Kandarin Hard, Fremennik Hard, Desert Elite, and Ardougne Elite all boost course XP or Marks rates.
