1. Prayer Overview & Core Mechanics

Prayer is one of OSRS's most impactful combat skills. Every level increases your prayer points, and certain thresholds unlock game-changing protection prayers (43 Prayer!) and offensive prayers (Piety at 70, Rigour/Augury at 74/77).

How Prayer Training Works

You gain Prayer XP by burying bones/ashes, offering them at altars, or processing ensouled heads. The key multipliers:

MethodXP MultiplierRisk Level
Burying bones (inventory)1xNone — but don't do this
Bone Crusher (Morytania Hard Diary)1x (auto-buried on kill)None
Ectofuntus4xNone, but slow
Gilded Altar (POH)3.5xNone — safest method
Chaos Altar (Wilderness Lv 12)3.5x + 50% bone savePK risk but much cheaper

4. Gilded Altar — Best for Main Accounts (3.5x XP)

The Gilded Altar in your Player-Owned House is the safest, most popular Prayer training method. With 2 burners lit, you get 3.5x the bone's base XP. This is what most players use for 70-99 Prayer.

Setup Requirements

  • Construction Level: 75 for Gilded Altar (or use someone else's house on World 330, Rimmington)
  • Burners: 2 incense burners lit with Marrentill herbs
  • Bones: Dragon bones or Superior Dragon Bones (or any bones you can afford)

How to Use World 330 (Free, No Construction)

  1. Go to World 330 (House Party world)
  2. Go to Rimmington (house portal)
  3. Look for a host advertising their Gilded Altar (usually named "Altar" or "G Altar")
  4. Enter house, light burners if needed (bring noted Marrentill + coins to unnote at Phials)
  5. Use bones on the altar

5. Chaos Altar — Wilderness Risk, Big Savings (3.5x + Bone Save)

The Chaos Altar in level 12 Wilderness offers the same 3.5x XP as Gilded Altar BUT with a 50% chance to save each bone. This effectively doubles your bones, making it ~7x effective XP per bone purchased. The downside: you're in the Wilderness and can be PK'd.

Setup

  • Location: Level 12 Wilderness, west of the Graveyard of Shadows
  • Fastest Route: Cemetery teleport (Arceuus spellbook) or Burning Amulet → Lava Maze teleport → run south
  • Inventory: 1 inventory of bones + nothing else you're not willing to lose
  • Safe Strategy: Bring 1 inventory at a time. Use bones → die/teleport → repeat

Risk Management

  • Only bring 1 inventory of bones. Never bring your whole stash.
  • Use a Burning Amulet for quick teleports (under 1K GP).
  • Switch to a low-population PvP world or play during off-peak hours.
  • If you see a white dot on the minimap, finish your current inventory and teleport.
  • Keep Protect Item prayer on if using noted bones.
  • Turn off Auto-Retaliate and hide attack options.

6. Ectofuntus — No Risk, 4x XP (Ironman Friendly)

The Ectofuntus gives 4x XP (the highest multiplier in the game), but it's significantly slower than Gilded Altar because you need to grind bones into bonemeal first.

How It Works

  1. Take bones to the Ectofuntus grinder (top floor of Ectofuntus temple, northwest of Port Phasmatys)
  2. Grind bones into bonemeal (use bones on the hopper → pull lever → collect bonemeal in pots)
  3. Fill buckets with slime from the Ectofuntus pool
  4. Worship at the Ectofuntus altar with bonemeal + bucket of slime

When to Use Ectofuntus

  • Ironman accounts with limited bones — maximize XP per bone
  • Players who absolutely want zero PK risk
  • Completing the Morytania Diary (requires Ectofuntus worship)
  • Not recommended for main accounts past diary requirements — too slow

XP/hr: ~30K-50K with dragon bones (4x XP but slow processing). XP per bone: Best in game at 4x.

8. Other Methods — Passive Prayer XP

Bone Crusher (Morytania Hard Diary)

  • Automatically buries bones on kill for 1x XP
  • With Morytania Elite Diary: 1.5x XP for bones buried in Morytania
  • Best used during Slayer tasks in Catacombs of Kourend
  • Passive: adds up to 200K+ XP over the course of 99 Slayer

Ash Sanctifier ( Kourend Hard Diary)

  • Automatically scatters demonic ashes on kill for 1x XP
  • Very useful during Abyssal Demon and Nechryael tasks

Offering Spells (Arceuus Spellbook)

  • Demonic Offering: 3x XP for ashes (requires 84 Magic)
  • Sinister Offering: 3x XP for bones (requires 92 Magic)
  • Good for combining Magic + Prayer training

10. Ironman 1-99 Prayer Strategy

Prayer is one of the hardest Ironman skills because you cannot buy bones. You must obtain every bone yourself.

Early Game (1-43)

  • Do all Prayer quests → ~44 Prayer entirely free
  • Pick up and bury Big Bones from Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon)
  • Use Ectofuntus for maximum XP per bone

Mid Game (43-70) — Green Dragons

  • Green Dragons (Wilderness): Kill in level 12-23 Wilderness for dragon bones + green d'hides (crafting/alch GP)
  • Myth's Guild Green Dragons: Safe alternative after Dragon Slayer II (requires 200 QP)
  • Use Chaos Altar for 7x effective XP per bone (best value!)
  • Alternatively, use Ectofuntus for safe 4x XP

Late Game (70-99) — PvM Bones

  • Vorkath: Drops superior dragon bones + blue d'hides. 30-40 kills/hr = ~30K base XP in bones/hr
  • Dragon Slayer tasks: Blue/Black/Red dragons drop dragon bones
  • Hydra: Drops hydra bones (110 base XP each). Excellent bones + profit
  • Dagannoth Kings: Dagannoth bones (125 base XP) + rings (Berserker, Archer, Seers)
  • Wilderness Bosses: Callisto/Vet'ion/Venenatis drop noted dragon bones

Passive Ironman Prayer XP

  • Bone Crusher (Morytania Hard) during Catacombs Slayer
  • Ash Sanctifier during Abyssal Demons/Nechryaels
  • Ensouled heads from Slayer tasks — bank and process in bulk

14. Pro Tips for Maximum Efficiency

  1. Chaos Altar Is Worth the Risk: Saving 50% of your bones is massive. For dragon bones to 99, that's ~75M GP saved. Even if you get PK'd occasionally, you'll still come out ahead.
  2. Use Superior Dragon Bones at High Levels: At 500K XP/hr vs 300K XP/hr for regular dragon bones, the time saved often justifies the higher GP/XP. Time saved = more money-making time.
  3. World 330 for Safety: If you absolutely cannot risk PK deaths, World 330 Rimmington has hosted Gilded Altars 24/7. Just use someone else's house.
  4. Lit Burners Are Critical: An unlit burner at Gilded Altar gives only 2x XP instead of 3.5x. Always bring Marrentill herbs + a tinderbox.
  5. Inventory Optimization: Use a Bonecrusher Necklace (Hydra Tail + Dragonbone Necklace) if using ensouled heads — restores prayer points on bone bury.
  6. Diary Synergy: Complete Morytania Hard Diary early. The Bone Crusher + Catacombs Slayer adds up to hundreds of thousands of passive XP over time.
  7. Prayer Bonus Gear: While using altars, wear prayer bonus gear (Proselyte, God Books, etc.) to reduce prayer drain between offerings.

15. FAQ

Q: How long does 1-99 Prayer take?

With dragon bones at Gilded Altar: ~40-45 hours. With superior dragon bones: ~25-30 hours. Chaos Altar: Same rates but 50% fewer bones needed.

Q: What's the cheapest GP/XP for Prayer?

Chaos Altar with dragon bones = ~6 GP/XP (due to 50% bone save). Ensouled heads can be 1.5-3 GP/XP but are much slower. Wyrm bones + Gilded Altar = ~8.6 GP/XP.

Q: Is Chaos Altar safe?

It's in level 12 Wilderness, so PKers can attack you. But if you only bring 1 inventory of bones and teleport at the first sign of trouble, you'll rarely die. The 50% bone save more than compensates for occasional losses.

Q: What Prayer level should I aim for?

43 minimum — Protect from Melee changes the entire game. 70 for Piety — BIS melee prayer, essential for PvM. 74/77 for Rigour/Augury — endgame PvM. 85+ for diary requirements and more prayer points.

Q: Best Prayer training for Ironman?

Green dragons (Wilderness) for dragon bones → Chaos Altar for 7x effective XP per bone. Late-game: Vorkath for superior dragon bones + Hydra/Slayer for passive bones. Always use Ectofuntus if you want max XP per bone without PK risk.

Q: Can I train Prayer on F2P?

Yes, but it's painful. Big Bones are the best F2P option (bury for 15 XP each). Hill Giants in Edgeville Dungeon or Giant Frogs in Lumbridge Swamp. F2P Prayer is extremely slow — expect 5K-10K XP/hr max.

Q: Are ensouled heads worth it?

For GP/XP, yes — some are under 2 GP/XP. But they're slower than altar methods (~80K vs 300K XP/hr). Use them when you want to combine combat + Prayer training, not for rushing 99.
