OSRS Waterfall Quest Guide 2026 — The No-Combat Powerquest

Beginner

Waterfall Quest is the single best early XP dump in Old School RuneScape. It has no combat, no skill requirements, and no puzzle-solving difficulty — just a safe run through Baxtorian Falls that hands you 13,750 Attack and 13,750 Strength XP (27,500 total) in about 15–20 minutes. This gold-standard 2026 walkthrough gives you a verified step list, the exact tomb restrictions, a rewards-value calculator, and an interactive checklist so you never get stuck.

1. 🌊 Overview & Why Waterfall Is the Best Starter Quest

Waterfall Quest (released 24 September 2002, quest #37) is a members-only intermediate-length short quest about the treasure of elven King Baxtorian and his wife Glarial, hidden behind the Baxtorian Falls. What makes it legendary is that it delivers a massive combat-XP injection with zero fighting. If you are a new member with low stats, this is the first thing you should do — it effectively gives you the equivalent of hours of Strength and Attack training for free.

FactVerified Value (OSRS Wiki, July 2026)
Members requiredYes
DifficultyIntermediate
LengthShort (~15–20 min)
Quest seriesNone
Skill / quest requirementsNone
What you must surviveLevel 86 Fire Giants & level 84 Moss Guardians (run past, no equipment)
Total XP awarded27,500 (13,750 Attack + 13,750 Strength)

🎯 Who Should Do This First

  • New members who want their first big XP boost before training.
  • 1-defence / 1-HP pures — the quest has no combat, so your defence stays untouched.
  • UIM / Ironmen — the diamonds and gold bars are useful, and the route teaches key travel hubs.
💡 The One Rule That Wins

You never have to kill anything. Every "danger" in this quest is a monster you simply run past in single-combat zones. Bring a little food, keep your Hitpoints above ~25, and you are safe the entire time.

2. 📋 Requirements & Items

The official requirement is just the ability to withstand and evade attacks from level 86 Fire Giants and level 84 Moss Guardians without any equipment. There are no skill levels and no prerequisite quests. The only things you must carry are a handful of low-value items.

RequirementDetailNotes
Skill requirementsNoneNo Attack, Strength, or other levels needed
Quest requirementsNoneCan be done on a brand-new member account
Items to bring6 Air runes, 6 Earth runes, 6 Water runes, 1 RopeAll buyable on the Grand Exchange for a few hundred GP

The three rune types are placed on pillars near the end; the rope is used to climb into the waterfall dungeon. None of these are consumed permanently in a way that blocks progress — just have them in your inventory at the right step.

3. 🥾 Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Step 1 — Talk to Almera & find the book

Go to Almera's house (west of the Coal Trucks, north-east of Baxtorian Falls). Use a games necklace → Barbarian Outpost and run south. She worries about her son Hudon. Board the log raft outside, crash onto the island, and talk to Hudon. Finish all his dialogue or the book will not spawn later. Swim south, wash ashore by a building, go upstairs, and search the southernmost bookcase on the east wall to get the book on Baxtorian. Read it or the key will not appear.

Step 2 — Get Glarial's Pebble

Travel to Tree Gnome Village (south-west of Ardougne). If you have not started Tree Gnome Village, you must navigate the hedge maze to find a ladder, grab the key from the oddly-coloured crate in the south-east room, unlock the west-room door, and talk to Golrie to receive Glarial's pebble. (Tip: it is smart to start Tree Gnome Village now by talking to King Bolren — then Elkoy can escort you out.) Low-level players can use the drop trick to bank an extra pebble in case they die.

Step 3 — Raid Glarial's Tomb

Head just south-east of Baxtorian Falls / north-west of the Fishing Guild. Bank all weapons, armour, runes, arrows and logs first — the tomb forbids them (jewellery like a games necklace is fine). Use the pebble on the tombstone to enter. Run past the level 84 Moss Guardians (attack a low-level skeleton or zombie so they can't reach you — single combat). Search the west chest for Glarial's amulet, then the south coffin for Glarial's urn. Climb the ladder out.

Step 4 — Reach the Chalice of Eternity

Return to Almera's house and board the raft again. Do not swim or touch the tree without the rope. Use your rope on the large grey rock, then rope on the dead tree to climb. Inside, take the eastern passage (run past level 52 Shadow spiders, level 45 Skeletons, level 16 Skeleton Mages) and search the northernmost crate for a key. Then take the western passage north past the level 86 Fire Giants to the chalice room (attack a shadow spider so the giants can't hit you).

Step 5 — Activate the chalice (the only fiddly bit)

Inside the chamber are six pillars, two statues, and the Chalice of Eternity. Use one Air, one Water, and one Earth rune on each of the six pillars. Then use Glarial's amulet on the Statue of Glarial and Glarial's urn on the Chalice of Eternity, and complete the dialogue. The ground rises — take your reward (you need 5 free inventory slots). Warning: any items dropped in the six-pillar chamber are lost once you use the amulet on the statue, so drop recoverables outside the door first.

💡 If You Get Flushed Out

If you leave or get washed out, the pillars stay active — but you must fetch another amulet from Glarial's Tomb to re-enter the dungeon. That is why the drop trick on the amulet/urn saves time.

6. ⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Mistake 1: "I'll just bring my gear into the tomb."

Wrong. Glarial's Tomb forbids weapons, armour (including capes), runes, arrows and logs. If you walk in wearing them you cannot progress. Bank everything prohibited first — jewellery is allowed.

❌ Mistake 2: "I skipped Hudon's dialogue."

Wrong. If you do not finish Hudon's full conversation, the book will not spawn in the building. If you do not read the book, the key will not appear later. Both are hard gates.

❌ Mistake 3: "I placed the runes on only one pillar."

Wrong. You must use one Air + one Water + one Earth rune on each of the six pillars, then use the amulet on the statue and the urn on the chalice. Partial placement does nothing.

❌ Mistake 4: "I dropped my loot inside the pillar room."

Any items you drop in the six-pillar chamber are unrecoverable once you use the amulet on the statue. Drop recoverables outside the door before activating.

❌ Mistake 5: "I forgot free inventory slots."

You need 5 free slots to accept the reward from the Chalice. Clear your bag before the final step or you will have to re-enter.

7. 🔓 Why This Quest Matters (Unlocks)

Waterfall Quest is a gateway. Its 27,500 XP is great, but the unlocks are what make it mandatory for any serious account:

  • Desert Treasure I — the ancient staffs (Ahhrim's, etc.) and the desert pyramid reward.
  • Legends' Quest — one of the biggest pre-99 milestones and a huge XP reward itself.
  • Roving Elves — required for the elite Western Provinces Diary and the Crystal equipement line.
  • Medium Kandarin Diary — the XP helps, and the diary has useful teleports.
  • The Path of Glouphrie — part of the gnome quest line.

On top of that, finishing it early gives you a solid Attack/Strength base so your first real fights (and the next quests below) go smoothly.

9. ❓ FAQ

Can I do Waterfall Quest on a free-to-play account?

No. Waterfall Quest is members-only. You need an active membership to start it and to enter Glarial's Tomb and the waterfall dungeon.

Do I need any combat stats?

No combat stats at all. You never fight — you only run past level 86 Fire Giants and level 84 Moss Guardians. Some food and ~15 Hitpoints make the run comfortable, but there is no requirement.

Why won't the book / key appear?

You must finish Hudon's dialogue and then read the book on Baxtorian. Skipping either step silently blocks the next item. Re-talk and read if something is missing.

Can I keep the pebble/amulet after the quest?

Yes. Keeping Glarial's pebble is recommended — it is required again for Roving Elves. The drop trick lets you bank spares cheaply.

How long does it take?

A first run is about 15–20 minutes with decent teleports. On repeat alt accounts with the routes memorised it drops to ~10 minutes.

10. 🏁 Final Tips

  • Bank before the tomb: weapons, armour, runes, arrows and logs are all banned inside Glarial's Tomb.
  • Finish every dialogue: Hudon's chat and reading the book are hard gates.
  • Six pillars, three runes each: Air + Water + Earth on all six, then amulet→statue, urn→chalice.
  • Keep 5 free slots for the reward and drop recoverables outside the pillar room.
  • Next step: roll straight into Tree Gnome Village — it shares the gnome hub and builds toward Monkey Madness.
