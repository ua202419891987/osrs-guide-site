1. Why Herb Runs Are the Best Passive GP in OSRS

Here's the reality of OSRS money making:

  • Vorkath: 2-3M/hr but requires full attention, 100+ combat, and Dragon Slayer II
  • Gauntlet: 4-5M/hr but requires Song of the Elves and high skill
  • Herb runs: 1.8M-3.6M/hr of active time, requirements as low as 32 Farming, doable on mobile while watching TV

Herb runs are the ultimate side hustle. You can do them between Slayer tasks, during work breaks, or even on your phone. No bossing skill required. No gear needed. Just teleports, seeds, and 5 minutes.

6. Maximizing Profit — Compost, Secateurs & Diary Perks

Compost — Always Use Ultracompost

Herb patches have a disease mechanic. Ultracompost reduces disease chance to ~10% (from ~25% with supercompost) and increases minimum yield by 50%. That's an extra ~1 herb per patch on average. At 7 patches, that's ~7 extra herbs per run. At 3K+ per herb, that's 21K extra per run — the ultracompost costs ~3K total. Net profit: +18K per run.

Magic Secateurs — Equip, Don't Inventory

The +10% yield bonus from Magic Secateurs only works when equipped, not just in your inventory. Keep them on during every harvest. If you forget, you lose ~10% profit on every patch.

In the Farming Guild's Anima patch, plant an Iasor seed (from Hespori). It reduces disease chance across ALL patches. With Iasor + ultracompost, disease becomes rare. If you're chasing max profit, Attas increases yield but you'll lose more patches to disease.

FAQ

Q: How often can I do herb runs?

Herbs take 80 minutes to grow (4 growth cycles × 20 minutes). You can do a run every 80 minutes. Most players do 3-5 runs per day (morning, lunch break, evening).

Q: Ranarr vs Snapdragon — which is better?

Ranarr is safer. Seeds are cheaper (25K vs 40K+), and if a patch dies, you lose less. Snapdragon has higher potential profit but higher variance. At 62-73 Farming, alternate: plant Snapdragon in disease-free patches (Trollheim, Hosidius) and Ranarr elsewhere.

Q: Do I need to water herbs?

No. Watering only affects the first growth cycle and herbs survive without it. Ultracompost protects better than watering. Never waste time watering herbs.

Q: What's the fastest way to level Farming for herb runs?

Tree runs. Fruit tree runs (papaya/palm) + hardwood trees (mahogany on Fossil Island) + calquat. Do tree runs for XP, herb runs for GP. They're complementary, not competing.

Final Tips

  • Use the Tool Leprechaun. Every patch has one. They note your herbs for free. Never bank raw herbs — always note them at the patch and continue.
  • Bottomless Compost Bucket is worth it. It doubles your ultracompost efficiency (one ultracompost = two uses). Pays for itself in ~50 runs.
  • Do herb runs on mobile. The route becomes muscle memory after 10 runs. Do it while commuting, watching TV, or between Slayer tasks on PC.
  • Check OSRS Wiki prices before buying seeds. Profit margins shift. If Snapdragon seeds spike to 50K+, stick to Lantadyme or Cadantine until prices normalize.
  • Combine with birdhouse runs for even more GP. Birdhouses take 50 minutes. Start a birdhouse run, do your herb run, and by the next herb run, birdhouses are ready again.
