OSRS Rune Spinning Profit Guide 2026 — Flax to Bowstrings & Best Locations

📅 Published: June 7, 2026  |  ⏱️ 8 min read  |  ✍️ OSRS Guru
🧵 Money Making — Crafting / Spinning — Updated June 2026

Spinning flax into bowstrings is one of the oldest and most reliable money-making methods in OSRS. It requires zero skill levels, zero quests, and generates consistent profit from the permanent demand for bowstrings in Fletching. This guide covers every location, profit calculation, and optimization tip for 2026.

Crafting XP/hr:~22,500 (15 XP per bowstring)Risk:Zero (safe method, no PvP)

1. How Flax Spinning Works

Take flax to any spinning wheel, click the wheel, and select "Spin Flax." Each flax becomes one bowstring, which gives 15 Crafting XP. Bowstrings are a core component for stringing bows (yew longbows, magic longbows) — Fletchers always need them, creating permanent demand.

5. Alternative Spinning Methods

Spinning Wool into Balls of Wool

Wool sells for 30-50 GP per ball. Shearing sheep (Lumbridge or Falador farm) provides free wool. This is more of a F2P method since profit is much lower than bowstrings. Each ball gives 2.5 Crafting XP.

Sinew into Crossbow Strings (Level 10)

Sinew from cooked meat can be spun into crossbow strings at level 10 Crafting. These sell for 100-150 GP each, similar to bowstrings. However, obtaining sinew (cooking meat, cutting sinew) is significantly slower than buying flax.

Magic Roots into Magic Strings (Level 19)

Magic roots (from chopping magic trees) can be spun into magic strings. These sell for more than bowstrings but the supply of magic roots is extremely limited, making this impractical for volume profit.

6. Tips to Maximize Profit

  1. Use Seers' Village spinning wheel. It's the closest wheel to a bank in the game. The time saved walking adds up to 15-20% more profit per hour compared to Lumbridge or Barbarian Village.
  2. Buy flax in massive bulk. Place a buy order for 100K-200K flax at a time. You'll get a slightly better price on bulk and won't have to stop your spinning session to buy more.
  3. Use keyboard/mouse shortcuts. Spacebar to initiate spinning, number keys for banking. These small optimizations increase your bowstrings per hour from ~1,200 to ~1,600+.
  4. Check GE prices before starting each session. Bowstring margins can fluctuate 10-15 GP day to day. Always verify the current flax buy price and bowstring sell price before committing capital.
  5. Combine with Fletching for more profit. If you have Fletching levels, string your own bows (bowstring + unstrung bow). The profit from a strung bow is typically higher than selling the bowstring alone.
  6. Sell bowstrings in the evening (US time). Fletchers buy supplies during peak hours. Listing your bowstrings between 6pm-10pm EST often results in faster sales at better prices.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is spinning flax good for F2P money making?

A: Yes, it's one of the best F2P zero-requirement methods. At 96K-115K GP/hr using the Lumbridge Castle spinning wheel, it outperforms most F2P skilling methods at low levels. The only better F2P methods require higher combat stats (Ogress Warriors, Moss Giants) or member skills.

Q: Should I pick flax or buy it from the GE?

A: Buy it. Picking flax at Seers' Village gives you free flax but takes time. At 3-5 GP per flax on the GE, the cost is negligible compared to the time you'd spend picking. The extra 44K GP/hr from buying flax and spending saved time on other activities easily outweighs the flax cost.

Q: Can I make more profit by stringing my own bows?

A: Usually yes, if you have the Fletching level. A strung yew longbow sells for more than an unstrung bow + bowstring sold separately. Check the GE: if (strung bow price) > (unstrung bow price + bowstring price), it's worth stringing them yourself. This requires higher Fletching levels (70 for yew, 85 for magic) but boosts overall profit.

🧵 Simple, Consistent, Reliable

Spinning flax won't make you rich overnight, but it will make you consistent GP with zero risk and zero requirements. It's the perfect method for brand new accounts, F2P players, or anyone looking for a semi-AFK money maker while watching videos. Over a week of casual spinning (2 hours/day), you'll earn ~2M GP — enough for your first whip or full rune gear.

Buy your flax, head to Seers' Village, and start spinning.
