🎯 What Makes a Method "AFK" — 3 Tiers

AFK (Away From Keyboard) in OSRS doesn't mean you can literally walk away from your computer indefinitely. Jagex has anti-AFK measures that log you out after 5 minutes of inactivity (or sooner in certain content). True "AFK" means the method requires minimal attention — you can click once every 1-3 minutes and still make progress or profit.

This guide categorizes every AFK money-making method into three tiers based on how frequently you need to interact with the game:

AFK Tier Click Frequency Can You Work/Study? Typical GP/hr Examples
True AFK Every 2-5 minutes Yes — light work 100k - 500k Ammonite Crabs, Fishing, Redwoods
Semi-AFK Every 30s - 2 min Yes — with breaks 400k - 800k MLM, Cooking, Plank Make
Low-Attention Every 10-30 seconds Maybe — watching only 400k - 2.5M Blast Furnace, Herb runs, Herbiboar

Important Note About the 5-Minute Logout Timer: OSRS will automatically log you out after approximately 5 minutes of zero input. Some content (like NMZ) has a shorter idle timer (90 seconds). To stay "AFK" longer, you need content that naturally requires interaction every few minutes — or you can use mouse keys (allowed by Jagex) to automate simple click patterns.

Mouse Keys Tip: Windows mouse keys (Left Alt + Left Shift + NumLock) let you automate camera movement or menu options. This is explicitly allowed by Jagex for simple 1:1 input actions. It's the secret to making methods like MLM and Blast Furnace truly AFK.

📊 How This Guide Ranks Methods

Every method in this guide is ranked on two dimensions:

  1. GP/hr: The expected profit per hour. For AFK methods, this is almost always lower than active methods — you're trading GP/hr for convenience.
  2. Attention Score (1-5): How much attention the method requires. 1 = completely AFK (click every 5 min), 5 = must watch constantly.

The "best" AFK method depends on your situation. If you're working from home, True AFK methods are the only realistic option. If you're just watching a movie, Low-Attention methods can work too.

✅ True AFK Methods (2+ Min Clicks)

These methods require a click only every 2-5 minutes (or whenever your inventory fills up). They are the best options for players who want to make GP while working or studying.

1️⃣ Ammonite Crabs (0-50K/hr)

Ammonite Crabs on Fossil Island are the premier AFK combat training method. They have low defense, don't deal damage if you have Protect from Melee on, and respawn instantly. While not a "money maker" in the traditional sense (they drop almost nothing), they are included here because AFK combat is often done while money-making elsewhere on a second account, or because the XP/hr is high enough that the time savings are valuable.

  • Requirements: 60 Combat recommended, 43 Prayer for protection
  • GP/hr: 0 - 50k (negligible drops, but alchables from other crabs nearby)
  • Attention Score: 1/5 — click every 3-5 minutes to re-engage
  • Best For: AFK combat training while doing something else

2️⃣ Sand Crabs (0-30K/hr)

Similar to Ammonite Crabs but accessible earlier. Located on Zeah (Hosidius). Drops are minimal but it's the most accessible AFK combat method in the game.

  • GP/hr: 0 - 30k
  • Attention Score: 1/5

3️⃣ Fishing Trawler (100K-1.8M/hr)

The Fishing Trawler is a minigame that gives Fishing XP and valuable catches (Angler pieces, Leaping fish, Sharks). It's largely AFK — you need to click every 15-30 seconds to fix the net, but this can be done with minimal attention.

  • Requirements: 40 Fishing (15 Fishing if using a friend to start the game)
  • GP/hr: 100k - 400k (depending on catches and Angler pieces)
  • Attention Score: 2/5 — occasional clicks to fix leaks
  • Notes: Completing the Angler outfit (4 pieces) gives +2.5% Fishing XP. Each piece is worth ≈500k-1M on the GE.

4️⃣ Fishing Karambwans (150K-1.8M/hr)

Karambwans are a high-value fish (≈1.4k GP each) used for the Tai Bwo Wannai Trio minigame and cooking. Fishing them is semi-AFK but can be made True AFK with the right setup.

  • Requirements: 65 Fishing, Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest partially completed
  • GP/hr: 150k - 400k (depending on catch rate)
  • Attention Score: 2/5
  • Notes: Karambwans also heal 18 HP in two bites, making them valuable for bossing. The demand is consistent.

5️⃣ Redwood Trees (70K-100K/hr)

Redwoods are the highest-level tree in OSRS and can be chopped AFK for long periods. Each log is worth ≈450 GP, and you can cut 400-400 logs per inventory.

  • Requirements: 90 Woodcutting, 75 Farming (to grow your own redwoods at the Farming Guild)
  • GP/hr: 150k - 400k
  • Attention Score: 1/5 — chops for 2-3 minutes per inventory
  • Notes: Combine with birdhouse runs on Fossil Island for passive Hunter XP and GP while cutting redwoods.

6️⃣ Star Mining (100K-1.8M/hr)

Shooting Stars land randomly across Gielinor every few hours. They degrade into 9 layers, each with increasing Mining requirement. Mining stars is very AFK (especially the lower layers) and yields Stardust (≈1.2k GP each) and various ores.

  • Requirements: 10 Mining (Level 1 star), 50+ for higher level stars
  • GP/hr: 100k - 400k (highly variable based on star frequency and layer)
  • Attention Score: 1-2/5 — very AFK, especially lower levels
  • Notes: Use the Star Timers plugin in RuneLite to know when stars land. Join the Friends Chat "stars" for notifications.

7️⃣ Anglerfish (1.8M-1.8M/hr)

Anglerfish are one of the most valuable fish in OSRS (≈1.8k GP each). They require 82 Fishing and completion of Underground Pass (or admission to the Fishing Hamlet via A Kingdom Divided). Fishing them is moderately AFK.

  • Requirements: 82 Fishing, A Kingdom Divided completed (for Fishing Hamlet access)
  • GP/hr: 400k - 500k
  • Attention Score: 2/5
  • Notes: Anglerfish heal 12-22 HP depending on your HP level, making them essential for TOB and other high-end content. Demand is always high.

8️⃣ Seaweed Spawning (Passive)

Planting Seaweed spores in the Fishing Hamlet pool is a passive Farming method. Each spore grows into 9 Giant seaweed (≈400 GP each) after 40 minutes. It's not AFK during planting, but the growth is passive.

  • GP per Run: ≈16k per pool (12 spores × 9 seaweed × 400 GP, minus spore cost)
  • Attention Score: 1/5 for the waiting period

🔄 Semi-AFK Methods (30s-2min)

These methods require a click or action every 30 seconds to 2 minutes. They're suitable for casual play, watching videos, or light multitasking — but not for focused work.

1️⃣ MLM (100K-1.8M/hr)

Motherlode Mine is the most popular mid-game Mining method and moderately AFK. You mine ores from the vein, which fill up your sack. Every few minutes you need to empty the sack at the hopper and pay for mining. With the upper level unlocked (72 Mining), the pay-dirt yield is much higher.

  • Requirements: 30 Mining (upper level requires 72), Goblin Diplomacy and The Dig Site for MLM access
  • GP/hr: 100k - 400k (depending on Mining level and sack size)
  • Attention Score: 3/5 — click every 30-60 seconds to mine a new vein
  • Notes: Use mouse keys to automate the 1-5 click sequence for mining. This makes MLM nearly True AFK. The Prospector outfit (from MLM points) gives +2.5% Mining XP.

2️⃣ Cooking (100K-1.8M/hr)

Cooking food on a range is one of the simplest AFK money makers. High-level foods like Sharks (≈1k GP each), Manta rays (≈1.8k GP each), and Anglerfish (≈1.8k GP each) offer good profits. The key is to use the Hosidius Kitchen range (reduces burn rate by 5%) or the Fort Forinthry range (even better).

  • Requirements: Varies by food (Shark = 80 Cooking, Manta ray = 91 Cooking)
  • GP/hr: 100k - 400k (depending on food type and burn rate)
  • Attention Score: 2/5 — click to cook, inventory takes 30-60 seconds to cook
  • Notes: Cooking gauntlets (from Family Crest) reduce burn rate significantly. At 99 Cooking with gauntlets, you stop burning most foods.

3️⃣ Cannonballs (60K-90K/hr)

Smelting steel bars into Cannonballs (via the Smithing skill at a furnace while having an ammo mould in your inventory) is a classic AFK money maker. Each steel bar makes 4 cannonballs. Cannonballs sell for ≈180 GP each on the GE.

  • Requirements: 35 Smithing, Dwarf Cannon quest completed (for ammo mould)
  • GP/hr: 400k - 400k (depending on Smithing level and furnace location)
  • Attention Score: 3/5 — must click to smelt each bar; can use mouse keys
  • Notes: Use the Edgeville furnace (closest to bank) or the Blast Furnace furnace. At 35-60 Smithing, this is also decent XP. Profit per bar: 1 steel bar (≈600 GP) → 4 cannonballs (≈720 GP) = ≈120 GP profit per bar.

4️⃣ Plank Make (100K-1.8M/hr)

The Plank Make spell converts logs into planks instantly from your bank or inventory. This is used by Construction training accounts, but you can also buy logs, cast Plank Make, and sell the planks for profit (or use them for your own Construction training).

  • Requirements: 86 Magic (Plank Make spell), Nature runes, Astral runes
  • GP/hr: 100k - 400k (as a pure money maker; much higher if you value the Construction XP)
  • Attention Score: 3/5 — must cast spell every 3-5 seconds
  • Notes: This method is more commonly used as a Construction training method (the GP "cost" is actually buying planks at GE price vs. spell cost). If you need Construction XP, this is the best method.

5️⃣ Fletching (100K-1.8M/hr)

Fletching bolts, darts, or bows is a common AFK skilling method. Broad bolts (after Horror from the Deep and 55 Slayer) and Amethyst bolts are profitable options at higher levels.

  • Requirements: Varies (Broad bolts = 55 Slayer + Horror from the Deep)
  • GP/hr: 100k - 500k (depending on what you fletch)
  • Attention Score: 2/5 — click to fletch, then wait 3-5 seconds per set
  • Notes: Fletching is also a fast XP method. Many players fletch while doing something else because the XP/hr is decent even at half attention.

🍷 Winemaker (100K-350K/hr)

Making wine (use a Grape on a Jug of water, wait for it to ferment) is very AFK. The wine sells for ≈2.4k GP each (Cooking 68+ for no fermentation failure). This is one of the simplest AFK methods in the game.

  • GP/hr: 100k - 350k
  • Attention Score: 1/5 — make 14 jugs, wait, collect

📋 Low-Attention Methods (10-30s)

These methods require near-constant attention but have short interaction cycles (10-30 seconds). They're suitable for dedicated "OSRS time" where you're focusing on the game but want something less intense than bossing.

1️⃣ Blast Furnace (1.8M-2.5M/hr)

Covered in detail in the Mid-Game guide, Blast Furnace deserves mention here because it can be made Semi-AFK with the right setup. Use mouse keys to automate the coal-addition sequence. At 60 Smithing, you can make Steel bars (≈550 GP profit/bar) at 700 bars/hr for ≈385k GP/hr.

  • Attention Score: 3-4/5 (without mouse keys), 2/5 (with mouse keys)
  • GP/hr: 400k - 2.5M

Herb Runs (Farming 32+)

Herb runs take 5-10 minutes of active attention (planting, teleporting between patches, harvesting) but the 80-90 minute grow time is completely passive. This is the definition of "low-attention passive income."

  • Active Time: 10 minutes per run
  • Passive Wait: 80-90 minutes
  • Profit per Run: 80k - 400k GP
  • Attention Score: 4/5 during the run, 1/5 during grow time

Birdhouse Runs (Hunter 5+)

Birdhouse runs are similar to herb runs — active for 5 minutes, then passive for 50 minutes. The GP and Hunter XP per run make this one of the best low-attention methods.

  • Active Time: 5 minutes per run
  • Passive Wait: 50 minutes
  • Profit per Run: 40k - 120k GP

Herbiboar (Hunter 80+ / 31+ Recommended)

Herbiboar is a Hunter boss on Fossil Island that drops herbs and Herbiboar shards (used for Stamina potions). The hunt takes about 2-3 minutes per kill and requires following tracks. It's moderately attention-intensive but very profitable.

  • Requirements: 80 Hunter, First Trunk mini-quest (Fossil Island)
  • GP/hr: 400k - 500k (depending on herbs dropped)
  • Attention Score: 4/5 — must follow tracks and click options

Giants' Foundry (Smithing 60+ / 80+ Recommended)

Giants' Foundry is a post-Firemaking minigame that gives excellent Smithing XP and some GP from selling the swords you create. It requires moderate attention (clicking to smith, managing the heat gauge) but can be done semi-AFK.

  • Requirements: 60 Smithing, Firemaking questline completed
  • GP/hr: 100k - 400k (mostly from selling the swords)
  • Attention Score: 3-4/5

⚔️ Combat AFK Money (1.8M-2M/hr)

Some combat methods are surprisingly AFK-friendly. These methods let you camp a single monster for extended periods with minimal clicking.

🏠 Gargoyles (1.8M-1.8M/hr)

Gargoyles are a Slayer task (75 Slayer required) that are very AFK. They have moderate defense but low damage. You need a Rock hammer (or Granite maul) to finish them off when they reach 9 HP. The drops include Granite maul (≈155K GP), Alchables, and consistent herb/seeds.

  • Requirements: 75 Slayer, 70 Combat, Rock hammer
  • GP/hr: 400k - 500k
  • Attention Score: 2-3/5 — need to smash with hammer at 9 HP
  • Notes: Use Slayer helmet (i) for bonus accuracy. Gargoyles in the Slayer Tower (Morytania) are safe from PKers.

Vyrewatch Sentinels (Combat 75+ / 50 Prayer)

Vyrewatch Sentinels in Slepe (requires Sins of the Father) are extremely AFK. They have a high Defense but low damage. With proper Prayer and food, you can AFK them for long periods. They drop Vyrewatch guts (used for Sunspear) and various alchables.

  • Requirements: Sins of the Father completed, 75 Combat, 43+ Prayer
  • GP/hr: 400k - 400k
  • Attention Score: 2/5 — very AFK once setup

🦎 Rune Dragons (1.8M-700K/hr)

Rune Dragons in the Lithkren Dungeon (requires Dragon Slayer II) drop Rune items, Dragon plateskirts, and various herbs. They are somewhat AFK but require anti-dragon shield and decent gear. The profit per hour is solid for an AFK combat method.

  • Requirements: Dragon Slayer II completed, 85 Combat, Anti-dragon shield
  • GP/hr: 400k - 700k
  • Attention Score: 3/5

Brutal Black Dragons (Combat 80+ / 70 Prayer)

Brutal Black Dragons in the Catacombs of Kourend drop Dragon bones, Black dragonhide, and various herbs. They are more dangerous than Rune Dragons (multi-combat area) but offer higher profit.

  • Requirements: 80 Combat, 43+ Prayer, Anti-dragon shield, Dragon Slayer completed
  • GP/hr: 500k - 900k
  • Attention Score: 3/5
  • Risk: Medium — Catacombs are a multi-combat PvM area; bring a teleport out

Nightmare Zone (NMZ) (Combat 60+ / 43+ Prayer)

NMZ is a combat minigame where you fight quest bosses in a dream world. With Dharok's set or full Obsidian, you can AFK NMZ for long periods. The profit comes from Herb boxes (earned with points) and consistent alchables.

  • Requirements: Completed at least 1 quest with a "boss" (e.g., Dragon Slayer, Tree Gnome Village)
  • GP/hr: 100k - 400k (plus XP)
  • Attention Score: 1-2/5 — can be fully AFK with proper setup (Dharok's + Prayer)
  • Notes: NMZ is more commonly used for combat training than money making, but the Herb boxes (≈25k GP value each) add up over time.

🛠️ Best AFK Setup for Work/Study

If you want to make OSRS GP while working or studying, you need the right hardware and software setup. Here's what works best based on thousands of hours of combined community experience.

🛠️ Hardware Setup

  • Dual Monitor (recommended): Run OSRS on a second monitor at low resolution (800×600 or 1024×768). You can glance over to check on your character without alt-tabbing.
  • Laptop + Phone: If you don't have dual monitors, play OSRS on your phone (official OSRS app) while working on your laptop. The mobile app is excellent for AFK methods.
  • Tablet Stand: Prop your phone/tablet at eye level so you can monitor your character while typing or reading.

RuneLite Plugins for AFK Play

  • Idle Notifier: Plays a sound when your character becomes idle (stops attacking, runs out of inventory space). Essential for AFK play.
  • Mouse Keys: As mentioned, Windows mouse keys let you automate simple click patterns. Enable via Left Alt + Left Shift + NumLock.
  • XTreme Lite: A RuneLite plugin pack that includes many QoL features. Note: some third-party clients are against Jagex rules — stick to official RuneLite.
  • Inventory Tags: Color-code your inventory so you can see at a glance if it's full.
  • Ground Items: Highlights valuable drops on the ground so you don't miss them while AFK.

Mobile AFK Tips

  • Enable "Disable Screen Timeout": In the OSRS mobile app settings, you can prevent your screen from dimming while the app is open.
  • Use "Tap to Move": In settings, enable tap-to-move so you don't need to swipe to walk.
  • Plug in Your Phone: OSRS mobile drains battery. Keep your phone charging while AFK playing.
  • Best Methods on Mobile: Fishing, Woodcutting, Mining (MLM), Cooking. Avoid combat on mobile unless you're actively watching — it's too easy to die.

❓ FAQ + AFK Daily Routine

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I really make money AFK in OSRS or is active bossing always better?

A: Active bossing is always better for GP/hr — a proficient Vorkath kill earns 3M-2.5M GP/hr, which no AFK method can match. But AFK methods let you earn GP while doing other things. If you have a full-time job, AFK methods might be the only way you can generate GP at all. The best approach is to combine both: AFK during work, active bossing on weekends.

Q: What's the absolute most AFK method that still makes decent GP?

A: Redwood trees (90 Woodcutting) and Star Mining (10+ Mining) are the most AFK methods with meaningful GP. Redwoods earn ≈400k GP/hr with a click every 2-3 minutes. Stars are even more AFK but less consistent (depends on spawn times).

Q: Is using mouse keys allowed? I don't want to get banned.

A: Yes, Windows built-in mouse keys (Left Alt + Left Shift + NumLock) are explicitly allowed by Jagex. What's NOT allowed is third-party macro software that plays the game for you. Stick to Windows mouse keys and you're safe.

Q: Can I AFK on mobile while my phone is in my pocket?

A: Technically yes, but it's risky. You might run out of food, get attacked by a PKer (in Wilderness), or log out due to the idle timer. At minimum, keep the screen on and check it every few minutes. For true pocket AFK, Fishing Trawler or NMZ (with proper setup) are the safest options.

Q: What's the best AFK method for a brand-new member (no stats)?

A: Sand Crabs (40 Combat) for AFK combat training, Cooking (cook food from the GE) for AFK skilling, and MLM (30 Mining) for AFK Mining. These require almost no stats and still generate some GP.
