1. 🎯 Why Finish F2P Quests Before Buying Membership

Buying membership is exciting. You want to teleport straight to the Grand Exchange, grab your best gear, and start exploring member areas. But if you skip the free-to-play quests, you are making a costly mistake — and here is exactly why.

1.1 ⏱️ Every Minute of Membership Costs GP or Real Money

Membership in 2026 costs $14.99/month or roughly 5.2M GP per bond. That means every hour you spend doing F2P quests as a member costs you about 2 cents in real money or 30,000 GP in bond value. More importantly, those hours could be spent on members-only content that actually makes you money.

If you spend 3 hours doing Cook's Assistant and Doric's Quest as a member, you have wasted 3 hours of your 14-day bond — hours you cannot get back.

1.2 📈 F2P Quests Give You a Level Head Start

Many F2P quests reward XP that completely skips the slowest, most boring early levels of skills. For example:

  • The Knight's Sword jumps you from level 1 to level 29 Smithing instantly — saving over an hour of mining and smithing bronze and iron items.
  • The Restless Ghost takes you from level 1 to level 9 Prayer in 5 minutes — skipping the tedious process of burying regular bones.
  • Witch's Potion gives enough Magic XP to cast Fire Strike, which is your best early-game combat spell.

These XP rewards are even more valuable when you consider that as a member, those early levels would still require you to use the same slow F2P methods — there is no "members speed-up" for levels 1-20.

1.3 🎫 Quest Points Are Required for Members Quests

Many essential members quests have Quest Point (QP) requirements. Dragon Slayer alone requires 32 QP just to start. By completing F2P quests first, you build a QP foundation so you can jump straight into members quests without backtracking.

Some examples of members quests with QP requirements:

  • Lost City — 31 QP (unlocks Dragon weapons)
  • Monkey Madness I — no direct QP requirement, but requires The Grand Tree (which requires completion of other quests)
  • Recipe for Disaster — 175 QP for the full quest line (the best gloves in the game)

1.4 🛡️ Unlock Essential Items and Areas

Several F2P quests unlock items or areas you will use even as a high-level member:

  • Rune Mysteries — unlocks the ability to mine Rune Essence and craft runes (essential for any account)
  • Demon Slayer — unlocks Silverlight, which upgrades to Darklight (used against demons in members)
  • Prince Ali Rescue — unlocks free passage through the Al Kharid gate (saves you 10gp every trip, and you will make hundreds of trips)
💡 Pro Tip

The Dragon Slayer quest gives 18,650 Strength XP and 18,650 Defence XP — that is enough to go from level 1 to level 33 in both skills. Do it as F2P so you enter members with a real combat baseline.

2. 🗺️ Must-Do F2P Quest List (Priority Order)

Below are the F2P quests you should complete, ranked by priority. Priority is based on XP rewards, unlock value, and speed. Quests marked ★ Essential should be done before anything else.

2.1 🍳 Cook's Assistant — ★ Essential (Top Priority)

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~2 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards300 Cooking XP · 500 GP (if you pick up the reward from the cook)
Start LocationLumbridge Castle kitchen

It takes 2 minutes — literally. You grab an egg (near the chicken coop across the river), a bucket of milk (Lumbridge cow field), and a pot of flour (Lumbridge mill, north of the castle). Bring them to the cook in Lumbridge Castle and you are done.

Why do it before membership: The 300 Cooking XP is small, but it unlocks access to the cook's range (burns less food). Plus, 1 QP for 2 minutes is the best QP-per-minute ratio in the game. There is zero reason to skip this.

2.2 ⛏️ Doric's Quest — ★ Essential

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~4 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone (15 Mining recommended for speed)
Rewards1,300 Mining XP · 180 GP · Use of Doric's anvils
Start LocationDoric's house, north of Falador

Doric needs 6 clay, 4 copper ore, and 2 iron ore. You can mine all of this in the Dwarven Mine just north of his house. The 1,300 Mining XP takes you from level 1 to level 10 Mining — skipping the painful bronze pickaxe grind.

Why do it before membership: 1,300 Mining XP for 4 minutes of work is incredibly efficient. Level 10 Mining lets you mine iron ore, which is a decent F2P money maker. This quest alone saves you roughly 15 minutes of slow Mining training.

2.3 👻 The Restless Ghost — ★ Essential

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~5 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards1,125 Prayer XP · Amulet of Ghostspeak
Start LocationLumbridge church (Father Aereck)

Talk to Father Aereck in the Lumbridge church, go to the graveyard south of Lumbridge, retrieve the ghost's skull from the Wizards' Tower, and return it. Done in 5 minutes.

The 1,125 Prayer XP takes you from level 1 to level 9 Prayer — skipping 100+ regular bone burials. The Amulet of Ghostspeak is required for several members quests including Animal Magnetism and The Restless Ghost's sequel content.

Why do it before membership: Prayer is one of the slowest skills to train at low levels. This quest gives you a massive head start and the amulet is mandatory for important members quests.

2.4 🧪 Witch's Potion — ★ Essential

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~5 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards325 Magic XP
Start LocationHetty the witch, Rimmington

Talk to Hetty in Rimmington. She needs an onion, burnt meat (cook raw rat meat until burnt), eye of newt (buy from Port Sarim magic shop for 3gp), and a rat's tail (kill any rat). Bring all four items back and you are done.

Why do it before membership: 325 Magic XP takes you from level 1 to level 5 Magic — unlocking Water Strike and the ability to use basic teleports. It also saves you the cost of mind runes you would otherwise spend splashing on rats. Level 5 Magic lets you start using enchanted jewelry immediately as a member.

2.5 🐑 Sheep Shearer — ★ Essential

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~3 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards150 Crafting XP · 60 GP
Start LocationLumbridge sheep field (north of the castle)

Shear 20 sheep. Bring the wool to the spinning wheel in Lumbridge Castle (second floor). Spin 20 balls of wool. Return to the farmer. Three minutes, tops.

Why do it before membership: 150 Crafting XP isn't huge, but this quest takes 3 minutes and gives 1 QP. The wool balls are also useful for stringing amulets later (Crafting training). Every QP counts for Dragon Slayer's 32 QP requirement.

2.6 👺 Goblin Diplomacy — ★ High Priority

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~5 minutes
Quest Points5 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards200 Crafting XP · 1 Gold Bar
Start LocationGoblin Village, north of Falador

Two goblin generals are fighting over armor color. You need to collect blue, orange, and brown dyes (buy from the dye shop in Lumbridge or make yourself), and bring the dyed armor to the generals. Takes about 5 minutes of dialogue and running.

Why do it before membership: This quest gives 5 QP — the highest QP reward of any novice F2P quest. It is more than 15% of what you need for Dragon Slayer. The gold bar can be crafted into jewelry or sold for ~150 GP.

2.7 🐔 Ernest the Chicken — ★ High Priority

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~15 minutes
Quest Points4 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards300 GP · Access to Draynor Manor (including the spade spawn)
Start LocationDraynor Manor (Veronica, outside the manor gates)

Ernest has been turned into a chicken by Professor Oddenstein in Draynor Manor. You need to navigate the manor, collect a pressure gauge, rubber tube, and oil can, fix the machine, and turn Ernest back. The puzzle at the end (pulling levers A, B, C, D, E, F in the right order) is the only real challenge.

Why do it before membership: 4 QP is substantial. Draynor Manor becomes a useful location — the spade spawn there saves you from buying one, and the manor is a clue scroll hotspot for both easy and medium clues as a member.

2.8 🔍 Misthalin Mystery — ★ Medium Priority

📋 Quest Quick Facts

DifficultyNovice
Length~15 minutes
Quest Points1 QP
RequirementsNone
Rewards600 Crafting XP · 250 GP · Access to the Gemstone Grotto
Start LocationLumbridge Castle (talk to the Murder Mystery NPC near the courtyard)

This murder mystery-style quest takes place in Lumbridge. You investigate a crime scene, collect clues, and identify the culprit. It involves some running around the castle and talking to NPCs. The story is genuinely well-written and the puzzle logic is satisfying.

Why do it before membership: 600 Crafting XP is decent for a novice quest, and the 250 GP adds to your early-game funds. The quest also introduces you to puzzle-solving mechanics you will encounter in members quests like Murder Mystery and One Small Favour.

4. ⚡ Fastest Route: Complete All F2P Quests in 3 Hours

Optimized route for speedrunning all 18 F2P quests. This route minimizes walking time by grouping quests by geographic region. Total estimated time: 2.5–3 hours.

4.1 📍 Phase 1 — Lumbridge Cluster (~40 minutes)

🏰 Lumbridge Area Quests (Start Here)

  1. Cook's Assistant (2 min) — Start at Lumbridge Castle kitchen. Grab items from chicken coop, cow field, and mill. All within 30-second walking distance.
  2. Sheep Shearer (3 min) — Sheep are north of the castle. Spin wool on second floor. Done.
  3. The Restless Ghost (5 min) — Church is in Lumbridge. Skull is at Wizards' Tower south. Run south to the tower, grab skull, return.
  4. Misthalin Mystery (15 min) — Also in Lumbridge Castle. Do this after Cook's Assistant since you are already there.

4.2 📍 Phase 2 — Draynor & Falador (~45 minutes)

🏘️ Draynor and Falador Area Quests

  1. Ernest the Chicken (15 min) — Draynor Manor is just west of Lumbridge. Navigate the manor, solve the lever puzzle.
  2. Doric's Quest (4 min) — Run north from Falador to Doric's house. Mine materials in Dwarven Mine directly north.
  3. Goblin Diplomacy (5 min) — Goblin Village is just north of Doric's. Dyes can be pre-purchased in Lumbridge before leaving Phase 1.
  4. Black Knights' Fortress (12 min) — The fortress is on the way back from Goblin Village toward Falador. Requires 12 QP (you will have enough by now).
  5. The Knight's Sword (15 min) — Start in Falador Castle (south of Doric's). You need 10 Mining. Mine blurite in the dungeon south of Falador.

4.3 📍 Phase 3 — Rimmington & Port Sarim (~35 minutes)

⚓ Port Sarim and Western Area Quests

  1. Witch's Potion (5 min) — Hetty is in Rimmington. Buy eye of newt from Port Sarim magic shop on the way. Kill rats near Rimmington for tail and meat.
  2. Imp Catcher (10 min) — Wizard Mizgog is in the Wizards' Tower (south of Draynor). You can do this earlier or later — imps spawn everywhere.
  3. Rune Mysteries (7 min) — Also at the Wizards' Tower. After completing, you unlock rune essence mining (useful for members runecrafting).
  4. Pirate's Treasure (10 min) — Starts in Port Sarim at the pub (The Rusty Anchor). Requires a trip to Karamja, so bring 30gp for the ship fare.

4.4 📍 Phase 4 — Al Kharid & Varrock (~40 minutes)

🏜️ Al Kharid and Varrock Area Quests

  1. Prince Ali Rescue (15 min) — Start in Al Kharid palace. If you have not unlocked the gate, pay the 10gp or go around through the Lumbridge swamp.
  2. Demon Slayer (15 min) — Start in Varrock Square. Defeat Delrith with Silverlight (must use the correct incantation).
  3. Shield of Arrav (10 min) — You need a partner. Find someone in the same world and coordinate which gang (Phoenix or Black Arm) each of you joins. Each gang has a different half of the quest.
  4. Vampyre Slayer (15 min) — Start in Draynor Village (you can do this after Phase 1). Buy garlic and a stake from the Draynor market. Defeat Count Draynor in the Draynor Manor basement.

4.5 📍 Phase 5 — Dragon Slayer (~30 minutes)

🐉 Final Boss: Dragon Slayer

  1. Talk to the Guildmaster in the Champions' Guild (south of Varrock). You need 32 QP to enter — you should have 42+ QP by now.
  2. Collect the 3 map pieces: Melzar's Maze (bring runes for magic attacks), the dungeon near Port Sarim (kill the goblin for the key), and the Oracle on Ice Mountain.
  3. Buy or make an anti-dragon shield (free from Duke of Lumbridge after starting the quest).
  4. Equip the anti-dragon shield, bring your best food (lobsters or swordfish), and fight Elvarg in the Crandor dungeon. Use Protect from Magic if you have 37 Prayer, or just tank with food.
  5. Return the head to the Guildmaster. You can now wear the Rune Platebody and the Green D'hide Body.
💡 Speedrun Tips
  • Buy all quest items before starting the route. Stock up on: bucket of milk, egg, pot of flour, dyes (blue, orange, red), eye of newt, garlic, stake, hammer, chisel, pickaxe, 30gp for ship fare, and a few teleport runes if you have the Magic level.
  • Use the Lumbridge Home Teleport between phases to reset your position. It has a 30-minute cooldown but saves significant walking time.
  • Do Shield of Arrav last if you cannot find a partner quickly. It only gives 1 QP, and you already have enough for Dragon Slayer without it.

5. 🎁 Post-Quest: What to Do Immediately After Buying Membership

You have finished all F2P quests. You have 44 QP, level 30+ combat stats, and a solid foundation. Here is exactly what to do in your first hour as a member:

5.1 🚀 Hour 1: Unlock Critical Members Teleports

Step 1: Do Druidic Ritual (10 minutes)

Unlocks the Herblore skill. Start in Taverley (walk west from Falador). This is the most important 10 minutes of your membership — without Herblore, you cannot make potions, and potions are essential for every boss fight.

Step 2: Start the Ardougne Easy Diary

The Ardougne cloak 1 gives unlimited teleports to the Ardougne Monastery — right next to a fairy ring and a spirit tree. This single teleport changes your entire account mobility.

Step 3: Complete Tree Gnome Village (15 minutes)

Unlocks spirit trees. Combined with the Ardougne cloak teleport, you can now reach the Grand Exchange (Varrock spirit tree), the Farming Guild (Farming Guild spirit tree), and several other key locations in seconds.

5.2 💰 Hour 2-3: Start Making GP

With your new members tools, you can make significantly more GP than F2P ever allowed. Start with one of these methods:

  • Herb runs (Farming level 9+) — Plant ranarr seeds, harvest ranarr weeds, sell for ~7,000 GP per weed. A single 5-minute herb run can profit 100,000+ GP. See our Herb Run Mastery Guide.
  • Fletching (level 10+) — Buy logs from the Grand Exchange, fletch into arrow shafts or unstrung bows. Easy profit with zero risk.
  • Looting in PvP worlds — Pick up dropped items in high-traffic PvP areas. No stats required. See our Low-Effort Money Making Guide.

5.3 🗡️ Hour 4+: Start Questing for Key Unlocks

Use the Best Quests for New Members guide (our complete walkthrough) to plan your next 10 quests. Priority unlocks include:

  • Waterfall Quest — 30 Attack + 30 Strength XP. Do it at level 3 combat.
  • Fight Arena — 12,175 Attack XP + 2,175 Thieving XP.
  • The Grand Tree — Unlocks gliders, 7,900 Agility XP, and access to Gnome Stronghold.
  • Tree Gnome Village — Spirit tree network access.

For a complete new member roadmap, check our First Week Progression Guide and OSRS Membership Guide 2026.

6. 💡 Common Mistakes to Avoid

New and returning players make these mistakes constantly. Do not be one of them.

6.1 ❌ Buying Membership Before Doing Any Quests

🚫 Mistake #1: Activating membership on a fresh account

If you buy membership before doing any F2P quests, you will either waste membership time doing free quests, or skip them entirely and miss critical XP rewards. Always complete F2P quests first — it takes one afternoon and saves you days of grinding.

6.2 ❌ Skipping Rune Mysteries

🚫 Mistake #2: Ignoring Rune Mysteries

Without Rune Mysteries, you cannot mine rune essence or craft runes. As a member, Runecrafting is one of the most profitable skills once you reach the Ourania Altar or the Abyss. Do not lock yourself out of it for a 7-minute quest.

6.3 ❌ Doing Shield of Arrav Without Planning

🚫 Mistake #3: Starting Shield of Arrav with no partner

This quest requires a partner in the opposite gang. Many players start it, join a gang, and then spend 30 minutes in Varrock shouting "need Phoenix gang partner!" — while a perfectly good partner is shouting "need Black Arm gang!" Use the official OSRS grouping system or the Shield of Arrav chat channel to find a partner before committing to a gang.

6.4 ❌ Fighting Elvarg Unprepared

🚫 Mistake #4: Underestimating Elvarg

Elvarg hits through all armour types with dragonfire. If you forget the anti-dragon shield, you will die in 3-4 hits. Bring the shield, 20+ lobsters or swordfish, and your best melee gear. If you have 37 Prayer, use Protect from Magic — it reduces dragonfire damage significantly. Elvarg is level 83 and can hit up to 10 with melee and 20+ with dragonfire if unprotected.

6.5 ❌ Buying Bonds Instead of Membership

🚫 Mistake #5: Using bonds when you can afford a subscription

In 2026, a bond costs ~5.2M GP for 14 days. If your real-life budget allows, paying $14.99/month is far better value — instead of spending 5.2M GP every two weeks (which is ~130M GP/year), you can use that GP for gear upgrades, skills, and supplies. 7. ❓ FAQ — F2P Quests Before Membership

Q: How many F2P quests are there in OSRS in 2026?

A: There are 18 free-to-play quests in Old School RuneScape as of June 2026. They range from novice-level 2-minute quests (Cook's Assistant) to the experienced-level Dragon Slayer, which requires 32 QP and combat stats against a level 83 dragon.

Q: What is the minimum time to complete all F2P quests?

A: A prepared player with all items pre-purchased and teleports optimized can complete all 18 F2P quests in approximately 2.5 to 3 hours. Without preparation, expect 4-5 hours due to walking time and item gathering.

Q: Which F2P quest gives the best XP reward?

A: Dragon Slayer gives the highest XP reward with 18,650 Strength XP and 18,650 Defence XP. For early-game value, The Knight's Sword gives 12,725 Smithing XP — enough to jump from level 1 to level 29 Smithing instantly, saving over an hour of training.

Q: Can I do Dragon Slayer at a low combat level?

A: Yes, but it is risky. The minimum recommended setup is 40+ combat level, an anti-dragon shield, and 20+ lobsters. If you have 37 Prayer (Protect from Magic), you can do it at a lower combat level. Many ironmen complete Dragon Slayer at combat level 30-35 with careful safe-spotting and prayer flicking. For a detailed guide, see our New Player Guide.

Q: Should I complete Below Ice Mountain and Corsair Curse?

A: Below Ice Mountain (released in 2021) is a F2P quest that unlocks the Ruins of Camdozaal, a mid-tier F2P training area with golems, fishing spots, and a mine. It is worth doing if you want variety in F2P training, but it does not contribute significantly to membership readiness. Corsair Curse is a short F2P quest on Corsair Cove and is required for Dragon Slayer II's pre-requisite chain in members. Complete it if you have time — it takes about 10 minutes and gives 2 QP.

Q: What quest item unlocks carry over to members?

A: Several F2P quest items have members upgrades or continued use: Silverlight (Demon Slayer) upgrades to Darklight and later Arclight, the Amulet of Ghostspeak (The Restless Ghost) is required for Animal Magnetism and several ghost-related members quests, and Rune Essence access (Rune Mysteries) is essential for Runecrafting. See our Best Quests for New Members guide for the full progression path.

Q: Is it worth doing F2P quests on an ironman?

A: Absolutely — arguably more so. On an ironman account, every XP reward matters because you cannot buy supplies from the Grand Exchange. The 12,725 Smithing XP from The Knight's Sword alone saves ironmen from mining and smelting hundreds of bronze and iron bars. For ironman-specific F2P strategies, see our F2P Ironman Guide.
