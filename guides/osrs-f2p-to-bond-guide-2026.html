2. Phase 0: Zero to Starter Cash (Day 1)

Every bond journey starts with the same first step — getting your first 10,000 GP.

📍 Stronghold of Security — 10K GP (15 min)

Located beneath Barbarian Village (north of Falador). Enter via the ladder at the center of the village.

  • Complete four floors of the stronghold
  • Reward: 10,000 GP + Fancy Boots (cosmetic)
  • Requirements: None — any account can complete this
  • Time: 10-15 minutes

🎯 Day 1 Checklist

  • Create account → Tutorial Island (15 min) → You're in Lumbridge
  • Walk north to Barbarian Village (2 min)
  • Complete Stronghold of Security (15 min) → 10K GP
  • Congratulations — you have your starter capital!

3. Phase 1: Zero Requirements (10K → 500K GP)

These methods require no skills and no quests. They work from the moment you leave Tutorial Island.

🐄 Method A: Cowhide Collecting (~50K GP/h)

East of Lumbridge Castle, the cow field is the classic F2P starter. Cows drop Cowhides (~150-200 GP each on GE) and Bones.

  • Attack cows (level 2) or pick up hides left by other players
  • Bank at Lumbridge Castle basement
  • Sell hides on the Grand Exchange
  • GP/h: 40K-60K — risk: none

🛒 Method B: Shop Arbitrage (~50K-100K GP/h)

Buy from NPC shops, sell on GE. Best items for Phase 1:

🦴 Method C: Wilderness Bone Yard (~80K-120K GP/h)

Bone Yard at level 20-25 Wilderness. Collect Big Bones (~300-400 GP each).

Risk: Medium — PKers patrol this area. Use the "Empty Inventory" method (bring nothing you can't lose).

💡 Phase 1 Strategy

Days 1-3: Start with Cowhides for 2-3 hours → get ~150K GP. Then switch to Shop Arbitrage (Ball of Wool) — the profits scale with your capital. By day 3, you should have ~300K-500K GP. Use the Stronghold 10K to buy your first inventory of shop items.

🏆 Best Phase 2 Method: Ogress Warriors

The Ogress Warriors in the Corsair Cove dungeons are widely considered the best F2P money maker. They drop:

  • Rune full helms, battleaxes, med helms (~5-10K each)
  • Nature runes, death runes, law runes
  • Rune scimitars (~15K each — rare but valuable)
  • Clue scrolls (medium)

Setup: 40+ Ranged (Maple shortbow + Adamant arrows) or 13+ Magic (Fire Strike). Complete The Corsair Curse and Dragon Slayer I quests to unlock the Corsair Cove dungeon entrance.

GP/h: 150K-250K — best consistent F2P money maker with low requirements.

💡 Efficiency Tip: Combine Methods

Train combat at Hill Giants → level up to 40 Ranged/Magic → switch to Ogress Warriors. While waiting for GE flips to fill, AFK at Ogress Warriors. This two-track approach maximizes your GP per hour.

🏆 Best Phase 3 Method: High Alchemy

Once you reach 55 Magic and have 5M+ GP, High Alchemy becomes the fastest F2P money maker. The strategy:

  1. Use a F2P alch calculator (OSRS Wiki has one) to find items with GE buy price < High Alch value
  2. Buy these items in bulk on the GE
  3. Cast High Level Alchemy on them — the difference between buy price and alch value is pure profit
  4. Repeat — some items have margins of 200-500 GP per alch

At 300K-500K GP/h, this method lets you earn the remaining 7.8M GP in 15-25 hours.

10. F2P to Bond FAQs

How long does it take to earn a bond in F2P?

At the most efficient methods (High Alchemy at 300K-500K GP/h), it takes about 25-40 hours of dedicated grinding. Using the recommended Ogress Warriors path (~150K-250K GP/h), expect 50-85 hours. At casual methods (Cowhides, 50K GP/h), it's 250+ hours.

Should I buy a bond with real money for my first membership?

You can't buy bonds with real money through the GE — you buy membership directly or use real currency to buy a bond from Jagex's website. If you're asking whether to pay $11 for one month vs. grinding 60+ hours in F2P, most players recommend paying once. The time you save is enormous, and after one month of member money makers, you'll sustain bonds indefinitely.

Is it worth earning a bond in F2P?

It depends on your situation. If you can't or won't spend real money, earning a bond in F2P is absolutely achievable — thousands of players do it. But be realistic about the time commitment. At 2 hours/day, expect 4-6 weeks using the Ogress Warriors path. Many players find the grind burns them out before they reach the bond.

What's the single best F2P money maker for bonds?

High Alchemy (300K-500K GP/h at 55 Magic). It requires the most preparation (training Magic and building capital) but pays off fastest. If you want a method with no Magic requirement, Ogress Warriors (150K-250K GP/h at 40+ Ranged + The Corsair Curse + Dragon Slayer) is the best option.

Can I sustain bonds forever as a member?

Yes, easily. Once you have ~80+ Ranged and complete Dragon Slayer II, Vorkath earns 3M-4M GP/h. That's 3-4 hours of bossing for 14 days of membership. Add herb runs (10 min/day for ~450K GP) and you're more than covered.

What if bond prices go up while I'm saving?

Bond prices fluctuate. In the last 6 months, bonds dropped from ~14.6M to ~12.8M. If you're 75% of the way to your goal and the price spikes, consider using your current GP to buy items that hold value (like rune items or ores) and sell them later when prices stabilize. This protects your progress against bond price volatility.

Do I need to complete Dragon Slayer for the bond grind?

Not strictly, but it's highly recommended. The Corsair Curse and Dragon Slayer unlock access to Ogress Warriors in Corsair Cove (150K-250K GP/h), which is the best mid-level F2P money maker. Without it, your best options are Hill Giants (100K-180K GP/h) or Moss Giants (100K-200K GP/h).

What items should I flip on the GE with limited F2P capital?

With under 1M GP: Feathers, cowhides, runes (fire, air, mind), raw lobsters/swordfish, and essence. With 1M-5M GP: Rune items (scimitars, full helms), dragon bones, yew logs, bowstrings. Check margins on the OSRS Wiki Prices site before committing.
