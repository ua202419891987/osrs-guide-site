① Notable Alternatives and Situational Switches

Not every boss is best fought in the theoretical BiS. Here are the most important situational swaps:

  • Inquisitor's Set — Beats Torva at Nightmare, Phosani's, and any crush-vulnerable content. Full Inquisitor ~500M.
  • Void Knight Set (Elite) — Best at ToA (free + damage/accuracy bonus), but loses to Masori/Armadyl in most other content.
  • Justiciar Set — Not a DPS set, but essential for facetanking at Inferno, Tank Role in ToB, and Colosseum waves.
  • Dragon Hunter Crossbow — Beats Twisted Bow at Vorkath, Myth's Guild Dragons, and certain CoX rooms (mutadile, vanguard).
  • Blowpipe — Still the king of small-target DPS for ranged, especially with Dragon Darts. Best for Zulrah phases, Scurrius, and general Slayer.
  • Kodai Wand — Indispensable as a switch for ancient magic casting. The unlimited water runes save inventory space.
  • Virtus Robe Top — BiS for ancient magic (barrage/burst) over Ancestral. Worth the swap for burst tasks and Inferno.
  • Sanguinesti Staff — Budget heals during Maging. The healing passive can save trips at GWD, Zulrah, and Kraken.

② The Bellator Ring — A Special Case

The Bellator Ring (from DT2 Vardorvis) is BiS for any content where you need maximum accuracy over raw strength — notably for the Scythe at ToB (Nylocas, Verzik). Consider adding it as a switch alongside your Ultor. Cost: ~65M.

💰 GP-Efficient Upgrade Order

The order in which you buy gear matters more than the gear itself. A player with 500M GP who buys Ancestral Robes before Rigour has made a catastrophic mistake. Here is the mathematically optimal upgrade path for each budget tier.

① Tier 0 — Prayer Upgrades (Always First)

These are permanent unlocks that benefit every combat style forever. Buy them before any gear item:

  • Rigour — 65M (Ranged DPS +25% / Defence +25%) — The single best upgrade in OSRS. Every ranged attack you ever make is 25% stronger.
  • Augury — 55M (Magic DPS +25% / Defence +25%) — Equally transformative for magic. Essential for Shadow/Barraging.
  • Piety — Free (King's Ransom quest). Already the best melee prayer.

💡 Cost per DPS point: Rigour costs ~65M and gives ~25% more ranged damage. Compare to Twisted Buckler (~14M for +8 ranged strength) — Rigour gives 3x the benefit per GP. Buy prayers first, always.

② Tier 1 — Weapon First, Armour Later (100M-500M Budget)

Weapons provide 3-5x more DPS per GP than armour. Follow this order strictly:

  1. Rigour + Augury — 120M total. Non-negotiable first purchase.
  2. Toxic Blowpipe + Dragon Darts — ~7M. Best value ranged DPS in the game.
  3. Trident of the Swamp — ~4M. All-purpose mage weapon for Zulrah, Kraken, and raids.
  4. Amulet of Torture + Anguish + Occult — ~27M total. Best GP-to-DPS jewellery.
  5. Barrows Gloves / Ferocious Gloves — Free to 600K. Never use anything worse.
  6. Primordial + Pegasian + Eternal Boots — ~46M total. Cerberus boots for all styles.
  7. Armadyl Chestplate + Chainskirt — ~66M. Only after weapon and jewellery upgrades.
  8. Ancestral Hat + Top + Bottom — ~335M. When you are ready for CoX maging.

③ Tier 2 — The Big Four Weapons (500M-2B Budget)

At this budget, you should target one mega-rare weapon at a time. Order by the content you enjoy most:

  1. Twisted Bow — ~880M. Highest priority mega-rare. Dominates Inferno, CoX, Zulrah, and any high-Magic boss.
  2. Scythe of Vitur — ~1.15B. Second priority. Transforms ToB, Phosani's, and size-dependent Slayer tasks.
  3. Tumeken's Shadow — ~980M. Third priority. BiS for ToA, CoX mage hand, and any magic-vulnerable content.
  4. Zaryte Crossbow — ~135M. Add this after your first mega-rare. Essential for Nex and bolt-spec fights.

④ Tier 3 — Max Armour Sets

After owning Rigour/Augury + all mega-rares, upgrade armour in this order for maximum DPS gain per GP:

  1. Masori Fortified Set — ~205M total. The ranged strength bonus is massive with Twisted Bow.
  2. Torva Set — ~375M total. Strength bonus over Bandos is ~+6 max hits. Big for Scythe.
  3. Avernic Defender — ~75M. +5 strength bonus. More DPS than Torva legs upgrade for 1/5 the cost.
  4. Ultor + Venator + Magus Rings — ~260M total. The DT2 rings are major DPS boosts for all styles.
  5. Elidinis' Ward (f) — ~55M. Best mage off-hand. Works with Shadow, Kodai, and Sang staff.
  6. Infernal Cape — Free. The ultimate melee cape. Grind it when you are ready.

📈 Upgrade Priority Cheat Sheet

First 150M GP: Rigour (65M) → Augury (55M) → Torture/Anguish/Occult (27M)

150M-500M GP: Blowpipe + D Darts (7M) → Trident (4M) → Boot Trio (46M) → Armadyl Set (66M) → Zaryte Vambraces (5M)

500M-1.5B GP: Twisted Bow (880M) → Scythe (1.15B) or Shadow (980M) — pick one based on your favourite content

1.5B-3B GP: Masori (205M) → Torva (375M) → Avernic (75M) → DT2 Rings (260M) → Elidinis Ward (55M)

💰 The Real Cost of "Max Gear"

Owning absolute BiS in all three combat styles simultaneously costs approximately 5.0-5.5B GP when accounting for prayer scrolls (Rigour + Augury ~120M shared), shared items (boots, blessings), and all three mega-rare weapons. This does not include untradeable items like Infernal Cape or Ava's Assembler, which require significant time investment regardless of wealth.

Most endgame players never own all three mega-rares simultaneously. The "rich endgame" standard is normally: own one mega-rare weapon (usually Tbow), plus BiS armour in your preferred style, plus Rigour + Augury. That is roughly 1.5-2.5B GP in gear.

🛠️ Budget Alternatives That Actually Work

You do not need full Torva + Scythe to enjoy endgame PvM. Here are the best budget alternatives for every slot that still clear content comfortably:

① Melee — Bandos + Tent Whip (~70M total)

  • Helm: Neitiznot Faceguard (~3.5M) instead of Torva Full Helm (~85M). Same strength bonus, less defence.
  • Body: Bandos Chestplate (~35M) instead of Torva Platebody (~165M). Slightly less strength, but fraction of the cost.
  • Legs: Bandos Tassets (~33M) instead of Torva Platelegs (~125M). Same logic — 95% of the DPS for 20% of the cost.
  • Weapon: Abyssal Tentacle (~1.5M + 250K per 10K charges) instead of Scythe (~1.15B). You lose multi-target DPS but single-target is 80% as effective.
  • Ring: Berserker Ring (i) (~3M) instead of Ultor (~120M). Major DPS difference but 40x cheaper.

② Ranged — Armadyl + Dragon Crossbow (~90M total)

  • Weapon: Dragon Hunter Crossbow (~95M) or regular Dragon Crossbow (~500K) instead of Tbow (~880M). DHCB is BiS at Vorkath anyway.
  • Body/Legs: Armadyl Chestplate (~38M) + Armadyl Chainskirt (~28M) instead of Masori Fortified (~205M). Only ~3% less DPS with Tbow.
  • Shield: Book of Law (~1M) instead of Twisted Buckler (~14M). Only +2 less ranged strength.
  • Ring: Archers Ring (i) (~2M) instead of Venator Ring (~95M). Serviceable until you have cash to burn.

③ Magic — Ahrim's + Trident (~30M total)

  • Weapon: Trident of the Swamp (~4M) instead of Tumeken's Shadow (~980M). The gap is huge, but Trident still clears 95% of content.
  • Body/Legs: Ahrim's Robes (~1.5M set) instead of Ancestral (~335M set). You lose ~8% magic damage, but save 333M.
  • Shield: Mage's Book (~800K) or Book of Darkness (~2M) instead of Elidinis' Ward (f) (~55M). Modest accuracy loss.
  • Ring: Seers Ring (i) (~500K) instead of Magus (~45M). Minimal impact with Trident.

💡 The 80/20 Rule: Budget gear setups achieve roughly 70-85% of max BiS DPS for 5-10% of the cost. A 30M Ahrim's + Trident setup handles Zulrah, Kraken, and cox mage hand just fine. Upgrade only when you are hitting a DPS wall in content like Inferno, ToB, or high-invocation ToA.

❓ FAQ — BiS Gear Questions

What is the single best gear upgrade in OSRS under 100M GP?

Rigour (65M) without question. It permanently increases all your ranged damage by 25% and ranged defence by 25%. Nothing else under 100M comes close to that impact — not a melee weapon, not armour, not jewellery. Augury (55M) is second place for magic users.

Should I buy Twisted Bow or Scythe of Vitur first?

Buy Twisted Bow first unless you primarily do Theatre of Blood or Phosani's Nightmare. The Tbow dominates more content (Inferno, CoX, Zulrah, GWD, Slayer bosses) and is more flexible. Scythe is stronger in its specific niches but less versatile overall.

Is Masori (f) worth the upgrade over Armadyl?

Yes for Tbow users, no for Blowpipe users. Masori Fortified provides +2 to +4 max hits depending on your other gear. With Twisted Bow, this translates to roughly 5-8% more DPS. With Blowpipe, the gain is smaller (~3%). Upgrade to Masori only after you own a Tbow.

Do I need the Infernal Cape or is Fire Cape still viable?

Infernal Cape is absolutely required for endgame PvM. It provides +8 strength bonus vs Fire Cape's +4. That is +4 max hits in melee. Inferno completion is the most significant melee DPS increase you can get after buying a Scythe. If you have not done Inferno yet, it should be your top goal as a melee main.

Avernic Defender vs Dragon Defender — is it worth 75M?

The Avernic Defender gives +3 strength and +3 attack over Dragon Defender. That translates to roughly +1 max hit on average — about a 3-4% DPS increase. At 75M, this is not efficient early on. Buy Avernic after you already own Torva, Scythe, and Ultor Ring.

Tumeken's Shadow vs Kodai Wand — which should I buy for mage?

Tumeken's Shadow is the primary weapon; Kodai is a niche switch. The Shadow is BiS for all powered-staff combat (high DPS maging at ToA, CoX, GWD). Kodai Wand is only better when you need ancient magic (barraging Slayer tasks, Inferno waves, Nylocas). Buy the Shadow first, then add Kodai as a switch later.

Are DT2 rings (Ultor/Venator/Magus) worth the price?

Absolutely yes for Ultor, situationally for Venator and Magus. Ultor Ring (+12 strength) is a massive upgrade over Berserker Ring (i) (+8). The DPS increase is ~4-6% for melee. Venator is strong with Tbow (+8 ranged strength vs Archers +4). Magus is least impactful unless paired with Tumeken's Shadow, where the passive triples the damage bonus.

What is the best melee weapon for 1x1 targets?

For single-target melee DPS on 1x1 NPCs, the Blade of Saeldor (c) (~75M) or Inquisitor's Mace (~350M) beats the Scythe of Vitur. The Scythe only hits 3 targets on a 3x3 NPC; on 1x1 it only gets 1 hit splat. Saeldor + full Torva + Ultor is the highest single-target melee DPS combo available.

How much GP do I need to start raiding efficiently?

CoX: 50M (Trident + Blowpipe + DWH + BGS + Void/Rags). ToA: 80M (Same + Bowfa + Crystal armour). ToB: 200M (Scythe or Blade + full melee gear + Blowpipe). You do not need max gear to raid — the first 50M gets you through entry-level raids just fine.
