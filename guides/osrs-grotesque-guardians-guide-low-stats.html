2. Dawn & Dusk Phase Mechanics

The Grotesque Guardians fight has two distinct phases. You fight Dawn first, then Dusk. Understanding each phase's mechanics is critical for low-stat survival.

PhaseAttack StylePrayerSpecial AttacksHP Threshold
Dawn (Phase 1)Ranged + MagicProtect from MissilesBurning gargoyles, fire drop100% → 0%
Dusk (Phase 2)Melee (+ rock throw)Protect from MeleeWing flap, rock throw100% → 0%

Dawn's Special Attacks (Phase 1):

  • Burning Gargoyles: Dawn summons 3 burning gargoyles that chase you. They deal heavy damage on contact. Run to the opposite side of the roof until they despawn. Do NOT try to tank them — they can combo 30+ damage quickly.
  • Fire Drop: Dawn flies up and orange tiles appear on the ground. After 2-3 seconds, fire drops on every orange tile. Move to any non-orange tile immediately. This attack hits 20-30+ if you're caught in it.
  • Enrage (50% HP): At half health, Dawn attacks faster. Eat to full HP before pushing past 50%.

Dusk's Special Attacks (Phase 2):

  • Wing Flap: Dusk flaps his wings and pushes you back several tiles. Step back one tile toward him immediately to resume attacking.
  • Rock Throw: Dusk throws a rock that cannot be prayed against. It deals up to 15 damage. This is chip damage — eat when HP drops below 40.
  • Enrage (50% HP): Dusk attacks faster. Use your special attacks here to burst him down before the increased DPS overwhelms your food supply.

6. Advanced Tips for Low-Stat Kills

  • Use a house tab between kills for banking. A POH with a rejuvenation pool lets you restore all stats, prayer, and HP instantly, then teleport back via the Slayer Ring to the Slayer Tower. This dramatically increases kills per hour.
  • Spec at the start of each phase. Use Dragon Claws or DDS special attacks immediately when Dawn spawns and again at Dusk's enrage phase. The burst damage shortens the fight's most dangerous windows.
  • Prioritize avoiding burning gargoyles over dealing damage. They deal significantly more damage than Dawn's auto-attacks. Run first, attack second.
  • Count Dawn's fire drop timing. After Dawn flies up, you have 2-3 seconds before fire hits. Position yourself near a safe tile BEFORE she flies up, so you only need to move 1-2 tiles.
  • Stay close to Dusk to minimize rock throws. Dusk throws rocks more frequently when you're far away. Hugging him reduces incoming chip damage.

7. Common Mistakes

  1. Standing in the fire drop. Dawn's fire tiles hit 20-30+. This is the #1 cause of death for new Guardians killers. Watch the ground and move immediately.
  2. Tanking burning gargoyles. They each hit 10+ and there are 3 of them. Running is always the right move.
  3. Not using Piety/offensive prayers. With lower stats, you need every DPS advantage. Piety increases your damage by 25% — that's worth the prayer drain.
  4. Forgetting to bring a spare roofing tile. If you die or teleport out, you need another tile to re-enter. Always bring a spare.
  5. Using the wrong combat style. Both Dawn and Dusk are weak to crush attacks. If using a whip (slash), you're losing damage. Consider an Abyssal Bludgeon or Zamorakian Hasta for crush.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What stats do I need for Grotesque Guardians?

A: Minimum: 75 Slayer, active Gargoyle task, and a roofing tile. Combat: 75+ Attack/Strength/Defence for consistent kills. A Slayer helmet (i) is the single most important gear piece — the 16.67% damage boost is massive. With 70+ melees you can still get kills but expect 1-2 kills per trip.

Q: How do I access the Grotesque Guardians?

A: Have an active Gargoyle task. Obtain a brittle roofing tile (gargoyle drop) or cornerstone tile (200 Slayer points). Use the tile on the roof entrance at the top of the Slayer Tower. The tile is consumed per trip. Cornerstone tiles from the Slayer shop are recommended.

Q: What is the best strategy for the Dawn phase?

A: During Dawn, use Protect from Missiles. When she summons burning gargoyles, run away — they despawn after a few seconds. When she flies up, watch for orange tiles and move to any safe tile. Spec at the start. At 50% HP, Dawn gains attack speed — eat up.

Q: Should I use crush or slash against Grotesque Guardians?

A: Both Dawn and Dusk are weak to crush attacks. An Abyssal Bludgeon or Zamorakian Hasta (crush style) will outperform an Abyssal Whip (slash style). However, the whip is still viable if you don't have a crush weapon. The Slayer Helmet (i) boost matters far more than the attack style.

💀 Master the Rooftop Guardians

Grotesque Guardians are one of the most mechanically engaging Slayer bosses in OSRS. The Dawn-to-Dusk transition, the special attacks, the enrage phases — mastering this fight feels genuinely rewarding. With low stats, focus on survival over speed. Each kill teaches you something new about positioning and timing.

Get your Slayer Helmet (i), grab a cornerstone tile, and take on the rooftop duo.
