OSRS Sailing Money Making Guide 2026: Profitable Voyages Ranked by GP/Hour

Sailing is one of the few skills that can pay you back while you train. From Deep Sea Trawling to Ice Dragon killing, this guide covers every confirmed Sailing money-making method with community-verified GP/hour estimates. All data sourced from community testing and OSRS Wiki.

For mid-level players, Mercenary Shipwreck Salvaging (73 Sailing) offers ~330K+ GP/h with minimal requirements. The AFK-friendly option is standard Shipwreck Salvaging, earning 20M+ GP all the way to 99.

🎣 Deep Sea Trawling — Marlin Fishing

GP/h: ~4,700,000 — Current highest GP/hour Sailing method

RequirementLevel
Sailing78
Fishing91
Construction72
ShipSloop with Trawling Net

① How It Works

Deep Sea Trawling at high levels targets Marlin — a new fish that heals 24 HP and sells for very high prices due to its healing value and rarity. You sail to deep sea zones, deploy your trawling net, and haul in the catch. Requires efficient navigation and strong ship upgrades to maximize hauls per hour.

② Requirements

  • Sloop (level 50) with 2 facility slots
  • Trawling Net facility installed
  • Teak Hull (31) or better for speed
  • Ironmen also need: Crafting 62, Woodcutting 66 for self-sufficient gear
💡 Note on GP/h

This GP/h assumes optimal gear and efficient routes. Actual earnings depend on Marlin GE prices, which fluctuate with supply. The Trawler's Trust drink (from Sea Charting) improves catch rates.

GP/h: ~4,500,000 (with Slayer task) / ~2,400,000 (without task)

① Location & Method

Ice Dragons are encountered through Sailing routes in frozen sea regions. They require 87 Sailing to access and offer high-value drops including Frost Dragon Bones (high Prayer XP), valuable runes, and rare equipment drops.

② With vs Without Slayer Task

When assigned Ice Dragons as a Slayer task, damage bonuses apply, kill times are faster, and supply costs are lower. Without a task, kills take longer and GP/h drops to ~2.3M. Always try to get a Slayer assignment before farming these.

③ Recommended Setup

  • Magic setup: Best-in-slot mage gear with Ice spells
  • Melee setup: High strength gear, Dragon Hunter Lance or equivalent
  • Prayer: Protect from Magic (43) recommended for their magic attacks
💡 Profit Tip

Frost Dragon Bones are in high demand for Prayer training. Prices fluctuate based on DXP events and meta shifts. Check GE prices before committing to extended trips.

GP/h: ~330,000+

RequirementLevel
Sailing73
Construction59
Magic55

① Low-Investment, Good Return

Mercenary Shipwrecks are special salvage zones unlocked at 73 Sailing. They contain high-value alchemy items, runes, and resources. The wrecks respawn frequently, making this a consistent money maker without the RNG of monster drops.

② Key Items to Expect

  • High-alchemy items (runite items, dragon equipment)
  • Runes (death, blood, soul)
  • Building materials (planks, nails, bars)
  • Occasional rare salvage (Sailor's Amulet)
💡 Best at 86+ With Dragon Hook

While the Dragon Salvaging Hook (86, 50M+ GP) is expensive, it only adds ~5% efficiency. The main profit driver is your Salvaging Sorting Station (42) and a high-capacity Cargo Hold.

① How Crystal Gathering Works

Using the Crystal Extractor facility on your ship, you gather crystals from crystal-rich sea zones. The extractor provides 350 Sailing XP per minute while active (requires clicking), and the crystals themselves are valuable crafting and Magic materials.

② Scaling Factors

  • Sailing level: Higher levels unlock better crystal zones
  • Extractor tier: Better extractors gather faster and yield higher-quality crystals
  • Route optimization: Knowledge of respawn timers and efficient routes doubles earnings

🚢 Trade & Cargo Routes

GP/h: ~350,000 to 600,000 (depends on route and cargo type)

① How It Works

Transporting high-value cargo between ports. Unlike Courier Tasks (which give XP), dedicated trade routes focus purely on profit. Buy goods cheap at one port and sell at another for a markup.

  • Market knowledge: Buy low at producing ports, sell high at consuming ports
💡 Consistent, Low-Variance Income

Unlike monster drops or salvaging, trade routes offer predictable, steady income. No RNG, no rare drops — just consistent GP based on your ship's efficiency. Excellent for players who prefer planning over combat.

❓ Sailing Money Making FAQs

What's the best Sailing money maker for low levels?

Courier Tasks (level 1+) offer modest profit while training. Shipwreck Salvaging (level 15+) becomes profitable from the start — even at low levels, salvaged materials sell on the GE. At level 53, Large Shipwrecks provide ~1.8M GP/h.

Is Sailing profitable overall?

Yes — unlike many buyable skills, Sailing can be net profitable. Shipwreck Salvaging from 15-99 earns 20M+ GP. Deep Sea Trawling and Ice Dragon killing generate millions per hour. However, premium ship upgrades (Rosewood hull, Dragon hook) are expensive investments.

How much GP can I make AFK with Sailing?

Shipwreck Salvaging is the AFK option. At level 53+, expect ~1.8M GP/h. At level 73+, mercenary wrecks offer ~330K+ GP/h. It's not the highest GP/h but requires the least attention of any Sailing money maker.

What's the highest GP/h Sailing method?

Deep Sea Trawling for Marlin at ~4.7M GP/h (78 Sailing, 91 Fishing). Ice Dragon killing on task at ~4.5M GP/h (87 Sailing). Both require high levels and good equipment but offer endgame-tier profit rates.

Do I need high Sailing to make good money?

Not necessarily. Shipwreck Salvaging starts at 15 and scales with you. Trade routes work at any level with the right route knowledge. High-level methods (Trawling, Ice Dragons) offer much higher GP/h but require 70-90+ Sailing.

Are trade routes better than salvaging for profit?

Trade routes offer consistent, low-variance income (350K-1.5M GP/h) without RNG. Salvaging has higher ceiling (330K+ at high levels) but depends on what loot the wrecks contain. For players who prefer predictable GP, trade routes are better. For gamblers, salvaging is more exciting.

What ship upgrades help with money making?

Priority order: Cargo Hold capacity → Ship speed (Hull + Sails) → Salvaging Hook tier → Crystal Extractor. Each upgrade directly increases GP/h by reducing travel time or increasing per-trip profit.

Can Ironmen make money with Sailing?

Yes. Shipwreck Salvaging provides materials, runes, and alchables. Resource gathering (trees, ores) gives self-sufficient materials. Thieving stalls (82 for ore, 87 for cannonballs) are especially useful for Ironmen needing ship-building supplies.
