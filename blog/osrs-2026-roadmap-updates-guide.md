## 1. Sailing Expansion — The First New Skill in Years

Sailing launched in late 2025 as OSRS's first new skill since **Construction in 2006** — yes, 20 years. The 2026 roadmap expands it significantly.

### What We Know

- **New islands and exploration zones** — the Sailing skill originally launched with a starter set of voyages. The 2026 expansion adds high-level content including new islands, resources, and exploration mechanics.
- **New vessels and customization** — better ships, upgrade paths, and cosmetic options.
- **High-level Sailing content** — activities and rewards aimed at level 70+ Sailing.

### Why This Matters for Traffic and Gameplay

Sailing is the biggest OSRS content addition of the decade. Every OSRS content creator, YouTuber, and guide site is covering it. If you have a Sailing guide on your site, **now is the time to refresh it with 2026 expansion details** — that content will rank.

### How to Prepare

- If you have not started Sailing yet, begin now. You do not want to be level 1 Sailing when the expansion drops — the early levels are slow and you will be competing with every other player for voyage slots.
- Stock up on **GP and supplies** — new Sailing content usually introduces new money sinks. Having a healthy bank helps.
- Read the [OSRS Sailing Guide on OSRS Guru](https://osrsguru.com/guides/osrs-sailing-guide-2026.html) — we keep it updated with the latest expansion info.

## 2. The Fourth Raid — First New Raid in 4 Years

The last major raid was **Tombs of Amascut (ToA)** in 2022. We are finally getting raid #4 in 2026.

### What We Know

- **Jagex has not revealed the name yet**, but the community is already speculating based on lore hints.
- Expected to be **high-end PvM content** targeted at level 110+ combat accounts with endgame gear.
- New unique reward table — expect new bis (best-in-slot) gear, cosmetics, and possibly a new pet.
- Will likely surpass ToA and ToB in difficulty, based on Jagex's pattern of making each raid more complex than the last.

### Why This Is Huge

Raids are the single biggest GP and content drivers in OSRS. When a new raid drops:
- **YouTube views explode** — every major OSRS creator puts out a day-1 guide.
- **New meta gear** — the unique rewards change the PvM landscape. ToA gave us the Tumeken's Shadow. What will raid #4 give us?
- **Raids are social content** — you need a team. Clans reactivate. Friends come back to the game.

### How to Prepare

- **Max your combat stats** — if you are not 99 Attack, Strength, Defence, Ranged, Magic, and Prayer, make 2026 the year you get there.
- **Learn ToB and ToA** — the new raid will assume you understand raid mechanics. If you cannot do ToB, you will struggle with raid #4.
- **Farm GP now** — bis gear for new raids is expensive on day 1. If you have 100M+ in the bank, you can buy the new meta gear immediately and start farming the raid efficiently.
- **Practice prayer flicking** — every new raid introduces more complex mechanics. If you cannot flick, start learning now.

Check out the [OSRS Boss Guides on OSRS Guru](https://osrsguru.com/bosses.html) — we have up-to-date strategies for ToB, ToA, CoX, and all the endgame content that will prepare you for raid #4.

## 3. Leagues IV — The Limited-Time Game Mode That Breaks the Economy

Leagues are OSRS's most popular limited-time game mode. You start a fresh account with massive XP and drop rate buffs, pick a region to unlock, and race to get as far as possible before the League ends.

### What We Know

- **Leagues IV is coming in Q3 2026**.
- **Leagues III (Trailblazer Reloaded)** was in 2023. Leagues IV will likely introduce new regions, new relics, and new exclusive cosmetics.
- Based on the pattern, Leagues IV will probably be a **different mechanic from Trailblazer** — possibly a return to the original League format (pick a region, locked to that region) or something entirely new.

### Why Leagues Matter

Leagues generate **massive traffic** for OSRS content creators. When Leagues III dropped:
- OSRS YouTube views hit all-time highs.
- Guide sites (including OSRS Wiki) saw traffic spikes of 300%+.
- The OSRS subreddit hit #1 on r/all multiple times during Leagues.

If you run an OSRS content site, **Leagues content is the single highest-ROI content you can publish in 2026**.

### How to Prepare

- **Follow OSRS Guru's Leagues hub** — we will have day-1 region guides, relic tier lists, and optimal pathing strategies.
- **Start thinking about your League strategy now** — the best players plan their region order and relic choices weeks in advance.
- **If you create content**, start drafting your Leagues guides now. The window between the League announcement and launch is when search volume peaks.
