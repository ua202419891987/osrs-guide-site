❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which money maker is best for beginners?

For absolute beginners (no stats, F2P), the best money maker is picking up items with zero requirements — cowhides, loot from ogress warriors, or telegrabbing Wine of Zamorak at 33 Magic. These methods earn 50K-200K GP/h with zero upfront investment. Read our F2P no-stats guide for the full list.

How much GP can I make F2P?

F2P money making caps around 200K-250K GP/h with telegrabbing Wine of Zamorak at 33 Magic. Other solid F2P methods include crafting gold amulets (50K-120K GP/h), killing ogress warriors (120K-180K GP/h with good melee stats), and collecting swamp tar (30K-60K GP/h with no requirements). For bond farming, expect 30-40 hours of F2P grinding per bond at current 13-14M bond prices.

Is flipping worth it in 2026?

Yes — especially after the 2026 GE update that removed the 2.147B max cash limit. GE flipping is the only money maker that requires zero combat, zero quests, and zero skill levels. With 1M starting capital, experienced flippers average 300K-1M GP/h. The key is learning margin checking and item selection. Our flipping guide for beginners walks you through the entire process starting with just 100K GP.

Best money maker for mid-game players?

Mid-game players (combat 60-100) have the most options. Green dragons (300K-500K GP/h) are the entry point. Slayer scales from 200K GP/h at level 50 to 3M+ GP/h at level 95+. Birdhouse + herb runs (200K-500K GP/day) are essential passive income. For skilling-focused players, blast furnace and hunter can push 500K-1M GP/h. Check our mid-game roadmap for the full progression path.

How to transition from F2P to members money making?

The transition is smoother than most players think. Once you buy your first bond, prioritize quests that unlock high-profit methods: Fairy Tale I (fairy rings), Lost City (dagannoth tasks), Dragon Slayer II (Vorkath). Start with green dragons (300K-500K GP/h) and birdhouse runs (50K-200K GP/day). Within a week of consistent play, you should be earning enough to sustain membership through bonds. See our bond farming guide for the step-by-step plan.

What are the best AFK money makers?

For maximum gold with minimum attention, these are the top AFK methods: Fishing infernal eels (Level 80, 300K-500K GP/h, click every 2-3 minutes), NMZ herb boxes (daily, 30K-80K GP/day for 1 minute of work), Smithing at blast furnace (Level 60, 500K-800K GP/h with moderate attention), and Farming herb runs (every 90 minutes, 100K-200K per run). Our AFK money making guide ranks 20+ methods by BOTH GP/hour and attention level.

Which bosses are best for consistent GP?

For consistent GP without relying on rare drops: Vorkath (3M-4.5M GP/h, regular drops are excellent), Zulrah (2.5M-5M GP/h, scales and unique drops), Giant Mole (500K-800K GP/h, very consistent for lower stats), and Sarrachis (1M-1.5M GP/h, good for mid-game irons). For the definitive ranking, see our boss profit comparison covering every boss from KQ to Nex.

💡 Final Tips

Here's the truth most players don't want to hear: the best money maker is the one you actually enjoy doing. Consistent 500K GP/h from a method you like will always beat a theoretical 5M GP/h from a boss you dread. Here's our advice:

  • Set a goal. Whether it's a bond, a Dragon Scimitar, or a Twisted Bow — knowing what you're saving for makes grinding purposeful.
  • Mix methods. Do 30 minutes of active PvM, then switch to AFK skilling while you watch a show. Your GP/h over a 4-hour session will be higher than burning out on one method.
  • Reinvest wisely. Every million GP should be working for you. Upgrade gear that unlocks better money makers, not cosmetics. See how to spend GP wisely for the full breakdown.
  • Never stop questing. Quests unlock the best money makers. Dragon Slayer II (Vorkath), Regicide (Zulrah), and Song of the Elves (Prif bossing) should be priority targets.

Now stop reading and go make some GP. Your bank isn't going to fill itself.
